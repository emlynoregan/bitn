Booborowie Hall was packed for the performances of A Pirate’s Life on Thursday afternoon and Thursday & Friday nights.  Judy Giles wrote the script and some of the music.  Friday’s performance was introduced by guest Xavier Minniecon, weather presenter on Channel 9 television.
