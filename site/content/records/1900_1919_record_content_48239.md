Sapper Harry S. Blunt of Hanson writes of the exploits of his brother Keith, who was wounded while rescuing a runner and was unconscious in the field for two days before being found.  He also refers to Clarrie Bishop’s death and to Hedley and Stan Bishop.  [In all c. ⅔ column.]
