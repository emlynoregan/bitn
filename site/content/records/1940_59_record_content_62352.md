Burra Primary School

The so-called ‘condemned cell’ has been renovated and is now a fine classroom, used by Miss Mounce.  With this change the school can be accommodated on the one site, whereas last year 1½ classes had to use St Mary’s Parish Hall.  [The 28 new scholars for the year are listed.]
