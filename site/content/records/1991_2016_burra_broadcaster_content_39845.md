Burra Yacht Club Regatta – Regulations for the race are printed and include:

Craft to be a maximum of two metres by 2 metres.

Craft to be man powered.

Maximum of two crew.

Paddles are allowed.

Craft must have a sail area of at least one square metre.

The other six rules are basically concerned with safety issues.
