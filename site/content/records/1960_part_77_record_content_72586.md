Copper Festival plans are taking shape.

A Petition to the Minister of Roads seeks his support for the sealing of National Route 64; the Burra-Booborowie-Spalding Road.

Football.  Best players for the Mid North League were chosen at a cabaret at Manoora Institute on Saturday night.

‘A’ Grade: the Mail Medal went to Greg Stone (Riverton)

‘B’ Grade winner was Ian Petherick of United

Senior Colts winner was Peter Koster of United

Junior Colts winner was Terry McKeough of United.
