Threatened plant found near Burra.  A small ground cover type native plant has been found at Ford’s Corner.  It is the small scurf pea; Cullen parvum.  It is rated as endangered.  Three or four clumps have been found at the site and it has also been found in the Mokota Conservation Park.  There are also a few plants south of Oodla Wirra.
