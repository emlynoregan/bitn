Salvation Army.  There was a good attendance at the Easter Meeting conducted by Stan Sullivan assisted by bandsmen from Peterborough.  On 24 April the Kent Town Salvation Army Band will be in Burra and an open air meeting will be held in Market Square at 6.15 p.m.
