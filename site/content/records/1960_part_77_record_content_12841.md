Advt.  Elder, Smith & Co. on instructions from Dr David Steele Sen. and Trustees of the ‘Koo-owie Trust, 	will auction ‘Koo-owie’ of 2,169 acres freehold, 12½ miles south of Burra, on 5 October at Burra RSL Hall.  Disposed as 34 paddocks with a substantial nine-roomed stone house etc.
