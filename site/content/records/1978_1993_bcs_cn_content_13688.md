National All Schools Athletics Championships will be contested in Sydney 12 -14 December.  Burra students Petrea Piggott and Justin Hall have been named in the SA team.

Petrea will compete in the 100, 200 & 400 m, the 90 m hurdles, triple jump, 4 x 100 m and the 4 x 400 m relays.

Justin will compete in the U17 400 m and the 4 x 400 m relay.
