Adchem Profile

Adchem, Burra, is the largest production facility of its kind in the world, producing high quality cupric oxide.  It has been developed to use a broad base of copper bearing materials.

Cupric Oxide has many uses and some of the principal ones are:

Copper chrome arsenate compounds used in the perma pinewood preservatives.

Production of friction material for brakes and clutches

As a catalyst in chemical industries

A colouring agent in glass and ceramics

A raw material for copper chemical production

Production of ferrites for the electronics industry

Animal feed supplements

Trace element fertilizers in agriculture

Manufacture of fungicides

A component in air bag safety systems in vehicles.
