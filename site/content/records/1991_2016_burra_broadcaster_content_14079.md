The Burra Art Gallery Board held its AGM on 21 September.  Since 1994 the major part of the gallery has been renovated.  There is a membership in excess of 100.  At the AGM elected were: Chairman, Peter Harvey; Secretary, Colin Endean and Treasurer, David Little.
