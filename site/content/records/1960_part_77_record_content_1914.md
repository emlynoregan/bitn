The Bishop of Pt Pirie, Rev Dr Bryan Gallagher will visit Burra next weekend.  He will probably attend the St Joseph’s School Physical Culture Display and Sports Day on Saturday afternoon and on Sunday will administer confirmation at St Joseph’s Church.
