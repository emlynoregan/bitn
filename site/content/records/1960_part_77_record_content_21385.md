Aberdeen Basketball Club held its wind-up function for the 1964 season, with a dinner at the Oddfellows’ Hall Burra North on 22 August.  Awards:

‘A’ Grade	Best player was Yvonne Williams

Most improved player was Lynette Gill

‘B’ Grade	Best player was Kaye Heinrich

Most improved player was Susan Gill

‘C’ Grade	Best player was Rosalind Gill

Most improved player was Susan Richards

The ‘C’ Grade team was runner-up in their final.
