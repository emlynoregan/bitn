November

A committee was formed for the Main Street Program.

Rural Youth State Committee held a meeting and workshop in Burra.

Local Band ‘Touch Wood’ won the Battle of the Bands in Burra.

Burra Uniting Church Op Shop celebrates 10 years of operation.

Burra Kindergarten children visit Geralka Farm.

Elizabeth Robertson was elected chairperson at the local National Trust AGM.

BCS AGM reported on developments in the agricultural course at BCS and it was announced that the final issue of the School Community Newsletter would be on 22 December.  It had filled a gap when the town was without a paper.
