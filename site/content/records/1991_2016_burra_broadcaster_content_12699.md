The SA Stud Merino Sheepbreeders Assoc. gala night on Tuesday drew visitors from across Australia and from Germany and the USA.  The award for Merino Capital Champion Ram went to Deloshanda Stud for the third year in a row.  The Champion Pair Award went to Old Ashrose Stud.  Sales reported from most studs were a slight improvement on last year.
