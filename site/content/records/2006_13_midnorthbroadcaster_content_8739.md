Sen. Constable Steven West has arrived in Burra. He comes to the town from tome with the Port Adelaide traffic division.  The family has moved into a new four bedroom home provided by the State Government.  Sen. Constable West has also served with the Federal Police at Adelaide Airport as well as at the Fort Largs Academy.
