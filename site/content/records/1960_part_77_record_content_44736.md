Burra Town Council, 17 February

Hill Street is to be sealed for 7 chains.

R. Toms was granted permission to camp in the mine area.

The Gestetner machine is to be sold to the DC of Hallett for $100.

The cost of a three foot chain fence with two double gates with a 12 ft opening it to be obtained for the next meeting.

Penguin rubbish bin to be placed at Hurley’s road-house.

Council requests an E & WS employee be stationed at Burra for maintenance work.
