Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club had a very successful AGM with about 30 attending.  Local veterinary surgeon Dr Jane Parker was the guest speaker.  Elected: President, Merilyn Baulderstone; Vice-President, Adrian Green and Treasurer, Stephanie Whitehead.  The Secretary is Bev. Rigby and Mike Thomas was elected a life member.
