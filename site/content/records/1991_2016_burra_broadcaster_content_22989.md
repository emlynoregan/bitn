Chris Baulderstone & Marcia South have taken over the Market Square Snack Bar from Chris’s father, Gerry.  The Baulderstone name has been evident in Market Square for 77 years.  Gerry’s father, Ted Baulderstone, ran a green grocery, followed by Gerry’s brother Allen and then Gerry and now Chris.  [Photo of the proprietors.]
