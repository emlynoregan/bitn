The Annual Mid Northern District Synod of the Methodist Church has been held at Hallett.  It was resolved to dissolve the present Synod and Crystal Brook, Gladstone and Jamestown will now join the Northern District.  Red Hill and Port Broughton will join the Yorke Peninsula District and Clare, Spalding, Hallett-Terowie and Burra will go to the Middle District.
