BCS.  The Interschool Swimming Carnival saw BCS dominating in Division 2 to win the shield for the third time in four years.  In the handicap competition BCS blitzed the opposition to win the shield with a margin of 115 points.  Three students broke records:

Kyron Statton in the U13 backstroke, Rachael Ford in U13 breaststroke and Karen Jennings in the open freestyle.
