Mr H.R. Shakeshaft will take up duties as Burra postmaster on 3 June.  He has previously worked at Burra Post Office for four years, since when he has served as Senior Postal Clerk at Gladstone and as Postmaster at Hawker.  His interests include swimming, golf, cricket and photography. He was Secretary of the Burra Cricket Club for two years while here previously.  He has been successful in National and International Photographic Exhibitions.
