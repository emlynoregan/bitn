Obituary.  William Henry Lloyd died 7 September 1964.  He was born at Erskine in 1889 and came with his parents to the Ironmine district and took up a property for farming.  He has remained there ever since.  He married Ivy Flower 28 July 1917.  He was a tower of strength in the Ironmine and Redruth Methodist Churches, serving in many ways.  For 26 years he was Superintendent of the Sunday school and a leader in young peopleâ€™s organisations.  He was Secretary of the Ironmine Trust until he retired to Burra and he served many times as Circuit Steward.  He was a life member of the Burra Show Society and President of the Burra Show Committee and of the Burra Library Committee.  He was a member of the Masonic Lodge.  He leaves a widow and two children: Mr Ray Lloyd and Meryl, Mrs R.J. White.
