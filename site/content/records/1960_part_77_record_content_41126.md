Obituary.  Mrs Amy Bannear of Saddleworth died in the Riverton Hospital on Saturday aged 89.  She was one of the finest cooks in the district and she and her husband W.F. Bannear catered for hundreds of sales, shows and the old Martindale Races.  [Born Amy Margaret Baldwin 1 October Walloway: died 28 July 1968.]
