Obituary.  Albert Gustav Blieschke of Farrell Flat died at Burra Hospital 13 April aged 73.  He was the son of the late Mr & Mrs Henry Blieschke and was born at Melrose 23 July 1891.  On 9 May 1922 he married Miss Gertrude Gersch of Kilkerran and began farming at Booleroo Centre.  Later in 1928 he farmed at Edillilie on Eyre Peninsula and in 1948 moved with his family to farm three miles north of Farrell Flat.  He retired in 1964 to live in Farrell Flat.  He is survived by his widow and two sons: Ron of Farrell Flat and Eric of Leigh Creek.

[Born 22 July 1891 Hundred of Wongyarra.]
