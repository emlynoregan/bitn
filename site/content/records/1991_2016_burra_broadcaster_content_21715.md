Uniting Church OpShop recently held its AGM and celebrated its 16th successful year of operation.  The committee regret the loss of Jill Piggott (Secretary) and Jenny Statton (Treasurer), both of whom are shortly leaving for Adelaide.  The OpShop is an important source of community and church support.
