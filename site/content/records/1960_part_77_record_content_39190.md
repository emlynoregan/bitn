Ivon Wardle JP was born in Burra and is now assistant Town Clerk of Murray Bridge.  He has just won the seat of Murray in the House of assembly for the LCL.  He was born at Burra 23 April 1919 to Stanley and Victoria Wardle, nee Smelt.  Mr Bert Wardle and Mr Lewis Gare are his uncles.  The family moved to Murray Bridge district in 1927.  He was educated at the Institute of Technology, the Adelaide Conservatorium and Wesley College.  After service in the RAAF he was ordained a Methodist Minister and served the Booborowie-Willalo Circuit 1949-53, while living at Spalding.  In 1953 he was appointed Immigration Chaplain and visited many parts of Europe and the UK.  Family health reasons compelled him to retire from the ministry.  He married Miss Dorothy Briggs and has three children.  The eldest, Malcolm, is a bank officer, Lynette is an infant teacher and Ian is a trainee teacher.
