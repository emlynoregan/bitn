Marriage.  Redruth Methodist Church

Photograph of Miss Glenda Rawlins of Mt Bryan married Mr Peter Jenvey.

The marriage was performed by the late Rev. Albert Bell.

[86, 14 (2) 9 Apr. 1963, p.7 adds the information that Glenda Enid Rawlins was the eldest daughter of Mr & Mrs C. Rawlins of Mt Bryan and Mr Peter John Jenvey came from Mogumber WA.]
