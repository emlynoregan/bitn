Advt.  Elders-GM, on instructions from Mrs N.T. Irwin & Mr W.E. Hawker, will conduct an auction in the Burra RSL Hall 22 November.

Lot 1.  ‘Coralinga’ 1,422 acres comprising sections 502-506 & 511-514 Hd Ayers, disposed as six paddocks with stone house, etc.

Lot 2.  ‘Finch’s’ 521 acres comprising sections 507, 509 & 510 Hd Ayers, disposed as two paddocks.

Both have been part of Bungaree Stud since 1922 – on the Burra-Booborowie Road, some four miles southeast of Booborowie.
