The RSL Annual Dinner was held on Saturday night.  Fifty people attended the meal provided by the Ladies Auxiliary.  The toast to ‘Fallen Comrades’ was made by Rev. Barry May.  [His speech is printed.]

Frank Pollard of Jamestown Sub-branch was made a life-member.  He has been an office-bearer and committeeman for over 20 years and has been coming to the Burra Annual Dinners for 30 years.

Four WWI veterans attended: Albert Bruce, Wilf Nankivell, Charlie Bennett and Jack Williams.
