September

Springvale Merino Stud had top fleece at the Royal Adelaide Show.

Ambulance members received medals for long service:

Brian Thamm (33 years)

Danny O’Bryan (19 years)

Ralph Oakley (26 years)

Peter Ryan (12 years)

Burra Cycling Classic weekend.

ABC produced a documentary on Burra

‘Mail’ Medal awarded to BBH’s Rob Stephens as the Association’s Best and Fairest for 1993.

BBH Junior Colts won the Premiership and the ‘B’ Grade were runners-up in their division.

Many suggestions for Burra’s Jubilee 150 were announced.

Burra Burra DC to commission an art work by Allyson Parsons (as part of J150).

Burra Burra DC acquires the old Post and Telegraph Office for $132,000.
