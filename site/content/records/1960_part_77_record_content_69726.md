Weather.  There has been unseasonal rain in the last week.

Poonunda (Jack Oates) reports 75 mm to 6 p.m. yesterday.  Burra reports 75.1 mm.

Other falls reported are Collinsville 290, ‘The Gums’ 192, Woolgangi 190 and Koomooloo 175.

[Presumably these figures are in points rather than mm.]
