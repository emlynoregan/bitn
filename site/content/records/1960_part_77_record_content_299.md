The Morrisons are leaving Burra after Colin Morrison had been teaching in the Burra District for 20 years and at BHS for the last three.  Mr J. Colin Morrison has been appointed headmaster at Wudinna Area School.  His BA degree was formally conferred at a ceremony in Adelaide last December.  He is presently the Secretary of the Burra RSL and was a fighter pilot and instructor in WWII, being awarded the AFC.  He played cricket and tennis in Burra.  Mrs Morrison was formerly Miss McWaters of Burra and has played tennis and badminton here.  She was a member of the Kooringa Methodist Guild.  They and there children Heather, Dean and Jane will leave soon for Wudinna.
