The Market Square Rotunda resounded to the beat of drummers from Burra Community School recently as part of ‘Drumbeat’; part of an electives program for middle school students aiming to promote social understanding and connection through a team drumming experience.  The program aims to raise self esteem and to enhance co-operation between participants.  Eight students from years six to nine participated.
