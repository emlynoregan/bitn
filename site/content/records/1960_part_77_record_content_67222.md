Burra North Red Cross.  Annual Report. Membership was 41 with an average attendance 14-18.  They met at the Aberdeen Football Club Rooms.  Major fund-raising for the year was catering for the Masonic Ball.  They also had a stall at the Copper Festival and served refreshments when the Blood Bank visited Burra.  In the year our Patroness Mrs Heinrich died and Mrs Cox, another staunch member, left to live at Aldersgate Village.  Elected: President, Mrs I. Hirschausen; Secretary, Mrs R.M. Jennison and Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.
