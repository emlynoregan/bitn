Burra Burra DC

Council agreed to place a streetlight in Grainger Street near the Guide Hall.

The S.T. Gill watercolours presently in the Burra Mine Museum will in the long term be displayed at the Burra Art Gallery.

On advice from the Environmental Protection Agency, Adelaide Chemical Co. has been allowed to dispose of non-friable asbestos waste in the Burra Dump.
