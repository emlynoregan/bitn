Burra Craft Fair will be held this weekend at Redruth Gaol.  Among about 29 stalls the other attractions will include face painting by Katrina Cardinale, fire engine rides, food and drinks.  Entrance is $3, with children under 12 free.  It is a joint effort of the National Trust BRTBA and Goyder Tourism Working Party.
