‘Avondale’ a farming property at Leighton, belonging to the Scholz family, was auctioned on 14 May at Burra Sports Complex.  It was sold in three lots:

Lot 1 of 819 acres was sold to local neighbours for $405,000.

Lot 2 of 539 acres sold for $265,000.

Lot 3, the homestead block of 238 acres sold for $129,000.

The average price was $500 per acre.
