Cricket.  At the annual wind-up function for the Mokota Cricket Association Matt Carmody, C. [Cletus] Furst & Ron Dewhirst were made life members.  Phillip Byles received the Cricketer of the Year Trophy for the third year in succession.  The Mokota Premiership Shield went to South Booborowie.
