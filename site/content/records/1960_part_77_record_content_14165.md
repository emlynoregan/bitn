Twenty Henley High School boys arrived by train on Wednesday night and were billeted locally.  On Friday they visited the Burra Mines and the local swimming pool.  Sports versus Burra are reported on page 8.

In tennis Burra High 14-127 defeated Henley High 3-81.

In Cricket Henley 7 for 186 declared defeated Burra 65 & 3 for 84.
