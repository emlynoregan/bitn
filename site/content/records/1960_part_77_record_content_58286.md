Burra Burra DC, 30 August

S.J. Woollacott was thanked for his efforts to preserve Redruth Gaol.

The Premier’s Department advises a subsidy on a one for one basis to a maximum of $5,000 is available for the proposed caravan park and amenities block – to be completed by 21 December 1971 and claimed for by 28 February 1972.

Mr F. Fennell is to be allowed to rent the ambulance shed until the Council requires it.

Burra Footballers Cricket Club held its AGM and elected: Joint Patrons, L.A. Beinke & S. Rasheed; President, S. Allen; Senior Vice-President, Rex Opperman; Secretary-Treasurer, J. Aylice and Assistant Secretary, A. Day.
