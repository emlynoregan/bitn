Kevin J. Schmidt of Farrell Flat was committed for sentence at the Supreme Court, having been charged with house breaking and larceny at Burra.  He was also committed for sentence for killing a sheep with intent to steal the carcase in the World’s End area.  On this charge Victor Edward Gill and Leonard Campbell also of Farrell Flat were committed for trial.
