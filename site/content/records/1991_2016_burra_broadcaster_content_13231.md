Burra Hospital Ladies’ Auxiliary held its AGM.

President Barbara Frances said the average attendance at meetings had been 13.4 members.  Catering for the 125 Veteran & Vintage Motor Cycle Rally made a profit of $1,804, the Melbourne Cup Luncheon raised $1.542 plus $152 from the sale of articles.  A high tea to mark the end of the Burra Burra DC raised $820.  During the year the Auxiliary purchased outdoor furniture for the secure area and verandah, mesh cot sides, glasses for catering and shelving for the support care suite.  Elected were: President Barbara Frances; Vice President, Jan Tiver; Secretary, Joan Berryman; Assistant Secretary, Margaret Dunstall and Treasurer, Kaye Boulger.
