Burra High School Parents & Friends Assoc. met 16 February and welcomed new teachers and headmaster Mr R. Mueller.  The Headmaster said the Department had a lot of trouble staffing high schools and the number had increased from 30 to 49 in the past five years.  Burra was at present a Junior Teacher short.  He outlined the organisation of the school for the year.
