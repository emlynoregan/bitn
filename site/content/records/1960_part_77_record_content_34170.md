The Mt Bryan Reunion at Mt & Mrs Bill Winchester’s ‘Roraka’ on 16 April is reported and those attending are listed in ⅔ column.

The Penguin Club welcomed the State President Mrs J. Grant-Williams to their last meeting.  Over 30 people attended, including the Mayor and Mayoress.
