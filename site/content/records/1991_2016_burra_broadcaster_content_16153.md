BCS.  The school’s Boer goats escaped through the fence last week and made a meal of some fifty four-year-old trees on the property of Glenys Christopher and Bob Skinner.  Yesterday they were found to have defoliated some 200 of their trees and eaten some to ground level.  [Photo.]
