‘The Braes’ – Brian & Marlene Richard’s property was sold at Hallett Hall on Friday 3 November.

Comprising 2,724 acres or 1.102 ha, it was sold for $925,000 to a local syndicate comprising:

Len & Rose Hill

Brian & Margaret Brooks & Family

Duncan & Julie-Anne McInnis

(All owners of nearby properties.)
