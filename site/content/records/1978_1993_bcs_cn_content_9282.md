19 April 1984

‘Polyphony’ the amazing vocal group from Melbourne will appear at Burra Town Hall in a cabaret setting on Wednesday 2 May.  They feature splendid singing and are hilariously funny.

Trevor Sheean	counter-tenor

Andrew Murrell	under-the-counter tenor

Daryl Emerson	tenor testosterone

Reg Ellery	basso profuso

Geoff Baird	basso almosto profuso
