This year’s church service was held at St Mary’s, conducted by Rev. G.D. Sibly.  The Mayor and Mayoress attended, with President H. Cornelius of the RSL and 14 ex-servicemen.  The Burra Choral Society, conducted by M.D. Ebsary, led the music and the occasional sermon was by Rev. M.A. Lind of the Burra Methodist Circuit.  More than 200 people attended and supper followed in the Parish Hall.
