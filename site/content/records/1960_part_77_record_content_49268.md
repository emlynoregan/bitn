Accidents

A car driven by Gregory D. Robson of Seaton Park overturned on the corner near the Burra Golf Course on Christmas Eve.  Two juvenile occupants were treated for bruises, shock and abrasions.

A car driven by William J. Corner of Burra collided with a strainer post on the Mt Bryan Road on 1 January.  The driver suffered abrasions and concussion.
