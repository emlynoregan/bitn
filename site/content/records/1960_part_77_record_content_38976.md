Cricket.  Phillip Byles (16) has won the 1967-68 H. Black Trophy presented to the most outstanding cricketer in the Mokota Association.  It is calculated on batting, bowling and fielding votes and on the number of catches.  Phillip scored 170 points, ahead of J. Honan with 156.  He is in his second season with the Koonaburra Club and is still eligible for junior cricket, but elected to play with the senior team.  For the season the highest run scorer was Kevin Cousins with 874.  Phillip Byles captured the most wickets at 54 and Kevin Cousins had the most catches with 19.
