Pastoral Properties to the east have a great potential for fire this season.  Rainfall in 1973 has been an all time record with over 16 inches on some properties.  There is abundant grass and bush.  Rain fell on Thursday-Friday with 90 points at Princess Royal, 102 at Koomooloo and 170 at Thistlebeds.
