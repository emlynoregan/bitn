Burra Regional Art Gallery.  The annual Art of the Flower and Garden exhibition was opened on 1 October with about 70 works entered.  Awards:

Alternative	Maria Holst Salmonsen (Penwortham) for her hanging felted pod.

Traditional	Roland weight (Riverton) for an oil painting of red geraniums.

Floral Art	Joyce Lloyd

Young Artist	Harrison Hitchcock, a Year 11 student from Peterborough High School.
