Burra Burra DC has been granted $54,000 to improve streetscaping and traffic flow in Market Square under the Community Employment Scheme.  Further funds will come from the Local Roads Asset Program.

Phase 1.  Placement of electrical mains underground, beginning on 7 October 1985 from Ware St-Commercial St junction through Market Square to Peacock’s Chimney and including Mt Pleasant Road to Drew Lane.  New streetlights.

Phase 2.  Streetscape and traffic flow changes, which are due to begin in February 1986.

Plans are on view at the Council Office.
