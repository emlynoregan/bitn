Mr E.P. Cookes, manager of the Bank of Adelaide at Booborowie for the past 2½ years, has been appointed manager at Currramulka.  Mr G.F. O’Connor from Woodside will replace him at Booborowie.  Mr O’Connor was educated at Marist Brothers College and has four young sons.
