12 August 1987

Burra Burra DC.  Chairman Cr Jack McMahon advises that a surplus of $11,718 has been achieved for the year ending 30 June 1987.  The major expenditure was on roadworks of $467,541.

For the financial year 1987-88:

Minimum rates in Burra will be 	$275

Minimum rates for Mt Bryan,

Booborowie and Farrell Flat will be	$190

Minimum rural rates will be 	$120

Estimated rate income will be $622,239, which represents an 11.6% increase.

Council has taken into account inflation and a cut in Government & Local Road Grants of 18.2%.

Council is aiming for a balanced budget for this financial year.
