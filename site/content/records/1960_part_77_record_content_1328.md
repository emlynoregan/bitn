Bowls

Mid North Bowling Association Champion of Champions Tournament was held at Burra last Saturday.

The honours went to Jack Goodenough of Clare.

Clare green won the Rinks Competition.

North Eastern Association Champion of Champions Tournament was held at Spalding on Saturday.

The title went to Bill Phin of Hallett, but in the Rinks Competition the Burra team of George Terry (Skipper), K. Parker, S. Pearce and Max Jacka were victorious.
