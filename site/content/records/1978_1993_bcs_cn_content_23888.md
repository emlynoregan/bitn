Northeast Women’s Bowling Association Championships were held in Burra 16, 23 & 30 March.  Winners:

Singles	V. Lloyd of Jamestown

Fours	N. Rynne, L. Lehmann, M. Robinson & P. Crawford of Jamestown

Pairs	J. Larsson & T. Noonan of Caltowie

Triples	A. Petersen, V. Bottrall & N. Bishop of Jamestown

Colour Fours	M. White, M. Beinke, M. Affloter & C. Woodgate of Booborowie.
