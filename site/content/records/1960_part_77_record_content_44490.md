Burra won the Lightning Carnival and both the ‘A’ and ‘B’ Grade Premierships.  The active social club had raised $550.  The coach Mr Kevin Lane was congratulated on a fine job.  Financially the club was sound with more than $700 in hand.  The club will again play in the North Eastern League in 1969.
