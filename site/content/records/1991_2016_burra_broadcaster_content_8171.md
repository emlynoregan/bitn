Burra Burra DC

Council is seeking finance to seal their 2 km section of the Farrell Flat to Mintaro Road.

Kooringa CoHousing Co-op. has permission to plant trees on the road reserve adjacent to their property.

Council will contact all traders, businesses and government agencies in the area for their comments on the general concept of an industrial area.
