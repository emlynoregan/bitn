Burra Copper Festival 1980

Plans are proceeding.  A brass band has been obtained for the Sunday procession and other musical groups are being sought.  An excellent puppet show for the children has been contacted.  Burra Community Entertainment Group has plans for a production of Belle of Burra Burra for Sunday night.
