Mr & Mrs Jack Summers & Mr & Mrs Bill Byles and their families were given a farewell social at Leighton Hall on Saturday night.  The main speakers were Mr Bruce stockman, Mr John Jacka and Mrs Maurice Collins.  An inscribed electric clock was presented to each family.
