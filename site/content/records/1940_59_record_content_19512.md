Obituary.  Mrs Susan Jane Snell who died recently at Broken Hill was the eldest daughter of the late Mr & Mrs Richard Thomas.  She married the late Mr Joseph Snell whilst in Burra and the couple resided here for some time before going to Broken Hill where she lived for the last 46Â½ years.  She was a staunch member of the Lily of the Valley Tent of the Rechabite Lodge and never lost her interest in the people of Burra and their activities.  [Born 1 December 1858, Tavistock Park (Burra): died 10 November 1944, Broken Hill.  See more on the next page of this issue.]
