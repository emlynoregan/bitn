Burra Hospital has received cheques totalling $604 from the land cropped for donation.  This will attract a two for one subsidy.  Mr T.R. Rogers reaped the wheat grown on land loaned by Koonoona Proprietors.  Mr Don Stockman reaped the wheat grown on the Hanson Reserve.
