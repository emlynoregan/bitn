Pigmy Blue Tongue Lizards have attracted the interest of Australian fauna smugglers.  A trespasser was observed behaving suspiciously on Thorogood’s grassland recently and on 15 August many diggings were found on the sanctuary where a trespasser had been digging for the lizard.
