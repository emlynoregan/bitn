Sally Shepley has been nominated for the Australian Training Award and is in the running to win the National Title along with twelve other nominees.  She has completed her Certificate Two in Retail (fashion) and is on her way to completing her Certificate Three, which offers management qualifications.  Founder and designer of ‘Saltbush’ Elspeth Radford said she was keen to keep Sally involved with ‘Saltbush’.  [Photo.]
