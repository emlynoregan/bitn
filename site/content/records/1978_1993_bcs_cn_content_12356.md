Burra Guides and Brownies held their AGM 11 March.  Elected: President, Liz Jennings; Vice-President, Kay Garrard; Acting Secretary, Gilda Pearce and Treasurer, Edith Meintjes.  Mary Van Gils has agreed to become Brownie Leader following the resignation of Donna Kelly due to work commitments.  Gilda Pearce is the new Guide Leader.
