Burra Town Council, Special Meeting 22 January

Approval was given for the Small Bore Club to hold a Prize Shoot on Sunday 1 April.

Council advises the Aberdeen Tennis Club that it is in favour of giving permission to hold a tennis tournament on Sundays, but is seeking legal opinion regarding the agreement.

The Club sought a new pit toilet and children’s convenience adjacent to the ladies’ toilet – advised that this is the responsibility of the Playground Committee.

Mr W.A. Holness of Paruna was appointed Town Clerk.

A minute of appreciation was recorded for the work of the outgoing Town Clerk, Mr Fullgrabe.
