The Copper Trail.  Organisers are calling for community members to become involved in the inaugural copper trail commemoration walk from Burra to Kadina, starting on 2 May.

Burra Girl Guides helped to clean up along the Burra Creek recently and along the main road north of Peacock’s Chimney.  [Photo.]
