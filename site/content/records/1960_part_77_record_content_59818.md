Methodist Services

With the dividing of the Hallett-Terowie Circuit two churches join the Burra Circuit; Mt Bryan and Willalo.  Willalo services will be fortnightly alternating between 3.45 p.m. and 11 a.m.

Mt Bryan services will be fortnightly alternating between 11 a.m. and 2 p.m.
