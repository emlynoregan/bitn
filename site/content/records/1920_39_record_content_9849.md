Kooringa Methodist Hall was the site on Saturday evening for farewells to Mr & Mrs William Geake, Miss D. Geake, Miss E. Jenkin, Mrs E.L. Davey, Mr & Mrs H.J. Davey and daughter, Mr & Mrs William Morgan and Misses Lilly, Joyce, Stella and Norma Morgan, Mr & Mrs G. Grossman and two children and Miss Gertie Bullen, all of whom are leaving the district.  Mr & Mrs J.P.H. Biddle were also to have attended, but did not get back from Adelaide in time.  [Details occupy â…” column.]
