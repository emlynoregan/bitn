Burra’s Biggest Morning Tea.  Lorna Sutton and helpers have raised $1,524 for the Cancer Council through the morning tea held recently in the back shed, together with bingo sessions, raffles and trading tables.  Some 43 cups of tea were served on the day to boost the sum raised.
