Burra Railway Station.  Following some concerns expressed concerning the station, Goyder Council will ask the owners of the building and Transport SA to undertake:

To seal the building against intrusions by vandals, pigeons and tourists.

To clean up pigeon manure and remove dead pigeons.

To undertake eradication of termites and monitor and destroy termite nests.

To replace damaged areas of verandah pitching plate on the south side of the building and to remove or repair verandah enclosure on the western end of the building.
