Council Election

Following the resignation of Mr Ian Allen as a Councillor for Burra Town Ward, two nominations to replace him have been received:

Mr Bob Steele (a former Councillor)

Mr Jeff Hirschausen

The election will take place on 9 September at the Burra Council Office.
