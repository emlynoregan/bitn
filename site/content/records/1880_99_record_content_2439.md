Redruth Court, 15 December

J. Richards, W. Ellis, & Mrs T. Hosking were each fined 5/- for stray goats

C. Lowe 10/- for a stray horse

E. Nolan 5/- for a stray horse

W.L.H. Bruse 5/- for a stray cow

G. Ford 5/- for stray swine
