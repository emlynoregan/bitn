BCS students visited Adelaide in conjunction with their elective subject ‘Aviation in Australia’.  It included:

Attending the dedication of the new look ‘Airlines of South Australia’ by the Premier Mr Tonkin.

A visit to air traffic control.

A tour through a TAA Boeing 727.

A try out of the Link Trainer, flight simulator at the Air Training Corps Headquarters in North Adelaide.
