Burra North Red Cross held its AGM.  There is a membership of 40 and an average attendance of 17.  Nine long service medals were presented at the December meeting.  The branch has sent a donation of $350 to headquarters.  Elected: President, Mrs I. Hirschausen; Vice-President, Mrs R. Thamm; Secretary, Reta Jennison & Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.
