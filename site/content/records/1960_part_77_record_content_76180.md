The [Methodist] Ladies’ Guilds of the Middle District converged on Burra on 7 August for a rally that combined the Home and Overseas Missions.  About 200 attended at Burra Methodist Church.  Mrs E. Hore managed proceedings and Rev. G. O’Neil, Middle District Chairman, conducted morning devotions.  Speakers included Mrs Gore (President of WHMA), Rev. Dean Brookes (on Inland Mission work), Rev. Ponciale’o of Samoa and Deaconess E. Simmons on Ba Hospital in Fiji.
