Burra Field Days have created big interest.  More than 40 exhibitors will be involved in the 2011 Elders Stud Merino Field Days on-property and on-field expo.  The on-property expo will be on Monday 21 March and the Stud Merino Expo at the Burra Oval on 22 March.  14 studs are participating in the on-property expo, with some 30 exhibitors at the oval.
