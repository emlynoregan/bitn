Thefts.  There has been a series of thefts from local premises recently:

A pump was stolen from the rear of premises in Kellett St Mt Bryan. ($300)

A Honda 5.5 HP petrol motor with attached pump was stolen from a Gum Creek property. ($700)

$1,000 worth of furniture was stolen from a property at Mt Bryan East.

Tools and camping equipment worth $800 was stolen from a camper trailer at Burra Motor Inn.
