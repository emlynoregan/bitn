Robertstown Railway.  The estimated total capital costs are £86,000 with an estimated revenue of £2,100 p.a. and working expenses of £2,000 p.a.  Interest p.a. of £3,340.  The length is 12¾ miles and would be 5’3” gauge built with 60lb rail.  Estimated nett loss would be £3,344 p.a.
