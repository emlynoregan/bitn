What a perfect day we had for the Burra Show!  Gate-takings were up, there was plenty to see, and the weather, was perfect.

Denis Keller, Chairman of the Regional Council of Goyder officially opened the Show.

Annual Shows are a showcase for the district, and the Burra Show did not disappoint.  Some sections were quite outstanding this year - flowers, cookery and so on.  President Ian Slade, and Secretary Len Newcombe both reported a very successful day.

The following were the major prize winners of the various sections:

Flowers

Champion Pansy L. Lewis, Champion Rose Bud J. Slade, best exhibit in pot plants P. Venning with a magnificent Azalea, Fuss Memorial price for aggregate Peter Kazlowski.

Cookery

‘Sunshine’ pumpkin scones J. Parish, Fruit Cake Teresa Earle, Boiled Fruit Cake Pat Greet, Beer Cake D.F. Hurst, Sponges T. Earle. ‘Sunshine’ Fruit Loaf T. Earle, Genoa Cake competition D.F. Hurst, Aggregate winner T. Earle, runner up Marlene Phillips.
