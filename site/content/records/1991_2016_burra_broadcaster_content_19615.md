Break-in.  Thieves broke into the Burra Sports Complex on Sunday morning and the damage and cost of items stolen is estimated to exceed $4,000.  Russell Disher, President of the BBH Social Club writes condemning the event and declaring that the Club will fight on to increase the pleasure and enjoyment of others.  [Report plus letter.]
