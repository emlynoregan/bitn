Bowls

Burra Green 103 defeated Clare Red 81

Burra Blue 116 defeated Clare Blue 87

Auburn 91 defeated Burra Gold 85

This concludes the Pennant Bowls season.  Clare Red Team is the Premiers for 1966.

In the Burra Club Johnnie Hill is the Singles Champion.

In the Burra Associates Pennant Competition, Burra Green has taken the 1966 pennant.

Burra Small Bore Rifle Club, Tuesday over double 25 yeards, best were K. Spackman, A. Angel & J. Bowden.
