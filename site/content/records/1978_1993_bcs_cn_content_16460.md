SA Secondary Schools Sports Association Track & Field Championships

BCS girls won the B1 Grade and will thus compete in the A2 Grade next year.  The School began competing in these championships in 1980 in the E2 Grade.

The boys tried hard, but will compete in the B2 Grade next year.

Best individual performance was from Cheryl Day and fine performances were also given by Petrea Piggott, Adam Day, Tony Secomb, Kelly Gibbs, Sarah Drew and Tasma Wooton.
