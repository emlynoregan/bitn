Meals on Wheels SA held an Awardee Fellowship Afternoon on Friday to thank long serving members of the Association.  It was held in the Allan Scott Room at the Morphettville Racecourse.  About 400 attended and those from the Goyder area were: Shana Nourse, Eunice Baker, Pauline Venning, Bev Fleming, Pearl Harvey & Alison Dunling.
