Apex has elected Jeff Hirschausen as President.  In 1975 it will boost town tourism with improved signage around Burra and will help beautify the area around the new ‘Burra Billabong’.  Murray Mitchell retired as President and Phil. Ogden and Lou Georgeou are losses by transfer.
