Burra’s National Australia Bank Branch will become an agency without a manager from 19 March and will open with reduced hours:	10 to 2 Monday, Wednesday and Thursday

10 to 5 with an hour closed for lunch on Friday

Rebecca Dollman and Robyn Baker will continue to work in Burra, while service centre manager Scott Draper will be redeployed as a relieving staff member around SA.
