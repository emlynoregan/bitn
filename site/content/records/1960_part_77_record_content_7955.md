Mr & Mrs Walter Smith’s family had a reunion at their Glenunga home on 3 September.  The family was formerly of Burra.  Visitors included:

Mr & Mrs Gordon Smith (Riverton) and daughters Dawn & Kay (Mrs Lambert of Auburn).

Mr & Mrs Dick Crang & sons Bill & Richard (recently relocated from Alice Springs to Adelaide).

Mrs Muriel Humphris & children John & Ann, from Arthur River WA.  (Husband Zac Humphris was unable to accompany them.)

Mr & Mrs Ron Smith (from Hamilton Victoria) and daughters Pam & Sue.
