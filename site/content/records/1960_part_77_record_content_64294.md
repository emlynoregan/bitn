Obituary.  Lawrence Ross who spent his childhood at Emu Downs, but most of his working years at Myponga died 2 December after a short illness.  He is survived by a wife and two daughters in the Robertstown area.

[Victor Lawrence Ross born 5 February 1910 Eudunda: died 2 December 1972 McLaren Vale, residence Myponga.]
