Salvation Army Harvest Festival was held last weekend.  Captain H. Raven attended and led Sunday’s meetings.  On Monday the Citadel was crowded for a program given by the local corps.  Mr Pederick was Chairman and Captain Cox led the opening prayer.  [Details in ⅔ column.]
