Burra Rifle Club competed at the Country Teams Meeting at the Dean Rifle Range at Port Adelaide.  This year they were placed 17th.

Burra Small Bore Rifle Club.  The best on handicap last Tuesday were B. Aworth, J. Brown and R. Francis.
