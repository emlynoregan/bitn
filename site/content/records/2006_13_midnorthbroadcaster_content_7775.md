Bicycle SA Outback Odyssey drew 150 members to the Burra Oval last week in the annual bike ride to the town of Blinman.  The three stage journey runs from Adelaide to Burra, Burra to Melrose and then Melrose to Blinman.  Active Catering of Spalding catered for the group while they were in Burra.
