Goyder Council Rates have increased substantially this year.  The overall increase is 3.2%, but town ratepayers will suffer more, with increases ranging from 12.64% in Hampden to 19.67% for Mt Bryan.

Burra rates rise by 15.01%, with rates now 0.4999 in the dollar.

Rural rate changes varied from a 0.16% fall in Hallett Ward to a 4.16% decrease in the Burra Rural Ward.
