St Peter’s Anglican Cathedral Choirboys will arrive by train on Friday with choirmaster Mr David Swale and Cathedral Precentor Rev. D.K. Rowney.  Mr Swale will play the organ at St Mary’s on Sunday at the 10.30 am sung Eucharist and the choirboys will lead the singing.  Rev. Rowney will be the preacher.  The boys are in Burra for their annual camp as part of their summer break while the Quire St Nicholas takes their place at the Cathedral.
