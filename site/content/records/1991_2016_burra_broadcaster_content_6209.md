Burra’s ‘Little Athletes’ did well in the Mid North Games held recently in Burra:

Tina Williams set three new records.

Boh Wall won gold, silver and bronze medals in the U13 Boys.

Wesley Thomson won a gold, two silver and two bronze.

Ryan Kerrigan scored four silver and a bronze in the U14.

Nicholas Taylor scored two bronze medals.

Danika Frahn got a silver in the U10 high jump.
