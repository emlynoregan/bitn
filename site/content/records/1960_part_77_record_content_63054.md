Staff.  Matron Fyfe and the Secretary-Manager Mr L. Packer have continued their excellent service.  Matron has been ably assisted by Sisters Byles, Ward, Wright, Brown, McDonald, Moller, Lucas & Carmody during the year and by Sisters Parker and Broad on staff at present as well as a very willing bans of nurses.  Sister Good has ably filled the role of Tutor Sister.  The domestic staff and handy-man have carried out their duties diligently.
