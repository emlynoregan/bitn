Advt.  Dalgety & Co. Ltd have instructions from the Administrators of the Estate of the late G.B. Morgan to sell land of 96 acres 21 perches with two houses.

Land in the Hundred of Kooringa; closed road of 3 roods 23 perches and part Section 1 of 6 acres 19 perches and allotment M of 4 acres 19 perches.

2a.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa; part Block 3 of Section 1 & other land containing 43 acres.

2b.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa; part Block 2 of Section 1 & other land containing 21 acres and 1 rood.

3a.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa being allotment 202 of 3 roods 14 perches; allotment 203 of 1 acre 1 rood 27 perches; allotment 205 of 3 roods 18 perches; part allotment 204 of 3 roods 16 perches.

3b.	Land in the Township of Burra of a closed road of 2 roods 32 perches.

4.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa being a portion of Section 1 of 15 acres 3 roods 32 perches.

5.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa being allotments 4 & 48 laid out as Millerton.

6.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa in the Township of Burra being a portion of Section 104 of 20 perches.

7.	Land in Hundred of Kooringa being apportion of allotment 125 of Part Section 1 with a cottage thereon.
