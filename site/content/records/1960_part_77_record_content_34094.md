Tourism.  Mr E.T. Baulderstone outlined Burra’s tourism potential and its tourism facilities at a special meeting of the Riverton District Chamber of Commerce in the RSL Rooms Riverton on 20 April.  Representatives discussed the facilities available along the newly proclaimed National Highway 32 from Tarlee through Riverton, Saddleworth and Burra to Hallett.  An action committee was formed and a committee of two representatives from each district was formed to promote facilities for tourists along Highway 32 from Adelaide to Broken Hill.  The next meeting will be in Burra 25 May.  Burra’s representatives will be Mr E.T. Baulderstone and Rex Hurley.
