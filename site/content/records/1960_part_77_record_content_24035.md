Football

The Mid North Association held its AGM at Saddleworth Institute 12 March and elected: President, L. Twartz (Saddleworth), Vice-President, S. Noske (Robertstown) and Patron, W.E. Blatchford.  A.E. Bannear of Saddleworth will be approached to take on the position of Secretary, for which no nominations were received.

Auburn, Riverton, Robertstown, Aberdeen and United will field A1, B & Colts teams.

Mintaro, Manoora, Sevenhill, Blyth and Farrell Flat will field A2 teams.

Watervale was granted 12 months recess.
