Liberal & Country League.  The first meeting of the newly formed Women’s Branch was held on 5 August.  Mrs I.J. Warnes presided.  She outlined its objects and said it needed someone as President with more ability and time than she had to give.  Elected were President, Mrs I.J. Warnes; Hon. Secretary, Miss Helen Wilkinson; Minute Secretary, Miss Gladys Kellock.  There was then an address by the Hon. A.P. Blesing.  [The whole report exceeds 2½ columns.]
