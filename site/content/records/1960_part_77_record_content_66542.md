Tennis.  A meeting last Wednesday decided to combine the Burra and Burra North [Aberdeen & Kooringa] Tennis Clubs.  Elected to the new Burra Tennis Club were:

Patron	R. Reed

President	J. Secomb

Vice-President	Don Lloyd

Junior Vice-President	R. Nicholls

Secretary	D. Calder

Assistant Secretary	Margaret Lloyd

Treasurer	R. Buckton

Assistant Treasurer	Miss C. Nicholls
