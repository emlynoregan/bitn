Comalco presented a trade night in Burra on 20 May to introduce the new low cost aluminium roofing and cladding sheet, Double Rib.  It is proving popular for pig pens and poultry sheds as it reflects heat and makes buildings up to 15° cooler in the summer heat.  It requires no painting.
