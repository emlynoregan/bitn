Local MP Ivan Venning presented two grant cheques in Burra on 23 November.  $250 went to Burra Day Care.  Marlene Wooton, co-ordinator said it would be expended in music therapy.  $250 also went to Barkers of Baldina Homes where Chairman Jock Duncan said it would go to building shelves in a communal storage shed.
