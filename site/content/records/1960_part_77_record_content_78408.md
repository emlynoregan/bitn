St Mary’s.  Last Wednesday evening the Bishop of Willochra Rt Rev. Bruce Rosier commissioned Rev. Barry May as Rector of the Burra Parish.  The procession was announced by a fanfare composed and played by organist Denis Nettleton.  The parish comprises St Mary’s Burra, St Edmund’s Booborowie, St Andrew’s Mt Bryan and St Catherine’s Hallett.  After the service the parishioners and visitors gathered for supper in the parish hall.  Fr Kevin Matthews and Rev. Alan Beames were welcomed to the occasion.  Mr Stanley Hawker welcomed the May family as Senior Trustee.
