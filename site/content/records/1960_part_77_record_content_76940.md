Ian Auhl’s Burra & District – A Pictorial Memoir is reviewed as a must buy for local residents or anyone with even a drop of Burra blood in their veins.  It comprises 200 pages and 320 photographs.  There are three sections in the foreword: The Burra Mine, The Burra Record and The Survival of a Mining Town, and then photographs under a series of headings covering the life of the town and district.  Dame Nancy Buttfield will launch the book on Sunday 9 November at 2 p.m.
