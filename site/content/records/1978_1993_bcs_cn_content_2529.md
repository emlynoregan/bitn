The Burra Guide Movement, due to popular demand have decided to expand their activities and have formed a Ranger Guide Unit.  A Ranger Guide is aged 14-18 and can be interested in a wide range of things including all facets of outdoor life, helping others, community affairs, furthering their education and becoming responsible citizens.  Activities are combined with other Church and Youth groups.  Expenses are nominal and no uniform is required.
