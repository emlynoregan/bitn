Regional Council of Goyder.  Allocation of Burra Ward Funds.

Burra Country Music Association $2,500 towards the 2015 festival.

Burra Bowling Club $6,750 for a fridge and ice-maker.

BCMC $3,500 to develop a Community nursery for the Burra Revegetation Group.

The Lewis Board $1,500 for trees.

BCMC $2,000 to upgrade the sound system at Burra Town Hall.

Burra District Open Gardens Group $2,000 towards their community event.

Friends of the Burra Railway Station $1,600 for a retaining wall.

Burra History Group $2,000 for heritage Plaques.

BRTBA $2,000 for Burra Fun Run.

BRTBA $2000 for 25 large pots and plants for the main street.
