The Miner’s Couch opened for business in Commercial Street on 16 February.  About 150 people attended.  Dick Cuttle, a former teacher at BCS, performed the official opening.  Proprietors are Ross and Sandy Sims, who come from Keith where Dick has been Principal for some years.  The Miner’s couch adds to the number of antique shops in the town.
