Badminton Carnival

The Final Match saw Burra No. 2 6-168 defeat Orroroo No.1 6-150.

Teams in the carnival came from Orroroo, Peterborough and Burra.

In the regular game Burra North Gold 12-204 defeated Redruth 0-90.
