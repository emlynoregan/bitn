CFS members were presented with awards at a ceremony in Burra.

Eight members [sic] of the district received 35 year long-service awards: Bill Pens, Kevin Cousins, John Burge, Ian Denton, Bob Gebhardt, Jim Quinn and Bob Simpson.  [This is 7.]

Life membership went to Chris Reed, David Thamm and Andy Thomas.  They were also recipients of 30 year service certificates, which also went to Leon Neale and Trevor Willis.
