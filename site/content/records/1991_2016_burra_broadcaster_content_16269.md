16269→Goyder Council has voted to support a twelve month trial allowing A-double vehicles or road trains access through Ludgvan Street to link the Barrier Highway with the Burra-Morgan Road – there will be a speed limit of 40 k.p.h. in the 60 k.p.h. zone for such vehicles and discouragement of the use of exhaust brakes.
