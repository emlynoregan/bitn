Mid Northern High School Sports were held at the Burra Race Course on Thursday 10 May.  Head Prefects read the names of old scholars who had made the supreme sacrifice in wars.  Mr H.J.B. Jennison as Chairman of the Burra High School Council opened the sports and Inspector A.O. McPherson presented the shields.  The Parents & Friends Association fed the multitude of over 300 adults and 700 students.  Shield results:

In the Open Contest 	In the Handicap Contest

Balaklava	124	197

Burra	115	255

Clare	141	141

Kapunda	73	259

Riverton	130	219
