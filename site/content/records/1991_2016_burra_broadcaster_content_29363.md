Maureen & Barry Wright have just undertaken the 7th edition of their book River Murray Charts.  This week 2.5 tonnes of paper on six pallets plus another pallet for the cover arrived at Burra Community Print for the project.  Printing will start shortly on this revised edition of charts of the river from Yarrawonga to Renmark plus 60 km up the Darling.
