About 30 visitors from Minda Home came to Burra for the weekend.  They spent the night at the school gymnasium.  Apex hosted the Saturday entertainment with a tour of historic sites and a light evening meal.  Sunday saw a challenge football match, Minda v. the local service clubs.  The Minda boys were all football lovers and saw this as the culmination of a great weekend.
