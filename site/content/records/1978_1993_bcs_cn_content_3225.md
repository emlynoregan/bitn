Burra Burra DC

The Highways Department will reseal the Barrier Highway from south of the Hospital to St Just Street.  Last Friday the Minister of Transport Mr Ian Wilson, his secretary and Mr Graham Gunn had coffee with Cr Jennison and Mr Don Roberts to discuss problems with the Koonoona roads and the high priority to be given to the sealing of the Burra-Morgan-Spalding Roads.
