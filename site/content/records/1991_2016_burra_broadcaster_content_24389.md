Peter Hooper, a former student at BDS & Clare HS has graduated as a Bachelor of Agricultural Science and now works with PIRSA at Keith as a crop consultant.  He is the son of Wes & Barb Hooper.

Sun Race 21.C.  This 2,300 km solar car and electric vehicle race from Adelaide to Sydney will pass through Burra on Sunday 11 February.  The race is in its 5th year.

Burra Community Development Committee and MNRDB have sponsored an E-Commerce Workshop in Burra for local business operators at the Burra Golf Course 20 February.
