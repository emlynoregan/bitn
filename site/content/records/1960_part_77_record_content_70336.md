Burra Kindergarten held its AGM on 4 March.  In the year an extension verandah was built and new blinds were installed.  $510-83 was raised.  Elected: President, Mrs David Jones; Vice-President, Mrs Gerald Wood; Secretary,  Mrs Trevor Simpson; Assistant Secretary, Mrs John Secomb; Treasurer, Mrs Kies van der Does and Patroness, Mrs Edith Carpenter.
