Mid North Field & Game Association reports a shoot held on Sunday in which the most successful shooters were P. Shannon and M. Mickel.  The next shoot will be 12 January 1975.  The Committee thanks the 15 people who helped when the shooting grounds were burned: this will enable shooting to continue through summer.
