The Kooringa Methodist Church held its Anniversary Services last Sunday.  The day was somewhat marred by intervals of heavy rain.  The minister in charge Rev. H.K. Bartlett preached three services.  On Monday an Indoor Bowls evening was held in the Sunday School, before which Rev. Bartlett read his report on the year and in the absence through indisposition of the Secretary-Treasurer of the Trust Mr Edgar Pearce, also presented the financial statement.
