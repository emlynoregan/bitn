Burra Businessmen are opposed to the new parking restrictions in Market Square. And have sent letters to the Council.  Council is prepared to meet a delegation.  Concerns have been expressed by Messrs R.A. Gill, R.A. Opperman, L. & T.K. Richardson, C & J. Pearce, M.G. Stockman and Burra Serv-Wel Store.
