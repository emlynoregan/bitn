Weather.  Rain today in Burra has produced 128 points so far.  Thunderstorms yesterday delivered good falls to parts of the district: Booborowie 114 points, Baldina 170, Old Koomooloo 74 and World’s End 162.

Mt Bryan reports 220 points from midnight to 9.45 a.m. today with Dust Hole Creek carrying fairly heavy flood waters.
