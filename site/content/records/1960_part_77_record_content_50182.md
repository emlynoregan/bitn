Burra Pre-School held its AGM at the Kindergarten on 2 March.  Only 17 members attended and elected: President, Mrs D. Cockington; Vice-President, Mrs M. Tiver; Secretary, Mrs R. Pritchard; Assistant Secretary, Mrs J. Clark and Treasurer, Mrs Kempster.  The main objective this year is to clear the $210 debt on the building.
