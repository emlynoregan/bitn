The Copper Trek was declared a great success when the walkers reached Kadina last Saturday to be greeted by the town crier.  Robyn Page, Chairman of the J150 Committee thanks a list of those who facilitated this event.

Geology Weekend, 20-21 May is another J150 event.  It is organised by the Geological Society of Australia and the Field Geology Club.

Saturday evening 8-10 p.m. there will be a public meeting on local mineral deposits and mining history in which Dr Wolfgang Preiss will speak on ‘The Geology & Mineral Deposits of the Burra Region’ and Greg Drew will talk on the ‘History of Mining in Burra’.
