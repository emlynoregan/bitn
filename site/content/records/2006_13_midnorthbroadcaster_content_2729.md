Goyder Council thanked retiring Councillors Stan Simper, Tim Stockman, Paul Schiller, Peter Niemz, Jim Reese, Colleen Woodgate and Laurie Sullivan.

Jim Reese has given over 30 years of service to local government, beginning with the Eudunda Council in 1976 as the youngest Councillor â€“ he retires as the oldest.

Laurie Sullivan began with the Hallett DC in 1983 and ended as Mayor of the Regional Council of Goyder.

Paul Schiller became a Councillor in 1984 and was Chairman of the Eudunda DC for six years before the 1997 amalgamation.

Colleen Woodgate has served on the Burra Burra DC and the Regional Council of Goyder for over 19 years.

Peter Niemz was elected to the Robertstown DC in 1982 and served until amalgamation in 1997.  He returned to the serve on the Goyder Council in 2004.

Stan Simper served on the Eudunda DC from 1993 to the amalgamation in 1997 and was elected to Goyder Council in 2000 for two further terms.

Tim Stockman has served on the Regional Council of Goyder for the last three years.
