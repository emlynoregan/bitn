Weather.  The last week has seen wind, rain, hail and snow.  Wednesday brought light rain of 20-30 points.

Thursday saw morning rain before a cold change.  At about 4 p.m. just south of Burra a strip of country had a very heavy fall of hail.  Hallett had a fairly heavy snowfall at about 7.30 p.m. and at Burra snow fell accompanied by rain.
