Burra Burra DC

Douglas Alexander will continue as Heritage Advisor.

The Education Department will subdivide the house from the Mt Bryan Primary School.

Woods & Bagot have been appointed consultants to undertake a Burra creek Conception Plan for future development.

Council will seek a grant of $35,000 from the Department of Planning and Environment for a project recommended in the Burra Creek Concept Plan.

B.C. Tonkin & Associates will assess the Common Effluent Drainage Scheme and recommend maintenance procedures and suitability for extensions to the scheme.

Burra Swimming Pool will operate this year until a new pool is built.

The flagpole outside the Burra Hotel will be relocated to the median strip between the rotunda and the War Memorial.

The doors to O’Connor’s Shed [Drew & Crewe’s Bulk Store] are to be repaired.

BCS.  The program for the R-7 Sports Day is printed.

Railways.  On Thursday morning police were alerted to a railway carriage that had had its brake released and was rolling unattended from Mt Bryan towards Burra.  A quick thinking person got onto the carriage and applied the brakes.  The carriage was brought to a halt near Collinsville.
