Burra Burra DC, 10 April

The Apex Club is to be asked what future is intended for the lawn tennis courts after they have been restored and who is to be responsible for their maintenance costs.

A.L. Clode & Son’s offer to buy a road at Burra North was accepted subject to road closing procedures.
