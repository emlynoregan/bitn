Burra Swimming Pool.  The season was opened last Saturday.  About 30 keen swimmers braved conditions a little colder than desired.  Norm Lihou has the filtration system working well.  Mr Stan Kellaway is taking care of the vacuuming of the pool.  Other tasks concerning the pool and canteen have been rostered.
