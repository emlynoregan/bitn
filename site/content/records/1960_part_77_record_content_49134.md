Obituary.  Henry Edgar Atkins aged 85 died at Keith and District hospital recently.  He left Hanson in 1947 where he and his wife had lived for over thirty years and he went to take up land at ‘Mount Charles’.  He later sold this and took up ‘Almond Vale’ at Keith, where he lived until retiring two and a half years ago.  He had been in ill health for twelve months.  He leaves a widow, two sons and two daughters: Edgar (Ashton), George (Maitland), Edith Mrs I. O’Brien (Keith) and Christine Mrs K. Burgess (Tintinara).  There are 12 grandchildren and 1 great-grandchild.

[Born 23 May 1885 Blyth: died 2 November 1969 Keith.]
