YMCA War Depot & Lady Galway Club House.

The visit announced last issue is here reported at length, running to almost 1 column.  There is also ½ column on the concert in the evening, including a brief history of the Lady Galway Club House by Mr G. Bull.  It is expected the total returns for the visit will be c. £145.
