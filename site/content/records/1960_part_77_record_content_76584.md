Football.  Mid North Football League Awards

1975 Mail Medal to Dean Willmott (Mintaro-Manoora)

Sec. Trophy for Goal Kicking to Dean Willmott (Mintaro-Manoora)

A Grade Runner-up Trophy to Lyall Ackland

A.W. Vater Trophy for B Grade Best & Fairest to Barry Dixon

Senior Colts L.A. Twartz Trophy for Best & Fairest to Peter Jonas (United)

Junior Colts, the President’s Trophy for Best & Fairest to Konrad Bettison (United)

Albert Bannear was given Life Membership of the League and also received a Merit Award from SANFL for 40 years’ Service to Country Football.
