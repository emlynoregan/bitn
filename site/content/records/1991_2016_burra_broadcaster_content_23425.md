Rachel Fry a student at Burra Community School has won a regional prize in the ABC Heywire Competition.  The competition required young people 16-22 to make a three-minute non-fiction radio story.  Rachel will now go to Canberra with 38 other regional winners to the Heywire Youth Issues Forum in February 2001.  [Photo]
