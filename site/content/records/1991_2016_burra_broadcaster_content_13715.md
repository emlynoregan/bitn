Winter Sleepout for the Homeless by a group of local youth outside the Uniting Church Hall on Saturday last raised awareness of the plight of the homeless and c. $200 for SA Mission.

Dying with Dignity.  Two representatives from Mary Potter Hospice will give a public address on palliative care for the dying in St Mary’s Hall on 26 August at 11.15 a.m.

Burra Picnic Race Club intends to improve toilets, yards and stalls before their next meeting.  At the annual committee dinner Ivan Hirschausen was given Life Membership, joining 1996 recipient John Maidment.
