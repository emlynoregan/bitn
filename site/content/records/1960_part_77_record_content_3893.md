Advt.  Elder, Smith & Co. Ltd have instructions from L.H. Thomas & Co. to sell ‘Springbank’ comprising 708 acres freehold located 3½ miles southeast of Burra at  Burra RSL Hall on 14 October.

The property comprises Sections 172, 173, 174 & 184 in the Hundred of Kooringa, with a solid stone house of four rooms and enclosed verandah with 32v lighting plant, telephone etc.
