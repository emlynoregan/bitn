Easter Weekend in Burra was busy.  2,100 visitors came through the door of the Visitor Centre and 127 passports were sold, equating to c. 900 people doing the Heritage Trail.  The caravan park was busy with up to 100 people per night and Paxton Square had three nights at capacity.  The Motor Inn was also full on three nights and three-quarters full on Monday night.  The Radford horse and wagon was active showing people around the town.
