Accidents

On the Thursday before Easter a car rolled over in Murkaby Lane about 30 miles east of Burra.  The driver was unhurt.

On Good Friday at 2.30 a.m. a car rolled over 3 miles north of Mt Bryan.  No one was hurt, but the car was a write off.

On Easter Monday a car overturned on the Burra-Booborowie Road.  Again the car was written off, but the driver was uninjured.

At 4.30 a.m. on Tuesday a car overturned on the Burra-Mt Bryan Road near the racecourse.  The driver escaped without a scratch, but the car was extensively damaged.

On Thursday morning a collision occurred between a utility and a truck at Cobb & Co.’s Corner.  No one was hurt, but the utility was badly damaged.
