The Bank of Australasia merged with the Union Bank in 1951 to form the Australia & New & New Zealand Bank Ltd.  The Bank of Australasia opened in Burra on 14 February 1860.  To mark the centenary 25 guests were entertained at the Burra Hotel on Friday 12 February.  The State Manager and his wife, Mr & Mrs E.K. Spence, attended.
