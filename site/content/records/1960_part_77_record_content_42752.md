Advt.  Southern Farmers.  Land for sale by tender.

Lot 1.	On account Mrs E.W. Woollacott & Miss M.S. Woollacott.  Freehold of 145 acres 2 roods Pt Section 1 Hd Kooringa with old stone house and sheds.  House presently unoccupied, but usable.

Lot 2.	Account of Miss R.J. Woollacott.  ‘Nankivells’ 124 acres 3 roods 20 perches Pt Section 143 Hd Kooringa.  Grazing land with dam, bore, troughs, etc.

Lot 3.	Account of Mrs E.W. Woollacott.  ‘Villis’s’.  678 acres Section 102 & town allotments in the Township of Douglas Hd of Baldina.  108 acres 37 perches freehold and 565 acres 1 rood leasehold.
