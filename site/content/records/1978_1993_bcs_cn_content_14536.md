Burra Venturers.  Burra’s newest Queen’s Scout (the 7th), Tim Letcher, attended the Annual Queen’s Scout & Queen’s Guide Dinner at Morphettville Function Centre on Friday 1 May.  Tim received his royal Certificate from the Governor, Sir Donald Dunstan at Government House on Sunday.

Alan Jacka, Ben Letcher, Paul Williams and Digby Taylor are stepping up from Scouts to the Venturer Unit on Friday 8 May.
