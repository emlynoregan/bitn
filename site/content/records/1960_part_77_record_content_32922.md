Theft.  More of the goods stolen from Jennison’s Tyre Service have been found and handed to Burra Police.  On Thursday last several radios, an ice box and three new tyres were found between St Mary’s Hall and the home of Mr & Mrs Jim Kelly.  A tape recorder and a new car radio are still missing.
