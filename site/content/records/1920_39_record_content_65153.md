[Mrs W. Geake died 22 December 1936.  See 58, 1, 5 Jan. 1937, page 2 and a more extended obituary 58, 2, 12 Jan. 1937, page 1.  She was born Elizabeth Vivian Jenkin 26 May 1858 at Kooringa.]
