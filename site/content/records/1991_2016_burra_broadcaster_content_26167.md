Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club has spent months preparing its new ground in readiness for its Open Day on 23 September.  Club President Merilyn Baulderstone welcomed invited guests, members and visitors.  Head Instructor Adrian Green spoke on the development of the site and Cr Colleen Woodgate cut the ribbon to open the new facility.  Members put on a display of dog obedience and agility, ending with a canine fashion parade.  [3 photos.]
