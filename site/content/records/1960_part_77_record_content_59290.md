A Blessing of the Fleece service was held at Teetulpa Station on 14 November.  It was conducted by Peterborough Ministers Fraternal aided by Salvation Army Bandsmen, some from Adelaide.  Former Burra man Harold Kakoschke did the actual shearing.  Over 80 people attended.
