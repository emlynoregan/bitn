Burra National Trust has received a bequest from the estate of the late Mrs Gwen Tennant of ‘Princess Royal’.  Mrs Tennant has given $5,000 to the Trust.  The late Mr & Mrs Andrew Tennant were great supporters of the National Trust and donated the building to the Trust which is now the Market Square Museum.
