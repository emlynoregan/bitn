Burra & District Swimming Pool Management Committee met on 24 July for the first time.  Chairman is I. Allen, Secretary G. Baulderstone and Treasurer Mr A.D. Bernhardt.  Mr E.T. Baulderstone thanked all participants before handing over to the new Chairman.  A constitution is being drafted.  A representative of Berger Paints will be consulted about the best preparation for the new season to make the surface easier to care for.
