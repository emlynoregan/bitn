Weather.  There were heavy falls of rain through the week and some minor flooding of houses and shops in Burra.  Some of this was due to overflowing tanks.  Thunder rumbled for more than 24 hours.  Some gaugings:

Woolgangi 210 points	Koomooloo 218 points	Quondong 184 points

The Gums 190 points	Kia-Ora 190 points	Pine Valley 160 points

Balah 190 points	Braemar 164 points	Hallett 185 points or 47 mm

Burra 263 points or 67 mm	Farrell Flat 161 points or 41 mm	Clare 188 points or 48 mm
