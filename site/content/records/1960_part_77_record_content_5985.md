Burra Burra Hospital Inc. decided at its August AGM that anyone wishing to become a life member could do so by donating £10.  The Board has now decided to use the donations so received to go towards the installation of an air conditioning unit in the nursery of the Midwifery Wing of the hospital, which will cost c. £250.
