Bishop Bruce Rosier will attend the Conference of the Rural Deanery of Clare to be held at St Mary’s on Sunday.  The Deanery comprises the parishes of Clare, Auburn-Riverton, Crystal Brook-Broughton Valley, Jamestown, Peterborough and Burra.
