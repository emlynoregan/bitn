Australia Day was celebrated in Burra at Thomas Pickett Reserve as usual.  Mt Bryan resident John Quinn received his award as Burra Citizen of the Year.  Burra Swimming Club cooked 300 breakfasts.  Mayor Peter Mattey presented the award to John Quinn who then cut the Australia Day cake, beautifully decorated by Colleen Spackman.
