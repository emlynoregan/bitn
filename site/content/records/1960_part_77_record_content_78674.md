Burra Easter Bowls Tournament

Men’s Pairs was won by B. Riggs & J. Topsfield of Burra

In Men’s Rinks on Saturday Morgan defeated Burra in the top section and Hallett won the bottom section.

On Monday two Hallett teams competed in the final of the Mixed Fours.
