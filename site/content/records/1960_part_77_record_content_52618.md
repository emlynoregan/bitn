Obituary.  Mr George McLean of Gawler died recently.  He was a strong supporter and exhibitor at Burra Shows in the poultry and pigeon sections and a judge in these sections at many shows including Burra.

[George Albert William McLean born 5 August 1896 Gawler: died 24 July 1970 Gawler.]
