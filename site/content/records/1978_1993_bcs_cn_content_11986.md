BCS Staff Changes

Gary Williams goes to Aberfoyle Park High School

Carolyn Bartlett goes to Pilgrim School (One of the four Hub Schools)

Barb Caudle goes to Cummins

Booborowie and Hallett Schools will get new principals.

Tony Colebatch will be Principal of Hindmarsh Primary.

Leo Vasilunas takes an advisory position in the area and will still live in the Booborowie area.
