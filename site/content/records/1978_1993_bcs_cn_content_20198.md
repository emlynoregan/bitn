28 March 1990

The Wool & Sheep Show Spectacular at Burra Oval on Wednesday evening attracted about 1,500 people.  W.B. Ashby of ‘Ashrose’ Hallett was awarded the Collins Perpetual Champion Ram Trophy.

The Wool Fashion Spectacular presented by the Australian Wool Corporation involved ten professional models.

Neil Andrew, MP for Wakefield, officially opened the evening’s entertainment.
