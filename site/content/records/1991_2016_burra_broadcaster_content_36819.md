‘Mackerode’ has been sold.  The new owners are Bob & Simon Rowe.  It has been owned by the Gebhardt Family from the 1860s.  It has been meticulously maintained along with its Heritage Listed Homestead of c. 1871.  In recent years it has become known for its carob plantation.  Simon Rowe said the property would be run as in the past, including the carob plantation.
