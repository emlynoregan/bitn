Christmas Shopping

Burra Traders wish to advise that Christmas late night shopping will be on Friday 23 December to 9 p.m.

A free film; The Cat From Outer Space will be screened at Burra Town Hall 5.15 p.m. to 7 p.m.

The hurdy-gurdy will operate 5.30 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.

Father Christmas will be at the Rotunda from 7.30 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.

Saturday 24 December will have normal shopping hours of 9.00 a.m. to 11.30 a.m.

The hurdy-gurdy will operate 9.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m.
