Victor Dodman & wife Gill are new arrivals in Burra from Queensland.  Victor has a connection with the town, having won the Burra-Adelaide Cycling Classic in 1968, when aged 58.  He has won titles and championships in every state of Australia.  He is now 82.  [Photo.]
