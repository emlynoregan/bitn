Football.  Mid North Association

B. Kluska, Captain-Coach of Auburn, was presented with the Mail Medal on Saturday.

The Jenner Trophy for best and fairest in ‘B’ Grade was presented to Barry Dixon, Captain-Coach of Manoora.

The Twartz Trophy for best and fairest in the Colts was presented to Bernie Schroeder of Riverton, who also won the SANFU Colts Medal.
