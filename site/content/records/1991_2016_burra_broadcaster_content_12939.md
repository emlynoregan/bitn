James Quinn

Am a current Councillor

A lifetime resident of Mt Bryan and married to Rosemary, with 8 children all gainfully employed or studying

Worked zealously for the Burra Burra DC 1983-87 and 1989-97

Offer a wide range of experience with Council procedure, policies, roles and responsibilities, at local state and national levels, having represented Burra at the National Local Government Assembly in Canberra in 1995.

Currently an executive member of Mid North Region of Local Government (since 1994)

Was Deputy Chairman of Burra Burra DC and Chairman of the Finance Committee and a member of the Finance Committee in the Regional Council of Goyder and a member of a number of other committees
