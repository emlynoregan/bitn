Burra Station has been closed since the late 1980s, but is now open as Delia Kraft’s ‘Burra Station Gallery’. 

On the walls are recorded a number of stories:

‘Noel Miller, Bill Kotz & Tom Prance have worked here.’

‘J.D. Jones, the last resident on at the Burra commenced at Burra 9th May 1981.  Completed last shift at Burra 5th August 1988, on VRIS…Amen.’

‘L.J. Robinson, start 28th April 1969.’

G. Hirschausen started April 29th 1968.’  Gary was a porter.

Others who worked there were Dave Robinson, Morris & Wayne Broad were porters.  Mr Green and Cynthia Burnette’s father were stationmasters.
