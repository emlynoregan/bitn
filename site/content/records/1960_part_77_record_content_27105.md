Burra High School.  A group of 44 First & Second Year students had a recreation weekend camp at Parnanga National Fitness Council Camp at O’Sullivan’s Beach near Adelaide.  The group with the Headmaster, Miss D. Jones and Mr K. Schwartz travelled by train to Adelaide and then by hired coach.  The weekend was a tremendous success.
