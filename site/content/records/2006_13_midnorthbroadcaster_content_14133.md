Clean Up Australia Day will be held on 4 March.  The CMC is supporting those who are prepared to clean up a section of the town.  They will provide bags and safety gear at Thomas Pickett Reserve.  Burra Waste Transfer Station will be open to bona fide residents of Goyder free of charge from10 a.m. to 3 p.m.  Similar arrangements have been made at Eudunda and Hallett.  Large waste bins will be provided at Booborowie, Mt Bryan, Robertstown and Terowie.
