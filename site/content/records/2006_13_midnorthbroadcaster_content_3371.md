Maureen & Barry Wright travelled 1,873 km along the Murray in 1973 and produced as a result River Murray Charts from Renmark to Yarrawonga.  After 30 years they are still highly regarded and are sold in over 150 outlets.  It won awards in tourism in 1992 & 1993.  Revisions have been made for new printings and from a print run of about 2,000 in 1975 numbers have increased to about 8,000 for each revision.  Maureen says around 60,000 have been produced over the years.  The newest edition will be Barry & Maureenâ€™s 8th.
