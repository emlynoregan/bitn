Burra’s Heidi Thompson has been asked to participate in the 2007 Voices Program, which coincides with APEC 2007.  There are only two SA representatives.  The program provides an opportunity for young people from APEC economies to learn about APEC and the challenges being faced in working towards a sustainable future for people in the Asia Pacific Region.
