Rob. George, a teacher at Burra High School, has written a one-act play The Prompt and with Maureen Sherlock and Coralie Plush, assisted by several other teachers, presented it at the local Apex Club.  It won first prize in the recent Clare Drama Festival and fourth prize in the Adelaide Advertiser Festival, against stiff opposition.
