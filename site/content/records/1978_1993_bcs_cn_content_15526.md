St John’s Lutheran congregation celebrated the 20th anniversary of the church with a service and luncheon on 1 November.  A large group attended.  The preacher was Pastor A.H. Schmidt, who was pastor of the congregation at the time of the dedication on 5 November 1967.  The Liturgist was Pastor M. Freund, the present minister, and the three readers were all former pastors: S. Kuchel, W. Borgas & P. Scherer.  Mrs Cynthia White was the organist.
