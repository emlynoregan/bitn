Accident.  A car driven by Ian Moore (19) of Gladstone overturned about four miles east of Booborowie on Sunday.  The driver sustained serious head injuries and was transferred to the RAH.  Two passengers Miss J. Moore of Ceduna and Miss C. Brown of Gladstone escaped serious injury.
