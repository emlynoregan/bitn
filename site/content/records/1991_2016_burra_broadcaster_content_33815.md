BRTBA held its AGM on 25 May.  The executive was re-elected: President, Sue Ryan; Vice President, Brenton Greenslade; Secretary, Dee Greenslade and Treasurer, Glynne Ryan.  Members voted to continue to manage the Burra Visitor Centre for the period 2005-08.  As well as managing the Visitor Centre, sub-committees also ran Jazz in the Monster Mine, Jailhouse Rock and the Antique fair.  Planning has begun for the inaugural Wheal Burra Burra Festival of Arts & Culture on 13-14 November.
