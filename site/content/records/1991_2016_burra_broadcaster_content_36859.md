Highways.  The Tourism Minister, Jane Lomax-Smith has approved the naming of a series of roads as Goyder Highway, Wilkins Highway, Thiele Highway and World’s End Highway:

Goyder Highway runs from the Sturt Highway near Monash to a junction with Highway 1 near Crystal Brook via Morgan, Burra & Gulnare.

Wilkins Highway is the Hallett to Jamestown Road.

The Thiele Highway begins at Gawler and goes via Kapunda and Eudunda to Morgan.

The World’s End Highway begins at Eudunda and passes through Robertstown to join the Goyder Highway.
