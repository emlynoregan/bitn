Mrs Mary Bevan is 93 today.

[As May Mannix on 27 January 1896 she had married Foon Tie in Kooringa.  After his death on 9 February 1900, following an accident, she married Robert Bevan 28 April 1900 at the residence of Mr Chin in Kooringa.  She died 8 March 1964 in Adelaide, residence Myrtlebank aged 97.  Robert Bevan died in 1920 aged 87.]
