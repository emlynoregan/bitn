The Burra History Group will be holding its first general meeting on Thursday, 14 September at 7.30 p.m. in the Burra Town Hall meeting room.  All those interested in the history of Burra are cordially invited to attend.  The meeting will be addressed by Meredith Satchell and Eric Fuss, who will outline the aims of the group, following which a committee will be elected.  Supper will be provided.
