Burra-Peterborough Road.  Following a deputation to the Minister of Roads Hon. N.L. Jude MLC on 14 October, advice has been received that a £12,000 grant has been made to maintain and repair this road.  A further £20,000 for construction work is expected in 1961-62.

The £12,000 is divided between Councils:	Burra Burra	£2,000

Hallett		£5,000

Peterborough	£1,500

£8,500

The remaining £3,500 is available for the improvement of watercourses by the construction of culverts and spoon drains.
