State Schools’ Athletics Championships were held last Saturday.

Clay Wooton competed well as a 12-year-old in the U14 division, without winning any medals.

Tasma Wooton, competing in the U18 section won gold medals in the javelin and the shot-put and got a bronze in the discus.

Cheryl Day competed in the U16 section and won gold medals in the 200 m and 400 m, came 2nd in the 100 m and 3rd in the 800 m.

Adam Day competed in the U18 section and won gold medals in the 400 m hurdles and 800 m and came 3rd in the javelin.
