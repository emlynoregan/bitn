Ms Ann Trevenen, the Grand Bard of Cornwall visited Burra on 18 May.  She is the first female Bard of Cornwall, Brittany and Wales and was guest of honour at the recent Kernewek Lowender.  In Burra she was shown around the town by John Biele.
