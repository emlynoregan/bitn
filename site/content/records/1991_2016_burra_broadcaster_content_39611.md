BCS.  A group of students travelled to Balaklava last Wednesday to participate in the SAPSASA carnival, competing with students from 20 primary and area schools.  They came in 6th place overall and Sophie Hall, William Baker and Hayden Webster will go on to compete in the Mid North Squad in Adelaide.
