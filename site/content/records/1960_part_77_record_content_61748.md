Burra High School

On Thursday representatives of Burra High and Primary School staff , School Council Members, District Councillors and officers of the Education and Public Buildings Departments met to consider the site for the new High School, which had been proposed for an area of land west of the Railway Station on Copperhouse Road.  After the meeting sites in the vicinity of the town itself will be further investigated and it is hoped the planned project will be completed by 1975.
