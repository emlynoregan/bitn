Council Elections.  Nominations for the 2010 Local Government elections opened yesterday and close on 21 September.

The inaugural ruling by the Independent Remuneration Tribunal has resulted in a 33% increase in allowances for members of the Regional Council of Goyder.  The Council has been placed in Group Four (the second lowest of the five groups) at $8,000 p.a.
