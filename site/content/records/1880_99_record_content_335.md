Harvest Festival at the new St Mary’s on 5 February.  Communion was at 8 a.m. with Morning Prayer at 11 a.m. and Thanksgiving at 3 p.m.  Rev. Archdeacon Dove preached.  There was a full choral service under direction of Mr Boult, the St Peter’s Cathedral organist, with choristers from the cathedral. This was followed by a public tea and a concert.  The program for the concert was: -

1.	Christmas Carol	The First Noel

2.	Choir	Let Me Like a Soldier Fall

3.	Quartette	Evening

4.	Piano Duet	Adagio & Allegro from 1st Symphony

5.	Song	The Chorister

6.	Glee by the Choir	The Hardy Norseman

7.	Piano Solo	Waltz in D

8.	Song	Blue Alsatian Mountain

9.	Christmas Carol	Emmanuel

10.	Christmas Carol	Boy’s Dream

11.	Glee	Oh Where, and O! Where

12.	Song	Cold and Grey

13.	Piano Duet	Fingal’s Cave

14.	Song	The Village Blacksmith

15.	Glee	Hail Smiling Morn

16.	Vocal Duet	The Moon Has Raised

17.	Song	Lehn’ Dein Wange

18.	Song	Bülluein[?] [partly illegible] Garten

19.	Quartette	Maying

20.	Humorous Song	Sold Again

21.	Glee	Men of Harlech

Unfortunately the piano was badly out of tune.
