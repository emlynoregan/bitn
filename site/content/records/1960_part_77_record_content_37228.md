Accident.  At about 6.15 p.m. on Saturday three miles southwest of Hanson Miss Pauline Miller of Hanson lost control of her Mini-Minor in dusty conditions caused by other vehicles and it rolled over.  She was found trapped in the upturned vehicle by members of the Bowling Club returning from Clare.  They turned the vehicle over and forced the doors open to free Miss Miller, who was unhurt except for some cuts to an ankle.
