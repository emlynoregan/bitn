Accident.  Just before 1 a.m. on Sunday morning a car collected a series of the verandah posts at the Redruth Camp Centre’s Courthouse Hotel as well as a light pole.  A 19-year-old driver was admitted to hospital with severe facial lacerations and his Holden sedan was badly damaged.  [Photo.]
