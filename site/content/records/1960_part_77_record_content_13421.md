Bowls.  Mr H. Binks-Williams opened the Burra Bowling Club’s 1962-63 season.  The rinks were well grassed, but the heavy rain on Friday had prevented a final cut and rolling, resulting in a slow, but true green.  56 players enjoyed a game.
