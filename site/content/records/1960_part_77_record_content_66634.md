Venture a free paper was distributed last week with the Record.  Postal regulations will prohibit this in the future. Pick-up points for Venture will be organised through the town and district.  In Burra they will be at: the Record Office, Burra Radio & Electrical Book-Shop, Ford’s Roadhouse, Gill’s Store and T.W. Wilkinson & Co.

[Places were also listed for Booborowie, Farrell Flat, Hallett, Mt Bryan, Robertstown and Spalding.]
