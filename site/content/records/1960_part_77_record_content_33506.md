Burra Primary School Welfare Club held its AGM, which about 60 mothers attended representing about 50% of the families at the school.  Mrs J. Carpenter was elected President for the fourth year in succession.  The club had expended nearly $600 in the year.  Mr I.L. Auhl the Headmaster thanked office bearers and members for their support.  Other officers elected: Vice-Presidents, Mesdames P. Kirk & R. Pearse; Secretary, Mrs L. Braendler; Assistant Secretary, Mrs T. Pendlebury and Treasurer, Mrs R. Broad.
