Burra Small Bore Rifle Club

Mrs Barbara Pain of Adelaide has accepted an invitation to attend the official opening of the range.  She has recently won the State Championship at Pt Pirie and will bring a number of experienced shooters with her.

Mr R. Fuss has recently offered to make two complete sets of target frames.  They will be in operation for tonight’s shoot over double 25 yards.

Best at Last Tuesday’s shoot were W. Corner, R. Gilbert and R. Kellock.
