Bushfires.  Some local CFS crews have joined other SA units in tackling the bushfires in the eastern states.

On 30 January Frank Jones & David Spackman went east and returned on Monday.

Mark Bellman & Michael Freak went to Melbourne on Sunday before proceeding to Albury-Wodonga.

Andy Thomas, David Nourse & Christopher Stolte left today (Wednesday) and another group plan to go next week.
