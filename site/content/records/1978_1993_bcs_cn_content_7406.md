BCS.  The Years 7-12 Swimming Carnival was held in inclement weather and was won by Draysey House with 354 points from Riggs 350.  Championship Winners:

U13 Girls	Catherine Barker

U13 Boys	Ian Kleinig

U14 Girls	Kristin Barton

U14 Boys	Simon Clode

U15 Girls	Rosalie Lloyd

U15 Boys	Rikki Loffler

Open Girls	Helen Stockman

Open Boys	Wayne Phillips
