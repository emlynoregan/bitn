Spencer Gulf Telecasters

Following completion of arrangements for underwriting the ordinary share issue, plans and specifications for the studio to be erected in Pt Pirie are being prepared.  Equipment has been ordered and transmission should be gin later in 1967.  The station will be GTS 4.
