Accident/Obituary.  A two vehicle head-on accident occurred at about 5 p.m. on the Manoora-Burra Road [19 January] and as a result two men, two women and two young girls from four different families are dead.  The only survivor is a ten-year-old girl [Leanne Affolter] who suffered a broke leg.  She is the daughter of Mr & Mrs Colin Affolter of Booborowie.  The accident occurred on a slight bend about sixteen miles south of Burra and involved a sedan driven by Dudley Ronald Field of Golflands and a utility driven by Russell Edward Nicholas aged 44 of the Wellington Hotel, Waterloo.  There were five passengers in the car.  Two died at the scene and two at Riverton Hospital.  The driver died while being conveyed to Adelaide.  The driver of the utility died at the scene soon after being released from the wreckage, which took about an hour.  Riverton and Burra ambulances attended the accident.  Dr L.G. Miller of Burra attended and police from Burra, Riverton and Saddleworth.  The deceased are:

Dudley Ronald Field (45) of Golflands [Died Gawler, residence Golflands.]

His wife Jean Kathleen Field (43) [Died Riverton, residence Golflands.]

His daughter Jennifer Mary Field (9) [Died Black Springs, residence Golflands.]

His mother Dorothy [Caroline] Vincent Field (71) of Leighton [Died Black Springs, residence Leighton.]

A niece Julie Anne Affolter (14) [Julie Dawn Affolter died Riverton, residence Booborowie.]

[Return Thanks Notice 10 March 1970 page 6 also says Julie Dawn Affolter.]
