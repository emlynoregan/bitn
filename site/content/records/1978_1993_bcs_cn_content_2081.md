BCS Council.  Mr Ron Thamm was re-elected at the first meeting for 1980.  Mr Thamm was the High School Council Chairman prior to the formation of BCS and so is in his fifth year as Chairman.  Mr Rod Bailey was re-elected as Deputy Chairman and Ron Shipard continues as Treasurer.  Miss Kath Millard was elected Secretary.

The Council decided that at this stage it was unnecessary to have monitors at the school crossing by the Town Hall.
