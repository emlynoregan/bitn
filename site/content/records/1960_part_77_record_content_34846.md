Obituary.  Mr Adam Boulton who died at home suddenly on 22 May was the son of the late Elizabeth & George Boulton, being the youngest of their eleven children.  He was born at Gulnare 13 May 1884 and came to Spalding with his parents in 1893 and remained till his death.  In 1924 he married Miss Alice Gale of Yacka.
