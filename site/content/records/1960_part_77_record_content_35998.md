The Burra High School Ball was held at the Burra Town Hall on Friday night and was very successful.  The ‘Astronotes’ provided the music.  The Parents and Friends’ Association sponsored the event and arranged a sumptuous supper.  Peter Morris of the High School staff was a capable MC.
