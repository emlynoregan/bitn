Mr Dean R. Bunney received a portion of his education at Burra School and was later Dux of the Kadina Public School and Kadina H.S.  He won a Law Bursary for the University of Adelaide and after two years at the University he enlisted in the RAN, seeing service in the Pacific on the Warramunga.  He was recently discharged and resumed his studies.  He is also secretary of the University Politics & International Relations Society.  Recently 260 candidates sat for examination to enter the diplomatic service.  Of them 12 received appointments and Dean, who is 20, was one of 3 South Australians who were successful.
