1 May 1980

Burra Mine

Firewood from the Mine is available again this year for local residents free of charge under the following conditions.

Timber removed is not sold.

Timber can be collected anytime from Roche Bros entrance gate from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m.  Monday to Friday, without prior notification.

Larger quantities are available from the crusher area Monday to Saturday from 4.30 p.m. to 6.00 p.m.  Subject to prior notification to the company switchboard during normal office hours.  (No children are permitted in this area.)
