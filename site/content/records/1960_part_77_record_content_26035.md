Accident/Obituary.  On Friday 10 September at about 5.15 p.m. a car with three young men in it crashed at Burra North.  The car with the three, who had been working on the new railway line at Olary, failed to take the bend at the Bon Accord Bridge and landed upside down in the creek.  The vehicle caught alight and incinerated the occupants.  Local police and the Accident Investigation Squad carried out enquiries.  The vehicle was a try top Land Rover.  The deceased were Graham Trevor Jackson (23) of La Trobe St, Blair Athol (driver)
