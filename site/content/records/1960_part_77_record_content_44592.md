In April each class visited the National Trust Museum in Burra.  Grade I visited the Post Office, the bakery and the Fire Station and with Grade II had a train & bus trip to Hanson.  Grades V & VI visited the Town Hall Library.  Grade V went to a shearing shed.  Junior Red Cross was active in the year and held its own fete as well as having several visiting speakers.  Grade VII produced its own magazine ‘The Monthly Review’.
