Burra Community School students do well at Year 12.  Fourteen student completed stage 2 SACE.  Nine A grades were received and 24 B grades.  All students who applied for tertiary admission gained their first preference.  Sarah Boaden, Tracey Bright, Abby Hartman and Kerry Howard all gained A’s and B’s for at least four of their five subjects.
