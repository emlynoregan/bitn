Paxton Square Cottages.  Goyder Council has contracted Bev Fleming & Howard Matson for an initial two-year term as managers of Paxton Square Cottages.  Council has also reviewed the tariffs:

2 rooms for 1 adult $25 and 3 rooms $35.

For 2 adults and four rooms $55 and each additional adult $10 and children $5.
