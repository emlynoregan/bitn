Albert Sampson writes from Mosman Park WA mentioning some former Burraites now in WA.

At Esperance: Clem Sampson, Ron Sampson & Harold Pearce.

At Kalgoorlie: Cliff Lee, son of Mr & Mrs Bill Lee.

At Arthur River: ‘Zac’ Humphrys, stepson of the late Tod Sandland.

At Kulicup: Ralton and Tom Wardle.

At Korredocking in the northern wheat belt: Bill Woollacott (General Store).

At Fremantle: Albert Sampson has his hairdressing & tobacconist business.
