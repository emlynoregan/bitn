Weather.  Patchy, but useful rain has been reported ranging from 26 points at World’s End through 39 at Burra and 50 points at Farrell Flat.  Robertstown received only 19.  Between 7 and 8 a.m. yesterday both Hallett and Mt Bryan reported light snow accompanied in some places by hail.
