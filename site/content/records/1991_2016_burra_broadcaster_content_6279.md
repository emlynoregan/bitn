Spencer Gulf Telecasters will soon screen promotional clips featuring people who live in Burra or have a connection with the town.  Seven profiles have been made of: Bob Lewis, Matt Reilly, Allyson Parsons, Andrew Gebhardt, Laurel Neill, Len Bence and Irene Hirschausen.
