Weather.  Further rains have been general, adding 50 to 60 points in the past week. Burra recorded 60 points.  The pastoral country received up to an inch.  It is understood that the mail truck was again bogged over the weekend and local firms were unable to truck sheep to the Burra Market on Friday.
