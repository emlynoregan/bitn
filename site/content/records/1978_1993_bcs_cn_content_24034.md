Adelaide Repertory Theatre Inc. will be presenting the play ‘Breaker Morant’ in Adelaide.  The Burra Tourist Office is organising a bus to enable residents to see the production on 30 May.  Bus and admission costs are $25 per person.   Contact the Tourist Office.
