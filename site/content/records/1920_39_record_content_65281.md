Mongolata Goldfield

Messrs W. Pexton & Sons Takati Claim report for December.  51 tons of ore was sent to the battery and produced 91 oz 6 dwt 13 gr plus by cyanide 5 oz 12 dwt 15 gr for a total of 96 oz 19 dwt 4 gr.
