Burra CWA held its AGM on 17 June.  Elected were: President, Barbara Frances; Secretary, Peg Ahwan and Treasurer, Daphne Mengersen.  Among other donations this year the branch gave:

The Flood Relief Fund			$200

CWA Dorothy Dolling Memorial Fund	$200

Lymphoedema Assessment Clinic

(Flinders University)			$200

$2,000 has been reserved for the Burra Baby Change Room.
