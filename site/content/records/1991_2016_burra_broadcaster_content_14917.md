Local Industry/Businesses

Burra Community Print successful printed 7,500 copies of River Murray Charts for local publisher Maureen Wright.

Richard Holbrook has continued his work with some of SA’s most famous timepieces, including that in Adelaide Town Hall.  He and Jenny have been most successful in establishing their Clock Shop and Tearooms and Griffiths’ Cottage B & B.

Mongolata Gold has been added to Commercial Street.

Henry Cullen retired in June from the Eastern Mail Run after covering over a million miles since his father took over the service in 1949.

Refire Resources have identified four exploration sites near Burra to be investigated in the future.
