Obituary.  Mrs Elizabeth Ward, relict of the late Richard Ward, died aged 81.  She was born Elizabeth White in Bristol, England, 1 September 1829 and married in Trinity Church, St Philips, Bristol.  She arrived in SA on the Emigrant in 1854 and went to live at Belalie (White Hutt) [Manning offers no help with White Hutt or Hut â€“ at least in this area.]  She came to live in Burra in 1870 where her husband was a storekeeper and general dealer.  Mr Ward died 18 February 1886.  She leaves one daughter, Miss E.A. Ward.  Major John Ward of the Salvation Army is a nephew.
