Football.  The voting for the Mail Medal was held at Booborowie last Saturday night.  Winner of the Mail Medal for 1972 was Ian Roberts of Blyth.  He also won the Mobil Trophy.

As runner-up Paul McCarthy of Clare South won the Burra Record Trophy.
