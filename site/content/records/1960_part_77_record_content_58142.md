Advt.  A 20-unit motel complex is planned for Burra with construction to begin as soon as sufficient funds are available.  Shares are offered in multiples of $500 exclusively to residents of Burra and District and a meeting is to be held at the Burra Town Hall 23 September for details of the proposal to be outlined.
