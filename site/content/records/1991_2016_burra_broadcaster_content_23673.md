Clare Valley Tourism Marketing Committee has limited funds to assist event organisers in maximising the potential of their marketing dollar.  Events organisers will have to demonstrate how and to what extent their event will generate tourism visitation and how they intend to ensure ongoing benefits for the local community.  Contact Kerry Stockman, Clare valley Marketing Manager.
