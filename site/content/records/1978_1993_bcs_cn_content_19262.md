Rob Linn, Historian, is in Burra working on a survey of eight Lower North towns identifying historic sites and buildings and making recommendations for the State Heritage List.  He has been commissioned by the State heritage Branch of the Department of Environment and Planning.  Burra already has 55 listings on the State heritage Register.
