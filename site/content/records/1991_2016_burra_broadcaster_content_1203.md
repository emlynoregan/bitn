The Antique Clock Workshop at 8 Morehead Street turns one year old.  Richard Holbrook has repaired and restored a variety of clocks and watches over the period.  He is the first qualified watchmaker to come to the area for at least 30 years.  The workshop has also earned a reputation for its Devonshire teas.
