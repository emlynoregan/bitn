Burra High School held its speech night in the Town Hall on Thursday.  Chairman of the High School Council H.J.B. Jennison presided.  After several items a moving address was given by Dr D.M. Steele, a former Chairman of the High School Council.  Then Mrs M.T. Kellock handed out the certificates and Mrs Steele gave out the prizes and sports awards.  Further items and reports followed.
