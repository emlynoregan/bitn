The Political Reform League meeting gets almost the whole of a page for its report, beginning with ¼ column on page 3.  At the end of the meeting the Mayor was elected President and Horace Oates the Secretary of the Burra Branch.  The aim was to improve things generally by working through the Liberal Federation – working to reform the Liberal Federation by its adoption of suggestions from the Political Reform League.
