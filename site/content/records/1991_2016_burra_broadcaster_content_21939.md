Notice.  The SA Government has purchased a 442.5 Ha property c. 5 km east of Mt Bryan to establish a conservation park for the protection of a remnant iron grass tussock grassland (Lomandra multiflora ssp. dura).  It will be called Mokota Conservation Park.
