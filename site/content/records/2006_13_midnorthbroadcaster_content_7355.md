Cricket.  Belalie-Mokota Cricket Assoc. Final

Burra 208 defeated Belalie-Mannanarie 171.  R. Sullivan for Burra scored an excellent 96, including 10 boundaries and a 6.  [Photo showing the point at which Marcus Hall stopped Bel/Man’s Bernie McInerney’s run after the catch off Thompson’s delivery and a photo of a run in the 112-run third wicket partnership between Rob Sullivan & Geoff Thompson, which was the catalyst for Burra’s victory in the 2008-09 Belalie-Mokota Cricket Association’s season decider on Saturday.]
