Bowls

1st Division	Burra Gold 77 defeated Clare Blue 66

Burra Green 71 defeated Saddleworth Blue 61

This result gave the pennant to Clare Red.

2nd Division	Hallett White 68 defeated Burra White 63

Saddleworth Red 90 defeated Burra Blue 46

Bowls Associates: in the pennant competition the teams finished in the order:

Peterborough Red, Spalding White, Peterborough Gold, Burra Gold, Spalding Blue, Peterborough Blue, Burra Green, Spalding Red, Hallett & Terowie.

Champion of Champions was Gloria Brackenridge of Peterborough and the Champion Pair were Dora Foote & Gwen Raymond of Peterborough.
