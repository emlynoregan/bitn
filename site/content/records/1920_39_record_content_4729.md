Charles Lowe was given a farewell from the Kooringa Masonic Lodge on Saturday.  He is going to Broken Hill.  He has been associated with the lodge for 37 years.  Mr Lowe was for some years Councillor for North Ward.  Mr & Mrs Lowe had been active in patriotic work and their two sons had seen active service.
