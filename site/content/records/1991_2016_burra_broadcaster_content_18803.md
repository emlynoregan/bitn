Accident.  On Sunday morning 12 April a B-double truck rolled over c. 4 km north of Hallett on the Barrier Highway, loaded with 100 head of cattle.  The driver and his two children were not seriously injured, and were treated at Burra Hospital.  Hallett SES and Hallett & Whyte Yarcowie CFS attended.  24 of the cattle had to be destroyed and five more are to be assessed for injury.
