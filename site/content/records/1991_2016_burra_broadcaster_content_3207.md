Farrell Flat hosted its first Sheepdog Trial recently.

The event was very successful.  Mr George Noble, President of the SA Working Sheepdog Association, congratulated the Mid North Sheepdog Training Club on its organisation and hoped the trials would become an annual event.

Winners:

Open – Old Mill Laddie, Mr A. Dinning (Naracoorte)

Improver – Clemenstville Kara, Mr A. Ireland

Novice – Clementsville Kara, Mr A. Ireland.
