Roger Boehm won 1st prize in the equine section of the recent Barossa Valley Festival Procession with his horse ‘Jock’ and his canoe-front wagonette.  Roger & his passengers, Elly Nicholls, Chris Reed and Maria Reed dressed as nuns and priests for the procession.
