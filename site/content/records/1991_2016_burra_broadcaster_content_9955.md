Cricket Grand Final

The one day grand final was held on Saturday at Jamestown.

Hallett 4 for 125 defeated Bundaleer 123 all out.

Cricket Awards for the season:

Cricketer of the Year		D. Leese (Bundaleer)

Bowling Aggregate		D. Leese (Bundaleer)		36 wickets

Bowling Average			J. Agnew (Burra)			11.05

Batting Aggregate		P. Francis (Bel/Man)		576

Batting Average			P. Mattey (Hallett)		169.3

Field Award			I. Dolphin (North Booborowie)

Junior Cricketer of the Year	P. Furst (Spalding)		68.3
