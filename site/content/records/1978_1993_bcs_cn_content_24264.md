SAPSASA Cross Country Championships were held recently at Bonython Park in Adelaide.  In the 11-year-olds section the best BCS student was Jade Phillips who came 5th, while in the 12-year-old group Skye Nicholls won the gold medal* and Carla Brenton came 6th.

[*Corrected in the issue of 12 August to a silver medal.]
