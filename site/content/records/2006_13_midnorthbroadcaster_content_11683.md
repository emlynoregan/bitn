Australia Day Celebrations at Burra drew a large and appreciative crowd in good weather.  Live choral singing by the Sing Australia Choir was enjoyed and the Burra Swimming Club provided breakfast at Thomas Pickett Reserve.  Andy Thomas was presented with the Australia Day award and cut the Australia Day Cake made and decorated by Colleen Spackman.  [Photo of Andy Thomas receiving his award from Cr Darryl Venning.]
