Classic Country – Mid North Tourism awards 1998 were presented at the Clare Country Club on Sunday 22 November.  Seventeen categories were covered.

Sylvia Pohlner of Tooralie Homestead was the winner of Alternative Accommodation.

Glynne Ryan of Ryan’s Deer Farm won a commendation.

Avian Pink won an award for Outstanding Contribution by an Individual.
