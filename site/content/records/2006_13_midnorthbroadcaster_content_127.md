Weather.  Last Friday major thunderstorms swept through Peterborough, Yatina, Pekina, Orroroo, Black Rock, Minvalara and Morgan areas.  Damage to roads was severe in the Orroroo and Carrieton areas.  Sudden falls of 20 to 50 mm were recorded in a number of places, but at Black Rock 330 points (83.8 mm) fell in one hour and at Magnetic Hill a fall of more than 100 mm was recorded in an hour.
