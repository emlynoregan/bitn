The Maypole Fair on 19 September in aid of the Redruth Methodist Sunday-school is reported in 1½ columns.  In 1923 they raised £275 and the aim for 1924 was for £400.  £1,800 was needed to open the school free from debt.  [No total is given here, but stall takings noted add up to £280-11-6.]
