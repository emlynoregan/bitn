Burra Mine

SAMIN Ltd has announced the acquisition of three mineral claims in the Murchison area of WA, containing tungsten reserves.  It has acquires 200,000 shares in Poseidon in return for technical services.  It will also soon seek to raise $1.75 million through an issue of 3.5 million fully paid 50c shares.  McPhar Geophysics Ltd & Western Collieries will participate in this issue considerably and it will also be open to the public.
