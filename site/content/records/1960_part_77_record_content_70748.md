Tennis: Junior Tennis Final

(‘C’ Grade)	Leighton 8-58 defeated Burra 4-55

This is Leighton’s first shield win since Junior Tennis began in the Association in 1967.

President of the Association, Mr Don Lloyd, presented the Shield to the Captain of Leighton, Barry Gent.
