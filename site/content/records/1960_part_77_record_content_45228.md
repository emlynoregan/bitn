Cricket.  The wind-up of the Mokota Association for the season was held at Booborowie on Friday.  The guest speaker was test cricketer Barry Jarmyn and he was accompanied by Mr Arthur Lee.  Barry Jarmyn presented Philip Byles with the Cricketer of the Year Trophy.
