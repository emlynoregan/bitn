Football.  Mid North League 1st Semi-Final

‘A’ Grade	Min-Man 24.17 defeated Auburn 9.7

‘B’ Grade	Riverton 14.14 defeated Aberdeen 1.1

Colts	Min-Man 2.11 defeated Robertstown 1.3

Junior Colts 	SM United 7.3 defeated Robertstown 3.4

The Mail Medal for the Mid North League for 1972 goes to Lindsay Schmidt of Robertstown, from Grantly Cullen of Riverton.  In the ‘B’ Grade the Jenner Trophy went to Barry Dixon of SM United.  The Twartz Trophy on Senior Colts went to Chris Boxall of SM United and in Junior Colts the winner was R. Bruce of Min-Man.
