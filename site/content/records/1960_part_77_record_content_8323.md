Burra North Children’s Playground is to be aided by the pictures this week.  The first meeting of the Children’s Playground Committee was held 11 October 1923, permission having been given by the Town Council for the use of Lowe’s Park as a playground.  Mr James Reed was Patron and Mr L.H. Foote President with W.J.C. Ewins and M.A. Radford as Vice-Presidents.  Max Morton was Secretary.  Practically all the buildings and pieces of equipment were donated by citizens.  The committee is desirous of improving the facilities.  The present Committee is President, F.H. Reed with Messrs S. Scroop, S.J. Woollacott, L. Hirschausen, L. White, A.B. Riggs, W. Carpenter and Mesdames S. Scroop, H. Jennison and W.H. Lloyd.
