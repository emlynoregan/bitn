Obituary.  Lt Col Glan Tiver, formerly of Burra, died suddenly at Box Hill North, (Melbourne).  He was educated at the Burra Schools and later became a partner in the Burra Motor Firm of Whitney and Tiver.  He enlisted as a private in WW II and was commissioned in New Guinea.  When hostilities ceased he held the rank of Major.  He became a member of the regular army and at the time of his death was a Lt Colonel.  He was accorded a full military funeral.  He is survived by a wife and two married daughters.  Mrs Tiver was formerly Miss Corris Walker.  Mrs Roy Jeffery of Burra is a sister to the deceased.

[Glanville Hilary Tiver born 15 July 1907 Kooringa.]
