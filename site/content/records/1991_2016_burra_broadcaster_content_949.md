Advt.  [Ian Oswald-Jacobs] will soon be holding an Aerial Photographic Display in the area.  Most property homesteads have been photographed in October this year.  Call and see Barrie and Shirley Mawby at:

Burra Civic Centre Wednesday 4 November 9 to 5 p.m.

Farrell Flat Hall Thursday 5 November 9 to 5 p.m.

Mintaro Hall Friday 6 November 9 to 5 p.m.

5 inch square coloured proofs of your property will be available and various sizes of enlargements will also be on display.  Photographs of all areas and of the towns are available.
