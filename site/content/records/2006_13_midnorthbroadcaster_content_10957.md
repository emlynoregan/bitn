Wind Farms

International Power Australia (IPA) is looking at the possibility of adding 11 turbines to the 26 approved for Willogoleche Hill.  The local community has been invited to an exhibition in the Hallett Institute Hall 20 October at which representatives of IPA and Wind Prospect Pty Ltd will be on hand to answer questions.
