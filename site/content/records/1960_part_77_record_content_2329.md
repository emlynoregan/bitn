Notice.  Burra Town Council Election Results

Mayor	Edward Thomas Baulderstone

East Ward	Herbert Cornelius

North Ward	Edwin Finch

The above were declared elected unopposed.

There being no nominations for West Ward a supplementary election is called for West Ward with nominations closing on 3 June and an election on 2 July if required.
