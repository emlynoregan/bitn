Mongolata Goldfield

Takati GM’s manager Mr W.K. Pexton reports 50½ tons put through for 133 oz 16dwt 6gr.

Curlew Mine reports 10½ oz pure gold banked last week/.

Byles’ Mongolata GM is raising good ore and the outlook is encouraging.
