Accident/Obituary.  Dudley Harold Garrard aged 32 of Booborowie died at Curnamona Station on Monday last week [17 April].  He was riding through saltbush country when he fell from his motor cycle about three miles west of the homestead and seems to have died instantly.  He was found by Mr Brian Treloar of Mooleulooloo Station from his aircraft, following unsuccessful searches on Tuesday and Wednesday.
