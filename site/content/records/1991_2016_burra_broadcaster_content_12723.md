The Australian Country Games were competed for at the weekend.  Luke Spackman won Athlete of the Day for the U13 to 15 Age Group.  He had won gold in the 200 m, 100 m, and the triple jump.

Other Burra athletes to win medals were:

Tina Williams with gold in the 100 m and 200 m and a silver in the triple and high jumps.

Boh Wall won silver in the 800 m and the triple jump and bronze medals for the 100 m, 200 m and discus.

Nicky Taylor won silver in the long jump and bronze in hurdles.
