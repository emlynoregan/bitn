Burra Pony Club held its AGM recently.  The past year saw several hunts and trail rides, a show-jumping competition and an annual gymkhana.  A number of members attended one day events at Clare, Pt Pirie and Moonta and a highlight of the year was the five-day riding school run by Mr Bob Button.

Elected: President, Mrs P. Brereton; Vice-President, Mr Bob Steele and Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs S. Thamm.
