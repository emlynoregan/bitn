A Kapunda-Burra Wheelbarrow Push will be a feature of Burra’s J150 celebrations.  It will take place over 21-22 January.  The route will be via Tarlee and Riverton to Saddleworth on day 1 and then to Burra via Manoora on day 2.  First prize is to be $1,000 and 2nd $500.  There will be a maximum of 20 teams with six runners per team.
