Thefts.  For the second time since the start of July articles have been stolen from a station sedan garaged at the Bon Accord Hotel.  On Saturday the car was broken into and a new tyre stolen.  Early in July the items stolen included a spare wheel and tyre, a car jack, wheel-brace, transistor radio and an umbrella.  The vehicle is owned by the licensee Mr F.K. Thurgood.
