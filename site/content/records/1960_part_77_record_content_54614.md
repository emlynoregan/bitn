Obituary.  Sydney Malcolm Sommerville died at the Clare Hospital 17 December 1970 aged 62.  He was the eldest son of the late Mr & Mrs Malcolm Sommerville of Hilltown.  After three years at Scotch College he went to live with his uncle at Spalding.  Thirty-five years ago he bought land in partnership with his brother Tom and lived there till his death.  He was active in local affairs.  He was Vice-President of the Bowling Club for three years and in 1970 resigned from the Presidency due to ill health.  He was the treasurer of the Spalding Presbyterian Church for thirty years.  He is survived by a wife, three daughters and two sons: Merilyn Mrs J. Wehrmann, Aileen Mrs M. Robinson, Lynette, Norman and Eric.  There is one grandson, Darren Robinson.

[Born at Clare 17 December 1908.]
