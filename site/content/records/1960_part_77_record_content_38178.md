Burra Primary School.  Enrolment for 1968 is 119 boys and 139 girls for a total of 258.  Last year’s enrolment of 41 in Grade I compares with this year’s 27.  [They are listed.]

New staff:

Miss Brigitte Neumeister comes from Western Teachers College

Miss Nadine McKenzie comes from Wattle Park Teachers College

Allocation of classes:

Miss Raelene Poticary takes Grade I, as she did in 1967.

Mrs Pens has Grade II.

Mrs Carpenter has Grade III.

Mrs Perry has Grade VI and Mr Lannan has Grade VII.
