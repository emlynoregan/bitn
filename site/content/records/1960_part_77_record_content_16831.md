Burra North Red Cross held its AGM 3 July.

Elected: President, Mrs H. Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames V. Cox & I. Hirschausen; Secretary, Mrs E. Heinrich; Assistant Secretary, Mrs R. Reed and Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.

Membership for the year was 39 and average attendance was 14.  Through the year their Patroness Mrs [F.M.] Pearce died.  In December £40 was donated to the Burra Hospital.  £55 was sent to Headquarters for the March Appeal.  The group met in the Aberdeen Croquet Hall.
