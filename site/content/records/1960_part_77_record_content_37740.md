A Civic Memorial Service will be held in St Mary’s Church on Thursday afternoon 21 December at 5.30 p.m. for Harold Holt, 18th Prime Minister of Australia, presumed drowned while swimming off Portsea, Victoria, last Sunday.  The Mayor will read the lesson.
