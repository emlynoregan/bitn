Lower Mid North Interschool Sports Day was held at Burra last Friday.  BCS was victorious for the 10th year in succession: Burra 1224 points, Kapunda 1012, Riverton 832 and Eudunda 671.

Outstanding individual performances were:

Cheryl Day with 8 first places

Mark Bowden (U13) with 7 first places

Danny Burton with 5 firsts and there were 4 firsts each to Adrian White and Clayton Helps.
