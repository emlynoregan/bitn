Rev. Roger Brown, President of the Methodist Conference, met members of the community at Redruth Memorial Hall last Tuesday evening.  He spoke on the missionary work and the essence of change sweeping through the Christian Church everywhere.  The meeting was also attended by Monsignor Hughes and Mr S. Preiss representing the Catholic Community and Rev. Sibly representing the Anglican Church.
