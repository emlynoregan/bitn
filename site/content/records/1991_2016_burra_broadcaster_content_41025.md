BRTBA Awards 2015 were presented at the Burra Railway Station on Tuesday evening.

A local Hero Award was presented to Les Warnes for his contributions to the tourism industry in Burra.

Best Presented Burra Business		Burra Newsagency

Best Tourist Attraction			Burra Passport

Business with the Most Helpful Staff	Oppy’s IGA

Friendliest Business in Burra		Gaslight Café

Burra’s Favourite Pub			Royal Exchange Hotel

Burra’s Favourite Tradie		George Sochacki

Best Local Event			Burra Antique & Collectables Fair

Burra’s Favourite Eating Place		La Pecora Nera
