Burra High School Parents & Friends’ Association

After two years as President Mrs D.H. Field retired.  Elected were: President, Mrs F. Matthews; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Francis & H. Griffen; Secretary, Mrs B. Bevan; Assistant Secretary, Mrs J. Bogisch and Treasurer, Mrs P.W. Carpenter.  The big events for the year were the Annual Ball, the Annual Banquet and the Northern School’s Athletics Day, held at Burra in May 1967.  This year’s School Social will be held on 5 April in the Town Hall as the climax to Sports Day.  Certificates for the Alternative Course examinations in 1967 will then be presented.
