Burra Regional Art Gallery opened its portrait competition on Saturday evening.  The event was opened and judged by artists Robert Hannaford and Alison Mitchell.  First prize went to Roger Kalleske of Kapunda with his installation entitled self portrait.  Second prize went to Lis Jones Ingman for her ‘Along Burra Creek’.
