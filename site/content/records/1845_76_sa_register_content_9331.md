Advertisement.  Burra Coper Works.  Until further notice the rates for cartage shall be:

To and from Port Wakefield	40/- per ton

To and From Port Adelaide	45/- per ton

James Hamilton, Manager, English and Australian Copper Company, Adelaide 20 August 1855.
