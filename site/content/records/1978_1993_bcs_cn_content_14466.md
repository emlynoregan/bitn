Robyn Page.  34 years old, married with one son.  Originally from Parramatta, NSW, but completed last two years of schooling at Barmera.  Then completed a Bachelor of Education degree in Adelaide and taught in Adelaide where I married my husband, Neil.  Moved to Burra in 1981 and for two years worked as an advisor for Mid North and York Peninsula Regional Education Office.  Presently working two days a week as a primary teacher at BCS.
