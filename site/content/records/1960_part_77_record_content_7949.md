Weather.  In the past week good general rains have delivered up to an inch, most of it from Thursday onwards.  Most dams are now full.  For the week some registrations to hand: Old Koomooloo 81 points, Koomooloo 92, Woolgangi 76, Balah 107, Grassville 141, Mongolata 120 and Burra 123.
