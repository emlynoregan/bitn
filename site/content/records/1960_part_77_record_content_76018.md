Meals on Wheels held its AGM at Burra Hospital 7 July

In the last year the organisation successfully supplied 3,408 meals.  Mrs L. Jennison is the new staff officer and elected were: Chair, Mrs L.A. Phillips; Vice-Chair, Mrs A.M. Odgers and Treasurer, Mrs E.L. Clode.
