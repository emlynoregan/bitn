Burra Kindergarten

There is an enrolment of 51 compared with 42 last year.  This gives a maximum daily attendance of 30.

22 are enrolled for daily attendance and 29 for several times a week.  [All are listed.]

It is worth noting the district importance of the kindergarten is shown by the 5 children from Hallett, 4 from Booborowie, 4 from Gum Creek and 2 from Mt Bryan.
