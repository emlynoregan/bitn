Burra Regional Art Gallery had standing room only for the opening of the Leonard Bence Retrospective Exhibition.  The display of 220 paintings was opened by Mary Woollacott.  Susan Roche, co-curator, welcomed guests and Peter Harvey followed up the opening by naming the long room the Leonard Pendrell Bence Room.  [Photo of Susan Roche, Leonard Bence and Mary Woollacott.]
