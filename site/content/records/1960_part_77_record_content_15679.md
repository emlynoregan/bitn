Bowls.  The season closed on Saturday.

The club had enjoyed an outstanding year.  The Burra Gold team won the pennant in the Mid-North Association.  In the North-Eastern Association Burra Blue failed by only two points from achieving a tie for top position.  The Easter Tournament was very successful.  The ladies had a successful year in raising £250 for the Men’s Club.

Trophies:

Club Champion	Brian Riggs

Handicap Singles	K. Phillips

Consistency	J. Minchin

Men’s Night Pairs	C. Turner & E. Pietsch

Championship Pairs	O. Holmes & W. Shipard

Mixed Pairs	V. Hood & O. Holmes

Associates’ Champion	Mrs D. Field

Associates’ Handicap Singles	Mrs D. Field
