The Zob-a-thon on 24 April for the local youth club raised in excess of $250 and was a financial success.  The gruelling 10 mile course was attacked by walkers, bike riders, horse and gig, prams and even a bathtub.  80 people enjoyed the spread in the hall afterwards.
