Burra Racing Club AGM was held and Mr W. Brown of ‘The Grove’ Stud was elected Chairman.  Andrew Tennant was elected Patron.  Race dates for this year are Wednesday 13 September. And Saturday 21 October.  The committee is calling for new members in the face of falling numbers of financial members in the past year.
