Mrs Edith Carpenter has taken up a position on Kangaroo Island and left Burra over the Christmas break.  She spent many years in Burra and was vitally involved in innumerable organisations including the St John Ambulance, Burra District Advisory Committee, The Arts Council and Burra Kindergarten as well as being Community Co-ordinator at BCS.
