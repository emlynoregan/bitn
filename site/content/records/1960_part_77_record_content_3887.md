Advt.  Elder, Smith & Co. Ltd & Goldsbrough Mort & Co. Ltd have instructions from J. & A.L. Kellock for the sale of ‘Wyoming’, 15,332 acres of Crown Perpetual Lease at the RSL Hall in Burra on 25 October.

Comprising Block 80 County of Burra of 14,370 acres

Sections 26, 27 & 86SE Hundred of Tomkinson of 962 acres 2 roods, of seven sheep-proof paddocks, 8 dams, 4 room G.I. cottage and shearing shed etc.  [89, 40, 18 October 1960, page 2 adds that the property has been in the Kellock Family for 60 years.]
