Scotty’s Grave.  Reprinted from the Adelaide Chronicle of 15 November 1934.  The grave is alongside the road between Marrabel and Kapunda.  ‘Scotty’ was among other things paymaster at the Burra Mine and made weekly trips to Adelaide for the Mining Company.
