Burra Golf Associates held their AGM on 23 September and elected: Patroness, Mrs F. Reed; President, Mrs R. Wuttke; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames D. Field & E. Jeffery; Secretary, Mrs r. Hillman; Treasurer, Mrs R. Gebhardt; Captain, Mrs J. Oates and Vice-Captain Mrs D. Kempster.

Trophy winners were:

‘A’ Grade Champion	Mrs E. Jeffery

‘B’ Grade Champion	Mrs J. Wiseman

‘C’ Grade Champion	Mrs. D. O’Reilly
