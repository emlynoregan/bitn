Goyder Council’s Heritage Fund is now in its 7th year, with a pool of $15,000 available for 2007-08 financial year.  People who qualify for a heritage grant can get up to half the cost of their job, to a maximum grant of $1,500.  A discussion with the Heritage Advisor, Mt Douglas Alexander is advisable before applying for a grant.
