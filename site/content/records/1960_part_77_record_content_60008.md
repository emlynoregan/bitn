Football.  North Eastern Football League held its AGM 14 February.  Booborowie and Hallett announced their amalgamation due to depleted numbers.  Mr M. Vandeleur of Clare was elected President of the League with R.N. Collins as Patron; Secretary-Treasurer, A.L. Fillmore.  For 1972 the League will comprise Spalding, Burra Clare North, Clare South and Booborowie-Hallett.
