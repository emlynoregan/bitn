Burra Burra DC, Burra Town Hall 9 October

The Department of Public Health is to be asked to expedite common effluent drain scheme plans.

It was resolved that the Grant-in-aid of $1,200 be allotted to work on the Thames Street Bridge.

It was resolved to buy $545 worth of carpet squares for the Town Hall Smoke Room (laying included).

The Minister for Local Government advises of a subsidy of $1,400 to assist in the purchase of 63 acres of Section 4 Hundred of Kooringa [the Ballast Quarry block].
