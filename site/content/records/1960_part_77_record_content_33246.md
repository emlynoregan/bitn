Lieutenant P.N. Dinham of Booborowie arrived home from Nui Dat, Vietnam, on 26 January, having contracted malaria while serving with the 6th Battalion Royal Australian Regiment there for eight months.  He is expected to be posted within Australia at the end of his sick leave.
