Burra Town Council, 4 March

Cr Jeffery asked that the Young Street Bridge [closed] be demolished and the pieces be placed in the depot.  Agreed.

PMG is to be informed that the Council will erect street names before allotting house numbers.

Approval was given for the erection of the Bowling Club toilet block.

L.M. Gordon was asked to remove a dangerous piece of wood overhanging the footbridge in Commercial Street and also asked to clear the mortar wall from the creek.
