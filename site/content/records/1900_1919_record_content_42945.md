Recruiting Meeting, Burra Institute, 4 August.

The hall was well filled.  Mr Crewes took the chair.  Speakers were Hon. J.H. Vaughan, Attorney General and W. Miller MP.  There were apologies from Hon. L. O’Loughlin.  The Attorney General’s speech is reported at length. At the end D.C. Eig and L.J.T. Turner signed application cards to volunteer.  (¾ column.)
