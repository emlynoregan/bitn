Burra Branch of the National Trust will hold its AGM on Friday 27 August in the Old Courthouse.  The guest speakers will be Dennis O’Hoy and James Thompson from Bendigo.  Dennis is a founding member of the Bendigo Trust.  They run the Central Deborah Mine, a working mine that also runs underground gold tours; talking tram tours and an underground function centre.
