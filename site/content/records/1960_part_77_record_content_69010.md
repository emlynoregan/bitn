Advt.  Elders-GM on instructions from K.R. Phillips Pty Ltd (K.R. & I.M. Phillips) will sell ‘The Gums’ comprising 30,000 acres P/L and F/H (12,141 ha).  It will be offered first as a whole and if not sold in three lots.

Lot 1.  ‘Homestead’ of 11,500 acres P/L with stone homestead of six main rooms etc.

Lot 2.  ‘Pooles’ of 12,500 acres P/L and F/H.

Lot 3.  ‘Kings’ of 6,000 acres P/L.
