J150 Events

The National Trust has the first of its J150 displays on show in BankSA, based on ‘Sport & Recreation.

Street banners produced for Burra’s J150 will adorn the town for 1995.

Burra Tennis Club will hold its ‘In Cellar Cool’ event on 18 February, featuring a jazz band and mini disco in the Unicorn Brewery Cellars.

St Mary’s Church will stage a Flower Festival 24 & 25 February.

The great, great, great, great, granddaughter of Richard Trevithick, inventor of the Cornish beam Engine visited Burra on Monday.

On the same day a great nephew of Sir Frederick Holder visited Burra.
