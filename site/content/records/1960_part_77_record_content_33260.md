Burra Rural Youth has had a busy week with about 40 members off to Elders-Gm Wool Stores at Pt Adelaide on Monday.  The ABC Competition saw two candidates go to Clare on Tuesday.  They were Anita Archer and Geoffrey Dare and the latter was placed second.
