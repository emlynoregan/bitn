Burra Women’s Branch of the LCL held its AGM in the CWA Rooms on Friday.  President Mrs R. Collins was in the chair.  An address was given by Geoffrey Dare on ‘Politics in America’.

Elected were: President, Mrs Ron Collins; Vice-President, Mrs R. Pearse; Secretary, Mrs N. Finch and Treasurer, Mrs T. Padbury.

Speeches were also given by Mr Allen, MP for Frome and Mr Geddes, the Member for Northern.
