BHS.  Parents met staff at a meeting organised by the Parents & Friends’ Association at the school on 17 February.  President Mrs A. Benger chaired the meeting.  Chairman of the High School Council Mr Darrell Field spoke briefly and the Headmaster Mr R.M. Farrow introduced the two new members of staff: Miss Newman and Mr Pennington.
