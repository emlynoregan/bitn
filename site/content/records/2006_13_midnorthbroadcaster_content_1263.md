The 18th Variety Club Bash will leave Gawler on Saturday 12 August with 70 cars headed for the first overnight stop at Koomooloo via Burra.  Owner of the station is now Hamish Dunn, only the third owner since it was established by I.J. Warnes in the late nineteenth century.
