Accident.  A Volkswagen sedan driven by Mr Carl Lawrence of Campbelltown collided with a Holden sedan driven by David Phillips of Burra at about 8 p.m. on Friday 8 October about 5 miles south of Hallett at Shattock’s Corner.  The Holden rolled over and the driver and his passengers, Angela Ryan and Mrs Joan Barrett, both of Burra, were admitted to the Burra Hospital.  Mrs Barrett was transferred to Adelaide Hospital the next day, it is believed with a shattered ankle.  Both vehicles were extensively damaged.
