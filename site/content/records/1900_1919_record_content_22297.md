Frank Cameron Howell, aged 10, was sent to Magill Reformatory till aged 18 for stealing ½ from the till of Mrs Bell’s store in Mt Bryan.  He had a record of reckless deeds, including riding under a carriage from Mt Bryan to Hallett and hiding on another train for many miles.
