Helen Jensen of Burra has an exhibition entitled ‘Near and Far’ at the Burra Regional Art Gallery, comprising oils, pastels and acrylics.  It is in the Bence Room for September.

Also at the gallery this month is an exhibition of cloth constructions by Canadian artist Wendy Toogood.
