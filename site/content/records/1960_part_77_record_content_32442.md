Obituary.  Louise Alice Fatchen of Black Springs died at Riverton Hospital on Saturday.

[Born Louisa Alice Broad 16 February 1896 at Bright: died 10 December 1966 at Riverton, residence Black Springs.  The name is incorrectly transcribed for the Deaths CD as Fathcen.  She married Wilfred Thomas Winders in 1919.  He died in 1923 and she married Arthur Edwin Raymond Fatchen in 1932.]
