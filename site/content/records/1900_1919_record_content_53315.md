Burra Rifle Club.  On Saturday the 2nd match for J.A. Riggs’ Trophy was fired and the best shots were A.L. Kellock 98, J.E. Pearce 88 & R.O. Robinson 75.  Mr Robinson won the trophy on aggregate with 155.
