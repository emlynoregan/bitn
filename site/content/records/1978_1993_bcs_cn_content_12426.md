Redruth Guild held its last meeting on 20 March at the Redruth Camp Centre.  Many former members attended, including:

Joyce Thomas	Mavis Satchell	Joan Fairchild

Shelia Fairchild	Joyce Fairchild	Joyce McGrath

The Beams	The Pitmans	The Haydons

The Hores.

[The last four being former ministers and their wives.]

Reta Jennison unveiled a commemorative plaque to mark over sixty years of service to the church and community given by the group.
