F.A.S. Field of Monavea has just reaped a paddock of 60 acres which averaged 33½ bushels to the acre.  The ground had been dressed with two cwt of phosphate to the acre and the grain was plump and of excellent quality.  It was reaped with a Massey-Harris harvester.
