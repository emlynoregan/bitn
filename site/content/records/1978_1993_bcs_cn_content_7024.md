Lewis Board of Management [Paxton Square]

The Chairman’s Report

It is hoped the work on 11 cottages on Bridge Terrace will be completed by February 1983.

A vigorous promotion policy will be needed to raise the $20,000 annually required to repay interest and capital on the debenture loan.
