The Rostrum Voice of Youth public speaking contest was held at Burra Community School on Thursday 29th April.  This year all the competitors were female.  Burra Penguin Club hosted the evening.  Although the five junior girls from BCS set a high standard, in the end Clemmie Wetherall from Clare H.S. won the junior section and Vanessa Paulenas from Balaklava H.S. won the senior section.
