Burra Burra DC’s assessment valuations have risen by 2.13% this year.  Rural values are largely static, but properties in towns rose significantly.  In Burra values rose 5 to 8% with smaller increases in Farrell Flat, Mt Bryan and Booborowie.  Retail, office and industrial values were static, but hotel assessments fell.
