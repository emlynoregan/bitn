Burra High School speech night will be in Burra Town Hall 13 December.  Last year’s Fulbright Scholar for SA Mr K.L. Berndt will be guest speaker and Mrs Berndt will present the prizes, certificates and trophies.  Arrangements for a school bus next year from Hanson via Hams’ Corner and Shafton are in their final stages.
