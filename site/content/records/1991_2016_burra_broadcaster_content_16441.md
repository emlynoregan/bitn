Rosie Lloyd was well known in Burra as a junior athlete and represented Australia in the U20 400 m, 800 m and 400 m hurdles and 400 m relay and competed in New Zealand, China and Japan.  More recently she has turned to netball and plays for state country netball champion team Western Border and has recently been chosen as a member of the team which will travel to Springvale, Victoria in September to play in the Interstate Invitation Carnival.
