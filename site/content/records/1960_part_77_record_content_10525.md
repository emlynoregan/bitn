Attempted Robbery.  Following the attempt to blow the strong room at Bence’s Ltd on 14 March, two men were charged in Burra Police Court on Friday and pleaded guilty to breaking and entering with intent.  They were Charles Francis Cefie and Maxwell George Christie.  They were remanded in custody for sentencing at the Supreme Court in Adelaide.
