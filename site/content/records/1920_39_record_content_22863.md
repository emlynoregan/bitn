Trix Pearce Memorial Service.  A special musical service was held in Kooringa Methodist Church arranged by G.E. Dane.  The church was crowded for the occasion and included people from outlying parts of the district and many returned soldiers.  The choir of 38 voices was led by G.E. Dane.
