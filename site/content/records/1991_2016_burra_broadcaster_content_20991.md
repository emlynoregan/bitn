Boob’s Alive is the name of the celebration starting at 7 p.m. at Booborowie to welcome in the new millennium.  There will be food, drink and entertainment at the Booborowie Oval.  Entertainment will be by Danny Cooper & DJ.  There will be a barbecue and Thai food as well as other stalls and bar facilities.
