Tourism

Around 80 people associated with the Northern Automotive Restoration Club were noticed in the district over the weekend.  More than 30 passports per day were sold to visitors over the long weekend.  More than 800 people visited the Tourist Office.  A motor cycle club passed through the town on Monday.
