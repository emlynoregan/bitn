Chemmart Burra is donating to two local community groups each month, based on the votes of customers spending more than $20.  First prize $250 and 2nd $100.  In May the prizes went to Burra Squash Club and Burra Early Learning Centre and other winners have been Burra Tennis Club, Burra Hockey Club and The Burra History Group.
