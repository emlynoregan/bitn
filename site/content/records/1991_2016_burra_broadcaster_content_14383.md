Dick Smith flew in for the launch of the project to restore the birthplace of Sir Hubert Wilkins, just north of the old Mt Bryan East School.  Also on hand were local farmer and Chairman of the Hallett Historical Group, John Honan and Councillor Laurie Sullivan.  They co-ordinated to launch the book Hallett, a History of Town & District 1977-1997.  Betty Dawson, a great niece of Sir Hubert spoke about the explorer.
