Burra’s Got Talent.  Burra Hospital is holding its own version of ‘Australia’s Got Talent’ at the Burra Town Hall on 30 March at 7 p.m.  Voting will be by gold coin donation after each performance.  All proceeds will go towards the purchase of new equipment for the hospital.
