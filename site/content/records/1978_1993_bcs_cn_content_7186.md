Burra Tourist Office

From 1 October 1982 the operation of the office was leased to O’Connor’s Tours.

Denny & Jenny O’Connor.

They operate the Office as a Tourist Information outlet and Burra Caravan Park Booking Office.

Their main source of revenue is profit from the sale of books, commission on the sale of National Trust Keys and commission on Caravan Park bookings.

Jenny O’Connor has a non paid position as Secretary to the Tourist Office Management Committee, Tourist Office Statistician, Controller of Stock and general liaison officer with interested bodies.

The Management Committee is the controlling body of Council under the Local Government Act and manages and controls the office in co-operation with the lessees and manages the promotion of Burra & District.  It also organises the printing and reprinting of books and brochures.
