Fire.  Children dropping a cigarette in dry grass on the bank of the Burra Creek started a fire on Saturday.  The fire quickly spread upslope and burnt a pile of creosote posts at the rear of Wesfarmers-Dalgety before a passing MFS truck extinguished it.
