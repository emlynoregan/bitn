Miss Gertrude M. Jordan of Ashford NSW wrote to ‘Mrs Maggs’.  She said she left Burra School in 1898 and worked her way through various teaching appointments in NSW to retire as a headmistress.  She is the only surviving member of the original Jordan Family of Burra.
