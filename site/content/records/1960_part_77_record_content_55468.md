Night Basketball held its Grand Final on 16 March.

Saints (Burra) 40 defeated Booborowie 17

Best & Fairest in the Grand Final was Miss J. Anderson.

Aberdeen Basketball Club held a wind-up tea at which President Mrs J. Broad introduced the Patroness Mrs D. Price who presented trophies.

Rovers Best & Fairest was Mrs Y. James.

Rebels Best & Fairest was Miss L. Jones.
