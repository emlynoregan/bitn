Kay Gare, aged 3, was rescued from a dam at ‘Glen View’ Hallett on 9 February, by her mother, Mrs Keith Gare.  She had wandered away from home at about 6.45 p.m. and hearing a cry, her mother found her floating in the dam, waded out up to her neck and managed the rescue.  Kay recovered after artificial respiration.
