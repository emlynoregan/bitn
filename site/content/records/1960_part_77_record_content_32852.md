Theft.  Jennison’s Tyre Service was broken into on the night of Monday 9 January and goods to a value in excess of $500 were stolen.  The rear door was forced.  Several of the items stolen were apparently later thrown from a car on the Morgan Road and found on Wednesday morning by Mr Alec Ford.
