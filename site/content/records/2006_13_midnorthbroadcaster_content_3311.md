Visitor Conference

On Wednesday more than 80 Information Officers and managers gathered ay Kapunda to focus for three days on what the Clare Valley has to offer visitors.  The plan was to put together a program to have the Clare Valley well marketed and promoted.  The group visited Kapunda, Eudunda, Burra, Mintaro, Clare, Blyth and Balaklava.  They had a dinner at Burra to the theme ‘SA Brilliant Blend’.  [Photo of the dinner in Burra Town Hall.]
