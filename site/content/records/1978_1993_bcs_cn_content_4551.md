13 August 1981

Obituary.  From the Advertiser 6 August 1981.

The leading SA violinist Daisy Kennedy has died in London aged 88.  She was born in Burra and showed exceptional talent as a child.  She later studied in Prague and became a leading performer in London.

[Daisy Fowler Kennedy born 16 January 1893 at Kooringa.  Daisy Kennedy was first married to the great pianist Benna Mosceiwitsh and then to John Drinkwater, the English poet, writer and playwright.]

Burra Golf Club.  Championships

A Grade	Stan Longford won his 27th club title.

B Grade	Alan Baulderstone

BCS.  The annual winter sports carnival was contested between BCS, Kapunda High School and Eudunda Area School on 4 August.  [Results are printed.]
