Burra Institute.  AGM elected President, P.J. Byrne; Vice-President, R.W. Nankivell and Secretary, J. Carpenter.

About 180 books were added in the year.  Subsidy for the year was £65-19-7, without which the library could not continue.  Subscribers are 89, having lost a few due to television.  Mrs Steer continues as librarian to do an enthusiastic job and thanks to Mrs K. Broad for repairing over 100 books.  The credit balance at 31 December was £3-1-0.
