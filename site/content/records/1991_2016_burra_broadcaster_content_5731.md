Burra Yacht Club is planning a regatta for 5 March.  There will be a procession from Barkers Homes at 10 a.m. to the lawns by the creek, followed by a full day of racing.  Regulations are strict: the yachts must have a maximum size of 2 m x 2 m, a minimum crew of 2 and the yacht must carry at least 1 m2 of sail and be powered by the crew.  [There will also be dragon boat races.]

There will also be on shore activities, stalls and activities for children.
