‘Johnny Green’ has been adopted as the symbol of the SA Mining Industry.  It will appear on SA Chamber of Mines stationery and literature.  The Chamber’s Journal entitled Johnny Green’s Journal for July carried a two page article on the legend of the miners’ mascot.
