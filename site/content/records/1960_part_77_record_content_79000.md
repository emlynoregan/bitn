ARHS will run a special steam train to Burra on Saturday.  Locals will be able to join the train for a run leaving at 1.15 p.m. to Hallett, arriving at 1.45 p.m.  Departing Hallett 2.14 p.m. and arriving at Burra at 2.58 p.m.  Tickets are $3 for adults and $1.50 for children.  The train will be hauled by Rx 207 built in 1913.
