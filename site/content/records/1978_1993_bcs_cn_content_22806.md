Darryl Venning has been appointed Property Salesman and Consultant from 12 July at Dalgety Bennetts Farmers, to coincide with Mr Kevin Kleinig’s retirement.  Darryl has more than ten years’ real estate experience in his twenty-one years of service to clients in the Clare, Jamestown and Burra Districts as a Stock and Station Agent.
