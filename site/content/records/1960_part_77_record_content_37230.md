A Guided Tour of Burra has been organised by the Rector and Wardens of St Mary’s on Sunday at 1.30 p.m. from the Parish Hall.  Locals are invited to join visitors to learn more about their town.  At 4.30 p.m. Mr Ian Auhl will show the coloured film made about Burra by Mr John Kingsford-Smith and Ampol for the National Trust.  Mr Auhl will also give a lecture.
