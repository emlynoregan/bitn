Burra Community School

Mr G. Searle SM opened the first assembly for the year and introduced the new staff: Principal, C. McCabe; SM, Brian Baker; Mr G. Bayly (Biology), Miss M. Beck (English), Mr R. Bednall (Physical Education), Miss M. Nahhay (Years I & II), Mr J. Hearse (Year VI), Mr E. Lane (Technical Studies), Mr Rick Pellew (Agricultural Science), Mr Wally Spehr (Science) and Ms Heather Thick (Commerce).

There are 13 students taking Matriculation and 42 students taking Agricultural Science.  Matriculation and Agricultural Science are being offered for the first time.
