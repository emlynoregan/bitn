15 February 1979

BCS held its Senior Swimming Carnival in perfect weather.  Draysey House won the shield.

Championship Winners:

U13 Girls	Elizabeth Robinson

U13 Boys	Tim Beinke

U14 Girls	Katrina Thomas

U14 Boys	Kym Polkinghorne

U15 Girls	Cheryl Hayes

U15 Boys	Gavin Wheeler

Open Girls	Lisa Smedley

Open Boys	David Nourse
