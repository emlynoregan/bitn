R. Byles of Samin Ltd, with the aid of slides, traced the development of mining from the fourteenth century to the present, to a capacity crowd.

Mr Ian Auhl showed the film of Burra made in 1915 by Mr Lester featuring Australia Day and Belgian Flag Day and a film made in Clare in 1928 as well as a Charlie Chaplin film of the same era, followed by a film of Burra made for the National Trust for TV.
