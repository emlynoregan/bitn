CWA members from Marrabel, Lyndoch and Mt Bryan met Mr S.J. Woollacott of the National Trust outside the Burra Museum on 27 May to have a good look at Burra’s past.  After the museum they visited the dugouts, Paxton Square, the Reformatory and St Mary’s Church.  Lunch was taken in the Burra CWA rooms.  In the afternoon they visited Samin for a look at the Mine and the processing plant with Mr G. O’Connor.
