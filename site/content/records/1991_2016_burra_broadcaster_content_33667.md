Burra Hospital Auxiliary has donated to the hospital a monitor/defibrillator valued at more than $13,000 and two electric beds.  The Bon Accord Hotel has donated a further $500 towards another electric bed.  During the year there have been donations for two more electric beds worth $4,156.  Holder Home has now been completed and will open on 23 May.
