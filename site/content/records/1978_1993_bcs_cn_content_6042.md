Burra & District Hospital Auxiliary held its AGM 4 June and elected: President, Dianne Beinke; Vice-Presidents, Margaret Alderson & Celine Stockman; Secretary, Lorraine Taylor; Assistant Secretary, Lesley Phillips and Treasurer, Marlene Wooton.

The nest project is for a foetal monitor, which doctors have asked the Auxiliary to buy.  In the past 12 months the Auxiliary has added extras worth $4,386 to the Hospital.
