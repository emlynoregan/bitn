Heidi Crick from Burra and Andrew Walter from Melrose were joint winners of the Rural Ambassador Award at the Clare Showgrounds on Saturday.  They will go on to represent the Northern Agricultural shows Assoc. at the state finals in September.  Heidi is Burra Show Inc. secretary.  [Photo.]
