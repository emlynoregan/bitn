Burra Hospital.  On the eastern side of the Burra Hospital foundation work is progressing on the new maternity wing.  When completed this will make a 40-bed hospital all on one floor level.  On the western side of the ‘General’ front verandah a sunroom is to be erected for the benefit of patients.  Following the resignation of Matron L. Fyfe, who had held the position for 13 years, Sister L.A. Parker has been appointed Matron from 7 December.  She comes from Katamatite and has been at Burra Hospital for 12 months.
