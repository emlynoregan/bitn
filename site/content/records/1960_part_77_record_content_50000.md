E.C. Allen writes with the response from the SAR to his request for better train times.  The SAR offers the possibility of departing Adelaide 5.25 p.m. Monday & Thursday and 6.35 p.m. Friday to Sunday OR departing Adelaide 6.35 p.m. daily.  He seeks feedback before responding to the offer.
