Burra Football Club held its AGM last Friday in the Town Hall Supper Room.  President John Merkel welcomed 30 members. Life Certificates were presented to Messrs L.A. (Bert) Beinke, K.J. Murphy and D. Edwards.  Elected were: Patron, Reg. Warnes; President, John Merkel; Vice-President, L.A. Beinke and Secretary & Treasurer, D. Zanker.  It was decided Burra could form a ‘B’ team or a Colts team, but no final decision was to be made before the Association meeting.
