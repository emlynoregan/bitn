Burra Rifle Club.  A match was fired on Saturday over 200, 500 & 600 yards.  99 was scored by A.L. Kellock, A.P. Harris, L.W. Gare, H.L. Riggs, R. Kellaway & A. Ford, though only A.L. Kellock did so from scratch.
