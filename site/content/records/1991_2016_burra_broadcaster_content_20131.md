Burra & Districts Combined Probus Club and some National Trust members travelled by bus on a Flinders Ranges trip on 18-19 September.  They left Burra at 10 a.m. on Saturday and had lunch at Melrose at Bluey Blundstone’s Blacksmith’s Shop.  At Pt Augusta they visited the Wadlata Interpretive Centre and on Sunday morning they caught the steam train at Quorn for the return trip to Woolshed Flat.
