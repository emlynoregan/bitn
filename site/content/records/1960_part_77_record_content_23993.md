Swimming.  Twelve members of the Burra & District Club took part in the age group Championships at the Olympic Pool in Adelaide and gained great experience.  Peter Broderick was in the final for 8 and Under 55 yards freestyle and Rosemary Ryan was 3rd in the 12 and Under 3 m diving.
