Burra Darts Club.  At the Aberdeen v. Kooringa match on 8 April Aberdeen was successful in all games, winning the teams’ match 501 up!  Most bullseyes were thrown by Bob Elliott.  Mrs V. Elliott was the most successful lady and Stan Goul won the individual score contest.
