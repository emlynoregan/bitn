F. Heinrich left Burra on Thursday on a ‘Richards’ 2 h.p. motor for Wonna.  Though the roads were bad he got there without mishap.  On the return trip he brought ‘Jack Kelly’ with him – a combined weight of 22 stone and he covered the 30 miles in 1¼ hours.
