Rail Services to Burra have been reduced from Sunday 1 February.

The train leaving Adelaide will now depart at 5 p.m. and reach Burra at 7.46 p.m. – 40 minutes earlier.

The services cancelled are:

From Adelaide	Tuesday evening	8.36 p.m. in Burra

Wednesday morning	11.40 a.m. in Burra

Friday morning	11.40 a.m. in Burra

Saturday evening	8.36 p.m. in Burra

From Burra	Monday evening	5.25 p.m. from Burra

Tuesday morning	7.23 a.m. from Burra

Wednesday afternoon	3.07 from Burra

18 services remain – nine in each direction.

Stateliner Bus Service will run Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday evening and Sunday from Peterborough to Adelaide and Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday from Adelaide to Peterborough.

Details of agents and departure times will be published shortly.
