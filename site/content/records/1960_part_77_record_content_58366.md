Postal Services

Changes to the times for clearances of letters reveals the location of letter receivers at this time.

Burra North boxes previously cleared at 4.30 p.m. Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday will have this clearance deleted and in future be cleared 2.15 p.m. and at 4.30 p.m. Thursday.

Railway Terrace

Ludgvan St

In Burra clearances will be at 2.30 p.m. and at 4.45 p.m. on Thursday.

Kingston St

Chapel Street
