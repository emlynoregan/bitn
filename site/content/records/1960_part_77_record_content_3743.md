Burra Rural Youth & St Joseph’s Church will give a farewell on 27 September in the RSL Hall to Pauline Williams and the J. O’Sullivan Family.  Pauline has worked at the Burra Record for six years and will leave to take up nursing at Mr Gambier early in October.  Mr & Mrs J. O’Sullivan will leave the district to take up land in WA.
