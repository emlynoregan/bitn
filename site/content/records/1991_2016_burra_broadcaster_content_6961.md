This was founded in Burra 29 September 1865.  Meetings were first held in the old Baptist Church [in Thames Street], but moved after a few years to the Lodge Room at the Burra Town Hall.  In 1876 Brother C.J. Pearce was elected secretary, a position he held for a period of 63 years.
