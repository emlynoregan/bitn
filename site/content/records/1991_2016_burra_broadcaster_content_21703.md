The Annual Twilight Jazz Affair was held at Petherton House, Hallett, on Saturday night in perfect weather.  The Sir Hubert Wilkins Memorial Committee organised well known Adelaide trad. Jazz line-up, Royal Garden Jazz Band led by Peter Hooper, and Susan and the Marmalade Quartet contributed their flavour of cabaret jazz.  Cabra College Swing Band and Choir showed an array of youthful talent.  In all some $5,500 was raised for the Sir Hubert Wilkins rebuilding project.
