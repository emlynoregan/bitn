Obituary.  Well known Farrell Flat grazier Mr Douglas McCloud Short died at Burra 23 July.  He was born at Deniliquin NSW in 1890 and came to SA with his parents as a young boy and attended Morphett Vale School.  His father has taken up farming at Penfield and later on property now the Parafield Aerodrome.  As a young man he carted hay by horse wagon to chaff mills at Enfield and Port Adelaide.  He was a keen footballer; winning medals at Salisbury.  In 1915 he married Dulcie Neal of Salisbury and in the 1920s they came to Farrell Flat where he was a farmer and later a sheep dealer.  He is survived by his wife, two sons and three daughters: Jack & Ken (Farrell Flat), Melva Mrs Berryman (Adelaide), Nancy Mrs L. Simpson (Farrell Flat) and Joan Mrs K. Cooper (Farrell Flat).
