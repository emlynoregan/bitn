Burra Trading Hours have been extended to 9 p.m. on 24 December when the usual Christmas activities will occur in Market Square.  Late night shopping in Booborowie will be on Friday 20 December.  Burra Traders have organised a free merry-go-round near the Burra Hotel for Tuesday afternoon and evening.

There will be a free picture show in the Town Hall, of cartoons etc. timed to finish about 8 p.m.

Father Christmas arrives at 8.30 p.m.
