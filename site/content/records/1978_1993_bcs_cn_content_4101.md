The Annual Mission Festival of the Clare-Blyth Lutheran Parish was hosted by Burra this year on 3 May.  Approximately 200 people attended the service in the Burra Town Hall.  The Light United Band accompanied the singing.  Preacher for the day was Rev. R.A. Weier of Booleroo Centre.
