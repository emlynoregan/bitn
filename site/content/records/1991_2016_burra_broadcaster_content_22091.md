‘All That Jazz’

The People’s Choice awards have now been finalised.  In the open section there was a three way tie between Evan Hawke’s ‘Rust in Peace’, Helen Jensen’s ‘The Morning After’ and Valerie Veitch’s ‘Duet’. 

The Students’ Choice gave first place to Julianne Pulford’s ‘Jazz Band Junkies’ followed by Evan Hawke’s ‘Rust in Peace’ with Valerie Veitch’s ‘Duet’ in third place.
