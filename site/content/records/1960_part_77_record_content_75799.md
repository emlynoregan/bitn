Obituary.  Mrs Tregilgas of Malvern died in the past week.  She was the mother of Mrs Clarrie Hore of Burra North and widow of the Rev. Eric Tregilgas.

[Born Muriel Marston 21 June 1890 at Mintaro.  Married Joseph Eric Everleigh Tregilgas as Muriel Lillian Marston in 1918 in Adelaide.]
