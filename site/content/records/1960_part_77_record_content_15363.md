Tenders are called for the erection of a new kitchen and general repairs to St Mary’s Church Hall, Burra.

Allan Day was farewelled by over 40 players and supporters of the Burra Football and Footballers’ Cricket Club at the Golf Clubhouse on Saturday night.  He was presented with a travelling rug.

Eaglehawks have invaded Mt Bryan East and are taking young lambs.  Mr George Dare has declared war and has quite a show of dead eagles on his fences.  Mr Brian Riggs saw ten eaglehawks dining of one of his ewes’ new born lambs yesterday.
