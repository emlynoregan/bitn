Burra Hospital Ladies’ Auxiliary Annual Report

Successful fundraising events included:

Catering for Jamestown Drama Group in June

Sausage sizzle for the great SA Bike Ride in September.

Melbourne Cup Luncheon, which raised $1,928.15.

A series of guest speakers was appreciated.

$350 was donated to aid the Diversional Therapy Program.
