Mrs Quinn Sen. Turned 90 on 25 July.

Tony Fillmore, who did his printing apprenticeship at Burra Record, visited Burra recently with his daughter Jessica, en route to their Tennant Creek home.

Mavis & Ken Short of Burra celebrated their Golden Wedding last Saturday, 3 August.

BCS.  New staff this term:

Allison Smith

Lyn Bradley

Sue-Ann de Vries

John Hawke.

Birth.  To Darren & Cher Male (nee Fahey) of Roxby Downs on 23 July, a daughter, Zoey Kate.

Obituary.  Merv Walker died at Burra Hospital on 31 July aged 90.

[Lindsay Mervyn Walker born 13 May 1906 at Aberdeen.  Buried at Burra 5 August 1996.]

Obituary.  Eileen Wright, mother of Barry Wright of Burra, died at Burra Hospital last Saturday, 3 August.

[Eileen May Wright buried at Burra 7 August 1996 aged 89.]
