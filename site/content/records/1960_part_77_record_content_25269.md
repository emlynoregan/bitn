Break-ins.  For the second time in five weeks the shop of Mr K.J. Murphy has been broken into.  Fancy goods, cigarettes, tobacco and razor blades have been stolen.  The theft took place on Saturday night.  Entry was obtained to Burra Meat Stores as well, but nothing was stolen.  The value of cigarettes and tobacco taken was £120-£130 and losses in all were c. £180.
