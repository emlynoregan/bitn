Burra M & BHA held its AGM on 4 July.

Attendance at the clinic is up, but membership remains low.  Elected: President, Mrs Pitcher; Secretary, Mrs Broderick and Treasurer, Mrs Spackman.  The Secretary presented the 3rd annual report.  The CWA were thanked for the use of their rooms.
