The North Eastern Hogget Competition was decided at Windy Brae, the property of J.T. & M.E. Drew of Booborowie on Wednesday 12 July.  The weather was ideal.  There were 8 pens, each of 25 hoggets.  First place went to W.G. Miller & Sons of Hallett.  Second place went to A.D. & C.A. McDonald of Leighton.
