St Mary’s.  A meeting in the vestry on Tuesday 27 Oct. saw a presentation to Rev. J. Stuart Wayland.  Dr Sangster made the presentation of a purse of 35 sovereigns.  E.C. Lockyer & T. Edwards also spoke.
