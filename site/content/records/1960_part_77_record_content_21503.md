Burra Small Bore Club held its AGM last Wednesday night.  The new season begins on 9 September.  Elected were: Captain, W. Corner; Vice-Captain, K. Spackman; Secretary, M.D .Thomas and Treasurer, R.E. Angel.

Adelaide Wool Sales last Wednesday and Thursday saw top district price go to R. Warnes & Sons of Woolgangi at 78d.  [Other local district prices are printed.]
