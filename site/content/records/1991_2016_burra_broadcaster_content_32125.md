McCafferty’s Greyhound Bus Service between Sydney and Adelaide via Broken hill will cease in late October 2003.  It currently runs through Burra, Hallett, Whyte Yarcowie and Terowie three days a week.  Fifty people have used the service to or from Burra between 1 January and 31 July 2003.
