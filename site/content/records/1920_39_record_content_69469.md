Redruth Methodist Circuit Quarterly Meeting on 8 January.  Mr F. Hansen continues to fill the extraordinary vacancy created by the death of Rev. A. Dyer, assisted by Rev. Tilbrook of Kooringa.  Membership is 200.  The Harvest Festivals were set for Ironmine on 6 March, Hanson 13 February and Redruth 13 March.  There was a small deficit for the quarter.
