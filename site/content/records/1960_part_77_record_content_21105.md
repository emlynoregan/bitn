Cricket.  A meeting at Willogoleche on Wednesday night of delegates of five clubs of the Mokota Association decided to form a Junior Association.  The clubs represented were Hallett, Booborowie, Spalding, Burra and Leighton.  Matches will be played in conjunction with ‘A’ Grade teams and the rules of the Senior Association will apply.  For the new Association: Patron, Michael Hawker; President, M. Carmody; Vice-President, H. Black and Secretary, J. Costello.  Juniors must be under 15 years of age on 1 September to be eligible to play.  The minimum age is at the discretion of the club.  Burra will field two teams to be known as Burra Footballers and Koonaburra.
