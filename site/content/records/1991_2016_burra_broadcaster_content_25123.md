Obituary.  Stuart George Thomas died at Pt Lincoln Hospital 15 April aged 75.  He was formerly of Clare, Hilltown, Cowell and Burra.  He was the son of Ralph & Tryphosa Thomas and husband of Pauline.  Funeral service at Burra Cemetery 20 April 10.30 a.m.  [Born 1 March 1926 at Kooringa.]
