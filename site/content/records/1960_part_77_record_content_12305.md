Burra Rural Youth Ball was held on Friday 20 July in the Town Hall.  Six debutantes were presented to Mr C.R. Kelly MHR and Mrs Kelly:

Miss Raelene Hood with Mr Peter Henderson

Miss Elaine Phillips with Mr Dean Pearce

Miss Elaine White with Mr Roger Humphrys

Miss Raelene Nankivell with Mr Dean Billing

Miss Marie Nourse with Mr Peter Bryant

Miss Barbara Irlam with Mr David Stockman
