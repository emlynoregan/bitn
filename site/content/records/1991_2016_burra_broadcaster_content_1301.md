Long involvement in many local organisations.

Particular involvement with Rural Youth, Burra Community Sports Club (being a foundation member & President), BCS, Burra Show Society (member for 34 years and sometime President), Burra Football Club and Farrell Flat Football Club.

Involved in Burra Picnic Race Club since inception.

Councillor since 1985, Deputy Chairman for 3 years and Chairman for the past 3 years.

Member of the Mid North Development Board to encourage employment and development in the area.

Member of Burra Bowling Club.
