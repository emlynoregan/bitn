R. Sandercock was given a farewell by members of the Burra Traders’ Association at the Burra Hotel on Wednesday afternoon.  Mr E.T. Baulderstone led the well wishers.  Mr Sandercock has been Secretary of the Association for four years.  A presentation was made and Mr Burnie Biggs was welcomed to the town and Association.
