Easter Bowls at Burra was a big tournament when 22 teams turned out and was held in conjunction with the Mid North Games, with gold, silver and bronze medals ‘up for grabs’.

The gold went to Robertstown 1, the silver to Morgan and the bronze to Balaklava.
