Notice.  Burra Town Council

Pursuant to Section 139 of the Local Government Act 1934-62, the Council has appointed C.H. Cummins to act as Councillor for West Ward to 30 June 1966.

[When no nominations had been received for the vacancy when the period expired on 4 December, Council acted on 21 December to co-opt C.H. Cummins as Councillor for West Ward.]
