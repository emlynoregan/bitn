Nathaniel O’Brien (15) of Burra won every category he entered at the SA Country Music Awards at Berri on 11 June.

Nathaniel had wins in the song-writer category, Gospel Senior, 16 & Under, Duo (with Emma-Jane Montgomery), Australian Bush Ballad and Country Rock categories and was runner-up in the Frank Harding Memorial Award of Excellence.  The event was well attended with fans from across Australia and New Zealand and over 40 contestants.
