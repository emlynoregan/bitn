Obituary.  The late Mrs Mabel Gill of Spalding died at Jamestown Hospital on 1 November.  She was born Mabel Elliott at Wilmington in 1888 and was educated first at Wilmington and then moved to Jamestown to complete her education.  At a young age she married Percy Gill and she lived at Spalding for over 60 years.  At first they attended the Presbyterian Church, but when it was demolished she joined the Methodist Guild.  She was also a member of the Spalding CWA and Red Cross.  Her husband predeceased her.  [Born Mabel Maud Elliott 29 March 1888 Wilmington: died Mabel Maude Gill 1 November 1968 Jamestown, residence Spalding.]
