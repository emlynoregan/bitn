Aberdeen Football Club

Trevor Wall, on behalf of his family, presented a shield for the best and fairest junior in memory of Robert Wall.

A clock in the club rooms was also unveiled by Bruce Stockman, Vice-President of the Mid North League and a Past President of the club.  It is in memory of Robert Wall and Phillip Elliott, both of whom played for the club.
