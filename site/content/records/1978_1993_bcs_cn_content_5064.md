Following the deaths of several BCS animals samples were sent to the Institute of Medical and Veterinary Science in Adelaide for analysis.  The normal copper level in the liver of a sheep is 0.5 to 9.4 units.  A merino ewe that died of pregnancy toxaemia had a reading of 15.1.  A merino hogget had one of 47.16.
