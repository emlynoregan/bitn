Mr & Mrs George Goodridge of Mt Bryan celebrated their Golden Wedding on 1 April with a gathering at Mt Bryan Hall in the CWA Room.  About 120 people sat down to tea in the supper room.  Mrs Goodridge had completed 53 years of faithful service to the Methodist Sunday School, first at the Old Church and later at the new one.  [A photograph of the couple is printed.]
