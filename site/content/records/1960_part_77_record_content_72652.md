Burra Jubilee Benevolent Society held its AGM on 28 August.  Rev. Morley was welcomed as a member.  Rev. Clarrie Hore was elected Chairman with Mr Atkins as Treasurer and Mrs H. Jennison as Secretary.  The society received a substantial amount from the estate of the late Mr John Barker.
