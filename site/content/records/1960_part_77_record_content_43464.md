Accident.  At about 11.30 p.m. on Friday a semi-trailer owned by S.E. Schulz of Renmark and driven by Peter E. Size of Renmark overturned on the Burra North-Morgan Road near Basin Farm.  It was taking 240 wethers for Elders-GM from Peterborough to Loxton.  Attending police worked till 3.30 a.m. freeing sheep from the bottom tray.  70 head of stock were killed or had to be destroyed.  The driver was kept in Burra Hospital over night for observation and was severely bruised.  Another truck picked up the sheep on Saturday morning and the District Council plant pulled the vehicle back onto its wheels.  Damage is estimated at $3,000.
