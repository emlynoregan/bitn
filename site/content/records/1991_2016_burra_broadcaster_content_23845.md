Burra Regional Art Gallery

In the recent Art of the Flower Exhibition Helen Jensen won the People’s Choice prize.

The new exhibition at the gallery is a collection of paintings, all winners of the Belalie Acquisition Prize – including that of Glenys Christopher, winner in 2000.  There is a special Christmas exhibition in the Bence Room.
