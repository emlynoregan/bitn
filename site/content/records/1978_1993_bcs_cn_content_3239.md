‘Belle of Burra Burra’ had an orchestra that included six students from BCS.

[Photo of five of them: Peter Davies, Philip Stolte & Tim Beinke (trumpets), Kevin Miller (saxophone), Heidi Queissner (clarinet).  Stephen Hoskins (recorder) was absent.

Others in the orchestra were Mrs Olive Oats (piano), David Woods (flute) and Brian Polkinghorne (drums).
