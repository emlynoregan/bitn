SA Track & Field Championships

Petrea Piggott won two gold medals: U16 300 m (a State residential record) & U16 200 m.

Adam Day got silvers in the U16 Javelin and the 300 m.

Both will now contest National Titles.

Rosie Lloyd, a former BCS student, won the U20 400 m hurdles and gained silver medals in the U20 800 m and the open 400 m hurdles.
