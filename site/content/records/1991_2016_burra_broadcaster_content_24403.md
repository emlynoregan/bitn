Burra Accommodation Houses had an Open House on 4 February.  Ten venues participated.  Not many people took advantage of this opportunity to inspect the accommodation, but those who did thoroughly enjoyed themselves.  It is estimated 20,000 people per year stay a night in Burra.  Participants were:

Paxton Square Cottages

Burra Motor Inn

Ryan’s Deer Farm

Burra View House

Brewery Cottage

Kookaburra Farm Stay

Bon Accord Cottage

Lavender Cottage

Somerset House
