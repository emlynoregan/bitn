Paper Bag Theatre

Karel & Eva Rehorek have a wealth of experience and have developed the novel approach of ‘Paper Bag Theatre’.  They will visit Burra on 1 & 2 November and will present:

Helen of Troy for Years 8 & 9

Jonah and the Whale for the Upper Primary School

Boodoolenek and the Foxes for the Junior Primary classes.
