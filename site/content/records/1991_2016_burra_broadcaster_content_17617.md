Willogoleche was auctioned at the Burra Sports Complex on 4 November.

Lot 1 comprising all the improvements is priced at $1.1 million and three parties are negotiating over that.

Lot 2 comprising 292.74 ha went to D.C. Gebhardt & Sons, Mr Bill Gebhardt of Mt Cone Stud for $425,000.

Lot 3 sold for $410,000 with the arable land going to Kym & Lyn Riggs and Rob Ashby of old Ashrose Stud purchased the strong hills grazing land.

Willogoleche was founded in 1842 when John Hallett took up the holding.
