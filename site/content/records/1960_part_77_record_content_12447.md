St Joseph’s Fete in the Burra RSL Hall on Friday drew a larger than usual crowd and took c. £170.  Mrs E.T. Baulderstone opened the event.  [Details in ¾ column.]

Burra Swimming Club held a successful concert in the Town Hall on Friday and raised £42.

Booborowie Amateur Hour raised £178 for the new football pavilion.

Burra Small Bore Club needs more members in attendance.  Only four attended on Saturday for the 25 yards shoot when A.G. Heinrich was the best shot.

St Johns-Aberdeen Football Club Ball was held recently.

There is a photo of the Mayor & Mayoress congratulating the winners of the Waltzing Competition, Mr & Mrs Ron Kelly of Burra North, with Mr R.G. Bernhardt, one of the judges.
