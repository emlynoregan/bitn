The Regional Council of Goyder is seeking to have the amount of the fines applicable for late payment of rates increased.  At present they can impose a fine of 2% on outstanding rates and then 0.9375% monthly.  The value of arrears is larger in 2008 than for 2007.  At 31 December 2008 the arrears were $838,873.06
