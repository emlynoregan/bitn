BCS.  Athletics: Coca Cola Games in Adelaide 27 & 28 October.  Three students represented BCS.

Medals won:

Cheryl Day: Silver in the 400 m and bronze in the U17 200 m.

Sheridan Coad and Danny Burton acquitted themselves creditably.

Burra School & Community News will hold a meeting at 8 p.m. in the BCS staffroom for the purpose of changing its constitution.
