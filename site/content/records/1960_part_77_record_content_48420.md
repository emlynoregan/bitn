Burra Golf Club held its AGM in the Clubhouse 5 November.

Only 14 members attended.  President John M. Gordon reported on a successful season.  Club Championship was won by Stan Longford.  Elected: President, J.M. Gordon; Secretary, H. Burnett and Treasurer, T. Jackson.
