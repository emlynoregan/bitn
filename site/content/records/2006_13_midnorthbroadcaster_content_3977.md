Burra’s OSHC has recently upgraded its equipment with new sand toys, games, puzzles and sports equipment.  Director Jodie Quinn said it had taken 2½ years to update services and provide new equipment.  Approximately $1,500 had been spent on new toys and equipment and a further $500 on office supplies and craft gear.
