Accident.  A semi-trailer driven by Mr Les Robinson overturned at Hanson at the Farrell Flat Road junction in the early hours of Friday morning, while bound for Broken Hill.  The vehicle continued into the yard of one of the railway cottages, struck a heap of rubbish and overturned onto the rear of the building.  The driver was unhurt and damage to the building was slight.
