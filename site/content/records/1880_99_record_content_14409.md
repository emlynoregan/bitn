Redruth Court.

J. Shortridge fined 14/- for driving loose cattle in Market Square.

T.H. Woollacott fined 4/- for driving 4 horses in Young St within prohibited hours.

W.A. Rabbich fined 10/- for two stray cows in Aberdeen.
