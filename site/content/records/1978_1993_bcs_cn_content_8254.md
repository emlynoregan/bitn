Burra Burra DC.  The following members retire on Saturday 1 October due to the effluxion of time.

Booborowie Ward	J.S. Hawker

Hanson Ward	J.A. Jacka

Mt Bryan Ward	A.J. Gebhardt

Notice is given that by the Proclamation of the Governor in the Government Gazette of 7 October 1982:

Baldina and Mongolata Wards will be united to form the Eastern Ward.

The Eastern Ward shall be represented by one Councillor.

The number of Councillors for the Burra Town Ward shall increase from three to four.

Consequently the following members of Council will retire from their respective offices:

Baldina Ward	A.A. Barker

Mongolata Ward	J.P. Oates

Burra Town Ward	P.M. Reed

H.R. Stockman

D.R. Woolley

Nominations are called up to noon 2 September 1983 with elections on 1 October 1983 if required.

Burra Burra DC.  The present Council, while acknowledging it is the decision of the Council to be elected in October, generally favours a change to night sittings.
