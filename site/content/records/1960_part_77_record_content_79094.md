Rx 207 should arrive at Burra at 12.14 p.m. and proceed to the golf clubhouse, arriving there at 12.16 p.m.  Passengers will disembark for lunch.  The train will then return to the station, ready for the 1.15 p.m. departure for Hallett.  After returning from Hallett the engine will be turned in readiness for its departure for Adelaide.
