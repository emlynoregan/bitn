MNRDB.  The CEO Craig Wilson has resigned, to be replaced by Geoffrey Gray from Carisbrook in Central Victoria.

SA Pop Vocal Contest.  This is run by promoter and vocal coach David McMenamin and finalists are competing for a recording package with Access Studio.  A group of Burra singers is in the finals: Brooke Starr, Tracey Rains, Jess Reid, Nathaniel O’Brien, Kiya Norris and Ruby Neiman.
