Rhiannon Phillips (21) is Burra Ward Young Citizen of the Year.  She came to Burra with her family-owned and managed business, SA Underwater Training Centre.  This is the only provider of approved occupation diver training in SA.  She is the only female diver trainer in Australia and runs several different diver training courses throughout the year.  She is a dedicated volunteer with Sa Ambulance, having joined in 2009.  She has a Certificate II in Emergency Services First Response and is currently training to upgrade to Certificate IV in Health Care (Ambulance).  She also washes the ambulance after call outs.  She has been a netball coach for the BBH Club and assisted in their fundraising.  She coached the 15 & under netball team in 2011 and played, umpired and trained the senior colts.  She has been a valuable mentor to the clubs with her selfless work ethic and offering encouragement to younger players.  She is also handy with a tennis racquet.  [Photo]
