Burra Burra Hospital.  For family or friends of patients at the hospital overnight accommodation is available at the Nurses’ Home or the Director of Nurses Residence if they are unable to obtain other accommodation in the town.  A minimal fee of $8 meal costs will be charged.  It is anticipated that initially this service will be available in emergency or hardship cases only.
