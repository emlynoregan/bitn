Burra RSL Women’s Auxiliary gathered last Tuesday to celebrate the group’s 60th anniversary.  The first meeting was held on Wednesday 27 March 1946 in Pearce’s Buildings.  Current President is Mary Anderson and Secretary-Treasurer is Betty Edwards.
