Obituary.  Robert (Bob) Finch died suddenly 20 April at his residence in Hindmarsh aged 64.  He was the son of William E.J. Finch and brother to Will (deceased), Ethel, Albert (deceased), Lizzie, Lily, Dora and Jack.

[Robert Leonard Finch born 20 April 1896 Kooringa.]
