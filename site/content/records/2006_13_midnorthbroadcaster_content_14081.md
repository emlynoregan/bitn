Nathaniel O’Brien (16) has returned from the Tamworth Country Music Festival with a bag of awards and prizes.

He was named Junior Aristocrat Country Entertainer of the Year.

He also won the Overall at Manilla, after taking out five of the sections; the Mt Franklins competition and second in the junior male vocal, and first for the best Australian song in the Capital Country Music Associations (CCMA) competitions.
