Staff comprised Lloyd Draysey acting headmaster, Marjorie Koehne, Joyce Brooks, Linda Linke and Junior Teachers Max Allen and Bethel Smit.  The enrolment was 63.  Mr A.B. Riggs presented the school with a wall clock.  School students raised £26 for the School’s Patriotic Fund.
