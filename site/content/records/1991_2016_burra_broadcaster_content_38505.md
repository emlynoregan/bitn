KESAB Awards

Shirley Broad was given a special mention for her well-presented garden.

BCS was given a Commendation Plaque.

Burra Senior Citizens received a plaque honouring 25 years of labour.

BCS Choir began in Term 3 as an elective run by Judy Giles for years 4-5.  Their latest performance of ‘The Lion Sleeps Tonight’ was met with much applause at the school’s Presentation Night on 8 December.
