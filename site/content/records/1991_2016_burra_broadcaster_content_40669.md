More than 700 people attended the service to mark the 100th anniversary of the Gallipoli landing.  The Burra Sing Australia Choir performed songs along with Kerry Davy and students from the BCS Choir.  Rev. Darren Lovell officiated and gave the address.  Mr Trigg recited The Ode of Remembrance and afterwards tea and coffee were served in the RSL rooms.
