Goyder Council adopted business and market plans for Burra Town Hall and the Art Gallery at its meeting on 20 February.  This follows community consultation and workshops conducted by consultants Anderson Muller.

Stage 2 will include appointing a part-time precinct manager, upgrading the Town Hall and appointing an energetic committee to work with the manager.
