Burra & District Youth Centre

Some of the regular activities include the Youth Centre Rage with a regular DJ, open nights where youth provide their own music and activities, special Vidiot nights, discos, dinner parties and Year 7 nights are also planned.  A small group of youths make up the most of the management committee and have to deal with items like the constitution, insurance, building maintenance and managing the budget.

Gail Edwards, Coordinator.
