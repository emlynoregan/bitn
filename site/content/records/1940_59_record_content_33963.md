Charlie Noyce reports his trip to the UK.  He went to the UK on the Stirling Castle 12 October 1946 and returned on the Stratheden 1 November 1947.  On the return ship were the Governor & Lady Norrie, three Indian Princes and a Princess and Gladys Moncrieff.  He reports cold weather in the UK and general food shortages.  Bread was rationed.
