Weather.  Thunderstorms have delivered very variable totals across the district in the period since 20 April.  To the east Balah scored 4 points, but Oakleigh got 70.  Many places received over half an inch.  Kooringa got 25 points in ten minutes at 4 p.m. on Sunday, deluging the large crowd at the Anzac Service.  At Aberdeen only 4 points fell.
