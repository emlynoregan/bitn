Accident.  A male truck driver, believed to be from Queensland, was hit by a vehicle as he stood by his truck on the Burra Bypass Road on Sunday night.  He sustained leg injuries and was airlifted to Adelaide for treatment.  The driver of the other vehicle did not stop, but a vehicle was later seized in relation to the incident.
