SA Country Racing Clubs Assoc. was formed at a meeting in Adelaide on 11 August.  The object is to promote and improve country racing in SA.

President, Gerald Gask, President of Northern Racing Clubs’ Association & Chairman of Burra Racing Club.

Vice-President, Mr Ray Hall of the Upper Northern District Racing Assoc.  & Chairman of Iron Knob Racing Club.

Secretary/Treasurer, Mr Alan Durward, Secretary of the Southern Racing Clubs’ Assoc. & Secretary of Strathalbyn Racing Club.

90, 34, 29 Aug. 1961, page 2
