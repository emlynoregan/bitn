Mid-North High School Sports were held at the Burra Racecourse on Friday 9 May.  Balaklava, Clare, Kapunda, Riverton & Burra took part.  The weather was fairly good.  Aggregate results: Clare 114⅓, Balaklava 98, Burra 90⅓, Kapunda 48 and Riverton 45⅓.

Results are printed on page 5 (numbered 7).
