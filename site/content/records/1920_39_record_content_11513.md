Undesirable Visitors.  Lancelot Maxwell Tumes (21) was fined £2 for indecent language and £5 for resisting police.  He has previous convictions for larceny.

Harold Black (20) was charged with being a rogue and vagabond and being unlawfully on the premises of R.W. Motherall.  He was sentenced to 3 months.  [Details in ½ column.]
