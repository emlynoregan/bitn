Football.  North Eastern League Grand Final gate takings were $402.30.

Presentations made were:

Mail Medal 		Barry Eberhard

Burra Record Trophy	Don Villis (runner-up for the Mail Medal)

Fairest & Most Brilliant ‘B’ Grade	Allan Day

Fairest & Most Brilliant Colts	B. Victor

‘A’ Grade	Booborowie 9.5 defeated Burra 7.12

‘B’ Grade	Burra 8.5 defeated Clare North 3.6

Colts	Spalding 10.6 defeated Clare North 5.7
