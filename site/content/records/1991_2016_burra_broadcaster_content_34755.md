Burra Regional Art Gallery

The present exhibition is ‘Art of the flower and Garden [Delights]’.  More than 30 paintings were entered in the competition for the prize, which went to Manoora artist Subylla Tydeman.  The exhibition was opened by Kelly De Giglio, Country Arts representative from Pt Augusta.
