Water Restrictions.  Level Three Restrictions operate.  Times change with the end of daylight saving.

Even numbered houses can use sprinklers 6 a.m. to 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. to 8 p.m. on Saturdays and even numbered houses for similar hours on Sundays.  Sprinklers are banned on weekdays.  Hand-held trigger nozzles can be used 6 a.m. to 9 a.m. and 5 p.m.to midnight on any day and watering cans and buckets at any time.
