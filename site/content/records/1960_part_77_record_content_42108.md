Burra Football Club celebrated premierships in ‘A’ & ‘B’ Grades with a dinner dance on Friday night. Presentations were made:

Best & Fairest in ‘A’ Grade	Barry Eberhard

Best & Fairest in ‘B’ Grade	A. Ryan with Adrian Johnson as runner-up.

Barry Eberhard also won the Forard Medal for most Mail medal votes for a Burra player.

Kevin Lane received the A.L. Collins Shield as Captain of the ‘A’ Grade team and Graham Kellock received the ANZ Bank Shield as captain of the ‘B’ Grade team.
