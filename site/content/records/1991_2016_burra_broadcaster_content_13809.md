Alan Botting is developing an exciting range of local tours – half and full day – including Redbanks, Burra Creek Gorge, Eudunda & Robertstown, Dare’s Hill and Hallett, Princess Royal Mine, Collinsville Stud, Terowie & Peterborough, Copper Ridge Station, Morgan, Moonta, Clare & Mintaro, as well as local sites like the Deer Farm, Alpaca Farm and Mongolata Mine.  Greyhound Pioneer will offer one and two night stopovers in Burra for those en route to Sydney and Brisbane and 4 & 5 night Burra packages are being negotiated.
