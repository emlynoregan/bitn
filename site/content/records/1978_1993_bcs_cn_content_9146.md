The Courthouse Craft Shop (In the old Courthouse Hotel) stocks an attractive range of third world and local crafts suitable for gifts.  Run by Community Aid Abroad, profits go to aid projects overseas.  Last year $950 was sent to South India and Korea.  Projects in 1984:

Assistance to MV Fetukai inter-island transport between islands of Vanuatu.

Aid for the Ecumenical Committee in El Salvador, which supports refugees.

Chairperson is Debbie Brown.
