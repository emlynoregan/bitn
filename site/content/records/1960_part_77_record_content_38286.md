J.H. Phelan is ALP Candidate for Burra.  He is a 46-year-old shearer of Hallett.  He is President of the Hallett RSL, on the Hallett Institute Committee and a member of the Burra RSL Committee.  He is a former potato grower and farmer in the South East and was wounded in action in New Guinea in WW II.
