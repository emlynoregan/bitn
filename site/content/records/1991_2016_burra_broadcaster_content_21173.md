Burra Visitor Numbers calculated from statistics kept by the Visitor Centre were higher for the October-December quarter of 1999 than for the same quarter in 1998.  There were significant increases in visitors from Victoria, NSW and Queensland as well as from overseas.  Victoria provides the greatest number of interstate visitors.

Burra Probus Club went to Lobethal by coach on 14 December to see the Christmas lights.
