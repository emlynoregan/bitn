Country Athletic Championships at Berri over the Easter Weekend were dominated by Burra athletes in the senior section.

Graham Kessell won Male Outstanding Athlete Award.  He threw 62.35 m with the 700 gm javelin and 61.00 m with the 800 gm javelin, placing him among the best in Australia.

17 athletes from Burra competed and between them won 51 medals.
