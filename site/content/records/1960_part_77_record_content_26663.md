Burra Small Bore Club.  The Annual Inter-Club Shoot between Burra, Morgan, Clare and Peterborough was held at Burra on Sunday to decide the Williams Shield.  Ross Gilbert elected to shoot for Clare.

The order of the clubs was:

Morgan	2229.62

Clare	2193.44

Peterborough	2148.53

Burra	2092.24

On Tuesday night a 75 yard shoot was fired.  Ross Gilbert headed both the handicap and rifle results.
