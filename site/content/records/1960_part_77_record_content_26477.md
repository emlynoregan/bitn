The Burra Bowling Season was opened by President Jack Topsfield on Saturday.  He presented the plaque to Les Phillips as a member of the Burra Green Team, which won the Mid North Association pennant last season.  Dick Harris, as longest serving member, rolled the first jack and Charlie Dare put down the first bowl.
