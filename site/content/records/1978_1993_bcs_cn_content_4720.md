24 September 1981

The 1981 Burra Cycling Classic was contested last Saturday.  Mrs Shirley Thamm had the honour of starting the race on Saturday 19 September.  Among the 33 starters was her brother, Doug Holly, currently Australian Veterans Champion.  The winner was Des Mitchell riding from 60 minutes.  Doug holly clocked the fastest time in 3 hours 39 minutes 35 seconds.  [Photo.]
