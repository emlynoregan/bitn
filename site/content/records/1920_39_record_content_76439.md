Koo-owie Merino Stud dispersal sale on 19 September was very successful.  Buyers came from all over SA.  Rams sold to 41½ guineas.  Stud ewes of 1½ years sold to £3-4-0.  Aged ewes to 25/- and 542 lambs at 16/3 with 431 wether lambs at 13/-.  A total of 464 rams and 2,929 ewes were sold.
