Mr & Mrs Don Halliday will leave shortly to make their new home in Adelaide.  They will be greatly missed on the Memorial Oval Committee.   Mr Halliday has also been a member of the Burra Town Hall Picture Committee for many years and helped with door-keeping etc.  A presentation was made at the meeting of the Memorial Oval Committee on Monday night.
