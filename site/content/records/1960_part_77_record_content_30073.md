M & BHA held its AGM on 20 June and elected: President, Mrs Barry Riggs; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames Odgers & McGee; Secretary, Mrs M.A. Tiver; Assistant Secretary, Mrs P. Dignan; Treasurer, Mrs D. Bannister.  The guest speaker was Sister Moody who spoke on ‘The Penguin Club’.  Secretary, Mrs Jan Tiver, reported on a successful year with a monthly attendance of 30 babies at the clinic.  A second-hand clothing mart and street stall had raised $178 and the annual badge day raised $43, while a Devonshire tea afternoon in March raised $78.
