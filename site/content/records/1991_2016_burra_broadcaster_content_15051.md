Christmas in Burra.  Festivities in Market Square had the place abuzz with excitement.  The pageant was well supported and the Clare Concert Band was present, along with the school band and the Magic Cave.  Several hundred people enjoyed a barbecue put on by the Burra Traders’ Association.  [Photo.]
