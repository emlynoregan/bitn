Stillborn Baby Memorial

The Goyder Council has given permission for the installation of a memorial to the stillborn babies buries in unmarked graves in the Burra Cemetery.  [When installed it was located within the Mortuary Chapel erected in 1883 by E.W. Goss.  See further at Burra Broadcaster, No. 537 16 January 2002.]
