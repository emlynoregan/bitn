Burra Swimming Pool

Chris Reed is leading volunteers in fencing the pool and needs more people to assist, as water cannot be admitted until it is fenced.  Working bees will be held on the 4 & 5 February.  It has been decided to give the paving job to a contractor as it was too big for volunteers.
