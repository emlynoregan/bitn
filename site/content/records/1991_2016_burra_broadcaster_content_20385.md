Burra Hospital Activities Program celebrated its 1st anniversary on 26 October.  There was a tea party in the courtyard with Colin Lloyd supplying the music.  Anthea Lines has been activities co-ordinator.  The community has raised $1,349.94 in the 12 months for craft supplies, hiring videos, special occasion food and drinks, etc.
