Accident.  A young son of Mr A. Fuss of Aberdeen (aged 3½), narrowly escaped death on Monday morning in the workshop when a piece of 1½” x 9’ timber fell from above and hit his left foot.  One toe was severed and another had to be amputated.  He is getting on satisfactorily.
