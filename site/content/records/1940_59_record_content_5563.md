Mongolata Goldfield

Baldina Mongolata GM Syndicate for the fortnight to 19 April.

Farnden’s Hill.  Stoping backs off Riggs’ Open Cut.  Commenced stope from intermediate shaft: advanced 8 ft in values c. 12 dwt per ton on a leader 3 inches wide.  4½ tons of ore on hand.

Lean’s Shaft.  Sinking winze on underlie cut two small leaders ½ inch and 1 inch wide of up to 3 dwt per ton.  Advanced 8 ft to 19 ft.
