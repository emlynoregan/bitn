BCS Year 8 student Brody McPharlin has been awarded a three-year boarding scholarship at St Peter’s College in Adelaide.  He shifted to Burra 2½ years ago, living on Saffron Downs.  His interests include design and he has aspirations towards a future in architecture.  [Photo.]
