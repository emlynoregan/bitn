Conferences

There were several state gatherings in Burra for J150, including the Area Schools Principals’ Conference, an SES Conference in September, the Christian Women Communicating Conference in August and the SA Association of State School Organisations Conference in October.

A Quilting Exhibition titled Needles Through Time was a highlight of J150 Craft Weekend.

A wall hanging celebrating the sesquicentennial was presented to the Council.
