Early Closing of Liquor Bars.  An address by J.W. Evans, ex-President of the Licensed Victuallers’ Association of NSW was given at the Rotunda on Monday night.  He spoke in favour of keeping the closing time at 11 p.m.  He is reported in c. 1¼ columns.
