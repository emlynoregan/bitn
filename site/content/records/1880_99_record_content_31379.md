The Health Account revenue has been:

1886	£130-12-11

1887	£111-8-5

1888	£89-15-9

In 1886 the debit was £1-18-11 and now it is £82-13-8 and it was thus essential to increase the rate from 2d to 3d as well as to minimise expenditure.
