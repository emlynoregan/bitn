Burra High School.  Prefects for 1969 were inducted in a ceremony in the School Memorial Gardens before a crowd of over 150 parents.  House Captains were also inducted.

Prefects

Girls	Boys

Chrystine Steele (Head)	R. Przibilla (Head)

Lynette Griffin (Deputy)	N. Hyde (Deputy)

Jennifer Catt	D. Bogisch

Judith Heinrich	D. Dare

Angela Quinn	D. Francis

Cheryl Willis	P. Grubb

Mr J.H. Pash, Inspector of High Schools gave a brief address.

Seven Scholarships were won by Burra High School students in 1968:

Commonwealth Scholarships: with a living allowance of $200 plus a book allowance of $50 and up to $150 reimbursement of compulsory fees, per annum, tenable for two years.
