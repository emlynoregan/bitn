Burra Bowling Club.  President’s Report 1972-73

The club again had a successful year.  Unfortunate obituaries among members this year were Jack Mitchell, Joe Lloyd, Jack Allen and Bill Carpenter.  Max Jacka, Bert Wardle and Bill Byles all went to live in Adelaide.
