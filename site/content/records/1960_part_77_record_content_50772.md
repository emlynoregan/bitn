Burra Anzac Service was held at the Memorial in Market Square last Sunday.  The Pt Pirie Pipe Band led 20-30 ex-servicemen in the march.  Rev. Milton Edwards conducted the service.  Wreaths were laid by Ben Kotz for the RSL, B.A. Riggs for the District Council and Peter Williams for the Demobilised Soldiers Assoc.  A combined church service was held in the evening at Burra Methodist Church, conducted by Mr N. Baynes.
