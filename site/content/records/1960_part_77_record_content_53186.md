Aberdeen Tennis Club held its AGM, which was poorly attended as usual.  The bank balance was in credit $121.  Mr 7 Mrs S. Scroop resigned the joint Secretary-Treasurer position after thirty years.  President Robert Reed reported on a successful year in which the ‘A’ Grade White team and the Junior team won their respective premierships.  Number one court was re-laid by Mr I. Hirschausen during the year.  Elected were: President, R. Reed; Vice-Presidents, Messrs C. Lloyd & S. Scroop and secretary-Treasurer, M. & D. Lloyd.
