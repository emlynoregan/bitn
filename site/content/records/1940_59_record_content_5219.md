Mongolata Goldfield

Baldina Mongolata GM Syndicate for the fortnight to 8 March.

Farnden’s Hill.  Stoping backs in drive for Riggs’ Open Cut advanced to 47 ft.  Country very broken and timbering was required.  Values low; ½ ton of 5 dwt ore obtained.

East View. Driving west at the south end of the lease advanced 16 ft to 153 ft in quartzite with no leader.
