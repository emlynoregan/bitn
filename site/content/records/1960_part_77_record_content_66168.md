Rail Tours to Burra.  The first SAR package rail tour to Burra ran yesterday and will be available each Monday.  Passengers depart Adelaide 8.45 a.m. and arrive at Burra 11.44 a.m.  Bus proprietor Alex Smith then takes passengers on a town tour.  Departure for Adelaide is at 3.18 p.m.
