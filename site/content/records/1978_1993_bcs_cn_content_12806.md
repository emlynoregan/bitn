Adelaide Children’s Hospital Auxiliary held its AGM on 30 June in the Aberdeen Clubrooms.  Elected: Patroness, Miss Colleen Spackman; President, Mrs Reva Phillips; Vice-President, Mrs Irene Hirschausen; Secretary, Miss Pat Taylor and Assistant Secretary & Treasurer, Mrs Marg Neale.

In the past 12 months Burra & District had 11 children admitted and 74 outpatients at the ACH and the Burra Auxiliary had raised $1,420 for 1985-86 and so far for 1986-87 had sent down $500.

The Auxiliary currently has 22 members.
