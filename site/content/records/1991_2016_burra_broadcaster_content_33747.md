Rostrum Voice of Youth competitors spoke at BCS on Thursday evening.  Five juniors spoke for six minutes and one senior spoke for eight minutes on their chosen topic.  Then all five had fifteen minutes to prepare their short notice speeches from three given topics.  From the group Chelsea Evans and Katie Thomas will compete in the semi-finals in Adelaide on 30 May.
