Burra Picnic Race Club presented John Maidment with a gift on Monday night in appreciation of eleven years as President of the club.  He has also been made a life member of the committee.  In his time as President the club has donated $30,000 to the RFDS, Burra Day Care, Burra Kindergarten, SES, Heartbeat and the Girl Guides.  The current President is John Oates.
