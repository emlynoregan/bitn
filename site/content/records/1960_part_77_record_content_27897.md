Nigel Dollman (4), son of Mr & Mrs Guy Dollman, vanished from the Burra Street when his mother popped into Wilkinson’s for a moment.  A family and then a police search proved fruitless, but at 2 p.m. the police were advised that he was at Yunta, having boarded a tourist coach at Burra.
