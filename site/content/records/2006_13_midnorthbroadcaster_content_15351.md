As part of the family reunion visits were also arranged to ‘Richmond Park’ at Balaklava; the home of James and Eliza Lewis, the parents of John Lewis.  And also to ‘Benacre’ at Glen Osmond, where John Lewis lived and which remained in the family for some 70 years until the death of Lance Lewis’s widow in 1969.  [Actually 23 May 1968.]
