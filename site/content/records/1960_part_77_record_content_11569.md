Burra-Hallett Road.  Following a question in Parliament from Mr Quirke, he was advised that it was intended to seal the road for 6 miles north from Cobb & Co.’s Corner and sub-base construction will be continue with the expectation that this will be completed to Hallett in the 1962-63 financial year.
