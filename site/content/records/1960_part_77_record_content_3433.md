Burra Burra DC, 1 August

A letter was received from the Town Council accepting the offer of the old depot property.

The Highways and Local Government Department advises that the Grant in Aid for 1960-61 is £378.  (To be divided equally between three towns.)

Also advising that FRA Grants 1960-61 will be £3,900 with Council contribution to be £600.

The Main Road Grants will be £9,100 with a Council contribution of £500.

The Hospital Contributions for the year are to be Clare £36 and Burra £1,380.
