Accidents

A semi-trailer carting hay lost six bales on the highway near Porters Lagoon during the week.  Fortunately other traffic managed to avoid them.

A two car collision occurred on the highway at the East Bungaree turn-off near Mt Bryan on Saturday morning.  Damage was extensive, but no one was injured.
