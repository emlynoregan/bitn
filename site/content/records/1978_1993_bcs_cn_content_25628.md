Burra Lions Club Presentation Ball was a great success.  The MC was Darryl Venning.  Presentations were made to Lions Club International past District Governor Dr Charles Mattner and Mrs Elizabeth Mattner.  The Lions Club President George Adrian Green presented a cheque from the club to Burra SES for $2,500.  It was received by Mr Andrew McInnis.
