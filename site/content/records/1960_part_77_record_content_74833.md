Burra Primary School Council held its AGM.  Chairman I.T. Allen reported on 1974.  A number of joint meetings were held with the High School Council for joint approaches to the Government and at present not many projects are under way pending the building program, which should begin soon.  Tenders for the new school are expected to be called in April, with work beginning in July.
