Burra Regional Art Gallery.  March’s exhibition features regional artists including some of Glenys Christopher’s watercolours from her European trip.  Other artists include Robert Heading (metallic collage image), Gerald Moore) pastels), Helen Jensen (flowers), Kate Bullen (local landscapes) and Vivien Ziersch (collages of raw materials and found objects).  The Bence Room features a display honouring Sir Hubert Wilkins’ exploits.
