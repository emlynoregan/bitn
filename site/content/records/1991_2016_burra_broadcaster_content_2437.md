Redbanks

At a meeting on 9 November the Burra Burra DC decided to accept the dedication of the whole of the Redbanks Reserve area as a Reserve for Recreation, Conservation and Education.  An interim management plan will keep the area closed to the public and off road vehicles will continue to be prohibited.
