Obituary.  Albert Henry Moore died 25 June at Pt Augusta Hospital aged 71.  He was the husband of Alma and the father of Audrey (Mrs Voumard of Nuriootpa), Cliff (Yardea Station) and Valda (Burra).  There are 11 grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren.  [Born 6 October 1892 Kooringa: died 25 June 1964 Pt Augusta, residence Pt Augusta West.]
