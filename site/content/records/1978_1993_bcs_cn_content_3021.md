At the Annual Australian Film Awards announced last Wednesday ‘Breaker Morant’ won almost all the major awards and Burra gained some free nation-wide publicity.  It won:

Best film of 1980

Best performance by a leading actor (Jack Thompson)

Best Director (Bruce Beresford)

Cinematography (Don McAlpine)
