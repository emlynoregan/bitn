Final Welcome Home at Hallett.  Four soldiers were welcomed back at Hallett when Mr Peter Bald led in the four.  The others were: Ken McInnes (3½ years service of which 19 months was in the Islands and the rest in Australia), Keith Jones (12 months in the Islands and 12 months in Japan) & Bronte Phin (2½ years in the RAAF in Australia).
