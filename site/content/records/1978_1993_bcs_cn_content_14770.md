Bus Service Changes

From Monday 1 June

To Adelaide daily (except Sunday) Departs Burra Roadhouse 8.10 a.m. and arrives Adelaide 10.25 a.m. at Franklin Street Terminal.

From Adelaide daily (except Saturday) Departs Franklin Street Terminal 5.40 p.m. and arrives at Burra Roadhouse 8.05 p.m.
