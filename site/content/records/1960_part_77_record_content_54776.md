Burra Mine

A meeting of the National Trust on Friday decided to launch a state-wide appeal for funds to resite as many old mine buildings as possible.  Mr S.J. Woollacott spoke on their historic value.  The first priority for saving buildings was decided as the Chimney at Waterman’s Shaft known as ‘Peacock’s’.  Elected were: President, L. Moore; Vice-Presidents, G. O’Connor & Les. Warnes; Secretary, Mrs C. O’Connor; Treasurer, D. Cockington; Research Officer, I. Auhl, Curator of Historical Properties, S.J. Woollacott and Early Buildings Officer, G. O’Connor.
