Marriage.  St Michael’s Church of England, Mitcham, Saturday

Jillian Ruth Sawtell, elder daughter of Mr & Mrs M.J. Sawtell of Lower Mitcham, married

Malcolm John Goodridge, elder son of Mr & Mrs J.E. Goodridge [sic] of Crystal Brook.

The couple will make their home at Stevenage near London in the UK.  Malcolm is the son of Mr & Mrs J.C. Goodridge* [sic], who commenced business in Burra as ‘Burra Radio & Electrical Service’.  Malcolm completed his secondary education in Burra and then obtained a Bachelor of Technology at Adelaide University.  Following his marriage he will go to England to take up duties with the British aircraft Corporation as an Electronic Engineer.  This will be his third trip to England in three years.  He was Dux of Burra Primary in 1949 and of Burra High School in 1953.
