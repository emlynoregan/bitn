Accident.  At Mt Bryan a motorcyclist collided with a car attempting to make a right hand turn.  The motorcyclist sustained minor injuries.  The car driver was issued an infringement notice for failure to indicate his intention to turn right.  The motorcyclist was issued with a notice for driving without a licence.  Total damages from the accident amounted to c. $5,000.
