Cycling.  Professional cyclist Roger Nicholls of Pt Pirie was successful in winning the 100 mile Burra-Adelaide Road Race on Saturday.  Fastest time was 4 hours 26 minutes by R. Panter of Victoria.  Quite a crowd gathered at Burra to see the limit man ‘pushed off’ by the Mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone.
