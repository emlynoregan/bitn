Municipal Election.

East Ward	J. Greenwood	84	elected

E.J. Harris	74

Informal		2

This turnout was remarkable.  There were 240 names on the roll and 177 possible voters allowing for absences etc.  160 of 177 possible electors voted, or a turnout of c.  90%.
