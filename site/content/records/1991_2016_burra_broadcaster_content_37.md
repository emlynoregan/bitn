The Kooringa Hotel was refurbished last year and has become one of the leading hotels in Burra.  Thursday nights are International Nights with anything from Chinese to SA Pie Cart menus.  The hotel has also recently upgraded its accommodation and has a beer garden and multi-purpose function centre.
