National Trust held its AGM at the Old Court House on 19 July.  Professor Rod Wells of Flinders University was the guest speaker.  He spoke of his work as a palaeontologist.  Among other aspects of his work, he said the Baldina Creek sites had been dated to 35,000 years ago and he also spoke of the recent Diprotodon find there.  Peter Birt, a Flinders University student, reported on the Mitchell Flat archaeological dig.

Elected were: Chairman, Maureen Wright; Vice-Chairman, Steve Boyes; Secretary, Colin Wood and Treasurer, Barry Wright.
