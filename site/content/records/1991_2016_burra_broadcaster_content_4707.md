‘Caroona’ owned by James Hawker, was sold by auction on Friday for $693,000.  About 140 attended the sale at the Burra Civic Centre.  The 12,945 ha property has frontages to Newikie and Witto Creeks and is largely rising hills with saltbush and mallee and is Perpetual Leasehold.  The new owner is Geoff Davis.
