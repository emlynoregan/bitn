St Mary’s.  Steps have been taken to elect a new trustee to replace the late Mr G.H. Dollman.  Mr Dollman had replaced Mr M.W. Bednall and had served for 16 years.  The present trustees are: M. de N. Lucas, J.R. Barker, A. Tennant, R.K. Pearse and R.R. Reed.
