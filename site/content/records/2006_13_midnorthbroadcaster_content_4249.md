Peterborough’s Steamtown – its railway heritage museum – is set to become world class after obtaining grant funding.  $600,000 is guaranteed and it may reach c. $1 million.  A new and more welcoming entrance is planned.  Wow factors and a theatre are seem as essential and a sound and light show will be included.
