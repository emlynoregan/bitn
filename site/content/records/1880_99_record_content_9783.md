New Buildings.  Despite bad times new buildings are planned in the neighbourhood.

1.	Woolshed at Booborowie to be built by Sara & Dunstan

2.	New railway station for Burra.

3.	New house in Kooringa for Mr McBride

4.	New house at Koonoona for Mr Sandland

5.	New house at Mt Bryan for R. Brown jnr
