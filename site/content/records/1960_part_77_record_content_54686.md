Burra High School.  New members of staff.

Miss B. Purins Senior Mistress comes from Pt Pirie.

Miss J. Baron & Miss J. Helbig come from Teachers College.

Mr A. Langham comes from Glengowrie High School.

Mr K. Costello comes from Teachers College.

The 1970 Leaving Exam results are printed.  Two outstanding students were Anne Brak and Lynette Kelly.  Each passed six subjects with three grade ones.
