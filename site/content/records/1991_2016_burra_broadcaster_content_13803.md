Burra Art Gallery.  The present exhibition ‘Too Dark for the Lighthorse’ is a display of photographs from the Australian War Memorial, Canberra, which explores the little known contribution of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders in the Defence Forces from the Boer War to the Vietnam Conflict.  It was opened by Rev. John Devenport.
