Accident.  A large branch of a tree fell on a tent in the Burra Caravan Park last Thursday.  The tourists were in the tent at the time.  Thankfully no one was injured.  [Photo.]

The Riley Car Club will visit Burra for Easter.  A lunch at the Redruth Gaol has been arranged.

Burra Burra DC has commissioned B.C. Tonkin & Associates to prepare a plan for possible extensions to the Common Effluent Drain System in Burra.  They will survey the town in the next two weeks.
