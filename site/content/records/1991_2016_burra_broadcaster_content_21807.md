The 2000 Elders SA Stud Merino Field Days were held over three days in the Mid North last week.  Day One saw 16 studs display at the Burra Oval.  Sales were strong and two studs achieved sales at $4,000.  Collinsville Stud Pty Ltd sold four rams at $3,000 each.  [Further details are given.]
