The Twilight Jazz Affair at Petherton was a resounding success.  Some 350 people attended and listened to music by E-Type Jazz, Eddy White All Stars and Gumbo Ya Ya.  Misty rain for about 30 minutes did not dampen the spirits of the revellers.  [2 photos.]
