Burra Regional Art Gallery.  The Art of the Flower exhibition had 50 painting submitted and the first prize was awarded to Sibylla Tydeman of Manoora for her painting of a bottlebrush.

The people’s choice award went to Sibylla Tydeman’s Arum Lilies.

The Children’s Floral Arrangement went to Kelly Douglas of Burra.

Joyce Lloyd won the Individual Flower Arrangement.

Christopher Catt and Lenice Pearce won the Group Floral Arrangement.
