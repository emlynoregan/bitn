Hallett Wind Farm.  Construction of Brown Hill Wind Farm, worth $236 million, is on track.  The Agl-owned farm is estimated to be the largest in Australia and will have 45 wind turbines on a 14 km area over the Brown Hill Ridge.  A trial turbine should be producing power in February.
