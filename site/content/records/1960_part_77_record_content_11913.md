Mt Bryan CWA held its AGM 20 June and elected President, Mrs D. Thomas; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames Crew & R. Dare; Secretary, Mrs Osterhage and Treasurer, Mrs Griffen.  Tribute was paid to the work of Mr & Mrs F. Jefferies over the years.
