BBH Football & Netball Club’s Great Burra Barrow Push on Sunday drew twelve teams compared with five the previous year.  Winners were ‘Road Ragers’ comprising Kerry Eldredge, Andrea Rowe, Jo Gebhardt & Chris Kerrigan.

Scott Donaldson of Wesfarmers Landmark’s Burra branch was awarded the Golden Gavel Award by the Society of Auctioneers & Appraisers of SA in the Under 29 Years section earlier this month.

Donna Kelly, driving ‘Harvey’ the wonder pony, gained first place in the Novice Pony in the SA Carriage Driving Championships at Owen.
