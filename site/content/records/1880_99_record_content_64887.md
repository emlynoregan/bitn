Burra Cycle Club held its first meeting at Victoria Park on Wednesday.

1.	A half-mile race in which competitors had to light a cigar, open an umbrella, mount a bicycle and ride to the end of the course with it.  Winners: Packard & H.O. Scott.

2. 	A slow race in which again Packard was the winner.

3.	An obstacle race with Packard again the winner.

4.	A steeplechase which Packard won from H.O. Scott.

5.	Egg & spoon race won by Richards.

6.	Half mile rescue race – competitors to ride ¼ mile, pick up a dummy, ride ¼ mile with their ‘rescue’.  Won by Packard from A. Walker.

7.	Bicycle tilting.  Won by Richards from Packard.

Mr Shortridge was ‘at home’ as secretary.

C. & A. Fuss and A. Harris were judges and Mr Hiddle the starter.

Takings benefited the Burra Benevolent Soc.
