BCS Speech Night

Principal Kevin Fahey spoke of the different perceptions people have about standards at the school and claimed that ‘We have a terrific school – they don’t come much better.’

He farewelled Dennis Wilson, Liz Pritchard and Robyn Nourse and teachers Peter Giles, Linda Smith and Deb Marks.  He welcomed Dick Secomb and Lisa O’Connor who will take up half-time positions in 2002.
