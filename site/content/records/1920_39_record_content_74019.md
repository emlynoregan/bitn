Advt.  Burra Races 9 March.  Gentlemen 4/-, Ladies 2/- (plus tax).

Free Transport to and from the course in Halls’ & Bevan’s Motor Buses.

Race Club Ball at the Institute in the evening will aid Bushfire Relief Fund.
