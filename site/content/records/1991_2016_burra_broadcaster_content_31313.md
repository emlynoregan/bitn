Uniting Church.  Frank Tuppin and his wife will begin their ministry in Burra from the start of 2004.  Currently they are at Murrayville in Victoria.  Lay preachers will conduct services until July, when for three months Rev. Tony Chapman will serve in Burra with his wife, as supply minister.
