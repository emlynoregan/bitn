Burra Basketball Club held its AGM on 6 March and elected as President Mrs Matthews; Vice-President, Diane Beinke and Secretary-Treasurer, Dianne Day.  1968 was the first season that saw ‘A’, ‘B’ & ‘C’ teams in the same uniforms, at a cost to the club of $215.75.  ‘D’ Grade was introduced for the first time and is for Primary School Children only.  Burra had one team.  The ‘B’ team got as far as the preliminary final.  ‘A’ team won the shield for the second time in succession.
