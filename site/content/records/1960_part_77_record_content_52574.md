Burra Methodist Church

About 240 people attended the Pleasant Sunday Afternoon.  Tom Eastman, the Rotary Exchange student from Kimball, Nebraska, was welcomed.  Entertainment was by the local quartet of singers and ‘Folkus Pax’, a young trio from the city.  Rev. Kyle Waters gave an address on the subject Give Them to Eat.  [Concerning overseas aid projects to increase food production in under-developed countries.]
