Burra Traders Association held its AGM and Mr John Barton was re-elected as President at the dinner held at the Commercial Hotel on 29 July.  It was decided to have a Daylight Disco on Christmas Eve, a Christmas Lottery and Father Christmas at 7 p.m.  It was also decided to make a donation of $120 to the Community News and $50 to Miss Showgirl.
