Burra Camera Club.  At the AGM elected were: President, L. Moore; Vice-Presidents R. Blackwell & H. Shakeshaft; Secretary, G. Dollman; Assistant Secretary, Mrs R.D. Dollman.

The president reported on a successful year.   Membership had increased to 25 and meetings had been a mixture of lectures, practical nights and social occasions.
