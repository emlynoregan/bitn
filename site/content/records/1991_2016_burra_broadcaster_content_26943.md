Burra Band ‘Orchiade’ joined with six other bands recently to launch a CD featuring a track from each band.  [Elsewhere in the article it says there are eight bands on the CD.]  Barry Wakelin MP attended the launch in Burra on Friday.  The CD is entitled Breaking New Ground.  Band members are Shaan Kelly, Graham Guthrie, Tristan Brak and Peter Manuel.  [More details and two photos are printed.]
