Obituary.  Mrs Mary Josephine Dare died recently in Adelaide.  She was born at Mt Bryan East; the eldest in the family of the late Mr & Mrs W.H. Quinn.  Her husband, Mr George Dare, died some 5 years ago.  They had lived in the Mt Bryan District until moving to Adelaide in the 1950s.

[Born 4 April 1894 Mt Bryan East: died 24 August 1972 St Peters.]
