Burra Pistol Club.  Fifty-two people attended the opening night.  President Sergeant B. Haynes welcomed visitors.  Vice-President of the Pistol League Mr I. Macfarlane attended.  Vice-Patron Tom Perry made a short speech and Patron Jack Oates fired the first official shot.  Forty shooters then went through five-shot rounds.  N. Doube and I. Macfarlane demonstrated the correct way to stand and breathe and handle the pistol.  Thirty-three of thirty-eight members attended.
