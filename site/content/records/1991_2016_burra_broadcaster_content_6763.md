Burra Arts Council’s 3rd Annual Art Exhibition is entitled ‘Jubilee’ and will be opened by Gaie Betts, Arts Officer for the Northern Country Arts Trust, on Friday 5 May at 8 p.m. in the former ‘Country Cousins’ shop at 13 Commercial Street, Burra and will run till Friday 12 May.
