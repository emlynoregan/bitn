BCS did very well at Wednesday’s SA Secondary Schools’ Track & Field Championships in Adelaide.

Burra won the Girls’ competition and came third in the boys’ competition.

Girls 326, ahead of Waikerie 295½

Boys: Waikerie 402½, Playford 356 and Burra 350½.
