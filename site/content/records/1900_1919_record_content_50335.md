Memorial & Intercession Service, last Sunday afternoon at the Institute.

The hall was filled to capacity.  The Mayor regretted the absence of the town’s ministers, who except for Ensign Day had had previous engagements.  The Mayor gave an address, which occupies over ½ column.  Mr Hawkes and Mr Winnall also gave speeches.  The district’s fallen now number 29.
