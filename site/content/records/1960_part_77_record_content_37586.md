Burra district Fund Raising Committee held its AGM.  The committees that combine to make this committee have raised $12-15,000 in the past ten years.  This money has been spent on hospital equipment and amenities for patients.  The Hospital Board had asked that this year an amount of $840 be allowed to be used for the building fund, with any surplus to go towards equipment and amenities.  The delegates present agreed.  Committees represented were:

The Hallett Committee

Burra North Hospital Aid

Farrell Flat CWA

Burra Cup Committee

Leighton Tennis Club

Booborowie CWA

Hanson CWA

Mt Bryan CWA

Burra CWA
