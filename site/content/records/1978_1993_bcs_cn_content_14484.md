Stephen Downs-Woolley.  Aged 32 and have lived in Burra for seven years.  A former police officer and dedicated to seeing the town and district fulfil its potential as a tourist and business centre.  A Past President of Apex.  Served on the Kindergarten and other Committees, including the Barrow Push, J150 Trade train and the Copper Festival.
