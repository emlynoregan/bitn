Phil Smyth, coach of 36ers was a special guest at the Sportsperson of the Year Dinner where the awards were:

Trevor Willis – Service to Sport Award

Gus Wenzel – Senior Sportsperson of the Year.

Carly Broad – Junior Sportsperson of the Year.

Junior Softball Team – Team of the Year.

Singles Champions in Tennis were Tim Simpson and Kaye Bellman.

Singles Junior Champions in Tennis were Rachel Riggs and Justin Webster.

Champion Golfers on the new course were Lesley Gosse and David Robinson.

The Art Gallery has staged through the year a series of exhibitions:

Calling the Country

E-merge

Regional Artists

The Quilters

State of the Art/Art of the State 5

Recent Works

Allyson Parsons’ Prints

Art of the Flower in which Sibylla Tydeman won the $500 first prize and also the People’s Choice [with a different painting].

In the Floral Art section Kelly Douglas won the Junior Prize, Joyce Lloyd the Individual Prize and Lenice Pearce & Chris Catt the Group Award.

An attendance figure in excess of 5,000 is believed to make it SA’s most visited regional gallery.
