BCS at the Royal Adelaide Show

Students have returned with a swag of prizes.

Year 6/7s exhibited poultry.

Year 8s entered the sheep judging.

Year 9s entered the Led Wether Goats competition.

Year 10 & 11s entered the Led Steers Section.

In poultry the students won first prizes for:

Large fowl, light breed, any other variety, cockerel (junior classes)

Large fowl, light breed, any other variety, pullet (junior classes)

Bantam, any variety hen or pullet (school children)

Australorp, large fowl, heavy breed, cockerel, (junior classes)

Bantam, Wyandotte, white cockerel, (junior classes)

And they won a number of other prizes too.

Nicole Shattock came 2nd in Merino Ram Judging (12-16 years)

In the Led Steers BCS won:

2nd light weight steer on the hoof

Best South Devon Carcase

Best South Devon combined on hoof and carcase

The Ked Steers Schools Project Grand Parade Award & Most Professional Show Team
