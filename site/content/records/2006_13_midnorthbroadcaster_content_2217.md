February

High copper prices persuade Adchem to strike a deal with Leigh Creek Copper Mines to obtain copper cement from a deposit near Copley.

John Burge was announced Citizen of the Year and more than 300 attend the Australia Day breakfast at Thomas Pickett Reserve.

Hallett swimming pool re-opened for the season after repairs.

AGL announced construction of Australia’s largest wind farm near Hallett.

600 attend Music in the Monster Mine with Beccy Cole, Murray Mac and ‘True Grit’.

Aberdeen Tennis Courts get a $50,000 grant for re-surfacing as artificial grass courts.
