Goyder Council is seeking a temporary manager for Paxton Square Cottages.  The existing arrangements expire on 31 August and they are seeking a manager for the next nine months.

Accident.  Three occupants of a small sedan escaped with minor injuries when the driver failed to stop at the T-junction at Ford’s Corner (Kooringa Road and Highway 64) last Thursday.  The car was seriously damaged.
