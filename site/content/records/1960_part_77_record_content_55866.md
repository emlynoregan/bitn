Burra High School Sports Day was held at the Burra Memorial Oval in ideal weather last Thursday.  Cup winners:

Senior Girls’ Cup	B. Kleinig

Junior Girls’ Cup	S. Spackman

Senior Boys’ Cup	M. Broderick

Junior Boys’ Cup	R. Wade

The House Competition went to Steele House.
