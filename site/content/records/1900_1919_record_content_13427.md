Redruth Court.

Peter Pascoe was charged with being the bailee of certain valuable property of William Phinn of Friederichswalde [since 1918 Tarnma] – to wit a cheque for £14.  There is ½ column of detail and the case was committed to Adelaide to the Supreme Court.
