Burra North Red Cross held its AGM 4 July and elected: President, Mrs Fay Jones; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames Aileen Kelly and Margaret Neale; Secretary, Mrs Joan Steele, Assistant Secretary, Mrs Mavis Wall, Treasurer, Mrs Florrie scroop and Assistant Secretary, Mrs Aileen Kelly.

The Secretary reported 54 financial members and an average attendance of 26 with ten new members this year.

[Details of excursions and activities are given.]

In March the branch sent $550 to Headquarters for the Annual March Appeal.
