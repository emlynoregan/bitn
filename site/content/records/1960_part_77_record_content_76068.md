Burra Burra DC, 14 July

A tender of $460 for the depot block was accepted.

Treasury has approved loans of $9,000 and $5,000, which have also been approved by the Local Government Office.

Crs Jennison and White moved the removal and repair of the water fountain on the main road and its relocation to the bank of the creek.
