Obituary.  Mr P.T. Sandland died at Moora WA 13 July aged 87.  He was born in SA and his home was part of the Koonoona Station.  He spent the greater part of his life in WA, buying the property ‘Burrabidgy’ at Moora in 1908.  As a young man he was a prominent footballer and at the time of his death the oldest holder of the Magarey Medal.

[Philip Thomas Sandland born 5 December 1882 Mannanarie, SA.]
