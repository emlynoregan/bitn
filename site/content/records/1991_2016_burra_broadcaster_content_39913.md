Burra Regional Art Gallery.  The exhibition, Art of the Flower, was opened on Sunday 19 October by Gypsy Sandow.  Two $400 prizes were awarded – one for a traditional work and one for an alternative work.

Shirley Thamm won the award for floral art and Tilley Leighton won the young artist’s award.
