Commercial Hotel patrons will be sorry to see the departure of John & Marg McLennan and family.  Reg & Mavis Wapels and their two youngest children (from a family of eight) will take over early in November.  Reg was a former manager of a Bennetts & Fisher Stock Agency Branch.
