‘Belle of Burra Burra’ played to full houses over the weekend.

Burra Swimming Pool Committee AGM did not have a large attendance.  If the maximum temperature in the Advertiser is estimated to be 20°C or over the pool will be open at 3.30 p.m. weekdays and 2 p.m. weekends and school holidays.  If it is estimated to be over 25°C on weekdays it will be opened at 2.30 p.m. to allow adults a quiet dip before students arrive.  Closing time is still 5.30 p.m.
