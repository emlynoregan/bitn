Gus Wenzel of Mid North SAPSASA urges local support for a lamington drive being held to raise fund to replace a tent stolen from the Adelaide University Sports Field during SAPSASA Hockey/Soccer week at the end of term 2.  The tent had been hired from Clare SES and it will cost $1,700 to replace.
