A Progress Committee was formed at a meeting at Farrell Flat Hall on 12 August.  Mr Leo Robinson of Burra chaired the meeting and was later elected President of the new Porter’s Lagoon Progress Committee.  The Vice-President is Gordon Webster.  The aim is to promote water safety for swimmers, skiers and boat owners.  Boat owners will be asked to pay a $2 membership fee to the committee which will control the foreshore.
