Mid North Regional Development Board (MNRDB) has delivered results.  Some major achievements in 2002-03:

Baiada Eudunda – a $700,000 capital investment in poultry expansion

O’Leary Walker Winery established at Leasingham, representing a $6.9 million investment

Clode’s Funeral Parlour at Clare – a $650,000 capital investment

Coffee n’ Cork restaurant and wine outlet in Clare – a $600,000 investment

Glenbower-Eudunda – a new $4 million piggery

2 CDMA mobile phone towers with a total investment of $564 million.

The Clare Valley Water Scheme construction has commenced – a $28 million scheme overall

Consultants are working on a report on the Bower District intensive primary production zone concept
