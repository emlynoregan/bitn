Goyder Council has struck the rate for 1999/2000.  The capital valuation made by the Valuer General and adopted by Council was $400,499,340 and the income for the year will be $1,920,213.30.  Differential rates have been adopted:

Hallett Ward: 0.3043 cents in the dollar

Burra Township: 0.7660

Remainder of Burra Ward: 0.4245

Eudunda Township: 0.4200

Remainder of Eudunda Ward: 0.3407

Robertstown Ward: 0.3407

STEDS fees Burra installed prior to 1995 $170.50 and after 1995 $178.20

STEDS fees Eudunda $54.20

Burra Leisure Activity Group held an open day at the Burra Community Activities centre on Wednesday 21 July.
