Sally Kellock & Leonie Quinn, ex-Burra students, graduated from Flinders University on 3 June.  Leonie is the daughter of Rosemary and Jim Quinn of Mt Bryan and Sally is the daughter of Judy and Graham Kellock of Burra.  Leonie received a 1st Class Honours Degree of Bachelor of Science and is now studying for her PhD (Medicine) in Molecular Biology.  She attended Mt Bryan Primary School, BCS to Year 10 and then Mercedes College.  She graduated in 1992 with a BSc at Adelaide University.

Sally received a Degree of Bachelor of education (Upper Primary-Lower Secondary).  She attended BCS to Year 10 and then Annesley College.  She is a Maths/Social Sciences teacher, presently at Stuart High School, Whyalla.
