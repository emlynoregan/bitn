National All Schools Track & Field Championships in Perth

Rosie Lloyd was chosen to lead the SA Team.

Rosie won gold in the 400 m hurdles and is now the National U19 400 m hurdles champion.

She also won bronze in the U19 400 m.

Graham Kessell was 4th in the U19 javelin.

Craig McDowell was 5th in the high jump.
