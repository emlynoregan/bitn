The 36th Annual Hallett & District Schools’ Athletic Assoc. Meeting was held at Hallett Oval on 19 April in ideal weather.  Burra Primary School competed for the first time.  Willalo & Canowie Belt had moved to another association, leaving only four of the original schools from 1928: Terowie, Whyte-Yarcowie, Hallett and Mt Bryan.  Burra entered as four houses to make an eight team competition.  Results:

Hallett	107

Aberkoorie (Burra Green)	78

The Bednall Trophy for Teams’ Races went to Hallett.

Hawker Cup for the Girls’ Championship to Susan Brown (Hallett).

Tiver Trophy for Boys’ Championship to Geoffrey Jones (Hallett).

The Highest Aggregate for boys was a tie between G. Jones of Hallett and K. Bannister of Aberkoorie (Burra Green).

The Highest Aggregate for girls went to Alison Miller (Hallett).

The gate was £45-17-0 and there was a good profit of £72-12-8 on cool drinks.
