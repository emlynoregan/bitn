Obituary.  Mary Jane Turnbull nee Morrison died 10 July in Adelaide, late of Daw Park and Burra. She was the wife of Daniel late of Mt Gambier aged 87.  She was the mother of Jean, essie [sic], Laurel and Gordon and of the late Colin and Della.  [‘essie’ is a misprint for Jessie.  The deceased was born Mary Jane Morrison 18 May 1889 Kooringa and married Daniel Turnbull at Kooringa in 1912.]
