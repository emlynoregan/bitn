Accident.  A semi-trailer prime mover that was parked in from of Beaut Fruit on Thursday evening spilled about 30 litres of diesel fuel from its tanks.  Proprietor Peter Kelly notified Council on Friday morning and the MFS set about cleaning it up.  The spill was well contained due to the gutter contours at that point.
