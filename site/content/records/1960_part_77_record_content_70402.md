Monday 18th 	Visited Piccadilly Valley market gardens and Ashton Cold Stores for grading and packing apples.  Went to Mr Trenorden’s apple orchard and to the Police Training School at Echunga in the afternoon.

Tuesday 19th 	Inspected Jacob’s milk and butter factory.  Swam in Mt Barker’s heated pool.  Visited Johnson’s Tannery and the Mt Barker School.

Wednesday 20th	Morning tour of Port Adelaide, visiting the wharves, Torrens Island Power Station and the Birkenhead Bridge.  Moscow Circus matinee in the afternoon and at night visited Mt Lofty Summit to see Adelaide’s lights.

Thursday 21st 	Went to the Road Safety Centre at Oaklands Park and toured the TAA facilities at Adelaide Airport, where they inspected a plane, returning to Mt Barker by train.

Friday 22nd  	Retuned to Burra.
