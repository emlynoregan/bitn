Accident/Obituary.  On 24 December at 6.30 p.m. a Toyota sedan owned by Clifford Howard Herbert of Boulder WA and driven by Felix Garrett Richards (22), a soldier of Mt Yokine, WA, overturned 15 miles east of Burra on the Morgan Road.  The driver was killed.  Two cars were travelling together to Albury where the deceased was to have been best man at a wedding.  Mr Richards was taken to the Burra Hospital in the other vehicle, but was dead on arrival.
