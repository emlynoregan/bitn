Burra Lions Club held a picnic race meeting on 13 March at Reilly’s Farm.  It was an unqualified success.  Burra Football Club ran the barbecue.  Spalding SES helped with portable generators.  Burra Yacht Club ran their chocolate wheel.  Garry James called the events.  The profit on the day was $1,956.72.
