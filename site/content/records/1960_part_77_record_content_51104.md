Hallett & Districts Athletic Assoc. combined sports was held at the Hallett Oval 24 April.  The school teams were Burra Green, Burra Purple, Hallett, Whyte Yarcowie, Mt Bryan.  [Results are printed.]

Trophy for the Highest Aggregate for an individual boy went to Geoffrey Bernhardt of Burra Green.

Trophy for the highest aggregate for an individual girl went to Sue Talent of Mt Bryan.

The Boysâ€™ Championship was won by Stephen Lake of Hallett.

Keryl Kakoschke of Burra Purple won the Hawker Cup.
