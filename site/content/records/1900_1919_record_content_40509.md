Burra Races on Saturday 16 January.

There was a fair attendance.  The meeting was conducted almost where the 1876 meeting had been held.  The results are printed.  The main race was the Burra Handicap over 1¼ miles for 80 sovereigns and was won by W. Standley’s ALRAF ridden by J. Liddle.
