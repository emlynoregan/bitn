Burra Football Club held a banquet in the RSL Hall attended to by about 50 people.  Mr Tony Fillmore welcomed everyone and expressed his thanks to Mr Ron Dewhirst for re-forming the Colts in the North Eastern League.  He went on to thank parents and trophy donors and those supplying transport.  Trophies:

Fairest & Best	Darrell Field

Most Consistent	Andrew Phillips

Best Forward	Graham Benger

Best Backman	Leon Hawke

Most Effective	Brian Kakoschke
