BCS results of Year 12, 2006.

Eleven students completed the year and most achieved the grades they needed for their chosen next step in life.  Five students left during the year and took up employment with training.  Two had already decided to do Stage Two over two years and will return in 2007.

Emma Shattock of Booborowie was 2006 School Dux and achieved As in all five Year 12 subjects.  She has been accepted into a BSc degree (Agriculture).

Brittany Willis and Maddie Jonas did very well and are going on to tertiary studies.
