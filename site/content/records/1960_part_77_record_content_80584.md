BCS.  The October mini courses have got away to an enthusiastic start.  Examples are: Blacksmithing, Rug Making & Leather Embossing, Estate Planning & Taxation, Insurance and Land Transactions, Evaluations for the man on the land.

A china painting group has been established.

Three canoes are available for use through the school, which bought one, one was provided by the Youth Club and one belongs to Mr Bednall.
