Burra Mine ‘Course of Action’

Work will begin within the next two weeks to dismantle the huge Cornish buildings which have stood for 120 years.  Mr G. Lloyd has been contracted to remove the buildings from their present sites.  He is a noted Adelaide builder known for his magnificent stonework around Adelaide and Sydney.  Mr Ian Auhl has been working tirelessly in bringing the project to this stage.
