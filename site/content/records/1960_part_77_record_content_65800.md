Diamond Wedding.  Mr & Mrs Klaebe celebrated their Diamond Wedding at Victor Harbor on Sunday 25 March, having been married at Peterborough Methodist Church 25 March 1913.  They later moved to Clare and then Hallett, where Mrs Klaebe was church organist for a number of years.  She was a Red Cross worker during the war and also secretary of the Fighting Forces Fund.
