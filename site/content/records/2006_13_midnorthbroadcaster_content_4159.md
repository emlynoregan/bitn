Weather.  Thunderstorms rolled across the Mid North in the past week.  On Monday Peterborough received 5.6 mm and Burra 25 mm.  On Tuesday there were massive falls and widespread local flooding.  Terowie received 50 mm, most of it in 40 minutes.  The Barrier Highway was cut â€“ in places until 2.30 a.m.  Flood damage is reported, especially from Terowie to Peterborough.  The falls also affected eastern properties.  Braemar Station received 104 mm (a record there) and Manunda 82.5 mm.  Good falls extended into the Flinders Ranges.
