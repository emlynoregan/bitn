Burra Hospital.  A tender has been let to Mr A. Mass for four private rooms and offices for the Matron and the Secretary-Manager.  Building should start within four weeks and the cost of the project, including furniture, equipment, architect’s fees and a new septic tank should be £UNREADABLE.
