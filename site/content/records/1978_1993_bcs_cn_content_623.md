Burra Squash Courts will be opened to the public on 26 March.

Mr Eric Clode has indicated bookings can be made by ringing Burra 4.

The official opening will be on 22 April.  Mr Clode hopes Mr Bill Reilly, former Test Cricketer and manager of Dunlop Slazenger will be able to open the centre.
