Advt.  Oats Pty Ltd has instructions from Messrs M.E. & E.A. Finch to sell all their properties known as ‘Pencarrow’ and ‘Bundey and Bright’.

1.  ‘Pencarrow’ of 766 acres freehold, located c. 3 miles north of Burra and comprising sections 169, 46, 45, 58 & 59 in the Hundred of Kooringa with a 10-roomed stone house etc.

2.  Bundey & Bright of 4,067 acres perpetual lease, located c. 25 miles southeast of Burra and comprising sections 54, 55, 56, 59, 213 & 214 in the Hundred of bright and sections 143 & 144 in the Hundred of Bundey.
