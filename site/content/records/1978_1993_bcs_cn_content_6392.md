Burra Swimming Pool

Applications are called for the positions of Swimming Pool Operator and Swimming Pool Supervisor.

The operator requires a Swimming Pool Operator’s Certificate issued by the Central Board of Health or must be prepared to attend a course to get one.  Ideally the supervisor will have a Royal Life Saving Society’s Bronze Medallion or higher and some form of first aid certificate.
