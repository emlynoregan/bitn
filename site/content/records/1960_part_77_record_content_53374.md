Accident.  A car driven by Miss Deidre Sutton of Booborowie overturned on the Emu Downs Road near Koonoona last Tuesday.  The driver and passenger, Terry Hocking, were admitted to the Burra Hospital and Hocking was later transferred to Adelaide.
