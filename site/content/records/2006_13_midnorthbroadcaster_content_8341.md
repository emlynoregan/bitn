Burra Community School attracted a full house to the gym on Wednesday and Thursday nights when more than 500 were on hand for the ‘Australiana’ production.  Students from Reception to Year 12 starred in the concert featuring music, dancing and poetry.  The Burra Junior Band led off the event which is described in a column of detail.
