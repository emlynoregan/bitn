Burra Pre-school Kindergarten held its AGM on 10 March.  Eighteen ladies attended, which is disappointing given that there are over 40 enrolments.  Elected: President, Mrs M. Slater; Vice President, Mrs M. Tiver; Secretary, Mrs P. McDowell; Assistant Secretary, Mrs Male and Treasurer, Mrs Dean Bernhardt.  The main efforts this year will be to repay the loan and to complete alterations to the kindergarten.
