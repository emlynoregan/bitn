Fortnightly Dances will be held in the Oddfellows’ Hall at Burra North commencing 1 June.  The Twist and Pride of Erin competitions will be held on alternate Saturdays.  3/- admission will aid the St John Ambulance and the Aberdeen Football Club Women’s Auxiliary.
