BCS.  All 13 students who began Year 12 completed the year.  12 of them aimed to complete their SACE in 2014 and 9 were successful.

The three highest achieving students and their Australian Tertiary Admission Ranks were:

Nathaniel O’Brien	89.6

Amy Berryman		86.6

Lachlan Haddow		86.3

Nathaniel has enrolled in Adelaide University to complete Bachelor of Teaching & Bachelor of Economics Degrees.

Amy and Lachlan have enrolled with UniSA.  Amy is to do a double degree in Commerce and Business and Lachlan a Bachelor of Business – Tourism & Event Management

Caleb Queissner-O’Dwyer is taking a gap year and working part-time.

Anthony Klingner has a full-time apprenticeship with Acrobat Engineering.

Tyla Anderson has been accepted into the Navy.

Tom Wiech is in full-time employment in agriculture at ‘Greenfields’.

Christie Sims is also employed in the agricultural industry.

Kimberley Williams has been accepted into nursing at TAFE.
