U.F.S. Rural Expo on Friday 29 August 1986 at Burra Town Hall.  5.30 p.m. to 9.30 p.m.

Barbecue Tea and refreshments.  Stalls and displays.  Entertainment by Clare Music and Local Artists.

Demonstrations include Farm Computers, Microwave Ovens, Food Processors and Kitchen Appliances.

Free advice on the Fringe Benefits Tax etc.
