Cricket.  Vic. Richardson the great cricketer and R.B. Hone interstate tennis player visited Burra and gave addresses at the Institute on Monday last week.  The mayor presided and quite a large audience attended.  Richardson spoke of the need to revitalise the grand old game.  He talked about the tour of England in 1930 and also about bodyline.
