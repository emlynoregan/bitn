Burra Community School has received three students from Germany through the Southern Cross Cultural Exchange program.  Nele (almost 16) will be living with Lisa & Mark Brooks near Hallett.  She comes from near Hanover.  Sophie (almost 16) comes from Buxtehude will be hosted by Trish & Mick Ritchie of Burra.  Laura (16) will stay with Christine Landorf and her daughters Priscilla and Stephanie at Booborowie.  She comes from the small town of Felmerholz.  All girls are in Year 11. Teresa Earle of Burra is the SCCE co-ordinator.  There is also a 17-year-old boy, Oliver, from Oregon staying with Kym & Carol Ackland in Orroroo.
