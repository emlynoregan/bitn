James Vincent (Jim) Quinn

Currently a Burra Ward Councillor and have been since 1997

Have long been involved with local government at all levels – Regional, State and National – President of Mid North Local Government Association and Executive Member of SA Local Government Association and Delegate to National Local Government Association

Have had involvement with Burra Ambulance, Mid North Passenger Transport Board, Rural Counselling Committee and the McDouall Stuart Board, which established the Burra Art Gallery.

Long experience in all local government matters in Burra and the district.

See myself as a voice for the people
