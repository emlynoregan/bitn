Following the resignation of Kym Haebich as Burra CFS captain because he is soon to leave the district, David Nourse has been appointed new captain, with Mark Bellman as 1st lieutenant, John Taylor as 2nd, David Thamm as 3rd and Chris Taylor as 4th.  The Haebich family was farewelled on the 20 March.
