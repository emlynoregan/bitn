Cecil Clarence Munday, cleaner, was charged with playing Coon Can for money in a public place at Kooringa on 6 August.  (Actually on sections 3039 & 3040: Nelson)  The case was adjourned and resumed on 8 August.  Twelve men were involved.  The defence said there was no proof they were playing an unlawful game.  The defendant denied they were playing for money.  He was fined Â£2 + 15/- costs and notice of appeal was given.
