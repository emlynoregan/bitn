SA Stockowners’ Association, Burra Branch held its AGM 10 April and elected: President, T.E. Ashby; Vice-Presidents; E.L. McWaters & D.C. Lord and Joint Secretaries, A.L. Wright & R.L. Wuttke.

Tennis Grand Finals

‘A’ Grade	Aberdeen 14-108 defeated Booborowie 6-83

‘B’ Grade	Spalding White 16 sets defeated Mt Bryan 4 sets
