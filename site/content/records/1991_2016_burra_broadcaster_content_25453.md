Burra Red Cross held its AGM.  The President Fay Jones expressed regret that the visiting blood donor service will not be coming to Burra in 2001.  The branch had an average attendance of 11.6 members per meeting.  Fundraising was very good in the last year.

The video and lunch day raised 		$390

Father’s Day Raffle & Trading Table	$1079

Antique Fair Trading Table		$992

Quiz Night				$965

Mini Deb Ball				$964

Community money this year was shared between Burra Kindergarten, Seniors’ Garden Project, Diversional Therapy at the Hospital and a contribution to the electric bed fund.
