Burra CWA held its AGM on 20 June and elected: President, Mrs H.J.B. Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames H.L. Riggs & A.D. Radford; Secretary, Mrs S. Scroop, Assistant Secretary, Mrs Alderson & Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.  Mrs Jennison reported an active year and the Secretary Mrs J.R. Ford reported a membership of 74 with an average attendance of 27.  She outlined the year’s activities in a report extending c. ¾ column.
