Burra National Trust AGM

Maureen Wright listed the projects for the branch as:

Repair and restoration of the beams of Morphett’s Enginehouse

History Trust accreditation

Updating of Passport noticeboards in BVC

Reinstating the audio system at the Bon Accord Museum

The restoration of the Buddle Pits

Silhouettes

Upgrading Passport Museum signs

Grassland trail at the mine site

Increasing the branch membership

Office bearers for 2005: Chairperson, Maureen Wright; Vice Chairperson, Ray Parker; Secretary, Susan Parker and Treasurer, Barry Wright.
