Obituary.  The death is mentioned of the death of a McIntyre – a mother and grandmother.

[This would seem likely to refer to Mary Lillian McIntyre nee Jones born 28 October 1886 Gladstone: died 11 September 1962 Solomontown.  This is confirmed by a Return Thanks Notice Burra Record 85, 43 (2), 30 Oct. 1962, page 3.  She was the wife of Patrick Joseph McIntyre and was married at Burra.  See also extended obituary Burra Record 85, 43 (2), 30 Oct. 1962, page 8.]
