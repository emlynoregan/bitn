Cricket.

Koonaburra 5 for 175 defeated North Booborowie 9 for 98.

Mt Bryan 7 for 230 declared (including D. Richardson 122) drew with Leighton 8 for 98 when the match had to be abandoned due to rain.

Booborowie 151 defeated Hallett 60.

Spalding 136 drew with Burra Footballers 9 for 87 when rain stopped play.

Junior Cricket: Gold drew with Red 75 runs each.
