State Cross Country Championships were run at Salisbury on 29 July.  BCS sent 15 competitors.  The Championships attract about 2,000 entrants.  The senior BCS runners all finished creditably; well up in the large fields of 100 to 200 runners.  The highlight of the day was in the U13 girls where in Division 1 Cheryl Day ran 4th in a field of 150 and in Division 2 Michele Smale & Libby Letcher came 1st & 2nd respectively.
