Obituary.  Bertram Villis, late of Moorok [sic: for Moorook] died at Loxton Hospital 23 October aged 75.  He was the husband of the late Margaret May Villis who died in 1950 and father of Syd., Bertram (Joe), and Mary.  He was a former resident of Burra.  [Born 24 December 1896 Kooringa.]
