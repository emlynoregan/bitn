Mid North District Athletics Day was held at Burra Community School on 14 September.  Burra came third in the Championship Pennant.  A small group of Burra students will join the Mid North District team to compete in Adelaide next term, namely: Amy Berryman, Anna Lehmann and Emma Stockman.

Glen & Kay Simpson celebrated their Golden Wedding on 12 September.
