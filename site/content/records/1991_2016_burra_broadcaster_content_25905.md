Obituary.  John Paul Howell died 13 May.  He was born and raised in Burra and then had a career in highway construction.  He worked on the Barrier Highway to Broken Hill, upgrading tourist roads in the Flinders Ranges, on the South-Eastern Freeway in Adelaide, on the Duke and Sturt Highways and on the road to Leigh Creek.
