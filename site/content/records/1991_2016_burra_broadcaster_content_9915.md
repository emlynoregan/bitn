‘Mongolata’ comprising 13,194 acres of grazing country 25 miles northeast of Burra was auctioned at Burra Civic Centre 22 March.  The top bid of $340,000 was accepted by vendors Heather & Brian Guthrie.  The Guthries will now concentrate their farming interests in the Hallett district.
