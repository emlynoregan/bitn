BCS.  The year promises to be busy:

The old Kooringa Building will be given a facelift – hopefully during the holiday at the end of term 1.

Almost $200,000 will be spent by the end of June on overdue maintenance – mostly painting.

Shade areas will be erected.

5BCS now has a radio studio.
