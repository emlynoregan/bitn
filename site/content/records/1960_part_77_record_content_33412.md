Burra High School.  Thirty-four students and seven teachers enjoyed a school camp and trek up Mt Remarkable at the weekend at a camp, which had been postponed because of heavy rain at the end of 1966.  Luncheon was taken at the summit of the climb.  The camp was a great success.
