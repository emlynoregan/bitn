New Police Officers.  Burra district has two new permanent police officers.  Brevet Sergeant Eddie Dilena has been appointed to the Burra station and Brevet Sergeant Charmaine Barlow to Hallett.  The couple, who are engaged, have been in the area for about five weeks.  Brv Sgt Dilena was at Hamley Bridge for 5 years, where he took over from former Burra officer George Kaiser and he is used to working in small towns.  Brv Sgt Barlow has worked at Balaklava and more recently at Pt Wakefield.  [Photo of the two officers.]
