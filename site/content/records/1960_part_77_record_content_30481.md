Weather.

Sundayâ€™s weather was dominated by gale force winds, but also featured rain, hail and snow.  East of the ranges it was dust that accompanied the wind.  Early on Sunday night light snow fell in Burra with heavier falls at Booborowie North and Hallett.  Light rain fell on Monday and the post office registered 33 points to 9 a.m.  At Princess Royal 58 points fell.  By Tuesday morning falls of up to one inch were reported from Hanson, Farrell Flat and Koonoona.  Mongolata reported 33 points and Baldina 38.  Mt Bryan East received 63.
