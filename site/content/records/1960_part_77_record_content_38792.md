Aberdeen Tennis Club held an outdoor luncheon in the garden of Mr & Mrs Geoff. Pearce.  It raised c. $130 towards the relating of the surface on No. 2 court.  Guest speaker was Mrs N. Orford, wife of the Sergeant in charge of the Burra Police, who spoke on ‘The Life of a Police Recruit’.  Takings were $167.40.
