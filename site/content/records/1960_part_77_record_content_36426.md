Football.  Burra Senior & Junior Colts held their end of season dinner at the RSL Hall, Burra, on Monday 18 September.  Approximately 100 people attended.  D. Anderson won the award for best & fairest senior Colt and Robin James was the best and fairest Junior Colt.
