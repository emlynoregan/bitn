Silverton Mines.  Second article in the series.

There are descriptions of Thackaringa, 15 miles from Silverton where the chief mines are the Pioneer and the Gipsy Girl.  Then of Silverton Town where the nearest mine is about 1½ miles away at Umberumberka.  11 miles a little to the east of north is Lake’s Camp with a number of claims.
