BCS.  Ten students competed in SAPSASA Cross Country Trials at Victoria Park in Adelaide on Friday.  Two girls finished in the first twenty of their division and so go on to the State Trials in Adelaide on Sunday: Maddi Lehmann and Vivien Murphy.
