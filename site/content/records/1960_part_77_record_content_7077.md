St Mary’s will celebrate a ‘Back to St Mary’s Day’ on 2 July; it being the Feast of the Visitation of the Blessed Virgin Mary.  It will be a special day of thanksgiving for the recent restoration of the interior and exterior of the church.  Present for the occasion will be Rev. R.S.T. Pettet (Rector 1948-53), F.J. Hopton whose father was Rector 1918-21, K.T. Jenkins of St Peter’s College and Rev. R.S. Jenner of Burra Methodist Church.  Program:

11 a.m. Holy Eucharist

1 p.m. Lunch at the RSL Rooms

3 p.m. Thanksgiving Service

4.30 p.m. Afternoon Tea in the RSL Rooms

250 have accepted invitations and a large gathering is assured.

At the morning service there will be the dedication of:

A new side chapel.

The High Altar given by Mrs O.A. Tattersall in memory of her parents.

Altar candlesticks given by Mr & Mrs H.J. Wilson & family in memory of their son and brother Roderick Richard Wilson SBA who was lost with HMAS Sydney in 1941.

A flower pedestal given by Mr & Mrs H.L. Riggs.

And other sanctuary furnishings.

The cross and candlesticks are being made of copper to preserve the link with Burra’s past and pieces of malachite will be inserted into the cross; taken from the old mine and donated by Mr E. Finch, Mrs L. & Mr S. Kellaway and Mr H.J. Wilson.
