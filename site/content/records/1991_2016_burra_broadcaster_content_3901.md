Burra Hospital Auxiliary held its AGM in May.  President Avis Webster reported on a very successful year.  The two major fund-raisers were the Melbourne Cup Luncheon and the Riley Car Club dinner.  The former raised $2,000 and the latter $2,600.  Mrs Celine stockman was elected President for 1994-95.
