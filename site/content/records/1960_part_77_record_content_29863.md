Burra RSL

At a special meeting at the RSL Club rooms last Wednesday a new committee was formed.  Mr Wilf Nankivell was elected President.  Others elected: Vice-Presidents, B.A. Riggs & C.L. Phillips; Treasurer, R. Wuttke and Secretary, B. Kotz.
