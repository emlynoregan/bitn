Football.  Mr W. Brown has been elected President of the North Easter Football League for 1971.  Other office bearers: Patron, R. Collins; Senior Vice-President, W.B. Ashby; Junior Vice-President, M. Vandeleur and Secretary-Treasurer, A.L. Fillmore.  The season will begin 17 April.
