Mongolata Goldfield

Wildildie GM

Southeast Drive extended to 70ft on a vein about1½” and 4-5dwt per ton, but expected to lead to better.  New shaft to 22ft: no values at bottom & so stopped sinking and opened out following low values ore, but values declining.
