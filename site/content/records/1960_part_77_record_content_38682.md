Aberdeen Basketball Club held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs B. Stockman; Vice-Presidents, Mrs F. Ford & Mrs R. Steele; Secretary, Mrs R. Cullen and Treasurer, Mrs H. Kakoschke.  The club had a good last season, being successful to the finals.  The court is now lit for night play in a project which cost $693 and the court has been top dressed for $330.  A number of successful money-raising events were held.
