Mid North Regional Tourism Awards 1997 were awarded at the Clare Country Club last Friday night.

Richard & Jenny Holbrook won two awards:

Catering Services Cafés/Takeaways/Tea Rooms

Specialist Accommodation – for Griffith’s Cottage

The Bon accord Cottage won a commendation in the Specialist Accommodation Category for the second year in a row.  [2 photos.]
