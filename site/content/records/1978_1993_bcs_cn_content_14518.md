BCS did very well at the SA Secondary Schools Sports Association Track & Field Championships.  It won the Girls Shield in the B2 Grade.

Petrea Piggott won four events from four starts.  Cheryl Day and Adam Day also did very well.  Overall the team won more than 50 first, second and third place certificates.  At Easter weekend at Renmark at the Country Championships BCS also did well.

The haul of 51 medals included 23 gold.  Petrea Piggott got 5 gold medals and set five new country records.  Tony Secomb won 4 gold and 1 silver.  Ian Kleinig won 4 gold.  Other gold medals went to Tricia Madigan (3), Tasma Wooton (3), Darren James (2), Andrew Kleinig (1) and Cheryl Day (1).
