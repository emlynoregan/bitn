Burra Golf Club held its AGM and elected President, R.E. Mason; Vice-Presidents, D.A. Jones & D. Kempster; Secretary, H.C. Burnett, Treasurer, R. E. Jackson, Captain, R. Hillman & Vice-Captain N. Hurst.  Retiring President John M. Gordon reported on a successful year with increases in numbers of both men and lady members.  The introduction of grazing sheep during the playing period had enabled play to continue in spring.  The open day had been a great success.   Mr Stan Longford won the Championship.
