Council Elections – Candidate Profiles

Jim Quinn

Born in Burra and lived in the district at Mt Bryan all his life

Served on the Council for 8 years

Currently Deputy Chairman

A farmer with plenty of knowledge of the needs of the rural community, including the need for good roads and drainage

Well aware of economic constraints and an astute director of available finance
