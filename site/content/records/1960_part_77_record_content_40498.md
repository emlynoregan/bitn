Obituary.  Jeanette Dulcie Murray died suddenly while visiting the home of her parents at Mt Bryan during the illness of her father, Mr C. Rawlins.  She, her husband and family recently moved to a new property at Gosse on Kangaroo Island.

[Born Jeannette Dulcie Rawlins 3 June 1944, Burra: died 23 June 1968 Mt Bryan, residence Gosse KI.]
