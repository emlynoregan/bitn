The Burra Masonic Lodge installed Bro. T.R. Norman as Worshipful Master.  During the ceremony the ladies were entertained in the RSL Hall by Mesdames Jim Riggs and Darrel Field.  A ball in the Town Hall followed.  The Hall was decorated by Mrs Herb Riggs and Mr Tom Perry.

Mt Bryan School Welfare Club catered for the supper.
