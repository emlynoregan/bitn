Kangaroo.  Foreign visitors often expect to see kangaroos in out main streets and on Monday 7th August they would have been in luck: a medium sized grey kangaroo raced down Commercial St, through Market Square and Market St to enter the Mine Hill somewhere near Peacock’s Chimney.
