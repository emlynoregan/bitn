Burra Football Club held a wind-up dinner at the Burra Town Hall on Friday night.  Presentations were made.

Best and Fairest Awards:

‘A’ Grade	Barry Eberhard

‘B’ Grade	Morris Allen

Colts	Dean Brown

Junior Colts	Andrew Spackman

Most Valuable Player in major round ‘A’ Grade was Russell Nicholls.
