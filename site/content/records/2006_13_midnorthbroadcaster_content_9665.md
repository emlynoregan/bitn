The Hallett 5 Wind Farm construction was announced on Thursday by AGL Energy Ltd.  The construction of the 52.5 MW project will begin in July and be completed in December.  It will comprise 25 s88 V3 turbines, each of 2.1MW and will cost a total of $120 million.  AGL also confirmed its commitment to establish a Community Fund program for $15,000 per annum.  Hallett 5 is located 5 km east of Hallett 1, which opened in mid 2008.  Hallett 2 (71 MW) is in the commissioning stage and Hallett 4 (132 MW) is under construction, with Hallett 3 (80 MW) in development.
