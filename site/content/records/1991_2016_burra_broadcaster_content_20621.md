Billy-Jai Cooper, a Yr12 student at BCS has been selected for a 12-month Rotary Youth Exchange to Belgium next year.  She has been enrolled at a local school about 30 km from Brussels and will live in Ecaussines.  She is currently President of the SRC and was sponsored by the Clare Rotary Club.
