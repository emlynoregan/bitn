Burra Hospital.  Sir Lyell McEwin, the Chief Secretary, visited the Hospital on Friday afternoon.  He was met by the Board Chairman Mr R.K. Pearse.  He was accompanied by Messrs John Gebhardt and A.D. Radford.  They inspected the new building, the walls of which should be completed this week and also the kitchen, which is being converted from coke cooking to gas and the pantry, which is due to be enlarged to cope with more patients.  Sister Leader, a triple certificated Sister from WA, is due to begin work here from mid-July.
