Burra Food and Wine Club

A gathering of people at the Deer Farm last week listened to Glynne Ryan talk about the formation of a Burra Food and Wine Club.  The club would meet on the first Thursday of each month.  Membership would cost $25 and each monthly menu would be costed at $35.
