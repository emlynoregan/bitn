Burra Burra DC

The Council rejected the first stage of an appeal by the Kooringa CoHousing Co-operative to use the ‘Rotaloo 950’ in their planned housing development.  These toilets are approved by SA Health Commission and were recommended by the Council’s own Development Committee.
