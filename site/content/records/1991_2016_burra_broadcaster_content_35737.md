Goyder Council

SA Government Grant of $80,000 and a Council contribution of $8,000 will go to natural disaster mitigation.

Application has been made to SA Tourism Commission for formal accreditation of the proposed self-drive Goyder Tourist Route.

A report on the suitability of the Adchem site for industrial development will be presented to the February Council meeting.
