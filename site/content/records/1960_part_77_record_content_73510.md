Obituary.  Mrs Arthur Przibilla, nee Winifred Dew, died 16 November.  She was born at Burra 80 years ago and was a staunch worker for the Methodist Church before she left here.  She is survived by a husband and a daughter; Joyce, Mrs Clements of Bletchley.  [Born 7 December 1893 at Springbank, Burra.]
