Advt.  Kooringa Band of Hope Magic Lantern Entertainment in the Wesleyan Lecture Hall 26 May.

1.  Bunyan’s Pilgrim’s Progress - 37 coloured pictures

2.  The Ticket of Leave Man - 11 first class views

3.  Robinson Crusoe - 11 pictures

4.  A variety of amusing, interesting and innocent views

Hymns from Sankey’s Songs will be sung during the evening.  6d, children 3d.
