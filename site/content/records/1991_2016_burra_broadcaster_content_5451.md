Burra Red Cross ended the year’s fundraising with a Luncheon Trading Table and Christmas Tree Gifts.  They raised $480.85.  The branch has given $361.21 to the local St John’s Ambulance Service, which will put it towards a Heart Start 3000 defibrillator.
