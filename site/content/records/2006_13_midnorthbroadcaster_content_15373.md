Centenary of the Neales Flat Lutheran Church

Significant dates:

12 September 1912.  Foundation stone laid for the building; then known as St John’s Lutheran Church.

11 December 1966.  The dwindling congregation united with Trinity Congregation.

1966-2009.  The building was used as a hall by the Trinity Congregation, Neales Flat Youth, the Agricultural Bureau, CFS, and Farmers Federation among others.

11 October 2009.  The building was rededicated to become to Neales Flat Lutheran Church.

26 August 2012 Stella Schutz [sic] (nee Weis) unveiled the centenary plaque.  She had also been present at the laying of the foundation stone, having been born Stella Larina Weis 25 May 1911 at Neales Flat.

[She actually married Norman Ernst Schultz 31 October 1936 at Emmaus Lutheran Church Eudunda.]
