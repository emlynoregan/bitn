Australia Day in Burra saw a good turnout for the breakfast event on the creek bank.  Judy Eberhard received a 30 year service award for her work with Meals on Wheels.

Gus Wenzel accepted the Australia Day Plaque on behalf of the Committee organising the 25th Anniversary of the Opening of the Burra Community School, which was the Community Event of the Year.

Bob Greet received his award as Citizen of the Year.  [7 photos]
