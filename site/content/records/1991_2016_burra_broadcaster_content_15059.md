Vandalism.  There have been several incidents of graffiti around the town and of a vehicle doing wheelies damaged the newly painted tennis court surface, the Leighton Cricket Club pitch and a fairway at the Burra Golf Course.  Three juveniles have been reported for the graffiti and one of them for the property damage.
