BCS.  Principal Kevin Fahey has decided to move on after ten years at the school, making him its longest serving principal.  He came to Burra in May 1992 and has overseen developments including:

The establishment of Goyder House

The establishment of Brewery Hill Farm

The development of the Middle School

The construction of the swimming pool

The growing role of IT

He is taking 4th term off on long service leave and will tour eastern Australia on a motorbike, after which he will take up a metropolitan appointment in 2003, prior to retirement.  [Photo.]
