2 August 1989

Burra Burra DC.  Supplementary Election

There is a vacancy for the office of Councillor for the whole of Area.

Nominations are called by 18 August with an election on 16 September if required.

State Cross Country Championships were run at Carisbrooke Park on 26 July.

Sheridan Coad won the U15 Division 2 event.

Cheryl Day came 8th in 100 girls in the U15 Division 1 and Cheree Thame was placed 15th.

Mrs Reta Jennison is leaving Burra to live at Resthaven in Adelaide.
