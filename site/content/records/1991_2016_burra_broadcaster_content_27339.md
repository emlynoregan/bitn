Burra Art Gallery is holding an exhibition of Allyson Parson’s works, opening on 8 March.  Works of Allyson and Marie Parsons will be represented by originals and prints on canvas.  In June the Gallery will present ‘Outback Art 2002’, an exhibition by well-known Broken Hill artists.
