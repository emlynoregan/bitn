The Valedictory Service will be held in Kooringa Methodist Church on 10 December and will be a ‘Mod Service’ using modern music.  Speech Night will be in the Town Hall on 12 December and the Annual Banquet will also be in the Town Hall, on 14 December.  There will be a social in the RSL Hall on 14 December.
