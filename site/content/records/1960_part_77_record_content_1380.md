Obituary.  Mr Lyal Webber, who spent most of his life at Mt Bryan East, died in Adelaide Hospital 21 February 1960 aged 67.  He was buried at Centennial Park Cemetery, Adelaide.

[Lyell Willcox Webber born 22 April 1892 Hindmarsh: died 21 February 1960 at Woodville, residence Alberton.]
