Burra heritage

The following items have been added to the long list of Burra structures on the Register of State heritage Items.

Kooringa Bridge, Commercial Street Burra.

Former Storeroom, yard and boundary wall, Market Road.  [i.e. The Mine Store & Yard in Market Street.]

Cottage 13 Truro Street.

Cottage 11 Truro Street.

The Department of Tourism has approved a dollar for dollar subsidy up to $3,458 for:

Signposting the route from Burra to the Gorge at World’s End.

The Lookout Area of the Burra Mine
