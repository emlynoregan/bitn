Burra Traders’ Association held its AGM 5 August 1991.

Elected: President, Stephen Angel; Vice-President, Yvonne Barton; Secretary, Colleen Spackman & Treasurer, Michael Bradshaw.

Some of the achievements of note were:

Street fairs on long weekends

Sponsoring ‘Skate Board Day’ at BCS

Sponsoring ‘Anti-Cancer Foundation’ at BCS

Traders Directory

A Business Seminar

Australia Day Breakfast & Entertainment

Nine page feature in the Northern Argus

Donation to the Burra Tourist Association

Family Dance & Entertainment on Waster Sunday.
