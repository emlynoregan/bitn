Burra Branch of the M & BHA held a Past & Present Fashion Parade in the Burra Town Hall on Friday Night.  It was organised by Mrs Barry Eberhard.  President Mrs B. Riggs welcomed those who attended.  Mrs Jack Oates played melodies from popular musicals in the background.  The organisation expects to clear $80.
