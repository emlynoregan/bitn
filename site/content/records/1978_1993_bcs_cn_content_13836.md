Matthew Hall, formerly of BCS, graduated from Royal Military College, Duntroon, on 9 December.  His parents Carmel and John Hall of Hallett travelled there for the occasion.  Re received his commission as a Lieutenant and his Graduation Certificate from the Governor General His Excellency the Rt Hon. Sir Ninian Stephen.  He will be posted to Brisbane in the Artillery Corps for the next two years.
