Obituary.  Mrs Emma Maria Lydia Schmidt nee Launer died at Kapunda 20 February aged 78.  She was wife of the late Michael Schmidt and lived at Robertstown for many years.  There were seven children, 29 grandchildren and 44 great-grandchildren.  [Born Emma Maria Lydia Launer 9 July 1894 Robertstown.]
