St Mary’s Advent Carol Service was attended by about 40 people.  Fr Geoffrey Sibly came from Adelaide to play the organ and a there was a quartet of Rosemary Nairn (soprano), Jan Cockington (contralto), Alan Edwards (tenor) and John Stephenson (bass).  John sang six items and Rosemary & Jan also sang two duets.
