BCS.  Staff Profile: Cath Scally

I volunteered to be transferred to the country at the end of a two year position as Acting English Senior at Gepps Cross Girls H.S.  I had previously taught at Modbury, Marden and Para Vista High Schools.  Burra was high on my list of preferred schools.
