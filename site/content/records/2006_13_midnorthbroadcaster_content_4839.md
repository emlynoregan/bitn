Advt.  Glenys Christopher has an exhibition of recent paintings from her travels, entitled From Burra to Benicia.  It will be on show at Scarlatti’s Gallery, Mt Surmon Wines, Stanley Flat, from 9 March to 13 April.  It will be opened by Jessica Dames on Sunday 9 March at 2 p.m.
