Kooringa Wesleyan Church Anniversary last Sunday.  Dr Stephenson from London preached morning and evening and Rev. S. Knight in the afternoon.  The tea meeting was on Wednesday when the Treasurer, F.W. Holder, showed a healthy state of accounts.
