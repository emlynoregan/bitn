Bon Accord Bridge.  An interstate truck driver was fined for driving a truck carrying over 10 tons across the bridge, which has a 3 ton limit.

Accidents.  On Thursday a PMG vehicle driven by R. Spackman collided with a car driven by Mrs D. Cockington near the Burra Post Office.

On the same day a car driven by Herbert Fridd of Burra North collided with one driven by Mr G. Male of Burra near the modern Meat Store.  Damage to both cars was minor.

A dog or Dogs mauled five lambs paddocked near the Pig and Whistle crossing on Saturday night and the sheep, owned by Mr Chris Queissner had to be destroyed.
