The Melbourne Cup Luncheon on 3 November at Burra Town Hall saw 140 ladies enjoying themselves greatly with magnificent food and wine.  Proceeds were $1,019, of which $800 has been presented to Mrs O. Oates, President of the Hospital Auxiliary for special equipment for patients.  The committee also presented a traymobile to the Town Hall Supper Room.
