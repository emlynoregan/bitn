Secondary Schools’ Athletics Carnival at Olympic Sports Field on Wednesday 12 April.  Burra competed in the B1 Division.

Cheryl Day won 5 out of 5 events contested.

Sheridan Coad was outstanding in the shot-put and javelin.

Mandy Drew was superb in the sprint races.

Tasma Wooton was unbeatable in discus and javelin.

Adam Day won the 200 m and 400 m hurdles.

Sharee O’Bryan won her shot-put.

Rhett Heness won the long jump.

Nathan Waters produced incredible throws in the javelin.

Dennis Walker excelled in shot-put and hurdles.

Petrea Piggott excelled in her 100 m, 200 m, 400 m and 800 m races.

Natalie Taylor shone in the javelin.

Despite competing with much larger schools the girls’ team ended coming 3rd and the boys were 7th.
