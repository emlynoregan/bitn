Nick Jackman has been appointed BBH coach for 2012.  Jackman is a former SANFL player with both Norwood and South Adelaide and has played for BBH in 2010 & 2011.  The 28-year-old acknowledged that by taking on the role he has relinquished any aspirations of returning to SANFL football.
