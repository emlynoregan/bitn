Christmas Shopping

Late night shopping in Burra Wednesday 24 December.

Free movies in Burra Town Hall for the children from 6 30 p.m.

Merry-go-round afternoon and evening.

Father Christmas will arrive and distribute gifts after the movies at c. 9 p.m.

Sponsored by the Burra Traders’ Association.
