Accident/Obituary.  A 30-year-old man died when the car he was driving overturned about fourteen miles north of Booborowie on Saturday on the Booborowie-Jamestown Road.  Mr Robert Walker McFarlane (30) was a station hand of Gulnare and was thrown from the car and died in the ambulance on the way to hospital.
