Burra Small Bore Club.

M. Alderson, R. Kellock and R. Alderson were best scorers over 25 yards on Tuesday.

For the Championship R. Gilbert with 737.14 out of 800 defeated R. Kellock with 515.0.

For the Handicap Championship R. Kellock & R. Alderson on 800 met in a shoot-off in which R. Kellock 94.2 defeated R. Alderson 92.2.
