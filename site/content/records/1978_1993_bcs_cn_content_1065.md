Burra Hospital Board conferred Life Membership on Mr Eric Ashby of Hallett during a dinner on Friday night.  He served the Board for 22 years before retiring and was Deputy Chairman for nine years.  A presentation was also made to Leslie, Mrs Andrew Phillips who is soon to retire as Matron.  Her successor will be Mr Peter Davies, currently Matron of Meningie Hospital.  He is married with two sons.
