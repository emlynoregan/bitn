Christmas Shopping in Burra will be till 9 p.m. on Friday 24 December and late night shopping in Booborowie will be tomorrow night, Wednesday 22 December.  Father Christmas will arrive in Market Square at 8.15 p.m.  Shops will be closed on 25, 27 & 28 December and 1 & 3 January 1977.
