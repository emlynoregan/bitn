Burra apex Club decided at its last meeting to hold a charter presentation night on 31 March 1973, beginning with a buffet dinner in the RSL Hall at 6.45 p.m.  A cabaret will follow in the Town Hall with the band ‘Lighter Shade of Pale’, from Adelaide.  On Sunday 1 April visitors will have a Barbecue Dinner at the Aberdeen Club Rooms.  The club is presently small and needs new members.  The President is Mr Jeff. Hirschausen.  On Sunday members trimmed trees along Burra Creek.  An area from opposite the old power house to almost the Caravan Park has been set aside for development as an Apex Park where large trees will provide shelter for barbecues and picnics.  Meeting on the second and fourth Thursday s in the month at Burra Hotel.  Barry Griffith is the Secretary.
