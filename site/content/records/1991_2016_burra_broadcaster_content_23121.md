Burra Regional Tourism and Business Association

Double sided A4 sheet explaining the change of name from The Burra and District Tourism Association following a meeting on 9 August 2000.  The new name was felt to be more inclusive of all people involved in tourism in the region, both directly and indirectly.  One side of the sheet was a Membership form.
