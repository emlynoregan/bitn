Bowls.  At the close of the bowls season in Burra President Murray Stockman reported on an enjoyable season, even if no team had won a pennant.  He reported that the club had now paid back all debentures and was left with a credit in the bank.  Consideration was being given to alteration or replacement of the clubhouse.  The Easter Tournament had been a success.  Trophies were presented:

Championship Trophy	Brian Riggs

Runner-up Trophy	M. Stockman
