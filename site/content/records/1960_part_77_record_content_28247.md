Burra Auxiliary of the Adelaide Children’s Hospital held its annual meeting and Mrs E.T. Baulderstone was elected President.  Mrs A. Benger is the Secretary-Treasurer.  A quiz and rag sales etc. raised £58.  £70 was sent to the 5AD Good Friday Appeal.  The bank account at the close of 1965 held £65-2-3 or $130.22.
