Rod Phillips has resigned from Burra MFS after 21 years of service, due to work commitments in Adelaide. Rod has been officer-in-charge at Burra for the last seven years.  He was presented with a Certificate of Service on Monday night, the first retained fire fighter in the state to receive the award.
