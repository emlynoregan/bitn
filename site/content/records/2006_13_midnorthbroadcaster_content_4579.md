The Drought Bus of the SA Government visited Burra yesterday.  The bus carries information about the range of payments and support on offer to residents of drought affected regions, both from Government and non-Government services and agencies.  Since being launched in November 2006 the bus has served the needs of nearly 14,000 customers.
