Burra Pre School Kindergarten held its AGM on 17 February.  Attendance was poor, though 40 Children are enrolled this year.  16 people attended the meeting and elected: President, Mrs C. Brereton; Secretary, Mrs G. Webster & Treasurer, Mrs D. Ebsary.  The project this year will be to add new toilets and a washroom.
