BCS.  New Staff [With Photos.]

Russell Wenzel (Gus) is the new Deputy Principal who transfers from Karcultaby Area School.

Ms Kay Frearson replaces Miss Millard.

Ms Anne Hayes will teach Mathematics and comes from Pt Lincoln High.

Mr Roger Frahn is the new Technical Studies teacher.

Mrs Lois Davison is the new Typing and Business Studies teacher.

Mr Peter Quick comes from Nailsworth High School to head the Communications Team.

Mr David Schell comes from Lock Area School and specialises in Primary Physical Education.

Mrs Julie Bull becomes the Director of the Burra Kindergarten.

Miss Many Lanspeary teaches at the Kindergarten.
