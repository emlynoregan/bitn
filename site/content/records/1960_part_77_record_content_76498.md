Porter’s Lagoon Progress Association held its 1st AGM, bur attendance was disappointing.  Chairman Leo Robinson was re-elected, with G. Webster as Vice-Chairman and J. Bowden as Secretary-Treasurer.  Weed control in the Lagoon is a problem that will be monitored.  A film on the 1975 Porter’s Lagoon Spectacular was shown.
