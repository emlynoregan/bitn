Burra CWA held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs R. Alderson; Vice-Presidents, Mrs R. Jennison & Mrs E. Baulderstone; Secretary, Mrs W. Nankivell; Assistant Secretary, Mrs E. Hore; Treasurer, Mrs E. Pietsch.

The secretary presented the 46th annual report of the Burra Branch of the CWA.  There were 10 meetings for the year with an average attendance of 24 from 48 members.
