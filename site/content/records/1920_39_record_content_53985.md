Redruth Methodist Sunday School held a Children’s Fancy Dress Frolic on 8 December in aid of the Christmas tree, which in turn was held last Saturday in the Memorial Hall.  Father Christmas and Mickey Mouse arrived in a goat cart with a billy-goat in the shafts.  Details in ⅓ column.
