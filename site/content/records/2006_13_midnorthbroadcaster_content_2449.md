Weather.  Heavy rain fell, beginning last Thursday.  It is a blessing for livestock producers, but creates problems for croppers and viticulturists, with the promotion of summer weeds and mildew on vines.  The area received some 46 mm on Thursday, with another 40-70 mm expected over the weekend.
