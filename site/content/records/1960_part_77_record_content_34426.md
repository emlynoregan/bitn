Burra Rural Youth.  Bill Brown of ‘The Grove’ Burra was the guest speaker on the topic of the Merino Sheep Industry in SA.  Burra was the best area in SA for the Merino.  This resulted in the development of the Stud Merino Industry here and in the Field Days, which are unique in Australia.
