Aberdeen Football Club Women’s Auxiliary held its AGM on 19 April and reported that £768 had been raised during the year.  Elected: President, Mrs S. Scroop; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames I. Hirschausen & R.M. Reed; Secretary, Mrs R. Gill; Assistant Secretary, Mrs J. Wall and Treasurer, Miss C. Reed.

A gala or sports day is planned for this year and a paper drive will be conducted.
