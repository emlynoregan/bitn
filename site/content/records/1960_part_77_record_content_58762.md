Burra Primary School held a walk-a-thon on Sunday and raised $550.  The route was three laps from the Memorial Oval to Burra Post Office, Burra North and back to the Oval via Smelts Road plus 10 laps of the oval to total 10 miles.  168 starters took part and over 90 completed the course.  Sharon Lane topped the money collection with $32.50. Other notable achievements were Philip Turner, Andrew Spackman & Ray Irlam who completed the course in 1 hour 55 minutes.  Grade 1 couple Jane Hurst and Wesley Bratten completed the course.  Headmaster Sid Allen pushed a wheelbarrow over the course to collect $25.
