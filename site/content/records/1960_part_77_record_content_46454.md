Obituary.  The body of missing Peterborough man, Mr Noel Rowe, a retired grazier, was recovered from a 200 ft shaft at the Golden Junction Mine near Oodlawirra yesterday afternoon.  Mr Rowe had been missing since the previous weekend and his car was found near the mine on Friday.

[Noel Lawrence Rowe born 21 February 1919 Peterborough: died 15 June 1969 near Oodlawirra, residence Peterborough.]
