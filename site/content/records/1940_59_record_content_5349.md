Salvation Army.  Harvest Festival was held 17-17 March.  Major Jones of Adelaide conducted the Sunday services, all three of which were well attended.  On Monday the combined Redruth and Kooringa Methodist Choirs presented a splendid musical program.  Mr G.E. Dane conducted the first part and Mr W.H. Gare the latter part.
