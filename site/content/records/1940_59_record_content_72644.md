Obituary.  John Theodore Schuppan was born 6 October 1884 at Emu Downs, the only son of the late Frederick Schuppan.  He was educated at the Lutheran School at Emu Downs and was a member of St Johnâ€™s Lutheran Church at Emu Downs, serving it as a Treasurer and a Trustee.  For six years he was a member of the old Apoinga DC and except for four years at Lyndoch, was an Emu Downs resident all his life.  He died suddenly at home on 7 April aged 71 and is survived by his wife, three sons and two daughters: Clem (Whyalla), Mervyn (Emu Downs), Kenneth (Adelaide), Ivy Mrs R. Hartman (Whyalla) and Esther Mrs M. Mickel (Farrell Flat).  There are seven grandchildren and one sister.
