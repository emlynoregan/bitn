John Snell celebrated his 70th birthday on Monday.  He came to Burra in 1853 and began work as a miner.  He joined the Bible Christian Church and was involved in it till Methodist Union, being Sunday school superintendent for 33 years.  He has been a member of Burra Burra Lodge MUIOOF for 48 years and a trustee of it for 45 and been through the chairs five times.
