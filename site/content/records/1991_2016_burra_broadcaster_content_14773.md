Tourism

Richard Holbrook and Jenny Field won awards at the Mid North Tourism Awards Night at Clare for the Antique Clock Workshop and Tearooms and for Griffiths’ Cottage B & B.

Bon Accord Cottage received a commendation at the Tourism Awards Night.

The Council owned and operated tourist sites in Burra were leased to the Burra Branch of the National Trust – i.e. the Tourist Information Office, Mine Area, Smelts Site, Hampton and the Unicorn Brewery Cellars.
