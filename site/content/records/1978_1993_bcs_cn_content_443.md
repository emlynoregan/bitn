Dr Oswald, wife Chris and son Thomas move this week to ‘Heathmont’, their new home in Burra.  The former owners, the Pollard Family, are renting a home until their new home is built.  Dr Oswald’s partner Dr Davies and his wife and daughter are expected in Burra soon.
