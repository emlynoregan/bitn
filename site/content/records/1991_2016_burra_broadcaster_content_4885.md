Burra Regional Art Gallery

A provisional Board of Management has been formed to establish the Burra Gallery at the Old Telegraph Station and Post Office building in Burra.  Chairman is Judge Andrew Wilson.  The Burra Burra DC has been asked to approve proposals outlined by the Board: to refurbish the building and to convert its use as a gallery and art museum.  On the board are:

Tony Barker – farmer and Burra Burra DC member

Vincent Burke – former Marketing Manager of the Adelaide Festival Centre

Jan Davis – proprietor of Miss Mabel’s Cottage

Jojo Gebhardt – representative of the rural community

Andrew Gebhardt – former Burra Burra DC member and former Chairman of the Lewis Board (Paxton Square Cottages)

Susan Roche – Convenor of the Gallery Board and Committee.
