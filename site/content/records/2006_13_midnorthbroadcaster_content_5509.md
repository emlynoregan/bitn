World Environment Day was marked by tree planting at Redbanks.  About a dozen volunteers joined Ranger Kate Burton and assistant Russell MacGregor.  Approximately 600 seedlings of indigenous mallee trees, grown by Roger Cross and Jenny Carter, were planted into rip lines prepared by Arthur Simpson.
