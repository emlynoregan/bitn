St Mary’s.  Bishop Rosier and a number of other clergy gathered at St Mary’s last Wednesday when the Bishop commissioned Rev. John Morley as the new Rector of the Burra Parish.  The church was well filled for the service.  Members of other denominations also attended.  [Details in 2 columns.]
