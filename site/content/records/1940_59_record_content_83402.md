Advt.  Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. on instructions from the executors of C.M. Beckwith deceased will auction ‘Rose Park’ Merino Stud in the Mt Bryan Hall 3 December comprising 2428½ acres of freehold and 994 acres leasehold.  Sections 125, 86, 127, Pt 126 & Pt 141 Hd Kingston of 648 acres 2 roods 20 perches.

And sections 84, 77, 59, 76, 81 & 78N Hd Mongolata of 2,774 acres.
