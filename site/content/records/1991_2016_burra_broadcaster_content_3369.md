Merino Field Days’ Gala Night was held at Burra Oval last Tuesday and attended by about 700 people for the judging of the Merino Capital Champion Ram and champion Pair.

The winner in both sections this year was Orrie Cowie Poll of Wanoka.

A fashion parade followed the judging, with garments by R.M .Williams, Saltbush Clothing Co., Shetwynd Australia and Drew & Crewes.  The Elders Wool Fashion collection capped off the evening.
