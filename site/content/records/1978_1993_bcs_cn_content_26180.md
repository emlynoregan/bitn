The Prom last Friday saw guests from a wide range of other schools attend, so that more than 200 people attended at the Town Hall.  The Hall was impressively decorated in a diamonds and Pearls theme.  The King and Queen of the Prom were Andrew Hirschausen and Natasha Burton.
