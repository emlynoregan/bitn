Melbourne Wool Sales this week saw prices rise 2.5%.  Prices for Rec C. Warnes & Co. of ‘The Gap’ are cited and vary from 293½ cents per kilo for 8 bales AAA com. to 168 cents per kilo for 6 bales AA Pcs.
