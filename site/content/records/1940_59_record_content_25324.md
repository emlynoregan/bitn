A Welcome Home Social was held at Leighton for seven local boys on Thursday evening when a large number of people attended.  The returned men were: Flt-Sgt Robert Steele, Cpl Os. Heinrich, Cpl Dudley Field, Pte C. Jettner, Pte Eric Field, Pte M. Hogan, Pte E. Goodridge.  Mr Jack Field presided. Mr A.D. McDonald spoke to welcome the boys back into civvies.

Mr M.T. Fuller spoke as President of Burra Sub-Branch of the RSL.

Mr H.J. B. Jennison represented the Rehabilitation Scheme.
