Burra Primary School.  The Passing Out Parade for Grade VII is reported.  The official guests were the Mayor E.T. Baulderstone, Chairman of the School Committee D.H. Field, the President of the Welfare Club Mrs H. Topsfield, the President of the High school Council Mr H. Jennison and the High School Headmaster Mr R.V. Mueller.  Mr Baulderstone presented prizes for their essay on Local Government to: Elaine Broderick, Susan Barry, Merilyn Neale and Glenda Kotz.  Dux of the School was Tony Richardson and top girl was Susan Barry.
