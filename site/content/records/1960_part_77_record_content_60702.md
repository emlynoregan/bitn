‘C’ Grade (Juniors)	 Kooringa 7-53 defeated Aberdeen 7-45

Men’s Singles Championship	R. Nicholls

Men’s Doubles Championship	R. & A. Nicholls

Women’s Singles Championship	B. Hargreaves

Women’s Doubles Championship	E. Nicholls & M. Lloyd

Men’s Handicap Singles 	B. Hehir

Women’s Handicap Singles	B. Villis

Boys Doubles		H. O’Connor & P. Webster

Ladies’ Plate		W. Ross

Men’s Plate		B. Hehir
