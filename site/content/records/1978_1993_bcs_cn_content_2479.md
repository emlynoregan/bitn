Vietnamese Refugee Children’s Visit

Twenty children and three adults will visit Burra, arriving for the weekend on 6 June.  Friday afternoon will be at BCS.  On Saturday they will see a farm and then have games in the gymnasium.  Sunday will include films and games and lunch before leaving.  They attend Pennington Primary School and live at Pennington Hostel.
