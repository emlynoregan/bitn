Burra Pony Club launched its 35th anniversary celebrations on Saturday with a dinner for past and present members.  Inaugural President Ray Wardle attended.  He and present President Penny Maynard cut a birthday cake made by Burra Bakery.  There was a power point photo presentation and a trivia quiz to stimulate 35 years of memories. On Sunday there was a dressage and show jumping day, with another scheduled for July.  [Results of the dressage events are printed.]
