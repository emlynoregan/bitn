Burra North Red Cross held its AGM on 1 July [check this date]

Elected: President, Mrs I. Hirschausen; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames V. Cox & H. Jennison; Secretary, Mrs H. Jennison & Treasurer, Mrs Scroop (as she has been since 1943).  Mrs J. Henderson as retiring Secretary reported on the past year.  They had 64 members with an average attendance of 15.  They had catered for the Masonic Ball in October.  Red Cross Calling collected $57.30 and this, together with another $240, was sent to Headquarters for the March Appeal.  Our Patroness Mrs Heinrich was 91 in September and in November she received a long service medal.  Mrs V. Cox received a long service medal in December.  [Further details in the report of c.
