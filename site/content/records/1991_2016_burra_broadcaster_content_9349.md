Burra Jubilee 150.  Report of Chairperson, Robyn Page.

The celebrations for the sesquicentenary of Burra were spread over the whole year.

Burra played host to many local, state and national bodies during the year and promotions included:

A week long promotion in the Adelaide Arcade in August.

A display at the Royal Adelaide Show.

Events in which the Jubilee150 Committee was directly involved included:

Writing and poetry workshops conducted by Frank McMahon.

Commemorative Copper Trek Burra to Kadina, involving some 700 students, staff, parents and community members in May.

J150 Gem & Mineral Exhibition in August.

Art & Craft Exhibitions in August.

Visit by the Adelaide Metropolitan Male Voice Choir in September.
