Dennis Hillman from Year 8 at BCS has won a trip to Sydney in the ‘Powerade Kick For Cash’ competition.  At half time in the Australia versus Solomons soccer match there on 3 September he will get a chance to kick five goals from the penalty line and win $2,000 for each one that finds the net.
