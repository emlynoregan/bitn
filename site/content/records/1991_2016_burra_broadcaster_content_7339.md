Burra Burra DC

The Council has adopted the following rates for the financial year ending 30 June 1996.

Burra Township 0.5662 cents in the dollar.

Land outside township 0.4632.

A fixed charge of $155.

Burra Common Effluent Drainage Scheme charge of $147 per unit and $105 for vacant land.
