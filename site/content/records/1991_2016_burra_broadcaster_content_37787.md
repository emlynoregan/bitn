Evan Hawke attended a reunion of workers celebrating the 25th anniversary of the Tarcoola-Alice Springs Railway, held at Pt Augusta on Saturday.  Evan created the ‘Iron Man’ sculpture at the spot of the 1,000,000th sleeper.  Also at the reunion were Keith Smith, now aged 90, Commissioner of the Railways at the time, and Joy Baluch, Mayor of Pt Augusta.
