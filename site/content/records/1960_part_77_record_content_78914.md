Burra Burra Pistol Club

The second meeting of the Pistol Club was held 29 April in the Burra North Court House.  Former members of the Burra Small Bore Rifle Club informed the meeting that a club shed, other equipment and over $300 would be transferred to the new club in the near future.  A constitution was adopted and elected were: Patron, J. Oates; Vice-Patrons, T. Perry & G.R. Pearce; President, Sergeant B. Haynes; Vice-President, J. Newton; Captain, A. Pearce; Vice-Captain, L. Broad and secretary, N. Kingsley.  [There were a number of lady members.]
