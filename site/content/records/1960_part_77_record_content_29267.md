Burra Town Council, 4 April

Mr Pollnitz is to be invited to open the Caravan Park.

The Town Clerk reported the dissolution of the War Memorial Oval Improvement Committee with its assets to revert to the Council.

The RAA representative inspected the Caravan Park and suggested that Council apply for a survey for the erection of signs on the road approaches.

St Joseph’s Convent School held its sports day on Saturday 15 April.  [Results are printed.]
