Miss Valerie Terry was farewelled in the Kooringa Methodist Sunday School Hall on Friday 26 February.  Entertainment took the form of games, items and competitions and was organised by the MYF.  Mrs M. Satchell sang, Mr J. Carpenter gave a guitar performance, Mrs J. Carpenter sang, Miss V. Williams performed a piano solo and Miss K. Webster a piano accordion solo.
