Richard, Shaun & Dawn Dollman of Burra Motor Co. are presently refurbishing the showroom area of their Thames Street premises in preparation for Burra Video Store moving in alongside the auto parts.  The showroom now has 146 m2 of floor space and a disabled unisex toilet.  [Photo.]
