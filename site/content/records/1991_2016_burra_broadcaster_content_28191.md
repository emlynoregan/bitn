John Worrall of Eudunda writes to congratulate the editor on the brilliant decision to publish a new Eudunda Observer within the exiting framework of the Burra Broadcaster – separate, but together.  He agrees that the new road is another step in bringing the region closer together.

He also writes in support of Barry Wakelin’s position on refugees and their supporters.
