The Metropolitan Male Voice Choir will visit Burra as part of Jubilee 150.

The choir was founded in 1928 and has had many successes since then.  Its concert tours have included New Zealand in 1989, WA in 1991 and Wales, Cornwall & Southern England in 1994.
