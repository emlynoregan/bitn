News came in after last week’s paper that C.W. Gare & Sons of ‘Three Trees’ lost sixteen fat wethers, drowned when Firewood Creek came down and a haystack was washed bodily half a mile and lodged against the permanent way.  It was here that the train was delayed 1½ hours.
