St Mary’s will hold its second Music Afternoon on Sunday 24 July at 2 p.m.  Artists will be: soprano Miss Rosemary Reed, tenor Allan Edwards and organist Geoffrey Sibly.  Proceeds will aid the grounds improvement scheme.
