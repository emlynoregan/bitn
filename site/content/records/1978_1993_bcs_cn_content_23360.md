Thefts

1.  Sue’s Shoes was broke into over the weekend of 16-17 November and shoes etc. were stolen to the value of $2,684.

2.  A break-in at the Hanson Pumping Station on 23/24 November resulted in the theft of a radio, transformer and battery valued in total at $1,900.
