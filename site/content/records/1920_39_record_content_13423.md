Rifle Club.  AGM is reported in1½ columns and is the 17th Annual Report.  Cash balance in hand £18-3-11 and the targets and telephone are in good order.  Membership is 33, the highest since 1915.  This year the shed has been shifted to rear of the 700 yard mound and the telephone extended to the shed.  The club challenged this year for the best country club in SA to be decided on a neutral range.  20 clubs responded and the contest was at Pt Adelaide.  It saw Burra victorious by 36 points.  Prizes were presented by the Mayor, M. Collins.  Supper was in J. Allen’s rooms.
