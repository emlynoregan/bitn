Obituary.  Mr Eugene Rooke died on Thursday 29 November at Nuriootpa.  He was formerly of Robertstown and was well known in Burra.  His wife died in early October.

[Mr Rooke is hard to trace on the SA Births CD, being cited there as John Augen Rocke, born 25 January 1895, Baldina.  He is cited on the Marriages CD as Johan Eugen Rooke.]
