Burra Community School students recently won first prize in the senior category of the SA Research & Development Poultry in Schools Competition.  Year 9 students Anita Butcher & Kiara Carlier-Sosa [Photo] received the award for their info-poster ‘Growth rates of caged and free ranged broiler chickens.’

Their fellow students, Luke Roth, Thomas Drew, Chris Scutcheon and Neil Maxted achieved third prize in the same category.
