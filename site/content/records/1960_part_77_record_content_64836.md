Weather.  Rain fell over the entire district for four days and nights with falls of 4 to 8 inches.  The Eastern Road beyond Woolgangi is impassable.  Burra received 372 points.  The inside country received about 4 inches, Poonunda 455 points, ‘The Gums’ 417, but to the northeast Pulpara and Mallett both report 800 points, while falls to the east range from 217 points at Braemar to 606 at Glenora.
