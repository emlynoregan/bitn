Koonaburra Cricket Club honoured Ron Pearse with life membership at a dinner at the Royal Exchange Hotel on Saturday last.  Ron has been President of the club since its inception in 1962 and was captain for many years.  He began playing cricket at Burra in 1951.  Ron congratulated the team on their grand final win against Booborowie, which was also being celebrated that night.
