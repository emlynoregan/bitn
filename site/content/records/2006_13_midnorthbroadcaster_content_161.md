Yorke Peninsula Coaches has announced a new ‘intertown bus service’ to run weekly connecting Manoora, Saddleworth, Riverton, Rhynie, Auburn, Leasingham, Watervale, Penwortham, Sevenhill, Burra, Hanson, Farrell Flat and Clare.  This would incorporate the present Burra-Clare intratown [sic] service operating on Thursdays.
