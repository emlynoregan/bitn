Burra Arts Council has received a $2,000 grant from Foundation SA to fund a professional writer to assist in phase two of their Community Writing project for J150.  The script produced will be presented as a production next year.  They have appointed Mij Tanith.  She has recently worked on the acclaimed ‘Skaubryn’.
