Accident.  A car turned over on the main Eastern Road on 12 July.  It was being driven towards Woolgangi when about ten miles east of Burra a kangaroo leapt in front of the car.  The driver, George Curtis Peters of Erindale, swerved and the car rolled.  The driver suffered a bruised hip.  Damage to the car is estimated at Â£200.
