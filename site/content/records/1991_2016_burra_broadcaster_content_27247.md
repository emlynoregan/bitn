Burra CWA held its 75th birthday celebrations last December.  In January at the Australia Day Celebrations the President Eunice Baker presented a donation of $7,000 to the Burra Cultural Centre Committee to be spent on upgrading and refurbishment of the proposed Mary Warnes Meeting Room at Burra Town Hall.  Mary Warnes founded the CWA in SA at Burra in December 1926.
