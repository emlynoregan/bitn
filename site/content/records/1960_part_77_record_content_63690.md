Obituary.  Bill Hempel died on Friday at Burra Hospital aged 80.  RSL Members paid their last respects at the cemetery on Sunday.  [William John Thomas Hempel born 18 August 1892 Robertstown: died 20 October 1972 Burra, residence Burra North.  A Notice to Creditors 95, 3, 30 Jan. 1973, page 6 reveals his address was 10 The Crescent Burra North.]
