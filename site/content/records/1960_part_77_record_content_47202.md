A Boy Scout Troop may be established in Burra.  About 40 enthusiastic boys and six parents attended a meeting on Friday in the Town Hall.  The Clare Troop would provide guidance and leadership for the first six months.  The co-operation of a solid body of parents id needed and the willingness of persons to fill the vital positions of Club Leader, Scout Master and Group Scout Leader.
