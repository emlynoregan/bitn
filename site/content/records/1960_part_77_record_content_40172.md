Accident.  A vehicle driven by Mr Peter Moxham of Redcliffe Station slid off the Robertstown Road about 15 miles from Robertstown on Tuesday.  It was extensively damaged when it hit a bank of earth and a tree.  Neither driver nor passengers were injured.
