Obituary.  John Reginald Skinner, the Overseer of the Robertstown DC, died in the RAH on 18 April as a result of injuries sustained about five weeks earlier at Robertstown when a tree limb crashed onto his head.  He is survived by a wife and a son.  [Born 8 March 1919, Adelaide.]
