Dogs Attack Sheep.  Killer dogs have attacked sheep in Burra and Leighton areas in the past week.  In the early hours of last Wednesday 33 ewes owned by Mr J.G. Carpenter were killed and many injured in the Smelts Paddock.  On the same night about 30 ewes of Mr Ross Berry of Leighton were killed and others injured.  On Thursday morning Mr Murray Stockman was awoken by a disturbance among sheep in a paddock near his house.  Two sheep had been killed and others injured.  He shot and killed a dog near the sheep.  In all over $600 worth of sheep have been killed.
