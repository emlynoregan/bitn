Wind Prospect Pty Ltd has announced the commencement of a feasibility study into the Hallett Wind Farm Proposal.  A public exhibition on the proposal will be held at Hallett Hall on Friday 17 October from 3 p.m. to 8 p.m.  This will provide information and highlight the virtues of the farm and discuss any issues.  About 150 turbines are proposed, connected via underground electric cables to the power grid.
