Burra Football Club’s Victory Ball drew a rather disappointing crowd to the Burra Town Hall on Friday night.  Door takings were c. £64.  Kev. Howard’s Gawler Band provided the music.  Awards were presented:

Fairest & Most Brilliant	B. Eberhard & D. Villis (Tie)

Most Effective		K. Lane

Best Forward		B. Eberhard

Best Backman		I. Allen

Most Consistent		K. Lane & R. Jenkins (Tie)

For the Redlegs

Most Effective		K. Walker

Best Improved		J. Pens

Best Junior		L. Tattersall

Most Consistent		R. James
