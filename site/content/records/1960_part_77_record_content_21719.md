Bowls.  A meeting sponsored by the local RSL at Booborowie has resolved to form a Bowling Club.  Mr A. Pearce was elected President, Mr F. Dinham is Secretary, Mr H. Quinn is Treasurer and Mr T.J. Bailey is Patron.  Mr A. Seekamp is Vice-President.  The green will be located on the Recreation Ground.
