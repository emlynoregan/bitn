Burra Library held its AGM.  President J.B. Topsfield said the library lacked the support it deserved.  The year had been financially successful.  In the year Mrs Fennell resigned as Librarian and has now been replaced by Mrs R. Pritchard.  Membership is 52 adults and 7 juveniles.  Elected: President, J.B. Topsfield; Secretary, B. Kotz; Treasurer, Edith Carpenter and Vice-Presidents, R.J. Steele & Mrs Jean Kotz.
