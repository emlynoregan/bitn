Megan Brooks has been successful in athletics this year at National Youth Championships (Track & Field) in Sydney in March, where she came 8th in U18 3,000 m and 7th in the U16 3,000 m.

In April she won the Pt Pirie 12 km Fun Run.

In May she came 3rd in the Clare Valley Fun Run.

In June she won the Barossa 5 km Fun Run.

In July she won the Pichi Richi 10 km Run.

In August in the National Cross Country Championships in Melbourne she was placed10/44 in U16 Division.

In the National Schools Cross Country Championships in Brisbane she came 13/40 in the U16 4 km event.
