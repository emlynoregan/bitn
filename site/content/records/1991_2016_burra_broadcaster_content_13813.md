Goyder Council

Council will three tandem tipper trucks at a cost of c. $322,000.

Mid North Communications will install a commercial radio system in the Council area including 30 mobile radios at a cost of $52,000.

Council will cease to accept responsibility for the care and control of Julia District War Memorial Hall in the face of diminishing economic viability.

Alan Botting will buy the Burra tourist Bus at a cost of $20,000 over three years.
