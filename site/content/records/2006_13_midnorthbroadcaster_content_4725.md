Donna Kelly & Rosemary Seaford conquered Mt Bryan for the 33rd time on 26 January as part of their training to climb Mr Kilimanjaro in august.  On this climb they were accompanied by Mark Polley.  Ms Kelly and Mrs Seaford are both Community Nurses at Burra Hospital and have worked together for 20 years.
