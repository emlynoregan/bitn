Ram Sales in Adelaide were strong this year – averaging +35% on the previous year.

Spalding’s Moorundie Park had a high sale of $80,000 and an average of $13,813 for 8 rams.

Collinsville had a top of $26,000 and an average of $11,094.

Orrie Cowie sold one ram for $23,000.

Stockman’s had a high of $20,000 and an average of $7,300.

East Bungaree sold 9 rams for an average of $5,055 and a top of $9,000.

Sullivan’s Greenfields topped at $16,000 and averaged $5,778.
