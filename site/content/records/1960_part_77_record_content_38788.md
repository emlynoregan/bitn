1968 Stud Merino Field Days are listed.  The program runs from 1 April at Gawler to 5 April at Whyte Yarcowie.

Thursday 4 April features Hanson, Burra & MT Bryan with M.R. & E.J. Humphrys ‘Glenowie’ Hanson, W. Brown & Co. ‘The Grove’ Burra, L.W. Gare & Sons ‘Mt Glenview’ Burra North, John Collins & Sons ‘Collinsville’ Mt Bryan, Mackerode Pty Ltd ‘Mackerode’ Mt Bryan, M.A. Gebhardt & Son ‘Woollana’ Mt Bryan and Melton Stud Pty Ltd ‘Melton’ Mt Bryan.
