Miss BBH Beauty Contest was held last Saturday night at the Burra Sports Complex.  Contestants paraded in swimsuits and formal dress.  The winner was chosen by audience donations and first was Ian ‘Wanda’ Dolphin with a tally of $108.  Miss Hanson USA, Bill ‘Wilma’ Stockman came second with the total raised by the guys/girls being $490.
