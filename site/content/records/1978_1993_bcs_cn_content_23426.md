18 December 1991

Burra Hospital

About 300 people attended the Hospital’s Open Day.  The project just completed cost over $480,000.  Mr Ian Dunn, Regional director – Southern – of Country Health Services Division of SA Health Commission, officially opened the new Palliative and support Care Suite, High Dependency Unit, Administrative & General Offices, Nurse Station, Allied Health Rooms, Gerontic Area, Patients’ Lounge & Dining Room and after assisting Mr Quinn, Board Chairman, to plant a commemorative tree, invited the crowd to inspect the hospital.
