Burra & Districts Dog Obedience Club has celebrated its 5th year of training.  Dr Andrew Coffey gave an informative talk on vaccination, worming, hydatids and heart worm.  Other speakers were Gary Illicevs and Constable Vince Henderson.  At the following AGM elected were: President, Merilyn Baulderstone; Vice-President, Adrian Green, Secretary, Pat Svensdotter and Treasurer, Jo Baulderstone.
