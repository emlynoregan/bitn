Obituary.  Ralph Thomas of ‘Hogback’ Station died in Adelaide on Friday aged 73.  He had been ill for some months.  RSL members formed a guard of honour at the Burra Cemetery and bearers were A. Benger, A. Hempel, A.C. Ford, P.B .Byles, A. Wright and J. Murphy.

[George Ralph Thomas born 15 April 1891 Hundred of Wonna: died 22 May 1964, Adelaide, residence Hogback Station.  See a little more at 89, 25, 30 June 1964, page 3.]
