He included an article from the Westralian on his brother, Ron Sampson, who moved to 2,500 acres at Dalyup, 25 miles west of Esperance in 1952 and became a pioneer farmer.  Though he started with no experience he now has a property carrying 600 cattle and 2,200 sheep and has built a second house for a son-in-law.
