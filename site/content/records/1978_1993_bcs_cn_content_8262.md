Notice is given that by the Proclamation of the Governor in the Government Gazette of 7 October 1982:

Baldina and Mongolata Wards will be united to form the Eastern Ward.

The Eastern Ward shall be represented by one Councillor.

The number of Councillors for the Burra Town Ward shall increase from three to four.
