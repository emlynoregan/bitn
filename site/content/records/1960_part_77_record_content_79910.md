Advt.  Elders-GM will auction ‘Windarra’ 19 km southwest of Burra on 27 August in Burra RSL Hall on instructions from Mr R.T. & Mrs A.L. Bailey.  The property in the Gum Creek district has been in the Bailey Family since 1857.  It will be offered as a whole first and if not sold then in eight lots.

[The eight lots are described and other details are given in an advertisement two columns wide by ¾ column.]
