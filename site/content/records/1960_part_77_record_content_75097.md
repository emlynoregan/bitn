Burra Burra DC has obtained $9,000 under the RED Scheme to be spent on six basketball courts, one netball court and beautification of the Burra Creek.  The entire project will cost $13,000.  The basketball courts will replace six lawn tennis courts at the Burra North Recreation Ground and the netball court will be added at the Burra Oval.
