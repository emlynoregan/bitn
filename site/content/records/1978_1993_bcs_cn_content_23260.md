Burra Swimming Pool will open on Saturday 9 November, weather permitting.  Opening times are dependant on forecast temperatures for Port Pirie.

If over 25°C on Weekends and school holidays 1.30 p.m. to 5.30 p.m. & weekdays 2.30 p.m. to 5.30 p.m.

If over 30°C as above plus 6.30 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.
