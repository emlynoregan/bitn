Burra Small Bore Club.  Best at a double 100 yards shoot were D. Griffiths, M. Thomas & K. Spackman.

On Sunday the Annual Prize Shoot was held.  Best off the rifle were Jack Cattermole (Peterborough) and W. Jenkins (Clare), while G. Frost of Peterborough was best on handicap.
