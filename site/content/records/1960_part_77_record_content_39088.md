Advt.  Elders-GM will well on instructions from K.R. Phillips Pty Ltd 461 acres of freehold lucerne property at Mt Bryan on Wednesday 17 April in Burra RSL Hall.

Lot 1.  247 acres 2 roods 20 perches disposed as 9 paddocks with a five-roomed stone house etc.

Lot 2.  illegible acres disposed as 4 paddocks.

Lot 3.  94 acres subdivided into 2 paddocks.

Located close to Mt Bryan Township.
