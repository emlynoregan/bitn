Burra Burra DC.  A plan for roadworks at Burra North to link the Barrier Highway with the Burra-Morgan Road is on display at the Council Office.  Comments in writing from interested persons should be forwarded to Council by 28 March.  [This is later sometimes referred to as the Ludgvan Street By-pass.]
