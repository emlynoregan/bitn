Accident.  A Utility driven by Peter Dare of Mt Bryan East was badly damaged when it rolled over on the Mt Bryan East Road 12 miles south of Hallett at about 1 a.m. Sunday morning.  Peter received a fractured arm and was later transferred from Burra Hospital to the RAH.
