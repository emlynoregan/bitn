The Country Music Cabaret at the Burra Town Hall on Saturday night was a runaway success.  Tracey Rains and Claypan got the show started and others to entertain the patrons were Tanya Cornish, Nathaniel O’Brien and Jess Reid.  Much of the credit goes to Cr Adrian Gill for initiating the event.
