M & BHA held its AGM recently in the Burra Town Hall.  Dr Geoffrey de Crespigny was the guest speaker.  Overall attendance this year was 441 for 11 visits or an average of 40 per month.  Elected: President, Mrs Ray Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames B. Bevan & D. Holmes; Secretary, Mrs T. Thamm & Treasurer, Mrs D. Barry.
