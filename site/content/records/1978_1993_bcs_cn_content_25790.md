Burra Burra DC

Resolved that cemetery leases in the district will not be resumed.

Burra Swimming Pool.  Tenders for the new pool have been called by SACON.

The Heritage Advisory Committee will report on the future of the Burra Railway Station following responses from AN, STA and SACBH.

CFS subsidy reduced to 59%.

The Burra Record printing equipment needs to be re-located.  Ownership of the same is to be clarified with the National Trust.

Redbanks – the public meeting favoured the Council’s taking the reserve under its care and control.  Council will decide at its August meeting whether to pursue negotiations with the Department of the Environment & Land Management.

Ian Kleinig has obtained his Commercial Pilot’s Licence.
