Burra High School.  The prefects were inducted last Wednesday.  Headmaster D. Calder welcomed parents, staff and students.  Mr Shinkfield presented the badges and gave the occasional address.

Prefects are:

(Boys) J. Lewis (Head), N. Webster (Deputy Head), I. Billing, G. Kleinig, K. Farrelly & R. Shattock.

(Girls) Necia Mickel (Head), Anne White (Deputy Head), Carol Haynes, Louise Catt, Sybil Wright & Cheryl Heitbrink.
