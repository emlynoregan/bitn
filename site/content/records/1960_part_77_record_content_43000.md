Burra Hospital Funds benefited by $150 from a display of handmade silverware and pictures at the Town Hall on Tuesday evening.  It featured work by silversmiths John Hale and John Hooft.  Other items were on loan from churches.  The paintings were by Bruce Swann and 26 of the 32 were for sale and were bought for prices ranging from $18 to $50.
