Harry S. Blunt, stationmaster at Hallett has been transferred to Hamley Bridge.

Miss M. Hughes of the staff of Drew & Crewes for some 8 years has left to take a position in Broken Hill.

The Weather has been hot with temperatures exceeding 100°F since Saturday.  Rain had fallen earlier on the night of the 4 January when 41 points were recorded locally and to the east patchy falls varied from 10 at Oakbank to 95 at The Gums and Braemar.  The latter had 2½ inches on Christmas Day.
