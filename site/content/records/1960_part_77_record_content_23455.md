Burra Library held its AGM.  President Miss Eunice Tiver reported a successful year.  Elected were President, Miss Eunice Tiver; Vice-Presidents, Messrs W. Carpenter & P. Byrne; Secretary, R.W. Nankivell and Treasurer, H. Cornelius.  During 1969 the President reported they had taken out another box subscription and had added two new books each month.  The institutes Association presented 120 books and there had been private donations as well.  Membership was up 2 or 3. Subscriptions were 30/- p.a. and 4/- for juveniles.  The annual trading table brought in Â£52.  During the year all duplicate books were culled and a number of juvenile books were also culled.  Committee meetings were held monthly.  The committee greatly regrets the loss of Mr W. Lloyd and the departure of the Treasurer Mrs K. Treleaven, who has moved to Woomera with her family.  Mr H. Cornelius stepped into that role.
