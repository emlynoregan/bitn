Aberdeen Football Club held its AGM with 42 players and supporters attending.  R. James was appointed to coach ‘A’ & ‘B’ Grade.  Bert Seaford will coach the Colts.  Elected were: Patron, R.M. Reed; President, Bruce Stockman; Vice-President, B.L. Hirschausen and Secretary-Treasurer, Arnold Heinrich.  The club will play in the Mid North League and use SANFL umpires.
