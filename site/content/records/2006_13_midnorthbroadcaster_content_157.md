Burra Girl Guides.  Kylie O’Connor recently became the first Burra Girl Guide to receive the highest honour in the Guides Australia system, the Queen’s Guide Award.  The award was presented by State Commissioner Margie Berleman and State Development Officer Robyn Frost.  Activities that counted towards her achieving the award included participation in the BBH Netball Club, work at the Burra Visitor Centre and her achievement in being made 2003 Australia Day Young Citizen of the Year for Burra.
