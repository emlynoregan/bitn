Burra Callisthenic Teams have done well at the State Championships held in Adelaide.

Sub-juniors received four fourth placings and a second, to come third overall.

Juniors received two fourth placings, three second placings and a third, to come second overall in the state.

President Teresa Earle said these were very good results, with which everyone was delighted.
