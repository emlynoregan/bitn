Miss Stevens	Love I Give My All (Besley)

Strange Music (Greig)

Waltz of My Heart from ‘The Dancing Years’ (Novello)

You’ll Never Walk Alone from ‘Carousel’ (Richard Rogers)

Mr Edmonds	My Heart and I (Richard Tauber)

Take a Pair of Sparkling Eyes from ‘The Gondoliers’ (Gilbert & Sullivan)

The Exodus Song (Gold)

Serenade from ‘The Student Prince’ (Romberg)

Mr Tillett	Claire de Lune (Debussy)

Warsaw Concerto (part)

Mr Roy Jeffery moved the vote of thanks.

Mr Reed thanked the Aberdeen Tennis Club for arranging the evening and in particular Mrs S. Scroop.

The evening concluded with the duets:

Dare I Believe My Heart from ‘The Gipsy Baron’ (Strauss)

Nobody Could Love You More (Lehar)

Merry Widow Waltz from ‘The Merry Widow’ (Lehar)

Deep in My Heart Dear from ‘The Student Prince’ (Romberg)
