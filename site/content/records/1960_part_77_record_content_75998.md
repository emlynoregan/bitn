Burra Red Cross held its AGM on 8 July.  Membership is 31 with an attendance ranging from 5 to 15.

President D.M. Baulderstone and Secretary M. Rich reported on the past year.

Elected: President, Mrs E.T. Baulderstone; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames M. Odgers & G. Gallagher; Secretary, Mrs H. Rich and Treasurer Mrs H. Heitbrink.
