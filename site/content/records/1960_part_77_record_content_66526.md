Burra Burra DC, 7 May

A letter is to be sent to Samin Ltd and to the National Trust that items in the Mine are Council property to be held by the Burra Branch of the National Trust of SA and an inventory of items is to be made for record purposes.

A not is to be sent to the owner of lot 64 to remove a dangerous wall and re-erect it.

Mr Ian Auhl was thanked for donating a framed copy of the original Government Gazette proclaiming the District of Burra in 1872.
