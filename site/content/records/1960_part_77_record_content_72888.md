Burra Football Club held its Victory Dinner at the RSL Hall on Saturday night and a barbecue on Sunday at the oval for the juniors.  Awards were presented:

Best & Fairest in Senior Colts went to N. Van Buuren.

Best & Fairest in Junior Colts went to C. Taylor.

On the Sunday there was a scratch match between the Junior Colts and Old Boys, in which Junior 3.5 defeated Old Boys 3.2.

Netball Trophies

Award	Highest Goal Scorer

‘A’ Grade	Judy Beinke & Bev. Kleinig (tie)	Vicki Brereton

‘B’ Grade	Dianne Day	Vicki McBride

‘C’ Grade	Joanne Bernhardt	Sandra Spackman

‘D’ Grade	Sharon Brereton	Sharon Brereton
