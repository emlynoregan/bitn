Burra Tourist Committee is threatening to be wound up unless greater interest is shown ‘from a generally apathetic bunch of citizens’.  A meeting to consider whether there should be a Copper Festival in 1974, the match the 1972 success, is called for Thursday at 8 p.m. in the Burra Town Hall.
