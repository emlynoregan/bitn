Burra Burra DC

Council calls for nominations for Councillors by 9 May.

Retiring Councillors are:

Burra Town Ward	R.B. Jennison

R.J. Jeffery

Hanson Ward		D.L. Lloyd

Baldina Ward		M.T. Kellock

Booborowie Ward	H.B. Quinn

The recent death of Cr T.J. Quinn creates a vacancy in Mt Bryan Ward.

Council has accepted a debenture loan from the Savings Bank of SA of $5,000 to cover the purchase of a back-hoe and part of the cost of a tip truck.
