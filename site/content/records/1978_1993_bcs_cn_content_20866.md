Hanson Soldiers’ Memorial Hall

Tenders are called for the demolition of the Hanson Soldiers’ Memorial Hall situated on Allotment 145 in the Hundred of Hanson.  Fixtures and fittings will be removed by the Hall Committee prior to demolition.  Tenders close 11 July.
