Obituary.  William Percival Heinrich died suddenly at his residence in Farrell Flat on 12 May.  He was born at Burra, the eldest son of Mrs M.A. Heinrich of Black Springs and the late Mr C.A. Heinrich.  He spent his early life at Black Springs and began working there on his father’s property.  Later he was employed by Horwood Bagshaw & played football for North Adelaide.  After five years in the CMF he married Joyce Olds in 1945 and after his discharge from the army they lived at Porter’s Lagoon.  For the last 16 years he has lived at Farrell Flat.  He was a keen sportsman and active in the Vigilance and Institute Committees and with the tennis and football clubs.  He was also a foundation member of the Farrell Flat Golf Club and its secretary for five years.

[William Percival Heinrich born 25 December 1917 Redruth: died 12 May 1969 Farrell Flat.]
