Kooringa Masonic Lodge installed Gerald Pens as Worshipful Master last Friday night.  A ball followed, at which Ray Jennison received his 50 year jewel.  In February 2000 the Kooringa Lodge will celebrate the 150th anniversary of its establishment.  A weekend of celebrations is being planned.
