SALA Textile Display was held on Sunday at the Mt Bryan Soldiers’ Memorial Hall.  Items on display included quilting, embroidery, teddy bears, dolls, candle wicking and spinning.  Also there were colourful boots by BCS students in keeping with the ‘Year of the Farmer’.  Photos:

Shana Nourse, Joyce Lloyd, Margaret Walsh and Shirley Thamm.

Pat Douthwaite, Amy Berryman & Kym Apostolides.

Lynette Suridge & Daphne Lines.
