Burra’s Young Citizen of the Year for 2003 is Kylie O’Brien, for her services to the Girl Guides and being active in the community.  In Guides, she was Junior Leader in 2000 and achieved the BP Award.  In the community she has been involved in Callisthenics, Netball, Tennis, Swimming and the Burra Show and recently gained her St John First Aid Certificate.
