The Burra Bash Boys will enter a car for the 3rd year running in the Variety Club Bash for 1995.  The crew will comprise Peter Nesbitt, Andrew Nourse, Richard Dollman and Rob Blott of Gawler.  There will be a ‘new’ car for 1995 – a 1964 EH Holden Stationwagon.
