St Mary’s Burra had a congregation in excess of 300 for the confirmation service last Sunday.  The Rector Fr Barry May celebrated Holy Communion and led the service and the Bishop of Willochra, Rt Rev. Bruce Rosier confirmed the candidates and preached the sermon.  The members of the Choir of St Jude’s Church, Brighton, who were spending the weekend in Burra, led the singing.  [The 18 confirmed are listed.]
