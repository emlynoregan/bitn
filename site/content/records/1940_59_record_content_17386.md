The Hindmarsh Citadel Band (previously the Bowden Band) arrived in Burra on Saturday in the charge of Bandmaster Heaven and accompanied by Adjutant Gibbons and Envoy Winfield.  A concert was given in the evening at the Salvation Army Hall.  On Sunday morning the band visited Burra Hospital and at 11 a.m. was at the Salvation Army Holiness meeting and also at the evening meeting led by Adj. Gibbons.
