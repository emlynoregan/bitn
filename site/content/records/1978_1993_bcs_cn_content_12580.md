Burra Arts Council

Floating Palais is a production to be staged at Burra Town Hall on Friday 27 June.  It will be an evening of dance, fun and music.  Burra Arts Council also arranged for the appearance of Dan Burt, the one-man band, at the Jubilee Industry Train.

The Poem by Frank McMahon that was placed first in an Australia Day Competition in NSW We Will Now Sing the School Hymn, Burra (SA) 1940, is printed.
