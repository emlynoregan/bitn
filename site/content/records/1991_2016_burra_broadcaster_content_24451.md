Burra Picnic Race Club will continue after a recent crisis meeting at the Commercial Hotel on 31 January brought forth the necessary support.  Rising insurance cover is of concern.  It has gone from c. $400 to $1,000.  As a result admission prices will have to rise slightly and site fees for stalls may have to be introduced.
