Accident/Obituary.  Paraskiras Skafidis (26) a motor cyclist of Peterborough was fatally injured on the Adelaide-Broken Hill Road about a mile north of Hallett on Monday last week.  In a cloud of dust he had a collision with a car coming the other way.  His condition deteriorated in the ambulance en route to Adelaide and he died in the Riverton Hospital.  [Paraskevas Skafidas aged 27 died 20 November 1961 Riverton, residence Peterborough.]
