Military Matters

Leigh Holmes, craftsman and armourer, and son of Graham and Jan Holmes of Leighton, is currently serving in Dili, East Timor.

Lt Anthony Moorhouse, grandson of Aileen Moorhouse, is back in Townsville after a stint in East Timor. He recently graduated from Duntroon, as did Aub & Anne Finch’s grandson, Damien.

Tony Barker celebrated his 60th birthday on 31 December at Baldina Station.

Marriage.  On 3 January Julie Pawelski married Andy Munro.  [Photo]

Birth.  To Karen & Shaun Rainey on 29 December 1999, a son, Rhys Callum, a grandson for Daph and Peter Mengersen.

Birth.  To Tricia and Andrew Parsons on 2 January, a daughter, Caitlin Jane, a granddaughter for Betty and Ron Thamm.

Gerald Wetherall, branch manager for Elders, is returning to WA.

Tony Wetherall, (Gerald’s brother) of Elders Stud Stock, is also going to WA.
