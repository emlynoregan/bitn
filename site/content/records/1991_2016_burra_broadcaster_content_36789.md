Burra Red Cross held its AGM on 7 June.  Elected were: President, Fay Jones, Vice-President, Pauline Venning; Secretary, Pat Kakoschke; Assistant Secretary, Jenny Coatupe; Treasurer, Jill Lunn and Assistant Treasurer, Murray Tiver.  In the past year the sale of Bingo tickets through four hotels raised $4,725.55 and members and volunteers for Red Cross Calling raised $2,068.
