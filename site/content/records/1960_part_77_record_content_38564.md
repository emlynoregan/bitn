Burra Football Club held its AGM on Friday in the Town Hall Supper Room with 42 members and supporters present.  Elected: President, L.A. Beinke; Vice-President, A.G. Baulderstone; Secretary, Russell Nicholls and Treasurer, G. Short.  Outgoing President E.C. Pietsch reported on a successful year financially and socially. [Thanks are expressed all round.]  Special thanks went to Coach, Barry Eberhard; Colts Manager, Tony Fillmore; Colts Coach, Jimmy James and his assistant Max Anderson and to Malcolm Beinke for wiring the new training lights without charge and to Mr Fuss for alterations and changes to the dressing rooms.  Congratulations went to Barry Eberhard for winning the Mail Medal for the second year in succession and for winning the Umpiresâ€™ Trophy for the State.  Significant costs for the year had been the installation of quartz floride [sic] lights at $518, new guernseys at $142, changes to the dressing room $63 and contribution to oval improvements $400.
