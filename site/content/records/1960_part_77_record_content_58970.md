Burra Golf Club held its AGM on 11 November and elected: President, R.E. Mason, Vice-Presidents, Don Kempster & I. Allen; Captain, R. Hillman; Vice-Captain, N. Hurst, Secretary, H.C. Burnett and Treasurer, S. Allan.

At the start of a successful year the scrapes were re-slagged and oiled and new cups were installed.  The open day was a success.
