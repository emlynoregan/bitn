BRTBA will hear Goyder Council CEO at its meeting on 28 August.  Steve Kerrigan will explain to them the CMC (Community Management Committee) concept.  At present all the 13 townships in Goyder have a CMC except for Burra.  BRTBA members will be asked to vote on the idea.  The CMC would provide a direct link between the community and the Council
