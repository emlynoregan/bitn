Notice.  Regional Council of Goyder has commenced a review of road names within the Council area.  It is considering renaming the entire road from Eudunda via Julia, Dunstan to Ngapala Road, Burra Road and Koonoona Road to become the Eudunda Road.  Ward plans are available at the Council offices at Burra & Eudunda.
