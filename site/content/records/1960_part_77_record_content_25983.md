Burra Colts Football Club held its wind-up dinner at the RSL Hall on Saturday.  Trophies were presented:

Fairest & Best	P. Brown

Best Backman	G. Nankivell

Best Forward	G. Benger

Most Improved	G. James

Most Consistent	S. Anderson

In the Junior Colts trophies went to K. Spackman, D. Spackman and D. Bogisch.
