Burra Basketball Club held it AGM, but only nine members attended.  Elected: President, Mrs A. Day; Vice-President, Miss J. Pens; Joint Secretaries, Mrs M. Anderson & W. Anderson.  This year it is intended to field one team in each of the A, B, & C grades.
