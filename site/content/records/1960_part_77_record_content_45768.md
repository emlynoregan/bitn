Lutheran parishioners of Blyth, Clare, Robertstown & Burra will gather at the Burra Town Hall on Sunday morning for a special service.  Special guests will be Rev. Martin Winkler, Director of Broadcasting for the Australian Lutheran Hour and Rev. Ron Gerhardy, Director of Missions in the South-West Pacific.  A luncheon will follow the 10.30 a.m. service.  The Blyth-Clare-Burra Parish Pastor is Rev. C.A. Scherer.
