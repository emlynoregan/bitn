It seems that sporting bodies will have to contribute more to the upkeep of recreation facilities.

Twelve model by-laws have been prepared by the Clerk for Council and for submission to the Local Government Office and the Crown Solicitor’s Office.  They cover:

Hoardings	Petrol Pumps	Proceedings of Council

Slaughter Houses	Traffic	Bees

Garbage Bins	Control of Horses	Meetings of Ratepayers

Nuisances	Water Reserves	Tents
