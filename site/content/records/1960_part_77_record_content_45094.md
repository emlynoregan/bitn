Burra Bowls Assoc. held its AGM 9 April and elected President, Pat Field; Vice-president, Ellen Scott; Junior Vice-President, Zita Brown; Treasurer, Laurel Neill; Secretary, Dorrie Pietsch.  Membership in 1968 was 26.  Foundation member Ida Harris died during the year.  Two teams were entered in pennant matches.  Pat Field was Club Champion.
