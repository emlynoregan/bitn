Rock and Roll Weekend

Numbers were up for last weekend’s festival.  Over 250 attended the dance at Redruth Gaol on Saturday night.  Burra accommodation was about ¾ full.  On Friday night Paul O’Bryan’s Band played till late at the Burra Hotel and they also played for the Rock ’n’ Roll dancing in Market Square on Saturday morning.

Mark Harvey, who has been learning the building trade from Paul Wilson of Burra and Gary Price of Clare, has been named the Housing Industry Association’s Apprentice of the Year for 2002.  He is one of 135 apprentices the HIA is responsible for in SA.  [Photo]
