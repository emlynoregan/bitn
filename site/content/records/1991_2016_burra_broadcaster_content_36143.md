Letter.  Jill Wilson writes to say how much she loves living in Burra.  There is always something to do.  She outlines her weekend as proof of that:

Friday night	Watched two top class movies at Redruth Gaol

Saturday		Manicure at the local beauty salon, thanks to a birthday voucher

Shopped at the local supermarket and fruit& vegetable shop

Had a pot of tea with friends at a local eating house

Went for lunch at a tennis team member’s home

Made a dessert to take to the Burra Tennis Club’s Presentation Night

Sunday		Morning church service followed by a lunch cooked by the Sunday school children

Afternoon bike ride on the bike track along Burra Creek
