Burra Burra DC

Steps are to be taken to sell the Market Square Café for non-payment of rates.

Other properties in the same position are to be placed in the hands of the solicitor within two months.

It was resolved to approach the Department of Lands with a view to closing the Towns of Douglas and Tracy.

Council accepted plans for the car park opposite the Town Hall, subject to cost estimates being approved.

The Lutheran Church sought assistance with flood control.

Burra Pistol Club.  A. Pearce was the winner of the Kamere Trophy for marksman.  M. Broad was most improved lady and G. Irlam most improved gentleman.  Best on the night were A. Pearce, L. Broad, L. Whitrod and M. Stolte.
