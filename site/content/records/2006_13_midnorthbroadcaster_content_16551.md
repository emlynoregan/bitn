Burra Junior Tennis Club had two teams in the Burra & Districts Tennis Association for the last season.  Aberdeen and Kooringa each boasted eight players.  The club enjoyed a great season.  Awards were given out at the end-of-season family barbecue on 24 March:

Kooringa

Most Consistent	Patrick Connell

Runner-up	Hayden Webster

Junior Encouragement Award 	Annie Kellock

Aberdeen

Most Consistent	Sean Millward

Runner-up	James Lines

Junior Encouragement Awards	Alastair Clarke.
