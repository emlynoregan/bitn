Obituary. Mr Frank Richards of Goodwood died 25 May.  His father was stationmaster at Mt Bryan many years ago and the late Mr Richards was a soldier in WWI and married one of the Misses Quinn of Mt Bryan.

[Frederick Francis Watts Richards born 26 November 1895 Littlehampton: died 25 May 1971 Goodwood.  He married Ellen Veronica Quinn, daughter of W.H. Quinn.]
