The Back to Burra Golfers Weekend was the J150 celebration for the Burra Golf Club.  It proved a great success.  A stableford event was held on Saturday and an ambrose event on Sunday.  A social night was held on Saturday, at which life membership was bestowed on Ray Jennison, John Nankivell and Frank Fennell.  A memorial to late Life Member Jack Bogisch was unveiled on Sunday morning.  [Results printed on page 11.]
