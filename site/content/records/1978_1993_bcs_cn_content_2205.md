BCS.

At the Interschool Swimming Carnival at Riverton on 11 March Burra lost the shield narrowly to Eudunda: Eudunda 111, Burra 102 and Kapunda 84.  [Individual results for Burra are printed.]

Burra Community Library has introduced a new microfiche reader for cataloguing information and discovery of current books published.  [Photo of Colin Fidock with the microfiche reader.]

Mrs Sue Scholz of Burra has become an entrant in the Mrs South Australia, Woman ’81 Quest held annually to raise money for the Crippled Children’s Association Regency Park Centre.

27 March 1980
