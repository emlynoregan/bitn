BCS students and those from other schools in the region went to Morgan on 19 September for a special day of celebration involving paddle steamers and other craft as part of the Centenary of Federation Source to Sea Voyage.  There was also Aboriginal dancing, horseshoe throwing and wool bales being loaded.
