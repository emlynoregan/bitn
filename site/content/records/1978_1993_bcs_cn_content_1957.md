Burra National Trust held its AGM last Monday.

Elected: President, Dudley Cockington; Vice-Presidents, Gerry O’Connor and Graham Heinrich; Secretary, John Walkley and Treasurer, Mrs Jan Culbertson.

A committee was formed to deal solely with the needs of the Redruth Gaol.

A ladies’ committee was set up to handle a major fund-raising drive.

Mrs Ruth Bateman has been elected Guide Officer to organise the provision of guides for buses.
