Football.  A medal awarded to Max Morton and engraved ‘B.F.A. Premiers 1912, M.J. Morton’ has recently been reported as found, according to Thursday’s Advertiser, by an unnamed man.  The family is unable to account for how it came to be lost and subsequently found.  [Details in ½ column.]
