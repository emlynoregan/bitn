Basketball.  Burra ‘C’ and ‘D’ Grades held a wind-up on Saturday.  A variety of trophies are listed: Jill Napper was Best & Fairest in ‘C’ Grade and Sybil Wright Best & Fairest for ‘D’ Grade.

The ‘A’ & ‘B’ Grade wind-up dinner will be held 18 October when the Best and Fairest trophies will be presented to Dianne Day for ‘A’ Grade and to Ann Matthews for ‘B’ Grade.
