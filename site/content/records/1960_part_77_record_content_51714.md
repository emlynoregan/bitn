Notice.  Burra Burra DC gives notice of intention to borrow $75,000 by means of a debenture loan over 15 years at 7.4% p.a. in order to:

Construct new Council Chambers and an administrative block.

Renovate the Burra Town Hall frontage.

Relocate the Burra Library to the present Council Chambers at 14 Commercial Street.

Install new toilets in the Burra Town Hall.
