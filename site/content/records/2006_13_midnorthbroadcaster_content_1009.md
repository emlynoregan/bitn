Meals on Wheels held its 2006 Regional Conference last Tuesday in the newly refurbished Burra Town Hall.  Guest speaker was Margaret Rohan Kelly, who spoke of ‘The Road to Happiness’.  The organisation has some 10,000 volunteers in SA.
