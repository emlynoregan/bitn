Burra Basketball Club held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs M .Beinke; Vice-President, Mrs L. Beare and Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs A. Day.  There will be five teams this year – Two ‘A’ Grade, and one in each of ‘B’, ‘C’ & ‘D’ Grades.  Heather Angel will coach the senior teams and Dianne Day the ‘D’ Grade.
