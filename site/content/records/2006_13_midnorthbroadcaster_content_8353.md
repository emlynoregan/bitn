Victorian Variety Bash entrants made an unscheduled stop in Burra on Friday afternoon.  They were en route from Wentworth to Clare for the night and decided to spend some time in Burra.  The vehicles and characters attracted plenty of attention.  After Clare their route took them to the Flinders Ranges and Alice Springs via Coober Pedy.
