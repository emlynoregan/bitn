Burra Town Council, 26 November

The Mayor reported that the old Redruth Gaol had been improved.

The Swimming Pool Committee was working well and a Ladies’ Committee had been formed.

The Oval Committee was functioning well.

It was suggested that a committee to run the Town Hall be formed next year.  Left in the hands of the Mayor.

It was resolved that the pictures on loan to the National Trust remain on loan only to the National Trust and are to be retained at the Burra Museum at the pleasure of the Corporation.  The action of repair is endorsed.

The closure of a road at the cemetery was in the hands of surveyors.

The renting out of the Corporation house was left in the Mayor’s hands.
