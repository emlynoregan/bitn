Pat Seigert, formerly of Hallett, was awarded a National Distinguished Medal for Leadership at the Red Cross State Conference.  She was active in Red Cross for many years at Hallett and since moving to Adelaide has been a member of the Kensington Branch, where she has been secretary for many years.
