Burra High School.  Jubilee.  Two photos:

A.K. Statton unveiling the plaque to commemorate the opening of the High School Jubilee Sports Area.

The old scholars who took part in the ‘Back to School Class’ with former Headmaster L.W. Carthew.

Mr & Mrs R.W. Humphrys of ‘Bonnie Brae’ Leighton celebrated their Golden Wedding.  Formerly of ‘Blink Bonnie’ Burra, they were married at Hanson Methodist Church 11 September 1912 by Rev. W.J. Bainger.

The Burra Show will be opened on 6 October by SA Commissioner of Police Brigadier J.G. McKinna.
