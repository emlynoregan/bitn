The last year was successful with an average attendance of 13.  In August a street stall raised $40.42.  Catering for the Masonic Ball in October raised $100.70.  Headquarters was given $400 to purchase a medikit to send to Asia.  Eight Christmas cakes were sent for distribution to sick ex-servicemen and women.  $220 was sent to Headquarters for their March Appeal.
