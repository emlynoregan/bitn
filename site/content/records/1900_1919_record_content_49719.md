Burra Cheer-up Society gave a farewell social on Monday for Driver S.M. Lane.  Driver Lane is in the artillery and leaves for Melbourne and thence for the front this week.  He was presented with a pair of field glasses and £13-£14 as the balance of donations received.  [¾ column of detail.]
