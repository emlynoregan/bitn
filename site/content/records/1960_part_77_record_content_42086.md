Burra High School Parents & Friends’ Assoc. had c. 100 to a meeting on Thursday to hear about courses for 1969 and likely changes in the coming years and to discuss the possibility of building a recreation room for students.  It was decided to investigate this further.  New staff member Miss Wilkinson was introduced and Senior Mistress Miss Newman spoke of the new summer uniform for girls, to be introduced in 1969.
