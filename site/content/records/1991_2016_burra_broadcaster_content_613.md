‘Four on the Floor’, a local band, made their public debut at Mt Bryan Hotel last Saturday night.  They had previously appeared there in June at a private function.  They comprise Mick Bradshaw, guitar, vocals and drum machine; Cory Heneker, Guitar; Reno Krause, bass and Kerry Green, vocals.
