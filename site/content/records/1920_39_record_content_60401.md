Mongolata Goldfield

Curlew Claim has crushed c. 26 tons for over 54 oz with tailings yet to come.

Terry’s Claim has crushed 6 tons 13 cwt for almost 9 oz.

Wildildie crushed 2½ tons for about 8 oz.
