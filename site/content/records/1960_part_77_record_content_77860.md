District School Enrolments 1876

Hallett: 9 new enrolments are listed.  Total enrolment is 74, which is up from 68 in 1975.

Booborowie School opened with 78 children, including 11 in Year I and those are listed.

Robertstown had 66 on the roll in 1975 and 65 in 1976, to become 70 by the end of March.  New students are listed.

Farrell Flat total enrolment is up from 22 in 1975 to 28 this year.  New Year I students are listed.
