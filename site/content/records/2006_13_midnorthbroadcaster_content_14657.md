Les Warnes who has been a member of the National Trust for 45 years has received an Honour Award.  Among other things, he helped with the relocation of Peacock’s Chimney in the 1970s and has cared for Burra’s three fire engines over the years.  He has made a number of donations to the Trust and still teaches other to drive the fire engines.
