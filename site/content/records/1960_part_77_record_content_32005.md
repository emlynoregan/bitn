St Stephen’s Catholic Church at Saddleworth was officially re-opened on Sunday after extensive renovations.  Special guests included His Grace Archbishop Gleeson, the Premier Mr Frank Walsh MP, Messrs J.S. Freebairn MP and P.H. Quirke MP.  The service was conducted by Rev. Fr K. Hay.
