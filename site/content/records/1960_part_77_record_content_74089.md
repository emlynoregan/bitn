Obituary.  Mr John Harold Torr aged 76, formerly of Farrell Flat, died 22 December 1974 in Adelaide.  In the district he farmed ‘Torlnga’; land first taken up by his grandfather.  He was born in Farrell Flat and spent most of his life there.  He was the son of Sydney and Ann Torr and was educated at Mintaro School.  Later the family moved to Gawler and Harold then attended Prince Alfred College.  In 1922 he married Clarice Denton of Farrell Flat.  The eldest of their five children, Glen, lost his life on Pathfinder operations with the RAAF in WWII.  Harold was interested in sports and served on many community committees.  He retired to Glenelg six years ago and spent much of his time there fishing.  He is survived by his wife and daughter Enid, Mrs Blieschke (Leighton); Brian Adelaide; Peter (Sydney) and Ern (Farrell Flat).  [Born at Farrell’s Flat 4 June 1898.]
