Burra Primary School opened with 244 on the roll.  Mr Sullivan, Mrs Sladden and Miss Coventry were replaced by Mr King, Miss Langsford & Miss Reed.  The erection of the sick bay has been completed.  31 infants were enrolled on Tuesday and are listed.
