Marriage.  19 April

Marilyn Hirschausen married Robert Brandt.

Robert has scored over 1,000 runs, including four centuries and a top score of 183, for the season, as captain of the Koonaburra team.  Yesterday he began work at Woomera where the couple will live after their marriage.
