Fire.  The north bound bus of Mortons of Broken Hill caught fire at Hallett on Sunday about 7.30 due to an engine fault.  Some lady passengers suffered shock, but all 45 passengers escaped along with most of the luggage.  The bus was completely burned out and damage is estimated at $24,000.  The EFS put out the fire.
