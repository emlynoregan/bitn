Country Music

Mandy Giles, formerly of Burra, won heat number 4 of the Telstra Road to Tamworth Competition at Pt Pirie recently.  There are 27 heats throughout Australia and each heat winner receives $1,000 to help them get to the 2007 Tamworth Country Music Festival.

Nathaniel O’Brien of Burra participated in the Pt Pirie Country Music Festival recently, where he won the 11-12-year-old and 15 and under gospel sections.

His sister Tracey Rains was placed 3rd in both the country/rock and gospel sections.

His father, Danny O’Brien won the open male section.

Jess Reid of Burra came 3rd in the traditional section.
