Methodist Transfers

Rev. Milton Edwards will move to Elizabeth.

Rev. & Mrs C.F. Hore will come to Burra in January.

From 1972 Hallett will move into the Jamestown Circuit and Geoff. & Blanche Diprose and family will move from Hallett to Karoonda.
