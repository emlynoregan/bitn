Burra RSL President T. Roger’s Report for 1985

Nine meetings were held and 62 members attended at some time during the year.  Four badge days were conducted and a Dawn Service was held on Anzac Day and a Remembrance service on 11 November.  There was an annual dinner at the Burra Hotel.
