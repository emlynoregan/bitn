Burra Hospital.  Mr & Mrs Cliff Lucas of Booborowie were recent patients and have donated an electrically adjustable bed to the hospital.  They have offered one additional bed for every three donated by the community.   The Ladies’ Auxiliary has taken up the challenge.  The beds cost $2,000 each.
