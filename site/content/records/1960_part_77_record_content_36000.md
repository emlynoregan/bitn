Geoff Dare of Mt Bryan East has won a trip to the USA next year.  He is 21 and a prominent Rural Youth Club member, being President 1965-66.  He recently won a Leadership Badge and was a member of the Burra ‘A’ Grade Debating Team.  He is editor of the Zone Magazine ‘Maunari’.  There were 70 applicants for three trips.
