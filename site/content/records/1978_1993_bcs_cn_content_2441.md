Burra Copper Festival 1980

The Submissions Committee met on 30 April to consider which project should benefit from any profits from the 1980 Festival.  It was decided to allocate all the profits to the Burra Burra DC for exclusive use in maintaining the water supply in the Burra Creek once mining operations cease.
