Hanson Agricultural Bureau arranged for a soil school at Hanson Hall conducted by Mr Gavin Young, the soils advisor with the Department of Agriculture, stationed at Jamestown.  There was a field day on 9 August attended by 30 farmers.  The course comprised two field days and six lectures, one night a week for six weeks.
