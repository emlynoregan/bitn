Mr Dean Brown, the new Minister for Trade & Industry visited Burra last Saturday morning.  He and Mrs Brown were in Burra for a function at the Burra Town Hall organised by the Burra Sub-branch of the Liberal Party.  Mr Brown indicated that the Public Buildings Department intends to install a solomit strawboard ceiling along the line of the existing rafters and the suspended luxolon ceiling would be completely removed, a new ceiling fitted and the walls repainted.  The work on both ends of the building would be completed for the start of the new school year.
