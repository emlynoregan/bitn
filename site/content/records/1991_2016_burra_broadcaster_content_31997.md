The Tourism forum at Burra Hotel on Wednesday 17 September will be addressed by three speakers:

Wendy Smith, IT Manager for SA Tourism Commission will speak on Atlas and IT Systems.

Anthony Perre, Tourism Accreditation Manager will address the value of accreditation.

David Row of SA Tourism Commission Industry Department will present an inspirational video on Pike’s Fish business.
