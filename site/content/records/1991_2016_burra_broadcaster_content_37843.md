Kooringa Masonic Lodge.  Last Tuesday 18 October Ashley Woodgate was presented with his 50 year jewel by Worshipful Master Gerald Pens.  Ashley was initiated into Hallett Lodge No. 100 on 12 October 1955, and attended it until it closed in 1978.  He then joined the Lodge of Concord 27 at Terowie until it too closed in the 1990s.  He then joined Kooringa Lodge No. 6.
