First Aid at Burra was active in 1998, with 101 people of the district added first aid to their skills.  35 from Booborowie completed CPR training, 16 from Hallett gained basic accreditation, 20 SES people did Senior First Aid, 8 First Aiders did their re-certification, 1 completed Early Childhood Certificate and 1 did a Basic Casualty Care Course.
