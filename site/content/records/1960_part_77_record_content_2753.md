Burra Burra DC, May

The tender of A.J. Bowley & Son for the supply and erection of a shed was accepted.

Election results:

Farrell Flat Ward	J.H.J. Stevens

Kooringa Ward	G.R. Finch

Leighton Ward	J.F .Hogan

Mongolata Ward	A.D. Radford

It was resolved that the [broken] flagpole be reinstated outside the Council Chamber.

E & WS Department is to be advised that preparations are in hand to seal the main street and requesting water pipes be installed before the final seal.

The question of a donation to the Ambulance Fund was deferred.

Appreciation was expressed for Cr E. Finch’s 39½ years of service to the Council and he was wished every success as a Burra Town Councillor.
