Redruth Ladies’ Guild Second Annual Flower & Cookery Show in Redruth Memorial Hall Friday 23 September.  The dry season and cold snaps reduced the standard and number of entries in the flowers.  Nevertheless the display was impressive and the cookery tables were outstanding.  Results are printed and with other details occupy about 2¼ columns.
