BCS.  Inter-School Swimming Competition at North Adelaide Aquatic Centre last Wednesday evening.

As always Burra was by far the smallest school competing.  Both Boys’ and Girls’ competitions were won by Trinity College.  Burra finished with great credit, coming 3rd in the Girls’ competition and 4th in the Boys’.
