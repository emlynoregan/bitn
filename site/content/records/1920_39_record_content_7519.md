Redruth Court.

John Sydney Greenwood aged 18 was charged with stealing a diamond and sapphire ring valued at £30 from Mrs Eleanora Spencer.  He pleaded guilty and was remanded for sentencing at Adelaide Criminal Sessions – details extend for over 1½ columns.
