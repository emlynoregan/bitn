Burra Golf Associates recently held their AGM and elected:

Patroness	Mrs F. Reed

President	Mrs R. Jennison

Vice-Presidents	Mrs N. Finch & Mrs J. Gordon

Secretary	Mrs P. Dignan

Treasurer	Mrs D. Baker

Captain	Mrs D. Kempster

Vice-Captain	Mrs R. Hillman

The new course proved challenging.  Standard scratch score went from 63 to 67.

Club Champion in 1st Division: Miss Judy Oates and in 2nd Division: Mrs C. van der Does.
