Burra Mine Site is one of three projects in the electorate of Grey to get a funding boost from the Federal Government.  The funding will help conserve native grassland on the 100-hectare State Heritage Are Burra Mine Site.  The Funds come from the Government’s Grassy Ecosystems Grants for South East Australia funded through the Federal Government’s Natural Heritage Trust.
