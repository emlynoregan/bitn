Schweppes Athletic Games in Adelaide

BCS students continued their success.

Rosie Lloyd on day 1 gained 2 gold medals in the U19 400 m hurdles and the U19 400 m flat race.

Petrea Piggott, Melissa Richardson, Tasma Wooton, Michael Kessell and Craig McDowell also competed well.

The second day it rained continuously, but Graham Kessell won gold in the senior javelin and Craig McDowell won gold in the high jump.
