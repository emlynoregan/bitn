Redruth Methodist Ladies’ Guild held a Gala Fair at the Redruth Memorial Hall last Saturday.  There were decorated bikes and trikes and a doll competition, musical items, stalls, slot cars and electric trains.  [Results of competitions are printed.]  The Christmas Bowl Appeal will benefit by c. $340.

Burra Traders will sponsor Christmas activities as usual and there will be late trading on Friday 23 December to 9.00 p.m.  Father Christmas will arrive at 9.10 p.m.  On Saturday 24th Christmas shops will trade till 11.30 a.m.

Dalgety NZ Loan Ltd will move into their new premises in Market Square [formerly Elder’s Building] and will be open for business there on Friday 25 November.  [Check tenses in this.]

St Mary’s will have a special Advent Service on Sunday night with a special quartet comprising Rosemary Reed, Jan Cockington, Alan Edwards and Geoffrey Sibly.

Accident.  A utility overturned on the Burra side of Brewery Hill on Sunday, but the driver Frank Baigent and passengers Dianne Menz and Ray Swinson all escaped injury.
