Anzac March & Service in Burra

On Sunday afternoon about 50 ex-servicemen attended the service of commemoration conducted by the Ministers’ Fraternal before the War Memorial in Market Square.  The march was led by the Pt Pirie Pipers’ Band and members of the RSL, Air Force, Ex-Servicemen’s and Fathers’ Associations.  Wreaths were laid by G.J. Kelly (RSL), T.G. Perry (Air Force Association), P. Williams (Demobilised Soldiers), W. Carpenter (Fathers’ Association), The Mayor and Mr E. Finch for the District Council.  Rev. R.S. Jenner gave the address.
