Burra Basketball Club held a wind-up dinner for the season on Wednesday.  Trophies were presented:

‘A’ Grade Best & Fairest	Heather Angel

‘B’ Grade Best & Fairest	Lynette Nicholls

‘C’ Grade Best & Fairest	Lynette Corner

‘C’ Grade Most Improved	Susanne Murphy

‘C’ Grade had a separate dinner on Saturday when its presentations were made.
