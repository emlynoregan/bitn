Weather.  The state has sweltered through a record fifteen day long heatwave with temperatures over 35°C for that period.  On 13 March the minimum overnight temperature was a record high for most of the state.  Eudunda’s minimum was 27.6°C.  This comes after 2007 was SA’s warmest year on record.
