E.B. Hawke contributes a poem What Price Friendship? - considering the cost of dog registration and wondering if his dog will get an acknowledgement when it reaches a 100 in doggy years and ending:

Taxes, tythes, tolls and scythes

The fro reaper gets you in the end

Carbon tax to bury a friend
