Basketball.  The opening of night play at Burra on Thursday was very successful.  The president of the club Mrs D. Broderick welcomed those present.  Mr E.T. Baulderstone congratulated the club on their quick progress in having the lights installed and called on R.M. Reed to throw the switch.  In the games that followed:

Burra Green 4 defeated Burra Red

Burra Blue & Gold 13 defeated Hallett 2

Burra Blue & Gold 4 defeated ‘B’ Grade Footballers 3

‘A’ Grade Footballers 8 defeated Burra Blue & Gold 3

Hallett 5 defeated Burra Blue & White 0

‘B’ Grade Footballers 5 defeated Hallett 2

Burra Blue & Gold 4 defeated Burra Blue & White 2
