Burra Local Historical Society is aiming to microfilm many of the historical records of the area – especially District School Registers – especially of schools no longer operating, old pastoral industry records, logs, minute books, cemetery records, organisational membership rolls, etc.  Contact Andrew Warnes or Tess Hopkins.
