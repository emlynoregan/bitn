The Aberdeen Football Club Inc. Recreation Centre will be opened by the Chairman of the Burra Burra DC Mr R. Jeffery on 17 April at 8 p.m.  Mr Stan Noske President of the Mid North League will also speak.  Facilities include: two pool tables, two table tennis tables, darts and quoits, two meeting rooms, kitchen, storeroom and toilets.
