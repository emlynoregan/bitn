Accident.  At 2.30 a.m. on Saturday a utility driven by Robert Neill collided with the railway guard rail on the Hanson Road about 5 miles west of Burra.  A passenger bus took him to Riverton Hospital for treatment to a lacerated top lip.  Damage to the vehicle was estimated at $1,000.
