Obituary.  John Harry of Mt Bryan died on Sunday afternoon, aged 58.  A resident of 35 years, he leaves a widow and two brothers: Mr Grif Harry of Mt Bryan and Mr George Harry of Broken Hill.

[Died 29 July 1900]
