Burra Netball Club held a season wind-up dinner at the Burra Hotel and the Juniors combined with Football Colts for a wind-up at the RSL Hall.  Trophies were presented:

‘A’ Grade	Heather Short (Burra Gold)

Dianne Day (Burra Blue)

‘B’ Grade	Jill Napper & Sybil Wright (tie)

‘C’ Grade	Cheryl Heitbrink

‘D’ Grade	Debbie Richardson

‘E’ Grade	Joanne Bernhardt
