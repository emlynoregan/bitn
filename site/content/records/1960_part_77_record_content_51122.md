Burra Basketball Club organised a ball at the Burra Town Hall on Friday night.  Door takings were $276.  The Matron and Belle Competition was judged by Miss South Australia, Miss Christine Howard.  Mr Graham Kellock was MC.  The Senior Matron was Mrs Brian Riggs.  Senior Belle was Miss Lyn Richardson.  Junior Matron was Mrs John Tiver and Junior Belle was Miss Joanne Matulick.
