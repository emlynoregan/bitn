Burra Kindergarten

Mrs Chris Bruce of Spalding has returned to Burra as Director.  She previously held the position in 1990 and till September 1991.  Also at the Kindy is Mrs Jacquie Grover of Burra, who returns to teaching after four years of leave.  Enrolment is presently 32.
