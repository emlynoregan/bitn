Des Woolley reminds residents to be careful of snakes in the home and not just the surrounds after finding a four foot brown snake under his fridge in the kitchen.  When the fridge was moved the snake made off outside and went under a car.  After the car of had driven off the snake was found dead, presumably having fallen from the vehicle and been run over.
