Andrew Moore

Lived in Burra about 30 years

Believes that issues that need addressing include:

A review of land rates

Changing dumping rates and opening times

Beautifying/cleaning up the creek area to make it more attractive to tourists

Promotion of more tourist drives

Improvement of unsealed roads to increase their safety

Reopening of Redbanks Reserve

Bituminising of the Burra-Morgan Road

Better tourist signage

Attention to flood mitigation

Tidier footpaths and gutters
