Burra Burra DC

All the following men nominated for councillor were elected unopposed for a term beginning 5 July.

Hanson Walsh	D.L. Lloyd

Baldina Ward	A.A. Barker

Booborowie Ward	J.S. Hawker

Mt Bryan Ward	A.J. Gebhardt

Burra Town Ward	R.B. Jennison

Another councillor is required for Burra Town Ward and nominations are called by 23 May in a supplementary election.
