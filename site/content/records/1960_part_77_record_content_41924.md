Obituary.  Miss Jean M. Harris OBE, BSc, Dip Ed died in Melbourne on Sunday after a short illness.  She had been Headmistress of Methodist Ladies’ College, Adelaide, for 32 years and had retired in May.

[Jean Margaret Harris born 19 January 1902 North Adelaide: died 15 September 1968 Melbourne, Victoria.]
