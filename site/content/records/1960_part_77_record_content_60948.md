Mr & Mrs (Tom & Win) Canny of Dongara WA and formerly of Gum Creek are celebrating their Golden Wedding on 5 May.  Daughter Aileen and husband will attend from Alice Springs, Pat and husband from Adelaide and three sons and their families from WA.  Celebrations will be held at the home of daughter Joan, Mrs John Roberts, in Perth.
