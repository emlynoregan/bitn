Burra RSL held its AGM on Friday night.  Mr H. Cornelius retired as President after two years due to ill health.  The new President is Mr T.R. Rogers with Mr H. Cornelius as Vice-President, Pensions Officer and Ambulance Representative.  C.L Phillips is the second Vice-President and also carries on as Acting Secretary pro tem.  Mr Ben Kotz is Treasurer.
