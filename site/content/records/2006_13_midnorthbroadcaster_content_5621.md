Flinders Mines Ltd (Formerly Flinders Diamonds Ltd) has found a significant number of diamonds on pastoral land north of Peterborough, after four years of searching.  They were recovered from soil surface samples.  They comprise 4 micro diamonds (above 0.5 mm in size) and 6 micro diamonds.
