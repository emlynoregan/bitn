Accident.  A car driven by Rev. J.T. O’Brien collided with another driven by Mr J.H. Carver of Glengowrie on the Burra by-pass road on Tuesday.  Greg William Carver (13) and Alec Potts in the ‘Carver’ vehicle received cuts and bruises.  Damage to the vehicles was estimated at $2,500.
