BCS Governing Council will hold its AGM on 7 March.

A review of 2004 will be provided.

Those retiring from a two year term will be: Sharon O’Connor, Michelle Cousins, Diana Martin, Malcolm Curnow, David Gebhardt and Jodi Lehmann and retiring from one year appointments: Sally Niemann, Vicki McDonald (Community Rep.) and Tracey Rains (Community Rep.)
