Burra Burra DC has applied to the Department of Tourism for funds to effect the following projects:

Better access road from Peacock’s Chimney to the mine lookout area.

Provision of a parking and turnaround area at the lookout near the mine.

Provision of a direction finder cairn at the lookout, to indicate places of interest.

Adequate signposting of the route to World’s End Gorge.

Replacement of the existing red arrow directional signs on the Historic Tour of Burra with Tourist Drive signs.

Purchase of six complete mine skips and their mounting on a light railway track in the form of an ore train near Peacock’s Chimney.
