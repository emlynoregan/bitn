The Hallett Catholic Church to be opened on 8 June has been given the name ‘Christ the Good Shepherd’.  On the closure of the Hallett Masonic Lodge No. 100 the building was offered to the Catholic Community as a free gift.  The official blessing and opening will be performed by the Bishop of Pt Pirie Rev. Bryan Gallagher, assisted by the Coadjutor Bishop Rev. Peter De Campo DD.
