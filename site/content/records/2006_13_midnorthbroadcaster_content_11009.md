Earthquakes have recently been felt in the region.  Most were very small, but a 3.2 magnitude shake on Friday was felt at Farrell Flat & Mintaro.  On Sunday there was a 4.4 quake about 101 km east of Peterborough and on 8 October a 3.2 quake occurred 12 km northwest of Clare.
