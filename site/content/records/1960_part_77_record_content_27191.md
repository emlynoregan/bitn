Burra High School held its first Valedictory Service last Sunday evening in the Kooringa Methodist Church.  About 130 people attended a family tea followed by the service with the theme ‘The New Vision for the New Age’.  The guest preacher was Rev. Douglas Hosking, Associate Director of Christian Education for the Methodist Church.
