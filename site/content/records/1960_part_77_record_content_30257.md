Mr A.J. Hill (Joff) began as Assistant District Clerk with the BBDC in 1936 and later became Town Clerk and Chief Administrative Officer of Northcote in Victoria.  He was educated at Burra and apart from five years in the RAAF has pursued a career in Municipal Government.  After the war Mr Hill resumed work with the BBDC before moving to the position of Treasurer and Accountant with the city of Williamstown in Victoria.  After nine years in this capacity he accepted the position of Town Clerk with the Kyabram Borough Council and after eighteen months moved to Northcote as Deputy Town Clerk, a position he has held for the past ten years.  While living in Burra, Joff was well known as a keen tennis player and was a popular member of the Kooringa Football Team.
