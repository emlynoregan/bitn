Burra & Districts Sportsperson of the Year awards were announced on Saturday night.  Kaye Bellman was named Sportsperson of the Year.  Sharon Roberts won the Service to Sport Award.  The BBH Under 15 Netball Team was selected as Team of the Year and Megan Brooks won Junior Sportsperson of the Year.  Guest speaker Rachel Sporn, Olympic Basketball star, gave an entertaining and inspirational speech.  [The other nominees are discussed.]
