Burra Hospital.  The work on the new building is progressing.  Foundations have been laid and 1,600 bricks have been used to bring them to the floor level of existing buildings.  The interior of the Nurses’ Home and the dining room are being painted.  An 8 x 5 ft cool room has been installed.  54 acres of wheat have been sown for the Board at Hanson.  Donations have reached £5,688-0-2.
