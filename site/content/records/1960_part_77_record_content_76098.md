Ian Auhl & Denis Marfleet’s new book Australia’s Earliest Mining Era: SA 1841-51 will be launched by Mr Warren Bonython on Monday at the Historical Museum of the SA Art Gallery.  In conjunction with it there will be an exhibition of paintings by S.T. Gill, which are featured in the book.
