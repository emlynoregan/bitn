Burra & District Stockowners’ Association held its AGM in the RSL Hall 17 April.  Guest speakers were D.C.W. Edwards Assistant State Secretary and H. McDiamid of Economic Wool Producers.  Elected: President, D.G. Radford; Vice-Presidents, R. Rooke & R. Cooke and Secretary, Arthur L. Wright.
