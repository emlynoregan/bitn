Obituary.  Rt Rev. Donald Redding died suddenly last week.  He was the parish priest of St Mary’s, Burra 1934-38.  He later became Bishop of Bunbury.  He saw active service in WWI and WW II.

[Donald Llewellyn Redding died 15 October 1969 Plympton aged 71.]
