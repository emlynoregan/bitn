BCS.  Four of the Year 10 students who participated in the First Edge Camp at Bungaree two months ago reported back on their experiences to the Burra Community Development Committee on Sunday afternoon.  Sponsors supported each student to the tune of $270.  Speaking of their experiences were: Josephine Williams, Rebecca Brooks, Denika Schell and Jo Guthrie.
