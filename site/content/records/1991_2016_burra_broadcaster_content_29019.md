Old Belcunda has been split up and sold at auction for $2.755 million on 2 October at Hallett Hall.

Lot 1 ‘Riggs’ went to John Quinn & family of Mt Bryan.

Lots 2 & 3 ‘Newikie’ and ‘Mt Bryan’ went to Collinsville.

Lot 4 ‘Old Belcunda’ block was bought by W.B. Gebhardt or nominee.

Lot 5 ‘Pine Creek’ went to Kym & Lyn Riggs.

The station was sold by the Gosse family.
