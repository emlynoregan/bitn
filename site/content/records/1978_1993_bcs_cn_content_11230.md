The Jubilee 150 Procession on New Year’s Eve in Adelaide will open the Jubilee Celebrations.  In 1936 Burra contributed a float to the State’s Centenary Celebrations and has been asked to do so again.  The Burra District Advisory Committee will establish a committee to oversee this.
