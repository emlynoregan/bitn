Bad weather kept some away, but there was still a good crowd at 3 p.m. when Rev. H.J. Pope, President of Conference preached a sermon in the State Schoolroom.  The memorial stone service was preached by Rev. W.F. James.  Mr Jesse Humphrys laid the first stone.  The President then laid the Wesley bicentenary stone.]
