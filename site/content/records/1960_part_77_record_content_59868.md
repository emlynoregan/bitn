RSPCA ran a state-wide essay contest.  Honourable mentions went to Maryann Shipard, Karen Mengersen, Lorraine Meintjes & Margaret Beare; all in the Junior Section and to Lyn Moreland in the Senior Section.  David Nourse gained 3rd place in the state in the Junior Section and appeared on Channel 7 recently to receive his prize.
