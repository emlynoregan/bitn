SA Country Athletics Championships at Barmera at Easter

BCS students performed well:

Rosalie Lloyd captured gold in the U16 400 m and the long jump and came second in the 100 m, 200 m and 800 m. events.

Carolyn Bernhardt won the U14 javelin.

Paul Nation won gold in the 100 m, 200 m, 400 m and 800 m and the Trophy for the Outstanding Athlete of the Championships.
