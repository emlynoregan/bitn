Accident.  On 1 August a man driving a Ford sedan collided with sheep being herded across the highway some two kilometres south of Burra.  Eight sheep were killed.  Damage to the car was c. $1,500 and the driver was reported for driving an unregistered and uninsured vehicle and for driving without a licence.
