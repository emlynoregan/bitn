Accident.  On 5 January at 3.30 p.m. a truck laden with bulk wheat and driven by Thomas K. McWaters collided with the ‘Blue Bird’ railcar at the crossing on the by-pass road near the Copperhouse Corner.  The train caught the rear of the truck.  The driver suffered concussion, fractured ribs and bruised legs.  The damage to the truck was estimated at $250, to the bin at $400 and to the train at $1,000.  Wheat worth $120 was also lost.
