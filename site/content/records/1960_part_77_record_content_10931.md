Burra High School.  The Jubilee History continued by R.V .Mueller.

1925

Miss Olive Drever joined the staff as 0.2 time Domestic Arts Teacher.  Enrolment was a record 42 with 21 new students and 6 probationary.  The school was conducted in three rooms on the western side of the Primary School Building. 17 March was speech afternoon.  The Director of Education and the Superintendent of High Schools visited and decided the Physics room should be remodelled and the yard gravelled.  On 27 June a bus and six cars took students to Kapunda for sports.

1926

There was a complete change of staff with the arrival of Mr E.A. Haese as Headmaster with Miss Dorothy Robinson and Elsie Tiver (Part Time Domestic Arts).  Enrolment was 40.  School caps came from James Marshall & Co. at 2/11 each.  On 28 September the Old Scholars held a reunion in the Parish Hall.
