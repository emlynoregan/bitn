Burra Pre-School Association raised £64 with a Devonshire Tea and a trading table at the home of Mrs Pitcher on Friday.  This will allow them to go ahead with the erection of cupboards, some new plumbing and general improvements.

Hanson Hall has had its interior repainted.

Burra Small Bore Club.  The season ended with a double 100 yards shoot.  In the handicap section Margaret Alderson narrowly defeated K. Spackman to win the yearly trophy.

For an unnamed trophy (probably the Championship) R. Gilbert took the honours [named elsewhere as getting the Marksman’s Trophy.]

In the Handicap Championship R. Kellock defeated R. Alderson in a shoot-off.

Yearly Trophy went to M. Alderson.
