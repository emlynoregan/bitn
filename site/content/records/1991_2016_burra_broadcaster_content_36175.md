Burra RSL held its AGM.  President Len Simpson said they had not only survived another year, but had three new members.  The Anzac Dawn Service 2004 had been conducted by Fr Quinn and owing to rain the Remembrance Day Service had been held in the club rooms.  Anzac Badge Day and Poppy Day were held thanks to Ben Kotz.  Thanks were recorded to Ben Kotz (Secretary) and Brian Riggs (Treasurer).
