Burra Burra DC

The Highways & Local Government Department advises £1,000 grant for the Hanson-Gum Creek Road.

Roads between Sections 1019 & 124, 561 & 562 Hd Hanson are to be closed and sold to M.B. Miller at £12 per acre.
