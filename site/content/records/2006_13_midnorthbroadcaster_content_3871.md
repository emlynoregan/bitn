Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club has been going for 16 years and is still giving local canines a lesson in manners.  The club meets every Sunday from February to November.  The club grounds are situated opposite the Burra Golf Club on the Barrier Highway.  Annual membership is $15 or family $20 and $2 weekly training subscription per dog.
