The SES Team answered a number of callouts over Thursday and Friday’s wild weather.

Cleared gutters at the Mobil Roadhouse that had led to flooding.

Attended the Burra Uniting Church to fix flapping ridge-capping.

Removed a fallen pine tree from Maureen Bevan’s car in the St Mary’s car park.

Assisted in disposing of dead sheep on Saturday.

Mounted a security guard on the downed helicopter on Sunday.
