Friends of Burra Parks was proclaimed as the 125th Friends of Parks group in SA at Burra recently.  About 50 people attended.  Elected were: Patrons, Chris Reed & Stephen Kerrigan; President, Ms Heidi Nicholson; Assistant President, Ms Sally Nieman; Vice President, Robert Heading and Treasurer, Libby Hallion.
