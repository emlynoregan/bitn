Football.  Games in the North Eastern and Mid North Football and Netball Associations commence on 28 April.

North Eastern football teams are South Clare, North Clare, Burra, Booborowie-Hallett, Spalding and Blyth. (six teams).

Each will field an ‘A’ Grade, ‘B’ Grade, Senior Colts and Junior Colts team.

Netball has the same teams for grades A, B, C & D.

In E grade there is no South Clare team.

‘F’ Grade comprises Booborowie-Hallett, Blyth, Burra, Spalding and Leighton.

‘G’ Grade comprises Booborowie-Hallett, Burra, North Clare and Blyth.
