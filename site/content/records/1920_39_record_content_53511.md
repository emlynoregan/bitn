Mr & Mrs Gordon Grubb were given a farewell at the Oddfellows Hall in Aberdeen on Tuesday.  Mr Grubb had been in Burra for a number of years as goods clerk and assistant stationmaster.  He has now been promoted and moved to Gawler.  While here he has shone at football.  A dance followed the speeches and presentations.  Mr A. Ratcliffe spoke and presented a travelling rug, an inscribed tobacco pouch and a gross of matches.  Mr Warnes the stationmaster spoke in eulogistic terms of Mr Grubbâ€™s capabilities and popularity.
