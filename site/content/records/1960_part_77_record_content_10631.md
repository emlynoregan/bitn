Dorothy Pederson, Isobel Rosewall and Ella Harris applied to be Junior Teachers.  Senior Cadets were taken in the Drill Hall by a Military Officer.  A medal presented by Mr Crewes was won by Fred Scholz and Frank Riggs won a scholarship to Roseworthy Agricultural College.  A seaside picnic was held at Semaphore.  On 5 October it was recorded that due to the bad season many children left to help at home.
