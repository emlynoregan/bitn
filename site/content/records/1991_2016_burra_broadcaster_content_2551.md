June

BCS Arbor Day with tree planting at ‘Kookaburra’.

A dingo reported killed at Caroona Station.

Kooringa Freemasons donate a new water heater to the Burra Day Care Activity Centre.

Leonie Fretwell appointed to co-ordinate Burra’s Jubilee 150.

Burra CWA donates an air and water cushioned chair for use at Burra Hospital.

Rock Music Workshop in Burra.

Daisy Kennedy Commemorative concert.

Burra Ambulance Service joined the state body of St John.
