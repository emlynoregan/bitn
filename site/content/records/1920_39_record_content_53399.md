Burra Show Luncheon.  President I.J. Warnes gave a Show Luncheon to 80-90 guests on Show Day.  Catering was by Mrs Rowe.  Sir John Melrose opened the show.  Speakers were partly drowned out by the noise of the rain on the roof.  Speakers were Sir John Melrose, Mr Alex Melrose, Mr A.D. McDonald MP, I.J. Warnes, Mayor of Burra Mr T.H.
