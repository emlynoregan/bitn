Accident.  Frank P. Sullivan of Hallett was injured on Sunday morning when the small sedan he was driving overturned at c. 7.30 a.m. on the Jamestown Road at a bend.  He suffered severe lacerations and bruised kidneys.  A passenger Adrian Phelps of Pt Pirie received cuts and bruises.  Both were admitted to Jamestown Hospital.
