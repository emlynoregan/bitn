Burra National Trust held its AGM.  Report items:

Nan & Les Warnes were given special tribute for their contribution after resigning from the committee this year following 30 years of National Trust membership.

During the year the Trust spent $27,152 on capital improvements to its sites.

Branch membership was 81

Elected were: Chairperson, Elizabeth Robertson; Vice-Chairperson, Maureen Wright; Secretary, Timothy Horsnell; Assistant Secretary, Prue McDonald & Treasurer, Joan de Metris.
