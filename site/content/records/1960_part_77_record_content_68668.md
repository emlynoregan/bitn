Mrs C.F. Carmody’s Leighton property realised $102.50 per acre when sold by Southern Farmers.  Lot 2 of 746 acres was bought by A.J. & E.J. Broad of Leighton.  ‘Kilmorey’, the homestead section, was passed in at auction, but later sold privately to Mr A. Scholz of Leighton, who had been the highest bidder at the auction.
