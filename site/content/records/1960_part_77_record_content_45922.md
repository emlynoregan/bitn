Burra Burra DC has decided to continue with a Chairman as head, rather than appoint a Mayor.  Nominations for a complete new Council are now being called.  Mr T.G .Perry has been appointed as District Clerk and Mr R.A. Moreland as Overseer.  A new assessment is being prepared to bring the town under the land values system of rating.
