North Eastern School Sports were held at Leighton Oval on Friday.  Mr Quirke MP opened the event.  He mentioned the recent raising of the school leaving age to 15.  Participating schools were: Leighton, Spalding, South Booborowie, Hilltown, St Joseph’s Convent, Booborowie & Willalo.  [Check this list.]
