Goyder Council has received applications for three Telstra mobile phone towers:

A 20 m tower on John Tilley’s land northwest of Hallett.

A 35 m tower on Brewery Hill at Burra.

A 35 m tower on Maurice Nitschke’s land at Eudunda at the junction of Marrabel and Kapunda Roads.

Provision has been made in the design for other users to share the structures.
