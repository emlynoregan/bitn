Burra Racing Club held a well-attended meeting at Kooringa on 4 December with President A.B. Hiddle in the chair.  Secretary W.H. Hardy reported the SA Jockey Club had allotted them 20 March 1907 for the next meeting.  L.W. Gebhardt has granted the use of the paddock a mile north on the Mt Bryan Road.
