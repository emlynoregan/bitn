The Mt Bryan East School was built in 1884, opened in 1885 and closed in 1947.  It was vacant until acquired by the National Trust in the early 1970s, but unfortunately the Trust lacked the funds to restore it and so it was transferred to the Department of Recreation & Sport in 1990.  [Photo.]
