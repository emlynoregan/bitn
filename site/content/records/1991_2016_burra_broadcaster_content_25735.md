Obituary.  Steve Burkert, principal of Robertstown School died at the weekend.  He was the head trainer for Eudunda Football Club and helped to see an RSMU player stretchered from the oval on Saturday.  After the player had left the field Steve suffered a massive heart attack and died.  He was aged 46 and was in his third year as principal of Robertstown Primary School and was a teacher there since 1994.  He had been in the district for 15 years, previously teaching at Eudunda Area School.  He had been involved in the Robertstown Ambulance Service, Aged care, the bowling, tennis and cricket club s and was a lay reader in the Lutheran Church.  He is survived by wife Cathy and daughters Joanne, Susie and Alison.
