Burra Footballers 153 v. Leighton 0 for 46

The Aberdeen & Burra Junior Cricket Club was formed on 24 October so that the three teams, Burra Gold, Purple and Green could be run under a joint committee.  They, with Hallett and Booborowie will compete in a five team association.  Patrons, Frank Ford & Malcolm Culbertson; President, A. Fillmore; Secretary, D. Robinson & Assistant Secretary, A.
