BCS.  Council AGM, 30 November elected: Chairman, Rod Bailey; Vice-Chairman, Tony Barker; Secretary, Hazel Stockman and Treasurer, Peter Hall.

Points made at the meeting:

The Agricultural Open Day was successful.

The AGM of School Parents had been successful with Mr Brian Prosser as guest speaker.

The Fundraising Committee had raised c. $6,200 in twelve months.

The School Council is looking for the use of alternative water for when the school is self-funded.

The compulsory fees for 1984 will be $40 per child.
