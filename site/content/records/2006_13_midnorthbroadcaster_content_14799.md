The New Goyder Visitor Guide has earned a positive response according to feedback from local businesses.  It was officially launched at the Regional Council of Goyder’s May meeting.  Over 60 businesses took part.  Among those reporting a very positive response have been ‘Hidden Treasures’ at Eudunda and the Mt Bryan Hotel.
