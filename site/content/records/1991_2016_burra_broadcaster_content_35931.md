Advt.  Ford’s Corner – For Sale – Diprose Creek Cottage and Gallery

145 acres with great views of the North Mt Lofty Ranges

Cottage built c. 1850, which has been restored to original character

Other historic ruins on property include a stone outhouse, a stone sheep dip and a slaughterhouse ruin.

A worker’s cottage has been restored as a gallery and studio and is plumbed and could easily make a great B & B
