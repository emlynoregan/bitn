CWA held its AGM last Friday with President Mrs I.J. Warnes in the chair.

A report of the year’s activities is given at length in some 2¾ columns (the last 1½ columns being on page 4.)  They were in credit £33-3-5.  Page 4 report of the election of officers: President, Mrs I.J. Warnes; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames J. Tennant, E. Jettner & C.D. Wilkinson; Hon. Secretary, Miss Minnie Rogers and Treasurer, Mrs S.H. Robinson.
