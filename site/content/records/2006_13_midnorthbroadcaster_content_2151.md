Burra Regional Art Gallery has had a moderately successful year financially and has welcomed almost 4,500 visitors.  There were twelve diverse collection exhibited: three single artist shows, two Country Arts touring exhibitions, a Glass Works show, the biennial Quilters Exhibition, a special display of paintings from the Namatjira family, Art of the Flower and a competition sponsored through the National Grasslands Conference.  Peter Harvey is retiring as Chairman/Treasurer/Secretary.
