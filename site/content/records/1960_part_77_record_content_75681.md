The Northern Fire Fighting Association will hold a special public meeting at the Soldiers’ Memorial Hall in Gladstone on 3 July.  This is to discuss moves by the Fire Fighter’s Association to interfere with the present powers of Councils and with the present arrangements of the EFS and in the light of this all interested were urged to attend.
