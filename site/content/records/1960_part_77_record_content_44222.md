Weather.  Good rains fell in the pastoral country last Friday.  The heaviest falls were further east.  Oakvale 148 points, Quondong 134, Sturt Vale 105, Canegrass 76, Balah 32, Woolgangi 33, Braemar 82 & Old Koomooloo 50.
