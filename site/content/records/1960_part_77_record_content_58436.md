The Kooringa Masonic Lodge installed Bro. Jeff. White as Worshipful Master on Wednesday followed by a ball in the Burra Town Hall attended by over 400.  With over 30 visiting Worshipful Masters, this was perhaps the most successful function in the history of the lodge.
