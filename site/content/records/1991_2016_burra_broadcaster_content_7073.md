Accident.  A two vehicle accident outside the Burra Hospital on 24 May produced no injuries, but caused c. $1,500 damage to the vehicles.

Fire.  On 24 May at c. 7 p.m. a shed fire in Taylor Street caused about $10,000 damage.  The fire seems to have started as the result of an electrical fault.
