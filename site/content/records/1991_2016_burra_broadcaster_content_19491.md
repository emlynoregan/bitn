Johne’s Infection

Further testing of sheep at Woollana Stud has shown that the infection is actually Bovine Johne’s Disease not Ovine Johne’s Disease.  The infection of sheep by the cattle strain of the disease is quite rare, but isolated cases have been found in sheep in Holland.
