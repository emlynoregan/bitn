SA Country Council of Music Stampede Showcase was held in Gawler on Saturday.  Tracey Rains was awarded the 2008 Rock Page Memorial Scholarship.  This gives an opportunity to attend the Country Music Association of Australia’s Country Music College in Tamworth on 2-17 January.

Nathaniel O’Brien (12) [Tracey’s brother] was awarded the SA council of Country Music Rising Star Award; the first time it has gone to such a young artist.  This year Nathaniel has been busy claiming:

2008 Junior Victorian Champion of Champions at Mildura Country Music Festival

Pt Augusta Junior Entertainer of the Year 2008

Overall Winner at Pt Pirie

Finalist in Aristocrats Junior Entertainer of the Year at Tamworth

And other awards in SA, NSW and Victoria.
