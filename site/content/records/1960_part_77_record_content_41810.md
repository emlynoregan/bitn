Football.  In the polling for the Mail Medal in the North Eastern Association Don Villis of Booborowie was the winner with 17 points from B. Eberhard of Burra with 16.  Barry Eberhard gets the Record Trophy.

In the ‘B’ Grade the Gilles Trophy went to D. Coulter of Clare North with A. Ryan of Burra in second place.  In the Colts L. Victor of Clare North was the winner, with S. Anderson of Burra runner-up.

In the Mid North League the Mail Medal went to Lindsay Schmidt of Robertstown from Glen Chamberlain of Saddleworth-Marrabel United.  The ‘B’ Grade Jenner Trophy was won by Barry Dixon of Manoora and in the Colts Leon Wanganeen of Mintaro was the winner.  Leading goal kicker in the league was Trevor Mosey of Robertstown.
