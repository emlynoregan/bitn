Chris Stegmeyer of the National Bank has been transferred to Maitland, to be replaced by Daryl Hardy from Maitland.

‘King’ Grubb and family are leaving the Burra Hotel this week.

Robert and Marilyn Brandt, newly married, left for Woomera yesterday.

Rev. Geoff. Sibly drove over from Canberra to play the organ at St Mary’s last night for the visit of Bishop Quarterman of North-West Texas.
