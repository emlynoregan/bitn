National Athletics Titles were held in Adelaide last weekend.

Adam Day missed the finals in the U16 300 m hurdles.

Petrea Piggott ran 5th in the U16 300 m hurdles and 6th in the U16 200 m.

She is only 14 and so can compete again next year.
