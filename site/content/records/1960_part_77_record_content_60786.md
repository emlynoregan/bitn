Tennis.  The Burra Tennis Association ended its season with a cabaret in the Burra Town Hall on Friday, which produced a profit of c. $130.  Aberdeen White won the ‘A’ Grade Shield, which was accepted by Captain Don Lloyd.  Aberdeen Purple won the ‘B’ Grade Shield, which was accepted by R. Taylor.  The Junior Shield had been presented to Kooringa at the end of the Grand Final.
