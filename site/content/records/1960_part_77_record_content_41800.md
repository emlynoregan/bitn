Major Peter Phillips was born at the Burra Hospital in 1935.  His father was Ray Phillips, who lived in the house which Goldsbrough’s managers have occupied for many years.  Ray Phillips was in partnership with his brother in a dental practice for many years before opening a practice at Kadina and Wallaroo, where he remained until enlisting in the AIF during WWII.  He died on active service in 1943.  Peter Phillips was educated at Rostrevor College and then went to Duntroon.  After graduation he served as a Lieutenant in Malaya and this year was awarded the Military Cross for bravery in Vietnam.
