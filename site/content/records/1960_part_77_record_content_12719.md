Burra Hospital.  85th Annual Report and AGM.

Chairman of the Board of Management R.K. Pearse reported finances in a sound condition with the overdraft reduced.  The daily average number of patients fell from 11.9 in 1960-61 to 10.1 in 1961-62.  Outpatient numbers fell to 430 and births to 63.  Mr Pearse said he would like to see plans for building new private rooms in the near future.  Brochures of the balance sheet had been printed and were available from the Secretary-Manager.
