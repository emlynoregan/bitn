Friends of the old Post & Telegraph Office support plans to:

Secure ownership of the building for the citizens of Burra.

Endeavour to get Australia Post to gift the building for Jubilee 150.

Recommend that the building be used for a Regional Art Gallery.

Seek Government grants to finance the establishment and maintenance costs.
