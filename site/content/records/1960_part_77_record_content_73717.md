After page 1 the layout of the paper varied from week to week, but the following outline is quite typical.

Page 1	Typically full of news, but frequently little of it relating to Burra.

Page 2	Large advertisements and sports.

Page 3	Large advertisements – typically more than half page – plus public notices and sports.

Page 4	Large advertisements and a mixture of sports, news and articles of general interest.

Page 5	Advertisements with some news and general interest items.

Page 6	Large advertisements, the cash column, public notices and entertainment.

Page 7	The ‘Out & About’ column with large advertisements and a small amount of sports or other items of general interest.

Page 8	The 50 Years Ago column and items of general interest.
