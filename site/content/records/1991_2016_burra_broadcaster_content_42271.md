Burra AGL Country Music Festival will be held on 18-20 November.  The headline act will be Doug Bruce and the Tailgaters.  Also featured will be Sandie Dodd, Dwayne Elix, Tracey Rains and Claypan (with Greg Burnett on guitar), Tanya Cornish, John Turner Jnr, Whiplash, The Twilight Zone and Bob Magor.  There will be a talent quest on the Saturday.  This, the third festival, is expected to be bigger than ever.  The event will begin with The Twilight Zone on Friday night after the official opening by Hon. Dan van Holst Pellekaan MP.
