Burra Burra DC on 12 September resolved to participate in the SA-Texas Twin Towns Project.  Subsequently Kerrville was recommended as a prospective twin town for Burra.  It is 60 miles northwest of San Antonio and noted as a tourism-recreation and art-craft centre and as a centre for the surrounding agricultural industries, which include wool and mohair production.
