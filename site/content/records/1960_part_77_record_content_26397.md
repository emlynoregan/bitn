Accident.  On Thursday a car driven by Kingsley Broad of Peterborough overturned near the junction of the Black Springs and Adelaide Roads about a mile outside Burra.  The car skidded on loose gravel.  The driver’s two daughters were passengers.  Rosemary (6) suffered lacerations and a fractured left leg.  Deborah (5) sustained lacerations and shock.  The three occupants were admitted to the Hospital and damage to the car was estimated at c. £300.
