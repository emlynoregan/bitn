Booborowie Primary School is preparing to celebrate its centenary over the weekend of 29 February 1992 & 1 March 1992.  The school began in the Methodist church on 29 February 1892 with 19 children and Miss Noras [sic?] Murphy as teacher.  The present school opened on 18 August 1918 with Miss Adelaide Campbell as teacher.
