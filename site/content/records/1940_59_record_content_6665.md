Burra CWA held its AGM in the Rest Room on 18 July.  The year’s activities are reported in c. 1⅓ columns.  Mrs Warnes had to resign as President due to her removal to live in Adelaide.  Mrs Warnes founded the CWA in South Australia in Burra in 1926.  The second branch was formed at Spalding in 1928 and the Adelaide & Metropolitan Branch was formed the same year.  Elected: President, Mrs G. Hawkes; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames J. Tennant & E. Jettner; Secretary & Treasurer, Miss M. Rogers & Minute Secretary, Mrs F.T. Sara.
