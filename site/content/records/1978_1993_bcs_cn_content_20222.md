Burra National Trust

Two valuable relics are missing from the Bon Accord Mine Museum.  They are a bronze alarm bell from the Monster Mine marked with the figures 5662 and a cast iron ball and chain.  If anyone knows of their whereabouts please contact any National Trust member.
