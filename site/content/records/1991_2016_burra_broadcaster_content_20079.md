Adchem has had another record month, as reported by Simon Kibble, Operations Manager at the Burra plant.  They had achieved a new monthly record production of 861.9 tonnes.  This level of copper oxide production was achieved with 30.8% of copper scrap and off spec. cathode, and 69.2% from copper cement.  In the past highest production months have been November 1992 with 749.1 tonnes, November 1998 with 778.3 tonnes and April 1999 at 833.5 tonnes.
