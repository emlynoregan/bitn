Obituary.  Tom Kellaway, formerly of Burra, died on Friday [18 April] aged 63.  He is survived by a widow and two sons.  He was a former footballer for Burra, Port Adelaide and State teams and played in the 1936 Australian Championship in Perth.  [Thomas Kellaway born 29 September 1911 Kooringa.]
