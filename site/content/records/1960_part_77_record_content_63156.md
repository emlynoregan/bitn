Burra Growth.  Apart from the initial program of Samin Ltd there has been other growth in the past two years and at present more is taking place.  The second stage of Saminâ€™s mining operations is under way and this will quadruple production.  Construction of Barkers of Baldina Cottage Homes has started at a cost of $102,000.  Additions and redevelopment at the hospital will give a new maternity section, kitchen and more private and shared rooms at a cost of $240,000.  Private and business works underway or just completed are worth c. $30,000.

Hotels in the town, through the addition of new toilets and other improvements have spent in excess of $25,000.  New stock saleyards have been erected and building of private homes has amounted to over $40,000.  In the 1970-71 period there was the housing project worth over $120,000 and the new Police Station was valued at $30,000.  The town is fast becoming known for its unique historic attractions and tourism is growing.

Adelaide Wool Sales, 12-14 September.  The highest district prices went to Willogoleche Proprietors of Hallett at 162c.  [Other local district prices are printed.]

Mr L. Moore of the Savings Bank of SA has been transferred to Loxton and his replacement as manager will be Mr Bob Buckton, who transfers from Saddleworth where both he and his wife were heavily involved in civic affairs.
