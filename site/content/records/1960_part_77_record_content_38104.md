Football.  Kevin Lane [Photo] has been appointed coach of the Burra ‘A’ Grade side for 1968.  R. James withdrew his application and has been appointed coach of Aberdeen.  In recent years Kevin Lane has been coach of Aberdeen in the Mid North Association.
