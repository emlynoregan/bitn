Burra Show this year will feature Darrell Lee and his four piece band, a ute competition, Obedience Dog Club displays, a Yard Dog Competition, a cooking section for men only, trade sites and of course horses-in-action all day.  The recipe for the men only orange cake is available from the Broadcaster office.  The ute competition will have sections for: Work Ute, Rural Ute, Feral Ute, Show/Town Ute and 4WD Ute.
