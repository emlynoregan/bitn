Accident.  Garry James of Koomooloo Station suffered a severely broken upper arm and the loss of a thumb when the car he was driving overturned on the Mt Bryan Road near Cobb & Co.’s Corner.  He and a passenger, Andrew Phillips of Burra, were taken to Burra Hospital by Patrick Brown of ‘The Grove’.  Garry was later transferred to Adelaide.  [An item on page 6 adds that Garry will be 19 next Saturday.]
