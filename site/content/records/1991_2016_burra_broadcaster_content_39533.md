SALA (SA Living Artists Festival)

Burra Regional Art gallery features works by Simon C. Smith and Janice Lane in an exhibition called ‘Allude’.

And an exhibition called ‘On Identity’ featuring works by local artists Russell Philip and Lisa Smedley.

The SALA exhibition will be opened by Dan van Hols Pellekaan on Sunday 27 July.

Nature Foundation SA is again calling for lizard researchers to help them locate and protect Burra’s resident population of the endangered Pygmy Bluetongue Lizard.
