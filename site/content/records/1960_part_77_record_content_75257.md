Burra Burra DC, 5 May

A streetlight will be placed on Sancreed Street.

A letter was received from Mr K. White about an old gum in the Mine Paddock.

Hanson Hall will be allowed to crop the Hanson Parklands on the west side of the railway line.

Application is to be made to the RED Scheme for $5,000 for repairs to Farrell Flat Hall.

It was resolved that tenders be called for the sale of the Corporation Depot block [in Kingston St] Burra.
