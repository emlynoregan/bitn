SA Tourism Awards for 2004 were announced at the Adelaide Convention Centre on 19 November.

Maureen Wright was presented with the Harry Dowling Award.  The citation said:

Maureen Wright has consistently and conscientiously dedicated her time and energy to the township of Burra, its tourism, heritage preservation and interpretation.

For more than 15 years she has operated Burra Heritage Cottages and has been in business with Saraâ€™s Antiques.  She has been a strong advocate for the Burra Passport and for the National Trust, of which she has been Chairperson.  She has served on a number of regional boards and committees over the years.  She has, with her husband Barry, published a book of navigational charts of the Murray River.
