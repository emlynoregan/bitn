NSW Variety Club Bash is coming to town on 30 July.  The cars, all pre 1966 vintage, will be nearing the end of their 3,500 km run to Tanunda.  Burra Community School will cater for their visit as a fund raising activity.  Upwards of 300 Bashers will lunch in the town.
