Cycling Record.  The 30-year-old cyclist Brian Gould broke the 39-year-old record for the Broken Hill-Adelaide distance by covering it in 5 hours 4 minutes less than the 1930 time of 22 hours 40 minutes.  He left Broken Hill at 6 p.m. Friday.  He stopped for ten minutes in Burra.  He covered the 314 miles in 17 hours 36 minutes.
