Education Week in Burra

Friday 28 April.  Mr J. Sladden was MC to a series of items in Burra Town Hall by students from Burra Primary, Burra High and St Joseph’s Convent School.  The Mayor E.T. Baulderstone introduced Mr R. King, who spoke on What are we doing for our youth?

Sunday 30 April there was a combined service at Kooringa Methodist Church where Rev. R. Jenner traced the history of education.

The schools’ visiting day was a success with many visiting parents.

The Camera and Rural Youth Clubs put on a very successful film evening in the school hall.
