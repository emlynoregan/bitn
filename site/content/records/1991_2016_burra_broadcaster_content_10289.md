BCS students won both the junior and senior heat finals in the ‘Voice of Youth’ held at Jamestown last Friday.  Senior spoke for eight minutes and juniors for six, with prepared speeches and both then had three minutes to deliver short notice speeches.  The winners were Evelyn Button and Rachel Vogel.  The semi-finals will be held at Caritas College at Pt Augusta on 2 June.
