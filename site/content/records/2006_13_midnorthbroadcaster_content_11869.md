Goyder Council has a $4,000 Youth Week Grant.  This will go toward a joint ‘Sk8 in the Valley’ run jointly with Clare and Gilbert Valley Councils.  There will be an expo at Clare Skate Park on 1 April featuring sponsored skateboard and BMX riders.  There will also be DJ/band entertainment, sumo suits, a boxing castle and sausage sizzle.
