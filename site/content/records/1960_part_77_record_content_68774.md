Property Sales

‘Ramara’, the 269 acre property of P.J. Thomas at Leighton, was bought by C.W. & M.E. Ramm of Leighton for $169 per acre.

H.L. Phillips’ World’s End Church Paddock of 395 Acres was passed in.

H.L. Phillips’ Mine Paddock of 71¼ acres was sold to L.R. Brown of Burra at $80 per acre.
