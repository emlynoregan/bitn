Notice.  Lindsay Hood of Kooringa apologises to William Malcolm Heath his late employer for endeavouring to get his customers to leave him unless he continued to employ him [Hood] as his driver.  Hood also admitted he had received all the wages he was entitled to and undertook not to do anything to damage the business in the future.  W.M.
