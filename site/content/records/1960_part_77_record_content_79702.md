Mr Ivan Milde of Saddleworth has been a mail contractor for 22 years and on 30 June carried the Royal Mail for the last time from Saddleworth to Black Springs, calling at Marrabel, Waterloo, Black Springs and Tarnma.  An article on his experiences runs for a little less than 2 columns.
