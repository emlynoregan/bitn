Burra Town Council, 19 October

The Council will seek a discussion with the Education Department Inspector concerning the maintenance of unmade tracks through Council property leased by the Department.

The footbridge opposite Justice Lane is to be repaired.

Council will advance £50 towards the purchase of pumping equipment at the oval, repayable at the rate of £100 p.a.
