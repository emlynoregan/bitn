Burra National Trust is holding a major fund-raising event in the Town Hall:

Wool on the Move is a presentation by the Australian Wool Corporation.  The $10 cost covers dinner, a practical presentation of the latest developments in clip presentation, wool handling and a shearing demonstration.  There will also be a fashion parade, films, competitions and prizes.
