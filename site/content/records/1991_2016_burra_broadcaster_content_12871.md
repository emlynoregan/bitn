The Tea Rose Duo will perform in Burra as part of the Burra Antique Fair & Cultural Weekend.  The concert will aid the Burra Art Gallery.  The duo play a wide range of music from Bach to Led Zeppelin, Scott Joplin to Schubert.  They have appeared at venues across Australia, at the Edinburgh Fringe and in Penang and have supported Dudley Moore and Stephen Grapelli.  They will perform in the Old Courthouse on 3 May at 8 p.m.
