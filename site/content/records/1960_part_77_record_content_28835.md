The Mid North Field Days were very successful with a good influx of visitors from across Australia.  At Booborowie about 1,100 were served with lunch and at Mt Bryan and Hallett about 800.  Collinsville scored two $5,000 ram sales and Rayner & Sons of Hallett sold their stud ram for $4,000.  [Others details are printed.]
