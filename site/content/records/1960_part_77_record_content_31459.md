Aberdeen Basketball Club held their annual dinner in the Oddfellows’ Hall and c. 50 attended.  Trophies were presented:

A Grade	Best Player		Leonie Ryan

Most Improved Player	Kay Boulger

B Grade	Best Player	Susan Gill

Most Improved Player	Raelene Hawke

C Grade	Best Player	Wendy Spackman

Most Improved Player	Sandra Jones

A dance followed the dinner.
