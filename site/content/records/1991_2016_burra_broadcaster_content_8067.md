Burra Arts Council took a busload of people to Clarevale Winery for a performance by the group ‘Pot Pourri’ on Saturday 24 September.  They heard Jazz & Blues by local artist Kristen Lawler and her band and after an Italian style supper Pot Pourri featured songs from popular operas and musicals.
