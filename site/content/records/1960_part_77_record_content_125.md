27 members attended the Zone Christmas Social at Jamestown on 14 December.  Three attended the Clare Social on 22 December.  28 members visited Burra and Heathmont Hospitals with boxed biscuits for patients and to sing Christmas carols.  Supper was provided at Heathmont by Matron and Mr Barnes.  Proceeds of their Christmas dance will buy a Nativity Scene for Market Square.
