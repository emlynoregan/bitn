Ernest Claude Allen MP has been endorsed to contest the Electorate of Frome for the LCL in the election on 30 May.  The ALP candidate will be Mr G.L. Casanova, a hotel licensee of Peterborough.  Mr Allen of ‘Glen-Rae’ Spalding was born 24 October 1910 at Blyth and is married with one daughter.  He has been MP for Burra in the House of Assembly since 1968, a JP for 21 years and President of the Spalding Branch of the LCL for ten years.  He was a member of the District Council of Spalding for twelve years and a member of a string of district committees. [Which are listed at length.]  He was a farmer and grazier for 37 years and sold his property to become a legislator.  [Photo.]
