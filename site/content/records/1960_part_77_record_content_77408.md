Changes at Burra Schools

High School Principal Pat Payne and Primary School Principal Howard Groome are both leaving Burra this year.  Ann Perry retires after many years of teaching.

Clarrie and Elsie Hore are moving from Burra to the Methodist Manse at Maitland.

Peter Osterhage, younger son of Mr & Mrs Ernst Osterhage of Mt Bryan will graduate as an Army Lieutenant from Portsea Officer Training School next Friday.  His wife Del will live in Perth while he studies for a Bachelor of Applied Science.
