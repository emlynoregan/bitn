Burra High School Reunion.  Among old scholars attending were:

Bob Ratcliffe, whose parents were licensees of the Royal Exchange Hotel, spent several school years on Burra.

Keith Schultz spent 1943-45 at BHS and was a resident until he left to work in Adelaide.  His older brother owned a greengrocer’s shop occupied now by Bob Heath.

Jill  Friebe (maiden name) enjoyed meeting class mates Claire Tiver (nee Lloyd) and Barbara Samuels (nee Humphrys).  Barbara’s husband Robin is also an old scholar and was an employee of the SAR.
