Mt Bryan Methodist Church held its Anniversary last Sunday.  The preacher was Rev. V. Goldney who returned to the area after 38 years.  Miss C. Reed was organist and the combined Burra-Redruth Choir was in attendance.  David Clarkson was soloist.
