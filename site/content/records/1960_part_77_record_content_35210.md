Accidents

A car driven by Gregory J. Adam of Tea Tree Gully overturned on the Booborowie-Spalding Road at about 5.30 p.m. on Saturday when a rear tyre blew out.   The driver and passengers Misses Raelene Hood, Judith Beinke and Sandra Williams were not injured.

At about 6.30 p.m. a car driven by Mr Roy Popplewell of Cappeedee rolled over in loose gravel on the way to the station from Hallett.   Neither the vehicle nor the driver suffered damage.

At about 2.45 p.m. Saturday a car driven by Mrs Trenowden of Hanson rolled over on the Farrell Flat-Clare Road about seven miles from Clare.  The car was extensively damaged, but neither the driver nor her children passengers were seriously injured.
