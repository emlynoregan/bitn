Recent Break-ins in Burra

1.  Burra Community Sports Complex; beer, cigarettes, spirits and chips were stolen.  A 17-year-old has been reported.

2.  Burra Hotel; money, alcohol and cigarettes were taken and at least three persons were involved.

3.  Animal & Plants Control Board building near the Mine Lookout; stationery was taken and later recovered.

4.  Burra Water Tank on the Mine Hill; UHF radio.
