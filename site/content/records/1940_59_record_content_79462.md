Rural Youth Bus Tour

Margaret Kellock gives a ½-column report on the Northern Bus Tour by Rural Youth members.  The tour encompassed Stradbrooke Stud, Clare, East Bungaree, Mt Bryan, Old Canowie Beef Shorthorn Stud, Golden North Dairies, Laura Honey Depot, Wirrabara State Forest, Mr Claire Scarfe’s apiary, Pt Augusta Power Station, Whyalla Dairy, Whyalla Shipyards and Blast Furnace, BHAS Smelters, Pt Pirie City, Pt Pirie Uranium Treatment Works, Burra and the Burra Mine Site.
