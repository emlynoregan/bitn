Anzac Day.  The 11th anniversary of the Gallipoli Landing was commemorated with an open air service at the Fallen Soldiers’ Monument.  The Square was nearly circled with cars and scores of people stood.  The secretary of the RSL Mr G.H. Dow made the arrangements.  Rev. F.B. Hewitson, Rev. R.H. Lee, Rev. H. Alvey & Ensign Fischer of the Salvation Army attended.  Rev. Alvey gave the address.  £8-9-10½ was collected for the AIF Cemetery Trust Fund.
