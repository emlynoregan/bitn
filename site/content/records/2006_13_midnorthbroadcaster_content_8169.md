Squash.  Burra players went to Clare for the annual Burra v. Clare challenge and the Yvonne Barton Memorial Shield.  The team comprised Neil Webster, Travis Thamm, Alan Ogden, Justin Webster, Danny Smith, Chelsea Webster, Peter McGuiness and Cameron Smith and although Clare won for the third year in a row, Burra produced a good level of competition.
