Obituary.  Olive Hempel had lived with her husband and family in the Spalding District for about 14 years and was an energetic worker in local organisations.  She was a member of Spalding CWA and had been President for three years.  She was a keen handicraft worker.  She was an active member of the Methodist Ladies’ Guild, a Past President of the Welfare Club and was Vice-President of the Ladies’ Bowling Club at the time of her death.  She will be remembered for her fine needlework and cake decorating.

[See also 90, 16 (2), 25 Apr. 1967, page 7.]

[Born Olive May Phillips 31 July 1912, Redruth.  She was first married to Kenneth Max Auhl.]
