Kooringa Mark Lodge No. 17.

As part of its 75th Anniversary Celebrations the lodge will place a park bench near the hand pump in Market Square.  It will be handed over to Burra Burra DC Chairman Mr Graham Kellock by Worshipful Master Melvyn Pearce, at a ceremony on Thursday 2 May at 5.45 p.m.
