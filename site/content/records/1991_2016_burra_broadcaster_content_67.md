Burra All Mediums Art Workshop.  The 19991 program started on the weekend with thirteen artists from across SA spending two days painting or outdoor sketching.  The group has the opportunity to hang their work in Gaslight Inn in Market Square, courtesy of Margaret and Steven Brenton.
