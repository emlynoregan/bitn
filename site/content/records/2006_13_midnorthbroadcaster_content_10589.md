The New Lewis Board met for the first time last week in the Paxton Square Cottages Booking Office.  The Board has been formed to run and plan the operations of the Paxton Square Cottages.  Members are Bev Fleming, Helen Stockman, Graham Kellock, Reva Cullen, Peter Reilly, Jane Kellock, Paul Wilson & Richard Nicholls.  Cr Peter Reilly is the Council representative on the Board.
