The Governor Sir Mark Oliphant has accepted an invitation to open the annual show.  The Bowling Club Rooms will house an art exhibition and wine tasting.  There will be a licensed dance in the Town Hall on Saturday with an event for teenagers in the RSL Hall.  Town bus tours will operate.  On Sunday there will be the usual church services and in the afternoon the Police Band will play at the unveiling of the relocated Peacockâ€™s Chimney.  The Band will then continue its performance in Market Square.  At night the Adelaide Glee Club will perform in the Town Hall.  On Monday at the Burra North Sports Ground there will be a motor cycle display and Marching Girls and other attractions are being arranged.  The Adult Education Department will stage a gem display at the school.
