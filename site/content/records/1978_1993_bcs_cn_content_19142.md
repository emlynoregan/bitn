Royal Adelaide Show

BCS students came second in the finals of the Briggs & Stratton engine tear down competition at the Show on 8 September.  The team comprised Troy Lloyd & Paul Tiver.  Troy Edwards & Scott Tiver finished in 5th place.

A Murray Bridge team won.
