SALA

Burra artist Tanya Waters has been awarded the SALA Week 2001 Rural Youth Art Encouragement Award.  It takes the form of a half scholarship at Adelaide Central School of Art to the value of $4,000 for an artist without formal training drawn from the SALA Week focus region of Burra & District.
