Burra High School prefects were inducted at an assembly on 2 March.

Boys: Keith Neill (Head), R. Neill, W. Standing, Michael Jones, Malcolm Sieber & Robert Brandt.

Girls: Barbara Hogan (Head), Raelene Hirschausen, Suzanne Harris, Rosemary Brown, Claire Kellock & Helen Phelps.

Burra Swimming Club completed its season with a poorly attended carnival on Saturday.  [Results are printed.]

For the season the Patron’s Trophy went to Judy Willmott.
