Burra Cubs and Scouts held their AGM last Friday.  Fundraising over the past twelve months enabled the establishment of the Venturer Unit, subsidised a Cub Camp at Bowman’s Park, and enabled the installation of some flood-lighting and the building of a fire ring.

Elected: President, Peter stockman; Vice-President, Wayne Galway; Secretary, Di Day; Assistant Secretary, Dot Huxtable; Treasurer, Valda Williams and Publicity Officer, Helen Stockman.

The new Akela is Mrs Donna Salter.
