‘Pipi Storm’ a group of actors and musicians sponsored by the Arts Council, visited BCS as part of Come Out ’81.

They presented a program ‘Either You Habit or You Don’t’, which explored the use and abuse of drugs in society and in the evening they produced a ‘Kids’ Cabaret’.  [Photo from the latter.]
