Obituary.  Don Kempster, a former manager of the National Australia Bank in Burra, died recently.

[Don Kempster was manager of the Burra Branch of the NAB 1970-76.

In late 1990 he and his wife Lyn returned to Burra and took over the Market Square Snack Bar.

Probably Donald Frank Kempster, born 22 October 1918, Kensington Park.]
