Burra Swimming Pool has Icebreakers five mornings a week from 7 a.m. to 7.30.  The Burra Swimming Club has resumed activities.  A Blue Light Disco will be held at the pool on Friday 14 December, supervised by police and the pool supervisor.  It will be smoke and alcohol free.
