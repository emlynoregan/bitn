A Long Letter from teachers at BCS expresses grave doubts that Partnerships 21 will improve outcomes for students, though it will appreciably increase workloads for the principal, deputy and co-ordinators and imposes more responsibility on School Councillors.  The financial/funding implications are also unclear and worrying.
