Denis Keller was awarded the Order of Australia Medal in the Australia Day Honours in 2006.  He received the medal in an Investiture ceremony at Government House on 6 April by Her Excellency the Governor, Marjorie Jackson-Nelson.  He received his award for services to Local Government over 27 years and to the Community of Robertstown.  [He had also been Chairman of the District Council of Goyder.]
