Advt.  Elders-GM.  Land for sale by tender on account of Mrs S.J. Woollacott & Miss M.S. Woollacott.

Lot 1.	‘The Farm’ 377 acres freehold Pt Section 9 Hd Kooringa with shearing shed, two bores, etc.  Disposed as two paddocks.

Lot 2.	‘Shorts’ 436 acres freehold Block 91 Hd Hanson 2½ miles east of Hanson.  2 dams etc.  Disposed as five paddocks.

Lot 3.	‘Woollacott’s’ 567 acres 2 roods freehold.  Pt Sections 182 & 183 Hd Hanson with two roomed old stone house etc.  Disposed as seven paddocks.

Lot 4.	‘Turner’s’ 278 acres 2 roods freehold.  Pt Section 85 Hd Hanson 4 miles south of Burra.  Disposed as two paddocks.

Lot 5.	‘Pumping Station’ 57 acres 1 rood 17 perches freehold.  Pt Section 146 Hd Hanson.  One paddock adjoins Township of Hanson.  Murray water connected.

Lot 6.	‘Township Block’ 16 acres 1 rood 8 perches.  Hanson Township blocks 1-8, 10-12, 80-87 and a road.  Annual licence 5580.  Murray water.

Lot 7.	‘Kalcootra’ 795 acres Sections 55 & 59 Hd Hallett.  Perpetual lease.  14 miles northeast of Mt Bryan.  Disposed as three paddocks.
