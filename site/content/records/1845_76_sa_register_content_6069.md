Advertisement.  The SA Mining Association calls for tenders by 15 May for renting the new Public Slaughterhouse at Kooringa.  The tenant will have the right to terminate the tenancy at any time on two monthsâ€™ notice and must abide by the rules that may from time to time be made to regulate the operations of the slaughterhouse.  The tenant will be at liberty to impose charges not exceeding:

Great cattle	3/- per head

Calves	1/- per head

Sheep	4d per head

All necessary tools and implements to be found by the tenant.

Henry Ayers, Secretary, 1 May 1851.
