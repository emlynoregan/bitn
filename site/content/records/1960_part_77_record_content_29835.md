Burra Town Council, 16 May.

The Town Clerk reported attending the funeral of the former Mayor, A.B. Riggs.

A representative of the Botanic Gardens will be invited to visit Burra to advise on ways of beautifying the Burra Creek.

The Council will lay the concrete floor in the new lunch room at the oval.

A message of sympathy will be sent to the widow and family of the late A.B. Riggs.
