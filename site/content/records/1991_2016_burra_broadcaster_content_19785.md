The Annual Christian Women Communicating Intl. live-in weekend will bring more than 60 women to the Redruth Camp Centre for 27-29 August.  The theme will be ‘Lord of the Seasons’ with speaker Mrs Kay Brien from Bungendore, NSW and she will be supported by Mrs Dee Heath from Pt Augusta, representing ‘Lifeline’.
