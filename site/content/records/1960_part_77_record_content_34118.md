Football

Burra 7.14 defeated United 6.5

G. Baulderstone has resigned as Secretary of the Burra Club, having taken the position of Association Secretary.  Mr Tony Fillmore has consented to fill in as Secretary for this year.

Gladstone 9.12 defeated Spalding 10.4

Senator Arnold Drury has been invited by Burra ALP Sub-Branch to address their AGM in the RSL Hall tonight, on the subject of the approaching referendums on the possible increase in the size of the House of Representatives and on the rights of Aborigines.
