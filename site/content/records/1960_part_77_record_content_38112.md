Rev. Milton Edwards was inducted into the Burra Methodist Circuit at Redruth on Sunday evening.  The service was conducted by Rev. D.U.G. Mack of Riverton and the address was by Rev. B. Miche of Eudunda.  Also present were Rev. D. Catford of Spalding and the [Anglican] Rector of Burra Rev. G.D. Sibly.  A choir of 13 was accompanied by Mrs J. Oates at the organ.  A social gathering followed.  Mr & Mrs Edwards come to Burra from Hawker.  The Mayor E.T. Baulderstone extended a welcome from the town.
