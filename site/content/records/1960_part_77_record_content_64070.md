Burra Burra DC.  The centenary of the Burra DC was commemorated with a dinner in the Town Hall on 27 November.  The District Council of Burra was proclaimed by H.E. Rt Hon. Sir James Fergusson on 27 November 1872.  The Hon. G.T. Virgo MP unveiled a commemorative bronze plaque on the northern wall of the Town Hall balcony.  The Aberdeen Football and Tennis Clubs’ Auxiliaries catered for the occasion.
