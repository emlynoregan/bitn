Dr Jane Parker will open Goyder Veterinary Services at No 1 Queen Street on 8 November.  She will share after hours work with Dr Jack Coffey and an emergency service will be available.  The practice will offer a full medical and surgical service and hospitalisation.  There will be an open day on Sunday 7 November.  [Photo.]
