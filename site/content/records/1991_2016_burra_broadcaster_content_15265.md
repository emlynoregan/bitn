Goyder Council

Better load limit signs will be made for the Kingston Street Bridge.

The National Trust will plant a woodlot on the site of the old Burra Dump in the Burra Mine site.

CEO Steve Kerrigan will seek to meet with the Minister of Transport re the case for sealing the arterial road to Robertstown.

Council has sent a letter of disappointment to the National Australia Bank.

CFS & MFS have been asked for their position regarding the future of the MFS in Burra.
