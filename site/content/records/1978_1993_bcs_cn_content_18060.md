SA State athletics Championships

Adam Day won gold in U18 400 m hurdles, silver in the U18 800 m and discus and bronze in the 4 x 400 m relay.

Petrea Piggott won gold in the U18 400 m hurdles and bronzes in the 400 m and U20 4 x 400 m relay.

Cheryl Day won gold in the U16 400 m, silver in the 800 m and bronze in the U20 4 x 400 m relay.
