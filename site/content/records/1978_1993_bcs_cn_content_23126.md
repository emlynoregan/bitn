District Clerk, Mr Rod Walsh, will leave Burra on 7 October to take up the position of Deputy District Clerk of the District Council of victor Harbor.  The new District Clerk will be Mr Stephen Kerrigan, District Clerk of the District Council of Lower Eyre Peninsula (Cummins).  He will arrive in Burra in late October and commence duty on 4 November.
