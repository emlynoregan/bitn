The ICOMOS Conference a couple of weeks ago had about 120 people attending.  Justin McCarthy, who worked on the restoration of Morphett’s Enginehouse and the Burra Mine Museum in the 1980s was the organiser.  Douglas Alexander, local Heritage Advisor attended and showed delegates around Burra.
