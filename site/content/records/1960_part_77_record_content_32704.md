Learn to Swim Campaign.  At Burra 140-160 youngsters are receiving instruction.  About 70 of them are taking part for the first time.  Mr Paul Tattersall is being assisted by Miss Rosemary Reed and Russell Nicholls.  Miss Sue Murphy will join them next week.
