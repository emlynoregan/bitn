Handel’s Messiah was performed at St Mary’s Church on Sunday afternoon 26 November.  The combined Woodville Choral Group and the Gawler Barossa Oratorio Choir numbering 120, along with soloists, presented the piece.  John Hall played the organ and Rosemary Nairn (nee Reed) conducted.
