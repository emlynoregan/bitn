Burra Quilters will be part of a Jubilee 150 Art & Craft Exhibition 19-20 August.  They will have a quilt exhibition ‘Needles Through Time’.  They will present a sewn banner to Burra Burra DC on 19 August – worked to a design by Len Bence.  Burra’s National Trust has loaned Australia’s oldest dress c. 1820 and hand sewn.
