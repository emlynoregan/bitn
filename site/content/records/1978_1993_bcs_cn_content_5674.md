Burra Sports Complex

Site works have commenced.  Burra Burra DC is preparing the site and the construction contract has been let to Precision Builders Pty Ltd of Holden Hill.  The complex will include two male change rooms, one female change room, an umpires’ change room, canteen and cooking facilities, bar facilities, a recreation area, storage facilities and a time keeper’s box.  It is expected to be completed in 16 weeks.
