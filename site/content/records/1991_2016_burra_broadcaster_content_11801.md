Break-ins at Booborowie

On 1-2 December three Booborowie premises were entered:

The General Store where cigarettes, confectionery and blank cassette tapes to a total value of c. $800 were stolen.

Bowling Club where the front window was broken and home-made biscuits and cash was stolen.

Booborowie Seed, which suffered the theft of a $200 Australian Mint gold coin.
