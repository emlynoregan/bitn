Burra Primary School

The winner of the Andrew Tennant Award for Dux of the School for 1966 was Ann Brak, the eldest daughter of Mr & Mrs J. Brak of Burra North.  She obtained 961 out of a possible 1,000.  The Brak family migrated from Holland about 14 years ago and Ann has attended school at Burra since Grade I.  [Top positions in other classes are listed.]
