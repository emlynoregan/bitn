Burra Primary School

Mr A.A. Kies has been appointed headmaster.  He comes to Burra from Marook [sic: Moorook?].  Since Mr Pattrick’s retirement Mr J.C. Morrison had been acting headmaster.  Miss E. Mounce retired in May after 2½ years at the school, prior to her marriage to Mr John Carpenter.  Staff are now: Mr A. Kies, Mr J.C .Morrison, Mr H. Kowald, Mrs T.G. Perry, Mrs W. Pens and Mrs H. Kowald.
