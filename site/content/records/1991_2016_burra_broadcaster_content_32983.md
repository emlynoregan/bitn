Vandalism and Theft.  On Saturday morning at 6 a.m. plate glass windows were broken in the front of Bernhard’s next to the hairdresser’s and at the Cook o’ Burra snack bar in Market Square.  A microwave and some food is reported taken from the latter.  A 20-year-old Burra man has been charged.
