Burra Red Cross held its AGM on 12 July.  Twenty members attended.  Elected: President, Mrs H.J. Topsfield; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Pitcher & E. Rooke; Secretary, Mrs N.P. Sieber and Treasurer, Mrs J.N. McBride.
