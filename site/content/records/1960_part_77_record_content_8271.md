Mr John Hammer, ANZ Bank accountant at Burra for four years, has been transferred to Mt Barker.  He has been in the district for 12 years, having transferred here in 1942 and joining the Air Force a year later.  He returned in 1946.  After five years in Adelaide he returned in 1957.  He has been active in town affairs and sport.  He is the President of the Lawn Tennis Club and Secretary/Treasurer of the Air Force Association and a member of the Golf Club.  His replacement will be Mr Don Zanker, presently officer in charge of the Savings Bank Department Adelaide Branch.
