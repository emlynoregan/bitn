An RAA Grant will provide $4,921 for the BCS Bus Zone and Car Park Safety Project.  The RAA has made grants totalling $51,529 across SA in this inaugural distribution.  The BCS project will see safety fencing the full length of the bus zone in the middle island of the main car park with two openings and a zebra crossing.
