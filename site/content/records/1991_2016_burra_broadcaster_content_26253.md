The Burra-Clare-Snowtown Health Service will receive up to $800,000 over three years to provide additional services, including physiotherapy, home based palliative care, podiatry, youth work, men’s health promotion and community development.  It will pay for four full time equivalent positions and an upgrade to transport services.
