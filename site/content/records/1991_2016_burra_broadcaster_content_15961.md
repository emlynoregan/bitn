BBH Social Club will re-introduce a Sportsperson of the Year Award at a dinner to be held at Burra Town Hall on 18 June.  The award was last presented in 1987.

Previous winners included Jean Roberts, John Redden, Di Day, Estelle Nicholls and Rick Williams.

Junior winners have included Rosie Lloyd, Darren James and Petrea Piggott.
