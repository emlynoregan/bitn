Aberdeen Football & Netball Auxiliaries had a very successful year both socially and financially.  The Football Auxiliary made a donation to the club of $878 at the start of the football season and since then $1,786 has been raised.  100 new chairs were purchased for the clubroom at $800.  Miss Clarice Reed was given life membership of the Football Club in recognition of eleven years as Treasurer of the Auxiliary.
