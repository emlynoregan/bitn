National Trust – Burra Branch held its 3rd AGM on Tuesday in the CWA Rooms.

Elected: President, Mr S.J .Woollacott; Vice-President, Mr I.L. Auhl; Secretary, Miss M.S. Woollacott; Treasurer, Mrs C. O’Connor.  A minute of appreciation was recorded for retiring President Mr A. Tennant.

The President’s Report is printed on page 4.  Highlights:

An appeal was launched for funds to repair the building purchased as a museum and of a target of $1,600 a sum of $1,261 was collected.

Headquarters loaned $420 to enable repairs to be completed.

The front room has been thoroughly renovated and the outside has seen new guttering, roof repairs, paving in the verandah, and has been repainted.

A committee was set up to sort donated exhibits.

In 3 to 4 months articles were sorted, labelled and arranged.

The Museum was opened by the Chief Secretary the Hon. A.J. Shard when the Premier was unable to come at the last minute.
