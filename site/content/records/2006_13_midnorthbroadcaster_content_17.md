Weather.  A supercell on 29 January developed between Orroroo and Peterborough and moved in to the Booborowie Valley.  Booborowie rainfall reports included falls of 24.4 mm and 30 mm in less than thirty minutes.  Rain in parts of the cell were recorded at rates up to 174.2 mm per hour.  Wind speeds exceeded 66 k.p.h.  The storm died out at about 5.15 p.m. to the west of Manoora.
