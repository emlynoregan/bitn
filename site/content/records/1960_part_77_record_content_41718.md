Burra Town Council, 26 August

Garden seat to be installed on the footpath near Matthews in Market Square.

The ladies’ toilet in the Town Hall has been repaired.

The new surface for the Town Hall floor is to be installed by mid-September.

A general clean-up of the cemetery has been effected.

The Minister of Tourism advises a grant of up to $162 for power points in the Caravan Park – to be completed by 21 December.

The Burra Oval Management Committee wants the Education Department to pay $50 p.a. rental of the oval – an increase from $20.

J.J. & B.C. Richards were granted approval to install one dual petrol bowser on their Burra north property.

The Town Clerk will negotiate with A.L. Clode & Son about selling a portion of Mary Crescent with a 25 ft frontage to Best Place to allow expansion of his business.

It was decided to declare a rate of 23 cents in the dollar and a special rate of 1⅔c in the dollar with a minimum rate on any one property of $14.

A meeting will be arranged on 21 September to discuss all matters relating to the amalgamation of the Burra Town Council with the Burra Burra District Council.
