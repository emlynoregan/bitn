BALI 2 BURRA SALA exhibition was opened at the White cedars Café on Sunday afternoon by Jessica Dames.  The exhibition has a Balinese theme, with exotic paintings and hebel stone sculptures reflective of Balinese culture, the works of sculptor and painter Wayan Dudug.
