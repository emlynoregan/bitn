‘Farella’ the property of R.J. & E.C. Gillett of Burra, located 15 miles east of Robertstown was sol on Thursday at the Robertstown Institute for £4-2-0 an acres to Messrs Eberhard & Co.

On Friday another small property of 52 acres was sold by the same owners to J.G. Carpenter for £28-10-0 per acre.
