Weather latterly has been extremely cold.  Last Friday was the coldest day on record at many SA centres.  Yunta recorded a maximum of 6.2ºC and Yongala one of 8.5ºC – though in Yongala’s case it had recorded a maximum of only 4.2º on 15 July 1984.
