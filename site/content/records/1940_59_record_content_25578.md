St Patrick’s Ball at the Burra Town Hall on Friday was one of the brightest of its kind in Burra for some time.  It was in favour of funds for St Joseph’s Church.  Dick Barratt was MC and Darrell Field’s Band provided the music.  Dancing continued to 2 a.m.  [Details in ¾ column.]
