Advt.  Elders-GM will sell on instructions from C.A. & F.M. McDonald a 1,000 acre freehold property known as ‘Rockhill’, 7 miles south of Booborowie, in the Burra RSL Hall 7 November.

Lot 1.	‘Homestead’ 326 acres, Sections 419, 420, 421 in the Hundred of Ayers, including a substantial stone house of six rooms, etc.

Lot 2.	326 acres, Sections 769, 770, 771 & 996 in the Hundred of Hanson, disposed in six paddocks.

Lot 3.	312 acres, Sections 584 & 585 in the Hundred of Hanson disposed as eight paddocks.
