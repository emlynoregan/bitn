Burra Burra DC.  The Council has received a proposal to close and sell part of Ursula Street, Burra.  Any representation on this question should be lodged in writing to the District Clerk by 1 May 1987.

Council has also received an application to lease part of Truro Street and an unnamed road dividing allotments 318 & 82 & 92.  Objections should be lodged within 14 days.
