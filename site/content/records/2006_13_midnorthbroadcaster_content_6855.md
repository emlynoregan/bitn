By-election Result

Independent candidate Geoff Brock, Mayor of Pt Pirie, has taken the seat of Frome from the Liberal Party. He ended the primary count just 30 votes ahead of the Labor candidate and so secured enough ALP preferences to win the seat by 665 votes.  [Photo]
