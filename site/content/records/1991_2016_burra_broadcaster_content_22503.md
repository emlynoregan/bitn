Heritage Horse Ride. This event saw two groups leave Broome WA on 19 April to ride to Sydney by 13 August.  The northern group are travelling via Darwin, Tennant Creek, Mt Isa, Rockhampton, Brisbane and Armidale.  The southern group is travelling via Geraldton, Perth, Esperance, Pt Augusta, Adelaide, Hobart, Melbourne, Albury and Canberra to reach Sydney.  The theme is ‘The Spirit that Built a Nation’.  The southern leg is due in Burra on Tuesday 20 June and will meet Northern Councils representatives and visit the school.  In the evening they will be guests of the Mid North Carriage Driving and Burra Pony Clubs at the Burra Showgrounds.
