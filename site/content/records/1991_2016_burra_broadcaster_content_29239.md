Burra Regional Art Gallery has an Australian Premier for a photographic exhibition spanning 1890-1940, entitled Images of Outback Australia. The display of 84 images has been curated by Dr Philip Jones of the SA Museum.  Seven photographers are represented:

Frank Gillen	anthropologist

Samuel Albert White	ornithologist

W.D. Walker	roving medico

George Aiston	policeman

Ernest Kramer	itinerant missionary

Cecil Hackett	anthropologist

Rex Battarbee	artist

The exhibition runs 31 October to 14 November.
