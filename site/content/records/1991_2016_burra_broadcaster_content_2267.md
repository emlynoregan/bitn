Battle of the Bands

About 150 people attended the event to see local band ‘Touch Wood’ take out the first prize in the over 25s section.  The band comprises Kerry Green, Brett Davey and Dale Barnes.  In the under 25s section the Yorke Peninsula Band ‘Farewell Captain Doozch’ came first and was also the People’s Choice.
