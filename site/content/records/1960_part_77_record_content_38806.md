Mid North Football League held its AGM in Saddleworth Institute 22 March.  Elected: Patron, W.E. Blatchford; President, B.A. Kench (Auburn); Vice-President, L.A. Twartz (Saddleworth).

‘A’ Grade teams for 1968: Aberdeen, Riverton, United, Auburn, Manoora, Mintaro & Robertstown.

‘B’ Grade teams for 1968: Riverton, Robertstown, Aberdeen & United.

Colts teams for 1968: Riverton, United, Robertstown, Auburn, Aberdeen & Mintaro.
