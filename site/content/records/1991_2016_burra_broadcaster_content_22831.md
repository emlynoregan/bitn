Matt Reilly of Burra recently took part in a 15 day expedition on the inland waterways of SA.  The small party was led by Rex Ellis & were the first to go down the Ana Branch of the Warburton to Lake Eyre, beginning the 600km round trip from Mungerannie Station.

Burra Community School Council has voted to implement a drugs policy following a recent confiscation of marijuana from a student.
