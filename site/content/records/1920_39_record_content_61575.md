Burra Liberal & Country League Women’s Branch held its AGM 5 June.  There was an address by Mr Willoughby.  Elected: President, Mrs I.J. Warnes; Secretary, Miss Wilkinson and Vice-Presidents, Mesdames E. Jettner, C.J. Murray, G.G. Hawkes, S.H. Robinson, J. Flower and Miss Rogers.  [The year’s activities are reported in ½ column.]
