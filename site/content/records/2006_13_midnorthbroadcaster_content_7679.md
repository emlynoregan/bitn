Burra Golf Club.  Gus Wenzel had a big summer of golf this season.  He won all three club trophies: the Jennison Summer Match Cup, the Barton Summer Stableford Challenge (for the 6th time) and the Sumer Eclectic event.  He also managed a hole in one on the 8th hole.  This is the second time he has scored a hole in one on that hole.
