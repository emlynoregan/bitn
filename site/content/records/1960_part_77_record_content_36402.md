Weather.  Showers over five days of the past week brought 52 points of rain to Burra.  On Wednesday there was a short, but heavy fall of hail at about 8.55 a.m.  It banked up against fences and buildings and lingered till the afternoon.  To the east the falls have been only 10-20 points.
