Don Barker, who was with the National Bank in Burra (1956-59) and was an active member of Rural Youth while here, has been a member of the ‘Therry Society’ and of the ‘Adelaide Theatre Group’.  He played in the world premier of [Patrick White’s] Season at Sarsaparilla staged recently in Adelaide.  He is presently in Sydney for a six week season with the Australian Elizabethan Theatre Trust.  He recently got married.
