Goyder Council has decided to increase its overall rate revenue by 7.1% for the coming year.

The rise in valuation across the whole area was 20.5%.

A fixed charge of $175 applies to each assessment and rates in the dollar are:

0.6662 for Burra township

0.3989 for Eudunda township

0.3354 for other towns and rural areas
