Burra High School.  The Annual Swimming Carnival was held on Friday last.  The competition was a great success with Draysey House coming out the victors.  Cup Winners:

Senior Boys’ Cup	David Popplewell

Senior Girls’ Cup	Jillian Eberhard

Junior Boy’s Cup	Michael Broderick

Junior Girls’ Cup	Vicki Bernhardt
