Burra Arts Council Branch

The 38th country branch of the SA Arts Council was formed here on 12 September.  The SA General Manager addressed a meeting of 13 and that meeting elected Mike Ford as President, Muriel Bence as Secretary and Ruth Bateman as Treasurer.  This will allow Burra to apply to be included on the itinerary of touring artists and companies.  Annual subscription is $2.
