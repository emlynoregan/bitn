Bowls.  Champion of Champions

In the singles at Hallett on Saturday last Joe Lloyd of Burra defeated Barry Huppatz of Saddleworth to take the title.

Champion Rink title went to Clare Green skippered by Jeff. Gilles.

In the Associates contest Burra Gold were runners-up.
