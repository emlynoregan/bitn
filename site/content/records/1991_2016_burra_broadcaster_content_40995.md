St Mary’s Pipe Organ fundraiser concert drew a crowd of about 70 people, who enjoyed the playing of four accomplished organists:

Michael Ford from Minlaton and a former BCS staff member.

Jan Cockington of Adelaide and formerly of Burra.

Denis Nettleton, who once worked in a Burra band

Rosemary Nairn nee Reed, who was born in Burra.

Admissions and donations raised more than $2,000.
