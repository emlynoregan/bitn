6 August 1981

Polio Vaccinations

The next series of the oral vaccine for poliomyelitis will be delivered on Friday 28 August at 4 p.m.

Applications close at the Council Office on Wednesday 19 August.

Burra Burra District Council Elections.  The following members of Council retire on 3 October by the effluxion of time:

Baldina Ward	 A.A. Barker

Booborowie Ward	J.S. Hawker

Hanson Ward	D.L. Lloyd

Mt Bryan Ward	A.J. Gebhardt

Burra Town Ward	R.B. Jennison

F.R. Ford

Nominations are called by 4 September with elections on 3 October if required.

The Burra Pre-School (Kindergarten) premises were sold last Friday for $10,000.  The kindergarten hopes to have its new premises available within three months.

The recent evening show of local entertainers and the Light United Brass Band resulted in a profit of $1,231 for the Kindergarten.

Burra Burra DC has lodged an objection to the route proposed to the west of the town for the giant new electrical power line.  [Port Augusta-Adelaide]

One argument is that it would likely interfere with a possible future air strip on the area near the Burra Mine overburden paddock.
