Mr R.H. (Dick) Smith of Elder, Smith & Co. will retire on 30 June after 50 years’ service with the company.  He was born at Bordertown, where he began working for Elder’s.  He has been stationed there four times and also at Pt Augusta, Crystal Brook and has managed branches at Wilmington, Bordertown, Orroroo and Burra before being promoted to the Stock Department in Adelaide in 1949.
