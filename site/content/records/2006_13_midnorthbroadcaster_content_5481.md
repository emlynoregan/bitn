BCS.  Nathaniel O’Brien and other students recently attended a music workshop in Pt Pirie as part of the Southern Flinders Live Music Festival.  It was a three day event and ended with a concert in the Keith Michell Theatre on the Friday night.  Nathaniel won the overall points trophy, Junior Gospel and Junior Vocal sections and was second in the Open Country Rock Duet, singing with his mother.
