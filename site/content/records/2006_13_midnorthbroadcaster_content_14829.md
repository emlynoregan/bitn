Catherine Fraser & Duncan Smith, a Scots/Australian fiddle and piano duo will visit Burra on 16 June as part of a series of concerts in country SA.  They will present a concert in the Scottish fiddle idiom.  The venue will be the Burra Uniting Church Hall at 7.30 p.m.
