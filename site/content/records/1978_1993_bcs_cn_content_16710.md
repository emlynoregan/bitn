Burra School & Community News

25 May 1988

Burra School 7 Community News

Donations are falling well short of what it costs to print the news each week.  An appeal is made to bridge the gap.

Birth.  To Jill & Paul Culbertson last week, a son.

Obituary.  Mrs Margaret Miller of Hallett died last week.

David Madigan leaves next Tuesday to join the Regular Army at Kapooka, NSW.

Redruth Gaol.  The opening, Bicentennial Day at BCS and the Breaker Morant re-trial all went off successfully.

Colin & Dianne Goodridge will be given a farewell tea at Leighton Hall on Saturday 28 May.

Burra CWA calls tenders for the repair and repainting of windows on its branch building.

Burra Uniting Church Fellowship trading table on 6 May raised $525.
