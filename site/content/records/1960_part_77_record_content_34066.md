Anzac Service.  The annual event was conducted last Sunday afternoon by the Rev. G.D. Sibly.  The Marananga Band attended and a fair number of the public.  Wreaths were laid on behalf of the Burra RSL, Demobilised Soldiers’ Assoc., Air Force Assoc., Fathers’ Assoc., Burra Town Council and the Burra Burra DC.

The evening ecumenical Anzac Service at Burra was held in St Mary’s conducted by the Rector Rev. Sibly with the sermon preached by Rev. M.A. Lind of the Methodist Church.

At Hallett the service was held in the Hallett Institute Supper Room, conducted by Rev. A. Houston.
