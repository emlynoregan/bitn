Burra Basketball Club had a smorgasbord tea at the Commercial Hotel on Tuesday evening to wind up the year.  Trophies were presented:

Burra Blues Best & Fairest	Miss D. Jones

Burra Gold Best & Fairest	Miss H. Angel

Burra Gold had a victory dinner at the Kooringa Hotel on Friday night.

‘C’ Grade had a dinner at the home of Captain E. Broderick and presented trophies to:

Best and Fairest	E. Broderick

Most Improved	R. Ryan.
