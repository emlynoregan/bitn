Advertorial.  The 9th Annual Jailhouse Rock Festival is almost here – 27 January 2007.

It kicks off at the Burra Hotel with Paul O’Bryan’s Band on Friday night.  On Saturday at 10.30 a.m. the Adelaide Rock ʼn’ Roll Demonstration Team will put on a show in Market Square.  In the afternoon Paul O’Bryan and friends will be at the Commercial Hotel.  At 7.30 p.m. the main event at the Redruth Gaol will be headlined by Razzle Dazzle.  On Sunday morning there will be a brunch on Thomas Pickett Reserve.
