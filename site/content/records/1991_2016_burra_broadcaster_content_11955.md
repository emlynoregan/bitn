The Burra Antique and Decorating Fair on the weekend of 11-12 May was combined with a cultural weekend organised by the Main Street Committee with grants from the Festivals Assistance Fund and the Country Arts Fund.  There was an art exhibition at the gallery, a photographic exhibition, a barber-shop quartet, a poetry workshop and a dinner dance.
