Burra Burra DC, 2 November meeting in the Town Hall.

Elphick Street will be closed and sold to L.E. Fuller for $10 plus legal fees.

The National Trust, three Town Ward Councillors and the Clerk will visit the old Redruth Gaol and list repairs.

Load limit signs are to be placed on town bridges:

5 tons on the Thames Street, Chapel Street, Queen Street and Kangaroo street bridges.

12 tons on the Ayers Street Bridge.

Burra Burra Sports Association wrote concerning the sports ground.  Council resolved to accept the Burra Sports Ground.

The Education Department wrote about closing of the road at Burra School.
