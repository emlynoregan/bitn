Burra Hospital.  Construction by Mr A. Mass of the new block has begun, comprising private rooms, Matron’s Office and Secretary-Manager’s Office.  The project will cost about £22,000.  Donations have reached £5,673-8-2.  The Hospital’s financial position has improved with better collection of patient fees and more patients in Hospital Benefits Funds.  Costs have fallen while wages are static.
