Advt.  Thimbles & Things 20a Commercial Street, Burra, will not be open over Easter, but from 25 to 28 March will sell all haberdashery, all knitting yarns, all remnants, all fashion and craft fabrics (not already discounted) and all gift lines at 20% below the marked price.
