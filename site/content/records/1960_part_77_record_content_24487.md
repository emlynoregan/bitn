Aberdeen Football Club Women’s Auxiliary held its AGM at the home of Miss C. Reed.  In the year this small group handled £495.  Elected: President, F. Scroop; Vice-Presidents, P. Kakoschke & V. Fiebig; Secretary, D. Gill; Assistant Secretary, D. Reed and Treasurer, C. Reed.
