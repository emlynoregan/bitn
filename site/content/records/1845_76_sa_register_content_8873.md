Advertisement.  The Burra Burra Mines.

Notice is hereby given that the large pumping engine at the Mines will begin forking the water in the first week of January 1855.  Constant employment will then be given to a large number of tutworkers, tributers, and also to mining carpenters, smiths, sawyers, fillers, landers, ore dressers, carters, labourers and others.  Apply to the undersigned or to Captain Roach at the Mine.

Henry Ayers, Secretary, SA Mining Association, Adelaide, 12 December 1854.
