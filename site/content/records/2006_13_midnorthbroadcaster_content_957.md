Golf.  Di Day & Reva Cullen won the Women’s Golf of SA Mid North District Foursome at Quorn recently.  It is only the third time in more than 26 years that Burra has won this prestigious trophy and the last time was in 1991, when Di Bernhardt and Di Day won it at Barossa Valley.
