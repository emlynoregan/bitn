Cricket.  The wind-up day for the season was wet and attendance was low. The shields were presented to the winning teams.  In the 1st Division the Mokota Shield went to Booborowie, while in the 2nd Division the Mayfield Shield went to Burra footballers.  Cricketer of the Year was John Honan of Hallett.
