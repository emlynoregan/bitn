Mr & Mrs M.B. Davey, formerly of Burra and now of Chamroc Ave. Prospect, celebrate their Golden Wedding tomorrow, having been married at Pine Point Methodist Church 9 March 1910.  They have three children: Lyell of Mallala, Win. Mrs S. Pearce of Burra and Mervyn (RAAF deceased).
