Letter from Roman & Betty Piwowar who were in Burra in 1981 on teacher exchange from Canada.  The letter outlines their lives since returning to Canada and those of their children, one of whom was born while they were here.

Letter.  Tiffany Murray, Project Manager of the Burra Cultural Centre writes to say that with the aim of establishing Burra Town Hall as a centre for weddings, she is establishing a registry of all associated services in Burra and calls on all to contact her if they need to be included.

Letter from Stephen Angel on behalf of the Year 11 Class of 1978.  He writes to express his gratitude for the great work of all involved with each of the events of the weekend of celebration.

Marriage.  Adelaide, 5 April

Elly Nicholls married John Monfries.
