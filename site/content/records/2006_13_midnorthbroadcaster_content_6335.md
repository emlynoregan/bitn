Ram Sales Continue

Collinsville sold 118 for an average $1,134 and a top of $10,500.

Other private sales previously included $30,000 for a Poll Merino to Wes Titley of Sherlock Poll Merino Stud and one to Argentinian buyers for $17,000.

Kelvale sold 100 at an average $1,366 with a top of $4.200.

Old Ashrose sold 80 for an average $983 and a top of $2,600.

Springvale North sold 74 for an average $591 and a top of $2,000.
