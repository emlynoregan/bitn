The Hon. T.M. Casey Minister of Tourism, Recreation and Sport advises that $5,000 has been granted as a dollar for dollar subsidy on the beautification of the Burra Creek area of the town.  It is anticipated that stone barrier walls will be erected on the bank across from the Town Hall.  Banks generally will be cleaned up and trees and ground cover planted.
