Obituary.  Mrs Thomas Nevin died at her residence in Millswood last Friday aged 67.  She was Agnes, second daughter of the late Mr & Mrs W. Rabbich of Aberdeen and lived in Burra till her marriage to Mr Thomas Nevin formerly a teacher at Copperhouse.  She was educated at Burra State School after which she entered the Education Department and was a teacher till her marriage.  Both Mr & Mrs Nevin visited Burra last year for the Back to Copperhouse school celebrations.  Prior to his retirement Mr Nevin was for many years headmaster at Victor Harbor.
