Burra Town & District Councils held a combined meeting on 5 November in the [District] Council Chamber in Commercial Street.  The Town and District Clerks, R.K. Baker and T.G. Perry respectively, also attended.  DC Chairman Cr G.R. Finch presided.  Cr Finch spoke in favour of the union of the two councils.

Mayor Baulderstone then also spoke in favour, mentioning points where improvements could be made.  Each member of the councils than spoke in favour of union.  Cr T. Quinn moved that the meeting was in favour of the union of the two councils.  Cr B. Kotz seconded and the motion was carried unanimously.

Cr T. Quinn moved and Cr C.L. Phillips seconded that the petition include a request for the mayor of the district to be retained.  Carried unanimously.  The meeting adjourned to a combined meeting to be called as soon as possible after the 2 December.
