AGL Hallett Wind Farm will host the 2014 Sydney to London Classic Car Marathon Rally on 17 April.  The Australian leg has been named the John Giddings Memorial Trophy after the Australian rally car enthusiast who died last year.  Cars will leave Sydney on 12 April and travel to Perth in 20 stages by 22 April.
