Goyder Council

John Brak has been appointed senior administration officer.

Various proposals for changes to the Market Square area are displayed in the Council foyer.  Comment is invited.

Burra Community Print has added another printing press in their Commercial Street premises.  The three-tonne Fuji press was off-loaded last week.

Howard Matson & Bev Fleming have been appointed interim managers at Paxton Square Cottages.
