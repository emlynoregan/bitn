Burra Town Hall will have an open day on 3 March from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m. to show off work done in upgrading the hall and to show what the BCCC has in mind for the next three years.  It will also serve as a welcome to new residents with tea and coffee and an information pack on the town and district.
