Mt Bryan Soldiers’ Memorial Hall on 20 March was the site of an Anniversary of Anzac Dinner and screening of a film made by Sir Hubert Wilkins during WWI, Bapaume to Bullencourt and another Australia at War, made in the 1950s using footage from a number of cinematographers, including Hurley and Wilkins and a third by Wilkins on the mail service from Australia to the theatres of war.  In May Sir Hubert Wilkins will be inducted into the Australian Cinematographers’ Society Hall of Fame.
