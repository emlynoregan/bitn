Bimbowrie Station has been bought by the SA Government for $2.3 million as a National Park.   It is located near the SA-NSW border and comprises 730 square kilometres.  Heritage buildings on it include the Field Centre used by Sir Douglas Mawson in his Geological Survey of the Olary Ranges.  The station has a number of interesting Geological features.  It lies some 30 km north of Olary on the southern edge of the Strzelecki Desert.
