Burra Football Club held its AGM.  Past President Mr W. Brown reported a successful season.  They won the ‘A’ Grade Premiership.  Members of the newly formed Aberdeen Football Club attended to put their case for having two clubs in the town.  Elected: Patron, Mr Reg. Warnes; President, Mr J. Merkel; Vice president Mr Bert Beinke and Secretary-Treasurer, Mr T. Forby.
