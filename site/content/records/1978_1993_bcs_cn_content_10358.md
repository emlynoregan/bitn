Ilene Campbell has retired after 31 years as cleaner of the State Bank of SA (formerly the Savings Bank of SA).  She has worked for a whole series of managers: J. Sorrell, B. Gryst, D. Bannister, W. Moore, R. Buckton, D. Nettleton, W. Jenner and P. Bartlett.
