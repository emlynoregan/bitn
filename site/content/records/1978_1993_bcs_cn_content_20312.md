The Orienteering Association of SA will hold National Championships in and around Burra over Easter 1990.

The program of events is printed, running from Friday 13 April to Saturday 21 April.

[Though the events from 18-21 April were in the Wilpena area of the Flinders Ranges.]

The latest information suggested 900 competitors and their families would attend.
