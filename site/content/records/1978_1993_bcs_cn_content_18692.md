Burra Hospital Ladies Auxiliary Annul Report

For the year there were 29 financial members and an average attendance of 12.  Fundraising this year came completely from catering: State Bank Cocktail Party, Lodge Ball, Copper Festival, Melbourne Cup Luncheon, Council Teas, Nursing & Area Health Convention and CFS Afternoon Teas.  Nett income was $3,574.85.

Elected were: President, Celine Stockman; Vice-Presidents, Pauline Venning & Betty Thamm; Secretary, Jan Tiver; Assistant Secretary, Joan Berryman and Treasurer, Kay Boulger.

Red Cross Calling Appeal raised $854.20.
