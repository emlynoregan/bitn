Burra Coursing Club met on 2 March and has decided to ask the NCC for two dates: 5 & 6 June and 7 & 8 August to hold a 24 dog stake at each meeting.  Elected were

Patron:	H.F. Saunders of Gum Creek

President:	F. Knowles

Treasurer:	J. Allen

Hon. Sec.:	H. Bosisto
