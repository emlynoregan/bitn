Porter’s Lagoon Spectacular on Sunday raised $2,000 for community projects in the Gilbert Valley area.  The day was sponsored by the Gilbert Valley Lions Club.  There were water skiing displays, yacht races, swimming races, sideshows, food stalls and booths.
