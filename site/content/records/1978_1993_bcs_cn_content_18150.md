Merino Capital Champion Ram and Special Video Sale at Burra Oval Wednesday 15 March.

6.00 p.m.	Dinner

7 .00p.m.	Merino Capital Champion Ram Judging

7.30 p.m.	Guest Speaker, Tim Marcus-Clark, Manager of the State Bank of SA

7.50 p.m.	Presentation of the R.N. Collins Trophy and Prizes

8.00 p.m.	Video Sale of Rams etc.

Champion Ram		$2,000

Reserve Champion	$1,000
