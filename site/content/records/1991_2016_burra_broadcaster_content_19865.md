Adelaide Festival of Arts.  Four country community celebrations will be held in conjunction with the Adelaide Festival; at Burra, Beachport, Penneshaw and Streaky Bay.  There will be a variety of daytime performances, installations, exhibitions, workshops and so forth.  There will be a public meeting on Friday 17 September to consider the options for Burra.  The event is planned for Sunday 12 March 2000 to coincide with the Twilight Jazz Affair.
