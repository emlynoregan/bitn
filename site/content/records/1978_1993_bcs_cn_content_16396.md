Interschool Athletics

BCS scored its 8th successive win last week, but there are signs its stranglehold is weakening.  Kapunda picked up 100 points on Burra this year.  Burra dominated the longer distance events and the relays.  The stars were:

Petrea Piggott with 5 wins

Cheryl Day with 8 wins and 3 records

Petrice Messenger with 4 wins

Mandy Drew, Adam Day, Julie-Anne Bernhardt and Rhett Heness with 3 wins each

[Other winners are listed.]

Team Scores: Burra 1299, Kapunda 1049, Riverton 850 and Eudunda 607.
