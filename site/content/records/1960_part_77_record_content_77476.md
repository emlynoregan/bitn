The Ironmine Methodist Church Trust Committee held its final meeting on Monday last week.  The sale and transfer of the property have been completed.  Committee members, formerly Trustees were thanked by the Chairman Rev. Clarrie Hore and special thanks were paid to Ray Lloyd who was Secretary and Treasurer since 1948.  The committee was then dissolved.
