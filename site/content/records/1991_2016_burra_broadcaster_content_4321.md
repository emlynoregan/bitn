Burra Tourism will be promoted at the Royal Adelaide Show, where 9 m2 have been booked in Centennial Hall.  The Tourist Office will have similar displays at the Caravan & Camping Show, Adelaide International Expo and the AFTA Travel Fair.  The window of the SA Travel Centre has been booked for March and September next year.
