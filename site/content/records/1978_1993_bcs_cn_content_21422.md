Burra Burra Hospital AGM Report

The new Board comprises Chairman Harry Quinn, Vice-Chairman, Ron Thamm and Dick Secomb, Celine Stockman, John Brak, Catherine McPherson, Gordon Wooton, Des Jennings (Robertstown Council), Geoff Dare (Hallett Council) and Colleen Woodgate (Burra Burra Council).

In his report Mr Quinn thanked staff, board members and the community for their support and congratulated the CEO Mr Chilton and Director of Nursing Mrs Phillips and Senior Staff for developing and planning renovations and obtaining Health Commission approval and funding of $200,000 for the project.  The transfer of the Day Care Centre function to the Aberdeen Football Clubrooms also attracted a $76,000 Government grant.  The relocation is expected to occur in October.

Burra National Trust is organising a Mystery Bus Tour for Sunday 14 October.

Adelaide Children’s Hospital Auxiliary is holding a Fashion Parade at the Civic Centre at 8 p.m. Friday 2 November.

Burra Catholic Women’s League raised $432.55 at their trading table on 14 September.
