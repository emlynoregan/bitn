BCS sent 22 athletes to the SA Country Amateur Athletics Championships.  Many performed personal bests, but the stars were:

Rosalie Lloyd	3 gold medals and two second places from five events.

Golds in U14 200 m, 400 m and long jump.

Craig McDowell	Gold in U14 Boys’ High Jump

Graham Kessell	Gold in U14 Boys’ Javelin
