Athletics

Two local athletes have been selected for the 1989 Shell junior Talent Squad.  This gives each $150 towards travel or equipment to be used by June 1990.  Congratulations to Adam Day for the 400 m hurdles and to Cheryl Day for the 100 m, 200 m and 400 m events.
