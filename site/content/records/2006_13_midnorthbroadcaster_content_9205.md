The Mid North Broadcaster welcomes a new editor, Craig Treloar, from the end of October.  He took over from Kate Harden.  He also edits the River News for Waikerie and the Mid Murray.  Mr Treloar holds the degree of Bachelor of Media from the University of Adelaide and began his career with the Murray Pioneer in Renmark.
