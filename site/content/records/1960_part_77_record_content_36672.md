Accident.  On 10 October about 8 p.m. a car driven by George Poulas of Plympton skidded at the intersection of the by-pass road and Koonoona Road and hit a steel pole.  The vehicle was extensively damaged and the driver suffered lacerations to his left arm, elbow, leg and nose and bruised kidneys.
