Court.

There is a long report of a case against Henry Goodwin, baker, charged with selling underweight bread.  The inspector found an average ½ oz shortage in 12 loaves he weighed.  Goodwin’s driver, the lad Arthur Woollacott, was charged with not carrying scales and weights.  Goodwin was fined £1 and the costs in both cases: the costs being £3-11-6.
