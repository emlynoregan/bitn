Accident/Obituary.  On Thursday afternoon on the main road near Black Springs a sedan car driven by Mrs R.E. Dewhirst and a utility driven by Mr C.C. Hopgood of Black Springs collided at the intersection with the Merildin chain road.  It was raining at the time.  As a consequence Mr C.C. Hopgood aged 72 was killed and Mr C.K. Hopgood (36) received severe chest and head injuries and died at the Burra Hospital about four hours later.  Mrs R.E. Dewhirst received head, facial and leg injuries and was taken to Riverton Hospital and later to Adelaide where her condition is now described as satisfactory.
