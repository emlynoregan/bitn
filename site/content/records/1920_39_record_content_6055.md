Mr & Mrs T.H. Woollacott have received a 5¾” bronze plaque sent by the King to the next of kin of soldiers who were connected with the Imperial Forces and fell in the war.  It is inscribed with the name of Robert James Woollacott and: He died for freedom and honour.
