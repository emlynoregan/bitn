Young Citizen of the Year is Kimberley Brak.  She has just completed her schooling at BCS, where she was a School House Captain, President of Goyder Youth Advisory Committee (YAC), Drummer in the band Toby’s Avengers, with Doug Wilson, Tristan Brak and Isaac Angel.  She is an instructor in charge of the VacSwim program and plays a role in the school swimming program too.  She plans to travel overseas later this year to work with ‘Camp America’ during its summer vacation.  [Photo.]
