Burra Town Council

R.K. Baker, Town Clerk, advises owners of land adjoining the private road between Kangaroo Street and Ware Street and bounded on the northeast by part allotments 43 & 47 and on the southwest by other portions of the same allotments, to remove any erection or obstruction on the road or after one month the Council will carry out the work and recover costs from the owners.
