Burra Burra DC

The grant for refurbishing the Nurses’ Quarters to a rural student hostel has been approved and Council will sign the agreement with the Commonwealth Government.  The preparation of plans for calling tenders will commence soon.

A co-ordinator for J150 celebrations is to be appointed.

A by-law for the control of moveable business signs is to be prepared.

O’Connor’s Shed [Drew & Crewe’s Bulk Store] has been leased to Adelaide Chemical Co.
