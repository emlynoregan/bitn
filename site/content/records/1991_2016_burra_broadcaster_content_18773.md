Mid North Sheepdog Trials were held at Farrell Flat over Easter, with combined three sheep trials and yard dog trials.  Winners:

Novice Yard Trial	Greg Kellock of Farrell Flat with Rolly.

Improver Yard Trial	David Kellock with Rose.

Open Yard Trial		R. Macklin of Tailem Bend with Sherwood Karl.

Novice 3 Sheep Trial	A. Ireland of Clements Gap with Fullers Astro.

Improver 3 Sheep Trial	S. Cross with Amadeus Geordie.

Open 3 Sheep Trial	M. Nuske of Cleve with Barunga Jinx.
