Accident.   A motor vehicle driven by Charles Rutherford collided with a stationary truck owned by the Natural Gas Authority in front of the RSL Building.  A passenger, John Nicholson, suffered concussion and abrasions.  The driver was charged with driving under the influence and fined $150 and had his licence suspended for six months.
