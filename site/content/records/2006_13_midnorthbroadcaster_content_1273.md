10th Balaklava Eisteddfod

Caitlin Drew won the Intermediate Contemporary Vocal Section, accompanied on guitar by Kerry Green.

She also came 3rd in the Intermediate Vocal Solo Section and came 3rd with Kerry Green in the Open Vocal Duet & Trio Section.
