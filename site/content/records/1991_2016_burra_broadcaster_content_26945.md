Thefts.  After a series of break-ins at the Burra Golf Club, the Sports Complex, BCS, Mintaro School and Blyth Post Office, police have arrested two Burra females and a former Burra man.  Some of the stolen goods have been recovered locally and some from pawn shops in Adelaide and also from Burra Creek.
