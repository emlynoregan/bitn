Local Board of Health

Nurse Woollacott’s licence for a maternity hospital was renewed.

G.C. Kuchenmeister was granted a licence to slaughter great cattle.

Leighton Strawberry Fete last Saturday in aid of Hall Funds raised about £40.  [Reported in ¾ column.]  [Sum raised corrected to £80, see XXXXI, 49, 10 Dec. 1919, page 2.]
