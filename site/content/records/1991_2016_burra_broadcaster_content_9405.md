Rod Peppiatt (33) and his wife Kathy with children Rhiannon and Duncan have been welcomed to Burra.  Rod will be inducted as the new minister for the Burra Uniting Church Parish on Friday night.  Rod has been an optical technician by trade and Kathy has been a school teacher.  Both come from Tasmania.
