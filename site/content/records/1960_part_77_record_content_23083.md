Burra High School Speech Night

A speech by Headmaster R.V. Mueller on the topic A Few Thoughts on Education is printed.

Prize winners are listed.

Dux of the School and winner of the D. McDonald Steele Memorial Prize was Kerry Costello.

Heather Lucas won library books in the D. McDonald Steele Memorial Award.

The A.B. Riggs Prize went to Dianne Wood.
