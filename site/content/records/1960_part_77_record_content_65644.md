Burra Primary School welfare Club held its AGM.  Secretary Shirley Thamm reported on the past year.  Average attendance was 28 from 67 financial members.  Total income for the year was $1,575.15 and $488 was spent on equipment.  At the Copper festival the Club gave tea to 30 members of the Police Band.  During the year there was a visit from children from the Nepabunna Aboriginal Community.  [Further detail of this is printed.]  Elected:

President	Mrs B. Stockman

Vice-Presidents	Mrs J. Ayliffe & Mrs C. Brereton

Secretary	Mrs M. Tiver

Assistant Secretary	Mrs R. Pritchard

Treasurer	Mrs R. Alderson
