The Baynes Family meeting at Willamulka last Sunday decided to hold a Family Reunion at Burra in 12 months to coincide with the release of a Baynes Family History being written by Roslyn Paterson of Bute.  Isaac & Emma Baynes and eight children left Cambridgeshire in 1855 and migrated first to Salisbury in SA.  Baynes children settled at Booborowie, Green Plains, Black Springs, Caltowie, Leighton and Baldina.
