Water Restrictions have been further tightened.  Sprinklers are now banned.  Hand held hoses with a trigger nozzle are permitted for even numbered houses on Saturday s 6 a.m. to 9 a.m. or 5 p.m. to 8 p.m. and for the same hours for odd numbered houses on Sundays.
