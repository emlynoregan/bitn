Burra Rural Youth held a progressive tea and navigational trial on 19 June.  Soup was at Mr Neil’s cheerful kitchen (near Farrell Flat), then the participants moves on to Mr & Mrs [illegible] and Mr & Mrs Nicholls at Blink Bonnie with sweets at Mr & Mrs Kitch Miller’s at Hallett.  Dennis Miller and crew in a Falcon were declared winners.
