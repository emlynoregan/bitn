Mid North Football League

This week the Secretary Mr A.E. Bannear tendered his resignation to the President on account of ill health.  He had served as Secretary for two periods; 1946-50 and 1965-72.  Mrs Bannear, who is Assistant Secretary, will be absent overseas from January 1973 to the end of the 1973 season.
