Noel Tiver of Toorak Gardens served in WWI and returned in 1919.  He married Miss Hazel Nutt in 1921 and they celebrated their Silver Wedding in 1946.  Mrs Tiver died in February 1948 and in March 1950 Mr Tiver married Dorothy, widow of the late Ray Thomas of Hallett and the Silver Anniversary of their wedding falls today.
