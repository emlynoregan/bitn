The Off-Leash Dog Park at Burra north is being developed and should be open to the public by late September.  Two areas will be developed at ‘Scroop Park’.  [Lowe Park]  One will cater for large boisterous dogs and the other for smaller dogs.  Shelters, bins, dog waste containers and drinking water will be provided.
