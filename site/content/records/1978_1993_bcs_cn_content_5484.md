BCS Staff News

Susan Langford comes from Berri Primary to teach in the 5/6 Unit.

Kym Reynolds takes over the Agriculture faculty after several years at Parndana Area School.

Sue Duncan will take French, having previously taught at St Anne’s and Gippsland Grammar Schools in Victoria.

Geoff Bayly returns after a year in Canada.

Marilyn Wall resumes half-time in Commercial Studies.

Alf Puckridge returns full-time.
