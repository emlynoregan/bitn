BCS.  Principal Kevin Fahey delivered his report for the year on Speech Night last Thursday.

He urged parents to reduce children’s TV watching and to improve reading skills and homework.  He highlighted the enthusiasm that was generated by the establishment of Brewery Hill Farm.  He urged encouraging a positive attitude among boys towards learning.

Musical items were included throughout the night.
