Council Elections.  A recent amendment to the Local Government Act gives a vote to the spouse of a person who owns a rateable property.  Persons wishing to vote in the Council election on 5 July must have their name on the roll by 20 June.  To be eligible rates must have been paid at least six days prior to the election.
