Mt Bryan School Committee organised a Miniature Debutante Ball at Mt Bryan Hall Friday 28 July.

The debutantes were presented to Mr E.C. Allen MP (Member for Frome) and Mrs Allen.

Debutantes and their partners:

Christine Quinn with Duncan McInnes

Robyn Oakley with Paul Simpson

Sue Talent with John Lehmann

Rosemary Talent with Philip Stolte

Darrylea Burton with David Wiggins

Kerry Pitman with Andrew Simpson

Sharon Brook with Ken Burton

Kathy Bolton with Mark Tiver

Susan Bolton with Charlie Wiggins.

Stephanie Seddon with Christopher Brooks

Tammy Walker with Craig McBride

Trilley Walker with Robert Walker

[The headline says 13 were present and the paper of 15 August supplies the missing couple:

Alison Quinn with Peter Quinn.]

The event raised about $130.

94, 30, 8 Aug. 1972, page 6
