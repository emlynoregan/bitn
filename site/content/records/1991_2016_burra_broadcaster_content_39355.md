Burra Meet & Greet arranged by the Burra CMC and Goyder Council, welcomed newcomers to the town at Burra Town Hall on Sunday.

Photos include:

Coralie & Bruce White from Leigh Creek, who will become permanent from November.

Ruth & John Phillips, living at Kookaburra.

Vic Spezzano and his sister Cheryl Young, who have moved into Taylor Street.

Steve & Sue Gower, who moved into Queen Street in March.

Graham Kirven and his wife Judy, who moved into Morehead Street recently.

Pat & Li Stubbin, who recently built a house on Ayers Street.

Ro Masters, new to Burra North.
