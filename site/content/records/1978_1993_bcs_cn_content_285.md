John Bannon the newly appointed Minister of Community Development visited BCS on Thursday.  He met the Principal Mr McCabe, the Chairman of the Advisory Council Mr Brian Polkinghorne and District Clerk Mr Perry.  He expressed interest in the development of the Community School and the many community initiatives sponsored by the District Council.
