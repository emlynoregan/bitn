Ian & Janet Slade have moved from Burra to their property near Pelican Point on the River.  They had spent 25 years at Princess Royal and in that tine had been active in the community.  Ian is a Past President of the Burra Show Society and both were involved in the Burra Boy Scouts and Ian has been a Board Member for Barkers of Baldina Aged Cottages.
