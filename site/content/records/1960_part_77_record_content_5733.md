Booborowie School.  The Annual Parents’ Meeting is reported in ⅓ column.

Burra High School.  Inspector Mr J.O. Glastonbury visited the school last week and Miss Clarke, a Craft Inspector, was present for a day also.  Mr Kim Horsnell has been appointed as a Junior Teacher.

Cricket

Koonoona 183 defeated Burra 99 & 6 for 43.

G. & D. Gask’s ‘Gilrose’ clearing sale is reported in c. ½ column.

Burra Small Bore Club shoot over s5 and 75 yards saw Jim Brown best off the rifle and Gordon Webster best with handicap.
