Tennis

‘A’ Grade	Spalding 11-89 defeated Aberdeen White 9-86

Mt Bryan 15-107 defeated Aberdeen Blue 5-67

‘B’ Grade	Aberdeen Black 16-96 defeated Booborowie 4-52

Aberdeen Red forfeited to Mt Bryan Gold

Spalding 11 sets defeated Leighton 9 sets

‘C’ Grade	Aberdeen 8-61 defeated Spalding White 4-39

Kooringa 10-70 defeated Booborowie 2-27

The Methodist Family Camp at Nunyara Youth Centre is reported as a very successful occasion, except for Mrs Lynden Beare who fractured a bone in her ankle while playing the intriguing game of ‘Piggy’ at National Park on Saturday afternoon.  [Details of the weekend in c. 1 column.]
