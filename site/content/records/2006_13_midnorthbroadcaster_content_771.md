Rostrum Voice of Youth 2006 entrants from Burra impressed judges at the heats held last Monday.  The winner of the heat was eventually Sharon Read of Clare in the Junior Section, in which Burra competitors were Priscilla Robinson, Tobi Strickland, Jess Starr, Jess Whittlesea and Lachlan Williams.

The Senior Burra competitor was Chelsea Evans and the winner in that heat was Amy Reid of Clare.
