Obituary.  Fred M. Pearce died at Burra Hospital last week aged 89.  He was a devoted Mason and member of the Kooringa Masonic Lodge since joining it 62 years ago in 1912.  He was worshipful Master in 1919.

[Frederick Moxey Pearce born 29 June 1884 Redruth: died 24 June 1974 Burra.]
