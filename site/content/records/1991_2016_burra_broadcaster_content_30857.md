Goyder Council Elections: Candidate Profiles – Burra Ward

Peter Reilly

Born in Burra & lived there all my life

Educated at Burra and Rostrevor College

Since then been a farmer and private contractor to several councils and Department of Road Transport

Interested in football, cricket and squash as a player and administrator

Have been involved with the Burra Picnic Race Club, Burra Yacht Club, Burr-Broken Hill Barrow Push and West Burra Land Care Group

Concerned about youth unemployment and training and the retention of the Burra Hospital.
