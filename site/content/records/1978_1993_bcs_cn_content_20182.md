Burra Scouting.  The District Commissioner of the Northern Districts, Mr Ian Slade, presented three Burra Venturers, Paul Williams, Andrew Jennings and Alan Jacka with their Queen’s Scout Award on Sunday night.  The Governor will present their certificates at Government House on 29 April.
