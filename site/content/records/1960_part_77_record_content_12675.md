Burra Golf Club held its Open Day, which drew 111 entries and 97 of them took part on the day despite cold, blustery conditions.  Winners:

36 Hole ‘A’ Grade Championship	Don Bachli (Former British Amateur Open Champion)

36 Hole ‘A’ Grade Handicap	E. McWaters

18 Hole Morning ‘A’ Grade	E. McWaters

18 Hole Afternoon ‘A’ Grade	R. Mueller

Veterans	E. Zanker

27 Hole ‘B’ Grade Championship	J. Tremlett

27 Hole ‘B’ Grade Handicap	J. Tremlett

18 Hole ‘B’ Grade Morning Handicap	D. Fuller

18 Hole ‘B’ Grade Afternoon Handicap	R. Baum

Diggers’ Cup	D. Fuller

Teams	Burra No. 4 (D. Bednall, J. Gebhardt & E. Jeffery)
