Regional Council of Goyder

General rates were 3% less than last year.

There is a fixed charge of $105.

Rate parity is being aimed for in less than ten years.

Total rate revenue 1997-98 $1,833,000.

Grants Commission Grant $965,000.

Roadworks Expenditure $1,478,390.

Major projects for 1997-98

Planning Amendment Report (i.e. Zoning & Land Use Review)

Development of Burra Creek Gorge and Redbanks Reserve.  (Using Open Space Grants of $30,000.)

Upgrading Burra Town Hall

Site works at Burra and Eudunda Town Ovals

Access ramp to Robertstown Peace Hall

Computer network between Burra office and branches.

A. & K. Eldredge have been required to lodge a complete development application seeking consent to undertake a used car lot use for their property on Market Street, Burra.

B. & S. Hill at the Mobil Roadhouse, Burra, have been required to remove signs and flashing light from the protuberance at the corner of Queen & Commercial Streets.

A. & C. Gill have been required to lodge a development application for advertising signs on their boundary fence.
