Obituary.  Mrs Alex. Harris aged 82 died in Kooringa on 30 July.  She was born at Tavistock, Cornwall 24 July 1844 and migrated with her parents Mr & Mrs James Bodinner when 13.  She came directly to Burra to live and on her marriage to Mr Alexander Harris of Kadina went there to live, but they later returned to Burra.  Her husband died some years ago.  She was alert till her final illness of only four days.  She was a conscientious member of the Kooringa Methodist Church.
