A Men’s Health Information Evening was held last Tuesday, with information delivered over dinner.  There were a series of speakers:

Lesley Phillips, Director of Nursing for the Burra-Clare-Snowtown Health Service, introduced the evening.

Ann Wiseman spoke on the risk of diabetes.

Jacqui Simpson spoke about asthma.

Barb Kirk considered the risks of skin cancer.

Dr Tim Simpson discussed prostate problems.

Mark Bellman took a quick look at farm safety.

Bret Hart from the Adelaide Crows was the final and entertaining speaker ranging widely over his experiences with the team.
