Burra Red Cross reported an average attendance at meetings for 1995-96 of 14.  $272.10 was donated to help with the Burra Kindergarten fence.  During the year $4,399.65 was raised and sent to headquarters.  Red Cross had a float in the J150 Procession.  During the year, long service awards were presented to Pat Kakoschke, Isabel Moxham, Irene Campbell and Joan Steele.

Also during the year Lorna James and Joan Steele died.

Elected were: President, F. Jones; Vice-Presidents, I. Hirschausen & A. Kelly; Secretary, J. Twin and Treasurer, P. Kakoschke.
