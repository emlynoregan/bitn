Gordon Webster of Booborowie was chosen in the State Veterans’ Team to shoot at the Veterans’ National Teams Championship at Bendigo.  He was part of the teams that won the Five Man Team and the Ten Man Team competitions and also gained a medal in the Queen’s Shoot, which followed the veterans’ events.
