Burra apex Club.  Service Directors Report 1975-76 (Dick Cuttle)

Dick Cuttle took over the role when Jeff Hirschausen was transferred to WA.

Apex has again run the Burra Pictures.

Stage 1 of upgrading the Burra North Playground has been completed.

We have the signs for the Burra Town Tour and these will be erected in the coming year.

A fitness bike has been donated to BCS.
