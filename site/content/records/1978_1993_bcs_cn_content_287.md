The Community Library held its first AGM recently.  Fr Kenny presided and Tony Barker was re-elected as Chairman.  Mrs Joan Ford is the secretary and Mrs Tess Hopkins is Treasurer.  The senior Librarian Mr Fidock reported there were 453 members of the library, rising to more than 900 when the school pupils were included.  This is from a district population of 2270.
