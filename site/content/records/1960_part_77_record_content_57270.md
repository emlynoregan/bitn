Burra LCL Women’s Branch held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs Ron Pearce; Vice-Presidents, Mrs Ron Collins & Mrs Walter Klem; Secretary, Mrs N. Finch; Assistant Secretary, Mrs Angus McInnes & Treasurer, Mrs Roger Dunn.  The guest speaker was Senator Nancy Buttfield whose topic was the Fight Against Drugs.
