BRTBA.  Annual report by President Sue Ryan.

The Association negotiated a new three-year lease with Council for managing the Visitor Centre.

Sub-committees ran:	Jailhouse Rock

Jazz in the Monster Mine

Antique Fair

Inaugural Wheal Burra Burra Festival

The first two of those events lost money.  Jazz in the Monster Mine is going Country for 2006 and has signed Beccy Cole.

Consultations were held with the Council over the development of a light industrial area for Burra.
