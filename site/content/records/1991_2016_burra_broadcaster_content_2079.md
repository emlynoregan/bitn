Burra Burra DC in conjunction with the Department of Education and Training and the Department of Housing and Construction has let a contract for the construction of a new community swimming pool.  [A plan is printed.]  It will be located adjacent to the BCS gymnasium.  The total cost will be $380,000.  The Council has entered into a joint use agreement with DEET regarding the use of the school gymnasium as part of the Community Swimming Pool Complex.  DEET will contribute $5,000 p.a. towards running costs.  The new pool will be half Olympic size â€“ 25 m x 12.5 m with a depth from 1.06 m to 2.54 m.  On the western side of the main pool there will be a brick building with an iron roof comprising a kiosk, supervisor room, toilet, shower and plant room.
