Burra & District Fallen Soldiers’ Memorial

A meeting Tuesday of last week heard that work was nearing completion.  The remaining parts have reached Adelaide and erection should be completed in about three weeks.  The bronze statue has been on view in A.W. Dobbie & Co.’s window in Adelaide for some weeks.  It is said to be the largest piece of statuary attempted in SA and weighs 10½cwt.
