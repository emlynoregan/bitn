First Burra Scouts Parent Group held its AGM last Friday

President Kevin Williams reported the major activity for the past year had been equipping the mobile kitchen.  This was completed with $800 from the Department of Recreation and Sport and $800 from the parent group.  Financially the Group had been very successful with a turnover of almost $10,000.  Elected: President, Kevin Williams; Vice-President, Peter Stockmen; Secretary, Di Day;  Assistant Secretary, Jean Kleinig; Treasurer, Barb Secomb and Publicity Officer, Helen Stockman.
