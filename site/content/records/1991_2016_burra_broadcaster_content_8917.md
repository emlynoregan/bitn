Tiver’s Row.  Maureen & Barry Wright have been notified they were awarded Best Project between $100,000 and $500,000 at the National Trust-St George’s Bank Heritage Conservation Awards.  The six restored cottages known as Tiver’s Row are typical of cottages built in the early years of Burra.
