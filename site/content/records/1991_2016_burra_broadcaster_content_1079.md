Brian Dolman of Steinhage Holden, Gawler, will bring the GMH Rodeo Cavalcade to Burra on Friday.  The display of GMH vehicles will be held at Richards’ Motors former car sale yard in Market Street.  Mr Dolman has been servicing the Mid North area for 12 months.  Vehicles on show will include:

One-tonne Rodeo 2.1 l diesel

Rodeo Crew Cab

4 x 4 Jackeroo

Holden Ute (V6 or V8)
