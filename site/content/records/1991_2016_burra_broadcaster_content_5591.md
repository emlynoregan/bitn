Graham Kellock is Burra’s Citizen of the Year

He was born in Burra in 1942 and is a fourth generation Burra resident.  Graham was educated by correspondence and at Burra Primary and High Schools.  He is a farmer with interests in the Kelvale Poll Merino Stud.  He has been involved in many of the districts organisations including:

Rural Youth – member, President 1960-61 & adviser

Burra Community & Sports Club – foundation member & President for four years

Burra School Councillor and Chairman of the Agriculture Committee

Burra Show Society – member for 36 years and President for two.

Burra Football Club – a past player, Captain of the ‘B’ Grade and one-time President

Farrell Flat Football Club – past player and President 1964

Burra Picnic Races – supporter since inception

Old Burra Swimming Pool – helped with construction

Burra Burra DC – Councillor since 1985, Deputy Chairman 3 years and Chairman 5 years

Mid North Regional Development Board – member and Chairman

Mid North Local Government Association Executive Committee Member

Burra Bowling Club – member and on committee

Member of the Redbanks Committee

Member of the Merino Capital Committee

Member of the Committee for Back to Burra schools for J150
