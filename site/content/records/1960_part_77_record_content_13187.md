Max Rayner  of Messrs L.B. Rayner & Son of ‘The Bluff’ Hallett is pictured with the Collinsville Merino stud ram he purchased for a new world record price of 12,500 guineas at the Annual Ram Sales.  The ram aged 2 years and 4 months was born and reared by Messrs John Collins & Sons of Collinsville Mt Bryan.
