Burra National Trust will publish a pamphlet of a walking tour of Burra.  The short version will be for people stopping for a one hour lunch break.

Market Square – Thames Street – Miners’ Dugouts – Welsh Place – Kooringa Hotel – Paxton Square – Market Square.

For those with a little more time there is a continuation from Paxton Square – Unicorn Brewery Cellars – BCS – St Joseph’s – the Convent – Mt Pleasant Road to Market Square.
