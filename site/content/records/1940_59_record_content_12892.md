Mongolata Goldfield

Baldina Mongolata GM Syndicate for fortnight to 6 February.

Farnden’s Hill.  Stoping advanced 5ft to 50ft.  The leader is irregular with values c. 15dwt per ton.  Leader shows a fair amount of micaceous iron.
