Oates Ltd sold ‘Ayers Park’ in the RDL Hall at Burra on Friday on account of Mr & Mrs R.J. Steele.

Lot 1.  ‘Heinrich’s’ of 429 acres went to Wandillah Pty Ltd of Burra at $114 per acre.

Lots 2 & 3.  ‘Homestead’ and ‘Brown’s’ went to Coolangatta Pty Ltd of Two Wells for $110 & $111 per acre respectively.
