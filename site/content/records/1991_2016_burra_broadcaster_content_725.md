Burra Lions have organised a Beerfest for 19 September.  It will give the people of Burra a chance to taste a variety of beers from Australia and Europe.  The cost will be $10 per head and that includes a tray of cold meats and cheese and a hot roll, compliments of McPhee Butchers.
