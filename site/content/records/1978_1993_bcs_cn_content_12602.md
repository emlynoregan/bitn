Guard of Honour by six Queen’s Scouts and Venturers and leader David Jennings in the full evening dress uniforms of the state’s early colonial soldiers.

Guests will receive old time dance cards with tasselled pencils attached.

Five piece dance orchestra ‘The MoneyPaley Dance Orchestra’.

Danse Victoriana – a group specialising in old style dancing, will perform two fifteen minute brackets.

Substantial buffet supper along the lines of suppers of 150 years ago.

Licensed bar.

Steve Woolley will take photographs of the occasion.

Non dancers can access the Dress Circle at $2 per head.
