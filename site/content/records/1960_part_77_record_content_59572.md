Weather.  The January rainfall has been well above average.  Burra so far has recorded 219 points.

To the east the rains have been the best for nearly twenty years.  Several watercourses have run and excellent intakes have been reported for dams on most properties.

Sturt Vale 510 points, Braemar 401, ‘The Gums’ 277, Thistlebeds 261, Koomooloo 205, Woolgangi 308, Canegrass 209 and Hogback 462 points.
