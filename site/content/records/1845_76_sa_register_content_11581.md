Kooringa Wesleyan Sunday School Children and their teachers walked in procession though the town on Easter Monday before partaking of tea, cake and fruit at the chapel.  Addresses on the benefits of attending Sunday school were delivered by Rev Messrs Flockart, Colwell, J.G Wright and others.
