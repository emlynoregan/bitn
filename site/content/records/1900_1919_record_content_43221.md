A report extends for over 2¾ columns of a case in which Johann Otto Beinke sued Joseph Howard for £5-5-0, being the price of 7 sheep sold to the defendant who pleaded not indebted and in turn sued Adolph Beinke for £30 for six weeks paddocking for 1,200 sheep For Feb.-Apr. 1913.
