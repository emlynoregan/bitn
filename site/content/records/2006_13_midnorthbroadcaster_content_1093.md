Burra Hockey.  Lachlan Williams & Anthony Cochrane were chosen to represent the Burra Hockey Club in the State Junior Championships in Adelaide over the June long weekend.  They have since been selected for the state squad of 35 Under 13s and will have the opportunity of attending several specialised training camps over the next few months.
