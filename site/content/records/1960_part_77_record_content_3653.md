Aberdeen Tennis Club held its AGM 1 September.  It was resolved to enter A & B teams for the next season.

Elected: Patron, F.H. Reed; President, Lloyd Turner; Secretary, S. Scroop and Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.

No. 1 court will be re-topped this year.
