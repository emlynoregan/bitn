Trophy Winners for the 1991-92 Season:

Darren James	Cricketer of the Year

Best Bowling Aggregate, 44

Best Bowling Average 12.53

Best Player in the finals with 139 n.o. & 4/42 in 2nd Semi-final

38 & 8/48 in Grand Final

Philip Siebum	Best Batting Average 54.4

Rob Stephens	Best batting Aggregate 578

Dave Meaney	Best Fielder

The premiership win breaks a thirteen year drought for the Burra Cricket Club.
