11 March 1992

Field Day Night at Burra Oval 17 March.

5.00 p.m.	Gates open

6.00 p.m.	Dinner with bar facilities

6.30 to 7.30 p.m.	Merino Capital Ram Award judging

7.40 p.m.	Presentation of prizes, ribbons & R.N. Collins Perpetual Trophy

Address by Rob Ashby, President of the Stud Merino Sheepbreeders Association

8.15 p.m.	Guest Speaker Mr Bruce Vaughan, Chairman of Dalgety Farmers Ltd.

8.45 p.m.	Fashion Parade

9.15 p.m.	Hot Dogs

9.30 p.m.	Wally Sparrow Country Music entertainer

Mid North Games 4-20 April will be held in Booborowie, Burra Clare, Jamestown and Spalding.

BCS students competed in the SAPSASA Swimming Trials at Balaklava last Wednesday.  A team will be selected to represent the Mid North at Swimming Championships in Adelaide 27 March.
