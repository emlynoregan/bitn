Burra Community School students enjoyed a successful term 2 elective program ‘Amazing Animals’ presented by Animal Rescue and Care Officer Ruth Norris.

Topics included: companion animals and how they assist in our lives, the need and importance of being a good owner, animal cruelty, ecology and interactions, vertebrate anatomy and physiology to show that animals feel pain, distress, enjoyment and different forms of intelligence.  Presenters were: Chris Green (Delta Dog safe presenter and dog instructor with K9 Connections Dog Club), Barry Richards (Compliance Officer with Regional Council of Goyder), Dr Mark Hutchinson (Senior Researcher in Herpetology at the SA Museum) and Kiya Norris (from Flinders University).
