Pekina 125th Anniversary Celebrations of white settlement will be held on the weekend of 2-3 October.

Saturday		Guided local tour

Memorabilia display

Afternoon tea in the Recreation Hall

A Dance in the Institute

Sunday	Church Service

Unveiling of the RSL Plaque

Roast lunch

Launch of the book No Place Like Pekina by John Mannion, assisted by Malcolm McKinnon

Carnival afternoon

Barbecue tea

The book is being printed by Burra Community Print in its biggest job yet at 380 pages.
