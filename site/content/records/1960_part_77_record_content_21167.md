  The RSL Annual Ball was held on Friday at the Burra Town Hall.  Door takings were £110.  A sherry party at the RSL clubrooms preceded the ball.  Dave Marshall’s Morgan Band supplied the music.  The ladies excelled themselves this year for the supper.
