Burra High School reopened this year with an enrolment of 151: 1st Year 53, 2nd Year 56, 3rd Year 26 and 4th Year 16.  There are two 1st Year classes and although 2nd Year is one class, it divides for all subjects.  The Head Prefects are Barbara Hogan and Bill Standing.
