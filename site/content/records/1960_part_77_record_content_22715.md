Obituary.  Bridget Teresa Preiss died 3 April aged 94.  She was born 19 December 1870, the daughter of the late Matthew & Ellen Clarke of Pekina.  She was the eldest of a family of eight, all of whom predeceased her.  She married Charles William Preiss on 12 April 1893.  There were nine children, of whom Lawrence Vincent died in infancy.  The family lived at Spalding, Pekina, Yongala and Carrieton before finally settling at Burra.  Bridget Teresa Preiss lived in Paxton Square for 53 years.
