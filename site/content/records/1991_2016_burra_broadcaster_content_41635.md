Burra Regional Art Gallery has 60 entries this year for the 2016 Portrait Competition under the theme ‘Capturing Identity’.  The exhibition was launched 2 April.

Winners:

Self Portrait	Roland Weight ‘Self’

Open	Margaret Walsh ‘Lis focusing for a portrait’

Michael Hocking’s and Jane Mason’s self-portraits were highly commended.

In the open section the entries by Michael Hocking, Tim Dell and Lisa Ingerson were also highly commended.
