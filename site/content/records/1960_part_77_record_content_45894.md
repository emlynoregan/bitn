Bishop Rt Rev. George Quarterman, of the Episcopalian Diocese of North-West Texas, visited Burra last week, where he was guest preacher at St Mary’s.  Rev. Geoff. Sibly played the organ.  Bishop Quarterman outlined the history of his diocese of 77,000 square miles.  Supper followed in the Parish Hall.
