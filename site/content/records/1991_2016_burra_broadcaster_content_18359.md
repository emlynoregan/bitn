Burra Quilters will hold a month-long exhibition in the Burra Regional Art Gallery.  The group now has over 30 members who meet every second Thursday of the month for sewing and a meeting and have a sewing day on the 4th Tuesday of each month.  At their AGM Karen Baum was re-elected President with Marie Kibble as Secretary and Helen Dunow as Treasurer.
