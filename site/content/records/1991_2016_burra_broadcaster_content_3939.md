The recent ‘Windows’ Art Exhibition in the old Post & Telegraph Office resulted in prizes for local artists:

In the People’s Choice prizes the 1st prize went to Allyson Parsons for RiceFlowers, the 2nd prize to Len Bence for Confolens – Laire Valley France [sic] and the 3rd prize went to Helen Jensen for Return to Dreaming.
