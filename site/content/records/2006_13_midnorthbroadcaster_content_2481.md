Booborowie Cemetery has been restored due to the actions of Booborowie Primary School, Booborowie Ladies’ Group, the Recreation Group Committee and Goyder Council.  Marie Broad & Shirley Madigan were instrumental in organising the restoration.  A Commonwealth History Project Fund grant of $1,500 and $5,000 from Goyder Council assisted.
