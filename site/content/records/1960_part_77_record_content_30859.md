Obituary.  Mr R.W. (Dick) Harris died at his home on Sunday night aged 70.  He had just completed 46 years service on the ‘Barker’ Property at Baldina.  He took a keen interest in flowers and was for many years an active helper at the Burra Show.  [Richard Walker Harris born 26 December 1895 at Kooringa: died 15 August 1966.  He was unmarried.]
