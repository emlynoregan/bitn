Market Square Museum

Unforseen circumstances have prevented the Premier from opening the Museum and this will now be done by the Chief Secretary and Minister for Health Mr Shard next Friday at 2 p.m.

Also attending will be the Mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone and Mrs Baulderstone, Mr Quirke MP, Mr H.C. Morphett immediate past president of the National Trust and Mr J.H.J. Stevens as Acting Chairman of the District Council and Mrs Stevens.  Pamphlets giving a brief history of Burra and District have been prepared an in the past few days the museum has been wired for electricity.  [Photo of the museum.]
