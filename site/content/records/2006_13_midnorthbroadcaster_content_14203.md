Goyder Council: Road Name Changes

Council has resolved that:

Road 137 from the intersection of the Burra-Clare Road to the Intersection with the Goyder highway be renamed Booborowie Road in the Hundreds of Hanson and Ayers.

Bowling Club Road in the Township of Booborowie is renamed Recreation Ground Road terminating at Quinn Road.

Tohl Road in the Township of Booborowie to continue and terminate at Recreation Ground Road, replacing that road named Willis Road.

Ernedale Road in the Hundred of Bright between World’s End Highway and Roselea Road to be renamed Erndale Road.

Bridge Terrace Burra between Kingston Street and Bridge Street East to be renamed Bridge Street.

Effective from 1 May 2012.  J.P. Brak, Chief Executive Officer.
