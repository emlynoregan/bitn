Advt.  Baldina Station will be auctioned on 18 September in the Burra RSL Hall on instructions from the trustees in the Estates of J.R. Barker (deceased) and M.R. Barker (deceased). Comprising 59,231 acres of freehold and perpetual lease.

[Five lots are described:

1a.	Homestead: 8 miles northeast of Burra of 12,642 acres.

1b.	Blowfly: 15 miles northeast of Burra of 12,213 acres.

2.	Grassville: 30 miles east of Burra of 33,747 acres.

3.	The Star: Section 164 Hundred of King of 447 acres.

4.	Red Hut: Section 176 Hundred of King of 43 acres.

5.	Ridge Paddock: Section 244 Hundred of Baldina of 139 acres.]
