Sgt Norm Orford will begin duties in the City on 18 June and his successor Sgt Haynes commences here on 16 June.  It sounds as if the new Police Station is almost ready for occupation.

Mrs Matthews, daughter of Mrs E. Simpson of Burra North, will return to Burra from Townsville during her husbandâ€™s tour of duty in Vietnam.

Terry Rule succeeds Daryl Hardy at the National Bank in Burra.  Daryl has gone to Renmark and Terry comes from Robertstown.

Tom & Marg. Jackson have transferred to the city.

Mike Hammer of Elders-GM has been transferred to the South East.

Geoff. Male and his wife will leave Burra shortly for Wilmington.

Mr & Mrs S. Thompson have closed their Terowie store and are holding a clearing sale of drapery and groceries in the shop next to Burra Meat Stores.
