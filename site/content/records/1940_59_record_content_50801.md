The Adelaide Wool Market has seen Burra producers top the market.  On the first day K.R. Phillips of ‘The Gums’ headed it with 184d and then E.L. McWaters of Sampson’s Well got 185d.  Thomas Bros of Hogback also managed 184d.  Other local prices ranged from 135¼d to 182d.
