S.J. Kerrigan writes as District Clerk on behalf of the Burra Burra DC.  He encloses a donation from the Council of $750 towards operating costs of the newsletter and on behalf of Councillors and staff passes on their appreciation for the sixteen years of service that volunteers have given in producing the Burra School & Community News.
