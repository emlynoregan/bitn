G. Butterworth was given a farewell social on Wednesday night in the Wesleyan Schoolroom at Redruth.  He has been church organist for some time and a noted vocalist.  He was elected captain of the Volunteer Co. some years ago and held the position until it was disbanded a few months ago.  In 1889 he was a member of the Town Council for North Ward.
