St Mary’s.  Rev. John H. Stephenson was inducted at St Mary’s on Wednesday evening.  The service was conducted by the Bishop of Willochra, Rt Rev. T.E. Jones.

Rev. G.D. Sibly was the organist.  Church Warden Robert Reed presented the minister elect to the Bishop.

After the service the welcome to the Stephensons continued in the Parish Hall.  Mayor E.T. Baulderstone welcomed the Stephensons to the town.  Mrs Matthews spoke for the Ladies’ Guild.  Mr John Murray of Hallett spoke for the Wardens.  Rev. Milton Edwards spoke for the Methodists and Monsignor Hughes represented the Catholic Church.  A number of other Anglican ministers were present.
