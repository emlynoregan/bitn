Burra Burra DC

Council decided not to proceed with a hard rubbish collection in Burra.

The Burra Passport agreement between the Council and the National Trust is finally being signed.

Council is liaising with the Department of Mines & Energy and the National Trust in deciding what to do about the mine shaft recently found in Linkson Street.
