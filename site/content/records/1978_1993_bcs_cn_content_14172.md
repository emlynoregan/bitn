Automatic Telephone Exchanges

Terowie and Whyte Yarcowie telephone exchanges will become automatic at 10 a.m. on Wednesday 18 March.  Past postmasters, telephonists, night boys and residents are invited to a barbecue to mark the event at Terowie Oval at 5 p.m. that day.
