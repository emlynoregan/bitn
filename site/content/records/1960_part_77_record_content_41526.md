Football

In the Mid north Football League the winner of the Mail Medal for Fairest and Most Brilliant Player for 1968 was L. Schmidt of Robertstown on a count-back from Glen chamberlain of Saddleworth-Marrabel United.

The Twartz Trophy for Colts was won by L. Wanganeen of Mintaro and the Jenner Trophy for the ‘B’ Grade went to B. Dixon of Manoora.  The leading goal-kicker was T. Mosey of Robertstown with 33.
