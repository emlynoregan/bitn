National Heart Campaign ‘Operation Heartbeat’

Only 12 attended a public meeting in Burra called by the Mayor E.T. Baulderstone about the above campaign.  It was decided that sufficient collectors could be arranged from the CWA, Burra North Red Cross, Burra Football Club, Rural Youth and the RSL to carry out a door knock collection in the town.
