Burra Hockey Club – History

1975	Women’s team founded as Burr/Clare Greens – renamed from 1976 as Burra.

1985	Junior team established.

1986	Men’s hockey team established.

1987	Mixed teams of indoor hockey established.

More recently there have been U14 and U17 teams, which won through to several grand finals and gained five premierships in the last 16 years:

Burra Men’s Team were premiers in 1990, 1995, 2000 and 2003.

Burra Women’s Team were premiers in 2001.

Burra players have also often been the best and fairest in the association:

Juniors	Danny Burton 1991 (U15)

Ben Dunstan 1993 (U15)

Anthony Cochrane 2005 (U14)

Men	Peter Cochrane 1990 & 1992

Digby Taylor 1995

Damien Brak 1997

Jason Gill 2000.

Strong supporters of the club have included Lorraine Taylor and Jan Jacka, who were both made Life members from 1995.  Also Kevin and Valda Williams and their sons Paul and Craig, Digby Taylor, Peter and Alex Stockman and their parents, Peter Cochrane and the Dunstan, Hooper, Dollman and Munro families.

In 2002 the junior division was reformed with a U13 team (now two teams), a U14 team and a U17 team.
