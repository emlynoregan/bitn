Burra Penguin Club ran three Voice of Youth heats at Burra Community School on 4 & 10 May.  BCS student Heidi Thompson won the first heat.  The next week Jessica Skinner won the Junior Heat.  In the Senior Heat Tristan Retelsdorf of Kapunda was the victor.  Heat winners got a cheque for $40 and Heidi Thompson & Jessica Skinner go on to the next round in Adelaide 28 May.
