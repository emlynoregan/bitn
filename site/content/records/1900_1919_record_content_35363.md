Redruth Court, 18 August.

George Frederick Greeves was charged with embezzling money from Drew & Crewes Ltd while driving a van for the firm.  The total defalcations amounted to over £20.  The evidence runs to c. ½ column.  He was committed for trial in Adelaide.
