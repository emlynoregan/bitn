Variety Club Bash.  This year’s event raised $835,000 for charity.  Local entry, Car 144, a 1960 Falcon, completed the course.  The road north to Innamincka proved challenging.  Penguin Books sponsored a vehicle driven by Richard Nitschke, formerly of Willogoleche.
