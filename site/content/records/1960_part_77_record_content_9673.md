Obituary.  Edith Maud Thomas died 17 January at Burra Hospital.  She was the wife of the late William Stephen Thomas (who died 1919) of Mt Bryan East.  She was the mother of Leslie, Perce and Eva (Mrs Wilks).

[Born Edith Maud Howe 2 May 1880 Gawler South: died 17 January 1962 Burra, residence Cumberland Park.]
