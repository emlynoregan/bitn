Accident.  An interstate semi-trailer loaded with 16½ tons of balled cotton [check: bailed?] overturned on the Mt Bryan Road at 3.45 a.m. on Thursday, while en route to Adelaide.  Driver Paul Kalas of Queensland suffered cuts and abrasions to his right arm and leg and a passenger Kenneth Joseph Freeman of NSW suffered minor injuries.  Damage to the trailer is estimated at $2,500.  Clode’s crane pulled the vehicle back onto its wheels.
