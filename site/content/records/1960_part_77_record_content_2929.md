Obituary.  Eva Maud Spackman died Sunday 26 June at Burra.  She was the widow of the late Edward Spackman and mother of Clarrie and Daphne Mrs Weeks, and grandmother of Jill.

[Born Eva Rebecca Maud Holmes 23 May 1883 Burra: died 26 June 1960 Booborowie, residence Burra.  Married Edward Spackman 1905.]
