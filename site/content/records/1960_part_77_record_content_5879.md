Burra Burra DC, 6 March

Tenders of F.J. Lomman & P.T. Cousins & Co. were accepted for the Booborowie Council Block.

[Accepting two tenders suggests one was for the pound equipment & material and the other for the land.]

Tender of Mr R.H. Edwards for Pt Block 32 Mt Bryan was accepted.

Permission to erect a gate on Booborowie Park Lands was granted.

Education Week.  A public meeting in the School Hall on 8 March resulted in the following tentative plans for the week.

Thursday 27 April	Primary School official opening of Education week

High School at combined sports at Kapunda

Friday 28 April	Official opening of Education Week in the Burra Town Hall
