The Department of Environment & Heritage conducted a controlled burn at Mokota Conservation Park near Mt Bryan last Saturday.  It was part of a plan to develop a program for the management of the park.  Mokota Conservation Park was declared in October 2000 as the first park in SA acquired for the protection of native grassland.
