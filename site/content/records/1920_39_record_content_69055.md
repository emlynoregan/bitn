‘Out East’ An article from The Chronicle by Eleanor Barbour describing a trip with Mrs I.J. Warnes, her son James and others to Koomooloo, home of Mr & Mrs Rex Warnes.  They called on Mr & Mrs Fred Gebhardt at ‘The Lagoon’ and stayed a night at Sturt Vale and visited North Koomooloo.  The article is a light weight ‘feel good’ story of about ¾ column.
