Peter & Pip Edson retired to Burra about 18 months ago after careers in teaching.  They have joined BRTBA and Peter is now the newly appointed President of that body.  They are also members of the National Trust and Peter is also a volunteer guide at the Burra Town Hall.  Pip is involved with the Burra Regional Art Gallery.  Both are also part of the Sing Australia Choir.
