SA Great Awards.  On 21 March the Minister for Regional Development and SA Great acknowledged SA Great Regional Award recipients with an afternoon tea in the Members’ Dining Room at Parliament House.  Locals present were:

Andrew Glen of Burra Visitor Centre, representing the Clare Valley Heartland Heritage Trail.

John Arthur, Chairman of Country North Community Services Board.

Cathy Slater, Board Member for Carers’ Link Lower North.
