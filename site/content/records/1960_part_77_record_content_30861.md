Football.  Mid North Association.

The Association’s goal-kicking trophy for 1966 goes to Graham Giles of United with 50 goals.

Aberdeen’s top goal kicker was Kevin Lane with 26.

A Grade	Mintaro 11.10 defeated Aberdeen 9.11

Riverton 11.16 defeated Auburn 11.2

Robertstown 9.15 defeated United 5.14

B Grade	Aberdeen 9.8 defeated Manoora 8.7

Riverton 14.3 defeated Watervale 6.7

United 72 [sic: perhaps 7.2] defeated Robertstown 3.8

Colts	Mintaro 8.9 defeated Aberdeen 2.4
