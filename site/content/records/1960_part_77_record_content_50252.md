70 mothers attended the meeting.

After spending $1,202-32 for the year the balance stood at $671-62.

Elected were: President, Mrs R. Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mrs B. Stockman & Mrs M. Tiver; Secretary, Mrs B. Thamm, Assistant Secretary, Mrs G. Webster & Treasurer, Mrs R. Alderson.
