The National Youth Week Plan B event was organised by the Clare & Gilbert Valley Council and hosted by Goyder Youth Advisory Committee in conjunction with the 2013 Be Active, Be Happy, Be You theme.  The event saw activities utilising the Burra Swimming Pool.  Activities included aqua balls, mini golf, face painting, bouncy castle and a giant beach zorb. [6 photos pages 8& 9.]
