Obituary.  Matilda Elizabeth Jane Farley, nee Mosey, wife of the late James Farley, died in Adelaide Hospital on 27 October aged 94.  Mrs Farley lived in the Brady Creek area till seven years ago, when she moved to Adelaide.  Recently she was an inmate of Carinya CWA Home, Clare.

[Actually she was 96, being born Mathilde Jane Elisabeth Mosey 16 May 1879 in the District of Kapunda.]
