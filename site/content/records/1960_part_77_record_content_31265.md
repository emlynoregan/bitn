Burra Town Council & the Hospital Board are concerned at delays in getting Murray water at Burra.  The Hospital id particularly concerned because of corrosion problems with piping and plant.  The Engineer-in-Chief of the E & WS Department advises that completion of the pipeline was dependent upon the delivery and installation of a pumping plant and it is not anticipated that the scheme will be operational before July 1967.
