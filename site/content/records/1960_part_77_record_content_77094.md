Burra Golf Club held its AGM and elected: President, A.G. Baulderstone; Senior Vice-President, Don Villis; Secretary, H. Burnett; Treasurer, P. Boucher; Captain, S. Longford and Vice-Captain, R. Hillman.

A very successful season was reported with a new clubhouse, which climaxed a year of hard work and many headaches.  The club funds benefited by $550 from a cabaret on 17 October.
