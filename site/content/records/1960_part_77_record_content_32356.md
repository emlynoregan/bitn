Burra High School.  The headmaster Mr Farrow in conjunction with Rev. M.A. Lind and Rev. G.D. Sibly has arranged for a Valedictory Service to be held in St Mary’s next Sunday night at 7 p.m.  The preacher will be the Rector of Clare Rev. Alan Baxter, former Director of Religious Education in the Diocese of Adelaide.
