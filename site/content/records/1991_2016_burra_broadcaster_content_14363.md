BBH Football Club has appointed Ron Lycett as the new senior coach for 1998.  He has played with several clubs, starting with Karoonda-Borrika, then ten years with Tailem Bend and a season at Goolwa, before moving to Burra in 1985.  He played 95 games with Burra and was the first captain of the merged Rams team
