Football

After a meeting on Wednesday the possibility of the affiliation of SANFL and SA Country Football League seems closer.  A twenty year dispute over umpires has moved a step closer to resolution.  SABFL will mow consider the proposal.

The Crown Lands Act Amendment Bill now before Parliament proposes to remove the existing limits of 4,000 acres or $36,000 unimproved value on the holding of Crown Perpetual Leases.  There are c. 16 millions acres freehold in SA and c. 20 million acres of Crown Perpetual Lease.
