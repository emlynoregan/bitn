Burra Primary School has announced that through the generosity of Mr Andrew Tennant an annual prize will be awarded in perpetuity to the Dux of the school each year.  A sum has been invested to yield approximately $20 p.a.  The award will go to the student with best results in the Progress Certificate Examination.
