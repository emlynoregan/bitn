Mrs Geo. Hanlin of ‘Ingleside’ Willalo will be 90 on 8 October.  Miss Alice Collins of Lucerne Downs North Booborowie is a sister and Mr Arthur Collins of Adelaide is a brother.  She is a daughter of the late Mr Henry Collins of Lucerndale, one of Mt Bryan’s early pioneers.
