Cricket.  Mokota Association Premiership

Hallett 152 defeated Burra Footballers 122.

After deciding the Premiership in the first innings Hallett batted again and were dismissed for 88.

For the Mayfield Shield North Booborowie 7 for 298 declared defeated Leighton 158.

Burra Rural Youth recently had a weekend trip to Loxton and the article records visits to a Friday night dance, the pumping station, winery, vineyard, fruit packing sheds, a barbecue, Saturday night dance and a trip on Sunday to Renmark and Berri.

Bowls.  At the Blyth Annual RSL Tournament the Burra team of T. Rogers, Cliff Turner, O. Holmes and A. Ford fought through to the final where it was defeated by West Croydon.
