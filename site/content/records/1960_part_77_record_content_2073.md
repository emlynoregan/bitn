St Joseph’s Convent School Annual Sports were held in the Church grounds on Saturday 30 April and were opened by His Worship the Mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone.

Afternoon tea was served in the Convent Schoolroom.  The program comprised a display of physical culture and team sports.
