Burra Break-ins.  Some of the property stolen from Messrs K. Murphy & J. Hill was found on two men arrested in Victoria.  They were at the time also in possession of a motor vehicle stolen from Queensland.  They will also be charged at Mt Gambier with other offences committed in the South East of the state.
