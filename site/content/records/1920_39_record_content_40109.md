Indoor Bowls.  Semi-finals in the singles championship:

H. Hill 16 defeated Mick Jones 7.

N. Villis 16 defeated I. Wohling 10.

In the final N. Villis 13 defeated H. Hill 9.

In previous years the winners had been:

1928	J. Allen Sen.

1929	S. Sara

1930	W. Kellaway Sen.
