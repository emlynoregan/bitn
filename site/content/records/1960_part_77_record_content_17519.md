Photos

Marriage.  Mt Bryan Methodist Church, 14 September

Mr & Mrs R. Kleinig (nee Jean Josephs)

Five Collinsville Stud Merino Rams at the Adelaide Royal Show

Top priced Merino Ram from the Royal Adelaide Show from John Collins of ‘Collinsville’ Mt Bryan, bought by Mr Kelvin Dunn of Barton Hill Proprietors, Black Springs, for 8,000 guineas.

Burra Bowling Club has let tenders for renovations to its clubrooms.

Burra Small Bore Club has elected Margaret Alderson as Captain for the ensuing twelve months and Keith Spackman as Vice-Captain.  Mr D. Thomas is Secretary.

G.M. Finch, son of Mr & Mrs G.R. Finch of Burra North, has been elected as Wannon Shire President (Victoria) for the next year.  He has been a Coleraine Riding Councillor for the past six years.

St Joseph’s Annual Catholic Fete was held at the RSL Hall on 27 September.  Takings were about £265.
