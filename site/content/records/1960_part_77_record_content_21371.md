Tally-Hi Shearing.  Vin Parks, Field Officer with the Australian Wool Board is conducting two shearing schools in the district this week.  They are arranged in conjunction with the Department of Agriculture to teach professional shearers the Tally-Hi method of shearing developed by the Wool Board.

Mr Parks will be at ‘Abberton Park’ on Thursday morning and at Mrs Ashton’s on Thursday afternoon.  There will be an Australian Wool Board film and Queensland Department of Agriculture film at the Burra Show Pavilion in the evening.  Mr Parks will be available for discussions and question in the evening.
