Burra Leisure Activity Group had 22 members taking part in a trip to Monarto Zoo on 15 June.  They had morning tea at Truro and lunch at Murray Bridge.  It was a long day, with a 7.15 a.m. departure and an 8 p.m. return, having covered 550 km.
