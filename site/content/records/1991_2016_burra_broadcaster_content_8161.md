Rockhill Sheep Dairy of Leighton with Adelaide cheese-maker La Vern, has been accredited with the highest points in the non-bovine cheese section at the Royal Melbourne Dairy Show recently.  Penny & Steve Fielke entered their Barinia Blue (locally known as Rockhill Blue) with outstanding results.

SA Street Machine Association’s ‘Cruise, Show ’n’ Shine’ will be in Burra on Saturday and Sunday 28 & 29 October.  [Issue 233, 19 October 1995, page 7, advised that this event would not happen.]
