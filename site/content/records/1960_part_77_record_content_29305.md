Mr & Mrs Jim Riggs were farewelled at a gathering at Farrell Flat Institute on Friday 29 April.  They had lived in the district for 16 years.  The Chairman of the Institute Mr E.R. Crossing was MC.  Presentations were made.

[Various speakers are reported.]  The family is leaving for a farming property in the Waikerie District.
