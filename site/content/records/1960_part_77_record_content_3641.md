The Education Department has acquired land at Clare for anew High School on 20 acres.

Burra High School has 120 students with an estimate of 130 next year.

An area of land for a new High School has been referred to the Public Buildings Department for investigation.  It would then be referred to the Land Board and then Cabinet for purchase approval, after which consideration would be given to the provision of a new school.
