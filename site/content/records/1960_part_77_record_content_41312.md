Marriage.  St Laurence’s Church North Adelaide, Saturday

Sheila Sullivan, only daughter of Mr & Mrs John Sullivan of ‘Greenfields’ Hallett married William Strong.

Mr Strong is a lecturer in Science at a University in Oswego, New York, where the couple will live.
