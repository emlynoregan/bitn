Vandalism to a water trough and windmill is reported from the property of B.A. Riggs Mr Bryan.  Wilful shooting caused the damage.  Gate signs were shot out.  Two rifles and 100 rounds of .22 ammunition were stolen from the Lomman property at Mt Bryan East.  Several youths were seen in the area in an old Vauxhall Velox.
