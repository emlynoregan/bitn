Accident/Obituary.  A head-on collision on the Burra Hanson Road about 2.30 p.m. yesterday resulted in the death instantly of a Broken Hill woman aged c. 52 and a 12-month-old girl died in Burra Hospital last night.  Two other people were sent to the Adelaide Hospital, one in a serious condition.  The four occupants of the other car were admitted to the Burra Hospital; driver John Reece Watkins of Oaklands suffered concussion and lacerations, his wife suffered belt burns, a six-year-old child had lacerations and a four-year-old sustained concussion.

[Viola May Parker died 3 April 1972 near Burra, residence Broken Hill aged 54 and Jodie Louise Carruthers died 3 April 1972 at Burra, residence Broken Hill aged 7 months.]
