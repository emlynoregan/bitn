Burra Christmas Pageant.  More than 300 people assembled to watch the pageant of 24 floats.  The Burra Hospital float was judged the best.  [Photo.]

The best decorated houses were Bob Greet’s in Burra North and Ric Williams’ in Burra.  Work & Western Clothing was deemed the best decorated shop.
