Notice.  Burra Town Council

A supplementary election is called for West Ward following the resignation of Cr. E.J. Lehmann.

Nominations are called by 12 October with poll on 2 November if required.

Burra Gold Associates had their closing day on Wednesday.  Trophies were presented by President Mrs D. Field:

‘A’ Grade Championship	Mrs J. Minchin
