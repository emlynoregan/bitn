Burra Regional Art Gallery

The Art of the Flower exhibition for 2000 was opened on 27 October by Ivan Pedersen.  The winner of the $500 prize this year was Una Grimshaw with her painting ‘Primaflora’.  In the floral arrangements the first prize in the children’s section went to Adam Kibble and in the group section was won by Lenice and Chris Catt.
