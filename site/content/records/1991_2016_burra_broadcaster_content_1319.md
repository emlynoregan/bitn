Lions Club will hold a May Presentation Ball on 8 May.  Couples to be presented to Lions Immediate Past District Governor, Dr Charles Mattner.  In the evening Burra Lions will hand over a cheque for $2,500 to the SES as their contribution to the ‘Jaws of Life’ recently purchased.

(These will be demonstrated at 9 a.m. on 8 May in Market Square.)
