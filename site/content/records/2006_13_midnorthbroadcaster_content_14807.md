Nathaniel O’Brien (16) has recently returned from performing as a guest artist on Norfolk Island as part of the Norfolk Island Music Festival.  He was a crowd favourite at the Australian Showcase Night.  He also performed at the local RSL, the Norfolk Island School and the Hoe Down Concert finale as well as playing guitar and accompanying Phoebe Ball of New Zealand.  He performed at the Leagues Club each afternoon, playing covers and his own songs.  [Photo]
