Obituary.  Rudolph Launer died at his home near Robertstown suddenly on 19 August aged 71.  He was the son of the late Mr & Mrs Gustav Launer of Emu Downs.  His father died 69 years ago on 1 August, leaving his mother to raise a large family.  All his siblings except Mr Fred Launer of Robertstown had predeceased Rudolph.

[Despite the above age being sited, he was Fredrich Rudolph Bernhard Launer, born 23 October 1898 Emu Downs.]
