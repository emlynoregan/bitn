Special Steam Train

During the weekend the Australian Railways Historical Society ran a historic train hauled by steam locomotive Rx 207 to Steamtown celebrations at Peterborough, passing through Burra en route.  Rx 207 consumed 10 kg coal per km and 101 litres of water per km.  [Photo.]
