Summary of Notable Events in Burra 1959

Burra & District Swimming Pool in use.

Vice-Regal visit.

Formation of Burra Redlegs football team.

Small Bore Rifle Club established.

Centenary of the Foresters’ Lodge – State Annual Meeting held in Burra.

Mid North Local Government Meeting held in Burra.

Opening of a private hospital for the aged.  [Heathmont]

TV sets installed in Burra.

$15,000 canvas for St Mary’s Church of England.

Death of the great townsman and nonagenarian Mrs E.P. Sara.

[Emma Price Sara nee Tiver d. 31 August aged 94.]

ETSA Electricity for Hilltown and Mt Bryan.

The Billiard Room was demolished.

The new post office was under construction.

New Roadhouse.

New dressing sheds at the War Memorial Oval.
