Sheep are fetching remarkable prices.  Dalgety & Co. sold Koonoona ewes 1½ to 4½ years for an average of £2-2-8 a head including c. 150 lambs.  About 400 culled for age by W. Hawker of Anama fetched £1-19-8 a head.
