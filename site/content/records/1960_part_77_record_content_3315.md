‘Regular Sufferer’ writes urging Sir Thomas Playford to try the Burra-Hallett Road after his remarks on the laying of bitumen from Jamestown to Peterborough.  The writer’s description is of a road full of potholes, corrugations and ‘dips’ caused by storms.
