Obituary.  Charles Henry Hawke (Charlie), formerly of Burra, died at Elliston Hospital 8 August aged 63.  He is survived by his widow Jean, stepson Robert, sons Evan and Leon and daughter Raelene Mrs Graham Waters of Burra.  [Born Charles Henry Symonds Hawke 19 May 1913 Adelaide.]
