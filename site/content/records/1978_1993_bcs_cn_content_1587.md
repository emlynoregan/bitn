Trine Bailey has been accepted as an exchange student and will leave for South Africa on 15 January 1980.  She is a matriculation student who will study at Lichtenburg High School about 130 miles west of Johannesburg in the Transvaal.  The school has 1,000 students and is in an Afrikaans speaking area.  She is sponsored by the Rotary Club of Clare.  BCS will host Yulinda Noortman, a South African student from Orange Free State in 1980.  She comes from Virginia, a mining town with a population of about 27,000.
