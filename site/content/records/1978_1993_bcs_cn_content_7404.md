Mr & Mrs John Quinn Sen. of Mt Bryan will celebrate their Golden Wedding on 27 February.  There will be a thanksgiving service at St Brendan’s Mt Bryan followed by a family luncheon.  They were the first couple to be married at St Brendan’s.  [Photo in issue of 3 March.]
