Obituary.  Frederick Binks-Williams, generally known as ‘Binks’, died in Adelaide on 24 April aged 87.

[He was the manager of Elder, Smith & Co. in Burra throughout the 1950s.  Born 3 October 1903 at Tarcoola as Frederick Hardinge Williams, whose father was Frederick Binks Williams (unhyphenated).  Both he and his father’s surname are hyphenated on his marriage in 1929.]
