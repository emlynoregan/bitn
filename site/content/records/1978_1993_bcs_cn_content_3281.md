Adelaide Children’s Hospital Auxiliary has presented long service awards:

Mrs Doreen Baulderstone and Mrs Reta Jennison	20 years

Mrs Betty Thamm and Mrs Irene Hirschausen		15 Years

Mrs Baulderstone and Mrs Jennison were inaugural members when the Auxiliary in Burra was formed in 1956.  Mrs Baulderstone was elected first President and served in that position for 22 years.
