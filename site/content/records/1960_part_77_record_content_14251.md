[Other prizes were distributed and are listed.]

Mr Syd Pearce, who has driven the Hanson school bus for the past two years, was given a presentation yesterday.

He is going to reside in Adelaide.

Burra Traders will open to 9 p.m. on 24 December.  A merry-go-round will operate and Father Christmas will come at 9.10 p.m.  There will be a tea break from 5.30 to 7 p.m.  Recorded music will be played.  A dance will be held in the Show Hall from 9 p.m. to midnight.
