Accident.  Five youths from Waikerie District were injured when a car they were travelling in rolled over on Sunday morning about a mile on the Morgan side of ‘The Gums’.  All five were conveyed to Burra Hospital by Mr Jeff Arnold. Four will likely leave hospital by tomorrow and one will stay a little longer.
