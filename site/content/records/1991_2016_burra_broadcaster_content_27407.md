Burra District Swimming Club had contestants at the competitions in Pt Pirie and Gladstone on Saturday.

Jacinta Lycett came 3rd in the 12 & Under freestyle handicap.

Doug Wilson came 3rd in the 11/12 freestyle scratch race.
