Stefan Tafra & Mark Bromley from Armidale NSW comprise the New England Guitar Duo who will perform at the Old Courthouse on 4 September, presenting a wide range of music from original compositions, jazz, blues, classical, ethnic and ‘new’.  They will also conduct a workshop for aspiring guitarists.
