Clare Hospital has appointed Mr Craig Stanbridge as CEO.  Mrs Lesley Phillips had been acting CEO and has now been re-appointed Director of Nursing at Burra.

Dr Rob Oswald was given a farewell at the Burra Hospital last week.  He is leaving Burra after 17 years here and moving to Streaky Bay.  Locums will staff the Burra practice until it is sold and a permanent replacement found.
