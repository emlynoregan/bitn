Burra Burra DC recently went to the Minister of Tourism Mrs Adamson, expressing its interest in her ‘heritage town’ concept.  They have requested a grant of $300,000 to enable the total restoration of Paxton Square as tourist accommodation and Mrs Adamson has been invited to visit to inspect the Square and other Burra tourist attractions.  The Minister has replied that she has received a preliminary report from her Department on the matter and she understands the Public Buildings Department has been asked to prepare detailed cost estimates and on site inspections have been arranged by all parties concerned.
