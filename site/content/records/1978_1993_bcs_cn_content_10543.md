Helen Stockman wrote an account of the flight to Melbourne with Singapore Airlines on 15 December 1984 to commemorate the opening of their services into SA.  She was one of 40 students from Burra, Yankalilla, Parndana and Booleroo Centre Schools.  There was a bus tour of Melbourne before the return flight.  [Photo.]
