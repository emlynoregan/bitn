Burra Burra DC, 5 July

Elections results:

Hanson Ward	S.T. Miller

Baldina Ward	M.T. Kellock

Mt Bryan Ward	T.J. Quinn

Cr G.R. Finch was elected Chairman.

A deputation has seen the Minister of Roads concerning the district’s problems.

Highways & Local Government Department advised a grant of £200 for work on their new depot site at Burra North.

A letter will be sent to the Burra Town Council asking for action on the opening of the stock route in the Corporation area between MR 134 & 138 Pt Section 1 Hundred of Kooringa.
