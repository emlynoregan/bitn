Mid North Hockey players did well at SAPSASA competitions last week.  The boys’ team included Lachlan Williams, John Robinson and Anthony Cochrane from the Burra Club.  The team did very well and were undefeated for the week, coming home with gold medals.  [Team photo.]

The girls’ team included Viv Murphy and Amy Wall.
