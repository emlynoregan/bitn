School-leavers Find Employment

Jeffery Allen has a traineeship in horticulture with the Regional Council of Goyder, while David Jones of Mt Bryan has a traineeship in civil level construction and Benita Penna of Clare a traineeship in building construction.

Dylan Bouts and Lee Wallis are both Maxima trainees in civil construction.

Justin Webster has begun working at Shippy’s hardware.

Karen Phillips is working at Décor Made Easy.

Sarah McInnes and Penny Benton are sharing a part-time position at the pharmacy.
