Accident.  A vehicle driven by Alfred Thomas Mildred of Somerton Park, who was travelling to Broken Hill, overturned on the old Black Springs Road at about 8.50 on Saturday morning.  It rolled over three times.  The driver and his wife Colleen were both wearing seat belts and were saved from serious injury.  The vehicle, only six weeks old, was a write off.
