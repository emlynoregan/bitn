Mr & Mrs Keith Schiller and family of Mt Bryan will transfer to Pt Lincoln.  Mr Schiller has been headmaster at Mt Bryan School and becomes deputy headmaster at Port Lincoln.  A farewell was tendered at St Andrew’s Patronal Festival on 2 December.
