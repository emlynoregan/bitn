Heritage Week 1987 from 5-12 April.

There are two main functions for Burra:

Compliments of O’Connor Tours and Burra National Trust there will be a free town tour for district persons on 10 a.m. 8 April.

Drive yourself to National Trust sites in Burra on 12 April between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. for free admission.  This includes the three museums in the town between 12.30 p.m. and 2.30 p.m.

(Market Square, Bon Accord and Malowen Lowarth)
