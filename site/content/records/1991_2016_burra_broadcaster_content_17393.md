Clare Show was held last Saturday and Burra entrants scooped the pool in the Ute Show.

Ron Shipard was 1st in the Ute Show with his 1951 RX Holden.

Damian Quinn was 2nd with his WB Holden.

James Tiver gained 1st prize in the 4WD Section.
