Accident.  A car driven by Lester W. Gare turned over on the eastern road just east of the Deep Creek causeway on Saturday at 3.10 p.m.  A passenger, Mr Steven N. Fuller of Burra, suffered internal injuries and a broken arm.  The driver suffered bruising.
