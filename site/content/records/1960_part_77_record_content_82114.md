Burra Pistol Club.  On 16 February the best shots were: A. Pearce, L. Broad, P. Edwards & J .Ford.

Burra Tennis Club held a social evening in the Golf Clubhouse on Saturday night to present ten life-members with badges and certificates.  Those recognised were the late Mr Frank Reed, Mr S. Scroop, Mrs F. Scroop, Mr Dick Reed, Mr B. Hirschausen, Mr I. Hirschausen, Mr R. Reed, Mrs A. Kelly and Mr & Mrs Don Lloyd.  A large Honour Board was also presented.

Booborowie Methodist Ladiesâ€™ Guild held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs A. McGuire; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames M. White and Sutton; Secretary, Mrs Janet Price & Treasurer, Mrs G. Dewhirst.  Mrs M. White retired as President after 13 years and Mrs M. Pearce retired as Treasurer after 16 years.

In future the group will be known as the Booborowie Uniting Church Fellowship.
