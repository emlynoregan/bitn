Burra Bowls Associates held their AGM and elected:

President	P. Field

Vice-Presidents	L. Neill & Z. Brown

Secretary	J. Jamieson

Treasurer	R. Philips

For the past season Melva Irlam is the singles champion and also Champion of Champions for the North Eastern Women’s Bowling Association.  A luncheon was given for Mrs I. Jacka (President), who is leaving the district.
