Aberdeen Roadhouse will re-open from 30 April 8 a.m. to 7 p.m. from Thursday to Monday.  Proprietors Rose and Phil Brooke will offer eat in or take away and concentrate on their home made produce, including their now familiar lines of soups, pasties, pies and quiches.  Catering for al manner of occasions is also available.
