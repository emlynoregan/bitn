Joe Edwards the local representative of the E & WS Department retired on 6 January.  The Town Council wants him to be replaced.  At present a man from the Spalding Depot has to respond to problems.  The E & WS Department has had a man in Burra since it took over pumping of the local water supply from the Town Council over 60 years ago.
