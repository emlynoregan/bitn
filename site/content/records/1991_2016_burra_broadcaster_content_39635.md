The Inaugural BRTBA Awards were held last week at Burra Railway Station, with seven business and tourism operators receiving awards, including the Burra Broadcaster.

Friendliest Business		Town Square Gallery & Café

Best Presented			Savii

Most Helpful Staff		Burra Visitor Information Centre

Best Local Event		Burra Antique Fair

Best Tourist Attraction		The Burra Heritage Passport

Favourite Place to East		La Pecora Nera

And for a special contribution to the promotion of business and tourism – Michelle Osborn of the Burra Broadcaster.
