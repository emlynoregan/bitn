Burra Methodist Church.  Next Sunday at 7.30 p.m. Mrs E. Wilks will present a stained glass memorial window and a bronze plaque commemorating the Ironmine Church (now closed).  After the dedication of these memorials the choir will sing the Christmas Cantata Born a King by John Peterson.  It will be an ecumenical occasion with Rev. Fr K. Matthews and Rev. Fr B. May assisting in the service.
