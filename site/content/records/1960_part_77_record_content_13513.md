Mr H.W. (Bill) Symonds last week took over as Manager of the Bank of Adelaide at Booborowie from Mr R.F. Gasmier who moves to Kimba after two years.  Mr Symonds previously played football and cricket at Angaston.  He was born at Gumeracha, but has most recently been at head Office and on suburban staffs.  [Photo.]
