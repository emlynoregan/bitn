Leighton School Welfare Club Debutante Ball was held last Friday at Leighton Hall when a crowd of over 300 attended.  Maurice Collins was the MC and Mr & Mrs Gerald Gask received the debutantes:

Coralie Eberhard with John Teague

Christine finch with Robert Long

Jenette Kleinig with Andrew Riggs

Janice Pens with Gerald Pens

Merle Lomman with Robert Edwards

Elizabeth Sutton with Alan Possingham

The Dominoes provided the music and the door takings if $250 will aid the school.

90, 21 (2), 30 May 1967, page 2 [Number used before in 1961.]
