Burra Show

The weather was not kind, but gate takings were 25% up on last year.  New events included log-chopping, yard dog, sheep counting and rouseabout events.  Record entries were received in horses, poultry and caged birds.  Flowers and vegetables were slightly up on last year.

Miss Show Girl was Amanda Drew

Miss Junior Show Girl was Jessica Schell.

Miss Sub-Junior Show Girl was Jane Lehmann.

Miss Mini Show Girl was Kylie O’Connor.
