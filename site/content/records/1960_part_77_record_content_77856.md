BCS Parents and Friends met at the school last Thursday.  Edith Carpenter as Co-ordinator for the Community School addressed the meeting.  The Principal Mr Chris McCabe introduced the new staff and addressed the meeting.  Ron Thamm chaired the meeting and steered the discussion smoothly through questions of open space teaching, fund-raising priorities etc.  Ian Allen outlined the achievements of 1975.  Tenders have been called for the painting of the exterior of the main building.  Nine parent representatives were elected to the School Council.  Mr McCabe stressed the need for community involvement if the concept of a Community School is to work.  An Open Week will be held 15-19 March when many different subjects will be offered to people interested in taking part.  Year 12 student Anne Carpenter also addressed the meeting, urging community support for the new school concept.
