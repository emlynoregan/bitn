Mr & Mrs Maurice H. Carne were given a farewell at Redruth Memorial Sunday School on 11 November.  After 6Â½ years here the family has gone to Pt Pirie.  Mr Carne is an officer in the National Bank.  Both Mr & Mrs Carne have been conscientious workers in the Redruth Methodist Church.  A presentation was made of a handsome blackwood traymobile and a fountain pen for Mr Carne and a cut glass rose bowl for Mrs Carne. Members of the banking fraternity farewelled Mr Carne on Monday afternoon 11 November.  He has been the accountant and teller at the National Bank in Burra.
