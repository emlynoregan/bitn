Advt.  Steve Woolley advises he has resigned from the SA Police and commenced business with Gerry Baulderstone in the video store 17 Commercial St, Burra.  Nearly 800 video tapes for hire.  Also selling a wide range of cameras and accessories and available as a photographer for weddings, parties, etc.  Colour photograph processing.
