Burra Racing Club held a successful autumn meeting on 9 March.  The course was in good order and the results are printed.  The main race was the Burra Handicap (1st £35, 2nd £7 & 3rd £3.) run over 1¼ miles and won by A. Reed’s rn g Glenaides ridden by M. Raven.
