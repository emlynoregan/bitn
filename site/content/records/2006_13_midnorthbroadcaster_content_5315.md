Graham Gunn MP, Member for Stuart, is retiring and there is a battle now for Liberal pre-selection.  There are three candidates: Neville Gibb, Dan van Holst Pellekaan and John Voumard.

Neville Gibb is a businessman from Jamestown.

John Voumard is a lawyer.

Dan van Holst Pellekaan is a successful businessman and currently Tourism Development Officer for Southern Flinders Regional Tourism Authority.  Selection will be by plebiscite at the Stuart Electoral College, Sunday 25 March.  [Photo of Mr Gunn.]
