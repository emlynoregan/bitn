Burra Arts Council held its 2nd Annual Art Exhibition over last weekend.  Eighteen artists, ten of them local, had work on display and 600 people viewed the exhibits.  Graham Kellock opened the exhibition.  People’s Choice winner was Allyson Parson’s ‘Rice Flowers’ and second was Leonard Bence’s ‘Confolens – Laire Valley, France’  [sic: Loire Valley?]
