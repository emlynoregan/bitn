Trooper E.C. Collins, son of R. Collins of Mt Bryan was a member of Burra Light Horse and enlisted at the start of the war, leaving for the front in October 1914 and landed at Gallipoli 12 May 1915.  He was severely wounded on 13 May, but returned after six weeks and then stayed another 2Â½ months before being invalided to Malta and then England.  Last September he went to France.  Last week news was received he was again wounded and is in hospital in London.
