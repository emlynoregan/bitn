Fire burnt 9 Ha of crop on the property of Dick & Barb Secomb on the side of the Burra-Spalding Road about 15 km north of Burra on Saturday afternoon 18 December.  Burra, Booborowie & Mt Bryan CFS units responded along with many private units and the police.
