Burra & District Ambulance

The new ambulance was handed over at an official ceremony in Market Square on Friday 1 July.  The committee secretary Mr R.W. Fullgrabe introduced the Mayor who welcomed Dr H.H. Hurst, Commissioner of the St John Ambulance Brigade and other officials.  Mr K.G. Shapter, Manager of the Order of St John for SA presented a cheque for £500 towards the cost of the vehicle.  Further donations are listed to bring the present total to £1,575.
