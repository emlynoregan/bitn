Burra Primary School.  End of year activities:

Monday.  Grade VII children and parents social in the RSL Hall.  Dancing was of a high standard.  For the Andrew Tennant Award there was a tie between Brenda Harris and Tony Jennison.  Brenda Harris thanked Mr & Mrs Cain for their dancing lessons.

Tuesday.  Grade VII visit to the Burra Mine.  Tennis tournament to continue through the week.

In the afternoon the school presented the operetta Cowboys and Indians to parents.

Wednesday.  Party Day.  Mrs Marlene Richards shows slides.

Thursday.  Assembly and presentation of prizes.

Earlier in the week Mr Ron Thamm spoke on St John Ambulance.
