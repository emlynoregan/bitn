Mr E.W. Dunhill will celebrate 75 years as a Methodist Local Preacher on Sunday 11 September.  He will take the morning services in the Burra Circuit after which a luncheon will be held in the Redruth Memorial Hall.  Mr Dunhill began his preaching in the Gumeracha Circuit and has served the church in WA as well as in SA.
