The Burra Hospital has benefited by $56 from a tillage field day at Hanson on the property of S.M. Stockman & Son on 21 March.  Hanson CWA ladies supplied luncheon at the hall for 71 people.  The day was organised by the Agricultural Bureau.  (President Don Stockman & Secretary Ron Shipard.)
