World’s End Gorge will receive a $100,000 make-over in the form if a natural resources management protection and revegetation project.  This is funded by a Native Vegetation Council grant.  Vegetation of the creek and floodplain will be protected and managed.  Vehicle access will be managed, woody weeds removed, feral animals controlled, eroded banks stabilised and interpretive signage installed.  Changes and progress of the project will be monitored.
