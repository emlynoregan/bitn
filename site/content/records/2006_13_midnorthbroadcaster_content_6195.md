2008 Ram Sales

East Bungaree sold their half share in Ram Buddy 23 for $20,000 at the Bendigo Sheep Show.

On property sales included a top price of $2,200 and an average price of $1,118.

The Barton Hill Ram Sale realised an average of $1,169 with a top of $3,400.

Greenfields Merino Ram Sale realised an average of $1,015 and a top of $3,400.
