School Enrolments

Burra Primary School

Headmaster Mr Ian Auhl reports an enrolment of 247 (124 boys and 123 girls); up 20 on last year.  There are 36 new students in Grade I to bring it to a total of 40.  [They are listed.]

Miss Kathlyn Rayner will teach 30 of them and Miss Lucy Raggio will take a composite of 20 students from Grades I, II and III.

Miss Brenda Richards comes from Mt Bryan School and takes Grade III.

Mr Ian Raftery comes from Loxton School and takes Grade IV.

Mr Philip Lannon has come from Pooraka School and takes Grade VII.

Mrs Perry, Mrs Jeffery & Mrs Pens are in charge of Grades VI, V and II.  Mrs Perry is Senior Assistant.

The Library is in the final process of establishment.  Books have been purchased and covered and the furniture is arriving.

The Chairman of the School Council Mr Ron Moreland is finalising the work of sealing the access roads.
