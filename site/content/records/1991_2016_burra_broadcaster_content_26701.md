BCS Staff Changes

Deb marks has a one-year appointment as Principal of Spalding Primary.

Helena Holman from Mt Bryan School becomes the Year 7 teacher.

Jenny Brooks and Anne Drew will be temporary placed teachers in 2002.

Peter Giles goes to Charles Campbell Secondary School after five years here in Sport and Physical Education.

Anna Roebuck will replace him.

Linda Smith goes to Balaklava High after twelve months here.

Lynette Klavins goes to Balaklava after five years here as Senior School Co-ordinator.

David Hutchinson has been appointed Senior School Co-ordinator.

Lisa O’Connor has been appointed Library Assistant.

Megan McDonald has been appointed Special Education Assistant.

Dick Secomb has been made Farmhand-Groundsman.

BCS Staff and students staged their Centenary of Federation Shoe called This Heart of Mine last Thursday and Friday nights at the Burra Town Hall, which was filled for the event.  Directed by Lisa Hughes and with Craig Clifford in charge of sound and lighting.
