Hallett Primary School held its Christmas Tree on Wednesday last.  Mr Costello has been transferred to Littlehampton after six years as Head Teacher at Hallett.  Miss Ruth Hughes has resigned after three years as assistant teacher at Hallett.  Progress Certificates were presented to:

Gaylene Phelps	Marilyn Wood	Rosemary Jones

Alison Miller	Donald Richardson	Andrew Anderson

Roslyn Phin	Valmai Jones	Mary Lewis

Boyd Tiver	Trevor Warner
