BCS held its Senior Swimming Carnival.  For the first time since BCS was established in 1876 Jennison House won the House Shield with 463 points from Steele 370.  [Group photo.]

Individual Champions:

U13 Girls	Mandy Baker

U13 Boys	Shaun Smart

U14 Girls	Amanda Hall

U14 Boys	Ian Kleinig

U15 Girls	Joanne Brooks

U15 Boys	Simon Clode

Open Girls	Tricia Klembt

Open Boys	Wayne Phillips
