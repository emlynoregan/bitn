26 June 1980

Senior Citizens.  After all the preliminary meetings the inaugural meeting of the Senior Citizens Group will be held on 3 July in the CWA Hall when the formal election of officers will take place.

BCS Parents & Friends’ Association was pleased to have made $424 from catering for the Civic Centre Opening.

BCS.  The School Ball will be held at the Burra Town Hall 8 p.m. to 1 a.m. 15 August.  Jack Kleinig’s Band.

Burra Apex Club on Sunday had a working bee to extend the school’s adventure playground and also made a donation of $200 worth of materials to the school.
