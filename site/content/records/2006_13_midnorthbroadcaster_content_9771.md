Bobfest 2010.  Local Burra band ‘The Sore Foot Effect’ will compete in this year’s Bobfest along with ten other bands at Clare Showgrounds on Saturday next.  The band has been together for about 12 months and has performed at school events, small town events.  Bobfest is a youth initiative of Clare and Gilbert Valley Council, Regional Council of Goyder and Light Regional Council.
