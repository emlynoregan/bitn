Alice Springs Masters Games 8-15 October

Angie Webster joined sisters Bonnie, Marie and Kitty and Cousin Christine Searle in the netball, with their team ‘Bits n Pieces’ winning the gold medal.

Sharyn ‘Chooky’ Roberts won a bag of medals, including gold in javelin and silver in the hammer throw and discus.  In golf ‘Chooky’ also managed a hole in one.

Melissa Cooper has come second in the ATV National Championships at Wonthaggi in Victoria.
