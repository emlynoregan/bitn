The Inaugural Burra Film Festival begins on Friday 3 November, with a launch at the Burra Regional Art Gallery.  The films to be screened are:

Saturday	Priscilla Queen of the Desert at 9.45 a.m.

Mad Max at 1.45 p.m.

The Unforgiven at 7.15 p.m.

The Proposition at 10.45 p.m. followed by Razorback

Sunday	The Lion King at 9.45 a.m.

Ten Canoes at 1 p.m.

Seven Brides for Seven Brothers at 3.45 p.m.
