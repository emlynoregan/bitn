Hallett & District Schools’ Athletic Association’s Annual Sports Meeting was held at the Hallett Oval on Friday in rather cold and blustery conditions.  Six schools competed: Hallett, Terowie, Whyte Yarcowie, Mt Bryan, Canowie Belt and Willalo.  Hallett scored best in aggregate.  [Results are printed.]
