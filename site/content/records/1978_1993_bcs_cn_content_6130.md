Burra Yacht Club has made donations in the past twelve months:

St John Ambulance	$65

Hockey Club	$25

Community News	$25

SES	$100

Sri Lankan Appeal	$50

Lions Senior Citizens Bus	$380

The Yacht Club will also add to the beautification of the Creek area by erecting a playground near the barbecue area at a cost of c. $1,300.

This year it is planned to have four races on 10 October:

Junior for 12 years and under

Senior for those 16 years and under

Ladies

Open

$500 has been donated as prizes.
