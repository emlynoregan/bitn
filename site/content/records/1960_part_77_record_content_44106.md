School Enrolments 1969

Burra Primary School

Enrolments in 1969 are 107 boys and 116 girls: total 223 or 35 less than in 1968.  28 were enrolled in Grade I.  [New admissions are listed.]  Mr John Cain comes from Teachers College.  He will drive the World’s End bus and teach Grade V.

Willalo.  The enrolment of 20 is the same as in 1968.  [New enrolments are listed.]  Mr B. Smith continues as head teacher.

Burra Kindergarten has 46 children.  [Names of those attending are given.]

St Joseph’s Convent School has 20 students and the four new enrolments are named.

Leighton School has 27 students compared with 30 in 1968.  Mr D. Munzberg remains in charge.

[New enrolments are listed.]

Black Springs School has no new enrolments this year.  The new teacher in charge is Jan Richards.  Enrolments are down 7 on 1968 to 11: Grade II, 1; Grade III, 4; Grade IV, 1; Grade VI, 2; and Grade VII, 3.

Booborowie School had 8 new admissions and 2 transfers for an enrolment of 62, but with the 4 Mellor children leaving, the actual enrolment will be 58.  [Newcomers are listed.]

Burra High School has 180 on the roll.  Seven of the staff of eleven are new faces.  L.D. Beare comes as Headmaster from being Deputy Headmaster at Kadina.  Other newcomers are Miss J. Russell, Miss K. Strahan, Miss L. Pfitzner, Mr D. Molze, Mr C. West and Mr K. Salt.  1968 was the last year for both the external and internally examined Intermediate courses.
