The estimated cost of buying 10’ of the approach to Kingston Street Bridge: - 
To Statton & Henderson 	£175 
Legal and transfer fees 	£50 
100’ of filling 	£100 
Sub-total:	£325 
Estimated value of 100’ 	£500 
Total:	£825 
The overseer reports that the Ayers St bridge mason work is decayed and needs rebuilding although it poses no immediate danger.
