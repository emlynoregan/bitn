District Schools Intake 1972

Booborowie has an enrolment of 80, up from 64 for 1971 (aided by the closure of Willalo).  [New students are listed.]  Miss P. Glencowe has joined the staff as infant teacher.  Mrs Blackwell takes Grades III, IV & V.  Headmaster Mr Blackwell.  This year’s enrolment is the highest since 82 in 1927 and 85 in 1926.

Hallett has 74 students this year, an increase from 64 in 1971.  The Headmaster is K. Whittington, with Miss Sue Polmear (Primary) and Miss Margaret Johnson (Infant).  [New students are listed.]

Burra Primary School for 1972 has 271 students compared with 248 in 1971.  New staff: Mr Grant Good comes from Tennant Creek, Miss Mignon Williams from Alice Springs and Miss Pam Lester from Wattle Park Teachers’ College.  [New students are listed.]

Burra High School begins the year with 156 students compered with last year’s 174.  [New students are listed.]  New staff members are: Mr D. Calder Headmaster), M. Atkinson, I. Coulls, A. Harvey, T. Mends and Mrs K. Lomman.

Burra Kindergarten.  Old and new students are listed.

Farrell Flat has no new pupils and no new staff.  There was a mid-year intake.  The 25 pupils now compares with 29 in February 1971.

Mt Bryan has 28 pupils compared with 29 last year.  Mrs Prior has moved to Loxton Primary School and Miss Christine Haskard replaces her.  There are three new students.
