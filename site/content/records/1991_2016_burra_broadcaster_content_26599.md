Obituary.  William Raymond (Ray) Holman died in Toowoomba, Queensland on 14 November.  He was formerly of Lucindale in SA.  He was the husband of Cora (dec.) and brother to Max, Jack (dec.) and Wyn.

[Born 26 July 1911 in the Hundred of Pirie, SA.]
