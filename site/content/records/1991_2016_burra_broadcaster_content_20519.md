The Regional Council of Goyder calls for people to suggest a name or names for the reserves adjacent to Burra Creek.  Either one name for all or separate names for the areas Black Bridge to St Just Street, St Just Street to the Caravan Park, The Caravan Park to the Bowling Club and the Bowling Club to Stock Street.
