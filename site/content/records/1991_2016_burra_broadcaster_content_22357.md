Jenny & Garry Gebhardt have taken over the Mobil Roadhouse.  They have been providing meal services for the Redruth Camp Centre in recent years and look forward to offering ‘home style’ cooking at the roadhouse.  Garry has been employed at McLeod’s in Clare for 15 years.
