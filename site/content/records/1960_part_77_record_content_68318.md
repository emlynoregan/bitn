The Quire St Nicholas, based in Adelaide, will sing the Eucharist on 7 October at St Mary’s.  Last year they came for the Copper Festival.  They are directed by Mr Cliff Boyd.  They will also sing some motets during the service.  The setting for the service will be by Palestrina.  [Note on page 7.]
