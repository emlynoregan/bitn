BCS students Maree Plew & Darren Schuppan recently received certificates of appreciation for their efforts in creating a design for the 20 cent commemorative coin for SA in the Centenary of Federation Coin Design Competition.  Their designs and those of former BCS student Jessica Skinner have been on display in Canberra.
