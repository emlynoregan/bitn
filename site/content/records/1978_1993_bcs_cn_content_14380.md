Burra School & Community News Management Committee

Elected 24 March: Chairperson, Mrs Barb Secomb; Secretary, Mrs Judy Kellock.

A grant of $1,000 has been received from the State Bank Community Grants Fund.

Back to Hanson held recently was very successful.  The oldest to attend was Mrs Dora Wade (84) and the youngest was a grandchild of Bob Trenowden, the second to last stationmaster.  Many old district families were represented.  A church service was conducted by Mr Norrie Baynes and Dorothy white (nee Woollacott) followed by a pooled lunch.  Mrs Dora Wade cut the cake, assisted by Mrs Laurel Neill and Mrs Flo Blieschke.  Organiser Pat Brereton has been widely thanked for achieving such a pleasant day.
