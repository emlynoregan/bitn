The Burra Burrs DC and the Clare DC boundary would remain as it is, but it is recommended that the southern three Hundreds of the Hallett DC (Anne, Hallett & Tomkinson) be merged with the Burra Burra DC.  The Burra Burrs DC has not yet had time to consider this recommendation.
