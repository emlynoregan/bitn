Changes in Commercial Street

Burra Video Store and Burra Radio & Electrical will be moving into what was Jennison’s on the north side of Commercial Street.

Gillian’s Plant & Garden Shop will move into the present video store.

Jennison’s itself on the south side is undergoing a transformation.

The ANZ Bank has had internal renovations.

Pearce’s Building has been re-painted.
