Weather

Good rain with up to 180 points fell over the inside country, but to the east falls were patchy.

Farrell Flat recorded 180 points, Booborowie 131, Hallett 19, Morgan 145 and Burra 99.

To the east the Burra to Morgan area reported about 100 points (1 inch), Redcliffe 78, Kia-Ora 47, Balah 40, Woolgangi 30, Thistlebeds 29, Faraway Hill 20 and Pine Valley 15.
