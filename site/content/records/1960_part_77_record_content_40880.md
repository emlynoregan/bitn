Advt.  Elders-GM on instructions from M.R. & E.J. Humphrys of Hanson will auction ‘Glenowie’ Hanson of 917 acres in the Burra RSL Hall on 9 August.  To be offered in three lots:

487 acres of freehold: sections 49, 50 & 842-845 in Hd Hanson in 9 paddocks with a seven-roomed stone house etc.

288 acres of Crown Perpetual Lease: sections 471-473 & 38 in Hd Hanson in five sheep-proof paddocks with a modern textured brick home of three bedrooms etc.

141½ acres freehold: sections 36 & 37 in Hd Hanson in two paddocks.
