Hallett Hall.  The new supper rooms, donated by Mrs Owen-Smyth, were opened on Friday.  Burra Mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone and Mrs Baulderstone attended as did Mr P.H. Quirke MP and Mrs Quirke.  A successful ball followed.
