Darren James, formerly of Burra has excelled at Barossa & Light Cricket Association, having been named A Grade Association Cricketer of the Year and collecting 12 trophies from the Association and from the Tanunda Cricket Club where he is currently A Grade Vice-Captain.

Burra Bowling Club held three tournaments over the Easter Weekend.

Friday Men’s Pairs was won by Stan Abrey & Graham Hann from Morgan.

Saturday’s Men’s Fours was won by Bruce Stockman and three visitors from the Millswood Club.

On Monday the Mixed Pairs was won by T. & M. Everall of Robertstown.
