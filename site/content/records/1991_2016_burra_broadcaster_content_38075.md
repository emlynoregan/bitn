BBH gets a new management team.  At the AGM of the BBH Football Club last week Peter McGuinness was elected the new President and Wayne Clonan becomes the Secretary.  Rob Sullivan continues as Treasurer.  Shane Darwin and Andrew Dredge joined the committee.
