Burra Councils to Unite

A joint sitting of the Burra Burra District Council and the Burra Town Council was held in the [DC] Council Chambers in Commercial Street on 3 December.  DC Chairman G.R. Finch presided.  Cr B.A. Riggs moved and Cr T. Quinn seconded that the merged Council comprise eight councillors from the District Council of Burra Burra and three from the Corporation of Burra.  Carried.

It was also resolved that in the new Council the Corporation would be represented by three Councillors from one ward, to be known as Burra Town Ward and that the ward boundaries would be those of the present Corporation.  The New Council will be called the District Council of Burra Burra.

It was also resolved that the rates would be levied under the unimproved land value system.
