Burra Primary School Welfare Club held its annual meeting recently.  Mrs A. Benger submitted the balance sheet.  After spending c. $800 for the year, they had $300 and with the voluntary contributions due in this week would have c. $450.  Elected were: President, Mrs J. Carpenter (3rd year in office); Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Thamm & P. Kirk; Secretary, Mrs C. Brereton and Treasurer, Mrs A. Benger.
