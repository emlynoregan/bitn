Burra LCL Women’s Branch held its AGM on 7 July in the Institute Supper Room.  Elected were: President, Mrs R.B. Pitcher; Vice-Presidents, Mrs G.S. Hawker, Mrs A.L. Kellock & Mrs J. Stevens; Secretary, Mrs J.W. Riggs and Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.  The Premier Sir Thomas Playford, Lady Playford and the Hon. W.W. Robinson were welcomed.  Sir Thomas addressed the meeting, stressing SA’s progress since WWII and continuing advances in the South East and at Whyalla.
