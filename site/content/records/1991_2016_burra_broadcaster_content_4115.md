Burra Reed Cross held its AGM on 7 June.  Elected were: President, Irene Hirschausen; Secretary, Jan Tiver and Treasurer, Alva Gillman.  After 32 years without sponsoring a baby in the Red Cross Baby Competition, Margaret Bruce has accepted the responsibility of entering her son Nicholas this past year.  Together with Red Cross she raised $4,791.  The Annual Red Cross Calling Appeal raised $1,400.
