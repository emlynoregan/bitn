M & BHA held its AGM 8 July and elected: President, Mrs B. Christie; Vice-President, Mrs B. Riggs; Secretary, Mrs J. Secomb & Treasurer, Mrs J. Bowden.  It was decided to pay off the remaining $80 on the clinic.  Members are considering having the exterior of the building painted.
