Mr Wayne Dodds, Burra manager for Oates Ltd, was severely injured on Sunday when he fell from a 50 ft pine tree at Brown Hill Creek in Adelaide.  Details of the incident are not known, but Mr Dodds is suffering from head and internal injuries, broken ribs, pelvis and ankle.
