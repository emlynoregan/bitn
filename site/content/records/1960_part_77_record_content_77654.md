Advt.  Elders-GM will conduct an auction and clearing sale for Trustees in the Estate of L.W. Thomas deceased.

In the Burra RSL Hall.  359.64 ha freehold:

Lot 1.  Homestead of 304.15 ha comprising sections 54-60 in the Hundred of Hallett with 4-roomed stone house etc.

Lot 2.  Holding paddock of 55.49 ha comprising section 52 Hundred of Hallett.

Clearing sale 11 miles northeast of Mt Bryan on the Mongolata Road.

[Details are in advertisements four columns wide by ½ column deep.]

Advt.  Elders-GM will auction ‘Glen View’: 2,733.8 ha freehold and leasehold, 19 km northeast of Mt Bryan on 19 February on instructions from Glen View Pastoral Co. Inc.  To be offered in 9 lots:

‘Back Paddock’ section 634

‘Webber’s Front Paddock’ section 644

‘Webber’s Homestead’ sections 641, 642, 643 & 666 with a 5-roomed stone house.

‘Scrub’ sections 89, 657 & 658

‘Ned Webber’s’ section 659W

‘Hill Paddock’ sections 661, 664, 668 & 667

‘Quinn’s’ sections 662 & 665
