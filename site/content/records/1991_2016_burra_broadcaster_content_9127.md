Other J150 Events

Back to Burra Golf Club weekend 8-9 June saw many former members and friends return to the town.

Under a sister town arrangement dating from 1983 members and staff of the Broken Hill City Council visited Burra on 22 July and attended the Gem & Minerals Exhibition.

Mr Nick Van Buuren, retired engineer with Adelaide Chemical Co. constructed a scale working model of Morphett’s Engine House.

The Jubilee 150 Committee held a promotion in Adelaide Arcade from 31 July to 6 August.
