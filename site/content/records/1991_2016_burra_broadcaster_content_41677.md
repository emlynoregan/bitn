Obituary.  Mr Les Warnes, formerly of Woolgangi Station has died.  Les was a generous benefactor to a number of Burra groups and projects and a book, written in by Meredith Satchell in collaboration with Les, Woolgangi and the East, recounts his love of the eastern country and reveals his extensive knowledge of the area.  [Lesley Charles Warnes borne 3 February 1922 at North Adelaide; died 17 April 2016 at Burra Hospital.]
