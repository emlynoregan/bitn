Tiddy ’Oggey Tea Rooms will re-open next Saturday 11 July.  Members of the old Court House Hotel Co-operative will operate them.  There will be fewer tables, more service and some craft activities.  Basic charges are being replaced by donations.  There will be no take-away pasties.  Open Wednesday to Sunday 12 noon to 4 p.m.
