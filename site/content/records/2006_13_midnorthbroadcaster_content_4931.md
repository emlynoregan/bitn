Cricket.  Grand Final of the Belalie-Mokota Association

In oppressive conditions the match was reduced to 35 overs per side and Burra 127 defeated Booborowie 108.

Booborowie suffered a late order collapse, losing their last six wickets for five runs.  Tony Brooks took 7 for 27 including 5 for 5 and a 3 wicket maiden over in his last three overs.   For the season the Association Wicket Keeping Trophy went to Peter McGuiness, while Geoff ‘Digger’ Thompson won the Bowling Aggregate Trophy (35) and the Bowling Average Trophy (9.05).
