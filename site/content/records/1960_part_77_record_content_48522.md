Burra Schools were visited by the Hon. Minister for Education Mrs J. Steele.  At the Primary School Mr E.C .Allen MP introduced the Head Mistress Miss M. Spratt who introduced Mr R. Jeffery Chairman of the DC and others, including staff.  There was an assembly of the students from Grades V, VI & VII.  Mrs Steele spoke to students of her visits to schools in the NT & SA after which the Minister visited the classrooms of the remaining students.  After morning tea Mr R. Moreland chaired a discussion which aired many questions including the future of the Primary School.  At the High School Mrs Steele addressed the students and inspected the grounds, where the lack of yard facilities and of room for further buildings was recognised.  She inspected the site for the proposed new school.

Mrs Steele also visited the Convent School of St Joseph and received a deputation from the Lewis Trust.
