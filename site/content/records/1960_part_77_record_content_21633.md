Burra Football Club held its Annual Dinner and Presentation Night in the RSL Hall last Saturday.  About 130 attended.  President L.A. Beinke welcomed those who attended.  Trophy winners:

‘A’ Grade

Fairest & Most Brilliant	R. Jenkins

Most Consistent	D. Buckingham

Most Effective	B. Eberhard

Best Backman	J. Phillips

Best Forward	W. Holness
