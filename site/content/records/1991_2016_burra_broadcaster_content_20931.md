Allyson Parsons’ Prints

Art of the Flower in which Sibylla Tydeman won the $500 first prize and also the People’s Choice [with a different painting].

In the Floral Art section Kelly Douglas won the Junior Prize, Joyce Lloyd the Individual Prize and Lenice Pearce & Chris Catt the Group Award.

An attendance figure in excess of 5,000 is believed to make it SA’s most visited regional gallery.
