Burra Town Council, 18 July

The Council declared a general rate of 19.75c in the dollar and a special rate of 1â…”c in the dollar.  Minimum rate payable to be $12.

The proposed works program will concentrate on Burra North roadworks.

Other aspects include extension of street lighting, the Memorial Oval fence, weed spraying, more street names, tree planting and general painting.

The Burra North Playground will receive some attention and the park by Merther Street and the Creek Reserve [from the Red Bridge to the Caravan Park] will both receive some beautification.
