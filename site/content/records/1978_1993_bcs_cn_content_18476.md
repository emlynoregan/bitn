Burra High School Students will hold a reunion next weekend.  (John Brak has been the chief organiser.)

Frank McMahon of Canberra and his friend Ian Auhl visited Burra last Monday.  Frank conducted several workshops for BCS students and read some of his poems including some with a Burra flavour.
