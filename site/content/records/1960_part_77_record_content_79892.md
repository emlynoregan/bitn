Burra Red Cross Branch held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs E. Baulderstone; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames A. Wright & G. Gallagher; Secretary, Mr H. Rich & Treasurer, Mr H. Heitbrink.  Membership was 27 with an average attendance of 8.  Despite low attendance, members give outstanding support both financially and with their time.
