Mr Lance Fiebig of Burra received the ‘Silver Star’ top award for Trial Driving in SA, presented at a dinner at the Hotel Elizabeth on Saturday night. Of the seven trials held in the 1964 season Mr Fiebig won four and was placed third in the other three.
