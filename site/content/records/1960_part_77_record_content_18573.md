The Methodist Church

Rev. R.S. & Mrs Jenner have been transferred to Kapunda.

Rev. & Mrs Lind come to Burra, having previously had circuits at Largs Bay and Whyalla.  The Linds have one daughter, Katrina, aged 18 months.

Rev. L. Kelsall has gone from Hallett to Salisbury and will be replaced by Rev. A.M. Houston and family from Pt Pirie.
