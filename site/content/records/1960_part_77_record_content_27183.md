Burra Camera Club held its AGM and elected: President, H. Shakeshaft; Vice-Presidents, M. Lind & R. Broad; Secretary, G. Dollman and Assistant Secretary, Mrs D. Dollman.  The club will investigate the possibility of purchasing a building to be erected at the show grounds for use by the club at shows.
