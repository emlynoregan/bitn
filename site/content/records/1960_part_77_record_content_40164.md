Pensioners’ League.  Port Adelaide Branch members gave their annual concert in the Burra Town Hall on 28 May.  The Mayor Mr Baulderstone welcomed them as did the local secretary of the League, Mr R.G. Bernhardt.  A bus load of pensioners attended from Peterborough.
