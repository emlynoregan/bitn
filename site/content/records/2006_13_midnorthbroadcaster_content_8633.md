Burra Leisure Activity Group held an afternoon of recognition for its volunteers last Tuesday.  Recognised for their service were Mrs Meryl White (30 years), Gordon Wooton (25 years) and also Olive Oates, Pat Taylor, Kath Irlam, Mary Anderson and Robert Reed.

[Photo of Volunteers co-ordinator for Yorke & Lower North Health Service, Verna Saunders, presenting a 25-years’ service award to Gordon Wooton and one of Verna Saunders and Burra Leisure Activity Group Co-ordinator, Marlene Wooton, presenting Mrs Meryl White of Burra with a certificate of acknowledgement and flowers in recognition of 30 years’ service.]
