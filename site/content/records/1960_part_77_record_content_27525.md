Elders-GM.  After a stay of 6½ years the Chief Clerk at Burra Mr R.N. Jenkins has been transferred to Bordertown.  While in Burra ‘Jenks’ has been associated with the football, tennis and table tennis.  He was also for a time secretary of the Swimming Club and a member of the Rural Youth Club.  He is replaced by Mr G.J. Timmins of Strathalbyn.
