Commercial Hotel.  Dion Biggs put his foot through a piece of cement in the yard of the Commercial Hotel and revealed an old well 6-8 ft across, 35 ft deep and containing 10 ft of water.  The Well will be filled in with debris from demolished walls from renovations at the Kooringa Hotel.
