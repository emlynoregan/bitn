Burra Institute Library Annual Meeting.  Elected: President, Miss E. Tiver; Vice-Presidents, Mr C. Haythorp & Mrs B. Kotz; Secretary, Mr Bill Nankivell and Treasurer, Mrs K. Treleaven.  Membership has fallen due to removals and television.  About 130 new books were added in the year, which ends in credit with £13-11-4 in the bank and £9-16-0 cash in hand.
