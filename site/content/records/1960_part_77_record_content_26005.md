Burra School parent bodies have been negotiating with the Burra Town Council and Council has now agreed to consider favourably the purchase of Council land in front of the school.  This comprises the area now used for the High School Oval etc. and the vacant land in front of the Primary School now used as a parking area.  Negotiations with the Highways Department should see approach roads to the school sealed by the end of the year.

Burra Schools’ Libraries’ Appeal stands at about £250.
