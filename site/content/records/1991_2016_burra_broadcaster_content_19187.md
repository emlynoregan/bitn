Greg Drew, Senior Geologist with PIRSA, is to give an illustrated talk on The History of Mining in Mexico including the Cornish Influence and Links with Burra, at the old Courthouse on 3 June.  He was recently in Mexico to present a paper at the 4th International Mining Industry History Conference and visited four main mining sites, two of which have strong Cornish connections.
