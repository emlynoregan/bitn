Land Auctions

‘Wyoming’ was passed in at 33/6 per acre, but subsequently sold for an undisclosed figure.

J.R. Phillips’ Hanson property of 602 acres freehold was passed in at £25-10-0 per acre.

H.L. Riggs’ Burra property of 52 acres was passed in at £16-10-0 per acre.

V.A.W. Riggs’ Burra property of 59 acres 3 roods was passed in at £20 per acre.

All were privately sold later at undisclosed prices.
