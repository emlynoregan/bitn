Burra-Morgan Road

One Kilometre was sealed early in the year, with a longer section let out to contract in the 1995-96 year.

The SA Government has announced that 20 km will be sealed commencing in April 1996.

The Great Bike Ride from Burra to Morgan on 13 August focussed attention on the road.
