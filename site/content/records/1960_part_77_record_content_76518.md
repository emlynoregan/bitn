Football.  North Eastern League Grand Final

‘A’ Grade	Burra 20.24 defeated South Clare 21.8 at Burra Oval

‘B’ Grade	South Clare 14.7 defeated North Clare 12.10

Senior Colts 	Burra 11.8 defeated North Clare 7.3

Junior Colts	South Clare 4.4 defeated Blyth 3.3

Awards:

Life Membership of North Eastern League awarded to R.E. Dewhirst & A.J. Cousins.

Taminga Hotel Goal Kicking Award to B. Pontt (South Clare)

1975 Mail Medal & Mobil Trophy to J. Wallace (South Clare)

AMP B Grade Goal Kicking Award to K. Bache (South Clare)

B Grade C. Pink Medal to C. Hall (Booborowie-Hallett)

Senior Colts ‘Bulldog’ Medal to A. Spackman (Burra)

Burra Record Trophy to M. Wanganeen (Blyth)
