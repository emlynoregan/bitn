Rev. Clarrie Hore was inducted into the Burra Methodist Circuit at a service on Friday night conducted by Rev. A.H.M. Ellison of Nuriootpa, Chairman of the Middle District and Rev. D. Gibson of Clare gave the address.  Mrs Jack Oates was the organist.  Supper followed in the Church Hall.
