Meals on Wheels held its AGM recently.  The group had delivered 2,203 meals for the year, with ten people currently receiving them.  Three members received 25-year long service awards on Australia Day: Betty Thamm, Anne Finch and Jan Tiver.  Elected were: President & Welfare Officer, Celine Stockman; Secretary, Jan Tiver and Treasurer & Staff Officer, Shana Nourse.
