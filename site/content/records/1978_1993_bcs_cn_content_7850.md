Mrs Stella Sandland, formerly of Burra and now of Adelaide, celebrated her 100th birthday recently.

[Born Stella Marion Lord born 19 May 1883 at Moonta Mines.  She married Clarence Houston Humphris in 1904 at Pt Germein.  He died in 1920 and she married William Humphrey Sandland in 1922 at Clare.  He died in 1942.]
