Burra Red Cross held its AGM.  For the year there were 25 members with an average attendance of 10 at the 8 meetings.  $450 was forwarded to Headquarters this financial year.  Elected for 1973-74 were: President, Mrs Baulderstone; Vice-Presidents, Mrs Wright & Mrs Odgers; Secretary, Mrs Rich and Treasurer, Mrs Heitbrink.
