Bus Services to Adelaide

Bus Australia, incorporating Briscoes and Murtons

To Adelaide

Daily except Saturday	Departs Burra 4.25 a.m.		Arrives Adelaide 6.35 a.m.

Daily except Sunday & Monday Holidays 	Departs Burra 8.15 a.m.		Arrives Adelaide 10.24 a.m.

Sundays except when a Monday Holiday	Departs Burra 6.00 p.m.		Arrives Adelaide 8.10 p.m.

From Adelaide

Monday to Friday 	Departs Murtons 4.30 p.m.	Arrives Burra 6.50 p.m.

Daily Except Saturday	Departs Briscoes 5.40 p.m.	Arrives Burra 8.05 p.m.

Saturday only	Departs Adelaide 6.30 p.m.	Arrives Burra 8.45 p.m.

Sunday only	Departs Adelaide 4.15 p.m.	Arrives Burra 6.30 p.m.
