Robberies

There has been a spate of robberies in Burra, with fuel the main target; the victims include the Highways Department Depot, the Burra Burra DC Depot and Clodes Hardware.  Electrical equipment worth $6,000 was stolen from buildings adjacent to the E & WS tanks at Hanson.  Thefts and vandalism at BCS have also been a problem.
