Elspeth Radford speaks of the latest clothing fashions featuring denim-wool, a product offering a 20% thermal benefit.  Saltbush Clothing is featuring it in men’s, women’s and kids’ pants and women’s skirts.  It is 11% wool with 195 micron wool used on the inside of the fabric.
