BCS Staff changes

Mr Rick Pellew moves to Parndana Area School after six years here.

Mr Kym Reynolds will replace his from Parndana Area School.

Mrs Marilyn Wall will return part-time as a Commercial teacher.

Miss Bronwyn Howard will be completing a year’s contract.

Mrs Joanna Demasi will be taking 12 months’ accouchement leave.
