Obituary.  Mrs Ethel M. Murphy of Booborowie died last week in Adelaide aged 83.

[Born Ethel Wall 31 January 1884 at Hampton, Burra: died Ethel Mary Murphy 27 August 1966 Magill, residence Booborowie.  She married James Michael Murphy as Ethel Mary Wall in 1908.]
