Burra Cricket Club AGM was well attended.  It was decided to field two senior teams in the Mokota Association.  Elected: Patron, Bert Beinke; President, Ray Wardlele; Vice-President, David Thiem; Secretary, Peter Opperman; Treasurer, Paul Smedley; Captains, Alan Day & Ron Smedley.

The Junior Club will field two teams for 1981-82.
