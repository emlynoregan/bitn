Mid North Football Association held its AGM on 19 March.  There was a long debate over policy, especially when AI and AII clubs differed.  Elected: Patron, W.E. Blatchford (President 1945-63), President, L.A. Twarts [sic] (Saddleworth); Senior Vice-President, Stan Noske (Robertstown) and secretary-Treasurer, Arthur Kench (Auburn).

The new Saddleworth-Marrabel United team was admitted.  Blyth was admitted to the AII Division.
