Centenary of the Burra Railway

The Australian Railway Historical Society will bring a special vintage train to Burra on 29 August to mark the 100th anniversary of the opening of the line. Anyone wishing to ride on the train should contact Mrs Kath Christie at 10 Thames St Burra.
