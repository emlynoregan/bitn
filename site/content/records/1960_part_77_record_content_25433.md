M & BHA held its AGM at the clinic on 12 July.  Twenty members attended.  Elected: President, Mrs M. Odgers; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Thamm & B. Bevan; Secretary, Mrs M. Tiver Jnr and Treasurer, Mrs D. Bannister.  Mrs P. Kirk was guest speaker.
