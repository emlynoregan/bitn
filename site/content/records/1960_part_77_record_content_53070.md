Cycling.  The Burra-Adelaide Cycling Classic will be held next Saturday.  This year it will be run by the Norwood Cycling Club, which id the oldest Cycling Club in the Southern Hemisphere.  The route will be: Market Square, Mt Bryan, Market Square, Manoora, Saddleworth, Riverton, Tarlee, Kapunda, Greenock, Sheoak Log, Gawler, Elizabeth Yorketown Road and Elizabeth Shopping Centre.
