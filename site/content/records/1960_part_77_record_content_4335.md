Burra Golf Associates held their AGM and elected: Patroness, Mrs A.L. Collins; President, Mrs Jim Riggs; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames D. Field & H. Topsfield and Secretary-Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.

The Secretary reported on a successful year with entries on open day up on previous years, improvements to the clubhouse facilities.  Portagas was installed.
