Ian & Denise List, owners of Burra Squash Courts have been given permission by the Goyder Council to sell caravans from their Burra North site.  Dallas and Paul O’Bryan will look after the Burra operation of Trade Winds Caravan Centre – a well-known Adelaide caravan retailer.
