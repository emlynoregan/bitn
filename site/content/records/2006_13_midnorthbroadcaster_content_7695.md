Cathy Slater of Spalding writes to acknowledge the great effort of students from Burra [Community School] and Riverton High School for raising money for Childhood Cancer.  Burra raised $300.65 and Riverton/Saddleworth collected $372.50.  Clare High School students collected $1068.40, which was almost $100 more than last year.
