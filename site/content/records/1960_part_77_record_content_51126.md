Mr Neil Hurst will take up his position as postmaster in Burra on 14 May.  He has been in charge at Morgan for three years.  He began as a Junior Postal Clerk in 1951 at Tanunda.  He has a wife and two daughters and is interested in golf, cricket and night bowls.  He has also been a Warden of the Church of England.  [Photo]
