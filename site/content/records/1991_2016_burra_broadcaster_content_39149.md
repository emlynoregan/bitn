The Midnight Oil Cottage [at Cobb & Co.’s Corner] is again to feature in a musical project.  The Adelaide Band ‘Sonic Divide’ spent two days this week filming at the cottage and nearby sites on Alex & Leanne Stockman’s property.  The debut video is for the track ‘I Wonder’ from the band’s self-titled album.
