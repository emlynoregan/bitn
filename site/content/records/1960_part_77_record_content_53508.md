Bowls.  Associates President Dorrie Pietsch welcomed about 100 bowlers and guests to the opening of the season on Wednesday 14 October.  The opening was performed by SAWBA Vice-President Mrs Ruth Dall.  The President of the Burra Club Mr Edgar Pietsch welcomed visitors and players to the opening of the season on Saturday last and called on Mr Binks-Williams to declare the season open.
