Burra Primary School held its Arbor Day on 13 August and it was well attended by parents and friends.  Trees were planted in fine weather.  Mr John Riggs gave the address and 14 Fuchsia Gums were planted; two by each grade, on the eastern boundary of the new oval.  [Planters are named.]
