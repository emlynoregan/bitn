BCS Secondary Camps for 1984:

These will be held in the week commencing 12 March.

Year 8 will be at Barmera.

Year 9 will go to the Iron Triangle.

Year 10 students will attend the Careers Campaign in Adelaide.

Year 11 students will be undertaking work experience away from Burra.

Year 12 students will go to Pt Vincent.
