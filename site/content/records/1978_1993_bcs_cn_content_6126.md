BCS.  In Community Week BCS students planted trees at the Miners’ Dugouts to screen adjacent properties, restored and painted the picket fence at the Market Square Museum and washed the Diamond T Fire Engine at the Bon Accord Museum.  They also carried out further cleaning up at the Redruth Gaol, the Powder Magazine and the Jinker.
