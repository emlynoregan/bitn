BCS.  The 1998 SRC members were presented to the Governor, Sir Eric Neal, on Monday morning at a student assembly.  Sir Eric gave an address especially directed to senior students.  A presentation was made to Jock & Rose Duncan for their support in making Gum Creek available for the SRC camp, held at the start of each school year.  [2 photos.]
