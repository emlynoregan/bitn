Burra Burra DC

1.  Request the Department of Lands to ban off road recreational vehicles and firearms at Redbanks.

2.  Received a grant of $10,000 to engage a consultant to prepare concept plans for Burra Creek redevelopment.

3.  Will support the concept of a community FM radio station.

4.  Will support the re-establishment of a Rural Youth Club.

5.  Congratulates the Lewis Board of Management for being awarded the 1992 SA State Bank Tourism Award for budget accommodation.  The Paxton Square Cottages will now be judged in the National Awards in Canberra 15-16 October.

6.  Clare Valley Taxi Service has been given permission to operate in the Burra District.
