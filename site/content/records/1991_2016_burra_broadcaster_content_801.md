Mr Stan Batten, previously a candidate for Grey Power in the Kavel by-election 9 May 1992, has now been endorsed as a candidate for the Australian Independent Alliance.  He will contest the Federal seat of Makin, presently held by Mr Peter Duncan of the Australian Labor Party.
