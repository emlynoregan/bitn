Animal Rescue & Care submits a letter decrying the necessary euthanasia of a female brown goshawk on 7 July after it had been caught in a rabbit trap.  This wanton act of cruelty carries a maximum fine of $1,250 and such traps can also endanger domestic animals and children as well as wildlife.
