Ashley Bailey is now working at the New McPhee’s Butchers’, which incorporates the Cold Stores.

Michael Day began a butchering apprenticeship at Maidment’s on Monday.

David Madigan of Booborowie will take part in his Army Passing Out Parade soon.

Peter & Sur Ryan, with Kirsty and Matthew will soon move.  Peter will take a position at Miltaburra School for next term and for 1989.  They will live at Wirrulla.

Laurence & Eileen Hein of Thames Street will celebrate their 50th wedding anniversary on 18 October, having been married at Monarto in 1938.

The Wood Family has taken over the Mt Bryan Hotel.  Daughter Shelly is a whiz in her wheelchair and is ay BCS, while Cheryl is at Mt Bryan Primary.

Paxton Square renovations are underway.  [Along Paxton Terrace.]
