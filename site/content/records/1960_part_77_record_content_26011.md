Mobil Shearing Championship 1965 was won by Peter Kelly of Buninyong, Victoria, repeating his 1962 effort.

Contenders were required to shear four sheep in twelve minutes for maximum points for speed.  Other points are awarded for the quality of the shearing:	Style and handling control	5

Absence of skin cuts	25

Absence of second cuts and wool

left on sheep	35

Position of fleece at finish	3

Workmanship	17

Speed	15

100

Second place went to S.C. Pittaway of Tarlee.
