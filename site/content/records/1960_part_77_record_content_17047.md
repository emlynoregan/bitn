SA Stockowners’ Association, Burra Branch held its AGM and elected: President G.S. Hawker, Vice-President, M. de N. Lucas and Secretaries, Messrs A.L. Wright & E. Orchard.

President for the past year, G.S. Hawker, greeted visitors and delivered his report.  Mr H. Pick, State President, addressed the meeting on the Wool Promotion Levy.  Mr H.G. Brooks, a member of the State Executive, addressed the meeting on the importance of improved wool production.
