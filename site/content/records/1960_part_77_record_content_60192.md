Adelaide Wool Sales, 29 February-2 March.  Prices fell an average 7½%.  [Local district prices are printed.]

St Mary’s Harvest Festival will be held next Sunday 12 March.

Football. Blyth Football Club has applied to join the North Eastern League and was accepted 1 March.  Blyth will be the sixth club and will field ‘A’ Grade, ‘B’ Grade and Senior Colts teams.
