Salvation Army

The Peterborough Salvation Army members visited Burra recently to collect for their Harvest Thanksgiving and to investigate the possibility of recommencing activities in Burra.  Those interested should contact Bro. S.D. (Stan) Sullivan c/o Jennison’s Tyre Service or at Graham Street.
