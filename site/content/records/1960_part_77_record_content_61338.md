Burra Burra DC, 1 May

Resolved that the Burra Picture patrons be warned that closing will be considered in one month if there is no improvement [in behaviour.]

Resolved that an old dwelling owned by H.C. Davies and M. Bernhardt be demolished by the Council plant when suitable.

Resolved to inform the SA Government that the Council is opposed to daylight saving.

Transfer noted of the transfer from E.A. Schutz deceased to Thomas Johnson Schultz and various parts of section 52 Hundred of Kooringa disposed as Lostwithiel allotments 19, 15, 16, 18, 23, & 17.
