Burra Tennis Club had Jeff Thomson as speaker at a fundraiser at Burra sports Complex last Thursday night.  About 40 people greeted the famous Australian fast bowler and the tennis club made up a large section of the crowd.  The very few cricket enthusiasts present had a great night.  [Photo.]
