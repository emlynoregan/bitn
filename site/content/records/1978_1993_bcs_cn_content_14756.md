Mt Bryan Telephone Service

On Tuesday 2 June Mt Bryan Telephone subscribers were converted to an automatic exchange.  To all numbers listed in the 1987 directory for Mt Bryan add 9340 in front so that Mt Bryan 1 becomes 934001 and Mt Bryan 32 becomes 934032.  There are five exceptions:

22 becomes 934088

24 becomes 934080

33 becomes 934078

44 becomes 934060

The CFS becomes 934000
