The Adult Secondary Education Assistance Scheme will encourage students to return to full-time matriculation level studies, with a maximum grant valued at $1,600 p.a.  In 1976 matriculation classes in English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geography and History will be offered at BHS.
