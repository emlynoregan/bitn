Mr Malcolm Blight and wife visited Burra on Sunday.  He taught at Burra High School in 1945 and is now at King’s College in Adelaide.  His great-grandfather had a blacksmith’s forge in Burra in the mining days, but when the mine closed he moved to Clare.
