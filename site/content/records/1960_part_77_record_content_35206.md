Art Exhibition.  Paintings at the Clare Town Hall this week feature, among locally prepared works, two aspects of the well-known property ‘Mackerode’ by Sydney artist Mr Robert Liddicoat ASTC.  As well as this artist’s work others featured include Lionel Bull MVAS of Melbourne and Alan Kidd of Queensland and original oils by London artist Jon Peaty.
