Aberdeen Tennis Club held its AGM and elected: Patron, F.H. Reed; President, Lance Phillips; Senior Vice-President, Ivan Hirschausen; Secretary & Treasurer, S. &. F. Scroop; ‘A’ Grade Captain, Kevin Lane; ‘A’ Grade Vice Captain, Murray Tiver Jnr; ‘B’ Grade Captain, Bob Reed and ‘B’ Grade Vice-Captain, Bert Seaford.

The season opens on 22 September.
