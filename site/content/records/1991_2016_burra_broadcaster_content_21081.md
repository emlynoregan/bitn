Father Adrian Head of St Joseph’s Burra has been transferred to the Quorn-Carrieton Parish from 29 January.  The new priest in Burra will be Father Laurie Quinn who grew up in the Mt Bryan area.  Fr Head will be farewelled on 23 January in the Town Hall supper room following the 10.30 a.m. mass.
