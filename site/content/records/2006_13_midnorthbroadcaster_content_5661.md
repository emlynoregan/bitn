The Bush to Burra Cattle Drive from 7-11 October will aim to raise funds for health services in the Mid North.  It is a 56 km ride from Whyte Yarcowie to Burra along part of the historic Kidman Stock Route.  The riders will drive c. 400 head of cattle.  Sixty riding positions are available over five days.  The completion coincides with the Burra Show.
