Burra Hospital.  A new electric bell system is being installed.  The surrounding area is being bituminised and the lawns top dressed.  The Nurses’ Home and the Midwifery quarters will soon be re-painted.  The cropping program has yielded £500-11-0 for the first advance on this season’s wheat.
