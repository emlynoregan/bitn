Burra Football Club held its AGM and elected: Patron, Reg. Warnes; President, L.A. Beinke; Vice-President, E. Pietsch; Secretary, W. Holness and Treasurer, A. Webber.

Gate takings in 1963 at £165-11-6 were up £35 on 1962.  The club had a balance of £238-18-0 and there was £183-14-4 in the benevolent fund.
