An Earthquake was recorded in the Burra area in the early hours of Friday morning.  It was centred about 10 km SW of Burra and registered 3 on the Richter Scale.  It was felt as far east as Wildildie.  A small tremor at 2.5 on the scale was recorded at 10.47 p.m. earlier the same night with its centre 20 km SE of Peterborough.
