School enrolments, staff changes etc. are given for Hallett, Farrell Flat, Booborowie and Leighton schools and for Burra Kindergarten.

Burra Kindergarten has an enrolment of 47.

Burra Primary School enrolment is 257 compared with 268 in 1972.

New staff: Miss Abbott comes from Poochera Special Rural School to take Grades IV & V

Mrs Henderson from Littlehampton will take Grades I & II

Mrs D. Hill is the new Clerical Assistant.

Teacher Aides are Mrs D. Reed & Miss C. Burnett.

Burra High School

Miss Coralie Plush is Senior Mistress and comes from overseas.  (Previously at Kidman Park Girls’ Technical High School.)

Kym Dixon & Robert George come from Adelaide Teachers College.

Jenny Morton comes from Adelaide Teachers College and Birdsville.

Patience Murphy is formerly of Burra and Maureen Sherlock comes from Vermont Girls’ Technical High School.

Mt Bryan Primary has an enrolment of 36.
