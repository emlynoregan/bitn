J.G. Duncan Hughes, LCL candidate for Wakefield, drew a fair audience to the Institute on Wednesday.  Hon. A.L. McEwin MLC also spoke and supported the Mayor’s views that a National Government should be formed.  The speech of Mr Duncan Hughes is reported in c. 1¼ columns.
