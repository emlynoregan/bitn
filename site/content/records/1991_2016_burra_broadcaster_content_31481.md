The Market Square/Best Place Development Plan is to Proceed

Work is already underway in the area adjacent to the Council Office.  Elements include:

Extending the car parking in front of the Council Office

Replacing grass and concrete around the rotunda with pavers

Upgrading the concrete island in Market Square

Negotiations are continuing with ETSA about the lighting upgrade including underground cables

New street trees will be planted

Public information signs will be made uniform

The plan will be implemented over a number of years
