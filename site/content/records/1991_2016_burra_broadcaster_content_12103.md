The Main Street Committee Chairman Richard Holbrook has retired and Russell Skinner has been elected in his place.  Russel and wife Jan have moved to Burra and live in the newly painted Pearce’s Buildings.

Burra Burra DC – December

Council approved work needed to make and finish the sealing of the road around Burra Oval.

Mt Bryan dump is nearing the end of its lifespan.

Springvale Road is to be renamed Ironmine Road.

Council & BCS will share the cost of sealing the road from Smelts Road to the school and the northern car park.  The total cost is c. $18,500.  The school will assume responsibility for the maintenance.

Vic & Monique Haddow have resigned as managers of Paxton Square Cottages at the end of their contract, but will continue in the role until Easter.  A Council sub-committee will select replacements.
