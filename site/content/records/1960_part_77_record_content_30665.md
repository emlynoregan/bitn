Burra Burra DC, 4 July.   (Partly already reported.)

A general rate of 0.8 cents in the dollar was declared with the following differential rates:

10c in the dollar for the townships of Booborowie, Farrell Flat and Mt Bryan and Pt Section 90 Pt Section 75 Mt Bryan East.  5c in the dollar for the Township of Hanson.  Minimum rate to be $3.

The Department of Lands advises that the Battery Reserve at Mongolata has been resumed.
