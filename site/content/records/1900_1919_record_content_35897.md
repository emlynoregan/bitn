Obituary.  John Bailey, husband of Elizabeth Bailey, died at Aberdeen on 9 October aged 67.  Leaving six sons and three daughters.

Mr Bailey arrived in Adelaide aged 9 and with his father helped to fence the Gawler railway.  His father then took land at Mintaro and later at Gum Creek.  Mr Bailey then bought land adjacent to his fatherâ€™s and held it till he sold it upon retiring to Aberdeen about two years ago.  The family are: Miss Sarah Bailey (NSW), Messrs George (Brinkworth), Alfred (Leighton), Henry (WA), James (Melbourne) and in Aberdeen the Misses Mabel & Mary and Messrs David and John.
