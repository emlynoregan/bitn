Burra High School Parents & Friends’ Assoc. had over 100 at its first meeting for the year.  President Mrs F. Matthews chaired the meeting.  The new headmaster Mr Beare explained the introduction of continuous assessment and interim reports to parents.  The Association’s AGM is planned for Tuesday 18 March.
