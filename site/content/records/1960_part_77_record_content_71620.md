Accident/Obituary.  At about 1.45 p.m. on Sunday about 22 miles east of Burra on the Morgan Road, a vehicle travelling towards Morgan overturned.  Two women passengers were admitted to the Burra Hospital with minor injuries, but a 61-year-old man from England and his 38-year-old son from Para Vista died.
