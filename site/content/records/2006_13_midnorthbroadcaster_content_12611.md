Burra Community School has taken out top prize in both senior and junior sections of the State-wide Poultry in School Competition.

Senior Section: 1st prize to BCS: Darcy Mudge, Luke Roth & Neil Maxted ($500).  (Teacher Ms Suzie Hams.)

Senior Section: 2nd prize to BCS: Nicole Redford, Bridgette Wiech, Anastasia Hammill & William Brooks ($250) (Teacher Ms Suzie Hams.)

Middle Section: 1st prize to BCS: Che Kelly & Katrina Creeny ($300).  (Teacher Ms Suzie Hams.)

Junior Section: 1st prize to BCS: Riley Webster ($200).  (Teacher Ms Suzie Hams.)

Junior Section: 2nd prize to BCS: Tom Hill ($100).  (Teacher Ms Suzie Hams.)
