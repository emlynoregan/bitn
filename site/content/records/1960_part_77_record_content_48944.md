Burra Burra DC, 1 December

Resolved that Samin be asked if the Powder Magazine will be left and that costs of repairs necessary are to be obtained.

Resolved that tenders be called for a new toilet block to serve the shops on Market Square.

Council is to endeavour to assist Samin Ltd to find a suitable air strip.

Signs are to be purchased and selected area [for] car body dump to be advertised.
