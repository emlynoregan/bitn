Letter. Helen Szuty for the Ratepayers’ Action Group writes concerning the public meeting for ratepayers at the Burra Town Hall on 4 September.  So far only Crs Gill and Reilly have accepted the invitation to attend.  The meeting is called to consider a range of topics including:

Recent rate increases.

The direction of Paxton Square Cottages.

The future of the Burra caravan and camping Park.

The prospects for Burra Community Swimming Pool.
