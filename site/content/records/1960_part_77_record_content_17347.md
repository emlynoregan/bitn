Accident/Obituary.  Robert Douglas Bone (21), bank clerk of Mile End, died almost instantly at Bendigo Station on Sunday [1 September] when his brother Graham John Bone (19), draftsman of Mile End, accidentally discharged a rifle and hit the deceased in the stomach.  They were setting up a photograph for a mock hold-up scene using a delayed action setting on the camera when the accident occurred.  Dr L.G. Miller of Burra and the Burra ambulance attended.  Police are investigating.
