The last Mass in the old church was held on 12 January 1958.

The church was then demolished.

The foundation stone of the new church was laid and blessed by His Grace Dr M. Beovich, Archbishop of Adelaide on 14 July 1958.  [Sic: but given following dates for events, presumably 1957.]

The new church was blessed and opened by the Auxiliary Bishop of Adelaide Rev. Dr J.W. Gleeson on 19 January 1958.
