Hazel O’Connor writes as Hon. Secretary of the National Trust:

The National Trust in Burra endorses the course of action proposed by Mr Ian Auhl in his recent letter.  The branch has decided to raise funds to resite the threatened mine buildings.  An appeal committee has been formed.
