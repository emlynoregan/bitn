Advt.  Dalgety-NZL on instructions from Mr J.L. Connors of Mt Bryan will sell 471 acres freehold at the RSL Hall, Burra, 8 March.  It is located 3 miles south of Mt Bryan and 9 miles north of Burra, comprising sections 174 and 33 of the Hundred of Kingston with a five-roomed stone dwelling etc.  [Improvements are described.]
