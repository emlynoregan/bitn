Burra Primary School Sports Day was held on 3 April on a damp day with cold winds.  Aberdeen House won for the fourth year in succession.  The Boys’ Championship went to Geoffrey Bernhardt and the Girls’ Championship to Margaret Allen.  [Details including results occupy c. 2⅓ columns.]
