Mid North Sheep Dog Training Club recently held a meeting on the Farrell Flat Oval.  Nineteen handlers and fourteen dogs attended.  Guest speaker was Lew Noble.  He will return to Farrell Flat on 21 August when the club is holding a dog trial to raise funds for Paula Gill, a Miss Australia award participant.
