Basketball.  North Eastern Grand Finals

‘A’ Grade	Booborowie 44 defeated Burra Gold 24

‘B’ Grade	Spalding Red 37 defeated Leighton 30

‘C’ Grade	Leighton 47 defeated Booborowie 19

‘D’ Grade	Hallett 14 defeated Spalding 13

Best ‘A’ Grade Player Award, the Julie Affolter Trophy, was awarded for the first time and went to Heather Short of Burra Gold.  The award commemorates Julie Affolter who died this year in a car accident.
