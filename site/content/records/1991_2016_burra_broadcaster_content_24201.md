Burra Clare Snowtown Health Service has received a grant of $51,400 to pursue a service planning process across the service area.  There will be extensive community consultation.  If a successful service delivery plan is developed there will be the opportunity for new money, particularly in primary health care.  Craig Stanbridge, CEO.
