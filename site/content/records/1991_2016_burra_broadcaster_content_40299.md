The Australia Day Celebrations at Pickett Reserve drew a crowd estimated at 180-200.  Maureen Bevan was presented with her Citizen of the Year Award and Burra Golf Club President, Dick Cleland accepted the award for Event of the Year – the Golf Club’s Centenary Celebrations.

Burra Sing Australia Choir, conducted by John Fuss, led the singing of traditional songs.  The guest speaker was Ian Falkenberg, District Manager of the Department of Environment, Water and Natural Resources.  The Australia Day Cake was shared among the crowd.  [Photo page 1 and 8 on page 8.]
