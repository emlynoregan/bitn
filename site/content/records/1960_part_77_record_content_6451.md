Friday 28 April.  Mr J. Sladden was MC to a series of items in Burra Town Hall by students from Burra Primary, Burra High and St Joseph’s Convent School.  The Mayor E.T. Baulderstone introduced Mr R. King, who spoke on What are we doing for our youth?
