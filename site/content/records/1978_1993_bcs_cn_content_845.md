Burra Town Hall

The District Clerk Mr Tom Perry says Council is confident of a dollar for dollar subsidy from the Arts Development Council and the SA Government to enable upgrading the Town Hall.  It is suggested a grant of $5,000 would enable the purchase of new stage drapes and lights and the repainting of the interior of the hall.
