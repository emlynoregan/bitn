Obituary.  Curio, the wonder buck jumper died earlier this year aged 28, but her offspring will not let her be forgotten.  It took ten years before anyone stayed on her for the required ten seconds and that was Allan Wood in 1953.  Offspring include: Son of Curio, Curio’s Special, Curiosity and Curio’s Last.
