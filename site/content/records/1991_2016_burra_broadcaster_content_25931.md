Burra Railway Station.  Hon. Graham Gunn MP for Stuart presented a cheque for $5,500 to Mr Steven Kerrigan, CEO of Goyder Council as a grant from the Department of Environment and Heritage to enable some essential conservation work at the Burra Railway Station.  The building is rapidly deteriorating.  The building is currently owned by Australian Southern Railroad while Transport SA owns the railway land.
