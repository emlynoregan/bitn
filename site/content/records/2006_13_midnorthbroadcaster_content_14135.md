Concerned Resident of Burra & District met with Member for Stuart, Dan van Holst Pellekaan at the White Cedars Café to discuss a range of issues last Wednesday morning.  The meeting was chaired by Lower North Health Advisory Council Member, Helen Edwards.  The future of the Burra Hospital and several other topics were discussed in the two hour meeting.
