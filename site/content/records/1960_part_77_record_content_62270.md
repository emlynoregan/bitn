Burra North Red Cross held its AGM in the Aberdeen Football Club Rooms on Wednesday 5 July.  During the year $400 was sent to the Red Cross March Appeal.  Elected: Patroness, Mrs G.C. Heinrich; President, Mrs I. Hirschausen; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames M. Cox & R. Thamm; Secretary, Mrs R.M. Jennison; Assistant Secretary, Mrs E. Heinrich; Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.
