P. & R. Stockman’s Springvale was founded 35 years ago and their sheep have achieved a top price of $12,000.  The Merino Stud was established in 1961 with a ram and a ewe from ‘The Grove’.  The Stockman brothers bought ‘The Grove’ in 1979 and have introduced breeding stock from Old Ashrose and Greenfields.  The Poll Merino Stud was established in 1977.  Shearing is spread in July, September and February.  The property comprises 2,000 ha – half is cropped annually to wheat, oats and barley.
