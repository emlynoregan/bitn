Burra Races were held on Wednesday in perfect weather and receipts were up, with an expected profit of about £180.  The crowd was estimated at 1,000 and the gate takings were £244.  The Burra CWA again catered for lunch and afternoon tea.  The racing was good.  [Results are printed on page 6.]
