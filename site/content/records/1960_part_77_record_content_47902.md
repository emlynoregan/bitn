Local Crime.  Two  break-ins on Thursday night have been reported.

Burra Motor Co. in Thames St was entered and thieves used the company’s own oxy-torch on the safe to steal c. $60.

At the Burra Hotel a window was forced and c. $25 taken from the till.

Attempts to enter T.W. Wilkinson’s & Co. and the Burra Stationers were unsuccessful.
