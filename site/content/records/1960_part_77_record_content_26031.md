Naturalization Ceremony

About 80 people attended the naturalization ceremony for Mrs Berendina Brak, Mr Jacob Brak and Mrs Mietje Pike, who became Australian citizens last Thursday evening at the Burra Town Hall.  The three had been citizens of Holland.  Mr Baulderstone administered the oath and Mr C.R. Kelly MHR & Mr P.H. Quirke MP attended the ceremony.
