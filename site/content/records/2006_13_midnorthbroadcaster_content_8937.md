Burra Miners Hockey Club wind-up and presentation night was held on 19 September.

Men, Best and Fairest	Darren Sweet

U17, Best and Fairest	Caleb Desmet

U14, Best and Fairest	Jackson Weiss

U17, Spectators’ Award	Caleb Desmet

U14, Spectators’ Award	Neil Maxted

U17, Coaches Award	John Robinson

U14, Coaches Award	Elisha Desmet

Club President, Shaun Dollman, presented Ros Newbey with the Valda & Kevin Williams Memorial Club Award in recognition of her contribution to the club.
