Michael & Kylie Day have opened a new butcher shop in Commercial Street called Day’s Quality Meats.  Michael is the son of Alan and Di Day of Burra and had an apprenticeship with John & Darryl Maidment – completing it at Meningie.  He has had a butcher shop in Clare for the last two years.
