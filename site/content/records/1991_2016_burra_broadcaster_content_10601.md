Burra Burra DC.

Work planned for 1996-97 includes:

$3,500 for Burra Baby Care Room.

$5,000 for ongoing headstone restoration at Burra Cemetery.

$3,500 to paint the Burra Art Gallery.

$8,500 for Redbanks Reserve development.

$12,000 for a concept plan for Market Square and a work practice policy regarding heritage.

Sealing around Burra Oval will also continue.

About $15,000 is provided for a unisex disabled toilet opposite the Town Hall.

Sealing of the Town Hall car park.
