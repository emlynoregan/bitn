Note that the paper refers to Mr Ullmann as Charles and W. Ullman.  There are other references to him as W.

There is only one Ullman registered in SA as dying aged 95: Frank Louis Ullman who was born 3 December 1874 at Kooringa and died 18 March 1970 at St Peters, residence College Park.  He was a son of C.A.W. Ullmann.  He could not have been the baker who had been in business in Kooringa by c. 1869 as stated in the Record:
