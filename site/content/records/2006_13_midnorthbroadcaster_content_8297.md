Saint Joseph’s Primary School, Peterborough students recently toured former churches in the district: Our Lady of Dolours at Yongala (1904-1960s), St Michael’s & St John’s Terowie (1872-1998), Our Lady of Mt Carmel at Dawson (1885-1969), St Celestine’s at Lancelot (1879-1902).

They also visited the Terowie Convent and School (1916-1966) and the site of demolished churches including St Sebastian’s near the airport (1878-98) and St Patrick’s at Nackara (1897-1962). [Photo of St Joseph’s Year 6/7 students at St Celestine’s Church at Lancelot, a ruin situated in a once thriving community and a photo of St Joseph’s students who were accompanied by parishioners John & Beth Sandland, Peter Malycha and Fr Paul Quirk at Our Lady of Mount Carmel at Dawson.]
