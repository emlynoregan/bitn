Burra to Adelaide cycling Classic will be held this Saturday.  21 riders will start in the 100 mile handicap departing Market Square at 10 a.m. and travelling via Manoora, Saddleworth, Riverton, Tarlee, Kapunda, Greenock, Daveyston, Sheoak Log, Gawler By-Pass to finish at Waterloo Corner.
