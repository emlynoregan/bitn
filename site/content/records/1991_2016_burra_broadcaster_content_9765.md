Letter.  Wendy Burge, Group Leader of the Nursing Mothers’ Association of Australia writes in support of a proposal to change the present Council storeroom, located between the Tourist Office and Boulger’s Supermarket, into a Baby Care Room.  Donations to meet the requirements of the change are sought.  Mr Stephen Angel has offered to provide floor coverings – response by 4 April please.
