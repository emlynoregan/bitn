Obituary.  Leonard Melrose Schmidt collapsed at Brady Creek tennis courts while playing on Saturday 12 March and died aged 37.  He is survived by his wife and three young children.

[Born 10 October 1922 Robertstown: died 12 March 1960 Emu Downs.]
