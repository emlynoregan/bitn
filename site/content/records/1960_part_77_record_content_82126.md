Burra Library AGM elected President, Mr R.J. Steele, Vice-Presidents, Mrs Ben Kotz and Mrs I. Hirschausen; Secretary, Mr Ben Kotz & Treasurer, Mrs E. Carpenter.  Support this year was appreciated from the Burra Burra DC and from a raffle.  A special meeting in December resolved to join the Burra Community Library, but the present arrangements will continue until the new library is established.
