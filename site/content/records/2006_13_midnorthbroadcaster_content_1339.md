Notice.  Goyder Council gives notice of intent to close the public road known as Hardy Street in Burra, between Allotments 360 in FP 21336, 160-162 in FP 212552, 1 in DP 28414, 51 in FP 217445, 12-14 in FP 104614 and 116 in FP 212475 in the Hundred of Kooringa.

Marked A in Preliminary Plan 06/0069, this portion to be merged with adjoining Allotments 1, 160, 161, 162 & 360 to form one allotment.  Objections to be lodged within 28 days.
