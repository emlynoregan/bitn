Burra High School

At 174 enrolments are down 6 on last February, but about the same as for the second half of 1969.  There are five new staff:

Mr J. Ayliffe is Senior Master and had been a teacher at the school in the past.  He replaces Mr Brand who has gone to Willunga High School as Deputy Headmaster.

Miss B. Kussadias is Senior Mistress.

Mr M. Allen comes from Pt Augusta.

Mr N. Williams and Miss J. Urlwin have been appointed from Teachers College.
