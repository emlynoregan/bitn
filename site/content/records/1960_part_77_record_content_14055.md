Burra Swimming Pool

On 28 November a decision was made to make an all-out effort to install a filtration plant in the pool at a cost of c. £4,000, to be subsidised pound for pound by the SA Government Tourist Bureau.  Admission charges for casual swimming will rise to 2/- (1/- for juniors).  Members’ Season Tickets £2-2-0 (over 14 years) and £1-1-0 (juniors).
