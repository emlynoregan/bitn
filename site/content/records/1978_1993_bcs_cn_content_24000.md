Burra Swimming Pool

Commonwealth Funds of $100,000 have been secured from the Community Cultural Recreation and Sporting Facilities Program to assist with the funding of the new Community Swimming Pool Complex in Burra.

The design will place the new pool on Education Department land adjacent to BCS and utilises the existing school canteen and gymnasium change room facilities.

Burra Burra DC on 27 April resolved that the Burra District Swimming Pool Management Committee should:

1.  Investigate all pool filtration systems and their associated costs.

2.  Investigate pool heating options and costs.

3.  Provide a draft conception plan.

4.  Report on the above to Council and

5. Recommend further action to Council

Council, the Swimming Pool Committee and BCS have made a submission calling for:

1.  The transfer of the pool site to Burra Burra DC as a gift.

2.  That the transfer costs be a gift by the Education department to the Council.

3.  That the Education department fund the pool during the 20 weeks of BCS use.

4.  That the Education Department enter into an agreement for the use of the canteen and change room facilities attached to the gymnasium.

5. That the education Department fund any of the costs of altering the BCS building deemed necessary to facilitate the project.

6.  That the Education Department make a contribution by way of a grant towards capital costs of the new pool project.
