The Savings Bank of SA, which opened in Adelaide on 11 March 1848, merged with the State Bank on 1 July 1984 to become the State Bank of SA.  It will have a network of 199 branches and be the biggest banking organisation in SA.  The Burra Branch of the Savings Bank of SA opened on 27 February 1912.
