Burra & District Floral Art Club held its AGM on 5 February and elected: President, Mrs B. Thamm; Vice-President, Mrs Joyce Lloyd and Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs D. Mengersen.  The club had 13 members.  Lavender bags were sent to the Adelaide National Flower Day.  Several pedestals were bought by the Burra Burra DC to be used for arrangements in the Town Hall.
