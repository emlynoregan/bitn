Accident.  At about 9.50 p.m. on Saturday a small sedan overturned at Mt Bryan.  The car was driven by Miss Susan Jackson of Cawker’s Well Station, Broken Hill, who was travelling to Adelaide.  The driver suffered minor injuries and a passenger, Robyn Jackson, was not injured, but a second passenger, Howard Rolfe of Hove, sustained a fractured cheek bone, concussion and bruises.  The car was extensively damaged.
