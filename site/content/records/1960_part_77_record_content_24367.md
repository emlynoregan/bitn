Burra Town Council

The amount of £139 plus £7 for the jinker shed to be paid as soon as accounts arrive so that the subsidy can be returned from the Tourist Bureau.

It was resolved that the north end of the shed be enclosed to house mine relics and items appertaining to the jinker.

L.W. Gare to be sold 1 chain x 12 chains of the north side of the Mine Paddock for £10 plus the transfer costs and with him to shift the trough to a suitable site.  [This needs clarification.]
