Burra High School Parents & Friends’ Assoc. welcomed new staff to the school on 18 February.  Prefects for the year have been elected: Brian Simpson (Head), Graham Brook (Deputy), Darrell Field, Garry Disher, Geoffrey Collins, Trevor Willis, Jan Seekamp (Head), Jill Przibilla (Deputy), Vicki Standing, Susan Murphy, Lynette Winders and Naomi Dare.
