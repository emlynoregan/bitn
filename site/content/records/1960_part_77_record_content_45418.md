St Mary’s.  Rt Rev. George Quarterman, Bishop of the Diocese of North-West Texas will be guest preacher on 5 May.  He is on a ten day visit to the Diocese of Willochra and will dedicate a new chapel at the Anglican Conference Centre at Melrose.  It has been paid for by the members of the church in Texas and children of his diocese have raised money for the chapel’s furnishings.  An organ recital by Rev. Geoffrey Sibly will precede the service.
