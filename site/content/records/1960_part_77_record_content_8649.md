Garfield Sobers will visit Burra and conduct a cricket school at the Burra Racecourse Oval on Monday 13 November from 10.30 a.m. to 12.30 p.m.  It is open to all boys over 10.  Mr Vic. Richardson will be a member of the party.  All are welcome to come and observe.
