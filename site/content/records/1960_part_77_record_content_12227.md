Advt.  Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. will auction for Messrs E.L. & L.M. Heinrich their 7-roomed double fronted stone dwelling with two kitchens, two bathrooms etc.  Readily convertible into two flats. Large u.g. room with 12,000 gallon u.g. tank and 600 gallon o.h. tank and outbuildings etc.  90 ft frontage to Morehead Street and depth 330 ft on Anne and Grainger Streets.  Allotments 62, 63, 64, 65 & 66 Burra North.
