Out and About by ‘Mrs Maggs’ (Mary Woollacott) was an important and very useful column for local births, birthdays, deaths and other events.  This is a valuable help for local historian, even if the obituaries etc. are exceeding brief.  This varied in location, but was often on page 7 and varied generally from one to two column
