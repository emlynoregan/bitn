The Burra-Adelaide Road Race was held on Saturday 14 August with Mr L. Wells, President of the League of SA Wheelmen sending off riders at 10 a.m.  Riders first went to Mt Bryan before returning and setting off for Adelaide.  The winner was M. Turner on a handicap of 25 minutes, in a time of 4 hours.
