The Burra Wool Pavilion

The cement floor has been laid and finished by Mr ‘Jimmy’ James.  Mr R. Fuss has since carried on fitting iron on walls and gable ends and installed most of the glass in the roof light.  He is now working on the interior fittings and preparing the doors.
