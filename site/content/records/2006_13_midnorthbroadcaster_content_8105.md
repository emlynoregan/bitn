The Bush to Burra Cattle Drive will be sponsored by AGL Energy again this year.  AGL is developing the Hallett complex of wind farms.  The drive will run from Thursday 1 October to Monday 5 October.  The 2008 event raised more than $21,000 for a range of health services throughout the region.  [2008 photo]
