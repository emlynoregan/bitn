Burra Mine

Work has resumed after the Christmas shut-down.  The aim is to start production on 31 January.  The general administration block and amenities section has been completed.  The plant workshop, stores and the large mine workshop are nearing completion.  The Boiler house and process plant are well advanced.  The primary and secondary crushers have been installed.  The ammonium storage facility will be filled this week.  Production is well underway in the quarry area.  Samin Ltd staff have arrived in the town and taken up residence.
