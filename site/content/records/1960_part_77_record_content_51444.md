State Election last Saturday

The count for Frome is incomplete with 6,926 votes counted out of 8,296.  Mr Allen LCL has a lead of 319 over Mr Casanova of the ALP.  Overall at this stage the ALP has taken 26 seats and the LCL 18 with Chaffey, Frome & Hanson still in doubt.  Frome totals at this stage of the count:

Allen	3440

Casanova	3121

McMahon	258

Informal	107
