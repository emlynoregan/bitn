Burra Bowling Club held its AGM last Wednesday in the club rooms.  The family of the late George Terry donated £25.  Elected: Patron, G.H. Dollman; President, J.B. Topsfield; Vice-Presidents, C.L. Phillips & M.G. Stockman; Secretary, D.H. Field & Treasurer, B.A. Riggs.  Full membership at £6-10-0 remains unchanged.  Night members pay £3-10-0.
