The 12th Wool Appraisement of the 1943-44 season was completed in Adelaide on 22 May and the Sandland Estate ‘Balah’ received the highest price of 20¼d for 2 bales of AAA.

Other good prices went to G. Tennant (Redcliffe) 	4AA	18¼d

Oakbank Ltd 	1AAA E	18d

G.M. Howard (Morgan Vale) 	3AAA W	17¾d

Gallagher Bros. (Quondong)	1AAA W	17d

G. Warnes (Glen Orros)	1Sup. AAA W	18d

Oakbank Ltd	43 Flc	18d

I.J. Warnes & Sons Ltd	Flc top	17¼d
