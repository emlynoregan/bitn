Burra LCL Women’s Branch held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs R.K. Pearse; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames J.T. Drew & W. Miller; Secretary, Mrs R. Collins; Assistant Secretary, Mrs J. Collins and Treasurer, Mrs R. Dunn.  The guest speaker was Sir Thomas Playford.  Sir Thomas spoke of the difficulties facing the LCL at this time:

Competition in overseas markets for primary products at a time of rising production costs.

The redistribution of electoral boundaries

Previously the country has 26 seats and the city 13

Now the country has 19 seats and the city 28.
