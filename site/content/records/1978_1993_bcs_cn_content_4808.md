Burra Pony Club staged a 20 mile Ride-a-Thon on Sunday to raise funds for the RFDS and Riding for the Disabled.  17 members took part and District Council Chairman Ray Jennison cut the ribbon to see them off in what could have been his last official act as Chairman before retiring.  [Photo.]
