Obituary.  Mr Albert Stephen Fahey died at Jamestown Hospital 28 April aged 56.  He was the second son of the late Stephen & May Fahey.  In 1936 he married Ida Przibilla of Bundaleer.  The couple became well known in the hotel business at Robertstown, Caltowie, Spalding, Adelaide, Peterborough and Gladstone.  He was residing at Spalding at the time of his death.  He is survived by a widow, two daughters and one son: Patricia Mrs Joyce of Cleve, Vivienne Mrs Page of Millicent and Gregory of Spalding.  [Born 10 March 1909 Spalding.]
