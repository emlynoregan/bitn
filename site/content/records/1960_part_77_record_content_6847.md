Burra Small Bore Club.  On Saturday a team from East Marden (Adelaide) competed against a Mid North Team at Morgan.  Teams were of ten shooters with the best 8 scorers to count.  East Marden 2204 defeated Mid North 2187. Burra shooters were the second best of the country clubs.  (Morgan 545 & Burra 540.)
