Burra Pistol Club.  Shoots were held on Wednesday and Saturday.

Kamere Trophy winner [check spelling of Kamere: it seems to change over time.] was A. Pearce.

Most improved lady was J. Ford and most improved gentleman was Jeff. Allen.

Best scorers were A. Pearce, N. Kingsley, M. Stolte & R. Barker.

For Sunday the Kamere Trophy winner was Andrew Pearce.  Most improved lady was V. Oakley and most improved gentleman was F. Ford.  Best scorers were A. Pearce, M. Stolte, J. Ford and M. Hoskins.
