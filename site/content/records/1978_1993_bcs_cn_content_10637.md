Burra Burra National Trust Branch: 1985 Objectives

Further restoration work on the right hand wing of the Redruth Gaol.

$12,000 grant will see three years restoration at the Police Lock-up and Stables.

Malowen Lowarth – interior display to be set up by Colin Fidock and helpers & garden to be laid out.

Advise Council on its plans to shift the stampers from Mongolata.

Collect a photo of every building in Burra with information and publish same in 1986-88.

Encourage Council to purchase Hampton and then aid them in site interpretation.

Kotz Cottage – clean area and cottage; make it safe and add it to the key hire scheme.

Old Jinker – erect a mesh fence – place the jinker on blocks – paint or shingle the roof.

Continue interpretive work at the Market Square Museum.

Work with the steering committee on the Copper Interpretation Centre.

Application made for an LGA Grant to begin erecting signs at Burra’s Historic Sites.
