The following farmers were fined for carrying excess weight of wheat to Burra.  The fine was 1/- per excess cwt.

H. Nourse	+ 21 cwt

G. Pryde		+ 8 cwt

A. Forrest	+ 4 cwt

W. Stockman	+ 16 cwt

R.H. Finch	+ 6 cwt

J. Nickles	+ 3 cwt
