Obituary.  Alan George Dare died 26 January at his residence at St Peters aged 73.  He was the husband of Mary Josephine and father of Eileen, Dorothy, Joan, Phyllis, George, Rita, Allen (dec.), Kathleen & Doreen.  There are 17 grandchildren.
