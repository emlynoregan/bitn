Cricket. A meeting was held at the Commercial Hotel on Saturday to establish a Kooringa 
Cricket Club. There were about 12 present. J. Snell proposed and H. Steele 2nd the proposal. 
Officers elected: Captain, T. Shortridge; Vice-Captain, J. Snell; Sec. & Treasurer, James 
Walker.
