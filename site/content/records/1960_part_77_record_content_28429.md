Burra Pre-School Kindergarten held its AGM on 7 March.  Attendance was a disappointing 17, considering that enrolment this year was over 50.  Elected: President, Mrs G. Brereton; Vice-President, Mrs B. Stockman; Secretaries, Mesdames Frank Fennell & John Nankivell and Treasurer, Mrs Guy Dollman Jnr. The main object this year will be to complete the building project presently under way.
