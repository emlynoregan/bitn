The Burra Burra Branch of the National Trust and BRTBA are holding a Craft Fair and Fire Engine Rally at the old Redruth Gaol in October.  Sites are available for $20 for the two days with trestle available for an extra $10 if required.  Sites are 3 m x 2 m and are undercover and secure at night.
