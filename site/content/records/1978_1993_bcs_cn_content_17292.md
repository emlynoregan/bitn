Registrations of interest are called for the painting of the exterior of the Council Offices and the Civic Centre.

Registrations of interest are called for the position of the Office Manager of the Burra Tourist Office under the jurisdiction of the Burra Tourist Association.

The Burra Tourist association was formed at a meeting on 5 September 1988.

Jenny & Denny O’Connor trading as O’Connor Tours have decided after seven years they will not renew their lease and the Burra Tourist Association will now be the new lessee [of the Tourist Office].
