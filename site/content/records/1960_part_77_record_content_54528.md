Obituary.  Arthur P. [Patrick] Keain died at Aquinas College 17 December 1970 aged 35 [residence Somerton Park].  He was the eldest son of Mr & Mrs B.V. Keain of Spalding.  He was born at Blyth and educated at St Joseph’s Convent Clare, Euromina School, Clare High School and Sacred Heart College.  He graduated with a BA (Hons) from the University of Adelaide and was a teacher at Woodville and Renmark High Schools before taking an administrative post at Adelaide University and then becoming Academic Registrar at Flinders University.  He was a keen bridge player and the Australian Open Individual Bridge Champion for 1964.
