Christmas Eve in Burra will see shops open till 9 p.m.  Father Christmas will arrive at 9.10 p.m. to distribute free ice creams and sweets.  A merry-go-round will operate in the day and evening.  Shop will be closed on Saturday 26 December and on Saturday 2 January.

Carols by Candlelight was held on Sunday 13 December and despite the rather cold conditions about £60 was raised.  Compere for the evening was Mr Alec Macaskell of Radio 5AD and guest artist was Mr Eric Smart, tenor.  The Pre-School Kindergarten presented a Nativity Scene and the Mayor delivered a Christmas message.

The Burra RSL held its Annual Reunion Dinner on Saturday night with State President Mr Eric Smith as the guest speaker.  The excellent buffet dinner was provided by the Ladies’ Auxiliary.
