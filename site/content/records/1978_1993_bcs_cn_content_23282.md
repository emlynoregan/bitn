Thefts

1.  Equipment worth $2,180 was stolen from a transport truck at the Burra Sale Yards on 24/25 October.

2.  Australian National Railways reported the further theft of 2.2 km of copper wire worth c. $2,600 from about 10 km south of Burra.  (Reported on 28 October.)

3.  Shearing combs worth c. $420 were stolen from Elders at Burra.
