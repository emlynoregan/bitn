BCS students competed at the recent interschool athletic championships at Kensington in the F Grade.  A number of BCS students gained 1st places:

Tina Williams, U16 girls’ triple jump, 100 m and 200 m.

Samantha Kelly, open girls’ long jump.

Kim Lindblom, U15 girls’ triple jump.

Karen Phillips, U15 girls’ 800 m.

Boh Wall, U15 boys, discus and 1,500 m.

Ben Dolphin, U16 boys’ javelin.

Adam Willis, U15 boys’ long jump and triple jump.

Jason Schuppan, open boys’ 400 m.

Luke Spackman, U15 boys’ 400 m.

Simon Breeding open boys’ 1,500 m walk.
