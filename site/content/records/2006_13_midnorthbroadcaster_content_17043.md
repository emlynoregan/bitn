Burra Regional Art Gallery & Pygmy Bluetongue Conservation Association have combined to promote an exhibition at the gallery displaying works that feature the unique lizard.  The ‘Little Lizard Dreaming Exhibition’ will open at the gallery on Sunday at 2 p.m.
