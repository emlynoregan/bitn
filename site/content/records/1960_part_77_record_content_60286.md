Football.  Representatives of the Mid North and North Eastern leagues met on Friday last at the Railway Hotel, Saddleworth, to discuss their proposed amalgamation.  Mr Stan Noske, President of the Mid North League and Mr M. Vandeleur, President of the North Eastern League, held wide ranging discussions.  It was announced that the North Eastern League would have similar discussions with the Broughton League.
