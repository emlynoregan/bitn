Burra Swimming Pool had an operating probity for the last season with an income of $2,071.41 and costs of $1,474.35 giving a profit of $597.06.  Loan repayments and interest charges, however, amounted to $656.72, leaving a deficit of $59.66.  The balance of the outstanding loan is at present $3,548.58.
