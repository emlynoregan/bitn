Goyder Council honoured two retiring Councillors at a special presentation at Burra Hotel on 20 April.  Dennis Keller served on the Robertstown DC 1977-97 before becoming the first Chairman of the Regional Council of Goyder, 1997-2004.   Graham Kellock served on Burra Burra DC 1985-97 before becoming Deputy Chairman of the Regional Council of Goyder 1997-2004.  [Photo]
