Advt.	NOTICE

In consequence of the rise in the price of cattle, the price of Meat, until further notice, will be as follows.

Roasting Beef	6d to 7d	per lb

Boiling do. 	5d	”

Corned do.	5d to 6d	”

Steak	7d	”

Fore Quarter Mutton	3d	”

Hind	do.	do.	4d	”

Legs		do.	4d	”

Sides		do.	3½d	”

Neck		do.	3½d	”

Sausages		6d	”

Chops			4½d	”

Pork			7d	”

R. AUSTIN

D. & J. O'LEARY

C. OHLMEYER

LIHOU & BROMLEY

W. SYMONS

C. & E. GEBHARDT
