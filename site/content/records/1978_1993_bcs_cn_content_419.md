Teachers on the Move

Tony Earle of Booborowie soon leaves for Mitcham.

Richard, Lee and Sarah Lockheed have moved to another house in Burra in Church Street.

The house they have vacated will be occupied by Geoff Schulze, his wife and two children.

Phillip Baulderstone has a position with the Bank of Adelaide.

Annette Broad has been accepted by Salisbury CAE.

Dennis Thamm is as apprentice in metallurgy with Bradford-Kendall.

Rev. David Thiem, his wife Annette and Baby Rebecca have been welcomed to the Uniting Church in Burra.
