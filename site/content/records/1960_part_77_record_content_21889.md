Local Government Essay Competition 1964

‘What public services do Councils provide?’

The 7th Grade students at Burra School provided 27 entries and 1st prize of £3-3-0 went to Daphne Launer and prizes of £1-1-0 each went to Kym Bannister and Brenda Willmott.
