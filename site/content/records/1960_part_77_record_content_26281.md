Accident/Obituary.  Last Tuesday at about 5.30 p.m. Virginia May Quinn aged 4 and Adrian Peter Quinn aged 2, children of Mr & Mrs J.V. Quinn of Mt Bryan were drowned in a dam on the property.  They had been playing on a pony near the woolshed and on being missed a search found them floating in the dam.  Artificial respiration was ineffective.
