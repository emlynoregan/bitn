Booborowie Basketball Club took $292 at a Hawaiian Luau.  Maurice Collins was MC and Mrs Allen led a Hawaiian ‘Conga’ Dance.  Miss Bernadette Broad of Booborowie was chosen Hawaiian Queen and Miss Louise Catt from the Primary School age group was chosen Hawaiian Princess.
