Burra Hospital received a visit recently from the newly created Wakefield Regional Health Service’s Chairman Mr James Maitland.  He said the process of regionalisation was going ahead although the new Health Services Bill was yet to pass through parliament, because the process is allowable under the Health Commission Act 1976.
