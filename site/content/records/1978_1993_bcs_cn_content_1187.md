Burra Burra DC.  A general meeting of the electors is called for 26 September for a general discussion concerning Council and Community affairs.

Burra Burra DC intends to close the following roads and sell them to the adjoining landowners:

Pt Murray St Mt Bryan Township east of the railway line.

Roads between sections 61, 62 & 66, 98 Hundred of Kingston.

Roads between sections 115,553,552 & 554, 555, 560, 561 Pt 562 Hundred of Ayers.

Road between sections 554 & 555 Hundred of Ayers.

Road between sections 244, 245 & 243 Hundred of Kingston.

Pt South Terrace, Burra between Chapel Street and Mitchell Flat.
