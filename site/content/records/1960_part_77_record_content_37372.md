A Pet Show was held at Burra North at the Redruth Memorial Hall on Saturday.  There was also a Doll Show and Yo-Yo Competition and a Lucky-Dip.  For the adults there was a White Elephant Stall and stalls selling strawberries, cake, ice cream and cold drinks.  Members of the Burra & District Choral Society staged a concert.  Proceeds were $305, which will be divided among various charities.  [Prize winners are listed.]
