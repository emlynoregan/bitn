Burra Regional Art Gallery

The first exhibition for the year opens on 11 February and is titled ‘Anlaby Works’.  It features paintings by Kate Jenkins and furniture by Peter Hart.  Other displays for the year will feature the works of regional artists in the Burra to Barossa exhibition and local jeweller Sue Barlow’s works.
