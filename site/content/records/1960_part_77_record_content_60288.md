Copper Festival

The Tourist Promotion Committee of Burra & District has plans for a Copper Festival on the Holiday Weekend 23 March.  [Actually 7-9 October.]  On Saturday there will be the Agricultural Show with a Ball at night.

On Monday there will be a gala Day at the oval.  Rural Youth will be asked to organise the Ball.  Mr Len Bence will be asked to organise an art show in the RSL Hall.  Sgt Haynes will be asked about the possibility of the Police Band attending and Samin Ltd will be asked to permit Mine Tours.  The break-down of the tourist dollar is estimated as:
