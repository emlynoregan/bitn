Mt Bryan Agricultural Bureau held their Annual Inspection of Pastures on 27 October, which this year was entitled Farm Field Day and Pasture Inspection.  Mr Bruce Hall of the Department of Agriculture in Jamestown attended.  Properties inspected were those of Mr McInnes and Mr Dave Thomas.
