Obituary.  May Elizabeth Murray died 30 October at Burra Hospital aged 81.  She was the wife of Donald James Murray and the mother of Irene (deceased) and Madge.  She is survived by 7 grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren.

[Born Mary Elizabeth Morris 24 July 1884 Adelaide.  Died as May Elizabeth Murray 30 October 1966 Burra, residence Burra North.  Married in 1907 and had children as Mary Elizabeth.]
