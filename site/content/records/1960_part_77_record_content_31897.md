Accident.  A sedan driven by Brian Kakoschke of Burra North overturned about ¾ mile south of Hallett at about 4.15 p.m. on Saturday.  The driver and passengers, Trevor Menze and Ian Jones, also of Burra North, all escaped with minor injuries, but the vehicle was extensively damaged.
