Notice.  Burra Burra District Council – Declaration of the Rate

General Rate 1½d in the pound.

A differential rate of 1/- in the pound has been declared for the townships of Booborowie, Mt Bryan, Hanson and Farrell Flat.  Minimum rate payment of 5/-.
