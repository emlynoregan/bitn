Burra Racing Club AGM was held on Friday at the Burra Institute.  Mr A.B. Hiddle was in the chair.  It was resolved to apply for 21 March for a meeting.  There is a substantial credit balance in hand.  W.H. Hardy was elected Secretary in place of W.T. Truscott who retired due to failing eyesight.  Patron, Hon. J. Lewis MLC; President, Mr J. Tennant; Treasurer, W.T. Truscott.  Stakes of Â£80 were arranged.
