Burra Uniting Church minister Frank Tuppin and his wife Helen are moving to Red Cliffs, Victoria soon, where he will also be chaplain at Red Cliffs Secondary College.  Two and a half years ago they came to Burra from Murrayville, Victoria.  Frank has been involved with the Burra, Farrell Flat and Mt Bryan Uniting Churches, as well as the lay ministering teams that cover Spalding, Koolinga, Snowtown, Watervale and Gulnare/Yacka and has been a CFS volunteer and local football umpire.
