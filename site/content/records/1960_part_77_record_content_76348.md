Burra Burra DC, 11 August

Crs Jennison & Phillips moved that the Council advise adjoining owners it intends to declare Mary Crescent a public road.  Carried.

Hanson Soldiers’ Memorial Hall Committee accepts the lease on the parklands at Hanson for the next three years.

The Fire Brigades Board offers the old Diamond T to the Council.

Road Closures:

The road dividing Block 13A & pt Block 13 of GRO Plan 20 of 1905 from Pt Section 1 Hd Kooringa is to be closed and the land vested in the Minister of Education.  [This road ran along the front of the school.]
