The Bon Accord Hotel took advantage of the Liquor Licensing Board allowing all night trading in conjunction with the Clipsal 500.  ‘The Bon’ had customers all night for both nights of the weekend.  The previous weekend ‘The Bon’ had a fire truck party and raised $175 for the local CFS.
