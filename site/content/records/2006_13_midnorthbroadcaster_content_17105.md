Shane Crawford, former AFL star, who is riding a bike across Australia to raise funds and awareness for Breast Cancer, arrived in Burra with his support crew on Monday.  He is riding an average of 170 km a day.  Local school children and the BBH Football Club hosted a meet and greet at the Burra Sports Complex.  [Photo.]
