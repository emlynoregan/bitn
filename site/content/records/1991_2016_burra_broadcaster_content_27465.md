SA Stud Merino Field Days have received renewed interest after trying times in recent years.  This year saw more visitors and good sales.  Greenfields Stud took the prize for Merino Capital Champion Ram for the second year running.  Rayner’s ‘The Bluff’ from Hallett took second place.  [Photos on pages 1 & 4.]
