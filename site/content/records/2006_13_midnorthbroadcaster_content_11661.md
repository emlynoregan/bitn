Goyder Council will hold seven of its 12 ordinary meetings for the year in the local wards.  These will take the form of Community Council meetings at which there will be a half-hour question time where Council will answer question that have been submitted from the community in time for inclusion on the agenda.  Three of these meeting will be in Burra, two in Eudunda and one each in Robertstown and Hallett.
