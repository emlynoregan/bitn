Tanya Waters’ work ‘Lochwinik’ is the winning entry for Best in Exhibition acquisitive prize of $5,000 sponsored by Light Regional Council.  This was announced at the Inaugural Rotary Club of Kapunda Kidman Art Show at the Soldiers’ Memorial Hall Kapunda.  There were over 300 entries.  Glenys Christopher of Burra was the winner in the watercolour section with her work ‘The Canola House’.
