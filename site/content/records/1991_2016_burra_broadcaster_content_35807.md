Burra Regional Art Gallery.  From 11 February two notable artists will have exhibits:

Colin Frith will explore through painting the ever changing surface of water in an exhibition called Veil.

Len Bence will exhibit 20-30 watercolours and oils of still lifes and scenes from Burra and district and together with his work will be sketches from 17 of his recent students.
