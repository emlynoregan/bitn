Burra Speak Easy Group was launched on Saturday 31 May.  It was co-ordinated by Gail Edwards and was held at Shared Arts on Chapel Street.  Guest speaker was Beryl Price, National President of the Penguin Club.  Speak Easy is a project of the Penguin Club of SA and is aimed at giving women the opportunity to share and communicate through regular meetings within a supportive structure.  The Burra Penguin Club has been meeting for nearly fifty years.  [Photo.]
