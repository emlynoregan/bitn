The Regional Festival of Arts event in Burra for 2000 will be called ‘Plenty’ and will feature a Brazilian Puppet Theatre, Slack Taxi Puppets and a Romanian Gypsy Band, as well as local performers, parades, food, etc.  It is scheduled for 12 March following the Twilight Jazz Affair on the 11 March.
