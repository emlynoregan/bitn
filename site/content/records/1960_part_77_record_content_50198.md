Obituary.  Mr Dougald Phillips died in Perth WA last Tuesday aged 76.  He lived in Booborowie district some years ago.  His family had taken the property ‘Eringunda’ when the land was subdivided early this century.  His mother was Katie Phillips.  He is survived by a widow, a daughter, Judith (WA) and son Peter (Willalo).

[Thomas Dugald Phillips born 26 May 1895 Wirrialpa Station.  His mother was Catherine Phillips nee McTaggart.  The son, Thomas David Peter Phillips was born 9 October 1926 Kooringa and his sister Judith was born there two years previously.  His wife was Marjorie Millicent nee Melville.]
