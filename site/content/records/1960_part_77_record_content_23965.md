Aberdeen Football Club at its AGM last Tuesday elected: Patron, L.W. Gare; President, B. Hirschausen; Senior Vice-President, R.M. Reed; Secretary, J.J. Richards and Assistant Secretary, R. Heinrich.

Burra Football Club has elected: Patron, Rex Warnes; President, Mr E. Pietsch; Vice-President, A. Baulderstone; Secretary, J. Herde and Treasurer, R. Pietsch.

Hallett Football Club elected President, Don Gare; Patron, A. L. Collins, Vice-President, K.G. Deer, secretary, Jim Sullivan, Assistant Secretary, R. Dawson and Treasurer, Laurie Sullivan.
