The Sir Hubert Wilkins Memorial Committee is holding a display of letters written by Sir Hubert to his sister Anne Cockshell.  The letters provide an insight into his views on such things as the war with Japan, his various expeditions and war journalism.  The ‘Letters to Home’ are part of History Month efforts at Hallett Town Hall from 11a.m. to 3 p.m. each Sunday in May.  Gold coin donation.
