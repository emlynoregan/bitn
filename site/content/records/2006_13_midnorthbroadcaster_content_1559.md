Bob & Idrienne Neill’s Wheetelande White Suffolk Stud has confirmed its high standing at the recent Adelaide Royal Show.  This year’s on property sale on 3 October will offer 100 White Suffolks.  Of particular interest this year will be the progeny of DG419 ($33,000), K0164 (Champion Melbourne 2004) and Wheetelande 2776, all having bred well.  [Photo.]
