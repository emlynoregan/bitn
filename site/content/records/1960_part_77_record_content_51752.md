Accident.  Gerald R. Pens (20) of Burra was seriously injured when the vehicle he was driving went over the bridge at Ford’s Corner on Friday night. The car landed upright twenty feet below.  Mr Pens was not found till 8.15 a.m. by Mr Colin Earle.  Dr L.G. Miller of Burra attended and Mr Pens has been transferred to the Royal Adelaide Hospital.
