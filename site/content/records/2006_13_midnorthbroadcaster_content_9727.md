Weather.  Rain and hail over the weekend gave a feel of winter to the district.  Falls were patchy and ranged from 2.8 mm at Point Pass to 86.6 mm at Manoora where local flooding caused some minor damage.  Many falls were in the 10 to 20 mm range with 17 at Burra Community School.  Black Springs with 50 mm was another wet spot.
