Aberdeen Tennis Club’s Variety Concert at the Town Hall on 25 March drew a capacity crowd and takings were £92.

Program:

High School Choir	Song of Australia and several classics

Mrs A. Barrett	Bellringing: The Bells of St Mary’s

Mrs Lindsay Thomas	Piano: from Rigoletto

Mrs M. Satchell	Songs:	Wouldn’t it be Luvelly

I Could Have Danced All Night

Miss Diedre Snell	Recitation: The Motor Car Boy and an encore

Mrs J. Carpenter	Song: All Packed Up In My Little Bottom Drawer

Marylin Hirschausen, Valerie Griffiths

And Angela Ryan	Piano Trio

Darrel Field	Saxophone Solo: Don’t Be Cross

Saxophone Solo: I’ll Take You Home Again Kathleen

Miss Carmel Fullgrabe	Song & Dance: Are My Ears on Straight?

Mr Lindsay Thomas	Song: The Company Sergeant Major

Miss Judy Field	Piano Solo

Miss Patricia Ryan	Piano Solo

Mrs R. Crewes	Song: O My Beloved Father

Song: The Merry Widow Waltz

Sandra Williams, Dianne Williams

And Dianne Mitchell	Ballet: Tip Toe Through the Tulips

Mrs R. Affolter	Song: A Little Bit of Love

Miss Christine Fullgrabe 	Song & Dance: Pretty Baby

Mrs G. Baulderstone	Recitation: Oo! Er!

Bill Crush & Roger Humphrys	Sketch: Physical Jerks

Mrs R. Angel	Song: I Could Have Danced All Night

Miss Coralie Fullgrabe	Dancer to chorus:

and chorus of Messrs T. Forby, T. Welsh,

D. Dawkins & J. Brady	I Do Like To Be Beside The Seaside

Redruth Sunday School Children	Item: Little White Duck
