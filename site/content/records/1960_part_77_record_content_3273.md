Burra Small Bore Rifle Club.  Presentation Night was held in the RSL Hall last Saturday.  Mrs Tom Ryan on the piano, Mr J. Brown on drums (relieved by Mr Dean Bernhardt) provided the music for dancing.

Mr R.G. Bernhardt made the presentations.  Trophies were won by Mr & Mrs W. Barker, Mr & Mrs John Taylor, Alan Day, Robin Eberhard, Jim Brown and Gordon Webster.
