Photo of two at St Laurence O’Toole Catholic Church on Sunday, for its final service, with no indication on page 11 of where it was, but on page 12 there is a photo of the last congregation and a history of this Eudunda church.

Father Quinn of Burra was assisted by Fr Shanahan from Kapunda for the closing service.

The first Catholic Church in Eudunda was established in 1886 and the present one has been the centre for the catholic community and worship in Eudunda for 49 years.

Father Quinn has celebrated Mass there twice a month for the last five years.

The first St Laurence O’Toole Church was built in 1886, next to the present building.

Fr Thomas Lee was the first priest.

The last marriage in the old church was of Margaret Ziersch & Ralph Harrison in 1956.

The Last baptism in the old church was of Kathleen Radloff in 1957.

The last Mass in the old church was held on 12 January 1958.

The church was then demolished.

The foundation stone of the new church was laid and blessed by His Grace Dr M. Beovich, Archbishop of Adelaide on 14 July 1958.  [Sic: but given following dates for events, presumably 1957.]

The new church was blessed and opened by the Auxiliary Bishop of Adelaide Rev. Dr J.W. Gleeson on 19 January 1958.

The first baptism in the new church was 19 January – Julie-Anne Harrison.

The first marriage in the new church was that of Margaret Hines and Brian White, 22 October 1960.

The last baptism in the church was William Mark Schutz on 19 February 2006.

The priests have been Fathers Honner, Griffith, George and Daly from the Riverton Parish; Fathers Moore, Sheedy, Papps, Vinn Tran and Shanahan from the Kapunda Parish and Fr Quinn from the Burra Parish.
