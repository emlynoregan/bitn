Weather.  Patchy rain in the week has aided crops.  Burra received 82 points.  District falls were typically 66 to 123 points, but falls to the east were much more variable: Kia Ora 94, Balah 26, Woolgangi 88, Braemar 130, Baldina 40 and The Gums 58.
