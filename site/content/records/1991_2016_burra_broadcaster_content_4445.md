Koomooloo Field Day

35 suppliers displayed their wares last Thursday, covering animal health, water supply, alternative solar power, radio-controlled monitoring, personal health, generators, fuel, motorbikes and pest control.  Peter Waters of Burra demonstrated meat cutting.  [2 Photos.]
