Barloo Wildlife Park at the south end of the town of Manoora has been opened to the public.  It is a private concern and the animals displayed include Rufus Rat Kangaroos, Wombats, Red and Grey Kangaroos, Wallabies, Emus, Brush Tail Possums from Tasmania, SA and Queensland and Guinea Pigs.
