Burra Methodist Church is organising a second camp at Nunyara Youth Centre at Belair over the coming weekend.  60 will attend comprising 24 adults, 15 children and 21 children.  35 will attend from Burra, 11 from Booborowie, 12 from Farrell Flat and 1 from Ironmine.
