Phoenix Copper continues to explore for mineral deposits in the district.  They have taken 12,187 samples as well as 1,050 soil and 466 rock samples from areas around the Monster Mine and at the Mongolata Gold Mine.  They have encouraging readings from south of the Monster Mine.  Phoenix has four principal project areas in SA.  [Map printed.]

Vandalism.  More than $500 damage to pop-up sprinklers on the Bridge Terrace Lawns was done on 13 December.

Accident.  A semi-trailer became detached and blocked Copperhouse Road [Street] briefly last Sunday near the stockyards.
