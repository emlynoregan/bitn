Kooringa Court, 25 April

John Jullien, Robert Roach, John Bowden and William Hampton were separately charged with having pigs at large. Each was fined 5/- plus £1 costs.

Richard Bosance was charged by Mr Coles the Crown Lands Ranger with allowing a large number of cattle to graze on waste lands without a licence.  Fined £3 with £1 costs.

William Thomas was charged with stealing firewood, but there not being a full Bench, entered into his own recognizance to appear when called upon.
