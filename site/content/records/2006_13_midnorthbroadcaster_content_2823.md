Jess Reid won the Adelaide Plains Talent Quest at the Adelaide Plains Cup Festival on 4 March.  She sang I Will Always Love You by Whitney Houston and as an encore after winning, Simply the Best by Tina Turner.  The prize was an international holiday to New Zealand to the value of $4,000.
