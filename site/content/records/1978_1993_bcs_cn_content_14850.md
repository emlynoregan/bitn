Obituary.  Mrs Ann Perry, wife of the former District Clerk, Mr Tom Perry, died recently.  She had been a teacher at Burra Primary School for many years and a Friend of the Burra Community Library.  She was largely responsible for the recently completed Burra Obituary Project.

[Born Annie Morrison, 11 August 1910 at Whitwarta: died June 1987 at Adelaide.]
