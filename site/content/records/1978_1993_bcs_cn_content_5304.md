Burra Kindergarten Opening

About 100 people attended the opening of the new kindergarten on Sunday afternoon.  Mr Graham Gunn, Member for Eyre, officially opened the building.  Those attending included:

Trevor Beneke, Immediate Past President of the Kindergarten Management Committee.

Mrs Peggy Shannon, formerly Peggy Rischbeith, Director of the Burra Kindergarten 1978-79.

Kindergarten Union representatives, Lance Hoar and Ian Stevenson.
