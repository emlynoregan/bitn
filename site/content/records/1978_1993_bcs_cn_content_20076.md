U20 & Open State Athletics Championships

Cheryl Day came 4th in the U20 100 m and 200 m events, but she won a gold medal as a member of the U16 4 x 400 m relay.

Adam Day gained a gold medal in the U20 400 m hurdles, a silver in the U20 110 m hurdles and finished 5th in the Open 400 m hurdles.
