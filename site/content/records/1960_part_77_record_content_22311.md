Remembrance Day was commemorated on 11 November at the Fallen Soldiers’ Memorial in Market Square.  Rev. Lind officiated at the service and wreaths were laid by representatives of the RSL, AFA, Demobilised Soldiers’ Association, the District & Town Councils and the Pensioners’ League.  [Rev. Lind’s address is printed.]
