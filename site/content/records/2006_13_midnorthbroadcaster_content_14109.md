Burra Branch of BankSA is 100

On 27 February the Burra branch of BankSA will celebrate its centenary.  On that date in 1912 the then Savings Bank of South Australia opened its office in the town.  The present Branch Manager is Lisa Brooks.  She was born in Burra and went to school here.  She now lives in Hallett.  She has had the position for nine and a half years.  The other member of staff is Sharon O’Connor.  Of the bank’s 118 branches in the state, 57 are in rural areas.
