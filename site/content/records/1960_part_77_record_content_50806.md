The Tennis Ball of the Burra & District Tennis Association was held at Booborowie on 24 April.  The profit will amount to c. $85.  Jim Quinn was MC and Miss Jan Drew of Leighton was chosen Miss Tennis for 1970.  Kevin Lane for Aberdeen White received the ‘A’ Grade Shield.  A. Thomas for Mt Bryan received the ‘B’ Grade Shield.  S. Lane for Aberdeen received the ‘C’ Grade Shield.  Mr & Mrs S. Scroop were congratulated and thanked on completing thirty years’ continuous service as joint secretaries of the Aberdeen Tennis Club.
