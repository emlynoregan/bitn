Burra Methodist Church celebrates the 60th anniversary of the present building.  A special 11 a.m. service will be held the weekend of the Copper Festival.  Guest Preacher will be the incoming President of the Conference, Rev. F.E. Tregilgas, who was a minister in Burra from 1949-51.
