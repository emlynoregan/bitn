Burra High School.  The Intermediate Class recently travelled to Clare to see a performance of Shakespeare’s Julius Caesar.  Mr L. Bence has donated £15 to the school and enabled it to purchase a selection of ten pictures.  Mr E. Zanker has presented an excellent painting of his to the headmaster.
