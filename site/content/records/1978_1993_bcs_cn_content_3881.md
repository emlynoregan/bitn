Community Aid Abroad will arrange a local part of The National Walk Against Want in Burra on 12 April.  The route is to be Burra to Farrell Flat.

So: get sponsors and walk as far as you can, alone, in a group, with a pet, with a baby in a pusher, but raise what you can.
