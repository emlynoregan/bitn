Burra Kindergarten

The newly completed extensions were opened on Friday by Mrs R.B. Pitcher, original President of the Pre Schools Association.  The associated Gala Afternoon raised $168.  Mrs Pitcher paid tribute to the Burra Branch of the Air Force Association.
