Phil Clode has been selected in two SA athletic teams in December.  On 8 & 9 December he represents SA State Secondary Schools in the Australian State Schools Open Age 400 m race at Olympic Park in Melbourne.  A week later he will attend the All Schools Track & Field Titles in Perth in the SA Team in the U19 4 x 400 m relay.
