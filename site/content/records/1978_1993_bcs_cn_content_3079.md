Burra Catholic Parish farewells Fr Shiel this week.  He has been relieving Fr Kenny who has been on leave travelling in Europe and Africa.  Fr Shiel has overseen the repainting of the interiors of the Booborowie and Mt Bryan Churches while here as well as renovations at St Joseph’s in Burra.
