BCS.  Interschool Swimming Carnival at Riverton

Burra won the Championship Shield and the Improvers Shield.

[Records broken are listed.]  Outstanding individual achievements:

Karen Jennings: six wins from six starts including five records.

Rohan Wenzel: six wins in six events.

Many Drew: three wins from three starts for three records.

Emma Butler: two wins and two records.

Especially notable were the Year 7 students who were probably the three youngest competitors in the carnival:

Kyron Statton: three wins from four starts including one record.

Rebecca Riggs: two wins from two starts.

Dianne Jones: two wins from two events.
