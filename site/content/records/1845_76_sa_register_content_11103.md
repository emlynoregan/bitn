Redruth Wesleyan Sunday School

The Anniversary services of the school were held on 27 September.  Sermons were preached by Mr William Moyle and Mr W. Orchard.  On Thursday 1 October upwards of 70 children marched around the streets of Redruth, accompanied by Rev. M. Wilson and the Kooringa Wesleyan Society Choir.  The usual tea and cake followed.
