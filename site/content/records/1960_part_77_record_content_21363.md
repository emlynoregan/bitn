Obituary.  Nellie Jane Woodgate aged 74 died 24 August at Jamestown Hospital.  She was the wife of the late Brassy Hamilton Woodgate, late of ‘Kentish Hills’ North Booborowie.  She was the mother of Marion N., Mrs K.W. White of Burra North.  [Born Nellie Jane Jones 9 July 1890 Quorn: died 24 August 1964 Jamestown, residence Booborowie North.  Her husband was Hamilton Ernest Brassey Joseph Woodgate.]
