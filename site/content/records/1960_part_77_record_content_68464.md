Advt.  Southern Farmers Land Auctions: Burra Town Hall 2 November

On instructions from P.J. Thomas & Co. Land one mile west of Burra comprising sections 2417, 418 & part 416 Hundred of Ayers.

On instructions from H.L. Phillips

Lot 1.  Land 16 miles from Burra on the main Burra-Eudunda Road.

[Paper of 16 October says this is 12 miles from Burra: Section 3 Hundred of Baldina.]

Lot 2.  Adjacent to the town boundary and known as The Mine Paddock.

[Paper of 16 October says this is Section 34 of the Hundred of Kooringa of 45 acres, but Section 34 Hd Kooringa is as Sod Hut.  The number might be correct as a subdivision of Section 1 Hd Kooringa.]
