January

Burra Swimming Pool re-opened after extensive maintenance.

Adchem contended with gas restrictions after the Moomba Gasfield fire.

Rev Frank Tuppin, his wife Helen and daughter Rebecca were welcomed.

Bob Greet was chosen as citizen of the year.

Community Event of the Year was the 25 Year Celebration of the Establishment of Burra Community School.
