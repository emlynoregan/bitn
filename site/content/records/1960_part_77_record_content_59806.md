Obituary.  Robert William Motherall, late 2nd Battalion 1st AIF, died at Clare Hospital 22 January aged 73.  He was the son of the late Mr & Mrs H.R. Motherall, husband of Dot. and father of Albert (deceased) and Norma Mrs Barker.  Grandfather of Robert and Dianne.  [Robert William Motherall born 6 September 1898 Kooringa.]
