Notice.  A public meeting is to be held in the Booborowie Memorial Hall on Monday 25 September.  It is sponsored by the United Farmers & Graziers’ Association, Booborowie Branch, to discuss the proposed increase of rates to 1.4c in the dollar and what action should be taken in response.  The Councillors and District Clerk have been invited to attend.
