Jeannie Kelly has been awarded Honorary Membership of the Burra Basketball Association.  Over the years she has been President for 2 years, Vice President for a year, Secretary for 5 years, Assistant Secretary for 3 years, Treasurer for 3 years, a Recorder for 2 years and has drawn up the program 8 times.  She has also been an Association selector, player and umpire.
