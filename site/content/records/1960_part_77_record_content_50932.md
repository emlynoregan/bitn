E.C. Allen MP writes expressing disappointment at SAR’s decision on train times.  He says feedback he got suggested the preferred option was a 5.25 p.m. departure Monday to Thursday and 6.35 p.m. Friday to Sunday.

Burra Methodist Sunday School held a picnic at Clare on Saturday.

Mr & Mrs Woollacott, Mary & Ruth leave for Melbourne on Friday, heading for Sydney and their overseas trip by P & O liner Iberia.  They are due back in mid-November.

Birth.  To Sue & Roger Dunn at QVMH 22 April, a son.

Birth.  To Dawn & Guy Dollman at Burra Hospital 25 April, a son.
