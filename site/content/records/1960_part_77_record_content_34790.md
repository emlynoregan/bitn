The Mokota Cricket Wind-up function was held at Booborowie on Thursday.  About 50 cricketers and supporters attended.  Mr W.B. Ashby, Patron of the Association, presented the Mokota Shield to First Division winners Booborowie.  It was received by David Drew.  Mr Alan Baulderstone received the Second Division Shield (Mayfield Shield) for the Burra Footballers, from the President Mr J. McMahon.  The Cricketer of the Year Award went to Rob Ashby of North Booborowie.
