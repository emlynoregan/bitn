Obituary.  Hedley (Ted) Lomman of Camden Park died aged 69.  He was born at Mt Bryan East, where he was a resident for 40 years.  [Born John Hedley Lomman 8 February 1899 Kooringa: died Hedley John Lomman 28 April 1968 Adelaide, residence Camden Park.]
