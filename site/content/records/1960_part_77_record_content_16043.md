National Trust

The Mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone has called a public meeting to consider the formation of a branch of the National Trust in Burra.  The guest speaker will be the President in SA Mr Morphett.  In the Mayor’s Parlour at the Town Hall on Friday 24 May at 8 p.m.
