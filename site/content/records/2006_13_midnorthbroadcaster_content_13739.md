Regional Council of Goyder held its eighth Strategic Planning Workshop in Burra last Tuesday.  About 40 community members attended.  Nine similar workshops will be held across the region in the first step in an extensive consultation process.  Some 300 responses were received to the Strategic Planning Surveys.
