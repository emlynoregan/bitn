The 4th Mid North Sheepdog Trials were held at Burra Showgrounds over the Saturday and Sunday of the Easter weekend as part of the Country Games, which ran from 30 March to 28 April.  There were 95 entrants.  The Mid North Sheepdog Training Club is based at Farrell Flat.
