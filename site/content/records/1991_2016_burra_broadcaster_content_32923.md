Obituary.  June Burdon nee Dollman, formerly of Burra, died in Sydney 30 December 2003.  She was the wife of Errol Burdon and sister to the late Guy Dollman.  She left Burra many years ago and since then had lived in Tasmania, overseas and in NSW for the last twenty years.  [Born June Dollman 15 June 1926 at Kooringa.]
