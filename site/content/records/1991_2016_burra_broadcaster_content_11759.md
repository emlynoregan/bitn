Burra Traders’ Association held their Christmas dinner meeting last week.  Guest speaker was Rob Olsen, executive officer of ‘Classic Country’, which promotes tourism in the Adelaide area and ‘Classic Country’ encompasses the Mid North and Yorke Peninsula.  85% of tourists come from SA and 78% from the Adelaide Metropolitan Area.
