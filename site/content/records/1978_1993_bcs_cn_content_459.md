BCS welcomes 11 new staff:

Mr Peter Ryan returns after relieving here for Term I in 1978, to teach Year 6.

Mr Les Couche, Regional Senior in Technical Studies, will spend 2½ days a week establishing Photography as part of the curriculum.

Mrs Joanna Demasi comes from Comaum to teach in Junior Primary.

Mr Demasi comes from Penola High School to teach Economics and Geography.

Mr Roger Boehm comes from Waikerie High School as an Art teacher.

Mr Geoff Schulze comes from Morphett Vale High School to teach Technical Studies.

Mr Greg King comes from Sturt College to teach Year 4.

Miss Kathleen O’Flaherty comes from Sturt College to teach Physical Education.

Mr Rob Zacher comes from Sturt College to teach Drama.

Miss Robyn Smith from Torrens College will teach Home Economics.
