Burra Main Street Committee held its AGM last week.  Guest speaker was Chairman of Angaston Main Street Committee, Paul Mariani.

Paul is from the US, but now owns Angas Park Fruit & Nut Co.   He spoke of success with KESAB Awards, undergrounding of power lines, street lighting, street furniture and tree planting.  A $20,000 grant had encouraged owners to improve shop fronts.
