The Anzac Day Dawn service last Wednesday drew about 40 persons.  Rev. John Stephenson conducted the service.  Wreaths were laid by Ben Kotz representing the local RSL and Roy Jeffery representing the District Council.  Annual services were also held at Spalding War Memorial on Sunday 29 April and at Saddleworth on Sunday.
