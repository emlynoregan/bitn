Obituary.  Thomas Richards, aged 71, died at Millerton on 23 June.  He was a resident of over 30 years, having arrived in the St Vincent on 9 September 1875.  He was born at Camborne, Cornwall, and worked as engine driver at Morphettâ€™s Shaft until the mine closed and then as engine driver for 19 years at Sara & Dunstan.  After that he ran a small grocery business.  He was Superintendent at Redruth Methodist church for many years and a staunch Rechabite.  He is survived by a widow, four sons and two daughters: Thomas Richards (Adelaide), W.J. Richards (Burra), Arthur Richards (Burra), James N. Richards (NZ), Mrs John Pryor (Crystal Brook), Mrs W.J. Williams (Iron Mine), also 29 grandchildren & 1 great-grandchild.
