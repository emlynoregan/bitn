Burra Town Hall Re-Opening

Large numbers of visitors took the opportunity to look through the refurbished hall and its display of memorabilia, between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. on Saturday.

The Police Band played for a successful dance on Friday night.  On Sunday 15 October ABC Radio presenter Peter Goers officially opened the hall and compered the afternoon concert.  Well-known musical identity Rosemary Nairn conducted throughout.
