Burra Golf Associates held their AGM and elected: Patroness, Mrs A.L. Collins; President, Mrs T. Padbury; Captain, Mrs M. Nicholls; Vice-Captain, Mrs D. Field; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames H. Topsfield & R. Wuttke; Secretary, Mrs E. Jeffery; Assistant Secretary, Mrs A. Wright and Treasurer, Mrs H. Crump.

Trophies were presented:

‘A’ Grade Championship	Miss E. Tiver

‘B’ Grade Championship	Mrs R. Gebhardt

Grace Barker Salver	Mrs M. Nicholls & Miss E. Tiver (Tie)

Best 2 Nett Scores ‘A’ Grade	Miss E. Tiver

Best 2 Nett Scores ‘B’ Grade	Mrs R. Gebhardt

Bogey Competition	Miss E. Tiver

18 Hole Eclectic ‘A’ Grade	Mrs Nicholls

18 Hole Eclectic ‘B’ Grade	Mrs Kemp

9 Hole Eclectic	Mrs Nicholls

Silver Spoon	Miss Judy Oates

Laurel Wreath	Miss E. Tiver

Monthly Pin ‘A’ Grade	Miss E. Tiver

Monthly Pin ‘B’ Grade	Mrs R. Gebhardt

The President Mrs K.E. Pearse reported on a most successful year in about ½ column of detail.
