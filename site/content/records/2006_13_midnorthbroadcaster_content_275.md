White River Stud of Pt Lincoln won Elders SA Ram of the Year and also the SA Pair.  Greenfields came second in both categories.  White River’s ‘Hayman 63’, last year’s Elders Ram of the Year was sold for $19,500 to Lindners of Wonga Station, Morgan.  [Photos page 11.]
