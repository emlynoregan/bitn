Empire Day was celebrated at the Burra Schools on Friday.  The children were addressed by the Mayor Mr F.T. Marston.

Burra rifle Club fired the 23rd stage of the W.G. Woodman Trophy over 600 & double 700 yards and the best scores were from P.W. Hogan off the rifle and with handicap from W. Hempel & E.C. Hopkins.
