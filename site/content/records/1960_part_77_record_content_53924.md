Booborowie School.  63 pupils visited Pt Pirie on Friday 20 November.  They visited BHAS Smelters and SA Bulk Handling, the Police & Ambulance Stations and a bakery, Northern Bottlers and had a ride on the Pt Pirie Pilot Launch.
