Primary School Students from Renmark, Berri, Barmera, Loxton and Waikerie combined with students from the Port Pirie District for a music camp ar Redruth Camp Centre.  Approximately 70 students made up the concert band, string orchestra and choir.  They presented an hour long concert on 7 August.  Years 4 & 5 BCS students attended with some senior members of the community.
