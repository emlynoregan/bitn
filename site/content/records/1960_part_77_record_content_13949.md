Burra & District Floral Art Club was formed on 12 November.  President, Mrs Ray Lloyd and Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs Brian Thamm.  Mrs Ray Mueller is the modern art demonstrator.  The club will meet monthly in the Domestic Arts room at the school.
