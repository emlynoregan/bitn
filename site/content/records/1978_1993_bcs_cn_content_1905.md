Mr Dexter Palmer has been appointed to Clare College of Further Education charged with developing course for those working in the wine, wool and wheat industries in the district.  He taught at Clare High School 1968-70, but transferred to DFE in 1977 after spending twelve months teaching in Fiji and was appointed to Naracoorte College.  He has organised a Sheep Classing Seminar in Clare 21 & 22 February.  In June there will be an Objective Clip Measurement School at Burra run by the Marleston College of Wool.
