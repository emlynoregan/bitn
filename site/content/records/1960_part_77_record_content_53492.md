Ironmine Methodist Church was crowded on 30 September when about 80 ladies gathered to hear a musical program presented by visitors from Adelaide: Mrs Melva Bartel (piano), Mrs Dorothy White (soprano), Miss Greig (contralto) and Mrs Crane (humorous recitations).
