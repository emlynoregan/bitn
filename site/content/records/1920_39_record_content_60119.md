Northern Districts High Schools Swimming Carnival was contested at Jamestown of Saturday with five schools competing.   Jamestown with 46 points won from Kadina 22, Pt Pirie 20, Burra 6 and Peterborough 1.  Burra sent along only two contestants and Warren Mumme won the 50 yards open breast stoke and the 75 yards open freestyle. [¼ column is devoted to describing Jamestown’s new pool and the excellent organisation of the occasion.]
