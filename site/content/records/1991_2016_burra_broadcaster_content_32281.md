Burra Show.   The 126th show* on Saturday 11 October drew a good crowd with gate takings of $2,272.  Dr Tim Simpson opened the Show.  The two biggest attractions were the Horses-in-Action and the non-stop action in the shearing shed, where the day started with a demonstration by a lady shearer, Amy Bailey-Hill of Burra.

Prince of Show		Samuel Rains

Princess of Show		Caitlin Drew

Baby of Show		Josephine Rains

Junior Princess of Show	Amy Berryman

[Selected results are printed along with 7 photos.]

[* Actually the 111th Burra Show, as a result of years missed due to WWI, WWII and periods of recess.]
