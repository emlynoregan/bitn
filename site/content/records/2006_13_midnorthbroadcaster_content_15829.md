Burra Community Library Assistant Mary Woollacott has been hand raising a grey kangaroo joey named Moses.  He has been a frequent visitor to the library and to Junior School classrooms.  ‘How’s Moses?’ has become a commonly asked question at the library.  He has been particularly popular with European exchange students and some other overseas visitors.
