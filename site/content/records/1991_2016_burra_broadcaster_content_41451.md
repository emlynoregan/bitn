The New Burra Water Tank will increase the town’s capacity from 1 megalitre to 2 megalitres.  It will be c. 11 m high and 16 m in diameter.  Once the new tank has been completed the old one will be demolished.  Construction began in September 2015 and will continue to June 2016.
