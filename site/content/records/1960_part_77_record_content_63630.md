Kooringa Masonic Lodge.  In recognition of 20 years of service as secretary of Kooringa Masonic Lodge Mr D.H. Field had Rank of Honour conferred upon him at the Gran Lodge Communication held at the Grand Lodge Masonic Temple North Terrace, Adelaide, on Wednesday night.  Other Burra members to hold office of the Grand Lodge are Bro. Brian Riggs who is Assistant Grand Director of Ceremonies and Bro. Dave Thomas who is a Grand Steward.
