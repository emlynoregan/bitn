Burra Bowls Associates held their AGM 6 May.  President Mrs H. Crump reported on a successful season.  The Green Team finished second on the premiership list and was runner-up in the championship rinks.  Elected: President, Mrs D. Field; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames J. Murphy & M. Kellock; Secretary, Mrs E.C. Pietsch & Treasurer, Mrs C. Neill.
