Property Sales

‘Rockhill’ at Leighton, the property of Mr C.A. McDonald & Mrs F.M. McDonald was sold by auction on 7 November in the Burra RSL Hall.  It had been in the family since 1869.

Lot 1.  Homestead and 326 acres was passed in at $170 per acre, but later sold to Mrs I.M. Gordon of Wahroonga.

Lot 2.  326 acres sold to W.B. McMahon of Booborowie for $131 per acre.

Lot 3.  312 acres was sold to M.B. & N.C. Miller for $140 per acre.

Melbourne Cup Day saw the ladies of the Cup Committee take over the Burra Town Hall and they and guests numbered nearly 200.  Some $800 was taken.

Kooringa Methodist Ladies’ Guild Fete on Friday 2 November at the Burra RSL Hall raised almost $300.

Southern Farmers Burra off-shears sale of sheep and lambs saw 2,500 yarded and demand was good. Prices ranged from $10.45 to $15.45.

The LCL Frome Campaign Fund barbecue and gymkhana raised $500 on 4 November.

Hanson CWA held a mystery tour on 18 October and enjoyed a very successful wander though the Barossa Valley.
