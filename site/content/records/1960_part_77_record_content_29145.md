Notice.  The 115th Anniversary of the Redruth Methodist Church will be held on 1 May.  The guest speaker will be Rev. H. Alvey.  He was circuit minister 1924-27.  The Mr & Mrs Edward Finch Memorial Organ will be dedicated.  On Monday 2 May there will be the anniversary Tea at 6 p.m. and at the public meeting at 7 p.m. Rev. H. Alvey will deliver his historical paper on Burra Methodism, which he presented to the Methodist Historical Society.
