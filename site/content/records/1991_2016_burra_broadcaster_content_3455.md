Advt.  Burra Traders’ Association and the Burra Arts Council present ‘Easter Fair’

Easter Sunday in Market Square and the Burra creek Picnic Area.

Market Square will feature shops, spit roast, merry-go-round, Easter Egg hunt, face-painting and fire engine rides.

Burra creek will offer live music from ‘Black Moon’, stalls, trash and treasure, arts and crafts, food and beer, Easter bonnet and bike decorating parade.
