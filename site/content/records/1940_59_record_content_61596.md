Cricket

Saturday saw the start of the two day series.

Farrell Flat 79 v. Burra 2 for 158.  In Burra’s innings I. Burdon was 92 not out.

Koonoona 89 v. Buffs 2 for 111.  In Buffs’ innings R. Seaford was 56 not out.
