Jan Tiver and Meryl White were Citizens of the Year and Heidi Thompson was Youth Citizen of the Year.

Meryl White

Foundation member of the Leisure Activity Group

Regular visitor to residents of Barker Homes and organises church services at Burra Hospital.

Was a member of Booborowie School Welfare Club

Was a member of the Parents and Friends of Burra High School

Much involved with Booborowie and Burra Bowling Clubs

An advisor to Spalding Rural Youth

Church organist, Fellowship Member, Sunday school superintendent and teacher.

Jan Tiver

Involved in:

Palliative Care

Meals on Wheels

Goyder House Committee

Volunteer driver for Burra Hospital

Care for elderly and underprivileged citizens

Burra Tennis Club

Ambulance driver

Domiciliary Care Convenor

School Council

Ecumenical Choir

The life of the Church

Heidi Thompson

Keen netballer, basketballer and tennis player

In the final five in Junior Miss SA in 1997, raising more than $1,500 for the Epilepsy Foundation.
