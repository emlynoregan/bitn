Burra Hospital

A group of concerned Burra and District residents is calling for an urgent meeting with the Health Minister, John Hill to discuss the current state of emergency services at Burra Hospital.  Four months after a meeting with Premier Mike Rann nothing has changed.  A petition expressing their concern is being circulated.
