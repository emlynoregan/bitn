Fire.  A grass fire at North Booborowie on 6 January burnt out a strip 2½ miles x ½ mile and destroyed c. 1,000 acres of feed and some fencing.  Proprietors affected were: J. Davies, (‘Mundunnie’), Broad Bros and R.R. Baker.
