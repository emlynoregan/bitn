Chris Parker has been appointed Policy Advisor to Warren Truss, Federal Minister for Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry.  His position is based in Canberra, but Jane and the children will stay in Burra until the New Year.  Chris was farewelled from the Governing Council of BCS recently, having been Chairperson for three years.
