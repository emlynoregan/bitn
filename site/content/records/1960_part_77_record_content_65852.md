Mokota Cricket Assoc.

North Booborowie 184 defeated Leighton 139 to win the Mayfield Shield (Second Division) at Willalo on Saturday.  Koonaburra won the Mokota Shield when it defeated Booborowie at the Burra Oval on Saturday.

Koonaburra 9 for 198 (including P. Byles 114) defeated Booborowie 125.

Tennis

‘A’ Grade	Grand Final

Aberdeen White 10-95 defeated Kooringa 10-92

‘B’ Grade	Preliminary Final

Aberdeen Purple 12-114 defeated Kooringa Gold 8-105

‘C’ Grade	Kooringa 6-54 defeated Aberdeen 6-46
