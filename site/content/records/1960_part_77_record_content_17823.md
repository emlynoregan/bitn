Porter’s Lagoon.  Boating enthusiasts from Clare and Burra Districts have combined with Burra Demobilised Soldiers Association and the previous Porter’s Lagoon Committee to form a club to be known as the Porter’s Lagoon Project.  President Mr Max Hilton of Hilltown, Secretary, Mr D.K. Springbett of Clare.  They have leased the north shore area and have plans for new change rooms, showers and toilets etc.  Functions will be held to raise money.  The first will be a carnival by the Adelaide Outboard Club with 11 events next Saturday and Sunday.
