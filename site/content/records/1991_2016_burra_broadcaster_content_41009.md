Burra Red Cross 2014-15 Report

In 2014 the centenary of the branch was marked by the unveiling of a plaque at the Centenary Rose Garden by Dan van Holst Pellekaan MP.  Red Cross Calling realised $2,374, a trading table brought in $368 and the Christmas Raffle $750.  Kath Lomman received her 30 year badge.
