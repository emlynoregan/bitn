Burra Burra DC

The arts Development Council has provided a subsidy of $5,000 towards the cost of upgrading the auditorium, stage lighting, drapes, etc. at the Town Hall.  A further application will be made for renovations to the dressing rooms, supper room, smoke lounge and front of the Hall.
