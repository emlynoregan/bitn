Craig Dunstan, newly appointed Clare Valley Tourism manager, was welcome to Goyder area on Friday by Goyder CEO John Brak.  The Council has committed $10,000 towards Clare Valley Tourism for the 2010/11 financial year and will review their commitment in the New Year.
