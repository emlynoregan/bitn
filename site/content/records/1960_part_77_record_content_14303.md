Burra Town Council, 3 December

Cr Jeffery reported that the Young St Footbridge had collapsed and he moved it be closed.  Carried.

Burra Cold Stores apparently sought an extension of the prohibited area in front of the doctor’s house because the Council replied that it did not feel inclined to restrict parking any further at this time.

Council approved placing a Christmas tree in Market Square.

Council agreed to pay water rates for the Aberdeen Croquet Club – except for excess water.

A letter of sympathy was sent to Mrs Tanswell on the death of D.E. Tanswell in recognition of the services rendered to the Corporation by her late husband.

Burra Small Bore Club.  The Double 75 yards Championship was won by G. Webster who was best off the rifle and on handicap.
