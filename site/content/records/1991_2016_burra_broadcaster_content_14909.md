Peacock’s Chimney

State Governor Sir Eric Neal & Lady Neal attended the unveiling of a plaque marking the 25th anniversary of the re-siting of the chimney.  Local citizens, members of the National Trust and the Main Street Committee gathered at the redeveloped site for a champagne breakfast.
