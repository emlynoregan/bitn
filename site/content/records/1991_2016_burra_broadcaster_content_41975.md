BCS at the Royal Adelaide Show

27 students represented the school in the steer, goat and sheep sections.

In the steers, Caitlin Parsons ranked 3rd in the Novice Handlers Section.

Cassie Sims was best student handler in her steer class.

The Murray Grey was placed 2nd in the Heavy Domestic Class.
