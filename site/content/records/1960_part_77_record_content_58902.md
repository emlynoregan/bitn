Novelty Fashion Parade

Mouth Organ Selection	Mrs A. Williams of Farrell Flat with Mrs Ron Piggott on piano

Two Popular Songs	Mr Ron Blackwell, accompanied by Mrs Blackwell

Skit ‘Some Get All the Breaks’

Club Swinging	Dianne Day accompanied by Mrs Cockington on piano

Bracket of Songs	Debbie Kirk

Men’s Ballet ‘The Dew Drop Fairies’*	Extract from the Nutcracker Suite featuring Ian Allen as the Fairy Queen

[* The ‘girls’ are names on page 3: Barry Eberhard, Sid Allan, Morris Allen, Brian Thamm, Dudley Cockington, Alan Baulderstone and Ian Allen.]

Burra Apex Auction Sale raised between $150 and $180, which will allow the club to begin carrying out its projects.

The Rural Youth Cabaret took c. $410 with the profit going to the Burra Hospital.

Booborowie Methodist Ladies’ Guild ‘Space Age’ Fete raised $520 on Saturday afternoon.

West Beach Primary School students from three Grade VI & VII classes visited Burra last week.  They visited the usual places in Burra, drew sketches and surveyed the local shopping centre.  The visit was run in co-operation with the Burra Primary School and its Headmaster Mr Allan.
