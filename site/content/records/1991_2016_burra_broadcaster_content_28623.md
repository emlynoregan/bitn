The Bicycle SA Outback Odyssey is visiting Burra Monday 23 & Tuesday 24 September.  Tuesday will be a rest day, after which the riders will head to Hallett.  There are expected to be about 350 riders.  They will have followed the Mawson Trail from Adelaide to Burra.  200 more riders will join in Hallett to do the shorter version of the ride to Wilpena by 1 October.
