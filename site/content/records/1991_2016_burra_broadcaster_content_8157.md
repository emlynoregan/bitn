Saltbush Clothing Co. won the SA Government & Telstra Small Business Award for businesses with fewer than six employees, last Friday night at the Hilton Hotel in Adelaide.  Accepting the award, Elspeth Radford said it was exciting for Burra and the Mid North.  The company will be represented in Melbourne on 31 October for the National Awards.
