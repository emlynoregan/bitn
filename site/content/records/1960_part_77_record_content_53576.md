Harold Bughurst of Hyde Park writes to acknowledge the great holiday weekend enjoyed by members of the Cowell-Cleve and Unley Camera Clubs, who visited Burra for their fifth annual inter-club competition.  Some 60 visiting photographers enjoyed the hospitality of the town and the Burra Camera Club, which provided a picnic lunch at the Gorge and arranged visits to Redbanks and Mongolata Goldfield.
