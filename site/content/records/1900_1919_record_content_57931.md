Mr Gilbert Horner, with the Commonwealth Electoral Department in Burra for 3½ years, has been transferred to Kadina where the Department has decided to make its centre.  He has been a strong worker for St Mary’s: Secretary of the Vestry and a member of the choir.  He is also a member of the Institute Committee and a worker in patriotic affairs, together with his wife who has been active in Red Cross.
