Peter Murphy who has been at Dalgety’s in Burra for four years will go to Kimba on 4 April to be replaced by Mr J. Merkel from Bordertown.

Peter was educated at St Joseph’s and Burra High School before joining the staff of Richardson, Kempe, Hogarth & Edwards in 1953.  He has been a member of the Aberdeen Tennis Club, Burra North Badminton, Burra Cricket Club and Burra Football Club as well as Burra Rural Youth.
