Burra Bowling Club held its AGM last Wednesday 28 June.  Elected: Patron, Dudley Jacka; President, Darrell Field; Vice-Presidents, Brian Riggs & Wally Scott; Secretary, Jack Topsfield and Treasurer, K. van der Does.  [Details of the operations of the club for the coming year are printed.]

The 1971-72 season was opened by Stan Woollacott, a past President, on 16 October 1971.  Two successful tournaments were conducted – a two day event on the Sunday & Monday of the January long weekend and the three-day Easter Tournament.  The financial position of the club is satisfactory.

[The rest of the report mainly thanks various people and groups who contributed to the club’s success.]
