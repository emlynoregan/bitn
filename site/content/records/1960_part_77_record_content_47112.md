A Miniature Debutante Ball was held in the Burra Town Hall on Friday night.  The evening resulted in takings of $420, with profits to aid the Aberdeen Basketball Club.

Debs & Partners were:

Michelle Greet & Gregory Mitchell

Annette Nankivell & Robyn Heinrich

Michelle Walker & Richard Dollman

Tricia McBride & Terry James

Robyn Jones & Raymond Irlam

Christine Lloyd & Stephen Taylor

Tracy Couzner & Michael Ford

Denise O’Reilly & Terry Bevan

Shauna O’Connor & Ian Gordon

Marie Moore & Kym Irlam

Debra Schuppan & Peter Thomas

Kaye Webster & Jamie Lane

Beverley Salter & Christopher Wall

Anne-Marie Stockman & Paul Miller

Keryl Spackman & Andrew Spackman

Susan Clode & Neville Kelly

Penelope Cullen & Anthony Kakoschke

Elizabeth Hallion & Jeffrey Thamm

Sharon Brereton & Robert Steele

Lyn Moreland & Tom Wardle

They were presented to Channel 9 personality Humphrey B. Bear, who was accompanied by Miss Ann North.
