Burra Town Council.  Elections.  Nominations:

Mayor	E.T. Baulderstone	elected unopposed

West Ward	R.A. Moreland	elected unopposed

East Ward	B. Kotz	elected unopposed

North Ward	No Nominations

A Supplementary Election is called for North Ward with nominations closing on 31 May.

An Extraordinary Vacancy has been caused in East Ward by the resignation of Cr Rev. G.D. Sibly.

Nominations for this Extraordinary Election will also close on 31 May.
