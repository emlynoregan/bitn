Burra Show Society held its AGM on Friday. Mr M.G. Stockman was re-elected President.  He appealed to local sheepbreeders for greater support.  G. Stanley Hawker remains the Patron.  The Vice-Presidents are Mr Rex Stockman and Mr M.T. Kellock.  Applications are called for the position of Secretary, as the previous Secretaries Mr & Mrs Barry Taylor have been transferred from the district.  Mr Roy Jeffery was made a Life Member.
