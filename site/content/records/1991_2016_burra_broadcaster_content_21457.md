Burra Art Society is planning a busy year with an exhibition in the Town Hall for the Festival Weekend and a camp at Quorn among scheduled activities.  Elected at the recent AGM were: Chairman, Moona Nookemba; Acting Secretary, Margot Leary and Treasurer, Lionel Bullen.  The group meets monthly in the Grainger St Guide Hall, Burra North.
