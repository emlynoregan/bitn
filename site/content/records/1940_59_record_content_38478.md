Obituary.  Miss Phillis Goodridge died 27 July 1948, aged 35.  She was born at Burra, the eldest daughter of Mr & Mrs T.E. Goodridge and has lived at Gum Creek all her life.  She was educated at Gum Creek and Leighton Schools and had been associated with the Ironmine Methodist Church all her life, being Superintendent of the Kindergarten for many years.  She had been associated with all the churchâ€™s social activities and was a one time member of the Ironmine Tennis Club.  In WWII she was a member of the Leighton Red Cross, the VSD and the Comforts Fund.
