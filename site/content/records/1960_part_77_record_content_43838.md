Burra Town Council, 15 January

Burra Pictures lost $57 in the past month with only 215 people attending.

It was resolved to appoint T.G. Perry as Acting Town Clerk till 30 June 1969 for two days a week and that the business of the Corporation would be conducted at the District Council offices.
