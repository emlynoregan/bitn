Football.  Mid North League

‘A’ Grade	The 1971 Mail Medal went to Barry Kotz of Robertstown.  The count was held at the Oddfellows’ Hall and organised by the Aberdeen FC.

‘B’ Grade	The Jenner Trophy went to Bradley Heinrich of Robertstown.

Colts	The trophy went to Ron Sandercock, Captain of Auburn.

Junior Colts	The trophy winner was Trevor Mullins of Riverton.
