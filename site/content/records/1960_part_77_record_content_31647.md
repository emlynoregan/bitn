Obituary.  Mr T.H. Woollacott died in Adelaide on 5 October aged 85.  He was mayor of Burra for many years in the period of the Great Depression and WWII.  For his outstanding service in those hard times he was awarded an MBE.  He is survived by two sons and a daughter.  His wife and three sons predeceased him by many years.

[Thomas Henry Woollacott born 1 October 1882 Nelson, Burra: died 5 October 1966 North Adelaide.]
