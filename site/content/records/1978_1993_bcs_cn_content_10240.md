Cornish Mining Tour

For those interested in the Mining Heritage and Geology of Cornwall.

Visit several operating tin mines.

Copper, tin and clay mining museums.

Historic mine sites and engine houses; some with Cornish beam engines.

Camborne School of Mines.

Historic Cornish houses and villages.

Cost c. $2,500 for two weeks in Cornwall.

Tour Leader: Greg Drew.
