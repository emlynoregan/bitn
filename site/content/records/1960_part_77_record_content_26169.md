Obituary.  Ronald Eric Stahlhut (46) (a returned man) and Robert Brian Stahlhut (18) were killed in a road accident on the West Coast.  Mr Stahlhut is survived by his wife Daisy, a son Darryl and a daughter, Vicki.

[Died 4 September and 5 September respectively at Streaky Bay, residence Spalding.]
