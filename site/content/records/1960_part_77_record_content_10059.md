Koonoona Estate Sale, Burra RSL Hall 23 February.

Lot 1.  92 acres 3 roods 24 perches was bought by C.V. Kotz of Emu Downs for £41 per acre.

Lot 2.  749 acres 2 roods15 perches was bought by E.H. & J.O. Friebe & O.H. Broad at £18 per acre.

Lot 3.  1272 acres 32 perches was passed in at £16 per acre.

Lot 4.  680 acres was bought by Broad Bros of Booborowie.  [Price unclear – perhaps £11-15-0 per acre.]

Portion of Hogback Sale, RSL Hall 23 February, of 6289 acres of perpetual lease was passed in at £1-10-0 per acre and sold after private negotiation to Messrs G.R. Thomas & Sons for an undisclosed figure.
