Burra Town Hall Management Committee was re-established at a meeting held on 5 March.  Chairman, Mr Barry Wright; Secretary, Mr David Thamm; Public Relations, Mrs Freda Pink and Council Representative, Mr Norm Maxted.  More committee members are sought.  Repairs have been carried out on the stage flooring and the Apex Club is repolishing the floor of the main hall.
