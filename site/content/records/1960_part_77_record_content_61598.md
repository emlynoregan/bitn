Birth.  To Ronda & Sid Allen at Burra Hospital last Wednesday, a daughter, Kathryn Michelle.

Birth.  To Kevin & Valda Williams nee Mitchell at Burra Hospital 4 June, a son.

Birth.  To Sue & Roger Dunn at Burra Hospital 4 June, a daughter.

Marriage.  Burra Methodist Church last Saturday

Julie Kerslake, youngest daughter of Mrs & the Late Mr M. Kerslake, married Ted Irlam.

Obituary.  Mr W. (Bill) Carpenter died on Thursday night following a short illness.  Prior to this he had been a very active person for his 82 years.  He had served as a Burra Councillor from 1943 to 1947 and again in 1949, resigning on 2 May 1950 to contest the Mayoral election.  He was Mayor from July 1950 for four years.  He is survived by a widow and five children: Joyce Mrs Bourman, Perce, Gladys Mrs Pens, Beryl Mrs Woodman and John.

[William Carpenter born 22 March 1890 Aberdeen: died 1 June 1972 Burra.]

Notice.  Tenders are called by the Burra Burra DC for the erection of new toilet accommodation and additions to the office block.
