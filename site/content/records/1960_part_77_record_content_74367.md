Burra Division of St John Ambulance Service has decided to buy a new ambulance to be air conditioned and fitted with modern equipment.  At the AGM on 19 February Ron Thamm was re-elected Chairman with Mrs Eileen Beinke as Secretary.  In the last year the ambulance travelled 7,601 km and involved 304.5 hours of voluntary work.  18 patients were transferred and there were 4 maternity and 23 medical transfers.  11 vehicle accidents were attended.  The call-out fee is to be $ X and 50 cents per km for non-members.  Membership subscriptions numbered 368 in 1973 and 482 in 1974.
