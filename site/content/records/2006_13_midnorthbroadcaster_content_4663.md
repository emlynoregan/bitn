Sir George Hubert Wilkins MC 1888-1958 was remembered with a plaque in North Terrace’s memorial walk in Adelaide last Wednesday.

Though not well known in Australia, Sir Hubert had an incredible life.  He was born at Mt Bryan East in 1888 and his achievements included:

Taking the first aerial war movies during the Balkan War in 1912.

Arctic exploration.

Antarctic exploration and photography.

The first trans-Arctic flight.

Circumnavigation of the earth in an airship.

First under ice voyage in the Arctic Ocean.

Intelligence work for the US in WWII.
