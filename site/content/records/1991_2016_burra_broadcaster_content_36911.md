Chelsea Evans & Katie Thomas were finalists in the 2005 Rostrum Voice of Youth.  With six other state finalists, they spoke in the House of assembly of the State Parliament in Adelaide on Saturday night 25 June.  Although neither was chosen to proceed to the National Titles in Tasmania, they acquitted themselves well and gave memorable speeches.
