Shadow Minister for Tourism, Terry Stephens was in Burra with Dan van Holst Pellekaan on Tuesday as they followed up the question of Phoenix Copper’s application to lift the Mine Reserve.  The local member’s view is ‘that rather than say “no”, the community should put the challenge to the company and state their conditions, which could potentially benefit both parties.
