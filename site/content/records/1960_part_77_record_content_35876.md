Mr & Mrs Art Barker have left Burra North for Crystal Brook where Art will be Far Northern Supervisor for the Highways Department.  He was three years on the Woomera-Pt Augusta Road and in June 1952 was foreman of the Burra Gang.  In 1955 he took over the North Eastern Gang with 1,300 miles of road and in 1966 became supervisor of Northern Roads with 6,500 miles to cover, worked for the E & WS Department by 100 men.
