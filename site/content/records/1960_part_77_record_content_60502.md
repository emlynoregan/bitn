Aberdeen Tennis Club has erected gate pillars at the entrance to the Aberdeen Tennis Courts in appreciation of Mr & Mrs Scroop’s 30 years of service (1940-70) as joint secretary-treasurers of the club and for the many hours spent working in this sports area.  Mr A.L. Clode, a Past President of the Club for eight years will unveil the plaque on Easter Sunday at 8 p.m. [Check – surely 2.30 p.m. as advertised!]
