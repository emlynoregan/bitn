13 March 1991

SA Merino Field Days are approaching: 11 to 21 March.

On 20 March the Merino Capital Ram Championships will be judged to win the R.N. Collins Perpetual Trophy.

Program:

Dinner

Judging at 6.30

Presentation of Trophies and Prizes 7.30

Mr John Kerrin, Federal Minister for Primary industry will speak at 8 p.m.

Alf Dundy’s comedy sheep dog routine 8.30 p.m.

Frankie Davidson, national recording star and entertainer 9 p.m.

ABC’s Ian Doyle will compere the evening.
