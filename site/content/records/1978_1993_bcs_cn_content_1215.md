Burra Basketball Club held its AGM on 16 August and elected: President, Peter Reed; Vice-President, Rob Zacher; Secretary, Judy Pritchard and Treasurer, Peter Shattock.

A total of 8 men’s and 8 women’s teams were nominated for the summer season.
