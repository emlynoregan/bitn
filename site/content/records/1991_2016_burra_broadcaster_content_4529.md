The Burra Passport has a new look and will now be available in four versions:

$20 plus a $5 key deposit that serves 2 adults and 4 children and includes the complimentary Discover Historic Burra booklet.  Each additional person $5 and each additional vehicle $5.

A Group Passport is $20 for the passport and $5 per person with $5 key deposit

An Educational Passport is $20 and $4.50 per person with $5 key deposit.

Locals to the Burra Burra DC get a 50% discount.
