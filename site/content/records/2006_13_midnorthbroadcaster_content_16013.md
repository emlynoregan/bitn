Burra Swimming Club has received a grant of $1,300 from the Active Clubs Program.  The cheque was handed over by local MP, Mr Dan van Holst Pellekaan.  The funds will allow the purchase of storage cupboards for equipment and a TV and DVD for the club to watch videos for training and coaching purposes.
