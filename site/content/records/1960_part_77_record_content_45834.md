Obituary.  John Kellaway, husband of the late Rachel Anne Kellaway of 58 Wood Ave Croydon and late of Burra, died at the Repatriation General Hospital 9 May aged 85.  He was the father of Lorna, Jack and Verna and step-father of Dick and Bill (deceased).  [Born 9 October 1883 Kooringa: died 9 May 1969 Daw Park, residence Croydon.]
