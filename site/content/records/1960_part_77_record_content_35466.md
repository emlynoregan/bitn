Accident.  At about 8 p.m. on Friday a car driven by Mr Graham Wiseman of Campbelltown overturned at the junction of the Burr-Adelaide and the Burra-Farrell Flat Roads.  The driver escaped injury, but the damage to the car was estimated at $400.
