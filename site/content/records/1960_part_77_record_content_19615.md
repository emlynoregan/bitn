M & BHA.  Lady Bonython, State President of the Mothers & Babies’ Health Association, will open the new clinic in West Bridge Street Burra on 24 April.  The committee has £350 in hand and requires a further £450 to open free of debt.  Donations will be acknowledged.  Mrs G. Stanley Hawker has donated £20.
