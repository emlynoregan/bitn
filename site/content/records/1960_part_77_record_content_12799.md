Obituary.  Mr James Lomman, formerly of Booborowie, died in his sleep on Monday 20 August at his old home ‘Whitefield’, now occupied by his son, George.

[Frederick James Lomman, born 15 May 1892 Kooringa: died 21 August 1962 Mt Bryan East, residence Booborowie.]
