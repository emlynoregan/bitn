Burra’s Jubilee 150 Festival ‘Burra Blasts Back’ will be held over the weekend of 30 September and 1 October.

It will include:

Activities by the creek

Arts, Crafts & Food stalls

Bush & Jazz Bands

Morris Dancers

Singers

Pipe Band

Maypole Dancing

Puppet Shows for Kids

Dan Burt the One Man Band

Fireworks near the Redruth Gaol on Saturday night

Dance at Redruth Gaol on Saturday night featuring the Wild Matildas

A Traditional Methodist Church service Sunday morning

A Grand Parade of Floats, Bands and Dancers from the Town Centre to Redruth Gaol to climax with a Victorian Picnic and an opening of the Gaol Interpretive Display

Pioneers’ Reception on Friday 29 September.
