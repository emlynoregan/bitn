Women’s Fellowship Concert in Redruth Hall on Friday evening saw the hall packed.  Concerts these days are a relatively rare event and it was pleasing to see the full hall.  Door takings were $112.  Program:

Compere: Mr Lynden Beare

Piano Duet	Mrs Dudley Cockington & Miss Carolyn Nicholls

Three Songs	Primary School Music Club directed by Mrs J. Carpenter

Skit ‘A Park Scene’

A Little Opera ‘Mother Goes Out’	Fellowship Members

Two Dance Items	Miss King’s Ballet Pupils

‘The Biggest Aspidistra in the World’	Mrs Carpenter (à la Gracie Fields)

Two Brackets of Folk Songs	Debbie Kirk

Hornpipe	Rev. Milton Edwards on his invention the ‘Oopsharmonia’

INTERVAL

Novelty Fashion Parade

Mouth Organ Selection	Mrs A. Williams of Farrell Flat with Mrs Ron Piggott on piano

Two Popular Songs	Mr Ron Blackwell, accompanied by Mrs Blackwell

Skit ‘Some Get All the Breaks’

Club Swinging	Dianne Day accompanied by Mrs Cockington on piano

Bracket of Songs	Debbie Kirk

Men’s Ballet ‘The Dew Drop Fairies’*	Extract from the Nutcracker Suite featuring Ian Allen as the Fairy Queen

[* The ‘girls’ are names on page 3: Barry Eberhard, Sid Allan, Morris Allen, Brian Thamm, Dudley Cockington, Alan Baulderstone and Ian Allen.]
