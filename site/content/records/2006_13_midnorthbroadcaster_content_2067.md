‘Mt Job’ is a project run by a not-for-profit community service, Employment Directions.  It launched this project in Burra last week, with 15 people attending, with the aim of having 50 people across the region attending by Christmas.  It aims to create a personal employment plan, prepare people for interviews and employment and develop local job opportunities.  It was also launched in Clare, Jamestown and Riverton.
