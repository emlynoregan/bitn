Proposed Expenditure on Roads & Bridges

[There is a lengthy list of proposed expenditure across the colony.  The Burra related items were as follows.]

*Tenders for urgent works have been invited for about £4,800 more.

Proposed appropriation of the surplus £20,000 (out of the £90,000) as an addition to the accompanying estimate of the Board’s expenditure for 1856:

Gawler & Kapunda	£2,000

Kooringa & Port Wakefield	£1,500

Burra Road north of Light	£500
