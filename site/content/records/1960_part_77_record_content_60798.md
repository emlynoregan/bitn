Mr & Mrs Tom Pickering visited from Hobart yesterday.  Mr Pickering was born her 50 years ago when his father was manager of the ANZ Bank from 1920-1930.  His sister Roma, Mrs Hannan of Henley Beach, accompanied him.  Mr Pickering is the Librarian in the Hobart Library.

[Tom Mansergh Pickering was born at Kooringa 8 August 1921 and died 26 October 2001.  He was also a jazz musician and received the Order of Australia 1982.]
