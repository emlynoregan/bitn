Harry Dutton & Murray Aunger were the first to cross Australia south to north by motor car when they reached Darwin in a 1908 25 h.p. Talbot.

The centenary is being celebrated by the National Motor Museum with a travelling exhibition with the 1908 Talbot as its centre piece.  The Talbot will arrive in Burra on 3 July and will be shown at BCS and in Market Square on 4 July before going on to Peterborough on5 July.  [Photo.]
