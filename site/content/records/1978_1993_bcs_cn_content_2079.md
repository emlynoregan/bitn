Jacqueline Kitchin, a Year 6 student at BCS is joining a group planning to ride horses from Pt Augusta to Adelaide to raise money for the Adelaide Children’s Hospital Easter Appeal.  She is seeking sponsors for her 330 km ride to end at Channel 7 in Adelaide on 29 March.
