Goyder Council Elections

Peter Mattey has secured the Hallett Ward seat.

Darryl Venning, Jane Kellock and Adrian Gill were elected for Burra Ward with 205, 204 and 195 first preference votes respectively.  The other candidates were Helen Szuty (132), Colleen Woodgate (87) and Tim Stockman (36).

Judy Partington won a Eudunda seat with 282 and Peter Dunn was second elected with 131, ahead of Peter Schiller 120.  With 169 first preferences John Neal was elected for Robertstown, ahead of Warren Mosey with 84 and Adrian Menzel with 49.
