Burra Community School has had easier involvement with the Southern Cross Cultural Exchange thanks to the efforts of local co-ordinator Teresa Earle.  Currently there are four exchange students enrolled:

Esteban Rios (16) is from Valencia, Spain and is in Year 10.  He is staying with the Ritchie family.

Francisco Arguello (17) is from Valladolid, Spain and is in Year 11 staying with the Earle family of Burra North.

German Student Lena Schaefer is 16 and come from Castrop-Rauxel.  She is 16 and is staying with the West family.

18-year-old Emmi van Der Burg is from Berkel en Rodenrys in The Netherlands and is staying with Mark and Lisa Brooks and family.
