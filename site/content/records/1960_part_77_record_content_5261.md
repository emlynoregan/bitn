Advt.  Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. have been instructed by the registered proprietor of C.J. Pearce & Son to offer for sale or lease on Friday 3 February the business premises with a frontage of 68 ft 6 inches to Commercial Street and a depth of c. 150 ft along Kangaroo Street: two shops with frontages of stone, workshop of wood and iron, stone living quarters on both floors of 14 rooms etc.
