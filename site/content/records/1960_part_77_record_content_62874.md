Burra Copper Festival Program

Saturday 7 October	Burra Show: opened by H.E. the Governor Sir Mark Oliphant.

Evening Ball at Burra Town Hall

Teenage Dance at the RSL Hall

Art Display at Burra Bowling Club

Sunday 8 October	Church Services: Methodist, Anglican Catholic & Lutheran.

1.30 p.m. Procession organised by the National Trust from Market Square to the Mine Entrance where Sir Mark Oliphant will unveil the resited Peacock’s Chimney.

The Police Band will play at Peacock’s Chimney and then at Market Square 3.30 & 4.30.

Gemmology display and demonstration at Burra School with copper articles and spinning and weaving.

Art display at Burra Bowling Club

Samin Ltd offers Mine Tours

Glee Club Concert Burra Town Hall 8 p.m.

Monday 9 October	Aberdeen Oval:

Merry-go-round

Children’s Races and Humphrey B. Bear

Aircraft flights over the town

Vintage Cars and penny-farthing bicycles

St John’s Brigade Band

Civil Defence display

Marching Girls at 11 a.m. & 2 p.m.

Netball Exhibition 1 p.m.

Shearing Demonstration

Animal Nursery

Hay Rides & Pony Rides

Min-Golf

Radio-controlled model aircraft

Motor Cycle Display

Samin Ltd Mine Tours every 15 minutes

Refreshments
