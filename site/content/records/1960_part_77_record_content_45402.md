║ William Charles Leonard West was Mayor from December 1906-November 1908.  Dr John Ikin Sangster Jnr was Mayor from December 1908-November 1909.

¶A new Act of Parliament, effective 2 November 1934 changed the Municipal Year from approximately a calendar year (actually December to November) to one running from July to June.  The life of the 1934 Council was extended to the 1st Saturday in July 1935.  This means that the later Mayors, beginning their terms in July, should be:

1937-39	S. Kellaway

1939-46	T.H. Woollacott

1946-48	H.J.B. Jennison

1948-50	F.T. Marston

1950-54	W. Carpenter

1954-69	E.T. Baulderstone.]
