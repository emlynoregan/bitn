Tanya Waters has been awarded an art scholarship by the SA Youth Arts Board.  She will study with well-known art teacher Heather Parker.  Tanya’s talent was recognised by the Burra Regional Art Gallery, which has sought a scholarship on her behalf – applications were made for three different scholarships.  [Photo.]
