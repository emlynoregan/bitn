Rifle Club.  The Gap Cup for 20 matches, of which 11 have been fired, is currently led by W. R. Lee 18, from F. W. Lown 14, A. B. Riggs 13 & R. Woodman 10.

In the 11th round W. R. Lee 97, H. L. Riggs 95 & F. W. Lown 92.
