Burra High School.  The Jubilee History continued by R.V. Mueller.

1959

The staff comprised M.W. Scriven Headmaster, J.D. Sladden, C. Morrison, G. Ireland, L. Pemberton, S. Crouch, R. Kowald and P. Kowald (Junior Teacher).  Enrolment was 115.  Sir Robert & Lady George visited the school on 29 June.

1960

Staff changes: C. Morrison & G. Ireland were transferred and A. Dadleff & J. Field (Junior Teacher) joined the staff.  Enrolment was 119.  A new rotary power mower and refrigerator were purchased and linoleum laid in the library.  Mr Beinke rewired the school, installing power, heater and clock points.
