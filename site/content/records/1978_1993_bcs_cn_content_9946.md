The Daydream Motel team has challenged any Burra Barrow Team to beat them in the Burra to Broken Hill Barrow Push to start at Burra on Wednesday 23 January 1985 and finish at Broken Hill the following Sunday.  The Daydream Team claims to be World Champions, having now won the two WA races, come second in the 1983 Burra-Broken Hill event and won the Medindie-Broken Hill race this year.
