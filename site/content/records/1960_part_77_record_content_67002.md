Obituary.  Myrtle Edith Hill died 23 June aged 73.  She was the wife of the late Horace Hill and mother of Melva (Mrs Ross Day), Barbara (Mrs Lewis) & Bob.  She was daughter of the late John & Jack Allen and a sister of Jack (deceased), Ida, Fred and Jean (deceased).  [Born 2 July 1899 Kooringa.]
