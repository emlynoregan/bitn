Heidi Thompson, daughter of Geoff & Dianne Thompson of Booborowie, married

Tim Crick, son of Michael & Gillian Crick of Goroke, Victoria.

The reception was held in the Burra Town Hall for c. 180 guests.  The couple will live at Burra North.
