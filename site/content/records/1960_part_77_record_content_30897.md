Booborowie Methodists & Anglicans will hold a joint service at the Anglican Church on Sunday 28 August.  Spalding Methodist Circuit Minister Rev. David Pill will read the lesson.  The address will be given by Rev. Keith Seaman of the Central Methodist Mission, who will speak on the work of the Christian Television Association.
