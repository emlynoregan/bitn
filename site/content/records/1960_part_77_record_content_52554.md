Football.  North Eastern League

‘A’ Grade	Burra 6.7 defeated Booborowie 2.1 in extreme conditions with strong wind, rain and hail.

Clare North 11.15 defeated Spalding 5.8

Hallett 9.15 defeated Clare South 2.5

‘B’ Grade	Booborowie forfeited to Burra due to illnesses, but a scratch game was played with D. Woolley and C. Bevan playing for Booborowie.  This game was abandoned in the third quarter due to driving rain and blinding hail stones when the scores were Burra 6.8 v. Booborowie 2.3.

Clare North 8.13 defeated Spalding 5.2

Hallett 4.2 defeated Clare South 2.11

Junior Colts	Burra defeated the visitors.  [No scores are given and the visitors are not named.]
