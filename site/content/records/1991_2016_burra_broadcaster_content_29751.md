Sue Ryan writes to say that a concerted effort to increase membership of BRTBA since their decision to take over the running of the Burra Visitor Centre from 1 January 2003 has seen membership double.  She urges others to support the organisation by becoming a Fried of the Burra Visitor Centre for $50 per annum.
