Burra Hospital Board.  The elections of 1 July were declared on 3 July.  In Burra 650 ratepayers voted and the 12 nominations resulted in:

Maurice Collins	538

W.H. Sandland	526

H. Morgan	501

E.J. Davey	493

P.J. Sampson	475

C. Bartholomaeus	167

W.R. Chambers	144

S.M. Lane	119

M.A. Radford	115

E.W. Crewes	91

A.S. Martin	85

P.J. Pickering	72

The first five were declared elected.
