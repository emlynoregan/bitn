Mt Bryan Progress Association (newly formed) has developed a landscape plan for the Mt Bryan Reserve area and suggested it be called ‘The Sir Hubert Wilkins Memorial Park’.  Council has adopted the plan in principle, with the exclusion of a proposed development along the old railway line corridor.
