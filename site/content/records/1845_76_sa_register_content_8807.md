Notice.  The English & Australian Copper Co.

The management of this company, having been resigned by Messrs Thomas Henry Williams and George Ewbank, will henceforth be undertaken by James Hamilton Esq. as sole manager.

R. Hicks, Solicitor, New Exchange Chambers, 4 September 1854.
