Bowls

1st Division	Saddleworth 72 defeated Burra 55

2nd Division	Burra Green 72 defeated Saddleworth 66

Hallett 82 defeated Burra Gold 68

3rd Division	Mintaro 89 defeated Burra 64

Burra Associates, 17 December

Burra Green 52 drew with Jamestown

Peterborough Blue 57 defeated Burra 55

Burra Methodist Church will hold an Induction Service for Rev. Alan Beames on Thursday 22 January 1976.

The Ironmine Methodist Church Trust Committee held its final meeting on Monday last week.  The sale and transfer of the property have been completed.  Committee members, formerly Trustees were thanked by the Chairman Rev. Clarrie Hore and special thanks were paid to Ray Lloyd who was Secretary and Treasurer since 1948.  The committee was then dissolved.
