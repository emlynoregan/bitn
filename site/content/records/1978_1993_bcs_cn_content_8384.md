J.J. Richards

Lived in Burra since 1951.

Employed by Burra Motor Co. for 13 years and then three years with Fiebig Motors before building and starting my own business in 1967.

Semi-retired for the past 5 years after my two daughters and sons-in-law took over the business.

Played and followed sport in Burra for 32 years.  Secretary of Koonoona Cricket Club for 2 years and for three years with Aberdeen Football Club.  Now President elect of the Golf Club.
