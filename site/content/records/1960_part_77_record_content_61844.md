Wool Day in Burra, organised by the Burra Red Cross and the Australian Wool Board, was attended by about 175 ladies from Burra.  A knitting contest was won by Mrs Bowden of Burra.  A wardrobe of woollen materials and garments was inspected.  Mrs T. Padbury of Mt Bryan was judged the best dressed lady in wool.  Proceeds $240.
