Burra Community Print and Burra Broadcaster.

After three years as manager and proprietor here Terry Wilson has taken a new position as managing editor of the Northern Argus at Clare.  Apprenticed to the family business in Pinnaroo, Terry was judge apprentice of the year by the Lithographic Institute in 1985.  He ran the print shop on Groote Eylandt and then worked in high quality colour printing in Adelaide before coming to Burra in 1993 to run his own business.  Burra Community Print will continue with Rob Wilson Sen. Editing the Broadcaster and Jill & Julie running the office and typesetting etc.
