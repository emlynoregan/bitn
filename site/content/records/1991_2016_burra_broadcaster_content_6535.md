Graham Kellock

A Councillor for the last 10 years – Deputy Chairman for 3 and Chairman for the last 5

Represented Burra on several committees outside the town and district

4 years on the Mid North Local Government Executive Committee, which covers 14 Council, only 8 of whom have committee representation

At this level have lobbied for the sealing of the Burra-Morgan Road and the Spalding Road

District roads are a major concern

In the last 18 months have left the above committee to concentrate on the Mid North Development Board, of which am presently Chairman – this board covers 8 Council areas and aims to encourage secondary industry and rural industries in the area.

Currently Council is working on a new management system for its tourist business enterprises

Have been involved in many organisations over the years, including:

Rural Youth Member, President 1960-61 and an advisor

Burra Community & Sports Club Committee & President for 4 years

Past Burra School Councillor and chairman of the Agriculture Committee for 2 years

Involved with the Burra Picnic Race Club from its inception

Player and Committee member of Burra Bowling Club

Member of the Merino Capital Committee

Member of the Redbanks Committee
