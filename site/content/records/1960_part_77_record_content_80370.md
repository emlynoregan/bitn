Burra Burra DC, 13 September

A legalistic paragraph declares Mary Crescent at Burra North closed and part of it is sold to E.A. & E.L. Clode and part to A.L. & E.G. Heinrich.

It was resolved to purchase a Ford petrol motor truck with air operated tailgate and high tensile steel tray for a changeover price of $18,700.
