The Twilight Jazz Affair will come to ‘Petherton House’, Hallett on Saturday 28 March.  From 4 p.m. E-Type Jazz, the Eddy White All Stars and Gumbo Ya Ya will perform for the evening.  The event is organised by the Sir Hubert Wilkins Memorial Trust to aid the program to restore his birthplace.
