Roaring Forties conducted representatives of Clare and Gilbert Valley Council and the Regional Council of Goyder over their Waterloo Wind farm site to answer question and view 11 of the 37 turbines completed.  Goyder Council was represented by Crs Bob Bradley (Burra Ward), Warren Mosey (Robertstown Ward) and Judy Partington (Eudunda Ward).
