Goyder Council, 28 May

A Council signage policy will be developed.

Council agreed in principal to lease the Eudunda Swimming Pool to the Eudunda Community Business & Tourist Committee.

Council will consider a 2.5% increase in general rates for 2013-14.

Council will negotiate with the Eudunda Community Children’s Centre to purchase their building and associated infrastructure for an amount equal to the centre’s debt; estimated to be $200,000.

Council agreed to Phoenix Copper carrying out exploration on Council land.

Council will enter into a partnership with Unity Housing for the provision of four dwellings targeting key worker housing on lots 2, 3, & 4 of DP 24193 Hd of Kooringa, Lower Thames Street and Roach Street, Burra.

Council allocated $1,500 from the Goyder Tourism Working Party budget to support development of two mobile phone applications for the Dare’s Hill and World’s End driving trails.

Goyder Youth Advisory Committee co-ordinator is to establish a Facebook page to enable young people across the region to communicate, network, discuss issues and have input into youth targeted events etc.

Heritage Plaques

Applications for free heritage plaques across the Goyder Region are called for.  Applications must be made on the prescribed form available from the Council’s Tourist Officer or downloaded from www.goyder,sa.gov.au

They are to be received by 4.30 p.m. Friday 2 August.
