Centenary of Federation Projects

The Burra Planning Group for Centenary Federation Projects outlines its plans:

Restoration of the portrait of F.W. Holder.

A Walk of Fame.

A centenary garden.

A school production.

A community back-to-Burra picnic.

A maypole.

Plus projects not requiring funding:

Visit of the Centenary of Federation Roadshow.

Art Gallery Exhibition ‘Home is Where the Heart Is’.
