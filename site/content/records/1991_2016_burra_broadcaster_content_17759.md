Burra Leisure Activity Centre has received a bequest from the late Bert Cornelius, who was a user of the then Burra Day Care Centre.  Beneficiaries in Bert’s estate were asked if they would like to make a donation to the Centre in Bert’s memory and they donated $2,000.
