Break-ins & Thefts

The Burra Post Office was broke into and oxy acetylene equipment was used in an unsuccessful attempt to get into the safe.  30 assorted compact discs were stolen.

A small amount of cash and a men’s watch was stolen the same night at a break-in at the Market Square Café.

Two residents of the St Just St and View Street area report thefts.

Vandalism.  Two floodlights at Burra Community School have been broken.
