Kids of the Uniting Church in Australia Camp Out was held this year on 24 & 25 March at Nuriootpa.  Burra Uniting church took 11 young people to the event, led by Ms Bronwyn Wilson, Mrs Carolyn Lovell and Darren Lovell, Uniting Church Minister.

[8 photographs are printed.  Those attending were Chelsea Kelly, Amber Williams, Joshua Badman, Bronwyn Wilson (Team Leader), Reece Bowen, Darren Lovell (Burra Uniting Church Minister), James Lines, Emily Lines, Jacob Witwer (Orroroo), Oscar Kelly, Emily Badman, Harry Lovell and Henry Witwer (Orroroo).]
