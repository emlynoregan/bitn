Since 1960 there have been 8 managers:

1960	Arthur Wright for 18 years and now retired.

1978	Robert Burns for 4 years, but no longer with the company and working interstate.

1982	Gary Tapscott – now National Manager Livestock & Live Export.

1987	Peter Rolstone – now Territory Sales Manager at Clare (1987-1990 & again 2000-2002)

1990	Rob Stephens – now Real Estate Manage Mid North & Yorke Peninsula.

1994	Tony Gooden – now Elders Queensland Livestock Manager & Australian Agricultural Company Marketing Manager based in Brisbane.

1998	Gerald Wetherall – now Elders Regional Manager based in Geraldton WA.

Present	Damien Webb Branch Manager for both Burra & Clare.
