Break and Enter.  Intruders broke into Burra Motor Co. in Thames Street on Wednesday 30 October.  They were reported to police at c. 1.45 a.m., but had fled by the time the police arrived.  They were later stopped in a vehicle at Tarlee, where the stolen property was recovered.  The value of the stolen goods was $5,000.
