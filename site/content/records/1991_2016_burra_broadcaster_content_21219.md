Burra Community School

Sixteen students sat Stage 2 SACE (Yr 12) and achieved 9 As and 38 Bs between them.  Three students gained all As and Bs.  The following students are going on to tertiary study:

Billie-Jai Cooper	Gained 19/20 for Technical Studies and arrived in Belgium 16 January as a Rotary Exchange Student

Rachel Vogel	Journalism at University of SA

Sarah McInnes	Business Management at Gawler TAFE

Anne Fry	B. Ed. Flinders University

David Ryan	Double Engineering & Science degree specialising in computer systems at University of Adelaide

Tina Williams	Certificate in Veterinary Nursing at Gilles Plains TAFE

Daniel Mengis	Advanced Diploma in Advertising and Graphic Design at Elizabeth TAFE

Melanie Wastell	B. Ed. At Flinders University

Dimity Arthur	Did her primary schooling at Burra and has gained entry to a course in Psychology.
