Notice.  Burra Burra Branch of the National Trust of SA

All are invited to a community meeting in the Burra Town Hall on 3 November 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. concerning the proposed nomination of the Australian Cornish Mining Site (Burra & Moonta Mines) for National and then World Heritage Listing.  The keynote speakers will be Professor Phillip Payton of the University of Exeter and Barry Gamble of the Cornwall County Council.
