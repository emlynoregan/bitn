Nathaniel O’Brien has spent the past few months passing through heats and then finals rounds in the inaugural Adelaide’s Next Superstar contest.  On Sunday in the final he sang Someone Else’s Dream and was pronounced the winner of the inaugural ANS contest.  Winners receive a $20,000 audio and visual recording package and a ticket to the ARIAs (Australian Recording Industry Awards in Sydney next Thursday, with flights and accommodation.
