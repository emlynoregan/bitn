The Program for the Second Copper Festival has been finalised:

Friday Night	A cartoon festival

Saturday	Burra Show opened by Mr Kelly MHR

Street Barbecue & Food Stalls

Historical Films on show at CWA Rooms

Evening Organ & Choral Music at St Mary’s

Teenage Dance at RSL Hall

Licensed Dance at Burra Town Hall

Sunday	St Mary’s Sung Eucharist with Quire St Nicholas

Catholic Rock Mass at Burra Town Hall

Mine Tours

Methodist Diamond Jubilee [i.e. 60th anniversary of the Kooringa Methodist Church building.]

Afternoon Band Music & Dancing in Market Square compered by ABC’s John Ovenden

Visit of the Premier, Don Dunstan

Art Show at the Bowling Club

Aeroplane Joy Rides

Model Boats at the Swimming Pool
