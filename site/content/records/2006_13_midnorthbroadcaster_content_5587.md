Nathaniel O’Brien went to Barmera on the Queen’s Birthday weekend for the 30th Annual SA Country Music Festival and Awards.  He entered four categories and came:

1st in the under 16 Vocal Section

1st in the 13 and under Gospel Section

3rd in the Open Australian Ballad Section

2nd in the Open Duet Section, singing with his mother, Karen.
