Burra High School.  The Prefects for 1961 were inducted on 21 March.  Head Prefects are Ellen Kemp & Robert Brooks.  Others are: Suzanne Harris, Raelene Hirschausen, Janet Sladden, Elaine White, Errol Hood, Ian McGrath, Robert Parker, William Standing and John Fullgrabe who replaced Kim Horsnell when he was appointed a Junior Teacher.
