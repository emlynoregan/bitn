Booborowie School played host to five American students under the American Field Services Scholarships International.  While in the area the students visited K.S. & E.M. Trengove’s Poll Short Horn Stud, John Sommerville’s piggery, Booborowie School, Booborowie Seed Pty Ltd, J. & F. Sullivan & Co.’s Merino Sheep Stud, shearing at John Drew’s ‘Windy Brae’, Burra Miners’ Dugouts and the Burra Mine.
