The Annual Burra Cycling Classic will be held on Saturday 15 September.  The limit riders will leave the Royal Exchange Hotel at noon and the backmarkers at 12.40 p.m.  The race is programmed to end at the Commercial Hotel between 3.45 and 4.00 p.m.  Council will be donating a copper trophy.  There will be a circuit event on Sunday morning.
