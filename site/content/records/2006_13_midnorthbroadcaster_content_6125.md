Len Bence will present his ‘Swan Song’ exhibition at the White Cedars Café after some sixty years of painting in and around the area.  His last exhibition was a retrospective at the Burra Regional Art Gallery in 1998.  Mr Bence is 85.  The exhibit will be opened on Sunday by Mary Woollacott.
