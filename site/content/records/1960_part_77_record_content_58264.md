Morgan Dockyard

[There is a lengthy article considering the effect of the closure of this dockyard on the township of Morgan.  It had 21 employees, of whom 12 had their own homes in Morgan.  The fortnightly paycheque was $2,300.  This blow to the town followed the closing of the railway.]
