A Lutheran Mission Festival was held at Burra on Sunday at the Burra Town Hall.  Lutherans from Blyth-Clare-Burra Parish attended.  Guest pastors were Martin Winkler & Ron Gerhardy, Directors of the Australian Lutheran Hour and Lutheran Mission Field respectively.  Parish Pastor P.A. Scherer conducted the service.  Members of the choir came from Blyth.  Pastor Gerhardy spoke of the Mission work in New Guinea and Indonesia in particular and Pastor Winkler of the work of the church within Australia.  A buffet luncheon followed in the supper room.
