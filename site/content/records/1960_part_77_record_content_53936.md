Yongala Rural School will not be closed as was proposed because it has 25 students and so there would be no saving of a teacher.

Peterborough Primary School is in a poor condition.

Mannanarie parents, whose children were to have shared the bus to Peterborough, have now asked that they go instead to Jamestown.
