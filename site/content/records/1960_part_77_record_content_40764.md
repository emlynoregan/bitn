Burra LCL Women’s Branch AGM elected last Friday President, Mrs J.P.T. Drew; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Pearce & W. Miller; Secretary, Mrs R. Collins; Assistant Secretary, Mrs T. Padbury and Treasurer, Mrs M. Kemp.  Guests included Sir Lyell & Lady McEwin and Mr & Mrs E.C. Allen.  The guest speaker was Hon. Mrs Jessie Cooper MLC.
