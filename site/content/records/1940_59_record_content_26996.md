Mr & Mrs Jack Harvey were given a farewell at Burra North Memorial Hall on Wednesday night.  Also farewelled was their daughter Veronica and Miss D. Keys.  [Mrs Harvey’s sister.]

[The report of speeches made and small presentations extends for 1¼ columns.  The Harveys and Miss Keys moved to Prospect where they ran a greengrocer’s business on Prospect Road at the tram terminus for a number of years.]
