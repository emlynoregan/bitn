Stud Merino Field Days

Entries for the ‘Pairs’ and ‘Ram’ competitions are good.

‘Pair’ to be judged at 5.30 p.m. from 21 entries, Tuesday evening, Gala Night.  The winner will represent SA in Melbourne in July.

The Ram of the Year will be judged at 6.45 p.m. from 24 entries.

Entertainment will follow – a 17-piece brass band – a comedian – a parade of woollen garments from Linda Ryan’s ‘Wool do it Australia’ fashions.

The Tennis Club will provide a roast meal from 6 p.m. and there will also be hot dogs, ice cream, drinks and bar facilities.
