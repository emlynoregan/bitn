Jailhouse Rock 2002 is returning to Redruth Gaol, with dancing in the exercise yard.  Top Adelaide band ‘Get Back’ will play popular 50s and 60s music until 1 a.m.  Paul O’Bryan’s band will be the support act and will kick of the weekend by playing at the Burra Hotel on Friday evening 22 February as well as featuring at a Rock ʼn’ Roll demonstration in Market Square on Saturday morning.
