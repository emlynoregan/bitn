Attempted Theft

On 6 November a trailer was stolen from an address in Queen Street.  It was later used in an unsuccessful attempt to steal a Telstra phone box situated on the footpath in Bridge Terrace.  A male from Terowie and another from Murray Bridge were subsequently arrested.
