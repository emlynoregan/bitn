Adelaide Wool Sales.  Eastern pastoralists again topped local district prices.  The top price went to A.J. & P.A. McBride of Braemar at 115½d per lb and K. Phillips of ‘The Gums’ at 115d per lb.

Leighton 24 defeated Spalding 14

Hallett 24 defeated Mt Bryan 14
