Ray McAuley writes a letter intimating that he will be a candidate for LCL endorsement for the Northern Legislative Council District at the next state election.  The Present Members are Sir Lyell McEwin and Mr Harry Edmonds.  Sir Lyell has said he will stand again, but Mr Edmonds is retiring.
