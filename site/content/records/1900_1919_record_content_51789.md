Major Lewis arrived home by midday train Saturday last after three years with the Light Horse in Egypt where he was seriously wounded last December.  He was met at the station by the Mayor and many other friends.  He is the guest of Mr I.J. Warnes who drove him to the Rotunda where the Ladiesâ€™ Band welcomed him and a large crowd was assembled.  Speeches followed by Mr Crewes, Rev. D.B. Bridgwood, Mr Winnall, Mr J. Drew representing the Cheer-up Soc. and Mr J. McLaren.  Major Lewis responded briefly.
