Burra Bowling Club held a general meeting on 17 October.  There will be two teams this season in Division 1 (Gold & Green) and two in Division 2 (Blue & White).  The 1868-69 season was opened last Saturday by Mayor E.T. Baulderstone.  The President of the Associates, Pat Field, cast the first jack and Bill Corner, the 1967-68 Club Champion, put down the first bowl.
