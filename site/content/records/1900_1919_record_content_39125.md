Volunteer

J. Murison, machinist for Vivian Lewis Ltd has been accepted as a volunteer for the 1st Contingent.  He will be in charge of the motors.  He was farewelled by brother Masons and presented with a safety razor and from Annie Pearce with a ‘housewife’.  [⅔ column of details.]  [A housewife = a small case for needles, threads etc.]
