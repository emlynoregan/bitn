Advt.  Elders-GM has instructions from Mr E.S. & Mrs E.L. Wilks to conduct a land auction at Burra Institute on Friday 4 November.

1.  ‘Wallinga’ Homestead – 342 acres – Section 68 Hundred of Hallett with a seven-roomed stone house etc.

2.  ‘Wallinga’ Paddocks of 890 acres perpetual lese – Sections 71 & 77 Hundred of Hallett.

3.  ‘Stone Hut’ 507 acres perpetual lease – Sections 74 & 75S Hundred of Hallett.

4.  ‘Kalcootra’ (Lily’s) – 795 acres perpetual lease – Sections 55 & 59 Hundred of Hallett.

5.  Paradise – 1,210 acres perpetual lease & 389 acres freehold – Sections 58M, 58S, 57, 56, 50 & 49 Hundred of Hallett and Sections 105, 137, 108 & 144 Hundred of Mongolata.

6.  ‘Bertieowie’ – 751 acres perpetual lease – Sections 84 & 87 Hundred of Hallett.

7.  ‘Back Paddock’ – 719 acres perpetual lease – Sections 79N, 79S, 76NE, 76SE Hundred of Hallett.
