Burra CWA had Mrs Hallion of Bumbunga, President and Secretary of the Warnes Group, as speaker for its July meeting.  She spoke of a recent trip to Alice Springs and Brisbane and of various aspects of the CWA, especially of the opening of the new building at headquarters by Sir Thomas Playford, scheduled for 9 August.
