Obituary.  Mrs Lillie May Cheffirs of Spalding died at the residence of her daughter Mrs K. Larwood at Jamestown.  She was born at Parnaroo in March 1896, a daughter of the late August and Anna Hennig.  She was one of a family of eight children.  In 1914-15 she moved with her parents to Bundaleer and on 10 March 1926 married Thomas Clarence Keith Cheffirs of Spalding.  He husband died some years ago.  She is survived by three sons and one daughter.  [Born Lillie May Hennig 20 March 1896 Parnaroo: died 11 August 1968 Jamestown.]
