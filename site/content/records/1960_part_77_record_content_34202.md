30th North Eastern Combined School Sports Meeting was held at Spalding.  The host school was Willalo.  Mr George Harris of Adelaide opened the event.  Mr Harris was a former Booborowie resident and was the first Secretary of the Combined Sports, holding the position for 10 years.  Schools participating were St Joseph’s, Hilltown, Booborowie, Spalding, Leighton & Willalo.  [Booborowie and Spalding each competed as two Houses rather than as a single school: Booborowie Red & Green and Spalding Hutt & Hill.]  [Results are printed.]
