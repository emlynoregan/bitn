The Burra Doctors have still not returned from Sri Lanka.  Though believed now to be safe, it is reported they were in a traumatic situation, which has seem them hospitalised in intensive care.  Ms Robb has spoken to Dr Fernando on the phone, but there was no explanation about what had happened to them.  It has not so far been possible to get a locum for Burra.  Dr Stewart Martin was available for two days last week.
