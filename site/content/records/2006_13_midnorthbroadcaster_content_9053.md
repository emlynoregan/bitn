Ruth Norris, honorary RSPCA officer was disgusted on Saturday to be called to rescue a kitten about a week old, which had been dumped near market Square in Burra.  There was also a dead kitten with the one she saved.  There are heavy penalties for dumping kittens and similar acts of cruelty.  Investigations are continuing.
