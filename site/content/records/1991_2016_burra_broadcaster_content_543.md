Advt.  Burra Picnic Race Club

Annual Picnic Race meeting, Sunday 12 April, 26 events including Tent Pegging, The Cup (1,400 m), Handicap, Class Race, Flying Handicap.  And for the kids, Neck Tie Race, Lemon Race, Can Race, Barrel Race and Sleepy Lizard Derby.

Bush Dance at the Race Track Saturday 11 April at 8 p.m.
