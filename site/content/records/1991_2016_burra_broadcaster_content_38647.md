Wind Farm.  Australia’s largest wind farm is to begin construction at Hallett in September this year, with commissioning due in December 2007.  It will be a 95 megawatt plant alongside its existing 180 megawatt gas fired peaking power station west of Hallett – at a cost of c. $236 million.
