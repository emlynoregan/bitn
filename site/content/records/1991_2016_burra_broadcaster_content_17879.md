Fire.  An oxygen cylinder, heated to an extreme temperature, exploded on a property east of Mt Bryan, causing structural damage to a workshop and igniting a small grassfire.  Damage was put at $8,000.  CFS units from Mt Bryan and Burra and four private units attended.
