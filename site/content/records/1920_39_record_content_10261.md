Accident.  Albert Norman Conquest (18½) suffered severe concussion when he fell from the top of a load of hay on Saturday at Black Springs.  He is a recent immigrant from the UK.  Drs Steele and Steven operated on him at the Burra Hospital, but his condition is critical.

[He subsequently died: see XXXXV, 12, 21 March 1923, page 4.]
