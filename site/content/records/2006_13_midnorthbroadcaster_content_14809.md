Obituary.  Lola Jennison died recently.  She and her husband, Ray Jennison, lived in Burra for many years before moving to Adelaide.  They were very active in town affairs.  Ray was very concerned with local government and was at one time Chairman of the Council.  Lola was a keen member of the National Trust and an active member of a number of other organisations in the town.
