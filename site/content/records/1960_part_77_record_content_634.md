The Country Tennis Carnival held recently in Adelaide was won by Mr Don Lloyd of Ironmine, who came away with the H.G. Hastings Trophy for outstanding sportsmanship and ability.  Other members of the Burra Association Team were D. Bruce, G. Sladden and F. Sullivan.
