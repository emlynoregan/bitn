Obituary.  Mr Keith Nivien [sic: for Vivian] Jones died at Jamestown Hospital 3 November.  He was born in Burra almost 38 years ago, the younger son of Mrs C.R. and the late Mr W.T. Jones and spent most of his life in Hallett.  He served for over two years in the AIF in WWII and on his discharge married Miss Irene Morris of Norwood.  They made their home at Hallett.  Keith took an interest in the town and district and was a member of the School Committee for many years and a prominent member of the Bowling Club and Football Club.  At his death he was engaged in his own business as a general contractor and wood merchant.  He is survived by a widow, daughter Heather, son Geoffrey, mother Mrs C.R. Jones of Hallett, brother Ray Jones of Melbourne and sister Mrs B. Smith of Booborowie North.

[Keith Vivian Jones born 14 November 1924 Redruth: died 2 November 1962 Jamestown, residence Hallett.]
