Accident.  A car driven by Stewart S. Meyer of Andrews overturned on Flagstaff Hill on the Burra-Booborowie Road on Thursday.  The car was travelling north on the Copperhouse Stock Road and failed to take the corner at ‘Flagstaff’.  The driver was unhurt and a passenger, John Hancock, suffered bruising.
