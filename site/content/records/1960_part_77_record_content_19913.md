Mr John D. Murphy will retire from the ANZ on 7 May.  He is manager of the branch at 394 Collins Street, Melbourne and retires after 43 years’ service, having joined the Bank of Australasia in 1921 at Kooringa, as a country boy from Oakbank SA.  In WWII he served in the Middle East and New Guinea.  He became a sub-inspector in 1947 and served briefly in Wellington, New Zealand, in that year also.  He held a number of executive positions in Melbourne and Sydney 1947-54 before becoming Senior Administrative Officer, General Manager’s Office, Melbourne, in 1955.  He has been manager of his present branch since 1958.  He married Miss Lila Thomas of Burra.
