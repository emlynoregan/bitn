Football.  The best footballers in each grade were selected at a cabaret at Spalding on Saturday night.

‘A’ Grade winner of the Mail Medal was Brenton Kuska of South Clare.

‘B’ Grade winner was Ken May of North Clare.

Senior Colts winner was Robert Stanway of South Clare.
