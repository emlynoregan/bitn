Advt.  Trustees in the estate of the late C. Klinberg have instructed Farmers Union & Elders-GM to conduct a sale of his property at Robertstown Peace Hall on 18 March.

Lot 1.  Block 5 of Section 196, Hundred of Apoinga known as Koo-owie, comprising 264 acres 1 rood.

Lot 2.  Section 2N of the Hundred of Bright and Pt Section 297 Hundred of Apoinga, comprising 334 acres 3 roods.
