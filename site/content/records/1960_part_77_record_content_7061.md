Burra Hospital.  The cropping project is up-dated in c. ½ column.

The urgent needs at the hospital have been listed as:

Air-conditioning in the Midwifery Wing Nursery

A new buzzer system

A wheel chair

An infra-red lamp

Personal scales for Midwifery

Heart Campaign Appeal donations from Burra and District are reported to be nearing £300.  In an associated Schools’ Essay Competition, Ian McGrath obtained the 4th year prize of £2 and also the second State Prize of £5.  Suzanne Harris got £2 for her effort in the 3rd year class, Peter Benger won £1 in 2nd Year and Ian Mickel £1 for 1st year.  Ian McGrath has been accepted as a Junior Recruit in the RAN and will report on 10 July to commence training on HMAS Leuin [sic: elsewhere Leeuwin] a sick berth attendant for a two month period of training.
