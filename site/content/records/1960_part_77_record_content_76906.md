Ian Auhl’s new book Burra & District a Pictorial Memoir will be launched at Baldina Homestead on 9 November by Dame Nancy Buttfield.  Matron Mary Holden of the Girls’ Reformatory was Dame Nancy’s great grandmother.  The grounds of the historic homestead will be open to the public from 1.30 p.m. to 5 p.m.  Admission $1.  Arrangements have been made by the National Trust.
