Di Day recently handed a cheque for $5,000, part of her winnings from the ‘Miss Popularity 2005’ film crew, to the Burra Hospital Auxiliary.  It will pay for a new patient lifter and special air mattress for the Palliative Care Suite.  [Photo.]
