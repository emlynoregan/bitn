Burra National Trust held a meeting in the CWA Rooms on Monday 14 April.  Mr Alexander from the National Trust in Adelaide spoke first, followed by Mr Ian Auhl.  He paid tribute to Mr A.E. Hiles, Hon. Secretary of the National Trust who was also present and who is soon to retire.  Mr Auhl stressed the extraordinary advantage Burra has over other SA towns in its wealth of historic interest and well preserved buildings.  He then showed the 1915 film of Burra.  Many present were able to identify people they had known and themselves as well.  A film of Willunga was also shown.
