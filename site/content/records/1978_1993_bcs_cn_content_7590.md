‘The Burra Boys’ Rob Zacher & Don Griffen have recently completed an album entitled ‘The Burra Boys’, which will be released tomorrow to coincide with the National Folk Festival being held this Easter in Adelaide.  The album features 12 original songs depicting Burra’s history.  The duo will appear in concert at SACAE Magill Campus at 10 a.m. on Sunday.  The album will be available in Market Square on Saturday 9 April.
