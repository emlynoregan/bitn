Burra Penguin Club celebrates the 50th anniversary of their inaugural meeting held on 23 September 1966, when 27 interested women met in Burra Town Hall.  Mary Woollacott was present on both occasions and gave an interesting address connecting the many shared experiences of members over the 50 years.  Other speakers also drew on their memories.  Mary Woollacott had the honour of cutting the birthday cake.  [2 photos.]
