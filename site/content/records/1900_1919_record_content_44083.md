Burra Cheer-up Society.

On 9 Nov. there was a farewell social for Driver Scott, Trooper Wyett and Pte Ford.  Pte Page, a returning soldier was welcomed back.

On 22 Nov. we farewelled Sgts Wilson and Aldridge.
