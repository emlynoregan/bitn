Advt.  Elders, Dalgety and Bullock & Wilkinson Ltd will auction 2,794 acres of freehold being a portion of Koonoona at the RSL Hall Burra on 23 February for the Executors of the Will of the late Kenneth L. Duffield.

Lot 1.  93 acres 3 roods 24 perches Pt Sections 201 & 202 & closed road Hd Apoinga disposed as 1 paddock.

Lot 2.  749 acres 2 roods 15 perches Block G Koonoona Estate of 399 acres 3 roods 15 perches with a stone building of 6 rooms etc. and Block H of 249 acres 3 roods.

Lot 3.  1,272 acres 32 perches Sections 184, 194, 190, 191, 189, 188, 187, 186, 429 & closed roads Hd Apoinga disposed as 2 paddocks.

Lot 4.  680 acres Sections 241, 242, 30, 250 & 251 Hd Apoinga disposed as 1 paddock.
