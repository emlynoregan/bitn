Greg Drew, Senior geologist with the Department of Industry and Resources was guest speaker at the National Trust AGM, on the topic The Burra Mine Revisited.  He invited those interested to join him the following morning in a walk through the buddle pit area.  He explained the place to about ten people who came out on a crisp cold morning.
