A.G. ‘Tom’ Heinrich & Mrs Heinrich are growing outside vegetables of a huge size on spring water at World’s End: carrots from 1¼ to 2½lb each, obelisk-shaped beetroot to 1lb and yellow quinces at 1¼lb each.  The water will not grow cucumbers or gladioli.
