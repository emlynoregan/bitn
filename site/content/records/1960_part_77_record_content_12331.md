Advt.  Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. will conduct a sale on 17 August of 780 acres of freehold land in the RSL Hall Burra for Messrs S. & S.A. Pearce.

Lot 1.	‘Leighton’ Sections 399, 404, 446 Hd Ayers.  Section 580 Hd Hanson, disposed as 5 paddocks totalling 489 acres 3 roods 30 perches.  Includes a five-roomed stone house etc.

Lot 2.	‘Springbank’ Section 178 Hd Kooringa of 273 acres disposed as 3 paddocks with a four-roomed stone cottage, etc.

Lot 3.	Allotment 115 Burra, frontage 215 ft 10 inches to Kingston St with stone house of four main rooms etc.

Lot 4.	Allotment Pt 33 & Pt 114 Burra with frontage 142 ft 7 inches to Queen St with four bedroomed home etc.

Lot 5.	Vacant land adjoining lot 4: Allotments Pt 34 & Pt 116 Burra with frontage 220 links to Queen Street.

Lot 6.	Vacant land: allotments 9. 10, 117-120, 121, 124, 130 & 131 and a closed road, Burra comprising 6 acres 1 rood 30 perches, lying west of Chapel Street between Allen Street and South Terrace.
