Burra Burra DC, 14 February

The tender of I. Hirschausen was accepted for sealing the car park opposite the Town Hall.

Taylor Street, Sancreed Street and The Crescent are to be sealed.

Ten park seats and six table assemblies are to be bought from Monier Pre-Stressed Concrete Pty Ltd for Burra Creek sites.
