Football.  North Eastern League

Don Sommerville of North Clare has won the Mail Medal for 1973.

Runner-up Michael Wanganeen of Blyth won the Record Trophy.

In ‘B’ Grade the Best & Fairest (for the G. Pink Medal) went to Jim Jolly of South Clare.

The Senior Colts medal went to Robert Goode of South Clare.
