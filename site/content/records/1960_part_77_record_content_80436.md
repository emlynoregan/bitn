Best & Fairest	Philip Clode

Most Improved	Stefan Thomas

Most Consistent	Peter Thomas

Most Constructive	Philip Jones

Best Team Man	Patrick O’ Connor

Best Position	Andrew Allen

Best Utility Player	Garry Southall

Best at Training	Craig May
