1916

The Head Teacher Mr J. Mundy with John Biddle Assistant in Charge.  Enrolment was 29.  There was a measles epidemic in the year.  Clifford Flower passed six subjects with one credit in the Junior Class and Josephine West topped the Primary Class.  (To be continued.)
