Red Cross & FFCF benefited from the Grand Fashion Parade on Friday in the Institute, sponsored by Bence’s Ltd.  At the evening session there were breaks of 10 minutes between parades, for dancing and at 11 p.m. a buffet supper.  Details take 1½ columns and the balance after all was £26-19-2.
