Accident.  On Sunday at c. 1.30 a.m. Mr Allen Hutchins of Ridgehaven was travelling to Broken Hill to visit his seriously ill mother when, about two miles south of Burra, he was dazzled by oncoming headlights on high beam.  In avoiding the vehicle the car rolled over.  He suffered concussion and should and neck injuries.  The damage to the car was relatively minor.
