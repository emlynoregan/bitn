Booborowie Tennis Club.  Fund raising for improvements has produced over £350 and concluded with a ball to wind up a Queen Competition.  Miss Kathleen Benton whose sponsors raised £172 was crowned Queen by Miss Port Adelaide (Miss Rosemary Searle).  [Two photos are printed.]
