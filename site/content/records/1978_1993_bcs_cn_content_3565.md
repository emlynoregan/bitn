Fire.  At about 11 .45 p.m. on Monday a fire started on the roadside just east of Burra and soon spread into the Princess Royal property.  About 1,000 ha of range country was burnt and some 2 km of fencing.  No stock losses have been reported.  The fire was extinguished about 5 a.m.
