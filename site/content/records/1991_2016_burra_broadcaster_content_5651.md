J150 Update

ETSA has sponsored the illumination of the creek area for the rest of 1995.

The J150 medallion is available for $15.50 from the Council Office.

The Flower festival at St Mary’s Church 24 & 25 February will also feature a display of tapestry kneelers and organ music by Jan Cockington, Judith Neale and David Gallasch.
