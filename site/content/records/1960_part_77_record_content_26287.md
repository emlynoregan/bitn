Trespassing on Eastern Properties

Gunther Fromel of Whyalla was fined £2 plus £2 costs for not possessing a gun licence, plus £15 for being an alien and not holding a firearms licence, plus £15 for being on land without permission.  Gerrit Hendrik Westerlaken of Stepney was fined £15 plus £2 costs for being on land without permission.  The offences occurred at Thistlebeds.
