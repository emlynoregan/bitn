Accidents

Allan James Haydon of the Burra Mine suffered a fractured skull when his car rolled over after a collision with a guide post on the Adelaide Road near ‘Brown’s’ on Thursday.  He was admitted to the Burra Hospital.

Cynthia J. Correll and Wendy Martin, nurses of Pt Pirie, escaped injury when their car rolled over eight miles west of Burra on Friday.
