Obituary.  Jazz Musician Tom Pickering died on 26 October.  He had been born in Burra in 1921, but moved to Tasmania when aged 12 and had lived there since.  He was awarded the Order of Australia in 1982 for services to jazz and to the Parliamentary Library, where he worked for many years.

[Tom Mansergh Pickering, born 8 August 1921, Kooringa.]
