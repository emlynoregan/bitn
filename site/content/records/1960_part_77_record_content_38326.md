W.J. Ahern is the DLP Candidate for Burra.  He was born in Port Lincoln and educated there.  He served an apprenticeship as a carpenter and joiner at Whyalla.  For the last ten years he and a partner have established a business as building contractors.  He is a member of the Clare Hospital Board and a Committee Member of Nadjuri Lodge Home for the Aged and Chairman of Clare Inter-Church Council and President of the Clare Branch of the DLP.
