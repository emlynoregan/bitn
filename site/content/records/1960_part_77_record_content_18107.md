M & BHA reported at a meeting on 2 December that the temporary Trust House had arrived and had been erected on the site next to the CWA Rooms.  After renovations it will be used as a clinic.

The Town Hall Project Fund has reached £500.

Carols by Candlelight will be held at Burra for the first time at the War Memorial Oval on 22 December.  The assistance of the Burra Choral Society has been enlisted.  Proceeds will aid the Freedom From Hunger Campaign.

Burra Hospital Building Appeal reaches £4,700-14-2.

Tennis.  ‘B’ Grade: Booborowie 11-86 defeated Leighton Gold 9-70.

Burra Kindergarten has come near the top of the list for affiliation with the Kindergarten Union following a most generous lease arrangement granted by the RAAF Association for their premises, which conform without alteration to the standard required.  A grounds committee under Mr Gordon Nourse will set to work on the site.
