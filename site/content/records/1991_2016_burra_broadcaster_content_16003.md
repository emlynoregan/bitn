Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club held its second Dog Owners Information Evening.

Guest speakers were Jackie Horton from RSPCA, Adelaide and David Merrill, ex-RAAF police dog handler.

The AGM followed and elected: President, Merilyn Baulderstone; Vice-President, Renee Wilson; Secretary, Len Newcombe and Treasurer, Jo Baulderstone.
