Advt.  Elders will conduct a land auction in Booborowie Hall 9 May.

They will offer ‘Lorraine’ and ‘Paradise’ 9 km north of Booborowie and 18 km east of Hallett respectively.

Lorraine (On instructions from S.L., A.W. & G.L. Clapp) comprises 423.5 ha: Section 468 Hundred of Anne (freehold) and also Lucerne Flats of 37.2 ha Sections 523 & 534 Hundred of Anne (freehold).

And ‘Paradise’ of 1,052.79 ha (perpetual lease) comprising Sections 56, 58m, 57, 58, 49, 50, 105, 108, 137 & 144 Hundreds of Hallett & Mongolata.
