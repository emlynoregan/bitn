Burra Swimming Pool Management Committee has agreed to the formation of the Burra District (Amateur) Swimming Club Inc. and application will be made for affiliation with SwimSA.  Colours will be blue and black and the club will be known as the ‘Burracudas’.  The club will serve the Hallett, Burra and Robertstown Wards of Goyder Council.  (Subject to SwimSA approval.)  Danny Smith was thanked for his huge efforts in co-ordinating the pre-swimming club activities over the summer and in being the driving force behind the formation of the club.
