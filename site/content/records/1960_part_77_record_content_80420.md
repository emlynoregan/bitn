The Burra Anglican Parish held a special Blessing of the Fleece service in the spacious Caroona Station Woolshed last Sunday.  About 150 people attended.  Thanks to James & Ryves Hawker for allowing the use of their property and to manager Mr Lawrie Anderson and his wife Daph. For their work in preparation.  Father Robert Niehus of Maitland gave the sermon and assisted Fr Barry May of Burra in the celebration of Holy Communion.  A barbecue followed the service.
