Burra Methodist Circuit will hold a special Circuit-wide combined service in the Booborowie Hall on Sunday 19 April.  Changes to the Circuit boundaries in 1969 brought Booborowie into the Burra Circuit and this service will help awareness that there is a ‘District Church’.
