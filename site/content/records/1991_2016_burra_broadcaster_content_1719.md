Burra Primary Classes are going ‘Undercover’.  The Years 2-3 children will visit the old Unicorn brewery Cellars on 25 August to listen to stories, poems and music.  Years 4-7 will go on 26 August.  Other ‘Undercover’ activities will include wearing hats on 23rd, masks on 24th, scarves on 25th and all of the above on 27th, plus a red nose for Red Nose Day.
