“Cousin Jack’s” is now running an exhibition Off the Sheep’s Back.  Focussed on sheep, it features screen printing, ceramics, appliqué wind cheaters, wall hangings, turned wood and copper ware.  It will remain till after the Mid North Merino Field Days.
