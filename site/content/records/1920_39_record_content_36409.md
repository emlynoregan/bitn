Mr & Mrs Guy Tiddy and sons leave Burra this week.  Mr Tiddy was a prominent football, tennis and table tennis player for Aberdeen for many years.  He has also been a member of the Burra Institute Committee and of the Choral and Orchestral Classes and was for a time secretary of the Burra Branch of the RSL.  He has been active in the Redruth Methodist Sunday School, Church and Choir.  Mrs Tiddy has been active in the church and was at one time treasurer of the Soldiersâ€™ Aid Society.
