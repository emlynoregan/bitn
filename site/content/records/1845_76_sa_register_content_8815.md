Tenders for Conveyance of Her Majesty’s Mails

The contractor will, if required have to provide a seat for a Post-Office Guard, which must be separated from seats provided for passengers.

The mails to Port Adelaide, Gawler and Kooringa are to be conveyed in well-appointed spring carts or four-wheeled carriages.

The mails to Clare, Strathalbyn and on the Truro line as far as Angaston are to be conveyed in well-appointed spring carts or four-wheeled carriages during the summer months and in carts or on horseback – at the option of the contractor – during winter months.

Contractors will be liable to a fine of 10/- for every ten minutes late in the time of their arrival and the same amount of fine if not at the Post Office ready to start at the time appointed.

Contractors are to carry and deliver all bags and letters entrusted to them…and to provide secure and watertight lockers for mails in carriages and saddlebags with waterproof coverings for bags on horseback.

The rate of travel for mails between Adelaide and Gawler is to be 7 miles per hour and for all other mails except to Port Adelaide to be 6 miles per hour, including stoppages.

Forms of tender may be had on application at the post office at Mount Barker, Kooringa, Wellington, Gawler Town and Mosquito Plains. [Near Naracoorte.]
