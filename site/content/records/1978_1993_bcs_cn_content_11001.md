Copper Interpretation Centre

It is intended to call tenders in June for the Copper Interpretation Centre to be erected in the Burra Mine as an important element of the Commonwealth funding the SA Jubilee 150 Board to the Burra Heritage Town Project for 1986.

Tenders will be called in June and August for conservation works associated with:

Bon Accord Mine Buildings

Morphett’s Pump House and Environs.
