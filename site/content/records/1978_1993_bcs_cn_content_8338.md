Burra Burra DC Election

Candidate Profiles:

Margaret Lois Alderson

Burra born and a resident ever since.

Actively involved in various local clubs.

John Barton

Community pharmacist of 10 West Street.

Married with three children at BCS.

Four terms as President of Burra Traders’ Association.

Readily accessible to all Town Ward residents.

My personal and business interests are in Burra and I would like to contribute more to the community.

Jan Davies

Moved to Burra with my husband in 1979.

Previously a Senior Registered Nurse and Midwife and special project officer in both public and private hospitals in Adelaide.

Have mostly been a mother in Burra, but for a short and successful foray into business with Ruth Bateman in ‘Nuts ’n’ Stuff’.

Involved with:	Childbirth Education Classes

Nursing Mothers’ Association

Playgroup

Early Education Committee

The Caring and Sharing Group

If elected would attempt to express all the views of the community.
