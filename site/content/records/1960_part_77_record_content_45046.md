Burra High School.  Leaving Students organised a dance on Friday night, which was well attended. Profit was $170.

The school’s sports day was held in difficult weather conditions with strong wind all the time.  Riggs House was successful.  Cup Winners:

Senior Girls’ Cup	C. Finch

Senior Boys’ Cup	R. Przibilla

Junior Girls’ Cup	B. Kleinig

Junior Boys’ Cup	S. Lane
