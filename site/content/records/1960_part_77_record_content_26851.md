Theft.  R.J. Hill Menswear Store was broken into on Tuesday night last week.  Sports coats, vests, suits, socks, trousers, jumpers and suit cases to the value of £729-9-4 were stolen.  Det. J. Williams of Pt Pirie CIB was called in.  The previous theft from Hill’s occurred on 6 June, when clothing to c. £200 value was stolen.  Jewellery and other items to c. £600 value were then stolen from K.J. Murphy’s gift shop on the same night.
