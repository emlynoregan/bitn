Lost Camper.  A young man reported lost in the vicinity of Caroona Station at the weekend was found on Sunday afternoon at c. 4.30 p.m. by the crew of the Police Search and Rescue Helicopter.  He had been part of a group camping at Terry Lomman’s crutching shed for the weekend.  [Further details and a photo are printed.]
