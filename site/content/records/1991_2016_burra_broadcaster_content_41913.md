Budd Norris, formerly of Burra, was a member of the AFL Central & Northern England Premiership side recently, when the Manchester Mozzies 23.18 (156) defeated the Nottingham Scorpions 11.7 (73).  Budd has been in the Mozzies premiership sides in 2014, 2015 and 2016.  Older brother Jake was a member in 2012, 2014 and 2015 premiership sides, but is now teaching in Asia.
