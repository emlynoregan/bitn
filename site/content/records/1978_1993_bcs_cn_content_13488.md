Burra Yacht Club

The regatta on Sunday of the long weekend was highly successful with a record crowd.

Results of races:

Juniors	Nautyless	crewed by Aaron Schiller & Justin Smart

Seniors	Nautyless	crew not named, but see Tracker James Perpetual Trophy note.

Ladies	Green Submarine	crewed by Mandy Baker & Jane Villis

Open	Pink Panther	crewed by Rikki Loffler & Aaron Slape

Tracker James Perpetual Trophy was awarded to Nautyless after which Captain Dave Parnell announced its retirement from all future competition.

In the inaugural ‘Draggin Boat’ event the Matulick Maulers sank the Red Devil.
