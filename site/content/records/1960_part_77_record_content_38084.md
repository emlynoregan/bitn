Weather.  Patchy, but heavy rain has fallen in the east on Oakvale Station.  Across the station falls in January have been: ‘Homestead’ 302 points, ‘Jacks’ 606, ‘Pearces’ 599, Mulga Bore 614 and Brook Bore 609.  Last year the rainfall was 301 points for the year.  All lakes and dams on the property have filled and the place is full of bird life.
