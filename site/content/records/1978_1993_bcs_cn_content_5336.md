The Market Square Pump, which has been restored by Mr Fred Atkin, was officially unveiled on Monday by Mr Atkin on behalf of the Burra National Trust.  This was 100 years to the day since the pimp was first commissioned.

[Photos of Year 5/6 students watching as the pump is turned by Mr Bruce Harry of the Department of Environment and Conservation, Mr John McDonald of the Department of Tourism and Mr Fred Atkin.]
