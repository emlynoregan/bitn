Burra Golf Club.  Stan Longford retained his ‘A’ Grade Championship on Sunday when he defeated Jim Minchin.  Four members went to Eudunda for their open day.  Roly Barnes won the ‘A’ Grade 27-hole Handicap.  Ray Jennison won the 18-hole Stableford.  The Burra Team of R. Barnes, R. Jennison & Clutterbuck won the teams’ event.
