Advt.  Elders-GM will auction ‘Glen View’: 2,733.8 ha freehold and leasehold, 19 km northeast of Mt Bryan on 19 February on instructions from Glen View Pastoral Co. Inc.  To be offered in 9 lots:

‘Back Paddock’ section 634

‘Webber’s Front Paddock’ section 644

‘Webber’s Homestead’ sections 641, 642, 643 & 666 with a 5-roomed stone house.

‘Scrub’ sections 89, 657 & 658

‘Ned Webber’s’ section 659W

‘Hill Paddock’ sections 661, 664, 668 & 667

‘Quinn’s’ sections 662 & 665

‘Mount’ sections 662 & 665

Glen View Homestead section 640 with homestead of 8 rooms etc.
