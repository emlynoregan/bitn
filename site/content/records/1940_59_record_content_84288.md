Weather.  It has been very hot across SA this week and Burra was no exception.  Although Burra’s reading are unofficial, on Friday it reached 109ºF [42.8ºC], Saturday peaked at 114ºF [45.6ºC] and Sunday was 108ºF [42.2ºC].
