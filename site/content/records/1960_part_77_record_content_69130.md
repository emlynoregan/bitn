Mrs Esther Clisby of Vale Park was 100 on 11 November.  She was born in Burra and went to work when 16 as a maid for Mr C. Drew.  She has been a widow for 15 years.  She had seven children and has 13 grandchildren, 11 great-grandchildren and 7 great-great-grandchildren.

[Born Esther Davey 11 November 1873 at Paratoo, daughter of Isaac & Eliza Davey nee Horsenell.]
