Weather.  More rain has halted seeding operations, though falls to the east have been very light and some parts have experienced their worst dust storms for some time.  In the agricultural areas for the past week to Friday morning falls were: Mt Bryan 119 points, Mt Bryan East 58, Hanson 194, Booborowie 100 and Burra 116 points.
