Goyder Council’s bid for Federal funding for a Copper Interpretive Centre has been unsuccessful.  Some 740 applications were submitted and sixty projects were chosen.  Seven were in SA, of which only one was in a rural area – Pt Lincoln’s Civic Hall Redevelopment.
