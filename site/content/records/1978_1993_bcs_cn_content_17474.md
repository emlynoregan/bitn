19 October 1988

Burra Burra DC.  Road Closure.

Ludgvan Street will be closed from the Redruth Bridge to Sancreed Street and at Lelante Street for an indefinite period from 20 October 1988.  The street will be barricaded and detours signposted for roadwork to be carried out in the area.
