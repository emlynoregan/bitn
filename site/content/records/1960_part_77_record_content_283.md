Arrivals in Burra

Mr Fullgrabe the new Town Clerk with his wife and five children.

Mrs Fullgrabe will teach ballet if numbers warrant it.

Rev. R.S. Jenner, Mrs Jenner and six children will arrive about 20 January.

New Teachers at Burra High School: Mr Hicks (Woodwork) and Miss Dadlleff from Adelaide Teachers College.

Mr A.L. Wright is the new manager at Elders and he, his wife and three children have arrived from Pinnaroo.
