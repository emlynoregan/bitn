Tour Guides for Redbanks Conservation Park

TAFE SA, The Goyder Tourism Working Group and the department of Environment and Heritage are seeking locals interested in setting up a business as tour guides in the park.  It is one of the richest mega fauna sites in SA.  Guide training is being offered.
