BCS.  Year 10 Society & Environment students visited four places of worship in Adelaide last Wednesday.

1.  The Mosque in Little Gilbert Street.

2.  St Peter’s Anglican Cathedral

Lunch was taken at the Hare Krishna restaurant in Hindley Street.

3.  The Synagogue of the Adelaide Orthodox Jewish Congregation at Glenside.

4.  The Thai Buddhist Temple at Thebarton.
