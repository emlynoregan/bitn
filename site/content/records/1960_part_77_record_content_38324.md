Ernest Claude Allen JP, LCL Candidate for Burra is a farmer of Andrews who has served 12 years on the Spalding District Council; eight as Chairman.  He has been an advisory member of Spalding Rural Youth and a former Fire Controller for the area.  He has been greatly involved with cricket, tennis and bowls in Spalding and Andrews.
