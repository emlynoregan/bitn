Burra Town Council, 16 December 1963

Council resolved to go ahead with levelling and clearing the old part of the cemetery according to the prepared plan.

The donation of the resuscitation unit [from BP Australia Ltd] was accepted with gratitude.

Crs Cornelius and Phillips moved and seconded that the 1914-19 Honour Roll be stored at the RSL Club Rooms.  Carried.

Resolved that the disposal of Council relics be left in the hands of the Mayor.
