Burra High School.  The junior school held a camp at the Diocesan Centre Melrose 8-10 November.  The swimming pool was a great attraction.  About 35 students were accompanied by Mr R. Farrow, Mrs Day, Miss P. Wilkinson and Mr & Mrs N. Brand.  Mt Remarkable was successfully scaled.
