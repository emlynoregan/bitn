Burra Coursing Club AGM was held 29 March in the Institute Lodge Room.  About 18 attended with Mr Ratcliffe in the chair.  Mr Culverhouse was elected Assistant Secretary to aid Mr J. Carpenter who is in Adelaide in Hospital.  Other officers were re-elected with some committee changes.  The next meet is to be on 6-8 June.  Financially the club is sound with Â£27 in hand.  The Patron is G.W. Gallagher and President A. Ratcliffe.
