Burra Small Bore Club

3 April.  Double 25 yards: Mike Thomas won the handicap and W. Barker was best off the rifle.

10 April.  Double 75 yards: Ron Kellock won on handicap and off the rifle.

17 April.  Double 50 yards: Mike Thomas won on handicap and off the rifle.

This season W. Barker took the championship Trophy over four shoots, the Marksman’s (aggregate) and also the Handicap Trophy.  Norma Barker won the main trophies in the Ladies’ Section.
