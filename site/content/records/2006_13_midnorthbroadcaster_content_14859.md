Nathaniel O’Brien won four first and two second awards at the recent SA Country Music Festival held in the Riverland recently.  He was runner-up in the Frank Harding Memorial Award.  Nathaniel scored firsts in Senior Gospel, Australian Bush Ballads, Country Rock and Duo (with Tanya Cornish) and was runner-up in 16U and Songwriter Pack.
