Mr & Mrs F. Jefferies were farewelled at the Mt Bryan Memorial Hall recently when locals filled the place to capacity.  Mr T. Quinn chaired the meeting.  Mr & Mrs Jefferies had been strong supporters of the community for 40 to 50 years.  Mrs Jefferies had been President of the Mt Bryan CWA and of the [Warnes] Group.  Both Mr & Mrs Jefferies had supported the Hall.  Mr Jefferies had been a member of the Agricultural Bureau for 40 years and had served two terms as President, four as Vice-President, 12 years as Secretary and 10 years as a committeeman.  A presentation of an envelope containing notes and silver was made.  There were several items, including Rev. Bell playing the bones to the accompaniment of the piano.  Dancing and supper completed the evening.

Ironmine Methodist Sunday School celebrated the 39th anniversary of the Sunday School Memorial Hall on 21 July.

Burra Primary School held its Arbor Day on 27 July.  Seven trees were planted after speeches by the Headmaster and Mr Stockman, Chairman of the School Committee.
