Kooringa Methodist Sunday School has been repainted after more than 40 years.  A re-opening service is to be held on 27 March at 3 p.m.  The Kooringa Methodist Guild provided funds for most of the paint.  A frolic is being organised for 8 April to pay for the balance of the paint.
