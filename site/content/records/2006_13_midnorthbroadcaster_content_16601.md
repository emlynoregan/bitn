Sandra and Hon. Tom McVeigh write a long letter giving an account of what really happened in the case of the 82-year-old man lost in Burra Gorge recently as reported in Edition No. 1069, 4 April 2013, page 1.  They express their great appreciation of all the hospitality, concern and kindness shown in the event.

Michael Jolly’s Carob Orchard near Booborowie was featured on the TV program Landline last Saturday.

Marriage.  Sarah Riggs, daughter of Jan & Richard Riggs, married Hague Dalziel of Barmera last week at ‘Wandillah’.

Birth.  To Bec Brooks & Ash Webster, a daughter, Elsie May.  She is the first grandchild for Jenny & Tony Brooks and for Neil & Angie Webster.

Obituary.  Mr Harold Prior of Hallett has died.
