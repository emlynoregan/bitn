Weather.  Very erratic thunderstorm activity has resulted in falls from nothing to over 100 points.  Mullaby reported 110 points and there were heavy falls between ‘The Gums’ and the gypsum mine.  Pine Valley had 18 points last Friday and it has been raining non-stop there from 5 a.m. Monday morning.  Braemar had two downpours on Friday of about 30 mm each and all the dams are full.  Canegrass reports 45 mm in two days.  Further north the Indian Pacific was halted by water damage to the line.
