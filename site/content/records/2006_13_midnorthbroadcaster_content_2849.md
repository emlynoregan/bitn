Police & Fire Games.  Both the Burra Police officers are taking part in the 2007 World Police & Fire Games in Adelaide, which began on 16 March.

Hall is competing in tennis and Dredge in a week of shooting events.  Hall also plays for the Kooringa Tennis Club.  The games are attracting more than 10,000 competitors from more than 60 countries.
