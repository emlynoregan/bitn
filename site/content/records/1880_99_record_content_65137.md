Advt.  Burra United Friendly Societies’ Grand Cycling Carnival and Athletic Sports.

Boxing Day, 26 December at the Polo Grounds.  11 a.m.

Cycling Programme	1. 3 mile bicycle handicap (1st £4)

2. 1 mile bicycle handicap (1st £6-10-0)

3. Local Burra Bicycle Club race 1½ miles (1st £1-10-0)

4. 2 mile bicycle handicap (1st £3-10-0)

5. Scratch race of 5 miles (1st £5-5-0)

6. ½ mile egg and spoon race (1st 10/-)

Pedestrian Programme	1. Allcomers 100 yards (1st £1-1-0)

2. Youth’s 100 yds handicap (1st 10/-)

3. Sheffield handicap 135 yds (1st £10)

4. Members handicap 135 yds (1st Trousers & vest made to order)

5. Sheffield hurdles handicap 120 yds, (10 x 3’3”) (1st £2-10-0)

6. Christmas handicap 440 yds (1st £3)

7. Tug of War 10 men aside (1st £2)

8. Juvenile handicap 100 yds (< 15 yrs) (1st Trophy from T. Burns)

Adults 1/-, Children (under 15) 6d, (under 5) free.
