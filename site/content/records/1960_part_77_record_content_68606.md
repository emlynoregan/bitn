Burra Boy Scouts & Cubs were enthralled by a display by the CMF and a talk on safety with explosives at Hanson on Friday night.  Also on display was a collection of rifles valued at over $2,000, the property of Pte Klem of Auburn.  Scouts will raffle a Monster Christmas Stocking this year.
