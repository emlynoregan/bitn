Aberdeen Tennis Club AGM elected: Patron, Mrs A. Lloyd; President, Mr D. O’Reilly; Vice-Presidents, Mr R. Reed & Mr M. Tiver and Secretary, Mrs D. Lloyd.

In the past season the club fielded two ‘A’ teams 2 ‘B’ teams and 1 junior team.  All grades were represented in the Grand Finals, with Juniors and ‘B’ Grade Red being successful.  The year was financially successful.  After 30 years as joint Secretary-Treasurers Mr & Mrs S. Scroop resigned from this important position.  Don & Marg Lloyd took over this role.
