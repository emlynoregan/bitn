Obituary.  John Bowman died last Thursday at the Burra Hospital aged 56.  He was the son of the late W.M. Bowman of One-Tree Hill and was born at Kanyaka Station near Gordon.  [Flinders Ranges]  He was for a few years manager of Mannanarie Station for W. Marchant and later in charge of Burnside Estate near Strathalbyn.  For many years he managed Sir John Duncanâ€™s Gum Creek Station, until it was subdivided, when he bought a portion and lived there till his death.  He married first Miss Dora Norris and they had three children: Herbert (with 3rd Light Horse in Egypt), Miss Vera Bowman (South Africa) & Miss Amy Bowman (at home).  In 1903 he married Miss Jessie Radford and there are two children: John & May.  He was a keen sportsman.

[Born 26 February 1861 Kanyaka Station: died 1 November 1917 Kooringa, residence Leighton.]
