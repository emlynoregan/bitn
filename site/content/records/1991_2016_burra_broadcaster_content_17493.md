Barkers of Baldina Cottage Homes held its AGM on 27 October.  Chairman Jock Duncan reported on the year.  The nine bathrooms in Pearse Row have been upgraded at a cost of c. $60,000.  As well as retiling and showers and fittings, they were fitted with ceiling heater/lights and handrails and two needed new hot water systems.

Jennison and Pearse Rows were painted outside and Paul Wilson fitted security doors to the Chapel Street row.  Jock Duncan was re-elected as Chairman of the Board.  James Hawker is Vice-President.
