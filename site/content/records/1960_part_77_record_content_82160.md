Booborowie will celebrate the centenary of its proclamation as a township on 29 March 1977.  A committee has been formed to make arrangements for celebrations on 30 April and 1 May 1977.  There will be a Back to School and a tea at the Hall followed by an Old Style Dance.  Sunday will begin with a combined church service in the Hall and the unveiling of a commemorative plaque and there will be a barbecue lunch.
