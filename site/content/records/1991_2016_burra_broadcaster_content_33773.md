Burra Show Society will host a night of entertainment at Burra Town Hall on 5 June as a fundraiser.  Special guests will be Ian List – a bush balladeer, Paul O’Bryan and his band, with visiting artists and local talent.

Admission $5 or $2.50 for children under 16.
