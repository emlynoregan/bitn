District Methodist Ladies’ Guild Rally at Burra Methodist Church on Wednesday drew 220 ladies from Guilds and Fellowships at Balaklava, Blyth, Brinkworth, Clare, Eudunda, Gulnare, Spalding, Gawler, Willaston, Two Wells, Farrell Flat, Ironmine, Willalo and Burra.  Deaconess Phillis Bonython, Superintendent Chaplain of the Methodist Women’s Welfare Department & Babies’ Home spoke on the significance of ‘The Widow’s Mite’. There was also an enjoyable musical program to accompany the address.  Proceeds from the luncheon and the offering amounted to $223.
