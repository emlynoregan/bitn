Tree Planters.  A report outlines the work of the Petherton Road Tree Planters, who have been active since 1993-94 and they have received Commonwealth funding of $2,535 for 1997-98 to continue revegetation and preservation of natural vegetation in the vicinity of the ‘Old Petherton Estate’.
