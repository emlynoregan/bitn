Obituary.  Mr & Mrs Rex Pickering had paused for a picnic during a Sunday drive when Mr Pickering suddenly collapsed.  He was aged 42 and came to Burra when aged 17.  He was instrumental in the opening of the local branch of Oates Ltd, with which firm he has been connected for 25 years.  At his death he was a Director of the firm as well as the Branch Manager.

[Rex John Pickering born 14 June 1918 at Semaphore: died 7 August 1960 at Mongolata, residence Burra North.]
