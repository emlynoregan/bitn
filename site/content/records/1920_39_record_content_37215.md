Wheat.  The average price of wheat for the last 69 years is printed.  The overall average has been 4/8¼ with the lowest price 2/3 in 1894.  The 1931 price is 2/4.  Since 1925 the prices have been:

1925	6/1	1928	5/5¼

1926	6/2½	1929	4/8¾

1927	5/3½	1930	4/2
