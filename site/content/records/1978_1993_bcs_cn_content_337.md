14 December 1978

Mr John Steinle, Director General of Education will be the guest speaker at the BCS Speech Day on Friday.

BCS Staff Changes

Mr Lane, Miss Stecker and Miss Bourke have all received 12 months’ leave and will be travelling.

Miss Martin will marry and move to Kidman Park HS.

Miss Liddiard will marry and move to Yorketown Area School.

Miss Innes who replaced Mr Vieth during the year will leave Burra.

Mrs Bednall has been granted accouchement leave.

Mr Roger Boehm an Art teacher from Waikerie HS replaces Miss Stecker.

Mr Geoff Schultz from Morphett Vale HS will take over from Mr Lane in Technical Studies.

Mr Joe Demasi comes from Penola HS to replace Miss Martin as Economics teacher.

Miss Joanna Campbell comes from Comaum Rural School to replace Miss Liddiard.

Miss Hearse has been appointed to Gawler East Primary to be replaced by Mr Peter Ryan.

Mr Maloney, Regional Reading Senior will spend more time at Burra in 1979.

Mr Robert Zacher comes as a Drama teacher.

Miss O’Flaherty will take Physical Education.

Miss Robyn Smith takes Home Economics.

BCS Staff have purchased a large painting on four panels featuring the Burra Hills, painted by former BCS Art teacher Russell Philip.

BCS Student Prize Winners for 1978 are listed.
