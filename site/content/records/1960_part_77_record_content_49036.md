Burra Pre-School Kindergarten had a successful year with the Director Miss Ashby and Assistant Miss Quinn.  There were 19 applicants to replace Miss Quinn, who will be married in the New Year and from them Mrs Dean Bernhardt has been selected.  An end of year Christmas Nativity was well attended and enjoyed by the children and visitors.
