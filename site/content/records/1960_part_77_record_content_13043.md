Basketball.  North Eastern Association

‘A’ Grade	Burra 23 defeated Hallett 17

Best and Fairest in the Association: ‘A’ Grade, Sheila Sullivan and ‘B’ Grade, Judy Willmott.

Burra Footballers’ Cricket Club held its AGM last Thursday and elected: Patron, L.A. Beinke; President, P. Pritchard; Vice-President, K. Murphy and Secretary & Treasurer, K.I. Wall.
