7 April

A Holden sedan driven by M.E. Dempsey, a shearer of Clare, collided with a Vauxhall sedan driven by Mr R.F. Keister of Seaton Park at 6.30 p.m. on the Burra-Farrell Flat Road in dusty conditions.
