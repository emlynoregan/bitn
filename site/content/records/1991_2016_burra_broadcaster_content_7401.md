Burra CWA held its AGM recently.  Elected: President, Barbara Francis; Vice Presidents, Celine Stockman & Eunice Baker; Secretary, Irene Hirschausen; Treasurer, Jean Jamieson.

Membership had grown in 1994-95, with an increased interest in handicrafts.  The branch had celebrated its 69th birthday and Mrs Dorothy Reed had celebrated her 90th.  Mrs Nora Heneker had been made a Life Member.  Meetings had been held at the Activity Centre at Burra North.
