Burra Women’s Branch of the Liberal Party held its AGM in Burra Town Hall Supper Room on 21 June.  Attendance was good and Dr Tonkin, Leader of the Opposition, was the guest speaker.  Member for Frome Mr Claude Allen and Mrs Allen also attended.  Dr Tonkin’s address is summarised at length.
