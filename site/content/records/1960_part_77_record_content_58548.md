Burra Primary School Grade VI children climbed Razorback near Mt Bryan on Saturday 16 October.  Mrs Carpenter and the children hiked seven miles while Mr & Mrs Cain took the bus to the barbecue.  After lunch the boys played football and the girls played softball before climbing the hill, led by Mr Cain.  Some children made sketches and others made jam dampers in the coals.
