Marc’s Meats, formerly JD’s will offer a new dimension to your butchering experience in the Mid North according to the new owner Marc. Van Kleef.  They will offer quality country killed meat as well as smallgoods and cordon bleu lines.  Michael Marschall and Jason Northcott are looking forward to living in Burra and providing customer service.  [Photo.]  The shop has been redecorated.
