Rev. W.F. James contributes an article of 1¾ columns on Memories of a Cornish Village.  The village was Chacewater, some 5 miles west of Truro and also about Salem, a little to the west of Chacewater.  Chacewater had a population of about 1,800 and possessed a brewery and eight hotels.
