Mrs A. White, Mr & Mrs C, White & Mr Max White, all of Porter Lagoon, were farewelled at Hanson Sunday School Hall on Thursday 29 March.  After many years in the town they have left to settle in Adelaide.  As well as appreciative speeches there were items of song, music and elocution.  Mrs C.
