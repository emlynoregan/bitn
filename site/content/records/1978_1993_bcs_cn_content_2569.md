12 June 1980

Burra Senior Citizens visited BCS on Thursday at the invitation of some Year 10 students who had prepared afternoon tea as part of their Home Economics Course.

Jacki Kitchin’s horse safari from Port Augusta to Adelaide raised $60 from fellow students for the Adelaide Children’s Hospital.

The Vietnamese Refugees’ Visit was most successful.

Barry & Mary Culbertson of the Royal Exchange Hotel are to leave Burra soon.

Anne Carpenter last week became the first female in SA to receive a Technical Studies Diploma at Bonython Hall.

Marriage.  Burra Uniting Church last Saturday

Jeannie Brodie married Neville Kelly.

Burra Arts Council’s presentation of Kiss Mr Goodnight Sergeant Major was a great success.

The Arts Council’s presentation for schools of Under Milkwood by Dylan Thomas will be put on for students on Friday 27 June at 9.30 a.m.  Any interested adults are welcome to attend.
