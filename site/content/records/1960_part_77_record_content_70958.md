Oddfellows Hall.  Some months ago the Hall was advertised for sale by tender.  Roche Bros Social Club is now the owner.  By agreement the Burra Burra Lodge and the Table Tennis Club have the use of the Hall.  After renovations and the building of a new toilet block the Hall will be available for hire.
