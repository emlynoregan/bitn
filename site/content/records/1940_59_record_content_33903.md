Sheep Sale.  At Goldsbrough, Mort & Co.’s Off-Shears Sale on Friday 5,528 of the 5,679 yarded were sold and one line of 107 1½-year-old ewes sold at a record £5-3-3 a head for flock sheep two months off shears.
