Pte Alan Kemble was given a social at the Institute on 25 August. He is the son of Mr & Mrs Jack Kemble and was accompanied by his young brother Tpr Mervyn Kemble of the 9/23 Light Horse Regiment, at present in training at Gawler with the Militia.  Mr S.H. Robinson, in moving the vote of thanks to the speakers, mentioned that his son Pte Robinson, who has been reported wounded, was doing well.
