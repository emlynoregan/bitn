Don Bruce of Farrell Flat and his mate Bob Searle of Snowtown have been working at Winnininnie, thirteen miles from Yunta and last week they came across a slab of slate inscribed T J. McInnes 1884, but with the J and 4 reversed.  Was slate ever mined at Winnininnie and who was T.J. McInnes?
