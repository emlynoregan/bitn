Burra: SA History Month

Burra History Group will launch a self-guided walk highlighting some of Kooringa’s historic buildings.

Burra Railway Station will be open for visitors to see the beautifully restored building and its railway history interpretive signage.

Market Square Museum.  There will be the official opening of the new displays at the museum.

St Mary’s Anglican Church will be open daily.  The church, built in 1879 is known for its fine stained glass windows.
