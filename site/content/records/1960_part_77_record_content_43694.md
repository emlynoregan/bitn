Fire Alert Day.  The local EFS unit complete with a full crew drove along the street and demonstrated fire fighting apparatus on 16 January as part of Fire Alert Day.  In the afternoon a fire broke out on Anama Property and private units as well as those from Burra, Booborowie and Spalding attended.
