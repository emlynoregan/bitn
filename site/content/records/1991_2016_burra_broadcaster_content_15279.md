The Season.  SA’s harvest has been a record for the third consecutive year.  Grain received topped 4,922,046 tonnes, compared with last season’s 4,921,236 tonnes.  The season started out poorly, but ended very well.  Grain from the South East is still being delivered and the final figure is expected to be c. 4.96 million tonnes.
