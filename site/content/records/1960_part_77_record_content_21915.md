Burra Golf Associates closed their season on 30 September and held their AGM.  They elected: Patroness, Mrs A.L. Collins; President, Mrs R.K. Pearse; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames T. Padbury & H. Topsfield; Secretary, Mrs E. Jeffery; Assistant Secretary, Mrs A. Wright; Treasurer, Mrs R. Mueller; Captain and Handicap Manager, Mrs M. Nicholls and Vice-Captain, Mrs D.H. Field.
