Miss E.M. Bailey’s property was sold at the RSL Hall on Friday.

Lot 1.  ‘Homestead’ of 423 acres was bought by Messrs S.T. Miller & Son for £49 per acre.

Lot 2.  258 acres went to Broad Bros. for £37 per acre.

Lot 3.  114 acres was bought by C.T. Bailey & Sons for £36 per acre.
