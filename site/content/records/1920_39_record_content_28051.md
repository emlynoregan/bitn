Redruth Manse Trust Oriental Fair was held last Friday in the Institute.  It is reported in 1¼ columns of detail and gross takings were £174.  [Personal interest: Mrs R. Fuss & Miss M. Rogers were convenors of the Flower Stall and Mr R. Fuss erected stalls and assisted with cartage of goods.]
