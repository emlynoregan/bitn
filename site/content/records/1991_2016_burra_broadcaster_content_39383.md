Burra Pony Club.  Three members, Georgia Calaby, Jaide Bass and Brooke Crowell, with Sindy Brown from ‘The Gums’ Pony Club will compete at the State championships in Adelaide later in the year, after qualifying for the Team on the Flat Competition at the Northern Zone Championships, held last weekend in Burra.
