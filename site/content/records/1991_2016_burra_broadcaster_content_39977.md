Burra Yacht Races, 15 November

Waters’ Burra Bakery took the title in all three categories.  The course started by the Skate Park and finished at the lock.  About 200 spectators urged on contestants [7 photos on page 8.]

Results:

Junior	Ruby & Cher Waters won from Mackenzie Williams and Reon James.

Ladies’	Ruby Waters and Iris Park won from Sharee O’Bryan and Billy Jo Bass.

Open	Chris Waters and Brett Wallis won from Sharee O’Bryan and Billy Jo Bass.

Novelty Prize went to Sam Quinn and Will Brooks.
