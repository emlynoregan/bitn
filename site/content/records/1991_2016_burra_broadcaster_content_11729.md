Letter.  Richard Nicholls of the Jailbreakers Committee writes to inform the community of his election as President with Caroline stockman as Secretary and Simon Drew as Treasurer.  The next Jailbreakers Ball will be held on 19 April 1997.  The committee has been able to hand over $3,900 to local sporting bodies.
