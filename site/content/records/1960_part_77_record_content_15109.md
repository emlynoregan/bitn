Burra Football Club held its AGM 26 February at Burra Town Hall.  It resolved to stay in the NEFL for the coming season.  The club will support a special meeting called by the War Memorial Committee to discuss oval improvements. John Ayliffe was appointed Captain-Coach.  He is a teacher at Burra High School.  Elected: Patron, Mr Reg. Warnes; President, Mr L.A. Beinke; Vice-President, Mr E. Orchard and Secretary-Treasurer, Mr D. Zanker.
