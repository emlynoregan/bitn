Fowl Fanciers

There is ¾ column on Burra fowl fanciers, who were notably J.D. Cave and G. Lehman, but also included C. Schutz, P.L. Killicoat, F.W. Holder, Mr Rabbich, M. Rayner, Mr Warburton & A. Loutit.
