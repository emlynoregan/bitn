Porter’s Lagoon Boating Carnival by the Outboard Motorboat Club of SA was quite successful with gate takings of £75.  (It was run in conjunction with the Demobilised Soldiers’ Assoc.)  Mr Lance Fiebig of Burra won two events.  As well as races there were boat rides and water skiing.  (Results are not to hand.)
