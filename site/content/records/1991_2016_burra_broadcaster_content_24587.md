The Deer Farming Field Day at Ryan’s Deer Farm on 18 February attracted 25 people, of whom six are interested in going into deer farming.  Glynne Ryan keeps his herd essentially for harvesting the velvet.  This is sold to Asian markets for the pharmaceutical industry, where it can fetch up to $135 per kilo.
