Christmas arrangements in Burra include late shopping on 24 December, a free picture show in the Town Hall at 6 p.m. (Flipper and the Pirates) and the arrival of Father Christmas in Market Square at 8.30 p.m.  The merry-go-round will operate near the Burra Hotel.
