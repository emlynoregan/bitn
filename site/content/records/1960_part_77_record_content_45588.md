Accident.  Miles Cockington aged 2 was knocked down by a car in Best Place on Saturday morning, but escaped serious injury, suffering concussion and lacerations.  He ran across the road into the path of a car driven by Mr David Loffler of Burra North.  Fortunately the car was travelling slowly.
