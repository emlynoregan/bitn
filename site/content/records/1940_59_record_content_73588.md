Burra Rifle Club held its AGM 7 July.  The Captain reported a successful year.  During the year there was a record number of possibles shot and Mr J.H .Schwier and Mr J. Brown equalled the range record.  There were return visits to Gawler and Clare and the club visited the Dean Rifle Range.  J.H. Schwier was successful in the Champion of Champions shoot fired at the Spalding Range.  H. Byles won the Marksmanâ€™s Trophy and the Club Championship, when he returned after an absence of some years.  Ron Kellock also returned.

Elected were: Captain, J. Brown; Vice-Captain, M.E. Mitchell; Secretary, R.G .Bernhardt and Armourer, J.H. Schwier.

For the coming year Syd Williams has donated the trophy to be known as the Darton Cottage Trophy.
