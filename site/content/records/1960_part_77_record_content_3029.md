Burra Women’s Branch of the LCL held its AGM on 1 July in the Town Hall.  An address was given by Mrs K.C. Wilson OBE.  Elected: President, Mrs R.B. Pitcher; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames A.L. Kellock, G.S. Hawker & J. Stevens; Secretary, Mrs J.W. Riggs and Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.
