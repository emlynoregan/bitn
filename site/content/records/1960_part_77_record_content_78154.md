Burra H.S. Parents & Friends’ Association.  Report by Shirley Thamm.

The seven meetings had an average attendance of 14.  Principal Miss Pat Payne proved to be friendly and maintained good communications with the parent body.  Profits from the School Sports Day were $240 and the High School Ball made a profit of $252.  Other income of $489 allowed a donation of $600 to be made for matriculation books.  $200 was donated for outdoor seating, $50 for indoor games and $50 for the mower fund.  The bank balance is presently $283 with a further $800 in deposit stock.
