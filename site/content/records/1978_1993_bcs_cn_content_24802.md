Burra Swimming Pool will be opened from Saturday 14 November.

On weekends and school holidays from 1.20 to 5.30 p.m.

Weekdays from 2.30 to 5.30 p.m.

These times apply when forecast temperature for Port Pirie is 25°C and above and if the forecast temperature is 30°C and above also from 6.30 to 9.30 p.m.
