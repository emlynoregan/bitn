Greening Urban SA project will run across the state and in Burra it will begin with a major effort to clean up the Burra creek adjacent to the Best Place business area.  Council will receive funds of $40,500 from the program and three people will be employed through the SA Local Government Group Training Scheme for a total of 39 weeks.
