Burra Golf Club held its AGM on 30 October.  Elected: President, M.G. Stockman; Vice-Presidents, Messrs J. Gordon & Bob Mason; Secretary, Roy Jefferies; Treasurer, M. Graham Mudge; Captain, H. Strawbridge & Vice-Captain, J. Bogisch.  The President reported on a successful year.  The open day attracted 106 nominations and was played in perfect weather.  The Club Championship was won by Stan Longford for the 15th time in 18 years.
