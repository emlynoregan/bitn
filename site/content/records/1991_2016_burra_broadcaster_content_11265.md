Burra Police Celebrate 150 years

To mark this event the Band of the SA Police will visit Burra on 26 November for a series of concerts.

In the daytime they will play for senior citizens and school children and in the evening will perform at Redruth Gaol.  There will also be a display of SA Police memorabilia at the Gaol.  SA Police horses will patrol the streets, with memorabilia at the Old Police stables & Lock-up.
