Burra Community School students have been raising money for the Victorian Bushfire Relief Fund.  On Friday a casual dress day raised $255.  Donations collected in Market Square raised $300 and all this activity with donations from letters to parents and collecting on 20 February raised about $2,000.

[Photo of Rohan Maidment, Chris Dix, teacher Gus Wenzel and Matt Connors from the Year 10 Geography class collecting donations last Friday.]
