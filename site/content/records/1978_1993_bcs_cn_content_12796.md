Rosie Lloyd has been chosen to represent Australia at a Junior Inter-Country Competition in China in August.  To do this she has to raise her fare of $2,000 plus expenses.  A street stall has been organised for Friday 18 July.  Any help in making or buying a cake would be appreciated.
