Mr & Mrs Frank Arnold were given a farewell at Hanson Hall on 14 March by members of the Hanson Agricultural Bureau, the CWA and residents of the Leighton District.  Mr Ron Shipard was MC.  Dancing was interspersed with other items, games and speeches.  The evening ended with a delicious supper.
