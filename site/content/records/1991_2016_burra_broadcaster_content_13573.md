Goyder Council.  The rates for 1997-98 were declared.

The total rates raised this rear have been reduced by 3% in each ward.

Capital valuation was $364,775,200, which was an increase of 5.4% on last year.  Total rate revenue will be $1,832,833 out of a total budget of $4.87 million.  There will be a fixed charge of $105 for each assessment.

Rates in the dollar:	Hallett	0.3151

Burra Town	0.7453

Burra Rural	0.4603

Robertstown	0.3570

Eudunda Town	0.4005

Eudunda Rural	0.3522

This is in line with achieving rate parity within ten years.
