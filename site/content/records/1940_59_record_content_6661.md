Mr A Mrs J.G. Rooke have celebrated their Golden Wedding.  They were married at the now demolished Baldina Church 16 July 1891.    Mrs Rooke was the eldest daughter of the late Mr & Mrs Kiekebusch of Baldina.  All of their six children attended the celebrations except for their eldest daughter Hadie, Mrs W. Davis, who lives in South Africa.  Present were: Dorothea, Mrs Albert J. Bruce; Sister Wanda Rooke of Adelaide; Mr Theodore Rooke and his wife; Mr Eugene Rooke and his wife and Mr Herbert Rooke.
