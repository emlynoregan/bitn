Swimming.  The Open Swimming Carnival at Burra on Sunday was not well attended due to the extreme heat.  Some also left when new of the fire between Clare and Farrell Flat was received.  The carnival itself went relatively well.  For Burra Garry Hirschausen, Sandra Williams and Jill Eberhard gained trophies.
