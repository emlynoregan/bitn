Mr R.C. Sexton, Manager of the Savings Bank for 6 years, leaves today for Strathalbyn.  In 1934 he had six months leave of absence due to ill health.  He has taken an active interest in the RSL while here, having served terms as President, Treasurer and a committeeman.  He was farewelled by fellow bank officers on Tuesday morning.
