Advt.  Dalgety’s & Elder, Smith’s will conduct a land sale at Mt Bryan Institute 18 April on instructions from F.H. Treweeke and trustees in the estate of E.M. Murray.

Lot 1.	Sections 554, 555, 560, Pt section 561, Pt section 562, Hd Ayers of 701 acres

Sections 552 & 553 Hd Ayers of 379 acres, 300 acres of which is arable.

Lot 2.	Sections 114 & 2539 Hd Ayers of 799 acres (200 arable) 6 miles northwest of Mt Bryan.
