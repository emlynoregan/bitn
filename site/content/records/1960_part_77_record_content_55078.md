North Eastern Football Association.  The annual report by President W.B. Ashby.

The Association lost to Gawler League in the 1st match of the Central Districts Carnival, but defeated old rivals Mid North on the Monday.  We beat Northern Areas on 4 July at Booborowie and won comfortably, and were then defeated by Broughton Football League on 25 July.

Congratulations to Russell Nicholls for winning the Mail Medal and to Brendan Kluska as runner-up and winning the Burra Record Trophy.  Congratulations to Burra for winning the Premiership for 1970.  The Junior Colts Shield went to Booborowie.

[Thanks were extended to various people, especially yo Tony Fillmore for his work as Secretary-Treasurer.]
