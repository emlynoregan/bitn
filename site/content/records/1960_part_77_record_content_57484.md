M & BHA held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs R. Stockman; Vice-President, Mrs S. Allen; Secretary, Mrs P.F. Hall; Secretary, Mrs L. Reed & Treasurer, Mrs J. Hirschausen.  The branch has 21 financial members.

The average attendance for the first half was 10, but then fell away to be very poor, being below quorum for one meeting, with just four present.  Sister Mudge resigned in May and Sister Conlon took her place.
