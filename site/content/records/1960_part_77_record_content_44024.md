Notice.  Mr Peter Christoforou announces he has closed his Men’s Hairdressing business at Burra North.  Goods remaining in the shop will be sold at half price.

Rod Blake formerly of Elders-GM has commenced work at Burra Rural Supplies as a sales representative.  He has been in Burra for 2½ years.

Bill Wood has taken up duties at the Booborowie School.
