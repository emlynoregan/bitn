Booborowie & Burra Quilters have received $4,500 from the Department of Community & Ageing for their important work as a community organisation and social network.  Club President, Maureen Fuss, received the cheque from Booborowie Primary School principal Ronnie Alderman.  The club, which has been going for about 20 years, meets fortnightly at the Booborowie Hall.
