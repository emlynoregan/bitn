Mt Bryan will celebrate the Centenary of Federation next Saturday.  A logo of Mt Bryan will be unveiled in Sir Hubert Wilkins Park by Kaylene Jaensch (nee Cockrum) who designed it.  There will be games and a decorated bike competition and a barbecue from 5.30 p.m.  There will be an ecumenical church service on Sunday at 10 a.m.
