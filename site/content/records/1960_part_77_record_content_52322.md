The Annual Rural Youth Deb of the North Ball was held at Spalding Hall on Friday 24 July. Twenty-two debs were presented to Miss SA Charity Queen Elizabeth Litchfield.  Miss Marjorie Wilson of Pt Broughton was Deb of the North for 1970.
