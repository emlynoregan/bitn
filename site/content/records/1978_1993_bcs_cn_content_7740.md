The Sportsman’s Dinner at the Sports Complex on 20 April was a huge success.  120 people attended.  The guest speaker was Mr Neil Balme, Norwood Football Club coach, accompanied by Mr Peter Pritchard, Burra Football Club coach 1961-62.  This was the first function held for the general community in the new complex and the local support given was very pleasing.
