25 January 1989

BCS Boarding House

Boarders from remote places will be moving in on 30 January.  Graham & Wendy Heness have been appointed supervisors.  The house is catering for 7-day boarders and 5-day boarders; the latter travelling home for the weekends.  Enquiries have already been made for 1990 and beyond.
