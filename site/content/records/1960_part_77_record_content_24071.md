SA Annual Merino Field Days drew interest from all around Australia.  WA graziers were particularly interested.  An East Bungaree ram went to Northampton WA for 2,000 guineas.  A Koonoona stud ram went to F. Mosey of Robertstown for 1,500 guineas.  W.B. Ashby & Sons sold a ram to WA for 2,000 guineas.  Crowds were a record.
