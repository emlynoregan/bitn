Council has called a meeting to ratify a constitution and to elect officers to the Burra War memorial Oval Management Committee.

The Council on 9 July adopted the Government Assessment of unimproved land values for the whole Council area as the Assessment for 1979-80.

A General Rate of 0.85c in the dollar was declared with additional differential rates as follows:

24c in the dollar for Booborowie, Mt Bryan and Hanson.

12c in the dollar for Farrell Flat.

3.6c in the dollar for Burra

Minimum rates are set at $50 on rural assessments, $75 for Farrell Flat, Booborowie, Hanson and Mt Bryan and $85 for Burra.

In Burra a separate rate was declared for properties served by the Effluent Drainage Scheme:

A minimum of $24 for vacant allotments.

$40-$50 for household units and other premises on a proportional basis.
