Back to Burra Schools

This event at the weekend of 21-22 October 1995 is expected to attract up to 1,000 former students and teachers.  A number of dinners have been arranged for Saturday evening.

On Sunday morning at 11.30 a.m. an assembly will be held at the school – taken by Mr Colin Morrison, past teacher at World’s End Primary and at Burra High Schools.  Mr Ray Jennison will speak on behalf of old scholars and Mr Graham Kellock as Chairman of the District Council.

School memorabilia will be displayed in the library.

St Joseph’s School building will have memorabilia of Mary MacKillop.

Information from senior citizens has been compiled into a booklet and a bus tour to district school sites has also been arranged.

Lobethal Harmony Choir will give a concert in the Uniting Church Hall on Saturday 21 October at 8 p.m.
