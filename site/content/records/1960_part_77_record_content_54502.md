Bowls.  The Burra Club will open its new clubhouse on 31 January.  Senior Vice-President of the Royal SA Bowling Association Mr Les. Luke will perform the opening.  At the last committee meeting Mr Charlie Dare resigned as supervisor of the greens after many years.
