BCS at the Jamestown Show.  Kimberly Mitchell and Matt Drennan took their school steer Bourbon to the Jamestown Show.  The steer placed:

1st for best prepared steer

2nd for best trade steer

2nd for open led steer

3rd in medium weight for carcase on the hook.

Kimberly placed 2nd for best handler.  [Photo.]
