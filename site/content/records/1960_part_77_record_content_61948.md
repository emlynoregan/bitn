Accident.  A semi-trailer rolled over about four miles east of Burra on Friday while travelling towards Morgan.  The vehicle was carrying two industrial tractors when a chain broke and the load shifted.  Damage to the vehicle was estimated at $4,000 and to the tractors as $500.
