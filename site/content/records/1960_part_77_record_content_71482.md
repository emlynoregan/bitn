Obituary.  Pearl Newland died suddenly 30 May 1974.  She was the elder daughter of the late Mr & Mrs W.A. Gebhardt of Burra and only sister of Lil, Mrs H. Byles.

[Born Pearl Gebhardt 22 May 1906 Kooringa.  Her first husband was Frederick K.G. Boase who died in 1940.]
