Rev. R.S. Jenner was inducted as Methodist Minister for the Burra Circuit on 20 January.  He comes from Minlaton to replace Rev. D.G. Haydon who has gone to Broken Hill.  Rev. Wilmes [Wilms previously] officiated and Rev. Hayward of Crystal Brook gave the address.
