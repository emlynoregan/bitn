11 April 1990

BCS.  On 6 April Patsy, Fatcat and Humbolt gave a performance to an audience in excess of 500 adults and children.

Schoolboys & Schoolgirls Track & Field Championships were held in Adelaide last Saturday.

Cheryl Day was 1st in the U16 400 m, and 3rd in the U16 100 m.
