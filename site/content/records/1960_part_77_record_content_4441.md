Accident.  At cricket at Burra Oval on Saturday Jim Richards of Burra and R. Keany of Koonoona collided while attempting to take a catch.  After examination by Dr L.G. Miller Jim was transferred to the RAH suffering from a broken nose, and a fractured cheek and jawbone.
