The Goyder Council recently had a visit from Rockhampton Regional Council Deputy Mayor, Rose Swadling & Cr Sandra O’Brien.  The visit was to discuss the history of the twin town relationship between Goyder and the former Shire of Mt Morgan (now incorporated in the Rockhampton Regional Council.  The relationship began in 2001 based on the two centres’ mining heritage.
