2014 In Review

January

Australia Day Breakfast at Pickett Reserve

Citizen of the Year was Angie Webster.

Junior Citizen of the year was Hannah Farrelly

Event of the Year was the Burra Show.

Heat wave with temperatures over 40ºC for several days in succession.

February

‘The Water Diviner’ was filmed in Burra and district.

Monster Mine Spectacular was held in record high temperatures, featuring Adelaide Band ‘Flirt’ and Kerry Davy.
