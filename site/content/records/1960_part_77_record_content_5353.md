Cricket

Koonoona 113 defeated Burra 82.

Burra 81 defeated Farrell Flat 64.

Burra Small Bore Club.  Over 25 yards Keith Spackman was 1st with Bill Barker best off the rifle.

Burra Rifle Club fired the 9th stage of the E.S. Williams Trophy over 600 & 700 yards.  Best were G. Kakoschke, M. Stolte & R. Francis.
