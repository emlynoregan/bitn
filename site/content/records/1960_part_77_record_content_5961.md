Burra Rifle Club fired the 11th stage of the annual trophy over double 800 yards.  There were four possible scored by W. Hempel, E. Hopkins, W. Barker and G. Webster.  Best off the rifle was Bill Hempel and best with handicap were W. Hempel, W. Barker and H. Byles.
