Marriage.  Next Saturday at the Presbyterian Church Mansfield, Victoria

Jenni Brond, a sister at Burra Hospital and from Mansfield, Victoria will marry

Trevor Wall, son of Mr & Mrs Johnny Wall of Burra North.  The couple will live in Burra.
