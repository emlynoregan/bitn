Kooringa & Redruth Methodist Sunday Schools held a combined demonstration last Saturday & Sunday.  There was a teachersâ€™ conference with a demonstration by the Primary Department at 4 p.m. on Saturday and then a tea.  At 5.30 p.m. the teachers and officers enjoyed a bright hour.  There was a public meeting on Sunday school matters in the evening.  Sunday morning saw a service at Redruth and a combined service at 3 p.m. in the Institute for Kooringa, Redruth, Hanson, Copperhouse and Ironmine scholars.  There was an evening service at Kooringa.
