1 April 1982

BCS Sports Day was held on 25 March.  Steele won the House Shield for the fifth time in seven years.

Champions [Photo.]

U13 Boys	Richard Mudge & Paul Nation

U13 Girls	Joanne Brooks

U14 Boys	Gary Charles

U14 Girls	Rosalie Lloyd

U15 Boys	Greg Wall

U15 Girls	Tracey Beinke

Open Boys 	Andrew Page & Trevor Kessell

Open Girls	Louise Hall

[Records set on the day are listed.]

BCS School Council.  Elected: Chairman, Ron Thamm; Deputy Chairman, Rod Bailey; Secretary, Edith Carpenter and Treasurer, Peter Hall.

Burra Youth Group AGM elected President, Bronwyn Steele, Vice-Presidents, David Hill & Tim Beinke; Secretary, Sally Hayes and Treasurer, Teresa Schwier.

BCS Film Club, 15 April

Christopher Reeve & Margot Kidder in Superman II (1981)

Natalie Simpson has been awarded a silver medal in the Duke of Edinburgh Award Scheme.

Burra Lions Club Auction last Sunday was successful.

At the Interschool Swimming Carnival recently Burra came third behind Eudunda and Kapunda.

Burra Cubs & Scouts spent the weekend of 19-21 March at Woodhouse in the Adelaide Hills.
