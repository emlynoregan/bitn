Jason Coley, David Merrill & Tracy Starr are the new owners of the Kooringa Hotel.  David and Tracy have two daughters, Brooke (13) and Jessica (10).  David previously lived in Burra for some years in the mid-1980s and returns after some time in Perth.  Jason comes from Adelaide.  [Photo of David & Tracy.]
