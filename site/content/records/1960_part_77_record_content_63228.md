Burra CWA conferred life membership on Mrs Rets Jennison at their meeting on 21 September.  The Life Membership Badge and Citation were presented by Divisional President Mrs Aitken of Clare.  Mrs Jennison has been a member for over 30 years, has been Branch President for four terms, served as Vice-President for many years and has been secretary several times, as well as being active in a number of other roles.
