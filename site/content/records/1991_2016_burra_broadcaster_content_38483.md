Diprotodon Fossils

A display of Diprotodon fossils and information boards is to be set up by the SA Museum and Associate Professor Rod Wells of Flinders University in the Foyer of the Regional Council of Goyder’s Offices, at Burra.  Hon. Jane Lomax-Smith, Minister for Tourism has been invited to open the display on 10 January 2006.
