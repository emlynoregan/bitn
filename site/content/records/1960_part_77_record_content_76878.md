Burra Golf Associates held their AGM 6 October and elected: President, Mrs N. Finch; Senior Vice-President, Mrs J. Gordon; Junior Vice-President, Mrs C. Neill; Secretary, Mrs M. Nicholls; Treasurer, Mrs D. Martin; Captain, Mrs J. Oates and Vice-Captain, Mrs E. Jeffery. There are 36 members and the Secretary reported a successful year with the opening of the new clubhouse as the outstanding event.
