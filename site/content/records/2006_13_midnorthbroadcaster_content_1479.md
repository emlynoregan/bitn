Royal Adelaide Show Sheep Sales

Local sheep studs did well:

Greenfields Hallett sold 6 rams for an average of $8,500 and a top of $12,500.

East Bungaree achieved a top price of $10,000 and an average of $4,300.

Leahcim Poll Merinos had a top of $10,000 and an average of $5,250.
