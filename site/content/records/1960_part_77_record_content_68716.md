Obituary.  Ivon Ernest Wohling died at the repatriation Hospital on 24 October aged 58.  He was the husband of Elva and father of Margaret, Maxine, Leslie, Alison & Karen.  He was the second son of the late Ernest (Curly) Wohling and Laura Wohling and brother to Ken, Melva & Betty.  [Born 7 July 1915 Kooringa.]
