1968 will be the last year for both the Internal and Externally Examined Intermediate Certificate.

Prefects for 1968:

Girls: Elaine Broderick (Head), Beverley Collins, Lynette Griffen, Janet Harris, Alison Miller and Rosemary Ryan.

Boys: Christopher freeman (Head), Phillip Byles, Brian Josephs, Roger Przibilla, Andrew Ryan and Dennis Short.

Willalo School has 21 students; the same as in 1967.  [New students are named.]

Mr B.R. Smith continues on as Head Teacher.

Leighton School has two more students this year, with a total of 28.  [New students are named.]

Mr D. Munzberg is the Head Teacher.

Booborowie School, with an enrolment of 63, is down 3 on last year.  Mr J. Kerin is Head Teacher, assisted by Mrs E. Brooks.

Telephone.  A new trunk line between Burra and Mt Bryan will be in service from 20 February.

Des Woolley of Jennison’s Tyre Service has won the Ford Motor Co.’s Pacemaker’s Award for the third year in succession.  He averaged four vehicle sales per week throughout the year.
