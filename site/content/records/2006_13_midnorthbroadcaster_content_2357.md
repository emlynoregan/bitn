Stephen Redden is the new assistant building surveyor for Goyder Council.  He has a Bachelor of Construction Management and Economics from UniSA and had worked in construction at Murray Bridge before being development officer with the City of Pt Lincoln Council for four years.  He has a wife, Linda, a daughter Holly (3) and a son Justin (1).
