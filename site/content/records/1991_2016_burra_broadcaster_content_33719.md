Andrew Glen of the Burra Visitor centre has initiated a new driving trail through the Lower Mid North Region.  The Heartland Trail encourages visitors to visit Gawler, Kapunda, Eudunda, Burra, Mintaro, Auburn and Two Wells.  It allows also for side visits to Freeling, Robertstown, Point Pass, Farrell Flat, Balaklava and Mallala.
