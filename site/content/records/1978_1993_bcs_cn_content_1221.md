Frank McMahon, a former student at Burra School, has released an anthology of poems entitled Tide Pools.  Among the collection there are eight poems concerning Burra:

Chapel Street

Burra

Two Cornish Engine Houses

Epitaph – Burra

Rosemary

World’s End Creek, Burra

The Citadel

Burra Colours

Frank, who visited Burra recently, works in Canberra with the CSIRO.  A copy of the volume has been donated to the Community Library.
