Advt.  On instructions from Mr A.C. & Mrs J.R. Ford, who are relinquishing their grazing interests, Elders-GM will auction ‘Basin Farm’ comprising 1,006 acres freehold at the Burra RSL Hall 28 April.

The Land comprises Sections 156, 142, 132 & Pt Section 1 Hundred of Kooringa and includes a stone homestead of 8 rooms etc.  ‘Basin Farm’ has been in the Ford Family for over 100 years and is close to the Burra Township, silos and saleyards.  It is suited to wool and cereal or stud purposes.  400 acres are arable and it enjoys a safe rainfall.
