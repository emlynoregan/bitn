The Burra Arts Council has arranged for the Tea Rose Duo to play at Burra on Saturday in the Old Courthouse.  They are a piano & violin duo, Bernard & Sarah Depasquale, based in Adelaide and who play a broad range of music from Bach to Pink Floyd.  ($15.)
