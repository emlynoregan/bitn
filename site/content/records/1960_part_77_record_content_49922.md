Burra Institute Library.  Annual Meeting

The President P.J. Byrne reported for the year.  The committee began in credit $10.11 and ended in credit $63.31.

Membership fell slightly.  Mr Perc. Byrne and Mrs Byrne will soon leave Burra to reside in the city.  He will be missed by the Library Committee, with which he has been associated for the past fourteen years.
