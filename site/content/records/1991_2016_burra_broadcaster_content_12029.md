Mary Woollacott caught up with Alan Baulderstone at his Burnside Village Fruit & Veg store recently.  His wife Marcia helps out there on a regular basis and their elder son Philip is running the Silver Fruit Bowl in North Adelaide.  Daughter Chris works with the CIB and son David has completed his intern year at Flinders Medical centre and moves to the Women’s and Children’s Hospital next year to follow his interest in paediatrics.
