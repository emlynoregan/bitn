Wendy Douglas & Louise Krause have launched ‘Offbeat’ which displays a wide range of local arts and crafts in their large bluestone building behind Drew & Crewes’ Building.  Works include lithographs, solid furniture, creative photography, handiwork, smocking, woolly products, pottery and metal art.
