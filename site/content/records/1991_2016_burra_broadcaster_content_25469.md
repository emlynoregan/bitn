Burra Penguin Club hosted members from the Clare group last Tuesday for a special event with a ‘Be Brave’ theme.  State President Mrs Rhonda Bannister was a special guest.  The evening began with a cemetery tour conducted by Barbara Wallis and the meeting followed at Pearce’s Buildings.  Several members and the State President spoke.
