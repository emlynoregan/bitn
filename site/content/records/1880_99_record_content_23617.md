Burra Burra Mine.  Six-monthly report.  Repairs to buildings have cost £211-0-5, and other expenses £627-11-2.  Income has been £1,092-14-8.  The balance in the account is £70,102-0-9.  In this period some leases have been granted and about 0.75 acres of land in Kooringa have been sold.  A surface pitch has been let to two tributers with them to receive ⅔ and SAMA ⅓ of the ore obtained.  They have been working for two months for a fairly good return.
