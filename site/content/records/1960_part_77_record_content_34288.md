Burra National Trust has been given the minute book of the original Burra Automobile Club from Mr Reg. Warnes.  It lists members present at one of the early meetings held in Landseer’s Store at Morgan on 10 February 1908.  The club had two sections: Car and Motor Cycle.  The book ends early in 1914.
