Burra Red Cross held its 49th AGM in the Mayor’s Parlour on 11 July at 2.30 p.m.  In the year a record sum of $301.26 had been raised for Red Cross purposes.  Elected: President, Mrs Crump; Vice-Presidents; Mesdames Baulderstone & R. Pearse; Secretary, Mrs J. Murphy and Treasurer, Mrs A. Wright.  [Details in c. 1 column.]
