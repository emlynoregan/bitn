The Burra Research Award was presented by Mr Tom Perry, former Chairman of the Burra Community Library Board of Management on Sunday 2 December.  The winner was Bernie Fahey ($300) and second was Mandy Scott.  Mr Colin Fidock then opened the extension to the Local History Room.
