Burra Small Bore Club.  The season’s Trophy Shoot has been completed:

Ross Gilbert won the Championship and came second in the Handicap Contest.

He won the Marksman’s Trophy, was second in the early aggregate and won the second half-yearly aggregate.

John Bowden won the Handicap Trophy and was second off the rifle.

Don Griffiths won the yearly aggregate and the first half-yearly aggregate, coming second in the Marksman’s Trophy.
