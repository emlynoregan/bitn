The Hampel Family will leave soon for Cummins where Graham has been transferred with Dalgety Bennett Farmers.

Senior Citizens held their AGM on Monday 4 July and elected: President, May Fahlbusch; Vice-President, Len Hentschke; Secretary, Artley Josephs and Treasurer, Audrey Little.

Mt Torrens Ringers & the Bedford Family Ringers will present a special concert in St Mary’s Hall at 7.30 p.m. on 20 July.

Advt.  Steve Woolley advises he has resigned from the SA Police and commenced business with Gerry Baulderstone in the video store 17 Commercial St, Burra.  Nearly 800 video tapes for hire.  Also selling a wide range of cameras and accessories and available as a photographer for weddings, parties, etc.  Colour photograph processing.

Burra Community Library Board invites you to enter their 7th Birthday Literary Competition.

Theme – impressions of your youth.  Prizes for poetry and prose in three age groups: Junior 9-12, Senior 13-16 and Adult 17+.  (Ages as of 1 August 1985.)
