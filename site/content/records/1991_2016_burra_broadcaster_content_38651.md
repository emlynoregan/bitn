Australia Day festivities at Thomas Pickett Reserve were attended by more than 300 people.

Anne Finch, Jan Tiver and Betty Thamm were recognised for 35 years contribution of service to Meals on Wheels and Margaret Dunstall & Nancy Simpson for 25 years, while Len Simpson, Clare Taylor and Avis Webster were acknowledged for 20 years and Enid Hill for 15 years.

The Leisure Activity Group was awarded Community Event of the year for their 60th anniversary of the End of WWII.

Jake Norris was Young Citizen of the Year.

John Burge was Australia Day Citizen of the Year.
