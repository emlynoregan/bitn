Accident.  A mini-sedan overturned on the road near Florieton on Saturday at about 10 a.m. and was discovered by Messrs Ron Thamm and John Taylor.  The 21-year-old driver was a school teacher of Berri who was travelling to Burra to visit her parents, who began working at Princess Royal yesterday.  She was shocked and bruised, but otherwise uninjured.
