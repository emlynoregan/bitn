Bosco Schpinesky’s Troupe, featuring Bosco Schpinesky’s Séance, 
Johnny, the Descriptive Irish vocalist and Charlie the Serious Individual, with 
Dick with Rheumatics, Ruth the Scotch Bard, Sue the six-year-old Wonder, 
Zacky Jones & Zacky Martin, Brown the Tragedian, and Johnson the Invisible. 
Part 1: Songs, Duets & Trios with Irish Comicalities 
Part 2: Legerdemain — Astounding Feats of Prestidigitation
