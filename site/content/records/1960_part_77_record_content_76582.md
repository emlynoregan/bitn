Burra Tennis Association held its AGM and reported a successful year.  There were 8 ‘A’ teams, 12 ‘B’ teams and 7 junior teams for 1975-76.  Elected: President, Alistair Murray (Hallett); Vice-Presidents, G. Matters (Spalding) & John Lucas (Hallett) and Secretary, Margaret Hillman (Mt Bryan).
