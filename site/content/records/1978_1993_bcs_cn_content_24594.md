Burra’s 115th Show [actually the 100th] was held on Saturday in cool windy conditions.  The Horses-in-Action had phenomenal entries with almost 700 and three rings in operation throughout the day.  Yard Dog Trials were added this year and drew fifteen entries.  Dog Jumping and a Wool Handling Competition also attracted the spectators.  [Major prize winners are listed.]

Showgirls were:

Senior	Melissa Brenton

Junior	Skye Nicholls

Sub-Junior	Rachel Riggs
