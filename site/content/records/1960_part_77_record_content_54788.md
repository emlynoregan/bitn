District School Enrolments 1971

Burra Primary School enrolment for 1970 was 236 and for 1971 is 268.  Miss Barbara Hogan joins the staff and Mrs Pens returns to it.  [New students are listed.]

Class sizes: VII, 32; VI, 39; V, 34; IV, 42; III, 34; II 33, Special ungraded, 21; I 37.

Burra High School numbers are unchanged from the start of 1970 at 174.  There are five new staff members:

Mr Moyle and Misses Kussadias & Urlwin have left.

Miss Purins comes as Senior Mistress from Pt Pirie.

Mr Langham comes from Glengowrie.

Mr Costello, a former Burra High School Student, Miss Baron and Miss Helbig all come from Teachers College.

[New enrolments are listed.]

Mt Bryan School opened in 1970 with 26 and this year has 29.  [New students are listed.]

The Head Teacher is Mr Thompson.

Booborowie School.  The Head Teacher is Mr Ron Blackwell with Mrs Blackwell and Mrs T. Brooks.  In 1970 there were 41 students and in 1971 there are 66.  [New students are listed.]

Leighton School.  Head Teacher is Mr Ogden with Mrs Ogden.  There are 29 students, an increase of 2 on 1970.

[New students are listed.]

Hallett School.  Head Teacher is Mr Whittington with Misses Polmear & Johnson.  The enrolment has risen by 1 since 1970 to reach 67.  [New students are listed.]

Willalo School.  The Head Teacher is Mrs Harvey from the Permanent Relieving Staff.  Enrolments have fallen by three to 15.  [New students are listed.]

Farrell Flat School.  Head Teacher is Mr R. Hurrell with Miss A.B. Schuyler.  In 1970 there were 25 pupils and in 1971 there are 29.  [The new student in Grade I is named.]
