Burra Art Experience Weekend, 17-18 June

There will be five separate exhibitions:

S.T. Gill Exhibition at Morphett’s Enginehouse.

‘Burra & Beyond’, the Marie & Allyson Parsons Exhibition at the Burra Art Gallery.

The Regional Art Society’s exhibition at the Old Bible Christian Chapel, Paxton Square.

Burra Arts Council exhibition at the former Burra Record Office.

Works by Russell Morrison at the Old Courthouse.
