The Barker Homes have been completed and are fully occupied.  The Board of Management plans an official opening on 27 May when Miss Priscilla Brown, niece of the late John Barker will perform the ceremony.  At Present finishing work being done includes bituminising carports and areas behind homes and a retaining wall.  Occupants are:

Mr A. Lucas	Mrs L. Watson	Mrs G. Blieschke

Mrs F. Kellock	Mrs J. Lee	Mr H. (Paddy) Cossons

Mrs B. Spackman	Mr A.L. Bence	Mrs D. Malthouse

[Three photographs of the homes and photographs of the residents are published.]

One photograph shows Dudley Jacka, head of D.T. Jacka & Co, the builders, handing the keys of the cottages over to Mr George Hewlett, works supervisor for the architects.
