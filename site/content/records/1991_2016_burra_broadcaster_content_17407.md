Mental Health Week had the title ‘It’s On for Young and Old’ 18-25 October.  Mental Health Workers spoke to students at BCS on Tuesday on bulimia and anorexia.  On Friday in the Burra Town Hall there was a talk on mood disorders.  BCS also made a banner for the week to be displayed in Adelaide at the Festival Theatre.
