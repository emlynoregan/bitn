Marriage.  Amy Wilson, daughter of Terry & Jill Wilson of Burra, married

Simeon Williams, son of Ken Williams of Mt Isa and Janette Godwell of Ingham, Queensland.

The wedding was held on 8 August at ‘Big Red’ near Birdsville, the first and highest of the 1,100 dunes of the Simpson Desert, standing about 36.5 m above sea level.
