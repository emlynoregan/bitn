Mt Bryan Tennis Club end of season wind-up was held on 15 March.  Mr John Quinn was made a Life Member.  All Life Members were present.  [Photo of Life Members: Ron Hillman (1979), Margaret Hillman (1979), Don Gebhardt (1976), John G. Quinn (1984), John L. Quinn (1959) and David Jones (1978).]

[Trophy winners are also listed.

Rosie Lloyd won a silver medal as a member of the State U18 4 x 400 m relay team and came 5th in the U18 400 m hurdles.
