Goyder Council

The new Regional Council of Goyder was launched on 3 February.

Denis Keller of Robertstown was elected its first Chairman.

Graham Kellock, formerly Chairman of the Burra Burra DC was chosen as Deputy Chairman.

Peter Boaden of Hallett became Director of Human Resources.

David Jennings of Eudunda became Director of Corporate Services.

In May there was the first election for the new Council â€“ conducted entirely by postal ballot.

The number of Councillors was reduced from 29 to 13.

There was a 63.8% response to the ballot.

Hallett and Robertstown each elected 2 Councillors, while Eudunda had 4 and Burra 5.

Community Committees are being formed in the major towns of Goyder to assist the Council in running towns.
