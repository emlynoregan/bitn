BCS staged the 3rd Annual Burra Invitation Cross Country Championships last week.  170 runners from 16 secondary schools took part over distances from 2,000 m to 5,000 m.

U13 Boys	Paul Miller of Norwood High School

U13 Girls	Tammy Sleep of St Mark’s, Pt Pirie

U15 Boys	Maurice Taylor of BCS

U15 Girls	Rosalie Lloyd of BCS

Open Boys	Alan Burchell of BCS

Open Girls	Fiona McDonald of Eudunda Area School

In each of the above sections the Team Shield went to BCS except for the Open Boys, which went to St Mark’s, Pt Pirie.
