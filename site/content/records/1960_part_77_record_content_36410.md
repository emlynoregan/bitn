Football.  Trophies were presented at the North Eastern Association Premiership games at Clare on Saturday.

The ‘Ashrose’ Shield went to the Burra Colts.

The Umpires’ Association Medal for the best and fairest in Colts was won by N. Smith of Clare North.

The ANZ Savings Bank Shield for the ‘B’ Grade was won by Clare South.

R. Brandt of Burra won the Gilles Trophy for fairest and most brilliant in ‘B’ Grade.

The Burra Record Trophy went to Don Villis of Booborowie.

The Mobil Oil Trophy and the Mail Medal went to Barry Eberhard of Burra.
