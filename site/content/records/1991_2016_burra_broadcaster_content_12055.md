Meal on Wheels in Burra had 20 volunteers in the year and delivered 2,245 meals.  For 1997 the elected officers are: President, Celine Stockman; Vice-President, Jan Tiver; Secretary, Pat Brereton; Treasurer-Supervisor, Shana Nourse and Welfare Officer, Marg Dunstall.
