Michael & James Honan came to Burra from Fermanagh in Northern Ireland in the Glentanner in 1855 and the Utopia in 1858 respectively, with their wives Margaret and Elizabeth.  There will be a reunion of their descendants in the Hallett Hall on Sunday 10 April.
