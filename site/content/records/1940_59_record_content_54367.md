North-Eastern Football Assoc. met at Booborowie 31 March.

This year competing teams will be: Terowie, Spalding, Leighton, Booborowie, Burra & Hallett.

Elected: President, L. Gill; Secretary, W. Holman; Patron, Mr Craig.

Matches will begin 3 May.  An Association match is organised with Whyalla South at Whyalla for the June Holiday and an Inter-Assoc. match with Balaklava will be played in July.  This yearâ€™s grand final will be played at Burra.
