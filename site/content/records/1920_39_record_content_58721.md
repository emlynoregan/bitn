Byles’ Mongolata GM for the fortnight to 16 November:

Crosscut off the main shaft was opened out at the 200 ft level, drove off the shaft south for 9 ft then turned east.  Cut the seam carrying values by dish sample some 3½ ft from the shaft.  Going east in nice looking lode 18 ft from the line of the shaft.  The lode is 3½ ft wide and showing values.

The battery is still crushing a parcel, having done about 210 tons to date.
