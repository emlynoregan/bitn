Burra Anglican Parish.  Rev. J. Stephenson Priest-in-Charge at Minnipa will visit and be the special preacher at St Andrew’s Mt Bryan on Thursday night for the Patronal Festival.  On Sunday he will be the special preacher for the Patronal Festival at St Edmund’s at Booborowie and he will also preach at Burra and Farrell Flat.  On Sunday night he will assist at the special Advent Carol Service at St Mary’s, Burra.
