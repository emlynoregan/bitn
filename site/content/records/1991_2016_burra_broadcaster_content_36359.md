Lower North Interschool Athletics Carnival was held at Burra on 30 March.  Students competed from Kapunda, Clare, Balaklava, Riverton and Burra.

Kapunda won the Improvers Shield.

Burra won the Handicap Shield.

Burra won the Division 2 Championship.

Clare won the Division 1 Championship.
