Coppertown Ceramics in Morehead Street will open for business this Saturday.  Proprietors are Gail & Stan Hucks.  The shop used to house Pearce’s Hardware and in the nineteenth century was Sara & Dunstan’s sawmill.  A feature of the new shop will be porcelain dolls.
