Burra High School.  The Jubilee History concluded by R.V. Mueller.

1961

The staff comprised R.V. Mueller headmaster, J.D. Sladden, K.G. Hicks, R.D. Hillman, B.I. McElroy, N.I. Lawes, J.E. Bogisch (TVA 0.6), E. McCabe (TVA 0.2) and K. Horsnell (Junior Teacher).  The enrolment was 125.

The Hanson bus run commenced and a new bus was put on the Booborowie run.   The headmaster was absent for six weeks due to illness and Mr J. Sladden was Acting Headmaster.  New pictures worth £50 worth were hung.  Desks were planed and varnished.  Two rain water tanks were installed.  A new Banda duplicating machine was purchased.  £120 was spent on hurdles.  The wood heap was enclosed in a galvanised iron fence and doors were hung on the tank stands.

Tothill Creek School celebrated its centenary on 3 November with a ‘Back-to-School function.
