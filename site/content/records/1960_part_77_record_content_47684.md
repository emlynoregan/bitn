Jack Lines has turned 90.  An item is reprinted from the Barrier Miner of 9 May.

Mr Lines was born in Burra and has been associated with the pastoral industry for 58 years.  He worked as a station hand on Mulyungari Station 70 miles west of Broken Hill.
