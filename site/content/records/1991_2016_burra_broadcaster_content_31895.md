Blyth and Clare Uniting Church Congregations write to the Editor advising that those congregations do not agree with or accept the decision of the National Assembly of the Uniting Church allowing practising homosexuals to be ordained for ministry in the church, believing that to be contrary to Holy Scripture and an assault on the family.
