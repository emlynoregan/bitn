Advt.  Southern Farmers will auction the property ‘Petherton’ Mt Bryan on instructions from F.H. Treweeke, comprising 2,251 acres freehold and located 6½ miles northwest of Mt Bryan.  To be offered in two lots:

Lot 1.  ‘Homestead’ 746 acres sections 539 Hd Anne and 533 Hd Hallett.

Lot 2.  1,505 acres comprising sections 550, 567, 551, Pts 566, 114 & 25, 39 Hd Ayers.
