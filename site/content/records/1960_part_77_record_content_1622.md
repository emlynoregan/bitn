Accident.  Jack Skinner, Overseer for the Robertstown District Council was bulldozing out two fifteen foot mallee trees on 21 March when a dead limb broke off and crashed on his head.  He was taken to Eudunda for treatment and then to the RAH with a probably fractured skull.

[See obituary 89, 15, 26 April 1960, page 1.]
