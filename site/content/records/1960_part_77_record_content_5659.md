Burra Town Council, 6 February

Building Application on Lot J Chapel Street on account of T. Ryan was approved.

Mr R.B. Lewis wrote concerning the proposed conference with Council concerning Paxton Square.  A letter will be sent to him saying Council wished to discuss health issues relating to the square.

The Highways Department is to be asked how long before action can be expected on a new Bon Accord Bridge.

Local Board of Health

Notice has been served on the owner of the old bakery at Burra North.
