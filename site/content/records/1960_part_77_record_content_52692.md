St Mary’s.  The 4th Bishop of Willochra the Rt Rev. S.B. Rosier will visit Burra on Monday.  The special Liturgical Reception service will be preceded by an organ recital by Dr J.V. Peters.  The Quire Saint Nicholas will also come from Adelaide for the occasion.  Supper in the Parish Hall will follow.  The Bishop will address the St Mary’s Guild in the Parish Hall on Tuesday at 2.30 p.m.
