Rev. Malcolm Thomas, a former Uniting Church minister at Burra, received a Medal of the Order of Australia in the Australia Day Honours List, for ministry and chaplaincy in the Outback.

Rob Wilson, father of Terry, Bronwyn and Lisa also received an OAM for services to the Mallee Region.  He lives at Pinnaroo, but frequently visits Burra.
