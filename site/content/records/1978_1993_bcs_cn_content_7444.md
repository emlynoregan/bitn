Paul Nation (BCS Year 9) shone in the U14 section of the Athletic Association of SA’s State Championships.  He got through to the finals of every event he entered and is now state record holder in two of them.

4th in the 100 m sprint.

1st in the 400 m in 56.8 seconds.

2nd in the 200 m.

1st in the 800 m in 2 min. 19.6 seconds – which was held only 25 minutes after the 200 m!
