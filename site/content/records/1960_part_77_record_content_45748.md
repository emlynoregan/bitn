Burra Basketball Debutante Ball at Burra Town Hall on Friday night took $350 at the door.  Eight debutants were presented to Mrs Wendy Ey.  Debutants and partners were:

Elaine Broderick with Peter Brown

Joy Goodridge with Robert Gilbert

Lynette Corner with Glen Brumley

Lorraine Jones with Ian Jones

Suzanne Richards with Michael Scholz

Marlene Rooke with Alan Drew

Allison Miller with Andrew Pearce

Judy Beinke with Kenneth Jones

Christine Baulderstone was flower girl and David Rasheed was a page boy.  Maurice Collins was MC.
