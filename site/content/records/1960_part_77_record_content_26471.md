Accident.  A car driven by Mrs R.J. Jeffery of Burra was struck by the Adelaide bound morning passenger steam train at the Manoora crossing on Saturday morning.  The car was picked up and rammed into the culvert and bank.  Mrs Jeffery was not seriously hurt, but her passenger, Mrs H.J. Topsfield, is still in a serious condition in the Royal Adelaide Hospital after being transferred from the Riverton Hospital.
