New Burra Residents will be welcomed at a gathering in the Town Hall on 18 March.  Over 50 houses have been filled by new residents in the past couple of years.  A ‘Welcome’ folder for new residents will be launched on the night.  (Spokesperson was Elspeth Redford.)
