The SA Stud Merino Field Days were held in Burra last Monday and Tuesday.

Merino Capital Ram was won by Hallett’s Greenfields and Southrose Tintinara won the Champion Pairs event. [Photo.]

New participants this year were Leachim Stud, Snowtown – Andrew & Rosemary Michael.

Southrose, Tintinara – Jim, Hillary and Jono Ashby.

Windarra Stud, between Keith and Kingston S.E. – Tom and Bev. Hanson.
