Robberies.  Two Burra stores were broken into on the night of 4 April.  The premises of P.H. & P.M. Sandercock (Sandy’s Serv-Wel Store) were entered and about £ stolen.  The front door was forced.  The intruder(s) left by the back door.  Pearce’s Hardware Store was also entered and a number of watches and a ring stolen.  Total value of goods taken was c. £130.  Police enquiries continue.
