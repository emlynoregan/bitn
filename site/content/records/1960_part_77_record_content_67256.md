P.D. Ryan of Burra North has had his home broken into and money and other items were stolen during the week before the 13 July.  About $500 was taken.  After investigation four juveniles and a 31-year-old person were apprehended and charged and remanded to appear at the Burra Court, sitting at Clare on 21 August. All but about $70 has been recovered.
