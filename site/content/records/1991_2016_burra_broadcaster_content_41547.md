Ski for Life.  The third consecutive Ski for Life event was held 11-13 March.  Ten relay teams skied the 500 km from Murray Bridge to Renmark in five days.  [How does this fit with 11-13 March?]  The Burra team: Kym Harvey, Luke Bavistock, Travis Thamm, Casey Cooper, Jack Rowe, Bec Rowe, Katherine Rowe and youngsters, Cash Cooper and Dustin Harvey, skied for 450 km and raised more than $3,000 for the prevention of male suicide and depression.
