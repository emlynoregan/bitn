Katie Treweeke, daughter of Mr & Mrs F.H. Treweeke of Petherton, Mt Bryan, is the local entrant in the SA section of the Miss Australia Quest.  A chief source of funding raised will be the Miss Australia Ball at Mt Bryan Hall on 7 July.  Proceeds aid the Woodville Spastic Centre.  Katie is 18 and is closely connecte4d with the Burra & District Branch of the Young Liberals.  She has aims of becoming a psychologist or a librarian and hopes to go to university next year to begin an Arts degree.
