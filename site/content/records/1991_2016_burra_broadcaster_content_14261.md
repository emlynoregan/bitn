The Burra Show was held on Saturday in good weather.  New features were the ute competition and tyre changing.  The Miners Revolution dance improved on last year’s event, with a larger crowd.  Sideshow alley was larger and gate takings were up for the second year in a row – from $3,286 to $3,734.

The show was opened by Mr Graham Gunn, State MP.

Princess of Show was Kylie O’Connor

Junior Princess of Show was Chelsea Webster

Prince of Show was Geffrey Thompson.
