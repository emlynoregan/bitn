Obituary.  Mr Blaine Gryst, a former manager of the Savings Bank of SA in Burra, died on 7 August in Adelaide.  (The family lived in Burra in the late 1950s.)

[Mr Gryst was manager at Burra from the start of 1956 to midway through 1960.

Blaine Ellerslie Gryst born 8 February 1917 at Torrensville.]
