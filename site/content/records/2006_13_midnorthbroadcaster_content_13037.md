Football.  Burra-Booborowie-Hallett Rams became North Eastern League 2011 ‘A’ Grade Premiers by defeated Blyth-Snowtown on Saturday.  Their opponents had been undefeated in the minor rounds.  The victory increases the credentials of Coach Daniel Schell.  The scores are cited on page 11: BBH 10.12 (72) defeated Blyth-Snowtown 8.10 (58).
