BCS Notable Events of 1998

Rachel Vogel was a semi-finalist in the Rostrum Voice of Youth, won a Max Fatchen Literature Award and had afternoon tea with the Governor.

David Ryan won a Max Fatchen Literature Award, was a member of the team that did well in the Business Week Competition, won a C.J. Dennis Poetry Award and received a high distinction in the Australian Chemical Institute Schools Competition.

Matthew Sobey (Year 3) won a high distinction in the Australian Schools Science Competition.

Heidi Thompson was the State Winner in the 2020 Essay Competition.

Anne Fry won the Asia Pacific Economic Community Essay Competition.

Angela Breeding and Louise McBride were two of the six finalists in the Australia-wide Henry Lawson Literary Competition.

Kate Reilly excelled in the State Cross Country Championships.

Kirsty Gebhardt won a bronze medal in SAPSASA Athletics.

Adam Willis won a Hartley Wood Scholarship for Cricket.

Ric Giles did well in the State Cross Country Championships.

Billie-Jai Cooper won an award in the C.J. Dennis Poetry Competition, took part in the Business Week Competition and won the Pierre de Coubertin Award recently.

Alysha Herrmann and Angela Breeding gained high distinction in the Australian Maths Competition.

Kylie Kaiser won an AFS Scholarship.
