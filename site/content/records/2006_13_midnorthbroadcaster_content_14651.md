Adelaide based Phoenix Copper listed on the Australian Securities Exchange in February 2008 and is focused on the discovery of copper and gold within its 4,200 km2 exploration licence holding in SA.  This includes the Burra Region and Yorke Peninsula, where it is currently conducting exploration drilling at its Minlaton project with two drilling rigs in operation.
