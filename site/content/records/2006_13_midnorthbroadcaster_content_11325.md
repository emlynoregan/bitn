The Mt Bryan Expedition Film is written up with background information about the expedition, the research that went into preparing the script and the shooting of the video.  The cast of locals is listed:

Henry Bryan	Scott Roberts

Governor, Lt-Col. Sir George Gawler	Casey Cooper

Captain Charles Sturt,

Assistance Commissioner	Luke Bavistock

Naval Lt William Jonathon Samuel

Pullen, OC Colonial Marines	Chris Braisher

Henry Inman, Inspector of Police	Tom Radford

John Craig, Aide	Nick van den Berg

Isaac Hearnshaw, Aide	Matt Hale

Julia Gawler, Governor’s daughter	Kate Reilly

Charlotte Sturt, Mrs Charles Sturt	Kelly Bavistock

Eliza Arbuckle, Sturt’s Maid	Lucy Smith

1839 Expedition Committee Members	Tony Sumner, Graham Radford, Elspeth Radford, Jackie Mattey, Matt Reilly, Tom Quinn & Mike Fretwell.

Writer, producer director and film-maker	Graeme McVitty

Assistant	Lorraine McVitty

Executive Producer	Tom Sumner

National Trust Locations & Costumes	Meredith Satchell, Paul Marsson & Terry Bull

Horse Handlers	Nick van den Berg, Scott Roberts, Suzanne & Dean Sharkie & Graham Radford

Special Props	Sally Hayes, Think Ink Productions, St Johns Ambulance Burra, Tony Sumner, Jackie Mattey

Aerial Photography Support	Heath Tiver, Glen Tiver

The documentary will be screened at Burra Town Hall on Friday 10 December.  Cost $5 for adults, children free.  A light supper will follow and proceeds go to the Burra Town Hall.
