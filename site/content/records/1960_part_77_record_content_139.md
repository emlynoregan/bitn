Accident.  Fred Atkins broke a bone in his shoulder last week when he fell while working in the ceiling of the Kooringa Methodist Sunday School.  He was knocking out the old plaster ceiling in preparation for a new one and fell 20 feet to the floor.  He also sustained a slight concussion, abrasions and bruising.
