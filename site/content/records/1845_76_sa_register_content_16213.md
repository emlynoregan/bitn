Mr Stuart’s Expedition

The following telegram was posted at the Exchange on Wednesday: -

‘John McDouall Stuart, commander of the South Australian Great Northern Exploring Expedition to the Commissioner of Crown Lands.

Kooringa, December 16, 1862.

‘Through you, I beg to inform His Excellency the Governor-in-Chief and the Government that I have accomplished the object of the expedition.

‘ Party all well.

‘I will be in town by the evening train to-morrow.’
