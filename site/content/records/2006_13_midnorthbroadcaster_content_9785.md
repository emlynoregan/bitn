The Liberal Party holds Stuart

Dan van Holst Pellekaan continued the Liberals strong hold on the seat of Stuart at the state election on Saturday.  He polled 52.1% of the primary vote with the ALP candidate Sean Holden on 36.7%.  After preferences the Liberals had 57.7% and Labor 42.3%.  A former national basketball player, Mr van Holst Pellekaan lives at Wilmington.
