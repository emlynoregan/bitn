Burra High School Jubilee Celebrations

Many old scholars will be returning for the planned events.  Celebrations begin at 12.55 p.m. on Thursday with a Back to School event.  Guides for old scholars will be H.J.B. Jennison, T. Perry, D. Field, M. Stockman, Mesdames M. Kellock, A. Benger & C. Neill.  A back to school class will be conducted by former headmaster Mr L. Carthew.  The Mayor will welcome visitors at the new tennis courts and Mr A.K. Statton, Superintendent of High Schools will address the assembly and unveil a plaque to open the area for future use.  Visits will be made to various areas of the school.  Speakers at the Birthday Celebrations will include Mr J.P. Biddle (Headmaster 1916-22), A.B. Riggs (Past President of the High School Council), K. Horsnell (Headmaster of the Primary School) and Mr J.W. Statton (First Headmaster).  Old scholars will present a concert in the evening at which speakers will include Dr D.M. Steele, H.J.B. Jennison and Brian Riggs (deputising for his father).  On Friday sporting contests between old and present scholars have been arranged.  The Golden Jubilee Ball will be held in the evening.
