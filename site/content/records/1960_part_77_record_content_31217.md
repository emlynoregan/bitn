Burra Football Club Colts held a dinner at the RSL Club Hall on Saturday 10 September at 6.30 p.m.  About 80 colts, parents, officials and supporters attended.  Team manager A. Fillmore said the main lack in the team’s activities was parental support.  He hoped that for next year a full set of jumpers could be purchased for the junior colts’ team.  Trophy Winners:

Senior Colts	Andrew Ryan

Dennis short

Grant Matthews

Garry James

Drew Anderson

Junior Colts	Robin James
