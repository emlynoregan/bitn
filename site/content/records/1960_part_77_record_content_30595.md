Burra Rural Youth – There is a report of a little more than 2 columns on the past year’s activities.  Items of note include:

The Burra Show night dance netted $68 for the Show Society.

Christmas Eve and Easter dances were successes both financially and socially.

The Debutante Ball took $342.

Merino Stud Tour.

Seminar on Rural Youth movement at Georgetown.

Inter-club debating.

Miss Industry Quest entrant Lyn Nicholls raised $600 for the Home for Incurables, attracting a $1,200 subsidy.

The Show Hall has been repainted and the lighting improved.

$68 was raised for the Royal Flying Doctor.

[The year’s program is printed.]
