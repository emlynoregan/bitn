Court.

John Smith of Ayers St was fined £2 + costs (total £7-8-6) for being unlawfully on the premises of Samuel Burns, blacksmith, at Kooringa on 18 June.  The report runs to c. 1⅔ columns.

Thomas Woollacott fined 2/6 + costs for a stray cow

H.C. Rundle from Murray Bridge is the new Kooringa postmaster.  Mr Jordan has gone to Balaklava.
