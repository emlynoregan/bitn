Broken Hill Barrow Push 1986

The list of teams nominated is printed.  Fifteen are listed and the local ones were: Burra Breathers c/o Mr Peter Bartlett, Burra; Collinsville Rams c/o Mr N. Garnett, ‘Collinsville’ Mt Bryan; Clare Valley Crushers, c/o George hart, Clare.

The Burra Barrow Birds are not listed, though previously cited as nominated and in an adjacent column they say they have nominated and are seeking two more girls to join the team.
