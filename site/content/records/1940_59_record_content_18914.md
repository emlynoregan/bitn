Red Cross & FFCF Gala Day brought their current fund raising effort to a close on Saturday.  It was held at the Showground.  There was a fine flower show and cake competition.  There were other displays such as Arts & Industries and antiques in the Centenary Hall.  There were stalls for drinks, lollies, afternoon tea and games in the Centenary Hall.
