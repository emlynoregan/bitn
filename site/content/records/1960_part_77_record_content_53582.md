Burra Primary School

59 students from Grades VI & VII visited Adelaide on 22 October.  They travelled by coach to Adelaide and visited Parliament as guests of Mr E.C .Allen MP and then went to Port Stanvac Oil Refinery.  After lunch they visited Adelaide Airport and the Chrysler factory on South Road.  At 4 p.m. they visited Cowandilla Demonstration School where they viewed new open unit type class rooms.  There was a final stop at Parliament House to see a sitting of the House before leaving at 5.40 p.m.  They got back to Burra at 9.10 p.m.
