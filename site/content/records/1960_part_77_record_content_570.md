Mr Neville Wright (21) of Adelaide was found on Sunday afternoon by searchers on Pine Valley Station after being lost for more than 24 hours.  Wright and two companions were camping on a dam c 15 miles southeast of Canegrass Homestead.  On Saturday he became separated from the others when he chased a wild goat.  He was tracked for ten miles by Messrs A.D. Radford and Bill and Gill Moxham.  Constable Clift of Burra Police went out, but Wright had been found by his arrival.
