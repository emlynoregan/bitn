Burra Burra DC

Final measurements are being collected for the Common Effluent Drainage Scheme.  The plans should be received before the end of June for presentation to the Government.  It is hoped the cost will be included in the list of estimates for 1976-77.

A volunteer has been accepted for the job of dog catcher.

Burning off has tidied up a number of town blocks.

New street signs have been erected for unmarked streets and to better mark others.

The map of the historic tour has been redone and upgraded.

It seems that sporting bodies will have to contribute more to the upkeep of recreation facilities.
