Fire Danger this season out east is high due to heavy growth of vegetation and reduced man power.  The Eastern Districts Fire Fighting Organisation met at Koomooloo with guest speakers Andrew Brown of the Department of Agriculture and Mr Brian Menadue of the EFS.  Elected were: Chairman-Supervisor, R.R. Clarke and Secretary, A.L. Wright.
