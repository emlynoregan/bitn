Point Pass will celebrate the Centenary of Federation on the weekend of 19-21 October, when their Point Pass Pioneer Water supply project will be opened.  On Friday 19 there will be a dance in the Point Pass Hall.  Saturday will see an evening informal gathering with inexpensive meals.  Sunday 21 will have a 10 a.m. community church service in Point Pass Park and at noon the project will be unveiled and a barbecue lunch held.
