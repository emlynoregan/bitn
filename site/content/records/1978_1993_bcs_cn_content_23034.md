The Burra registry will be closed as from 30 August 1991 and all applications in future will have to be made to the Deputy Registrar of Births, Deaths and Marriages, GPO Box 1351, Adelaide SA, 5001.

It was a government decision to close all country registries.

The first entry in Burra was written as early as 1848.

Shirley Thamm, District Registrar.
