The final meeting on 23 July had the task of redistributing the remaining funds of $701.13.

$30 to Mrs Baulderstone Memorial Fund.

$34.13 to Mrs Bowden for cleaning.

$300 to be spent on books and mobiles for the clinic.

$287 to Burra Hospital to purchase equipment for hospitalised mothers or young children.

$50 to CAFHS Headquarters in Adelaide in trust for any future re-establishment of CAFHS Volunteer Association in Burra.
