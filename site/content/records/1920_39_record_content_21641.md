Cricket.  On 18 December Stockbreeders 314 defeated Kooringa 143.

For the Stockbreeders B.L. Anderson retired on 85 and H.W. Tiver was not out 75.

Farrell’s Flat 89 & 32 was defeated by Kooringa 8 for 155.
