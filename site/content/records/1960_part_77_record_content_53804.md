Accident.  A semi-trailer with a load of mixed goods valued at $30,000 capsized at the corner of Paradise and Quarry Streets on Thursday.  It was bound for WA.  Driver Sipke Poede of Swan Hill, Victoria, escaped injury.  Mrs Poede, a passenger, received bruising and a lacerated forehead. Vehicle damage was estimated at $2,000 with unknown damage to the load.
