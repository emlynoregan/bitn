Christmas in Burra

Senior Citizens will erect the Nativity Scene in Market square.

Burra Festival of Carols will be held in the Civic Centre on Sunday 12 December at 8 p.m.

Burra Traders will sponsor the usual late night trading and entertainment on Friday 24 December, with a disco in front of the Civic Centre and of course the arrival of Father Christmas.

Paxton Square.  Ten newly renovated and two modified Paxton Square cottages will be opening for visitor accommodation in the new year.

Burra Hospital Auxiliary is holding a 'Christmas Around the World Festival' on Friday 3 December from 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. at Burra Town Hall.  Exhibits – Trading Table – Entertainment.  [Program printed.]
