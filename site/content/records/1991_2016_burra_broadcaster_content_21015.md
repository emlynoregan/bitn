Telstra Adelaide Festival 2000 Production Team with chef Gay Bilson inspected the Smelts Stables site where it is intended to run the ‘Plenty’ event on 12 March 2000.  They also met with food and service providers and walked the route of the planned procession.  They also met with representatives of the Twilight Jazz Affair, which will be held at ‘Petherton’ the night before the ‘Plenty’ event.
