Obituary.  Albert Henry Farley of Robertstown died 17 November at Eudunda Hospital aged 84.  He is survived by his wife, Winifred and daughter, Mavis.  There are three grandchildren, Marilyn, Geoffrey & Ian.

[Born 19 September 1889 near Robertstown.]
