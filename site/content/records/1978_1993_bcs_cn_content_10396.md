Rosie Lloyd established herself as one of Australia’s top junior athletes by winning a silver medal at the National All Schools Championships in Brisbane last weekend in the U19 400 m hurdles.  She ran 5th in the U17 400 m and was in the U19 team that came 4th in the 4 x 400 m relay.
