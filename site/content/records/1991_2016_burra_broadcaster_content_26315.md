Goyder Council will spend $8,000 on upgrading the Burra Town Hall, $12,000 to re-roof the Farrell Flat Hall, $6,000 on Eudunda Sports Complex fencing and $6,000 to re-seal the Eudunda hard courts.

Other spending approved was $1,000 for the repair of water damage to Farrell Flat Hall, $2,000 for the erection of shades at the Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club grounds and $2,000 on the installation of irrigation there.
