‘Gum View’ at World’s End was sold by auction on Friday by Elders-GM on account of J.E. Rooke & Mrs E. Rooke.  The homestead block of 1,136 acres went to Ken Phillips at $33 per acre.  Two lots of 395 and 45 acres went to Dalgety & NZ Loan of Burra for $34 & $10 per acre respectively.  Ken Phillips also bought 132 acres at $26.50 per acre and 197 acres at $20.50 per acre.  The last section of 982 acres went to Mrs J.M. Rooke at $9.50 per acre.
