Burra Oval is undergoing a $70,000 redevelopment.  An area is being prepared for two netball courts for winter months and three tennis courts in summer.  All running north-south.  The present netball courts will be dug up to make way for the new development.  Burra Sports Complex, BBH Netball Club and Burra Tennis Club are jointly arranging finance and Council has contributed $6,000 worth of earthworks.  [Photo.]
