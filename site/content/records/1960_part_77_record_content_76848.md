Burra Tennis Club.  The new tennis courts will be opened on 19 October by the Patron Mr Robert Reed.  They have been erected by the efforts of the Burra Burra DC, RED Scheme and the Burra Tennis Club.  Top players from Adelaide will give demonstration matches.
