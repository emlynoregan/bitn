Mona Johnson, a former Burra resident, has celebrated her 80th birthday.  She was a shearers’ cook for many years.  Among her guests celebrating the occasion were Sylvia & Tony Ryan, Calene & Shef Rasheed, Peter Murphy, Tim & Lorraine Kakoschke and Mona’s late husband Syd’s sisters, Zita & Dorothy, all old Burraites.
