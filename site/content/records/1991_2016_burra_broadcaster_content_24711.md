The Fourth Twilight Jazz Affair at Petherton House, Hallett was very successful and raised c. $7,500 for the restoration of Sir Hubert Wilkins’ family home at Mt Bryan East.  In all, these events have raised more than $20,000 for this cause.  Just over 400 attended the event on Saturday night.  Featured were: Samba Suave, Cabra Jazz Junctions and Dukes of Jazz.  Dancing continued to midnight.
