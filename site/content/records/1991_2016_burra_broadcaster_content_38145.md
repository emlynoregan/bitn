The National Trust of SA celebrated its 50th anniversary in September with a special event at Government House in Adelaide.  From Burra, Marj. Edmonds and John Arthur were guests.  Governor Marjorie Jackson-Nelson, as Patron, gave a short address and the main address was given by State President Anita Aspinal.
