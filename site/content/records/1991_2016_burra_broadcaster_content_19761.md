Wilbur Besanko of Terowie recently celebrated his 90th birthday.  He has been an active community member for a long time:

Served on the Hallett Dc for 20 years

Was on the Emergency Fire Service (now the CFS)

Served on the District Ambulance Committee

President of the Terowie Institute

A JP for more than 30 years

A foundation member of the Mid North Tourist Association

Member of the Terowie branch of the National Trust
