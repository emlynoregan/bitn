The team was presented with cricket caps to commemorate their successive premierships.

Belalie-Mokota Cricketer of the Year was Geoff ‘Digger’ Thompson.  He also won the Best Player in the Finals award.  Darren Schuppan won the Best Bowling Aggregate and Average award.  Jason Schuppan won the Batting Aggregate award with 391 runs for the season.  He also won the Association Honours for Opening Batsman.  Tim Crick won the Batting Average award.  Best Fielder was Peter McGuiness.  Ben Tiver gained the award for Best Team Man [photo page 7]. An encouragement Award went to John ‘Noodle’ Robinson who, as a junior, filled in whenever needed and often played two games on a Saturday.  [Photo of Geoff ‘Digger’ Thompson.]
