Marriage.  Kadina Methodist Church recently

Maxine Ruth Brown, eldest daughter of Mr & Mrs Allan Brown of Kadina, married

Francis Alexander Fillmore (Joe), elder son of Mr & the late Mrs F.A. Fillmore of Burra.

His brother ‘Tony’ was best man.  The couple will live at Amberley Queensland where the bridegroom has an RAAF posting.
