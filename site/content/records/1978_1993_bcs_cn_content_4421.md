Burra Burra DC.  Proposed subdivision at Burra North

A public meeting will be called in the near future to discuss this subdivision proposal and the development of a sports complex at the War Memorial Oval.

No decision has been made regarding the demolition of the public toilet bloc adjoining the proposed subdivision.
