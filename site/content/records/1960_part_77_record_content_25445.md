Man Lost at Thistlebeds

Two inexperienced shooters got into trouble at Thistlebeds-Baldina area on Sunday when one became lost.  Hendrick Westerlaken (26) and Gunther Fromel (28) were five miles from Thistlebeds when the latter became lost about 10 a.m.  After searching for his friend Westerlaken sought help at Thistlebeds at about 5 p.m.  F-C Watts, R. Moreland, W. Corner, Constable Terry and Jim Brown of Baldina searched till midnight.  Mr Kellock searched on horseback until dawn and others joined the search on Monday.  Mr M. Tiver used his plane to help at 11 a.m. on Monday.  Fromel was found at 12.40 p.m., having spent the night in Mr G. Lommanâ€™s crutching shed.  The men were questioned at Burra Police Station concerning trespassing and shooting on the properties concerned.
