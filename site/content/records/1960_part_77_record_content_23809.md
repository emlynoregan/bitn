Burra & Districts Children’s Hospital Auxiliary held its AGM and elected Mrs Baulderstone President for 1965 with Mrs Benger as Secretary.  There were 11 financial members in 1964.  The collection of rags and paper raised £29-13-8.  The 5AD Good Friday Appeal brought in £12-3-0 and a badge day in September raised £23-10-0.
