The Annual Anzac March on Sunday was interrupted by heavy rain and the service was transferred from outside to the RSL Rooms.  The wreaths were laid after the service by Ben Kotz for the RSL and R.J. Jeffery of the Burra Burra DC.  Rev. C. Hore conducted the service and the Pt Pirie Pipe Band attended.  The combined church service in the evening was at the Methodist Church.
