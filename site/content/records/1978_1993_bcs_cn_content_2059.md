Copper Festival 1980

The Steering Committee decided to hold a public meeting on 1 April to discuss a program.  Activities would be concentrated on Sunday afternoon of the October long weekend with the Burra Show and the Show Ball on the Saturday.

Sunday:

Morning Church Services

Noon till dark – Copper Carnival on the Town Oval

Town Oval – food, entertainment, stalls, pony rides, highlight events like wheelbarrow rides

Burra Entertainment Group Production in the evening

Monday – no organised activities.

Numerous community groups have indicated support.

It has been suggested proceeds go towards beautification of the Burra Creek area.
