Burra High School opened on Tuesday with an enrolment of 125; the highest ever.  There are 70 boys and 55 girls. Leaving 9, Intermediate 30, 2nd Year 39 and 1st year 47.  New Staff:

Mr R.V. Mueller from Murray Bridge

Mr R.D. Hillman from Henley

Mr B.I. McElroy from Teachers’ College

Miss N.I. Lawes from Pt Pirie

Mrs J.E. Bogisch from Burra

Miss E. McCabe from Gawler AEC one day a week.

Burra Primary School.  Mr A. Kies Headmaster has been transferred to Walkerville and has been replaced by Mr K. Horsnell, formerly Deputy Headmaster at Murray Bridge.  Mr H. Kowald, acting Chief Assistant has been appointed Chief Assistant at Angaston.  Mr Sullivan comes from McLaren Vale and was formerly with the Queensland Education Department.  There is new flooring in two rooms and a new roof.  Enrolments at 250 are up 22.  Grade 1 49, Grade II 32, Grade III 25, Grade IV 31, Grade V 43, Grade VI 38 and Grade VII 32.

[New admissions to Grade I are named.]

St Joseph’s Convent School has 35 students.  Numbers and staff are unchanged.

[The 7 new students are listed.]

Booborowie School.  Miss Pat Oxley has been transferred to Dover Gardens infant School.  Miss Rachel Woolford comes from Wirrabara.  The enrolment of 66 is the highest for 25 years.  [12 new enrolments are listed.]

Weather.  Thunderstorms delivered 37 points at Burra on Wednesday to 9 a.m. and 8 points later in the day.

Miss Raylene Edwards is being sponsored by Mrs R.B. Pitcher and Mr L.P. Bence as Burra’s entrant in the Sky Queen Contest in aid of the Adelaide Children’s Hospital Building Fund.

Marriage.  St Andrew’s Anglican Church Mt Bryan, 10 December 1960

Mary Edwards married Arthur Talent.

This was the first wedding at this church since Mary’s parents were married there 27 October 1934.
