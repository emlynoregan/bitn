The 2012 Burra to Morgan Historic Car Run will take place on 14 October More than 100 people and 50 vehicles will participate.  They will include veteran (pre 1919), Vintage (pre 1931) and classic vehicles.  The run recalls the first run of the Burra Motor Club in 1906.
