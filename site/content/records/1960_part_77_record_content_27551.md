Accident/Obituaries.

Over the New Year weekend five young people were killed in a light plane crash at Canopus Station east of Burra.  The pilot was Russell Tiver (23) the son of Mr & Mrs M.H. Tiver of Burra.  He had been conducting sight-seeing tours over the 400,000 acre property, owned by Mr W.G. Snell, when the accident occurred.  The passengers were:

David Stanley Radford (15) and William John Radford (12) who were the only children of David Radford of Canegrass Station.  And also Janne Elizabeth Daniel (16) and Peter John Daniel (13) who were the only children of Mr & Mrs S.D. Daniel of Kulpara.

A private funeral was conducted at Burra Cemetery for Mr Tiver and for the others private services were held in Adelaide.

[Russell Gordon Tiver born 8 July 1942 Abberton Park, Burra: died 1 January 1966 Canopus Station.]
