Pat & Carissa Svensdotter and their dogs impressed judges at the Obedience and Agility trials in Whyalla last month, claiming a first and third place in jumping excellence. Pat and Carissa are both instructors with the Burra and Districts Obedience Dog Club.  The dog operates from behind the Aberdeen tennis courts, opposite the Burra golf club.  [Photo]
