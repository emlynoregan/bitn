Skateboard Ramp.  At a meeting on 2 August the proposal for a skateboard ramp in Burra was discussed.  It was resolved that:

There was a need for a ramp.

What was required was a half pipe ramp partially buried in the ground.

Permission be sought from BCS to install it on school grounds.

An active fundraising campaign be initiated.
