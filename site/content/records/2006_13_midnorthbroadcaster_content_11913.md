Burra SES volunteer Phil Colls has recently returned from the cyclone devastated areas of North Queensland where he joined a taskforce of 55 SA volunteers.    Mr Colls is the training co-ordinator with the Burra SES.  The group was sent to Innisfail where the major work was removing large fallen trees and other recovery operations.
