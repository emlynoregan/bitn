The new Kingston Street Cottages.  [Between Welsh Place and the old Salvation Army Citadel.]

Pat and Darrell Field have moved into one of the cottages and Mr & Mrs Maurice Jeffery have another after many years at Hallett.  Mrs Thelma Harris from Booborowie has another, while Beryl & Bruddy Kakoschke have taken the fourth.
