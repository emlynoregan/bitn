The Burra Burra Pistol Club was formed at a meeting held on Wednesday 14 April.  Elected: President, Sergeant Bruce Haynes of Burra; Secretary, Nick Kingsley of Mt Bryan and Treasurer, Phil Edwards of Burra.  A further meeting is scheduled for 28 April.  Over 40 people indicated their interest.  Four members of the SA Target Pistol League were present, including the President.  Initially air pistol shoots can be held in any building with 10 m of space.  Progress will later be made to the use of live ammunition.
