Burra High School.  Examination Results.  Grade 4 or higher results are published.  The most outstanding result was that of Intermediate student Christopher Allan Freeman, son of Mr & Mrs A. Freeman of Booborowie, who gained English 3, History 1, Maths I 1, Maths II 1, Physics 1, Chemistry 1 and Woodwork 1.

(Grade 1 is the top 10% in the state, 2 is the next 15%, 3 the next 25%, 4 the next 25%, 5 the next 15% and 6 the last 10 %.)
