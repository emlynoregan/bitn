Accident.  Karen Don, the 9-year-old daughter of Mr & Mrs Don, employed at Stud Park, Mt Bryan, was taken to the Children’s Hospital on Saturday after her nightdress caught alight while she was standing near the fire.  Her condition is said to be satisfactory.
