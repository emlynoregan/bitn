BCS.  The Annual Invitation Cross Country Championships were held on 26 June.  Some 200 runners from 15 schools participated in races from 2 km to 5 km.  Jamestown H.S. took home three of the six shields.  Burra took both of the Open Shields and Willunga H.S. gained that for the U13 Boys.
