Obituary.  Mrs David Radford late of Glenora Station died on 2 January.  She was the only daughter of Mr & Mrs George Roberts and was born in Manchester in 1846.  She arrived in SA ten years later with her parents who went to live at Angaston as farmers.  In 1865 she married David Radford who took land near Quorn where they lived for about 15 years. In 1891 they came to Burra.  She was closely associated with the Redruth Methodist Church, the WCTU and the Womenâ€™s Foreign Missions Auxiliary.  The family comprises Mr S.O. Radford (Gulnare), 13 grandchildren and 2 great-grandchildren.  [Born Jane Roberts.]
