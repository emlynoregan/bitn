Burra Pictures

The Burra Arts Council and the BBDC and Spalding DC have combined to see that films return to the Burra Town Hall this Saturday night.  The first film to be presented on 11 July will star Clint Eastwood in Unforgiven.

[An advertisement is copied from the Burra Record in 1933 publicising the opening night for ‘Talkies in Burra’:

Will Rogers, Gieta Nissen & Marguerite Churchill in Ambassador Bill

Ralph Bellamy & Tommy Conton in Dangerous Year.]
