Burra [Melbourne] Cup Committee will have a chicken and wine luncheon at Burra Town Hall on Tuesday 6 November.  A donation of $4 can be made at the door.  Proceeds will aid the Royal Flying Doctor Service, Burra M & BHA and Burra Girl guides.
