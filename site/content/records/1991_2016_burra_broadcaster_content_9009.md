New Year’s Ball in July

Burra Hospital Auxiliary staged an Irish New Year’s Ball on 1 July in the Burra Town Hall.

A feature was the dancing of Desme White’s ‘Heritage Dancers’ who performed in 18th and 19th century costumes.
