Letter.  Maureen Wright responds to M. Campbell re Burra Passport costs.

At $30 for four people the passport costs $7.50 per person and includes a book that sells separately for $5.  For an extra $3 per person entry to Morphett’s Enginehouse and the Bon Accord Museum can be obtained – representing a saving of 50%.  Entrance to Sovereign Hill, Ballarat cost $15.50 per adult and to the Swan Hill Pioneer Settlement $10 per adult.
