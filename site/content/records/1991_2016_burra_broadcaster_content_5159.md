Burra Swimming Pool

The new Burra Community Pool will be officially opened next Friday at 2.30 p.m. by Rob Kerin MP.  There will then be free swimming for all.  The Lions Club will have their donut machine operating and canteen facilities will be available.  Visitors can bring a barbecue and BYO (but NO glass).  Early morning swimming will commence on 1 December.
