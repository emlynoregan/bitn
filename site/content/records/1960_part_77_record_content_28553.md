BHS.  The Parents & Friends’ Association held its annual meeting on 15 March.  Elected: President, Mrs D.H. Field; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames J.L. Riggs & R.F. Lloyd; Secretary, Mrs R.C. Gent; Assistant Secretary, Mrs G.F. Lomman and Treasurer, Mrs P.W. Carpenter.

In the past year there were 57 members and an average attendance of 33.

[The year’s activities are reported.]

This year it is intended to install four fans in the two iron classrooms and $400 is made available fro a dressing shed when needed.
