Margie Whittlesea from Collinsville Stud Mt Bryan ahs won first prize in the Stock Journal photo competition with a spectacular image taken at Arcoona Station, Woomera.  First prize was a Canon 10 megapixel digital camera and $300 dollars cash.  The photo is of heavy clouds and an approaching dust storm with a large part of a mob of 6,000 sheep gathered for shearing in the foreground.
