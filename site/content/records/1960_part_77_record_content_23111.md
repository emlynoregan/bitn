Pranks.  On Monday Mr T.G. Perry had to remove a very smelly dead rabbit from the flagpole of the District Council.  Three more were seen swinging from the flagpole near Gill’s store at Burra North.  Following New Year Eve Mr Terry had to remove a pushbike from the flagpole.
