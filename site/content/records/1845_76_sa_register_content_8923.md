Advertisement.  Burra Burra Mines

The usual public survey will be held at the Mine on 25 January when a number of tutwork bargains and tribute pitches (including a few in the lower levels) will be offered.  The ‘take’ to expire 29 March.

Henry Ayers, Secretary, SA Mining Association, Adelaide, 9 January 1855.
