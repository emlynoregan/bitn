Kate Reilly of BCM was recently selected to compete in the National All Schools cross Country Championships in Darwin.  She ran in a relay race and placed 31 out of 55 in the U-16 4 km race.

Last week she won the Athletics SA U-16 trophy for most points accrued through the winter season.
