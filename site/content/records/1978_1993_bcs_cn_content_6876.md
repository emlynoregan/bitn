Burra Burra DC.  Supplementary Election for Burra Town Ward.

Helen Stockman has become the first woman elected to the Burra Burra DC.  [Photo with Council Chairman James Hawker.]

Helen has been an active contributor to the community for a number of years.  She has been Secretary of the National Trust, co-editor of the ‘Community News’, a past President of the Kindergarten Committee and a representative on the Burra District Advisory Committee (which she will now relinquish).
