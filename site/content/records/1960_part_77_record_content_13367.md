Burra Town Council, 1 October

Council decided to enforce its by-laws regarding dogs to reduce the stray dog problem.

A letter is to be sent to Mr G. Nourse about the straying of his Alsatian dog.

Adelaide Wool Sales.  The top local price was 73d received by S.T. Miller & Sons of Gum Creek.
