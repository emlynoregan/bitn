Obituary.  Glenton Charles Frederick Eig was born at Burra 7 August 1920.  He was the eldest son of Mrs G. & the late Mr D. Eig.  In WWII he served in the Middle East and in New Guinea.  After the war he joined the PMG and was stationed at the Adelaide GPO for 27 years.  He is survived by his mother, a brother David and sister Dorothy.

[Born Glenton Charles Fredrick Eig 7 August 1920 Kooringa: died Glendon Charles Frederick Eig (single) 3 February 1971 Adelaide, residence Hamley Bridge.]
