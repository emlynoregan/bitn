Goyder Council at its July meeting adopted the rate for 2003-04.  The differential rates adopted were:

0.7278 cents in the dollar for Burra township

0.4744 for Eudunda township

0.3867 for all land in the Hallett and Robertstown Wards and for remaining areas of Burra and Eudunda Wards.

This represents an increase of 8% in Burra town rates and 5% in the rural areas and 5.5% over the Council area as a whole.
