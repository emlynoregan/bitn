Burra Community & Sports Club presented two Life Memberships on 26 September.  They went to Di Day and Trevor Wall for Outstanding Service.  Di Day has been Treasurer for 23 years, after taking over from Colin Baker.  She has had a number of other executive tasks in that time, as well as being a member of the Ladiesâ€™ Auxiliary.  Trevor Wall has been a committee member since its inception, holding many positions, including President and Bar Manager for ten years.  He is currently Vice President.  The Burra Community & Sports Club was formed in 1979 for the purpose of improving facilities at Burra War Memorial Oval.
