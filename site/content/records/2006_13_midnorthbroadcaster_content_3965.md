BBH Social Club is hosting ‘The Back to BBH Ball’ this Friday night at Burra Town Hall.  The celebration incorporates the Senior Football and Netball Presentations for 2007, which is the 21st anniversary of the merging of the Burra, Booborowie and Hallett Football and Netball Clubs.  A special framed collection of past Mail Medallists’ memorabilia has been prepared by Don Villis.
