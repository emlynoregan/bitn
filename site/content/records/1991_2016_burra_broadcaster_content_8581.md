The Burra Hospital Auxiliary’s Melbourne Cup Luncheon last Tuesday was highly successful.  The $1,598 profit enabled the purchase of two recliner chairs for private rooms.

Di Day netted a large range of prizes from her appearance on Wheel of Fortune on Channel 7.

A trip for two to Surfers Paradise for 7 days

A bedding package

Sunglasses

A water purifier

Coffee plunger

Beach umbrella

Place mats, coasters

Skin care products

An ‘able’ table
