Burra RSL held its AGM on Friday 13 March and elected: President, T.R. Rogers; Vice-Presidents, B. Kotz & W.H. Nankivell; Secretary, W.J. Fridd and Treasurer, R.J. Kellock.

The President reported ten meetings for the year with an average attendance of eight.  New lights were installed in the Hall and Clubrooms.  A liquor licence was obtained and a bar room was furnished.  An Anzac Service was held on 22 April and a Remembrance Day service on 11 November, which was attended by the upper grades of the Primary School and representatives from the High School.  Badge Days were held for Anzac Day, Legacy, War Veterans’ Home and Poppy Day.  A snooker tournament was won by Ron Wuttke.  The annual dinner was held in the club rooms with food prepared by the Ladies’ Auxiliary.
