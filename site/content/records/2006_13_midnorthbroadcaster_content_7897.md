BBH Football Club held a fund raising night of fun on Saturday at the Burra Sports Complex.  $700 was raised.  The boys paraded in fashion clothing, mostly from the local op shop.  Those strutting their stuff were Andrew Dredge, Nick Stacey, Shawn Rayson, Boh Wall, Andrew Purchase, Ian Dolling, Daniel Booth and Peter Mann.
