St Mary’s Church Concert

On Sunday 16 November ladies from the Woodville Choir under the baton of Rosemary Nairn (nee Reed) will perform at St Mary’s.  There will also be performances on the organ by four organists:

Dennis Nettleton – a former manager of the Savings Bank of SA in Burra

Mike Ford – a former teacher at BCS

Brian Smedley – a cousin of Ron Smedley

Rosemary Nairn
