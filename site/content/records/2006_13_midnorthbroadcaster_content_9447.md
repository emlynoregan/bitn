AGL presented Community Funding cheques to various persons and bodies last week.

Jim Quinn: $1,400 on behalf of the Hallett Bowling Club for club shirts.

Jim Low for Burra Community School: $1,200 towards a hot plate stove for the Home Economics Centre.

Andy Thomas: $3,000 on behalf of the Mt Bryan Soldiers Memorial Hall towards maintenance.

Shirley Madigan & Stewart Catt: $1,780 for the Booborowie Hall for chairs and the Booborowie Bowling Club.

John Ryan: $7,500 for the Mt Bryan Progress Assoc. towards a tractor for landscaping and maintenance of public areas.

Di Day: $1,120 on behalf of the Burra Golf Club towards flags and flag sticks for 18 holes.
