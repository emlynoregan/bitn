Burra Town Council, October meeting

The Town Clerk is to investigate methods of financing a house for occupation by the Town Clerk.

Council resolved to take over the assets and liabilities of the Burra & District Amateur Swimming Club and the Burra Swimming Pool be thereafter under the control and management of the Council.

Resolved that the position of Pool Attendant be advertised.

Resolved an immediate start be made to prepare the pool for use and a Swimming Pool Account be set up by Council.

1 November

The Town Clerk is to obtain plans and estimates of solid brick and brick veneer houses.

L.L. Fiebig to be advised ‘that the care, maintenance and control of the toilet block adjacent to his Service Station is his soul [sic] responsibility.’

Swimming pool admission is to be 2/- (20 c) and 1/- (10 c) for a child under 14.

Season’s tickets £1-10-0 ($3) for children and £3 ($6) for adults.

Pool Hours 1.30-6.30 p.m. Saturday, Sunday and School Holidays & 2.30-6.30 p.m. school days.
