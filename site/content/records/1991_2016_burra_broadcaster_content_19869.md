Josephine Burke, a part-time Burra resident, co-ordinates more than sixty state-wide insurance personnel for Elders.  She recently received the Elders State Office Service Award for 1999 at a function in the Adelaide Hilton Hotel.  Former Australian Cricket Captain Mark Taylor made the presentation.  [Photo.]
