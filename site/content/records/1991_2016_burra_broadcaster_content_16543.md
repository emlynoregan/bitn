Vandalism.  About $200 damage has been done to the Mt Bryan Tennis Clubhouse when a dropper was rammed into a fibro wall.

A break-in at Burra Sports Complex resulted in $600 damage and the theft of liquor and cigarettes to the value of c. $400.

A Break-in at a house in Thames Street resulted in smashed holes in the ceiling and doors etc.
