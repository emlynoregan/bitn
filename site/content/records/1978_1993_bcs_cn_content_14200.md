The Back to Hanson next Sunday is expected to see representatives of the Dixon, Dempsey, Hogan, Rogers and Humphrys Families among others.  There will be a shared lunch in the hall, a church service, an auction, the release of balloons, a cake cutting and the sale of souvenirs.  The last Back-to-Hanson was in 1936.
