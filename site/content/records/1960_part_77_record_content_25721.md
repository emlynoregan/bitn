Murray Water.  A start will soon be made on a 10 inch main from Hanson to Burra.  E & WS have chosen a site for a pumping station 50 ft x 100 ft near Hanson on property of S.J. Woollacott and construction will begin early in 1966.  The Booborowie water scheme is nearing completion.
