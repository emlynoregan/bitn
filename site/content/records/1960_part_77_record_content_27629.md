Weather.  Rain in the past week fell from patchy thunderstorms across the pastoral areas.  Dams were filled on Oakvale, Koomooloo, Old Koomooloo, Morganvale and Sturtvale, where falls ranged from 110 to 140 points.  Elsewhere fall varied from 16 to 70 points, while in Burra only 3 points were recorded.
