SA Film Co. TAV Productions has been in Burra conducting an overview of youth issues, especially those pertaining to the Youth Advisory Committee, with a focus on local government and funding.  They visited BCS and the Goyder Council CEO, John Brak.  They interviewed four students about what they liked about the town and what they thought needed improving.
