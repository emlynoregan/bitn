Helen Szuty

Moved to Burra 1999.  Editor and publisher of the magazine ‘The Visitors Monthly’ (now published every two months).  An Independent Member of the ACT Legislative Assembly for three years from 1992.  Since 2008 spokesperson for the Ratepayers Action Group.  Concerned with a rates review, review of the Council’s Development Assessment Plan, representation review and strategic management plan review.
