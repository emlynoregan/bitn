Rosemary Nairn (nee Reed) OAM

Rosemary was awarded the Order of Australia Medal on Sunday for her service to the community of SA.  After leaving Burra as a schoolgirl she moved to Lameroo in 1960 when she married and has been involved in teaching music and conducting ever since.  She has been involved in such groups as the Woodville City Choir and currently is director of the Primary Schools Music Festival.  [Photo.]
