Weather.  Light soaking rain fell throughout the agricultural district on Wednesday night and Thursday, yielding from 65 to 100 points.  The best falls were in the East.  Burra received 77 points for the week and Mt Bryan 65, but to the East Woolgangi reported 137, Old Koomooloo 180, Braemar 155, Kia-Ora 127, while Oakbank received only 68.
