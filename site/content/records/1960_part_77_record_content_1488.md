Burra Primary School Welfare Club held its annual meeting 1 March.

Elected: President, Mrs J. Phillips; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames D. Field & H. Topsfield; Secretary, Mrs M. Mitchell; Assistant Secretary, Mrs M. Broderick and Treasurer Mrs J. Bogisch.

Last year the assistance to the school included the long awaited bicycle racks freework for Grades I & II, sports equipment, desk bags for the Grade VI room, a new pillow and rug for the sick room and ice creams and drinks for the Christmas Party.
