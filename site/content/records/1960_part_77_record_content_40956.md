Arbor Day was held at Burra Primary School last Friday and more than 110 visitors attended.  The school was open for visitors and the theme for the day was Australian Trees.  Mrs M. Rayner of Hallett delivered the address after which 14 trees were planted around the school oval.  [Tree planters are listed.]
