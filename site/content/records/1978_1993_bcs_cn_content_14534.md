Jennison’s will cease trading on 15 May having leased their premises to Elders Pastoral.  Neil Webster & Andrew Nourse of our staff will commence trading on 19 May as Burra auto Repairs from our crash repair building – entrance off Kangaroo Street.  They will operate an authorised Ford Parts Service and Warrant 7 outlet as agents for Vanstone Ford of Clare.  Our surplus equipment and furniture etc. will be auctioned by Elders Pastoral 27 May at noon.
