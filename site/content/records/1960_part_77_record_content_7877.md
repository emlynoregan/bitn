Booborowie Robbery.  A possible clue to the robbery of Dinham Bros. at Booborowie is the finding of oxy-acetylene cutting equipment and a cash box near Porter’s Lagoon. The oxy equipment was stolen from the SA Harbour’s Board at Pt Adelaide about a month ago.  Investigation continues.
