Elder, Smith & Co. Ltd celebrated 75 years of sales at Burra.  They began on 25 November 1887 with their first monthly sale at the Bon Accord Yards.  25,000 sheep and 300 cattle were advertised; 15,000 of them from Paratoo.  At the time Liston, Shakes & Co. held sales monthly at Young’s Yards and Morris, Sandland & Barritt also operated at the Bon accord Yards.  On 16 November 1962 the combined agents will yard 22,000 sheep.  Now Elders have merged with Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. Ltd.  The record yarding for SA occurred when Elder’s yarded 44,000 sheep and 750 cattle on 27 September 1895.
