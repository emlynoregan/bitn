Football.  Inter-Association Match

Northern Areas Association 13.5 defeated North Eastern Association 12.9

Northern Areas has eight teams to choose from and North Eastern has four.  Peter Sullivan of Booborowie won the trophy for the North Eastern Association and Peter Kitschke won the trophy for the Northern Areas.
