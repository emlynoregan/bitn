Burra Town Council, 15 March

Alterations to the men’s section of the toilet block were approved.

Council will contribute £20 towards the building of the toilet block at the Aberdeen tennis courts.

It was resolved that the shed for the ‘Old Jinker’ be erected on a site adjacent to the Fire Station in Market Street.
