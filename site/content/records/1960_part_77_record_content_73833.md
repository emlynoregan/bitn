Burra Golf Club plans a new Club House and intends to borrow $9,000 from the Savings Bank of SA.  The site has been levelled.  A meeting of ratepayers to endorse the choice of site has been called for 10 February.  The total cost is estimated at $25,000and part payment of $9,000 will be made by the Minister of Local Government under Parks & Reserves grants.
