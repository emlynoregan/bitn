Advertisement.  To Tallow-chandlers, ironmongers, saddlers and others.  Tenders are called by the SA Mining Association for the following items:

120,000 candles (Miners’ Dips) strung 12 to the pound.

Iron: square and flat, about 12 tons, Smiths’ bellows and anvils.  100 dozen horseshoes, 3 lb per pair.

Miners’ shovels, axes, files, saws and other items of ironmongery, full particulars of which are available at the office of the Association.

Saddlery: 1 dozen collars, 1 dozen pairs of cart hames, 1 dozen cart saddles complete, 1½ dozen extra breechings for same, 6 whalebone brushes, 6 curry combs, 1 dozen chain back bends.

Sundries: 300 gallons of Black Oil, 50 Bath Bricks, 2 cwt White Lead, 1 cask of Whiting, 1 cask of Chalk and 1 Barrel of Tar.

Goods to be of the best quality and workmanship and to be delivered before the 30 April next.  Terms cash on delivery.
