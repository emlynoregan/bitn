The Caring & Sharing Group was unable to form a committee at its AGM on 14 November, but an extraordinary meeting has been called for 4 December to fill vacancies for Co-ordinator, Secretary, Treasurer, Library Liaison Officer, Morning Tea Hostess and five committee members.  The group is completing its second successful year and is funded by Burra Burra DC and the Tertiary Education Authority of SA and has the support of local doctors and visiting specialists.  Please consider the value of this community service.
