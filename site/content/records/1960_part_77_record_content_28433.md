Burra Football Club held its annual meeting and revealed it was in a sound financial position.  The General Account stood at $627.26 with $310.35 in the Benevolent Fund and $400 in the Social Fund.  Elected: Patron, Reg. Warnes; President, E.C. Pietsch; Vice-President, A. Baulderstone; Secretary, G. Baulderstone and Treasurer, Ray Pietsch.  The trainers are W. Corner and M. Anderson, while A. Fillmore will manage the Colts.
