Burra Swimming Pool is open 2 p.m. to 5.30 p.m. if the temperature as advertised on GTS4 weather is 25°C or more.  It will be open 6.30 p.m. to 9.00 p.m. if the forecast is for 30°C or more.  The supervisor may close the pool if fewer than twelve persons are present.
