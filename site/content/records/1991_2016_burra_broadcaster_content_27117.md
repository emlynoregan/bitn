Sharyn Roberts is Burra Citizen of the Year.  She has been heavily involved with BBH Netball Club, Burra Basketball Association, Burra Golf Club, Burra Community Sports Club, Burra Tennis Club and Burra Softball Club in executive roles.  She moved to Burra in 1979 and was an active member of Burra Rural Youth 1980-84.  She then moved to Adelaide for employment and was moved back in 1989 with Elders, after which she became involved with the townâ€™s sporting bodies.  In netball she has been a delegate and selector to the North Eastern Association, a trainer, coach and umpire.  She had a similar involvement with the Burra Basketball Club and started the Burra Softball Club in the 1994-95 season.  In Rural Youth she held the positions of President and Secretary.  Sharyn is a Customer Service Officer with Elders in Burra.  [Photo.]
