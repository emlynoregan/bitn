Burra Traders’ Association will hold its Easter Street Fair in Market Square on Easter Sunday.  There will be a barbecue and balloons, the old fire engine will offer rides and the Burra Art Group will hold a display on the lawns.  There will also be a Great Traders Easter Egg Hunt.
