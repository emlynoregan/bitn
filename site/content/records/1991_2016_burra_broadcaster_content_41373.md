Notice

Australian Government Australian Heritage Council

The AHC is conducting an assessment of the potential National Heritage Values of the Australian Cornish Mining Heritage Sites at Burra and the Copper Triangle.

It invites owners and occupiers and indigenous people with rights or interests to provide comment by 15 January 2016 on whether these sites should be included on the National Heritage List.
