The ADC School of Dance has classes in the Burra Town Hall every Wednesday during school term.  The Burra U12 Modern Dance Class won Best Overall Class out of many from Burra, Clare &Balaklava classes at the end of year performance at Clare last Saturday.  Burra has U9, U12 and an Open Class.
