Graham M. Kellock

I am a fourth generation resident and with my wife and family have been involved in many organisations in the town and district.  For example:

Rural Youth

Burra Community & Sports Club

Burra School Council

BCS Agricultural Committee

Burra Show Society

Burra & Farrell Flat Football Clubs

Burra PicnicRace Club

Helped to build the Burra Swimming Pool

Councillor since 1985; Chairman for the past three years and Deputy-Chairman for the three before that

Member of the Mid North Development Board

Merino Capital Committee

Lewis Board
