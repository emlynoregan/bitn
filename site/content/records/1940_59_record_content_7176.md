The Mid North High School Sports were held at Riverton on Wednesday between Riverton, Balaklava, Burra, Clare and Kapunda High Schools.  Results giving points scored in each event are printed.  Grand aggregates: Balaklava 67½, Riverton 58½, Clare 55, Burra 50½ and Kapunda 34½.
