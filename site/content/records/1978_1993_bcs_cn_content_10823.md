SA Country Athletics Championships were held at Barmera over the Easter Weekend.

19 athletes from the Burra District gained 65 medals.

Best performances were from:

Graham Kessell – U18 javelin

Craig McDowell – high jump

Tammy Walker, Sally Kellock and Tasma Wooton who all set new school records in discus, in the open, U13 and U14 events respectively.

Rosie Lloyd – U18 400 m and 800 m

Overall best were Petrea Piggott and Michael Kessell who both won 4 gold and 1 silver from their five events.

Michael was named Champion Male Country Athlete.

Rosie Lloyd won 3 gold from 3 events.

The overall results were 27 gold, 22 silver and 16 bronze.
