Burra Swimming Pool.  Work on the new amenities block is proceeding on schedule.  Roof framing is going up.  The pool is due to open 3 December, though completion of the new work is scheduled for early March 2012.  Mackenzie Homes & Commercial is building the new amenities block and Statewide Pools will upgrade filters and pumps in time for the opening of the season.
