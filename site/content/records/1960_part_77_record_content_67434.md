Golf.  Burra’s Club Championship Semi-Finals were won by Peter Dignan & Stan Longford in ‘A’ Grade.  In ‘B’ Grade the winners were D. Villis and J. Bogisch Jnr.  In ‘C’ Grade Allen Baulderstone and E. Zanker were the winners.

Table Tennis.  Burra 17 defeated Spalding 4.

At the end of the third round in ‘A’ Grade Burra Blue and Burra Gold top the table.

In the ‘B’ Grade, with two matches to go, Burra Blue is 5th and Burra Gold is 10th (last).
