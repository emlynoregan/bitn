Naturalisation Ceremony

The first such ceremony in the Burra Burra DC area was held at Mt Bryan on Friday night.  On the stage were Mr M. de N. Lucas, Chairman of the Burra Burra DC, G. Stanley Hawker MP, Hon. E.H. Edmunds MLC, the Mayor of Burra E.T. Baulderstone, Mr E. Willis President of the Mt Bryan Agricultural Bureau, Mr M. Falkai, Mrs J.E. Falkai and Lazzlo Falkai.  The oath of allegiance was taken by the Falkai family.  Mrs Falkai is already a member of the CWA.  Mr Baulderstone spoke for the Burra Town Council.
