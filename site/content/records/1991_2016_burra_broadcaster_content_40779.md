National Merino Challenge.  Three BCS students, Angus Wiech, Ben Heinrich and Soibhan Austin, attended the National Merino Challenge at Adelaide Showgrounds with secondary and tertiary students from across Australia.  They competed in seven activities to get a cumulative score, from which a winner in each division was found.

Angus Wiech gained 3rd place in the secondary division and received some handy souvenirs and $150.

Overall it was a great experience, yielding important knowledge and useful contacts.
