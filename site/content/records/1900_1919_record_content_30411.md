Salvation Army.  The local corps was visited by Mrs Commissioner Hay, Brigadier Sharp and other officers on Friday.  Their visit was to aid the Self Denial Fund.  There was a meeting with the Mayor and Mayoress at the Institute followed by addresses and refreshments in the afternoon.  In the evening at the barracks Mrs Hay spoke on Tragedies of slum life in London and Australia to a fair audience.  Upwards of Â£20 was raised by the visit.
