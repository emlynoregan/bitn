Boy Scouts & Cubs.  The investiture of the boys was held at the Scout Hall in Hanson on Friday night when 18 Scouts and 25 Cubs were invested.  Mr Maurice Davies invested the Cubs and Mr Bill Barker conducted the ceremony for the Scouts.  The movement now goes into recess till 6 February.
