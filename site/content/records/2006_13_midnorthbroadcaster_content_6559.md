Burra CFS has received a new fire truck; Burra 34.  This is the second of the new trucks following one received almost two years ago; Burra 24P.  This has a 2,000 litre water capacity and 4WD.  The new truck has 3,000 litre water capacity and 4WD.  It replaces the two wheel drive Burra 22 appliance.  Burra CFS also recently acquired a 4WD Quick Response Vehicle with 600 litre capacity.
