North Eastern Basketball Association held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs M. Benton; Vice-President, Mrs D. Day and Secretary, Mrs B. Dewhirst. This year Terowie have amalgamated with Hallett.  The age limit of 14 for ‘C’ Grade has been removed. ‘D’ Grade was introduced for Primary School girls only.  Teams for 1968:

Booborowie	A, B, C, D

Burra	A, B, C, D

Clare North	A

Clare South	A
