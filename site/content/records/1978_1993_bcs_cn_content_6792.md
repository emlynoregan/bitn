Burra Yacht Club Regatta Day was a complete success, with a full program of yacht and bike races, model boat demonstrations and refreshments.  Financially the day was better than last year with $2,200 profit.  Combined with activities by Apex, Lions, Scouts etc. the total profit in Burra for the day was somewhere between $3,500 and $4,000.  The League of SA Wheelmen had a very enjoyable day and indicated their willingness to return.
