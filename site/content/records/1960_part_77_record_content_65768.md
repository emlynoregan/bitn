Burra Apex Club.  On Saturday about 250 people attended a Dinner & Cabaret in the Burra Town Hall to see the Burra Apex Club receive its Charter.  Mr Roy Jeffery as Chairman of the District Council congratulated members, especially as they were the first service club in the town.  Jeff Hirschausen, as President of the Burra Apex Club, welcomed guests.  Regional Director Neil Wilkinson gave an address and the charter was presented by Bob England, Immediate Past President of the Zone, and the induction was performed by the President Tom Chapman.  [Members are listed.]  On Sunday visiting Apexians and families were given a barbecue at the Aberdeen Club Rooms.
