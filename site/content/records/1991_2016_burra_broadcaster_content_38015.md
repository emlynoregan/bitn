Oliver Darwin Holthuyzen was the Burra Red Cross Branch Baby of the Year 2004-05 and raised $10,248.17 and received 2nd prize in Category 5 for towns with 1,000 to 2,000 population.  Oliver received a silver mug, a certificate and a bag of goodies from Red Cross.  [Photo.]
