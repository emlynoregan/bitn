Obituary.  Mrs Frank Cahill nee Kathleen Quinn died in Adelaide on Good Friday.  She was a teacher before her marriage, travelling to Streaky Bay by boat and coming home to Mt Bryan once a year for holidays.  She is survived by three sons and three daughters, all of Adelaide.  [Born Elizabeth Kathleen Quinn 23 January 1896 Mt Bryan East: died 12 April 1968 Myrtle Bank, residence Walkerville.]
