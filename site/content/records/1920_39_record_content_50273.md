Kooringa Football Club met at the Institute Lodge Room last week.  Mt Bryan & Aberdeen were dropping out of the Association and Spalding was coming in.  This will create more interest.  Dick Reed the Captain of Aberdeen last year has accepted the position of Vice-Captain of Kooringa.  Other former Aberdeen players will also play for Kooringa.
