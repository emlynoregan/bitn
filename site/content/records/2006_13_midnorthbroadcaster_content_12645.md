Jazz in the Monster Mine is to be re-named following feedback from the public.  The responses indicated people came particularly for the unique setting and great atmosphere, but were interested in a broader range of musical types.  The 2012 event on Saturday 11 February will be called ‘Monster Mine Spectacular’ and will feature Adelaide Band ‘Cornsey’s All Star Rock Band to headline a mix of music from the 60s, 70s, 80s and the current period.
