Robyn Page has been announced as Burra’s Citizen of the Year.  She is a local school teacher and Chair of the J150 Committee.  She has also directed the school’s musical productions and been acting as Senior Student Co-ordinator at BCS.

The Event of the Year Award has gone to the Back to Burra Schools Committee.
