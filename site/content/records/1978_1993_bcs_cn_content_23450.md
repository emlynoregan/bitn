BCS.  Staff changes:

Principal Mike Day will join the Education Review Team for a term in 1992.

Gus Wenzel will be Acting Principal for Term 1 1992.

Mark Butler goes to Le Fevre HS.

Jo Everett goes to Magill Junior Primary School.

Maureen Kemp goes to Morialta HS.

Keith Knight goes to Campbelltown.

Dave Schell will become Co-ordinator at riverton HS.

Roger Boehm, Sue Langford, Cynthia Robinson and Peter Ryan have the right to stay at BCS for a further term under the ten year scheme.

Jim Jackaman comes as Primary Co-ordinator from Willunga.

Geraldine Farmer comes from Oakbank as a secondary science teacher.

Helen Gapp comes to the Early Childhood area.

Mary Abbott returns from leave for Year 3.
