Burra Red Cross has a membership of only 21 and would welcome new members.  Red Cross Calling was the main fund raiser and donors gave $514.  An Opportunity Shop, Trading Table, Raffle and Morning Tea at the Civic Centre raised $485.  With other activities $1,500 was raised in the past twelve months.
