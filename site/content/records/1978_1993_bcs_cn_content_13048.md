Burra Burra Hospital Inc. will hold its Annual Meeting and Election of Board Members and the following members retire on 16 September due to the effluxion of time.

Mr H.B. Quinn	Mr R.L. Thamm	Mr T.R. Drew

Mr R.T. Bailey	Mr J.H. Maidment	Mrs J.S. Tiver

They are eligible for re-election.  Nominations are called by 1 September and a ballot will be held if required on 16 September at the AGM to be held in the Hospital Board Room at 8 p.m.
