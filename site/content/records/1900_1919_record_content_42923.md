Red Cross Bazaar at the Institute last Friday.

The weather was about as bad as possible, but despite that c. £200 was raised.  Mr Crewes introduced Mrs Hawker of ‘Bungaree’ to open the bazaar.  Her speech and descriptions of the stalls etc. extend for c. 1¼ columns.
