Burra Town Hall Concert

The Hall was well filled when over 300 people attended the concert given by Thomas Edmonds, Genty Stevens and Peter Tillett on Saturday night.  After the National Anthem Robert Reed welcomed the artists.  The excerpts from opera were accompanied by the story and anecdotes, which ensured the audience understood the scene.

Program:

Miss Stevens	Love and Music from ‘Tosca’ (Puccini)

As I Sit Here (Sanderson)

The Stars (Montague Phillips)

Poor Wand’ring One from ‘The Pirates of Penzance’ (Gilbert & Sullivan)

Mr Edmonds	The Stock-Rider’s Song (William James)

The Scottish Soldier

Drake Goes West Sanderson)

Danny Boy

Peter Tillett accompanied the above and played the solos:

Toccata (Paridies)
