At Recent AGMs Chris Reed gave way to Mrs Bev Fleming as President of the Tourist Association and Steven Angel was replaced by Mrs Sue Cochrane as President of the Traders’ Association.  Steven in his report remarked on the success of the Traders’ Association with its annual Christmas celebrations, its Australia Day Breakfast and the Street Fairs.
