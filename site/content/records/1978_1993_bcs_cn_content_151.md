30 August 1978

Cricket.  Last Thursday a meeting of the steering committee decided to go ahead with the formation of the Burra Cricket Club, which will field two teams in the local Association in the coming season.  The first AGM will be held tonight at the Kooringa Hotel at 8 p.m.
