Governor’s Visit.  The members of the Burra Burra DC and their wives were introduced to the Governor, Sir James Harrison and Lady Harrison at the Town Hall.  On behalf of the Council Mr Brian Riggs presented the Governor with a framed sketch of Market Square prepared by Maurice Perry and a copy of the Burra Sketchbook.
