Burra Races were run in hot weather, which reduced attendance somewhat.  The main race was the Burra Handicap over 1¼ miles.  1st £50, 2nd £10 & 3rd £5.  Won by H.E. Gregory’s Wee Gun ridden by G. Hale.  Results are printed.
