Burra Regional Army Cadet Unit

The 1st parade on 7 February saw quite a good turn-out.  A parent/sponsor committee was formed with Bob Simpson as Chairperson, Carolyn Nottle as Secretary and Mrs Swift as Treasurer.  After going to Adelaide for testing and assessment, the following structure has been set up:

Officer Commanding	Lt David Jennings ACC

Adjutant 		Lt Peter Rankine ACC

Officers Assisting	Lt Gary Hirschausen ACC

Lt Helen Kurtzer ACC

The unit has two sections and NCOs have been appointed:

Andrew Jennings

Lance Corporals		Kylie Sutherland
