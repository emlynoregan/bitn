Thorogoods of Burra were finalists in the Premier’s Food and Fibre Awards (Innovation) and also in the SA Great Regional Awards.  Tony will give a short presentation on their cider enterprise to a group of 400 business people at as SA Great Luncheon on 30 November, followed by a cider tasting in the Festival Theatre in Adelaide.
