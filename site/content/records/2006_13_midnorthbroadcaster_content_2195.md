Regional Council of Goyder

Burra Christmas Fair Committee was granted $1,250 for street decorations.

Farrell Flat Hall kitchen was granted $500 for renovations.

Burra Town Hall Committee was granted $4,200 for an air conditioner and $1,270 for a ladder.

Council aims to provide a sealed east-west link from Robertstown to Farrell Flat and will contribute one third of the total cost of $2.624 million.  The road is part sealed and another 20.7 km will be sealed over the next three years.
