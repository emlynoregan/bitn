Cycling.  Brian Gould (photo) top SA and former Broken Hill cyclist will attempt to lower the 39-year-old Broken Hill-Adelaide cycling record on 3 & 4 October and will stop in Burra for 10 minutes.  The record was set 6 September 1930 by Jack (Count) Stafford who covered the 314 miles in 22 hours 40 minutes.  His route will follow the highway to Broken Hill via Oodlawirra, Terowie, Burra (c. 8 a.m. Saturday), Manoora, Riverton, Tarlee, Gawler to Adelaide Town Hall.  [Details in Â¾ column.]
