Junior Citizen of the Year: Cheryl Day

Cheryl has an outstanding record in athletics.  She was a member of the BCS team 1986-90 and represented the school in local, interschool and Coca Cola Games and was an individual competitor for Schoolgirlsâ€™ Championships.  She represented Burra and the State in the All Schools Championship in 1987 in Canberra and at the Pacific Games in Sydney.  She competed in the National Titles in Canberra in 1988, Brisbane 1989 and Melbourne 1990 and was a member of the Australian Youth Team to the USA & Canada last July.  She has also been a very active member of the community.
