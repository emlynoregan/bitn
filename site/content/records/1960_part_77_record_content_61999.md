Obituary.  Mr James Goulter, a great supporter of country shows died last week at Crystal Brook aged 81.  For 48 years he was a keen exhibitor of fowls, ducks and geese and it is estimated his birds won 37,000 prizes throughout Australia.  [James Eber Goulter born 1 May 1891 Crystal Brook: died 27 June 1972 Crystal Brook.]
