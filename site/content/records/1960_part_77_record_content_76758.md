Advt.  Elders-GH have instructions from Koonoona Proprietors to auction 2075 ha or 5,127 acres, being portions of the historic Koonoona Station, in 14 conveniently sized lots at Burra Institute 22 October.

1,511 ha south of Koonoona Homestead in 9 lots with frontages to the old Burra-Adelaide Road and to Robertstown Road.  Also 555.3 ha at the northern end of Koonoona and 8.5 km south of Burra, in four lots with frontages to the old Burra-Adelaide Road and Springbank Road.  And 8.9 ha of perpetual lease in one paddock on the Main Adelaide-Burra Road adjacent to Hanson Siding.
