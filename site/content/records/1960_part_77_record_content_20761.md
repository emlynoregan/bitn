Burra Memorial Oval.  The bore has progressed from 80 ft to 170 ft and it is intended to stop at 175 ft.  A test at 95 ft made 400 gallons.  A test on Wednesday last at 115 ft made 1,000 gallons, with the static level of water at 45 ft from the surface.  A test at 145 ft yielded 1,200 to 1,300 gallons per hour.  On Monday morning a test at 100 ft yielded UNREADABLE with the bore then at 160 ft and in the afternoon it was making 2,000 gallons per hour and with water of reasonable quality.
