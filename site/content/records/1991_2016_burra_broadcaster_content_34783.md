BCS, will hold this year’s major fundraiser ‘Variety Night’ in Burra Town Hall on Friday 22 October.  The fundraising target for this year was $5,000.  The fundraising committee this year has assisted in the purchase of:

Chairs for the Junior Primary School that cost $3,000

Sports Uniforms for Years 6-12 at $4,000

New stoves, washing machines and dryer for the Home Economics Centre that cost in all $4,000.
