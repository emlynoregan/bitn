Cricket

The annual wind-up and social evening of the Mokota Cricket Association was held at Booborowie on 6 May.  Patron Mr W.B. Ashley presented the Mokota Shield, for competition among the top four teams, to the Hallett Captain John Honan.  For competition among the lower four teams the Mayfield Shield was presented to Messrs Holmes and Matt Carmody of the Leighton Club.
