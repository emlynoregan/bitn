Burra Footballers Cricket Club held a wind-up at the Commercial Hotel recently for the 1970-71 season.  Trophies were presented:

Best Batting Aggregate (428)	A. Day

Best Batting Average (26.7)	A. Day

Best Bowling Aggregate (19)	J. Ayliffe

Best Bowling Average (19.4)	S. Allan

Best Fielder		D. Robinson

Most Improved Junior	A. White

Best All Round Performance	S. Allan

Life membership was conferred on Mr Ron Wuttke and a suitably inscribed silver tray was presented.  (Mr Wuttke was unable to attend.)
