Burra Town Council.

Cr Hardy moved a letter be written to the Commissioner of Police re vandalism in the town.

It was resolved to write to the Hydraulic Engineer asking if water to extinguish fires would be available and at what cost.  Resolved also to buy 150” of 1½” hose for fire use.
