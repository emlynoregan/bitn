BCS.  The School Council’s tender for $25,156 for the development of the school grounds has been accepted.  This includes developing the hockey field, oval and numerous other areas with trees and shrubs around the buildings.  About eight acres has to be grasses.

Principal Mr McCabe has invited interested parents to join a school curriculum committee.

A successful swimming carnival was held on 25 February, when Riggs won the House Shield.

Individual winners were:
