The Gaining Ground for Goyder Group organised a seminar on ‘Producing for Profit’ on 3 March.  It attracted c. 60 farmers.  The major sponsor was the Primary Industry Bank of Australia.  The speaker was Dr Albert Rovira, an agricultural and environmental consultant, who spoke particularly on root diseases in pastures and crops.  Other speakers were Ian Boothby, Jack McMahon, Steve Ball, Tim Grosser, Mick Faulkner, Mike Krause, Brenton Lynch, Alan Humphris, Joe Keynes and Mark Williams.
