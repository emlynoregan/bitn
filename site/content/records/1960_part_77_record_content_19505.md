Obituary.  Bridget Teresa Preiss died 3 April at Burra Hospital.  She was 93 and was the wife of the late Charles William Preiss and mother of Sylvester, Elizabeth, Margaret, John, Joseph, Veronica, Irene and Francis (deceased).  There are 25 grandchildren and 37 great-grandchildren.

[Born and married as Bridget Clark, but had children as Bridget Teresa, Bridget Theresea and Bridget Theresia Preiss.  Born 19 December 1870 South Rhine SA.  See also 87, 50 (3), 15 Dec. 1964, page 5.]
