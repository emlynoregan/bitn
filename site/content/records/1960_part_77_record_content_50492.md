Burra Burra DC, 16 March

It was resolved that an architect draw up block plans for new Council Chambers and the matter be advertised.

It was resolved that a price be obtained for the renovation of the Council house.

The Highways Department will be asked to close the Bon Accord Bridge and provide a ford in its place.

The SA Housing Trust will be asked to provide rental housing in Burra.

Mr E.C .Allen MP is to be asked to enquire about a Burra North by-pass.

Application is to be made for a special grant of $1,000 for the Eastern Road.
