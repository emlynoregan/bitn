Mt Bryan Churches.  The Mt Bryan Uniting Church closed last year.  It was the last active church in the town.  Last Sunday a plaque was unveiled by Burra’s Catholic Priest Fr Laurie Quinn to commemorate all the town’s former churches: Catholic, Anglican and Methodist/Uniting.  [Photo.]
