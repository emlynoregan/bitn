Pte Crabb was given a social Leighton on 22 January.  Douglas Keynes spoke for the RSL and Mr Roy Humphrys for the Ayers School Committee and the Soldiersâ€™ Welfare Committee.  Mr Crabb had been in charge of Ayers School before volunteering for service and he had taken a prominent part in all sports and amusements.  Other speakers included T.J. Canny and Jack Field.  Mrs E. Jettner presented a parcel from the Cupboards Comforts Fund and Mr Humphrys presented a fountain pen.  Pte Crabb responded.  Then supper and dancing followed.
