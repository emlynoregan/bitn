RSL Annual Reunion Dinner

About 55 attended the dinner on Saturday night.  Representatives came from Riverton, Farrell Flat, Jamestown, Spalding, Booborowie, Hallett and Adelaide.  Rev. John Stephenson proposed ‘Fallen Comrades’ and R.J. Steele proposed ‘The League’.  Ben Kotz as President read the apologies and other toasts were proposed by Keith Hanlin & Arthur Wright.
