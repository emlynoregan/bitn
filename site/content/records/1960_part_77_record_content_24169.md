Burra Primary School

The annual parents’ meeting and the Welfare Club meeting on Tuesday were both well attended with more than 50 at each.  Sports Day will be on 23 April.  Twelve members of the ABC Symphony Orchestra visited the school last week and entertained the children from Grades IV to VII for an hour.
