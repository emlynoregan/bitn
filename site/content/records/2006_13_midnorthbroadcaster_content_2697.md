The Lab Rats were the live band at the Bon Accord last Saturday, their third gig at the venue since forming in 2005.  Headlined by former local girl Stephanie Crowley, they are Mick McClounan (rhythm guitar & vocals), Andy Cienciala (drums & vocals), Craig Avery (lead guitar & vocals) and James Bosworth (bass).
