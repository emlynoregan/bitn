Rabbits are very bad.  One party caught over 1,000 in rugged country, but the skin market has declined for all sorts:	Good bucks	7¾ to 8½d per lb

Seconds		6 to 6½ per lb

Does		3¼ to 3¾d per lb

Small		2 to 3d per lb
