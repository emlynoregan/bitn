Burra Branch of the Mothers’ & Babies’ Health Association held its AGM recently.  Life membership was conferred on Mrs Murray Odgers.  Elected were: President, Mrs Peter McDowell; Senior Vice-President, Mrs Alan Day; Junior Vice-President, Mrs Gladys Hall; Secretary, Mrs Loren Reed; Assistant Secretary, Mrs Sue Ogden and Treasurer, Mrs John Secomb.  There are 20 financial members with an average attendance of 15.
