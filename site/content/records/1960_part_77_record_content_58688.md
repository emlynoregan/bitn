Weather.  Rainfall for the weekend at Burra was 78 points and was accompanied by some hail.  All tennis matches for the Burra Association were cancelled and pennant bowls matches were also cancelled for Burra teams.  Cricket games began, but had to be abandoned when Spalding was 1 for 23 v. Burra Footballers.
