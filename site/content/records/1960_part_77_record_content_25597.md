Accident.  Miss D.A. Ezra of Broken Hill lost control of a VW sedan on the bend at Burra Racecourse on Friday at 10.45 p.m.  The car rolled over.  Of the four occupants two suffered bruising only and one was treated at the Burra Hospital for a lacerated forehead.  The front passenger was the only one wearing a seat belt and the only one uninjured.
