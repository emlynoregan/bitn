Burra Visitor Information Centre has received a lifeline from the Goyder Council with a $15,000 rescue package agreed to yesterday.  BRTBA was offered an interest free loan facility of $15,000 over a 7.5 year period, from the Burra Ward Capital Fund.  The conditions for the loan are:

Complete fianancial statements to be provided to the Finance Manager monthly.

The Business Plan and a revamp of governance procedures are to be provided by the April 2011 Council meeting.

A second Council representative (Jane Kellock) will be included on the BVIC Management Committee.
