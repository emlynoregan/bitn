Burra Burra DC.  Applications are called for the position of Caretaker of Porter Lagoon Recreational Area.  The successful applicant will have to maintain the toilets, refuse bins etc. at the area and collect fees from boat owners for the seasonal or daily use of the Lagoon.  These will form the remuneration for the position.
