Vandalism.  St Mary’s has had vandals in who stole two small organ pipes, misplaced others and damaged two.  Replacement pipes will cost c. $10.  The theft was from the 15th stop added recently by Rev. G.D .Sibly.  It is not known when the theft could have occurred.
