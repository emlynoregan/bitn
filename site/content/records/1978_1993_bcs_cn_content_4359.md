Burra Burra DC Chairman Ray Jennison has been presented with a gavel made from original Burra Mine timbers by Mr Brian Polkinghorne of the Court House Co-operative.  Brian thanked the Council for all their help and interest in the co-operative.  This is the first article the co-operative made from old mine timber.
