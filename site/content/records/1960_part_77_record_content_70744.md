Accident.  A two-car collision in heavy fog occurred near Brown’s Corner three miles west of Burra on Sunday morning.  Four people were injured: Mrs Claudia Mitchell suffered a fractured leg and hip and lacerations, John Mitchell suffered a ruptured spleen and broken knee cap, Wayne Brereton, the driver of to other car, suffered concussion and lacerations, as did his passenger, Dean Bogisch.  Mr & Mrs Mitchell and Dean were later transferred to the RAH.
