Burra Water Tank Replacement.  To meet growing demand SA Water is replacing the water tank on West Street [i.e. on Mine Hill].  Work commenced this week and is expected to be completed in mid-2016.  All precautions will be taken in removing asbestos from the roof of the existing tank.  The new tank will be of 2 million litre capacity.
