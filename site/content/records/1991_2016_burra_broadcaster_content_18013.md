Tourism grants this year included: a one-off grant of $10,000 for local heritage purposes, $3,600 for a landscaping plan for Paxton Square, $877 to BCS to support sporting activities, but Goyder Council’s bid to get $795,000 for a Copper Interpretive Centre was unsuccessful.
