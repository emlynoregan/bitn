Obituary.  Mrs Dafney Kakoschke of Burra North died on 25 January. She was the widow of the late Gus Kakoschke and mother of Sheila, Brudy, Isabel, Harold, Iris and Gordon.

[Born as Dafferty Wall 4 November 1897 at Hampton (Burra), but married as Dafney Wall.]
