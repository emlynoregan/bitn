Tanya Waters of Burra won the Best in Exhibition prize at the Clare Rotary Art Exhibition.  Her prize of $1,500 sponsored by the Clare & Gilbert Valley Council is an Acquisitive Award.  This year there were a record 505 entries with 54 in the Youth Section.
