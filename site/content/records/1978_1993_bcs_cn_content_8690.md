BCS at Coca Cola Games in Adelaide last weekend.

The school sent eight students as part of the Country West Team.  Three came home with four gold, two silver and two bronze medals between them.

Rosie Lloyd	Gold in the U19 400 m hurdles

Gold in the 4 x 100 m relay team

Bronze in the U17 400 m hurdles

Bronze in the 4 x 400 m relay team

Leanne Messenger	Gold in the U19 100 m

Gold in the U19 200 m

Silver in the Long Jump

Paul Nation	Silver in the 800 m
