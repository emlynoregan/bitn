Notice.  Burra Town Council.  Increased charges at Burra Cemetery from 2 November 1942.

10 ft grave	£2-5-0

6 ft grave	£1-2-6

7 ft grave	£1-10-0

8½ ft grave	£1-15-0

Stillborns	£0-11-6

Single Block	£2-10-0

Double Block	£5-0-0

Extras: removing cement 5/- and Sunday Burials 7/6.
