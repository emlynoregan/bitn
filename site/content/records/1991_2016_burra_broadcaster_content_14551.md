Jamie Pollard has been appointed caretaker of Redbanks Reserve.

Management of the Hallett Pool has been transferred to Hallett Community Management Committee with Joy McDowell as pool manager for this season.

Hallett Community Development Authority has been dissolved with assets equally shared between Hallett and Terowie Community Management Committees (recently formed).

Robertstown Special Event Committee has been dissolved and its reserve funds of $1,490 transferred to the Robertstown Community Management Committee (recently formed).

Burra Main Street Committee is being contacted to establish their interest in managing the Town Hall.
