The original District of Burra was proclaimed on 27 November 1872.  Arrangements are in hand for a Centenary Dinner in the Town Hall on 27 November 1972.  The Minister for Local Government, Hon G.T. Virgo will attend, along with Councillors and wives and former Councillors and wives, or widows.  Former Councillors will be invited from the merged Councils of Burra, Hanson, Booborowie and Mt Bryan.  Information is sought on the names and addresses of any of the above: please contact the District Council Office.
