Burra Burra National Trust held its AGM last Thursday

The Chairman’s report highlighted initiatives for the past year:

Soundscapes installed at the Bon Accord Museum

Repairs at Morphett’s Enginehouse made progress.

Facilities at the Bon Accord cottage were upgraded.

The Museum Pass was introduced.

Various occupational health and safety procedures were addressed.

Elected: Chairman, Raymond Parker; Vice Chairman, Maureen Wright; Secretary, Susan Parker and Treasurer, Barry Wright.

Guest speaker was Mr Alan Graham, recently appointed CEO of National Trust SA, who spoke of the present position of the Trust, its priorities and future plans.
