Obituary.  Rebecca Parker died 8 January at Burra Hospital aged 92.  She was the wife of the late George Parker and mother of Vida, Mill, Cecil, Cass, Lill & Joe (deceased).

[Born Rebecca Flower 27 May 1869 at Stony Gap.  See extended obituary 92, 3, 23 Jan. 1962, page 5.]

[Mill was Mildred Calliss, Cass was Mary Catherine and Lill was Rachael Lillian.]
