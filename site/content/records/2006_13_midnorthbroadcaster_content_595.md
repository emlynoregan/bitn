Boolcoomatta Station of 63,000 ha has been declared a conservation park.  It has been handed over to the Australian Bush Heritage Fund to manage it.  The $2 million purchase price was funded with a $1.3 million grant from the Federal Government and $700,000 from the Nature Foundation of SA.  It lies to the west of Broken Hill.
