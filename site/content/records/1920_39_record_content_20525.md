Burra Motor Sporting Club held a motor cycle race on 21 August from near the rifle butts on the main road, over Brewery Hill and Breakneck then via the stock road behind the rifle butts and a hill climb in M. Collins’s Brewery Paddock.  Six competitors took part.  Jack Williams on his 2¼ h.p. Raleigh came first.  In car driving between pegs W.H. Spier won in his Amilcar.  Riding between pegs for motor cycle was won by J. Priess on a 2¼ h.p. Raleigh.  About 200 people attended with 20 cars and 14 motor cycles.
