Burra Kindergarten held its AGM and elected: Patroness and Public Officer, Mrs E. Carpenter; President, Mr P. Fiebig; Vice-President, Mrs Marilyn Fiebig; Secretary, Brenda Villis and Assistant Secretary, Marcia Baulderstone.  In the year fund raising produced $1,170 gross and $807.44 nett.
