BCS.  Year 12 results were good with most Year 12 students gaining entry to a University, a Business College Course or TAFE.  Natalie Jennings and Sheree Jones gained perfect scores in Typing.

Natalie Jennings – University of SA

Sheree Jones – a career in Office Administration

Sheridan Coad – A Banking & Finance Course

Karen Jennings – a Physical Education Teaching Course

Allan Pearce – a BA at Magill Campus

Daniel Neill & Adam Lomman – Agricultural Production at Roseworthy Campus

Donna Clapp – B. Ed. At Flinders University

Belinda Sichler – Tourist & Hospitality at TAFE

Matthew Opperman, Rob Gill, Christie McCann, Christian White and Bianca Dunstan have all gained employment.
