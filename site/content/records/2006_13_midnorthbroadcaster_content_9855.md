Portrait Exhibition

There was a gallery of faces at the Burra Community School on Thursday night for the awarding of prizes relating to a portrait exhibition.  The winning portraits by the students will be displayed at the Burra Regional Art Gallery as part of its Portrait Exhibition.  Winners were:

Reception/Year 1	Posie Dunn

Year 2/3	Jack Rayson

Year 4/5	Sam Chandler

Year 6/7	Trudy Potter

Year 8/9	Thomas Drew

Year 10	Morgan Mayne

There were certificates for other places and for highly recommended efforts.
