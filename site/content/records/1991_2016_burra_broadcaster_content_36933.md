Burra Probus Club hosted the entertainment group ‘Just Us’ on 21 June.  The group presented an ‘Over 55s Concert’ at the old Court House in Burra North.  Joining Burra Probus members in the audience were members of Beefacres & Kadina Probus Clubs, the Leisure Activity Group and Burra Senior Citizens.
