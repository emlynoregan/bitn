Obituary.  Mr Ron Pearse, formerly of Koonoona, died on 28 May.  He was long an active member of the community, being the inaugural chairman in 1970 of the Barker Cottage Board and was also Chairman of the Burra Hospital Board for many years as well as being a noteworthy figure in the stud Merino industry.
