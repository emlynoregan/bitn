National Simultaneous Story Time was conducted last week.  At 11 a.m. across the country 100,000 people gathered to hear the story of Pete the Sheep.  At Burra Community School students from reception to year two gathered to hear librarian Bev Low read the story with visual props supplied by Principal Jim Low.
