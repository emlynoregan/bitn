Burra Rifle Club.  The final stage of the Championship was shot on Saturday 9 April

The Off the Rifle Champion was C. Edwards with 352, just ahead of G. Webster on 351.

In the Handicap Section Ed Hopkins was the winner with 348, with A.G. Heinrich on second place with 346.
