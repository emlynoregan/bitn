Burra Tourism association.  A meeting of tourism operators in Burra last Thursday resolved to establish an association – though they were not anxious to add another organisation to the town’s supply and so the Burra Traders’ Association will be asked to reconsider its role or even to disband in favour of a single new body to cover both roles.
