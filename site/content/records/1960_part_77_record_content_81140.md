BCS Year 7 students recently went for a camp at Christie Downs from Monday to Friday.

Students reported on the experience in an article of a little more than 2 columns.

Monday

The group travelled by train to Adelaide and then by train to Christie Downs before walking for 20 minutes to the camp site.  The rest of the day was spent in the local area.

Tuesday was spent at the beach.

Wednesday

They went by train to Adelaide, visiting the Public Library, the War Memorial and Parliament House.  After lunch in the Botanic Gardens they returned to Parliament House to witness Question Time and then went to Elder Park.

Thursday

There was a scavenger hunt and sports at the camp.

Friday

There were games and packing up as well as tidying the camp in the morning.  In the afternoon they went to a skating rink before catching the evening train to Burra.
