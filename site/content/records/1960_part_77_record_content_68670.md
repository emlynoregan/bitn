A Barbecue and Gymkhana will be held at the Burra Showgrounds on Sunday.  Adults $1, Children 5-14 $0.05.  It will be opened by Dr Bruce Eastick, Leader of the SA Opposition.  Proceeds will aid Frome LCL Electorate Committee Funds.
