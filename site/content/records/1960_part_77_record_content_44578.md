Classrooms were open to inspection before the meeting.  Though it had spent over $900 for the year, it still held $450.  It was decided to put $60 towards a new Gestetner machine, to buy new sprinklers for the oval, to purchase a Freeway Sports Combination Unit and to donate $15 to each teacher for the purchase of aids etc.  Elected: President, Mrs Lola Jennison; Secretary, Mrs Avis Webster and Assistant Secretary & Treasurer Mrs Margaret Alderson.  Mrs Kirk, the retiring President, reported that the expenditure for the year had been $932 and the balance was $454.  The library received $457 worth of new books.  Service provided at the annual sports day raised $157 despite unkind weather.  Instead of a number of smaller events, money making was concentrated on the Gala Evening, which raised a nett $261.  When the new prefabricated building was completed the teachers gained a staffroom and curtains, paint, a cupboard etc. were provided by the Welfare Club.  The Club will contribute $150 towards a new amplifier, which has been ordered.  A variety of teaching aids and sports equipment has been purchased.
