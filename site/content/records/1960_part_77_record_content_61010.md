Wool Day.  The Australian Wool Board is organising a Wool Day at the Burra Town Hall foe 20 June.   Burra Red Cross is sponsoring this wool promotion day, which will feature illustrated talks, commentaries, question time and competitions. A lavish wardrobe of wool fashion will be on show.  A competition will find the speediest knitter.
