Legislative Council Election last Saturday saw the return of Messrs Lucas and Pascoe.  Results:

Burra Division	North-Eastern District overall

Lucas	479	2,759

Pascoe	597	2,751

Moody	281	1,508

Russell	334	1,420

(Plumpers were not significant.)
