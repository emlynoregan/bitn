BCS.  The Student Representative Council is hosting a Farmers’ Breakfast this month to raise funds for the ‘Aussie Helpers’ Foundation.  The foundation helps people dealing with genuine hardship, including those affected by drought, bushfires and floods.  It also distributes aged pensioner food relief in rural communities.
