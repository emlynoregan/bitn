‘Barton Hill’ was sold at Manoora Hall in April in lots to various buyers:

132 ha to Tilba Trust for $300,000.

49 ha to the Eckermann family of Waterloo for $100,000.

123 ha to S.A. Nicholls of Waterloo for $195,000.

163 ha to the Faint family of Saddleworth for $257,000.
