Aberdeen Football Club will continue this year as a member of the Mid North Association, despite rumours to the contrary.  The AGM on 14 February elected President, M.C. Broad; Vice-President, P. Reed; Secretary, G. Hirschausen and Treasurer, Arnold Heinrich. They will enter ‘A’ Grade, ‘B’ Grade and Senior Colts teams.
