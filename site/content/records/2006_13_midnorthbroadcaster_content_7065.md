Advt.  The Commercial Hotel in Burra has changed hands.  The new owner is Adrian Gill.  The bar manager is Helen Carmody.   A new menu will be available seven days a week, including fresh fish delivered weekly from Wallaroo.  The hotel is a strong supporter of local sporting clubs and has an active social club.  Sporting TAB facilities, Sky TV and pokies are all available.
