Fires.  Three CFS units quickly responded to a grass and crop fire that threatened the homestead of Cappeedee last Thursday and were able to prevent its spread.  Eleven units fought a fire on Sunday at White Hut, confining the damage to 50 acres. [c. 20 ha.]
