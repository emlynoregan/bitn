8 March 1992

Clean Up Australia Day on 1 March saw about 40 residents participating along the Burra creek.  Almost a truckload of rubbish was collected.

BCS.  Last week the Years 7-12 squad competed at the Interschool Carnival at the Adelaide Aquatic Centre.  The girls finished 5th overall and the boys 6th.

BCS.  Year 8 students spent a week at Barmera during week four of the term.

Monday: visit to Barmera Estate Winery & to Berrivale & swimming in Lake Bonney.

Tuesday: Nookamka Aquatic Centre for sailboarding, kayaking, canoeing & small boat handling.  Problem solving activities.

Wednesday: Monash Playground, Joe Bredl’s Reptile Park, Renmark Pool & a barbecue at Paringa.

Thursday: Aquatic Centre and Camp Concert.

Friday: Clean-up and return to Burra.

[Photo of the yachting experience.]

Mr & Mrs Eric Ashby will be given a community farewell at the Hallett Oval at noon on Sunday 15 March.
