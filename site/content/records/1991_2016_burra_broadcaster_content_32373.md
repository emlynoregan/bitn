A Woody Weeds Field Day will be held at Sturtvale Station on Wednesday 12 November, conducted by the Eastern Districts Soil Conservation Board.  It will concentrate on control measures for a large infestation of Boxthorn at Manunda Creek.  Onion weed will be a secondary focus.
