Obituary.  Jamil Said (68), brother of Lyla Rasheed of Glynde, died on Tuesday 30 January.

Mrs Rasheed left Burra last week to live at Glynde and was farewelled from Burra.  Her son Sheff Rasheed has taken over the Commercial Hotel.
