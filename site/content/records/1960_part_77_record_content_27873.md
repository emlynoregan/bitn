Burra Sub-Branch of ALP

Senator Reg Bishop was guest speaker on 24 January.  Others were Senator Arnold and the endorsed candidate for Wakefield, Jack Phelan.  Senator Drury outlined efforts of Labor Members to get improved benefits for ex-servicemen from the Boer War and WWI.
