Council has resolved to assist the Community Library by increasing the annual book allocation from 290 titles to 571 titles.  It has also agreed to assist with funding proposed extensions to the library staff facilities for an estimated cost to Council of $960.

Council is concerned about stray dog control.

From 1 March owners of stray dogs will be liable for an on the spot fine of $20 or a penalty of up to $100.
