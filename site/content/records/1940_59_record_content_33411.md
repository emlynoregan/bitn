Obituary.  Lancelot Motherall was killed yesterday at North Mine Broken Hill in a fall of stone.  Although he was extricated with all possible speed he died about an hour later.  He was born in Burra 42 years ago, the son of Mrs M.A. & the late Mr H.R. Motherall.  Before going to Broken Hill about 10 years ago he was employed at Curlew Mine Mongolata as a part owner, before which he worked on his fatherâ€™s eastern pastoral property.  He was educated at Hanson School.  He was not married.
