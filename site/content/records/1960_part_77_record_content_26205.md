Obituary.  Mr Gus Mas late of Saddleworth died two hours after getting off the liner Orsova following his return from Spain.  After living in Australia for just over 60 years, he returned to his native Spain about two years ago and had just decided to live out his days back in Australia.  [Augustin Mas died 22 September 1965 at Woodville, being of no fixed abode.  He was aged 74.]
