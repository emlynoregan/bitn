Burra & District Floral Art Club held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs R. Fuller and Secretary, Mrs R. Pritchard.  A flower display will be held again this year to aid the floor appeal.  Membership in 1967 was 25, up from 17 in 1966.  Average at meetings was 11.
