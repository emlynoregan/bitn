BCS.  The first speech day for BCS will be held in the Memorial Gardens at 11 a.m. next Friday.  Guest speaker is Mr Kevin Duggan, SA Crown Prosecutor.  The Primary students held their sports day on Friday 26 November.  Champions were:

Junior Girl	A. Stockman

Junior Boy	P. Clarke & A. Allen (tie)

Senior Girl	L. Mason

Senior Boy	K. Lynch
