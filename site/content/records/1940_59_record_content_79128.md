The official party comprised: The mayor Mr E.T. Baulderstone and Mrs Baulderstone, The Minister of defence Sir Philip McBride and Lady McBride, The Member for Burra Mr P.H. Quirke MP and Mrs Quirke, Chairman of the Burra Burra DC Mr A.D. Radford and Mrs Radford and the Town Clerk Mr E.R. Davey and Mrs Davey.  Mr Baulderstone outlined the importance of the Town Hall and the proposed improvements to it and welcomed the official guests.  The debutantes were:
