North Eastern Small Bore Association held a Prize Shoot at Burra on 7 April and drew shooters from Broken Hill, Renmark, Morgan, Waikerie, Burra, Riverton, Clare, Terowie, Peterborough, Port Pirie, Port Augusta and Whyalla.  The only possible for the day was scored by W. Jenkins of Clare.  In the aggregate M. Anderson of Pt Augusta was first and in the handicap aggregate S. Birt of Terowie came first with W. Corner of Burra in second place.

On Tuesday night over double 50 yards M. Thomas, P. Mengersen & M. Alderson scored best.
