Advt.  Elders-GM will conduct a sale of land in the Burra RSL Hall 11 May for the proprietors of Mt Glen View Stud Pty Ltd of ‘The Pines’ and ‘Glen Dare’ Merino Stud.

‘The Pines’ 889 acres freehold on the northern edge of Burra Township: Part Sections 7, 8, 9, 30, 2278 & section 316 Hundred of Kooringa with stone bungalow of 8 rooms etc.

‘Glen Dare’ 1,953 acres freehold c. 9 miles northeast of Mt Bryan to be offered in four lots.
