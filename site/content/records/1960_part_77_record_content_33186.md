Farrell Flat School.  The enrolment is 36, with three new Grade I students.  Mr A. Buckingham came from Glenburnie near Mt Gambier to become Headmaster.  He replaces Mr K. Reincke who has gone to Ramco School as Headmaster.  Miss A. Archer is in charge of Grades I, II & III.
