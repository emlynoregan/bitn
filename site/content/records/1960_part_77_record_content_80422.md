Accident/Obituary.  A Piper Cherokee 180 aircraft crashed east of Mt Bryan Township soon after 8 a.m. on Friday in thick fog.  The crash was about two miles northeast of Belcunda Homestead.  The four people on board died instantly.  The pilot was Quentin Thorpe of Victor Harbor and his wife, Helen and the other passengers were his parents, Mr & Mrs V. Thorpe of Peake.
