The Burra Cycling Classic will take place on Saturday 7 August, promoted by the League of SA Wheelmen.  The 160 km Cycling Handicap will begin at 10 a.m.  The route will be Burra, Mt Bryan, Burra, Saddleworth, Marrabel, and Kapunda with a finish on the Freeling to Gawler Road.  Traffic lights and traffic have long prevented an Adelaide finish.  40-50 riders are expected to compete.
