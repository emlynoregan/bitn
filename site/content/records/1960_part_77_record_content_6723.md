Marriage, Auburn Methodist Church 8 April

Janet Stephens, only daughter of Mr & Mrs M.B .Stephens of Auburn, married

Don Schwier, only son of Mrs M. Schwier & the late Mr L.P. Schwier of Burra North.

The couple will live at Riverton where Don is employed in the PMG Depot.
