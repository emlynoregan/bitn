Table Tennis Tournament at Booborowie by the North-Eastern Table Tennis Association last Wednesday and Thursday attracted 55 entrants.

Men’s Singles Champion	A. Day

Men’s Doubles Champions	A. Day & C. van der Does

Men’s Handicap Singles	B. Hall

Mixed Handicap Doubles	A. Phillips & D. Richardson

Ladies’ Handicap Doubles	C. Angel & D. Richardson

Men’s Handicap Doubles	N. Kelly & D. Gent

Mixed Doubles Champions	C. van der Does & D. Day

Junior Girls’ Singles Champion	D. Richardson

Boys’ Singles Champion	N. Webster

Junior Girls’ Handicap Singles	C. Angel

Junior Boys’ Handicap Singles	N. Webster

Men’s No. 1 Plate	W. Warner

Ladies’ No. 1 Plate	A. Webster

Men’s No. 2 Plate	B. Hall

Ladies No. 2 Plate	J. Goodridge
