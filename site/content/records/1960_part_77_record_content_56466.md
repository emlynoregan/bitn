Burra Burra DC, 3 May

Tenders are to be called for construction of caravan park amenities.

Plans for office alterations are to be submitted next meeting.  The plans are to include access to the ladies public toilet.

Burra Town Hall renovations are to include

Installation of one pedestal in the men’s toilets.

Replacement of ladies’ toilet pedestals.

Floor coverings for the dressing room.

A moveable screen to divide the dressing room.

Costs for the maintenance of the Ambulance-EFS building are to be apportioned 60% Ambulance and 40% EFS or DC.

Cr Jennison’s motion to donate $100 to the Burra Mine Buildings Preservation Fund lapsed for want of a seconder.

Crs T. Quinn & Phillips motion to donate $50 was carried.

Burra Tourist Promotion Committee wrote about re-instating the old drinking fountain.  Council will inspect it and ascertain the cost.
