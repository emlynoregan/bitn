Burra Small Bore Club.  On Sunday the Burra team won the annual teams’ shoot for the Williams’ Shield held at Burra.  The shield has been competed for each year for ten years and at last Burra has won it.  Burra 2,624 defeated Morgan 2,408 and Clare 2,327. Top scorer for the day was M. Stanley (Morgan) 380.6 and for Burra L. Moore, 351.3.
