St John Ambulance Brigade gave a First Aid Demonstration in Burra Town Hall on Thursday night before about 60 people.  The Mayor welcomed the unit and the audience.  He also made an appeal for more cadets and senior members.  Mouth to mouth artificial respiration was demonstrated on a manikin.
