Mrs Edith O’Leary nee Berryman celebrated her 100th birthday on 8 December.  She was born on a sheep station near Burra.  [This would appear to be Ada O’Leary who was born Ada Berryman 8 December 1867 at Spring Bank, Burra, and who died 22 March 1971 at Dulwich.  She married Arthur O’Leary in 1919.]
