Kevin Wallis

Resident in Burra for 8½ years

Joined the Council in 1993

Served on several committees to enhance Burra’s tourist status

Sat on the Tourist Resource Committee, the Cemetery Committee and the Mine Museum Committee among others

Hopes to be re-elected to help carry through the reconstruction of the tourist management arrangements that has been started

Wishes to be able to continue work on such ongoing projects as:

Securing Government commitment to seal the Burra-Morgan Road

The reopening of Redbanks Reserve

Development of the Art Gallery
