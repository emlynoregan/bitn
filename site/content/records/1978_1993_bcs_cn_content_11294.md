Burra Burra Hospital Inc.

The following Board Members retire on 10 September due to the effluxion of time.

Messrs H.B. Quinn, T.R. Drew, M.B. Scholz, R.L. Thamm, R.T. Bailey and Mrs J.S. Tiver.

They are all eligible for re-election.  Nominations close 31 August.

A ballot if required will be held at the AGM on 10 September at 8 p.m. in the Boardroom of the Hospital.
