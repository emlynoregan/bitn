Twelve took part in the Gallipoli Landing on 25 April 1915:

Harold King (19)	Esson Rule (20)	Fred Wheatley (36)

Fred Harvey (25)	George Thomson (22)	Vernon Day (17)

Hartley Harris (25)	Arthur Stewart (29)	Eric Roach (24)

John Camp (23)	Harry Skinner (23)	Roy Baynes (24)

John Camp died of wounds on the hospital ship HMHS Gascon on 15 May and was thought then to be the first Burra boy to be killed in the was at Gallipoli.  Later it was reported that Harry Skinner was missing in action and subsequently it was established he had been killed on 2 May.
