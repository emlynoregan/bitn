Trek ’92

31 groups of senior secondary students are undertaking a link-walk between Parachilna and Cape Borda on Kangaroo Island over a period of three months.

Marion HS took over from a group of Duke of Edinburgh Award Scheme walkers at Mt Bryan East on Thursday 13 August and handed over to Nuriootpa HS students at BCS on Monday 17 August.
