RSL Members visited local schools recently to speak of their experiences and of the significance of Anzac Day.  Burra, Booborowie and Mt Bryan Schools were visited.  The schools joined together for a service last Thursday in the BCS courtyard.  RSL members in attendance included Brian Riggs, Len Simpson, Marty Hogan, Ben Kotz, Bill Kearney, Ron Kelly and Harry Quinn, with Bob Disher from Booborowie.
