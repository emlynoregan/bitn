County Athletics Championships at Barmera

BCS sent a team of six and came away with 4 golds, 5 seconds and 3 thirds.

Rosalie Lloyd (U15): 1st in 1,500 m, 1st in 400 m and 2nd in 800 m

Danny Greet (U17): 1st in 1,500 m and 1st in 800 m

Greg Wall (U15): 2nd in 800 m and 2nd in shot put

Libby Robinson (U17): 3rd in 100 m and 2nd in javelin

Sally Hayes: 3rd in 100 m

Fiona Wheeler: 2nd in 800 m walk
