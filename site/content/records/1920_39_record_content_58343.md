LCL Burra Women’s Branch heard an address from the LCL General Secretary, Mr A.S. Dunk, on Friday last, in the Eastern Telephone Building.  President Mrs I.J. Warnes presided.  The Hon. A.L. McEwin MLC also attended.  [The speeches of both men are reported in c. 1½ columns.]
