Burra High School enrolments were 144 compared with 125 last year.  There were 76 boys and 68 girls.

Leaving 11, Intermediate 39, 2nd Year 38 and 1st Year 56.  Newcomers are named as are prefects and house leaders.  Mr J. Murphy just arrived from England with his family, commenced duty on Tuesday.
