Simon Butcher has been appointed new director of Burra Out of School Hours Care, taking over from Jodie Quinn next Monday.  Simon has been working in child care for more than six years in Adelaide and spent eight years teaching English in Latvia.  Jodie has been director since January 2005.  The program for the coming holidays will run 15 December to 23 December and for three weeks from 4 January 2009.
