Probus Club AGM was held in March and officers for 1997-98 are: President, Bob Skinner; Vice-Presidents, Freda Pink & Marty Hogan.  Linda Sutton is relieving secretary.

The Isolated Children’s Parents’ Association annual conference will be held in the Burra Town Hall this year.  Dot Sandland, life member of the North Eastern branch will open it and the guest speaker will be Xavier Minniecon from the ABC.  The Minister of Education, Rob Lucas will attend.
