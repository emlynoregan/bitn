Schools

The Northeast Combined School Sports Days was hosted in April by Hallett.  C. 140 children from Mt Bryan, Booborowie, Spalding and Hallett Primary Schools took part.

BCS staged the musical Joseph and the Amazing Technicolour Dreamcoat by Andrew Lloyd Webber in October.

The old Mt Bryan East school building was renovated for walkers on the Heysen and Mawson Trails.
