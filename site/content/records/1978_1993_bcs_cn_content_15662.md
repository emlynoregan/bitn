BCS.  Speech Day will be on Friday 18 December in the school gymnasium.  Guest speaker is Robert Oatey, outstanding SA sportsman and former Captain Coach of Norwood Football Club and also a tennis player and coach of note.

BCS Athletics

Adam Day set a new U16 record for the 300 m hurdles two weeks ago at 40.6 seconds, ).4 seconds faster than the old record.  Last Saturday he set a new state record in the U16 400 m hurdles of 57.86 seconds.  The previous record was 58.22 seconds.

Tom Secomb won the right to compete in the Nationals in Canberra next March along with Adam Day and Petrea Piggott.  Adam and Petrea fly to Brisbane tomorrow to contest the National All Schools Championships.

Andrew & Jill Thamm and children left Burra on Monday to live in Darwin for three years, where Andrew has a posting with the National Australia Bank.

John Lloyd of Burra, who has a degree in Engineering, has obtained a position in Melbourne and Carmel and the two boys will follow him soon.

Bob Angel retired from Telecom last Friday on notching up his three score years.
