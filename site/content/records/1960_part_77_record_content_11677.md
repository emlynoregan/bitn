Burra Primary School will host a conference on Friday 22 June for 35 teachers from as far north as Yunta and Olary and south to Manoora as well as from the Pt Pirie District.  It will be directed by Mid-North Inspector Mr A.E. Wood.  Assistant Superintendent of Rural Schools Mr K. Polkinghorne will open it.  Tow Inspectors from Kenya will also attend.
