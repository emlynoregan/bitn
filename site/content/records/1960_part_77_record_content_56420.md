Football.  Mid North League

‘A’ Grade	SM United 15.15 defeated Aberdeen 7.7

Riverton 16.17 defeated Min-Man 12.13

Robertstown 22.19 defeated Auburn 3.3

‘B’ Grade	Aberdeen 12.9 defeated SM United 0.3

Riverton 7.13 defeated Min-Man 6.1

Colts	SM United 11.11 defeated Aberdeen 2.1

Riverton 16.10 defeated Min-Man 2.2

Auburn forfeited to Robertstown

Junior Colts	SM United 4.8 defeated Aberdeen 0.0

Riverton 7.8 defeated Min-Man 6.1

The Mid North and North Eastern leagues are discussing amalgamation, which would produce 10 ‘A’ Grade sides, 10 ‘B’ Grade sides, 10 Colts and 10 Junior Colts sides.
