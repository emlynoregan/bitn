Jennison’s Tyre Service advises that as from 1 November 1985 they have sold their tyre business to R.W. & G.R. Smedley, trading as Burra Tyres, who will operate from 25 Commercial St Burra.  Jennison’s will continue with the Ford and Toyota Commercial Franchise and BVS Electrical Sales from their premises at 28-30 Commercial Street.
