Theft.  Police are warning people against trespassing on property and stealing sheep, baled wool and firewood.  G.F. Lomman of Murong, twenty miles east of Burra, reports the loss of 107 mixed sheep and 50-60 lambs.  K.R. Phillips of ‘The Gums’ reports the loss of a bale of wool.  E.G. & D.M. Kellock are missing three bales of wool.

(Bales of wool are valued at c. £100 each.

Mr & Mrs I. L. Auhl have been introduced to the Burra Primary School Welfare Club.  Mr Auhl takes over as headmaster from Mr K. Horsnell.

The Farrell Flat by-pass road has now been completed along with the new access roads to the town.

Burra & District choral Society has elected: President, Mr J. Oates; Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs T. O’Connor and Conductor, Mr R. Mueller.  Accompanist is Mrs J. Oates and assistant is Mrs J. Carpenter.
