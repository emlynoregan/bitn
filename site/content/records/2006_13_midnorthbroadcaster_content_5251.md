Burra’s Sharyn Roberts has become the first female manager of an Elders branch in SA.  She was in Adelaide for five years before coming to Burra and has been with Elders for 23 years in total.

Carmen Jacka from Clare has been appointed Elders Merchandise Sales Representative in Burra.
