Burra Regional Art Gallery has a new exhibition titled ‘Seesaw’, which explores the issues of reconciliation between Aboriginals and others in Australia.  The exhibition comes from Ceduna and showcases the talents of four indigenous artists and four white Australian artists.  It runs until 20 July.
