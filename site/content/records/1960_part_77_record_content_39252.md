Anzac Service.  The Port Pirie Pipe Band led the march at Burra on Sunday.  After the wreath laying at the Memorial the service was conducted in the RSL Hall.  In the evening a combined service was held in the Redruth Methodist Church, conducted by Rev. Milton Edwards with Rev. Geoff Sibly (Anglican) giving the address and reading the lesson, as his last official function as Rector of Burra.
