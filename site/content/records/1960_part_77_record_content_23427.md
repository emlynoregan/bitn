Darts Club.  The old Burra Hotel Darts Club has virtually been in recess.  The shield is currently held by Burra and after a visit to Morgan on 21 February, which will decide who retains the shield; the club will cease to exist.  A new Darts Club will be formed, to be called the Burra Darts Club.
