Bowls.  The traditional Australia Day Tournament at Burra saw a great day of competition.  The winning team was Rink 1 from the Barossa captained by Roger Mattschoss.  Teams came from Burra, Tanunda, Beaumont, Hallett, Barmera, Saddleworth, Eudunda, Riverton, Robertstown, Salisbury, Mintaro, Melrose, Auburn, Booborowie, Kapunda and Nuriootpa.
