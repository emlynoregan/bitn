Mid North Basketball Association AGM elected: Patron, Mrs C.H. Dunn (Tarlee); President, Miss Grace Wilksch (Robertstown), Senior Vice-President, Mrs P. Willmott (Manoora); Junior Vice-President, Mrs G. White (Riverton) and Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs K.C .Phillips (Waterloo).  An Aberdeen team was admitted and will field teams in the A Grade, B1 Grade and C2 Grade.
