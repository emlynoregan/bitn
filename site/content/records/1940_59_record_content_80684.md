Booborowie Institute.  Yearly activities were reported at the AGM by Chairman A.W. Pearce.

The balance for the year shows a loss of £134.  The year’s activities were marked by the completion and opening of the hall extensions with a ball on 22 March and the opening on 23 March, which generated a nett £650.  Mr J.C .Hawker opened the hall.  The hall now shows Cinemascope movies.  Again a barley project was conducted and should realise £200 to £250.
