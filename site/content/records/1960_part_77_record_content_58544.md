Obituary.  Mrs Esther Ann Pearce died suddenly at her residence in Spalding on 30 September aged 85.  She was a daughter of the late Mr & Mrs Joseph Elliott and was born at Wilmington 26 December 1885.  She married Frederick George Pearce in 1916 and came to live at Spalding until her death.  She was a regular attender of the Methodist Guild, Red Cross, CWA, RSL Auxiliary and Senior Citizens and ‘actively associated with the Presbyteri it was disbanded in 1968’ [sic: text corrupt].  She is survived by a daughter Leila Mrs M. Dunn of Kangaroo Island and son Mervyn of Spalding.  Another son, Fred, was killed on active service in WW II.  Her husband died many years ago.
