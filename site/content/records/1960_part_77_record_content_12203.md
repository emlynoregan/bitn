The Governor will visit Burra on 9 November and will also be present at the Civic Ball to be held that night in the Town Hall.

Burra Town Council ended the financial year £39 in credit after closing the 1960-61 year £189 in debit.

M & BHA held its AGM recently in the Burra Town Hall.  Dr Geoffrey de Crespigny was the guest speaker.  Overall attendance this year was 441 for 11 visits or an average of 40 per month.  Elected: President, Mrs Ray Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames B. Bevan & D. Holmes; Secretary, Mrs T. Thamm & Treasurer, Mrs D. Barry.

Burra High School.  The Jubilee History continued by R.V .Mueller.

1940

The staff comprised W. Richards Headmaster, Lloyd Draysey, Marjorie Koehne, Hilda Simmonds and Junior Teachers John Womersley and Patricia Aughey.  During the year Mr Richards was appointed Education Officer in charge of RAAF in SA with commissioned rank and Mr Draysey became acting headmaster.

1941

The school journals are missing for the period 1941-55.

Staff comprised Lloyd Draysey acting headmaster, Marjorie Koehne, Joyce Brooks, Linda Linke and Junior Teachers Max Allen and Bethel Smit.  The enrolment was 63.  Mr A.B. Riggs presented the school with a wall clock.  School students raised £26 for the School’s Patriotic Fund.
