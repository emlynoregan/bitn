BCS

The 120th anniversary of the laying of the foundation stone on 27 February 1887 [sic: for 1877] was celebrated.

Steele House won the BCS Sports Day.

Luke Spackman was ‘athlete of the day’ in the Australian Country Games, winning in the triple jump, 100 m and 200 m.

Tina Williams won gold at the Australian Country Games in the 100m and 200 m.

The northern car park at BCS was sealed in April.
