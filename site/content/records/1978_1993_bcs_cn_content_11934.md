Mt Bryan Gala Weekend

The Treasurer’s report is printed.

A profit of c. $1,500 was made and ten clubs in the district have already received a dividend of $125 from the proceeds, namely: Mt Bryan Soldiers’ Memorial Hall, Mt Bryan Tennis Club, Mt Bryan CFS, Mt Bryan School, Mt Bryan Table Tennis Club, Mt Bryan Playgroup, Mt Bryan Red Cross, Mt Bryan Uniting Church Fellowship, Mt Bryan St Andrew’s Guild and Mt Bryan Darts Club.
