Burra Rural Youth held its AGM 26 July and elected: President, Geoff. Dare; Vice-President, Robert Neill; Secretary, Hazel Hentschke; Social Secretary, Bev. Atkins & Treasurer, John Teague.  The report was of a very successful year with a variety of informative speakers and visits.  There was a Debutante Ball 23 July, which raised Â£85.  Membership was 33 to 36.  Through the year 15 members left and 18 joined.  Average attendance was 17 persons.  A bag and bottle drive was raising money for the Flying Doctor Service.
