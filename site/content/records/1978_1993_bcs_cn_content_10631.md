Burra Burra DC.  Nominations are called for Councillors by 4 April with elections on 4 May as required.  One Councillor is required for each of Eastern, Mt Bryan, Kooringa, Hanson, Farrell Flat, Leighton and Booborowie Wards and four for Burra Town Ward.
