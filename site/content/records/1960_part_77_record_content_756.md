The New School Year

St Joseph’s Convent School has an enrolment of 36; down one on 1959.  [New pupils are listed.]

Burra Primary School starts the year with 228 compared with 256 in 1958 [and 249 in 1959 according to another paragraph].  [New enrolments are listed.]

The staff is the same as last year:

Miss Coventry	Grade I	33

Mrs Pens	Grade II	25

Mrs Perry	Grade III	30

Mrs Jeffery	Grade IV	39

Mrs Sladden 	Grade V	36

Mr Kowald	Grade VI	35

Mr Kies	Grade VII	30

Burra High School has a record 122 this year; up from 116 in 1959.

First Year	49

Second Year	40

Intermediate	25

Leaving	8

Prefects and House captains were elected on Thursday and Friday.

The Head Boy Prefect is Don Topsfield

The Head Girl Prefect is Trenna Eberhard

[Other prefects and House Officers are listed.]

This year two new houses, Jennison and Riggs have been added to Draysey and Steele.

In the 1959 PEB Examinations three students gained Leaving Certificates with 89% subject passes.

Twenty-two students gained Intermediate Certificates with a 77% subject pass rate and there were nine credits.
