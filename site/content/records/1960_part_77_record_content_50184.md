Burra & District Tourist Promotion Committee was formed at a well attended meeting on Wednesday night.  Mr Correll of the Government Tourist Bureau was the main speaker and he stressed that tourists needed access to good food and accommodation and had to feel welcome.  A good caravan and camping site was essential.
