Burra Football Club held its AGM in the Burra Town Hall Supper Room on Friday.  No one was prepared to stand for President, Secretary of Vice-President.  Eventually Allan Baulderstone was re-elected President with J. Secomb as Vice-President and Alan Day as Secretary.  [Page 3 mentions that Ken Salt was the Treasurer.]
