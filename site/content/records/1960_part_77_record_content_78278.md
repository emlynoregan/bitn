Burra Kindergarten held its AGM on 1 March.

Elected were: President, Mrs E. Nicholls; Vice-President, Mrs V. Secomb; Secretary, Mrs M. Schwarz; Assistant Secretary, Mrs B. Villis and Treasurer, Mrs B. Armstrong.

The annual opportunity shop will be held on 8 & 9 April in the RSL Hall.

B.E. Villis reported on 1975:

The project for the year was heating and insulation.  The ceiling was insulated at a cost of $220 and an oil heater was installed for $356.  The windows facing Queen Street got new blinds.  A heatcrete incinerator was purchased.  In the 12 months $984.42 was raised.  Toys and teaching aids worth $408-94 were purchased.
