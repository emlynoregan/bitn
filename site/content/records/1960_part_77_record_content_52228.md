Thomas Eastman, the Rotary Exchange student from Nebraska USA will arrive at Adelaide airport on Saturday at 10.15 a.m.  He will be under the care and responsibility of the Methodist Circuit in Burra during his stay here.  His first host family will be Mr & Mrs Gordon Webster where he will spend the first six weeks.
