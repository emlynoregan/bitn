A Fire in the kitchen at the Mobil Roadhouse on 28 November did about $2,000 damage.  Fryers caught alight, but staff were able to contain the fire until MFS & CFS units arrived.  After a five hour clean-up by staff the kitchen was operational again by 3 p.m. the same day.
