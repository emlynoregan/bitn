Burra Rural Youth Debutante Ball drew a large crowd to the Burra Town Hall on Friday night despite an inch of rain and drenched roads.  Twelve debutantes were presented to Sir Philip and Lady McBride.  Door takings were £170.  There was a pre-ball dinner at the Burra Hotel at which the debutantes presented gifts to the ladies who trained then.  The Rural Youth Ball Committee, Executive and Advisory Members entertained the guests of honour at a pre-ball party at the Town Hall.  [Details occupy c. 1½ columns.]

The debutantes were:

Judith Byrne with John Irlam

Valda Edwards with Arthur Simpson

Kay Heinrich with Peter Sandercock

Beth Hempel with Richard Price

Lynette Kotz with John Kelly

Valda Michell with Ross Wood

Anne Opie with Gordon Neill

Janice Schwartz with Graham Woolford

Lorraine Schwartz with Roger Jones

Jill Stolte with Murray Stolte

Pamela Sutton with Alan Day

Margaret Willmott with Peter Murphy
