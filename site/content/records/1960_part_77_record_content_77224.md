Ken White, local Shell distributor, was given a presentation dinner last Wednesday night to mark his 25 years with Shell.  Among those present was Hugh Clutterbuck, now District Sales Manager who was succeeded at Burra by Barry Taylor.  Barry Taylor left Burra ten years ago and now lives at Broken Hill.  He will soon go to Port Pirie as Area Manager.
