The Mt Bryan Reunion was held in perfect weather on 2 November.  At the school roll call the two oldest living scholars present were Mr Lyle Bell of Glenelg [85] and Mr John R. Simpson [87] of Rose Park.  In the evening a ball was held and CWA members served supper.  On Sunday there was a church service in the school grounds conducted by Fr Matthews and Rev. Hore.  A pooled lunch followed.

Booborowie Annual ‘Rainbow’ Fete was held on 16 November.  Mrs Hore the wife of the Minister in the Burra Circuit opened the fete.  She and her husband would be moving to Maitland in the New Year (not Moonta as earlier announced).  Stalls and takings are listed.  Gross takings were $912.87.
