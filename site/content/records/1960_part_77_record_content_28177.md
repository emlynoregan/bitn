Burra National Trust Branch has recently acquired the building next to the Fire Station in Market Square with the object of preserving items of historical interest.  Officers of the branch are: President, Mr A. Tennant; Secretary, Miss M. Woollacott and Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.
