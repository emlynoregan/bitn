North East Combined School Sports were held on Spalding Oval 19 April.  It was opened by Mr Claude Allen MP.  Participating schools were: Hilltown, Spalding, St Joseph’s Convent, Booborowie, Willalo and Leighton.  The Quirke Cup went to Hilltown.  [Results are printed.]
