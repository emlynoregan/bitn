Burra Kindergarten has welcomed its new Director Mrs Chris Bruce of Spalding.  She has previously served four years at Brinkworth and five and a half at Spalding.  Office bearers for 1990 are: President, Les Smale; Vice-President, Kathy Smale; Secretary, Helen Earle; Treasurer, Josie Turner and Public Officer & School Council Representative, Patrice McInnes.
