Monsignor Hughes Retires

On Tuesday 24 August Monsignor P.V. Hughes said his last Mass at St Joseph’s to bring to an end 22 years as Parish Priest.  At age 80he has been called to live with Bishop Bryan Gallagher at Pt Pirie with the title ‘Pastor Emeritus of Burra’.  A tea followed at the Town Hall with Eric Clode providing the music.  Bishop Gallagher made some remarks and provided details of Monsignor Hughes’ career.  Monsignor Clune of Spalding also spoke of a long association with Monsignor Hughes, dating from his ordination in 1926.  Roy Jeffery spoke on behalf of the District Council.  Jim Quinn as Chairman of the Burra Parish Committee also spoke, expressing the congregation’s gratitude for his service and presented him with a wallet of notes.  Mr Laurence Sullivan of Willalo endorsed the sentiments.  Monsignor Hughes responded.
