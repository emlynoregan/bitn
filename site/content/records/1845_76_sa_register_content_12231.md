A meeting at the Burra Hotel on Monday night was held to form a company to work a mine on a section adjoining the Royal Mining Co. at Karkulto.  A company was formed with 1,000 shares of Â£2 each, payable in instalments of 5/-, the shares were taken up that evening and another meeting will now be held to decide on how the work is to proceed.  The lease is on the toll of one-twentieth for 21 years and the mine is to be called the Pinyonga.
