The 2007 Burra Cycling Classic was run by the Norwood Cycling Club on 18 & 19 August.  Ninety junior and senior cyclists took part.  This year the cyclists competed over a course around the town.  Senior riders raced over five laps and the juniors over three.  70 riders competed in the senior event.  [Photo in Commercial Street.]
