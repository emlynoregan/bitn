Senior Citizens held their first AGM 3 July.  Mrs Laurel Neill reported on activities leading up to this meeting and thanked the steering committee for its work.

Elected: President, Len Hentschke; Vice-President, Fred Atkin; Secretary, Mrs Laurel Neill and Treasurer, Mrs Nell Perry.  The facilities of the new Civic Centre were offered by the Burra Burra DC Chairman Mr R. Jennison.
