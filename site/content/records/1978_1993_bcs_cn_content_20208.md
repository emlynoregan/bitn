Burra Burra DC

Telecom has submitted a report of their findings and recommendations re a proposed Burra Broadcasting Site.  State heritage Branch has also been involved in talks about the suitability of the site.

SA Planning Commission has approved a new CFS shed at Booborowie.

Work at the Burra Caravan Park is nearing completion.  The amenities block has been upgraded and sites are now clearly defined and connected to new sullage and power inlets.

Documentation for the lease of the Goods Shed and Railway Station from Australian National to the DC is in its final stages.

Paul Williams won a trip to New Zealand at the Wool & Sheep Show Spectacular.

Cheryl Day was at the U16 National Titles in Melbourne at the weekend.  She came 6th in the 400 m final, which was run less than an hour after the heat.  She was in the 4 x 400 m relay team that came 4th.
