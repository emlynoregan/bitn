Burra Burra DC, first meeting of combined Councils 14 May

G.R. Finch was elected Chairman and E.T. Baulderstone was elected Deputy Chairman.

Department of Public Health is to be asked to give early attention to drafting plans for a common effluent drainage scheme.

Cr Baulderstone moved the Council make provision for the office of Mayor.  Motion lost.

Nominations for Council were called for 30 May.

Resolved that the Burra Pictures be placed in the hands of Burra Town Ward representatives, J. Topsfield and S. Kellaway with the District Clerk to act as secretary.
