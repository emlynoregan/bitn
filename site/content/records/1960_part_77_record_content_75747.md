Liberal Party.  The Burra Women’s Branch AGM had Mrs Judy Roberts as guest speaker.  Retiring President Mrs Ron Collins took the chair.  Other guests included Hon. R. Geddes MLC, E.C. Allen MP and Hon. Ren de Garis MLC.  The report concentrated on the dire peril of Australia’s slide into socialism under the Federal Labor Government and the activities of the local branch in the past year.  Mrs Roberts’ speech was concerned with the role of SA in ‘becoming the pilot state for Socialism’ under Mr Dunstan.  Elected were: President, Mrs Ron Pearse; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames Ron Collins and Tony Barker; Secretary, Mrs John Gordon and Treasurer, Mrs Charles O’Connor.
