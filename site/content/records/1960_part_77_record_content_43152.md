The Annual RSL Dinner was held on Saturday night.  There was a good representation of visitors who outnumbered local members.

Sister Marita of St Joseph’s Convent has been transferred to Kadina after three years in Burra.  Sister Vianney replaces her.

Dry Cleaning.  Burra will have a new plant in the New Year.

Commonwealth Scholarships have been awarded to:

Lynette Griffen, Angela Quinn and Christine Steele; all of Burra High School.
