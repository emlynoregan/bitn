Burra Bowling Club held its AGM 24 June.  Thirty members attended and elected: Patron, Dudley Jacka; President, Edgar Pietsch; Vice-Presidents, Darrell Field & Brian Riggs; Secretary, Wally Scott and Treasurer, Barry Riggs.
