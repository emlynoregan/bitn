Advt.  Level 3 water restrictions are in force.

Sprinklers are restricted to 5 a.m. to 8 a.m. and 8 pm. to 11 p.m., with even numbered properties permitted on Saturday s and odd numbered properties on Sundays.  Sprinklers are not permitted during the week.

Households may use hand held hoses fitted with a trigger nozzle any day 8 pm. to 8 a.m. and buckets and watering cans are allowed at any time.
