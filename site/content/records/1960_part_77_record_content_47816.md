Burra Golf Associates.  The AGM was held at the clubhouse 17 September.

[Mrs E. Nicholls] as President reported on a very successful season.  [She uses only first names in referring to the trophy winners, but Open Day winners are listed at 91, 24 (2), 24 June 1969, page 1.]

Elected were: Patroness, Mrs A.L. Collins; President, Mrs R. Wuttke; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames D. Field & M. Nicholls; Secretary, Mrs R. Hillman; Assistant Secretary, Mrs F. Reed; Treasurer, Mrs R. Gordon; Captain, Mrs J. Oates and Vice-Captain, Mrs J. Wiseman.
