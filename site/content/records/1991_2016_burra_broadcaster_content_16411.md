Four Burra Artists have banded together to present their work to the public in a number of venues.  They have begun by presenting works from the four at the Commercial Hotel on 5 July.  The artists are: Helen Jensen, Wendy Douglas, Glenys Christopher and Grace Delaney.  The group goes by the name ‘Four on the Turps’.
