The 71st Marrabel Rodeo will be held on 5 October.  The Marrabel Rodeo Club has staged the event since 1935.  Many of the local community groups and sporting clubs raise funds through catering, cleaning up and running various aspects of the event.  About 180 top competitors are expected.  Prize money exceeds $20,000.
