Mongolata Goldfield

Baldina Mongolata GM Syndicate for fortnight to 23 July.

Lean’s Shaft.  Driving on 90ft level: reef erratic showing 6dwt by dish.  Carted 5½ tons to the battery for a return of 33oz 15dwt of gold plus 17dwt 4gr by cyanide.
