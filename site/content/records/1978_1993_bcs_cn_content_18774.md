The Balloon Academy Pty Ltd anticipate providing hot air balloon ‘champagne flights’ soon from Burra.  Anyone with objections to the flight paths crossing their property should lodge them with the Secretary-Treasurer of the Burra Tourist Association c/o the District Council of Burra Burra before 30 June 1989.

BCS has been entered in the 1989 SAFM-State Bank Rock ’N’ Roll Eisteddfod and rehearsals are proceeding.

The Street Fair on Sunday was enjoyed by many visitors and locals.
