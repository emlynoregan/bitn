Cheryl Boswell nee Finch, a former Burra resident, has been chosen as the December winner of the News-Caltex Sportswoman of the Year Award.  She was the only SA female athlete to be chosen for the national team at the Pacific Conference Games in New Zealand, where she will compete in the 100 m hurdles.  Cheryl will join eleven other monthly winners on 16 February in the Festival Theatre for the judging of Sportswoman of the Year.
