Burra Women’s Golfers Pennant Team has been victorious; defeating Blyth yesterday.  Members were: Judy Oates, Jacky Dignan, Estelle Nicholls, Martha Jeffery and Lyn Kempster.  The men’s team also won the pennant competition this year.
