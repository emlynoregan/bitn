Paxton Square Cottages.  The Lewis Board has started renovations on some cottages.  Stonework has begun on a couple of cottages and some painting has been undertaken.  Some bathroom cladding has been renewed and new dining suites have been placed in two cottages.  Upgrades will continue as finances permit.
