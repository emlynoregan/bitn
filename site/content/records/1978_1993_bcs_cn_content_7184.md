The Great Barrow Push from Burra to Broken Hill will start on 26 January 1983 when Herb Elliot will start the race.  Teams from all over Australia will compete for the $10,000 1st prize.  Entries close 17 December.  To date no local team has entered.  There will be preliminary celebrations along Burra creek on the night of 25 January.  [As noted from the issue of 9 December.]
