Goyder Council

Burra Ward Funds were given to:

Burra History Group $2000 for ten heritage plaques.

Burra Bowling Club $3,903 for a results board and verandah improvements.

Burra Community Christmas Fair Committee $2,000

Burra CMC $2,000 towards a digital projector for the Burra Town Hall.

BRTBA $3,500 for marketing and promoting the Burra Antique Fair.

Friends of Burra Railway Station $2,000 for garden development.

Burra Early Learning Centre $XXXX for a fund day fundraiser.

Burra Inter-Church Council $800 towards Christmas Carols.

Burra Community & Sports Club $6,500 to resurface the oval with roll-out turf.
