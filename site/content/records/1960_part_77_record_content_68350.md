Notice is given of a meeting of Farrell Flat members of the Church of England in St Mary’s Hall, Burra, to consider the transfer of funds presently held in the names of the Farrell Flat Church of England Building Fund and the Farrell Flat Ladies’ Guild to the Synod of the Church of England Diocese of Willochra, to be held in trust on behalf of the Farrell Flat area in the Parish of Burra.
