Obituary.  Ben Dunstan of Mt Bryan East died at Burra Hospital 3 June aged 87.  He had been President of the Mt Bryan Agricultural Bureau for many years and was a life member.  He was also President of the Mt Bryan East Fire Fighting Association; a member of the Oddfellows and Masonic Lodges and of the Liberal and Country League, and was very interested in politics.  He was the first scholar (of 7) enrolled at the Mt Bryan East School when it opened in 1885 under the tuition of Mr Whittington.  His knowledge of the early days of the districtâ€™s settlement was second to none.  In his youth he was a keen cricketer.  He had charge of the Mt Bryan East Post Office till it closed a few years ago.  His wife died in 1957.  The only children were Mr Jack Dunstan of Mylor and Mr Hugh Dunstan of Whyte Yarcowie.  There is one granddaughter.  [Benjamin Hugh Knuckey Dunstan born 28 August 1875 Redruth: died 3 June 1963 Burra, residence Mt Bryan East.]
