Burra Races were successfully held on Wednesday in the paddock of E.N. Finch at Copperhouse.  The main race was the Burra Handicap for £20, £3-10-0 & £1-10-0, run over 1¼ miles.  It was won by T.D. McGahan’s b.g. Mackay, aged, carrying 9 st 12 lb and ridden by the owner.
