Burra Regional Art Gallery will open three exhibitions on 2 March.

Jumping Fences is a landscape exhibition featuring artists of Eyre Peninsula and the Mid North and ranging over indigenous and non-indigenous, academic and self trained artists.

Alison Mitchell’s recent works

International Baltic Mini-Textile Exhibition – small scale textile works from the collection of the Museum of the City of Gdynia in Poland.
