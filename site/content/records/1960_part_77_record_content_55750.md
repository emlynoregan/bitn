Bowls.  The Burra Easter Tournament was a great success in perfect weather.

Friday: Men’s Pairs won by Bill Corner & Johnnie Hill.

Saturday: Men’s Rinks won by Gordon Miller’s Cadell Rink comprising R. Haese, K. Dunk & D. Wright.

Monday: Mixed Rinks won by Zoe Wood, Reg Corner & Estelle Mitchell.
