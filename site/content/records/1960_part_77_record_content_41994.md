Football.  North Eastern Grand Finals

Colts	Clare South 9.4 defeated Clare North 4.5

Junior Colts	Spalding 2.4 defeated Clare North 2.4

Presentations were made to Lindsay Victor as Best & Fairest Colt and to Don Coulter as Best & Fairest of the ‘B’ Grade.  L. Gordon of Clare South received the Ashrose shield on behalf of the Colts Team.  The Burra Record Trophy to the runner-up in the Mail Medal was presented to Barry Eberhard and the Mail Medal and the Mobil Oil Trophy was presented to the Best & Fairest ‘A’ Grade Don Villis.
