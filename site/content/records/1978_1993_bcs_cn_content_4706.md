St John’s Ambulance Service, Burra & District Branch held its AGM.

During the year the service made 70 trips to Adelaide and 55 local calls – 6 accident and 48 for sickness [sic].

During the year the Ford Transit Ambulance was purchased and to this approximately $17,000 worth of equipment was fitted.

Elected for 1981-82: Chairman, R.L. Thamm; Deputy Chairman, P. Davies and Secretary-Treasurer, E.M. Beinke.
