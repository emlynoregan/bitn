East Bungaree Sheep Stud has been placed on the market by its owners, the Litchfield Family.  It runs 3,000 ewes, 1,000 ewe-hoggets and sells 600 to 700 rams per year.  It comprises one parcel of 2,500 acres [1,012 ha] north of Booborowie.
