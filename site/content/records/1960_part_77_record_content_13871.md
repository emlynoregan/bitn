Mr & Mrs Norm Fairchild are leaving Booborowie at the end of the month to live in Whyalla.  Mrs Fairchild was given a farewell by the school welfare club, of which she had been an active member for seven years and President for three.  She also received a presentation from the congregation of St Edmund’s Anglican Church.
