The Burra Cycling Classic in 1998 will be run on 12 & 13 September.  The main race will be a 125 km handicap event from Burra to Riverton and return.  On Sunday there will be scratch events from Leighton Cricket Club to Mt Bryan and return and events for juniors on both days.
