Christmas Festivities in Burra

Sunday 29 November

Children’s Cake Decorating Competition, with cakes to be delivered to The Lucky Dip.

Cakes will be auctioned to benefit the Burra Hospital.

Family Fun Night – barbecue and drinks in Market Square – proceeds to Burra Swimming Pool.

Carols by Candlelight at 7.30 p.m. in Market Square.

Christmas Lights Competition, with voting slips from Décor Made Easy.

Free Christmas Raffle ticket from 1 December with the purchase of goods to value of $20 or more from local shops.
