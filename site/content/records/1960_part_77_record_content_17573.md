Burra Football Club.  The Colts held a Victory Dinner in the RSL Hall.  Trophies:

Fairest & Best	Kingsley Southall

Most Consistent	Graham Benger

Most Effective 	Andrew Phillips

Best Backman	Leon Hawke

Most Improved	Ashley Nicholls [sic]

Most Improved 	John Bogisch [sic]

In the Parents v. Colts match at the Burra oval on Saturday the Colts were the winners.
