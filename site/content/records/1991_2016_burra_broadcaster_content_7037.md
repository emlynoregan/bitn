MFS Annual Inspection Day is reported.

Michael Scholz and Chris Kelly were promoted to senior firefighters.

Michael Scholz and Les Gardner were awarded a National Service Medal for 15 years’ service to the MFS.

A competition ‘fast hose drill’ was run against the clock and another demonstration was staged at the railway station – involving also CFS and ambulance personnel.  [2 photos.]
