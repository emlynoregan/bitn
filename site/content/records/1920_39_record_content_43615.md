Scouts.  H.M.G. Gratton Chief Commissioner of Scouts for SA and F.J. Mills General Secretary, known as ‘Twinkler’ visited Burra on 11 October.  The Institute was engaged for the rally.  After being given a guard of honour the visitors were greeted by the Mayor.  Cr Jennison supported the Mayor and the visitors then addressed the gathering.  [The report runs for 2¾ columns.]
