St Mary’s farewelled Bishop David & Marion McCall on Sunday 25 June.  They are moving to Bunbury in WA.  On the same weekend Archdeacon Brian Smith christened Samuel Burge, the grandson of John & Kath Burge of Farrell Flat and Bishop David confirmed Elizabeth Hounsell, Bianca Seaford and Chloe Seaford.  Luncheon followed in the Church Hall.
