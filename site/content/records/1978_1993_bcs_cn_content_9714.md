Burra Pony Club held a riding instruction school by leading EFA Instructor Ms Veronica Vincent.  The teacher and students ignored freezing cold and foggy conditions on Saturday 14 July and on Sunday morning they continued despite a clear, freezing morning and then rain in the early afternoon, which turned to snow by 3 p.m.
