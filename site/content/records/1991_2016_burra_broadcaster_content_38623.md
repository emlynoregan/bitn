Australia Day Awards

Burra Ward Australia Day Citizen of the Year for 2006 is John Burge, a Farrell Flat Farmer.  He has a long involvement with the Anglican Church, the local Council, Rural Watch, CFS/EFS, hospital management and school councils.  Though nearing his 80th birthday, he still works full-time on his farm.

Burra Ward Young Citizen of the Year id Jake Norris.  He is an aspiring archaeologist and has wide community involvement.  He coached BBH Mini Colts last year with Jesse O’Brien.

Burra Rainfall figures are printed for 1936-2005.
