SA Secondary Schools Sports Association Championships were held in Adelaide last Friday and the Athletics Association of SA Championships were held on Saturday and Sunday.

Burra sent 55 students on Friday and won the Girls’ Trophy and was runner-up in the Boys’ Trophy.

On Saturday and Sunday they had a team of 9 girls and 8 boys.  The girls came 4th and the boys 7th in competition with over 100 schools.

Friday’s outstanding performers were Rosie Lloyd, Petrea Piggott, Justin Hall, Ian Kleinig, Richard Mudge, Michael Kessell, Graham Kessell and Craig McDowell.
