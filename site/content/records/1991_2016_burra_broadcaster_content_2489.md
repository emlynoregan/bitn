March

Booborowie celebrated a sealed road from the township to the Burra-Spalding Road.

SA Merino Field Days were well attended.

Burra Burra DC obtained a $50,000 grant to assist in flood damage repairs.

With increased water storage, water sports were held at Porters Lagoon.

AdChem appointed Tony Tindall [earlier reports have Tindale] as new manager.

Burra Ambulance launches an appeal for funds.

Dynix computerised system opened at Burra Library.

Jock Duncan elected President of the Royal Agricultural and Horticultural Society; the third generation of the family to hold this position.

Victorian ‘Bash’ cars visit Burra for a lunchtime stop at the Burra Oval.
