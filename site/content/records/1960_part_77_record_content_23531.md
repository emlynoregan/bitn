Mr & Mrs A.J. (Tony) Ryan were tendered a farewell social in the RSL Hall on Saturday night.  Mr Ryan has taken a position with the Waite Research Centre as a Shearing Instructor for SA.  Planned dancing was cancelled due to the heat.  The Mayor and Mayoress attended and the Mayor presented a wallet of notes to Mr Ryan.
