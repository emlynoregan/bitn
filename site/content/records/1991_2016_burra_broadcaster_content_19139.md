Burra Visitor Centre was busy over the Adelaide Cup Weekend.  214 visitors came through the door and 160 passports were purchased.  393 of the 400 beds in the town were occupied.  The Clare Gourmet Weekend and the opening of the new Burra Golf Course caused the influx.
