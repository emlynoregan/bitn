Burra Regional Art Gallery has an exhibition of Polish born artist Grazyna Tranda, entitled My Sacrum.

This comprises tapestries, woven miniatures, drawings and spatial compositions.  It is part of Country Arts SA touring exhibition.

[Sacrum.  Origin: short for Late Latin os sacrum, translating Greek hieron osteon, sacred bone (from the belief that the soul resides in it).]
