Burra Bowls Tournament

With fees and donations this should bring in about $200, which with Government subsidy means about $600 for the Burra Hospital.  In the Cosmopolitan Rinks Tournament Section 1 the Burra Rink of Murray & Stella Stockman with Darrell & Pat Field won the final.
