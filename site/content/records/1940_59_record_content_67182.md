Cricket

Koonoona defeated Farrell Flat and will now challenge Burra for the Premiership.

Koonoona 7 for 156 defeated Farrell Flat 64 & 35.

In the other match Burra 133 & 1 for 62 defeated Buffs 60 & 7 for 132 declared.
