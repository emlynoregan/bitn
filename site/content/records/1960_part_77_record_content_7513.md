Burra Court of Summary Jurisdiction

Three youths were prosecuted for the use of a spotlight from a motor vehicle on a road for the purposes of shooting.

Maxwell John Burton of Broadview

Desmond John Pascoe of Hampstead

Allan Murray Fiedler of Broadview

Fines of £5 were imposed.

Barry Stewart Brown of Payneham was fined £5 for driving a vehicle with an ineffective silencer.
