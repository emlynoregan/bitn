The Tourist Promotion Committee met on 24 September and elected Chairman, Ian Allen and Secretary, Mrs Irene Hirschausen.  It was decided to hold a Copper festival in October 1977.  This would coincide with the centenaries of the Burra Show Society and the Burra Model School.
