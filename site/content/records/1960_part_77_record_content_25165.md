Mr Ken Williams used the fire brigade on Saturday to rescue a magpie caught by a leg in a gum tree off Ayers Street.  The bird was in a bad way, having been pecked by others and ‘Dr’ Bob Broad amputated the damaged leg.  The bird seemed to be responding well, but died today.
