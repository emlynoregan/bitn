Burra Meals on Wheels held its AGM on 8 July and elected Chairperson, Mr G. Camp; Vice-Chairperson, Mrs M. Morris; Secretary, Mrs Marcia Baulderstone and Treasurer, Mr H. Heitbrink.

In another busy year some 3,700 meals were delivered and more volunteer drivers are required urgently.
