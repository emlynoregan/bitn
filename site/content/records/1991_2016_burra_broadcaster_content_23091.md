Don Lloyd has taken the honour of hitting the first hole-in-one at the new Burra Golf Course.  He achieved this on Wednesday 9 August on the 15th hole.  Rob Reiman of Gawler Caddieshack, who has been sponsoring the Burra Golf Club for the past four years, presented Don with a cheque for $50.
