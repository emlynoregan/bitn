Advt.  Elders-GM will sell ‘Caroona’ on Friday 10 August at 2.30 p.m. in the RSL Hall, Burra.

The property is 18-20 miles northeast of Mt Bryan and Burra, of 47,084¼ acres, perpetual lease and is offered in four lots.

Lot I	‘Homestead’ of 14,677 acres

Lot II	‘Woolshed’ of 18,419 acres

Lot III	‘Killicoat’s’ of 9,560 acres

Lot IV	‘Jones’ of 4,428¼ acres
