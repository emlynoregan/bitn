Burra Anzac Memorial Service was conducted at the Market Square Memorial on Sunday.  The crowd was larger than ever.  The march from the RSL Club Rooms was led by the Hindmarsh Salvation Army Band under Bandmaster Heaven.  Burra VDC and Booborowie VDC members followed the returned soldiers in the march.  Rev. Barrett announced the order of the service.  Captain Cox of the Salvation Army led in prayer, Rev. H. Hobbs gave the address and N.C. Woodards, representing the Church of England read the lesson.
