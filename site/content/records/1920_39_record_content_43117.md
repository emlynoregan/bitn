Combined High School Sports were held 31 August at Victoria Park.

Kapunda, Riverton, Clare, Balaklava & Burra contested the day.  Kapunda scored a runaway victory with 117½ points to Clare’s 52, then Riverton 46, Burra 32 and Balaklava 11½.  Results are printed.
