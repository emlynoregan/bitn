Kindergarten Union of SA’s Mobile Unit will tour the Mid North from 27 October to 7 November for the first time to promote interest and provide information on its services.  It will visit Auburn, Mintaro, Farrell Flat, Blyth, Clare, Spalding and Booborowie.
