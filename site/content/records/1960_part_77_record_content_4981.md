The RSL Annual Dinner was enjoyed by about 50 people.  The Ladies’ Auxiliary presented their usual fine spread.  President Mr G.J. Kelly presided.  W.H. Nankivell was presented with a Life Membership Certificate in recognition of his work for the Association over many years.
