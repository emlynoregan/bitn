Barkers of Baldina Aged Homes AGM was held on 2 November.  Chairman Jock Duncan described the year as a steady one.  The last three bathrooms in Pearse Row were upgraded.  Basic maintenance was carried out where required.  Three residents died during the year and also, in June 1998 Mr Ron Pearse, a founding member and past Chairman of the Board died.  Mr Jock Duncan was re-elected Chairman, with Ron Thamm as Vice-Chairman.  John Arthur is the Secretary.
