Obituary.  Reta Jennison, one of the best known members of the Burra Community and who contributed so much to the welfare of this town, died in Adelaide last Saturday aged 92.

[Born Reta Marion Thomas 17 July 1900 at Malvern: died 28 November 1992 at Resthaven, Mitcham.  Married Horton Joseph Barraclough Jennison in 1923.]
