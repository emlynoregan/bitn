Burra Town Council

The financial statement to 30 June 1968 showed the bank overdraft had been reduced from $4,312 to $1,342.  Council will continue to provide trees to persons prepared to care for them.  The opening of a new section for burials at Burra Cemetery will be discussed at the next meeting.  A Federal Rural Areas Grant of $600 was allotted for the construction of Hardy Street.  (Along the edge of the Burra North sale yards.)
