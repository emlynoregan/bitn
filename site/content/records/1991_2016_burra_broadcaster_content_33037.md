Police from Eudunda and Burra addressed the members of Goyder Council on 20 January re the apparent increase in vandalism in the region.  They pointed out that more interaction with the community is needed.  Council has authorised $5,000 to be spent on efforts to reduce vandalism with Police and Council keeping a close watch on progress.
