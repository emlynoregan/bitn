Notice.  Porter’s Lagoon Progress Association

Power boat owners are advised that on SUNDAY 23 February the Gilbert Valley Lions Club have been given control over Porter’s Lagoon Reserve.  All owners of power boats are requested not to use the Lagoon before 6 p.m. on that day.  J. Bowden, Hon. Sec.
