Water Quality Training

The SA Murray-Darling Basin Natural Resources Management Board (SA MDB NRM) is encouraging local people to learn about local water resources at a water quality workshop in Burra next month.  The workshop will discuss the interaction between groundwater and surface water and explain the monitoring that is being done.  And will be at the Burra Bowling Club 10 a.m. to 12.30 p.m. 10 June.
