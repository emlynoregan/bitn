The Burra Cycling Classic was held last weekend.  It featured a 125 km Burra-Riverton-Burra event on Saturday.  Damien Lynch won the handicap section in 3 hours 24 minutes 36 seconds and Patrick Marcucci had the fastest time of 2 hours 59 minutes 18 seconds.  A street circuit event was raced on Sunday.
