Economic Wool Producers held successful meetings recently in Hawker, Jamestown and Burra.  More than 200 woolgrowers attended to discuss ways of reducing wool selling costs and maximising competition.  Mr Jim Maple-Brown, Chairman of Directors, addressed the meetings.  A main aim is to reduce labour costs.  Objective measurement was also discussed.
