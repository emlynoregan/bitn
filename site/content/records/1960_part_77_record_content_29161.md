Burra Table Tennis held its AGM and elected: Patron, W.R. Teague; President, Mr A. Day; Secretary, Miss D. Jones.

Results of the tournament held last week:

Men’s Championship Singles	B. Christie

Men’s Handicap Singles	J. Tiver

Men’s Championship Doubles 	[A.] Fillmore & Tiver

Men’s Handicap Doubles	[A.] Fillmore & Tiver

Women’s Championship Singles	D. Jones

Women’s Handicap Singles	L. Nicholls

Women’s Championship Doubles	Jones & Nicholls

Women’s Handicap Doubles	Woollacott & Winders
