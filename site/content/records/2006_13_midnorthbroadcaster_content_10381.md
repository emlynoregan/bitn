Ken Bruce of Booborowie travelled to North Tuppal Station between Deniliquin and Tocumwal for a blade shearing event in June.  He took part in the event where 72 rams were blade shorn in a recreation of the Tom Roberts’ painting ‘The Shearing of the Rams’.
