SALA.  Each year SALA Week honours one regional area and in 2001 Burra will be the focus.

Burra Arts Society is hosting the Professional Development Forum on 12 August.

Burra Regional Art Gallery will host a food and music event on 12 August.

Burra Regional Art Gallery will have an installation art work overseen and designed by Mid North artists Marcus Possingham & Roland Weight.

Diprose Studio & Gallery is featuring watercolours, pastels and paintings by Glenys Christopher.
