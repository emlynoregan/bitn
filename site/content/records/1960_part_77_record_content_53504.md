Tom Eastman the Rotary Exchange student has moved to the home of Mr & Mrs John Drew of Booborowie.  Until now he has been staying with Mr & Mrs Gordon Webster of Burra.  In the September school holidays he had the opportunity to visit the Snowy Mountains and early this month went to Adelaide to a botany Convention.  Over the Christmas Holidays Tom will be with Mr & Mrs W. Klem of Farrell Flat.
