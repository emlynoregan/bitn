Mt Bryan School

The vast majority of Mt Bryan parents have voted to as the Minister of Education to close their school, principal Karyn-Lee Paech said this week.  Students will go to Burra Community School, though one student from Hallett may go instead to Jamestown.  In preparation students will visit BCS once a week on Wednesday s beginning next week.  BCS Principal said the extra 15 students would enable current staffing levels to be maintained.
