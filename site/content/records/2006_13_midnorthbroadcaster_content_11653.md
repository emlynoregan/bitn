Jess Humphrys has been appointed the new Goyder Tourism Officer.  She will work with the Goyder Tourism Working Party to offer support in a part-time capacity from 10 January.  She will work to promote and publicise the programs and activities of GTWP which has developed the Goyder Tourism Strategic Plan.  She is currently working on History Month (May).  [Photo.]
