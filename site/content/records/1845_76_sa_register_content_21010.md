Henry Scott for Bungaree, Canowie, Mount Brown, Corunna, Cariewerloo, Yadlamulka and Mubko Runs

Isaac Killicoat for Alberton Park [sic: for Abberton Park]

Joseph Gilbert for his runs at Mr Bryan.
