Burra Rifle Club.  Saturday saw the last match for the L.A. Lewis Trophy.  Best scores for the day were A.L. Kellock 67 and G.L. Nutt 59, but the best aggregate scores were A.L. Kellock 121 from J.A. Riggs 110.
