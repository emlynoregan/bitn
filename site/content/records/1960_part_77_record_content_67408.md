Burra Primary School Council has convened an Education Symposium at the Burra Town Hall on 10 August.  The Director General of Education Mr A.W. Jones will address the meeting, which id directed at parents.  Education is a continuing process.  Methods and subjects are changing and parents need to know what is going on to stimulate and encourage their children and to play a greater role in the whole process.

[An extended summary of Mr Jones’ career follows.]

Other speakers:

Mrs T. Nolan, immediate past President of the Welfare Clubs of SA.

Mr Ted O’Keefe of Special Education will speak on Developmental Reading.

Peter Jackson, Headmaster of Eudunda Area School will speak on Individual Progression in SA Schools

[Notes on Mr Jackson appear on page 4.]

Mr S.L. McMillan of the Department of Further Education will speak on the role of his department.

[Notes on Mr McMillan appear on page 4.]

Mr Kevin Heath, Director of the Centre of Personal Encounter [COPE], a Division of the Marriage Guidance Council, will speak on Health Education for Schools.
