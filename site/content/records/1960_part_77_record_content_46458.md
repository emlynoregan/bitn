Accident.  A car in which three young Broken Hill men were travelling overturned near Hallett on Saturday at about 6.30 a.m.  Driver Jeffery Colley (20) and his brother Graham (17) were treated at Burra Hospital for head injuries and Barry Squire (21) was sent to the RAH with spinal injuries.
