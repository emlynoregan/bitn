Burra Footballers Cricket Club held a dinner at the Commercial Hotel on 9 June.  Thanks were extended to Mr Bert Beinke (Patron) who stood the cost (c. £80) of materials until the members could raise funds to reimburse him.  The club eventually ended the season in credit £17.  Trophies were presented:

Best Batting Average	Barry Eberhard

Best Bowling Average	Roley Barnes

Best Fielding	John Merkel

Most Catches	Keith Treleaven

Best All-Rounder	John Merkel
