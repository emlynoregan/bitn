SAR

Since the opening of the [standard gauge] Indian Pacific rail link from Sydney to Perth through Peterborough traffic on the Burra line has increased.  There are now 52 passenger and goods services per week compared with 41 previously.  An express goods using bogey exchange facility at Peterborough goes through each night.
