Accident.  A car failed to negotiate a right hand bend near Brewery Hill and careered down a steep grade at about 9 p.m. on Saturday.  Fortunately it did not roll over and the four occupants were saved from serious injury.  The driver, Mrs Matson of Tea Tree Gully, sustained minor injuries and of the three children Mark received head injuries and the two younger were unhurt.
