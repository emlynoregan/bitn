Burra Bowls.  The season opened on Sunday.  President Darrell Field called on Jack Stephenson, President of the Mid North Association, to declare the greens open for play.  Edgar Pietsch, Club Champion, sent down the first bowl after Pat Field, Associates Champion, had cast the jack.
