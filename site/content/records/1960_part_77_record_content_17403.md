The Burra North Dance organised by the Women’s Auxiliaries of the Aberdeen Football Club and the St John’s Ambulance Brigade was successfully held at the Oddfellows’ Hall on Friday night.  Door takings were £38.  In the Pride of Erin contest the winners were Miss Marie Nourse and Mr A. Quirke of Clare, while the Twist Contest winners were Mrs & Mrs Gordon Wooton.
