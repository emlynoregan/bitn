Burra Primary School Welfare Club held its AGM 7 March.  In the past year there had been 58 financial members and an average attendance of 31.  Sports Day was successful and $217 was raised.  The Club provided a luncheon in the Burra Town Hall in August, which raised $232.  Throughout the year the Club spent $1,251 on sports equipment, library books, teaching aids and first aid equipment.  The income was $1,295.  Elected: President, Mrs B. Stockman; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames I. Allen & Haynes; Secretary, Mrs B.P. Thamm; Assistant Secretary, Mrs M. Tiver and Treasurer, Mrs Ray Alderson.
