Notice.  SA Mining Association. The twenty-seventh dividend of 100% has been declared by the Directors and will be paid to the shareholders on 4 June.

Advertisement.  SA Mining Association

Survey Day.  The usual public survey will be held at the Burra Burra Mines on Thursday 29 May when a number of Tutwork Bargains and Tribute Pitches will be offered.  The ‘Take’ to expire on 31 July next.

Henry Ayers, Secretary, May 16, 1856.
