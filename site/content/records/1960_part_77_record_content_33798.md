Obituary.  Mary E.C. O’Connell (nee Howell) died 15 March in hospital, late of Kensington Gardens and Burra. She was the mother of Kath Mrs Sowter (Kensington Gardens), Jo (Kensington Park), Bill (Pt Lincoln) and Mary Mrs Kotz (Prospect).  There are nine grandchildren.

[Born Cora Howell 18 October 1883 Mt Bryan: died Mary Elizabeth Cora O’Connell 16 March 1967 Prospect, residence Kensington.]
