WISE (Women’s Information, Support & Enrichment) is a group that has been meeting fortnightly since October 1996.  It provides information for quality of life and health in an informal format and has stringent guidelines of confidentiality and respect for each other’s views.  The group meets on alternate Mondays at the St John’s Ambulance Centre.
