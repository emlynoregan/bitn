Obituary.  Mr Percival Gordon Mosey died at the Eudunda Hospital on 18 November aged 80.  He was a WWI soldier and one of 13 children of whom only Mr Todd Mosey now survives.  He lived on a farm at Bradey Creek before retiring into Robertstown.  He is survived by a wife and five children: Eric, Heather, Don, Morris and Peter.  There are 13 grandchildren.

[Although the SA Births CD has him registered as Percevil Gorden Mosey born 16 February 1896 at Burra, he was married as Percival Gordon Mosey.]
