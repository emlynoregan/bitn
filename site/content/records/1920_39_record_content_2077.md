The Governor, Sir A. Weigall & Lady Weigall and Miss Priscilla Weigall visited Hallett informally on 9 September on their return from Peterborough.  They visited the school and Institute and on Friday evening the Governor showed some films in the Institute, that the family had taken to be sent to England.
