Burra Youth Club will continue to meet in the Salvation Army Citadel each Friday from 7-9 p.m. despite the recent announcement by the Salvation Army that it was to resume activities in Burra.  The Youth Club has no connection with the Salvation Army and merely rents the hall from that body.
