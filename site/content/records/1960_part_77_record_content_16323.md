National Trust of SA

At a meeting held on 17 May called by the Mayor it was decided to form a branch in Burra.  Mr Morphett, Chairman of the National Trust in SA outlined the aims: to preserve land and buildings of outstanding beauty or of architectural, historic or scientific interest and to protect the flora and fauna of SA.  The branch will be known as the Burra Burra & District Branch.  Elected were: Chairman, A. Tennant; Secretary, Mrs R.B. Pitcher and Treasurer, Miss E. Tiver.
