Advt. Elder, Smith & Co. Ltd have instructions from Mrs J.R. Phillips (who is leaving the district) to conduct a sale of 602 acres of freehold land at Hanson in the RSL Hall At Burra on 25 October.  The land comprises Section 69 in the Hundred of Hanson and lies 8 miles southwest of Burra and 3 miles west of Hanson.
