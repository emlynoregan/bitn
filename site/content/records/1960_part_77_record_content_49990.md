Burra Primary School Parents’ Meeting

The Welfare Club reported on an active year of support for the school with the Treasurer reporting the spending of more than $1,800 on a wide range of purchases.  The Treasurer of the School Committee reported the sale of the school crop for more than $70.  As Chairman Mr R. Moreland outlined the year’s work.  Headmistress Miss Spratt spoke on aspects of child development and the school.  The School Committee for 1970 was elected.  Notable achievements during the year had been:

The easement for use of the Burra North children is underway with tenders called for fencing and paving.

E & WS Department has just finished laying the new main to serve the school hydrants.
