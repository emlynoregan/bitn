St Mary’s Ladies’ Guild held its AGM recently.  Margaret Hurst reported on the year’s activities.  Average attendance had been 12.  [Details are given in a little more than one column.]

Kooringa Methodist Ladies’ Guild held its AGM 3 March.  Mrs E.T. Baulderstone was re-elected President.  Mrs F. Fennell was elected Secretary, with Mrs W. Nankivell as Treasurer.  Mrs M. Williams as retiring Secretary reported on the year.  [Details occupy about ½ column.]

Booborowie Methodist Ladies’ Guild held its AGM, which is reported in ¾ column.
