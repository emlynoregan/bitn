Burra Lions Club will present its ‘Breaker Morant’ Cabaret at Redruth Gaol on 22 November.  There will be two large marquees, one 100 ft x 20 ft and the other 40 ft x 20 ft erected in the northern compound.  A large dance floor will be laid.
