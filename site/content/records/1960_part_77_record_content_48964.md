Accident/Obituary.  A 19-year-old driver died in Burra Hospital yesterday morning [22 December] after the car he was driving overturned on Sunday afternoon on the Burra-Hanson Road near the railway crossing at Brown’s Corner.  He was Brian James Howe, a press operator of Elizabeth Field.  He was thrown from the vehicle.  A passenger, Robert Mitchell of Wingfield, escaped with bruises.
