Weather.  Thursday had showers and on Sunday there were variable falls from a few points to 160 at Morgan.  Princess Royal recorded 156 points, ‘The Gums’ 100, World’s End 80, Mt Bryan 10, Burra 44, Balah 21 and Mt Bryan East 3 points.  More rain fell at ‘The Gums’ today with 50 points to 11 a.m. and rain still falling.  A further 30 points have been reported from Princess Royal.
