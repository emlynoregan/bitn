Obituary.  Frederick H. Collins died 12 December 1967 in NSW.  For the past 20 years Mr Collins lived at the home of his daughter Mrs Glasson at ‘Mt Pleasant’ Dungowan, via Tamworth.  He was the second son of John & Catherine Collins of ‘Collinsville’, Mt Bryan and is survived by his brothers: Newton, Arthur and Lindsay.  He had an abiding love of cricket and was one of the founders of the Country Carnival and Captain of the Stockbreeders side until he left the state.  [Frederick Henry Reginald Collins born 20 April 1885 Aberdeen, Burra.]
