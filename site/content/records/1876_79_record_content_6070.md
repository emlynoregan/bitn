Cricket. Kapunda v. Burra at Aberdeen. The Kapunda team arrived Friday evening and stayed at the Commercial Hotel. Play began at 9.30 a.m. on Saturday. Burra 53 & 26 for 5 defeated Kapunda 56 & 22.
