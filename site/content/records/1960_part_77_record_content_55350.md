Mid North Football League, met on 5 March.  It elected: Patron, W.E. Blatchford of Riverton; President, S. Noske of Robertstown; Senior Vice-President, C.S. Rowett of SA United and Assistant Secretary, Mrs A.E. Bannear.

[The report on 1970 and arrangements for the 1971 season are printed.]

Life Membership is to be given to Mr R.G. Crouch prior to the Grand Final.
