Burra District Advisory Committee

Achievements of the past year are reported by Chairman Brian Polkinghorne:

Fortnightly visits by the Department of Social Security were arranged.

The Burra Town Hall Committee was inaugurated.

The tree planting committee has been active with c. 170 planted in streets and with the help of BCS students 300 were planted along the creek and next autumn 344 will be planted along the Hanson Road.
