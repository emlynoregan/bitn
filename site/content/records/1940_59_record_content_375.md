W.J. Smith, licensee of the Kooringa Hotel charged with unlawful supply of liquor on 22 November 1939 when Eric Smith, his son, supplied John Webber Seeley.  Fined £5 + 12/6 costs.  Seeley fined £5 + 10/- costs.
