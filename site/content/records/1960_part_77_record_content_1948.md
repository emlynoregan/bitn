Leighton School Parents & Friends’ Association held its AGM.

Retiring Chairman Mr Scholz reported on improvements in the past year, including:

A new classroom and furnishings

Bituminised surroundings of the buildings

A new floor in the old classroom

The residence and garage renovated

The front fence painted and a new eastern fence erected

The play area to the west levelled and enlarged

Elected: Chairman, C.A. McDonald and Secretary-Treasurer, R. Gent.
