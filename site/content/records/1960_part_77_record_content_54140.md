Burra High School held its annual Valedictory Service at St Mary’s Church on Sunday at 7 p.m.  The church was almost filled.  The service was conducted by Rev. John Stephenson, who also played the organ.  Special preacher was Assistant Chaplain of Pulteney Grammar School, Rev. J.G.M. Gent.  Lessons were read by Headmaster L. Beare and the Methodist Minister Rev. M.H. Edwards.  Supper followed in the Parish Hall.
