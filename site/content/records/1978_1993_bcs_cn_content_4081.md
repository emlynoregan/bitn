The Bore Site opposite the Mine entrance was opened on 21 April by the Chairman of Directors of Adelaide & Wallaroo Fertilizers Ltd Mr D.M. Fowler.  Chairman of the Burra Burra DC Mr Ray Jennison thanked the company for its donation of $10,000 to enable the bore to operate and allow the Burra Creek to keep flowing.  This site also has a small fountain and the area around it will be landscaped.  A memorial bronze plaque on a plinth has been placed at the site.  [Photo.]
