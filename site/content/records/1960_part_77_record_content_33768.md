Burra Rural Youth Concert in Burra Town Hall on 31 March served up a very varied program.  The compere was Ron Dewhirst of Booborowie.

Overture	Duet played by Mrs Jack Oates and daughter Judy

Club Twirling	Dianne Day

Two Items	Cascades Band

Four Rural Youth Sketches

Duet & separate items	Mesdames G. Williams & J. Denton: mouth organ & piano accordion respectively

I’ll Take You Home Again Kathleen	Andy Graham (Also a later humorous song.)

Songs from Carousel & My Fair Lady	Burra & District Choral Society

Old Mother Hubbard Quartet	Mesdames Stolte & Jennison & Messrs Edwards & Sibly

The Fishermen of England	Burra & District Choral society

A selection from the Musicales	The Leighton Trio of Young Ladies

A Mannequin Parade of Slumber

& Beach Wear	Eight Lovely ‘Girls’ compered by Naomi Dare

The profit of $85 will benefit the Burra Hospital by $45.50.
