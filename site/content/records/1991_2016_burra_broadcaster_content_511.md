Burra Cricket Club will play Hallett next weekend in the Grand Final of the Belalie-Mokota Cricket Association.  Burra finished as minor premiers.  Alan Day writes in with brief profiles of the players:

Shane (Rambo) Ramsey Captain

Rob (Whack) Stevens Vie-Captain

Darren (Jumbo) James, who scored 139 n.o. in the second semi-final and took 4 for 42.

Paul (Bridgestone) Smedley, who has scored 197 on Burra Oval this season.

Dave (Rod Marsh) Meaney

Trent (Two Dogs) Waters

Peter, (ServWel) Opperman

Mark (Joel Garner) Prowse

Mark (Filks) Fielke
