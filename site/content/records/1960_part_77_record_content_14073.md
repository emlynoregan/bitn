Elder, Smith & Co. on instructions from Robert C. Warnes invites tenders for 25,759 acres of perpetual lease in two lots 19-35 miles east of Burra.

Lot 1.	‘Chalk Cliffs’ of 9,874 acres 19 miles east of Burra comprising Sections 140-142, 143-144, 156N, 156S, 157-159, 161-163 Hd King.  Sections 2-8, 19-23, 150 & 152-153 Hd Rees.  Disposed as 5 sheep paddocks, with a stone house of 4 rooms etc.

Lot 2.	‘Nolans’ 15,885 acres 35 miles east of Burra.  Block 64 East Hd King disposed as 7 sheep paddocks with two-roomed wood and iron cottage etc.
