The Year in Review

January

Y2K failed to create chaos.

Local celebrations of the new millennium focused on 2000 Boobs Alive.

Australia Day Awards: Burra Ward Citizen of the Year is Ian Denton.

Burra Ward Community Event is the Greening of the Burra Golf Course.

SA’s Citizen of the Year was Barbara Rayner of Hallett.

Book: Burra & Beyond launched.  600 ha fire at Mt Bryan- mostly ‘Mokota’.

February

Kooringa Masonic Lodge 150 years old – Norm Maxted writes its history.

HiChick project begins.

Dugouts archaeological project announced.

Some local flooding 21 February.

Perlite facility application to Council.

March

‘Effie’ fundraising begins for the Variety Club Bash.

‘Plenty’ Festival & ‘All That Jazz’ Exhibition.

SA Stud Merino Field Days.

Welcome to Burra for newcomers.

Pony Club 25 years old.

April

Picnic race meeting at Thistlebeds.

MIM unsuccessful search for copper north of Burra.

Clode’s Hardware closes at end of month.

Burra Community School wins major prize in the SA Prime Lamb Carcase competition.

National Parks & Wildlife announce an office for Burra.

May

Princess Royal sold to CR & S Pty Ltd.

Annual Burra Antique Fair

‘Fragments of the Past’ exhibition at the gallery – from the dugouts archaeological dig.
