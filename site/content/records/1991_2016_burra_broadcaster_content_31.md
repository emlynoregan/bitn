Burra Tennis Club will screen Paul Hogan’s most recent film Almost an Angel at Burra Town Hall 15 February.  This will see a return of movies to the Town Hall after a gap of almost three years.  A chicken and champagne supper will follow the film.  $7 for the film and $12.50 for the film and supper.  Children $3.
