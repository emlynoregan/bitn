BCS.  The led steer team is on track for the Royal Show.  They will have a Murray Gray at 360 kg in the lightweight class, a Poll Hereford at 490 kg in the heavyweight class and a South Devon in the heavyweight Jap Ox class.  Approximately 30 schools compete at the show.  [Photo.]
