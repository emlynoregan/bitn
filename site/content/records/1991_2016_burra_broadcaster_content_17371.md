Sheep Sale.  Last Wednesday’s Burra combined agents’ sheep sale saw the yarding of 22,000 sheep.  The sale was described as buoyant, especially for young ewes, with prices ranging from $20 to $40.80, with most in the $32 to $37 range for the better types.  [Further details and a photo printed.]
