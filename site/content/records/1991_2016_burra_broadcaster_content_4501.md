Maureen Wright of Burra has been appointed a deputy member of the recently formed State Heritage Authority.  Maureen has been chair of the Burra Branch of the National Trust for many years and is a member of the Heritage Advisory Committee in Burra.  The State Heritage Authority provides advice to the Government on funding and heritage agreements.
