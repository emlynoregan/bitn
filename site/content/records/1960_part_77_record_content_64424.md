Obituary.  William Henry Quinn died 15 December at St Andrew’s Hospital Adelaide aged 68.  He was the son of the late William Henry Quinn and Ann Quinn of Mt Bryan and brother to Tom Ellen (Mrs Richards), Ann and John.  (And to Mary & Kathleen deceased.)  [Born 6 January 1904 Aberdeen: died 15 December 1972 Adelaide, residence Mt Bryan.]
