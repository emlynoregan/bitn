Burra Sheep Sales last week saw the disposal of about 15,500 sheep for a total of c. $300,000.

Top price for stud Merino Ewes was $191, a record for SA, sold by Barton Hills Proprietors.

The Annual Collinsville Ewe Sale on 16 October saw prices well in advance of last year.

1972, 808 ewes averaged $11.95

388 lambs averaged $7.40

1973, 1,283 ewes averaged $33.84

586 lambs averaged $16.44

Overall sale average was $33.85.

Top price to Barton Hills for 10 selected stud ewes was $191.

W.B. Ashby & Sons offered 37 and sold 37 for an average $37.30.

J. & F. Sullivan & Co. offered 50 and sold 50 for an average $46.25.

Willogoleche Ltd offered 30 and sold 30 for an average $22.50.

J.T. & M.E. Drew offered 24 and sold 24 for an average $32.08

R.W. Lehmann offered 20 and sold 20 for an average $55.00.

Lehmann Bros offered 40 and sold 40 for an average $19.75.

John Collins & Sons offered 1,062and sold 476 for an average $49.57.
