Merino Stud Field Days were held last week.  Collinsville sold 6 rams for over 1,000 guineas each, two of them at 2,000 guineas, both of which went to WA.  Other studs visited were Barton Hill, The Grove, Woollana, Mackerode, Mt Glen View, The Bluff, Narowie, Cappeedee, Ashrose, Willogoleche, Greenfields, Melton, North Bungaree and Somme Brae.
