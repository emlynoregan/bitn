Weather.  In the latter part of last week general rains of one to more than three inches were recorded.  Examples of totals: Sturt Vale 152 points, Quondong 107, Mt Bryan East up to 360, Hogback 338, Woolgangi 292, Koomooloo 186 and Burra 240.  Page 3 adds the information that Dust Hole Creek carried its biggest flood in years.
