Fire.  Mt Bryan CFS and other firefighting units extinguished a potentially dangerous fire at the northern end of Mt Bryan on Monday 30 May.   An LPG gas leak in a tow truck caused the fire, which extensively damaged the vehicle.  The Barrier Highway was closed for about one hour.
