Weather.  Good general soaking rains have fallen.  Falls of an inch and more have been general.  Mt Bryan East had 116 points and ‘The Gums’ reports 80.  Mr G. Webster reports 120 points at Hanson.  Burra had 98 points to 3.45 p.m.  Out East, Old Koomooloo had 83 points and Thistlebeds 80.
