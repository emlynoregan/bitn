The Folk Federation of SA will hold a Folk Dance Festival in Burra over the October long weekend at BCS.  As a warn-up exercise they plan a folk dance in Burra Town Hall on 9 June.  The band will describe each dance and people attending from Adelaide will walk through the dances before actual dancing takes place.
