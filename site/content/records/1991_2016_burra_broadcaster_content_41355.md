Burra National Trust Craft Fair was held 31 October and 1 November in ideal conditions, but had a low attendance, perhaps due to competing attractions.  There was a good range of goods on sale.  Burra Men’s shed and Mama Lin catered for the lunches and the National Trust sold coffee and cake.
