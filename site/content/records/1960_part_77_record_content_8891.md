Obituary.  The body of Mr Bert Schultz, late of Eudunda, was recovered by the Police Aqua Lung Squad from the River Murray near Bryant’s Creek on Sunday morning.  [In 1978 this creek near Morgan was renamed Bryan Creek.]

[Bertram Albert Schultz born 30 November 1905 Kapunda: died 19 November 1961 Morgan, residence Somerton.]
