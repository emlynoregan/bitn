Obituary.  Mr John Sampson died at his Bridge Street residence on 20 April aged 78.  He had lived in Kooringa for 63 years, having arrived in SA on La Belle Alliance in July 1847.  In his youth he was employed at the Burra Mine, but later had an auctioneerâ€™s and general agency business and was for years the Bailiff at the Local Court at Redruth.  He was a Town Councillor for many years and Mayor for three terms.  He was a member of the Oddfellows Lodge for more than 55 years and the Chairman of Trustees.  He leaves a widow and one son, Mr A. Sampson of Broken Hill and several daughters, who left Burra many years ago.  [Born 17 December 1831.]
