Burra Arts Council’s ‘Four Way Focus’ Exhibition at Kookaburra concludes on Australia Day:

George Vrastaminos – series of classic cars in acrylic

Stephen Richardson – silver gelatine prints on abstract themes

Dieter Hauptmann – scientific microscape of crystals in polarised light

Dick Cleland – local and Australian landscapes in black and white.
