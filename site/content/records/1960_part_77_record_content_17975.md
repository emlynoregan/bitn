Morgan-Whyalla Pipeline Duplication.  Sir Thomas Playford said recently in Parliament that in 1963-64 some 36 miles of pipe will be laid and the 100 million gallon reservoir at Lincoln Gap will be completed.  By the end of 1964 it is anticipated 83 miles will be laid.
