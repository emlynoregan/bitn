Burra racing Club will receive $540 from the TAB operations in SA for the 15 months of operation since its inception on 29 March 1967.

Burra High School Ball was held on Friday night in the Burra Town Hall.  The event made a profit of nearly $200 for the Parents & Friends’ Association.  Music was by Pfitzner’s Band.  Mr Maurice Collins was MC.
