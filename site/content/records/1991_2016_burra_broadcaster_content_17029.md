On-Property Sheep Sales

Two inaugural stud sheep sales were held last week.

Kelvale Poll Merino Stud on 21 September had a top price of $2,000 and an average of $512.

Greenfields of Hallett sold 220 stud Merino ewes to a top price of $230 and an average of $57.15 and 151 poll Merino ewes to a top price of $155 and an average of $48.21.

60 Merino rams had a top price of $3,100 and an average of $1,049.

37 of 40 poll Merino rams sold for a top of $2,900 and an average of $773.
