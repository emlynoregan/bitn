Accident.  12.30 a.m. on Wednesday a car driven by Mr D. Pennington, a teacher of Burra, overturned on the Hallett-Burra Road two miles south of Hallett.  The driver suffered concussion and a severely lacerated leg.  Damage to the vehicle was estimated at $900.
