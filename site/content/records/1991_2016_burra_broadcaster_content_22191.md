Mid North Sheep Dog Training Club held trials at Farrell Flat over Easter.  Winners:

Three sheep section was won by Rob Macklin with Sherwood Karl.

The Honeyvale improver was won by Anthony Ireland with Clementsville Bell.

Friskies Lucky Dog Open was won by Stephan Cross with Armadeus Pearl. [sic]
