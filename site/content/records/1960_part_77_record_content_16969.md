Weather.  Light showers fell early in the week and then on Sunday a further 50-100 points fell throughout the district.  The Burra creek ran strongly and cut roads in several places.  Burra 122 points for the week, Mt Bryan 111, Booborowie 132, World’s End 56.

Aberdeen Football Club held a car trial on Sunday: won by Barry Allen and Jim Richards.
