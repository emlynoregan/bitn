LCL District Committee met on Friday in Burra Town Hall and was chaired by Mr Cliff Lucas of Hallett.  Speakers were Dr Bruce Eastick, recently elected Leader of LCL in SA and Mr E.C. Allen, Member for Frome.  At 3 p.m. the Member for Wakefield in the House of Representatives Hon. C.R. Kelly addressed a meeting which was open to the public.
