The 2006 Variety Bash overnighted at Koomooloo on Saturday, en route to Broken Hill.  They made a donation towards a computer and webcam for owners Henry & Jackie Dunn’s son, Jock, aged 3.  They also made a donation to Remote Isolated Children’s Education (RICE), among other donations to charity.  Bash vehicles must have been built before June 1970 and have a maximum crew of four.
