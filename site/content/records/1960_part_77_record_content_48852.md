School Teachers moving:

Mr & Mrs Nigel Brand with Debbie, Stuart & Nicola will move to Willunga where Nigel will be Deputy Headmaster of the High School.

Ross Johnson & his wife move to the city where he has a position at Taperoo High School.

Miss Raelene Potticary will go to Darwin.

Mr & Mrs Bill Wood from Booborowie School will move to an outer metropolitan school.

Mr John Ayliffe, well know here, has been appointed Senior Master at Burra High School.
