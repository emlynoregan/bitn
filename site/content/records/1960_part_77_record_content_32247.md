Margaret Loutit, daughter of Mr & Mrs Roy Jeffery of Burra has obtained a PhD for research in Microbiology at the University of Otago in New Zealand.  Her early education was at Burra School followed by MLC, where she gained scholarships for Intermediate, Leaving and Leaving Honours years and in her final year was Head Prefect of the Boarding House and Dux of the College.  She won a scholarship to the University Adelaide and was awarded the John Bagot Scholarship in her first year.  She obtained a Master of Science Degree at the University of Adelaide before marrying Dr John Loutit.
