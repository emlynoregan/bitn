Advt.  Elders-GM announce a property and clearing sale in the Estate of the late George Brittle [sic: for Brettle] Morgan, of that land comprising part allotments 43 & 47 [of Kooringa] subject to rights of way and a particularly described right of way – being a private road 25 ft x 188 ft 7 inches from Kangaroo Street to Ware Street and likely to be declared a public road.  With a substantial stone house of 9 main rooms etc. with cellar, 10,000 gallon underground tank and galvanised iron garage 35 ft x 13 ft; known as ‘Somerset House’.  Also furniture and sundries.
