Harold Raymond and His Gloombusters recently visited Burra and Spalding in severely wintry conditions, nevertheless it was successful.  Gross receipts at Burra amounted to £81-10-0, at Clare £66-17-11 and at Spalding £58-3-0.  Nett proceeds will aid Townsend House, Brighton, Home and School for blind and deaf children.
