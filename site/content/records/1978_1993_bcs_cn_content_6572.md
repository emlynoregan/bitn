Burra Postmaster Mr Les Saunders suggests to the National Trust Branch that Burra have a pictorial postmark for use on the 130,000 articles postmarked annually in Burra.  The Trust will take the matter up with the Burra Burra DC, the Tourist Office and the Burra Traders’ Association.
