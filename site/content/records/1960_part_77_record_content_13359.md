Cricket.  Mokota Association

At a recent meeting Mr C. Furst was again elected President with Mr Ken Deer as Association Secretary.

At a meeting of the Burra Cricket Club and the Koonoona Cricket Club on 29 September it was decided to merge the two clubs and enter the Mokota Association.  The team is to be known as Koonaburra.  President is Mr Ron Pearse and Secretary Mr A. Fillmore. The Mokota Association this year comprises Spalding, Booborowie, Hallett, Mt Bryan, North Booborowie, Burra Footballers, Koonaburra and Leighton.
