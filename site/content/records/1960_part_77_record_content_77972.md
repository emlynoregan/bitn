Burra Community School

A new Parents and Friends Association was formed on Thursday to support the school and keep abreast of current educational trends.  President is Mrs L. Kemp; Vice-President, Mr B. Stockman; Secretary, Mrs H. Reed and Treasurer, Mrs S. Nourse.
