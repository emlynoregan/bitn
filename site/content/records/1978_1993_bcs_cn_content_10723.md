Rosie Lloyd competed in the U20 titles in Canberra last weekend.  She won the silver medal in the 400 m hurdles and in the process broke to SA U18 record for the event.  Her father Don Lloyd was a member of the local A Grade tennis team, which won the Grand Final at the weekend.
