BRTBA is organising film nights at the Burra Town Hall at approximately one month intervals and these are proving to be quite popular.  First film at 7 p.m. and second at 9 p.m.  Coming features:

2 February	102 Dalmatians

Unbreakable

2 March		Coyote Ugly

What Women Want

Burra will host a Biathlon for the first time on 14 April.  Contestants will cycle in and around Burra Oval.  There will be several categories:

Children		1.5 km cycle and 0.5 km run

Short Course	7 km cycle and 2.7 km run

Long Course	34 km cycle and 5.4 km run

The long course is also open to pairs and teams.  Proceeds will aid Burra Emergency Services.  Entry Fee $10.
