The many special features will include:

Blade and mechanical shearing competition

Wool testing demonstration

Video film ‘Proud Pedigree’

Elders historical film of Market Day sale in Burra (1930s)

Historical photos loaned by local Merino Studs

Fashion Parade by ‘Looking Good Boutique’

Sunday 12 October	Procession at 11 a.m. including:

Dan Burt: one man band

Humbolt

Royal Caledonian Pipe Band

Cavalcade of cars

Jubilee Wombat

Dick Frankel’s Jazz Disciples

Tanunda Junior Brass Band

Burra Yacht Club Regatta

Bullock dray and Clydesdale rides

Puppet Shows

Monday13 October	Bullockies Breakfast

Start of the Gulf Road Re-enactment to be officially opened by Kym Bonython, Chairman of the J150 Board at 11 a.m. at the old stone house on Smelts Road.

Friday to Monday	Arts & Crafts Fair at the Civic Centre

Evenings	Performances of the Flinders University Drama Centre production She’ll be Riot at Redruth Gaol.

Teas at the Sports Complex from 6 to 7.30 p.m. Saturday and Sunday

Burra Rural Youth licensed disco at Burra Town Hall on Saturday night

Burra Yacht Club Cabaret at Burra Town Hall Sunday night
