Maggie Beer will attend a Burra Community School meeting on 12 April to discuss the Stephanie Alexander Kitchen Garden Program for which Maggie is the State Ambassador.  The program aims:

To increase students’ knowledge confidence and skills in cooking and gardening.

To develop a greater understanding of healthy and sustainable eating among students.

To increase students’ willingness to try new foods.

To engage disengaged students.

To benefit the family home as students share and apply their learning.
