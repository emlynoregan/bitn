Trip to Renmark

A trip for c. 50 members of Burra, Clare and Auburn National Trust and Burra and Eudunda Probus Clubs was organised for 5 & 6 November.  On Thursday they visited Morgan Pumping Station, Overland Corner Hotel, Monash, Rushton Rose Gardens, Renmano Winery and stayed at Renmark Hotel/Motel.  They had an evening trip on the River Rambler, which included dinner.  On Friday they stopped at a craft shop, a dried fruit co-operative, Berrivale and visited Olivewood, home of the Chafffey Bros and Willabalangaloo Flora and Fauna Park.  They lunched at Loxton Pioneer Village.
