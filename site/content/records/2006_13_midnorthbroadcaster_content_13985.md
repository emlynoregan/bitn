Fire.  A grass fire broke out at about 3 p.m. on Thursday in the area between Braefoot and Monavale Roads west of Burra.  It is believed a header starter the fire, which took about two hours to extinguish.  Burra & Mt Bryan CFS attended along with numerous farm vehicles.
