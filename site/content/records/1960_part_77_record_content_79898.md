BCS will hold its first Annual Ball on Friday 20 August in the Burra Town Hall.  The ball will be attended by Mr Claude Allen MP, who will launch the ‘Buy a Brick’ campaign.  This is to raise funds for the School Community Hall.  Years VI & VII students are planning an end-of-year social.
