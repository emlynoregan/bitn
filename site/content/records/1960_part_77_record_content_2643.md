St Mary’s Church

On the first anniversary of her death a memorial window will be dedicated to Mrs Mary Jane Warnes.  It has been given by her sons Rex, Jim and Tom Warnes and will be dedicated by Rev. L.R. Lenthall, who was the Rector of St Mary’s 1953-58, at a service on Sunday 19 June at 3 p.m.
