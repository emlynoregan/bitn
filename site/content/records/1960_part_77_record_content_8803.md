The Advertiser Cricket School at Burra yesterday attracted c. 400 including 250 schoolboys from the district.  The party comprised Garry Sobers, Gil Langley, Vic Richardson & Allan Lyons.  They were introduced by Mr C. Furst of the Mokota Cricket Association.  After coaching, the boys were tested on what they had learned and Ron Warrior (13) an Aboriginal boy from Hallett was the winner and received a new bat autographed by Sobers and another one for his school.
