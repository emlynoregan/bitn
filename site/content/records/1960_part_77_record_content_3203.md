Obituary.  Mrs E. Opperman was born on 24 December 1884 at Burra, a daughter of the late Mr & Mrs William Hardy.  Both Mr & Mrs Opperman spent their entire lives in Burra.  Mrs Opperman was an active member of the Red Cross and one of many who assisted greatly in the war years.  She was a regular attendant at St Maryâ€™s Church until stricken with a long illness.  She is survived by Murray of Booleroo Centre, Ann Mrs Andrews of Adelaide, Lila Mrs Leedham of Burra and Rex of Burra.  There are six grandchildren and four great grandchildren.

[Born Esther Hardy 24 December 1883 Kooringa: died 24 June 1960 Woodville, residence Burra.  Married Albert Ernest Charlie Oppermann in 1904.  The name was changed sometime to Opperman sometime after the birth of the last child in 1915.]
