Angela Quinn, who is training and working with the Missionary Sisters of Service in Tasmania, wrote to the paper with congratulations on the Copper festival and on the paper’s souvenir issue of 10 October and with Christmas wishes for all.  She then contributes almost half a column on the work of the Order of Missionary Sisters of Service.
