The RSL Dinner on Saturday night drew 50 members and visitors.  Wilf Nankivell was congratulated along with his co-officers for keeping the sub-branch alive.  Rev. Morley was present and proposed ‘Fallen Comrades’.  Branch President Bob Steele invited Brian Riggs to speak on his recent visit to ‘Watch Hill’ in Devon where a special Memorial Service was held on 28 September.  Arthur Wright proposed ‘Visitors and Kindred Associations and Howard Griffen toasted the ladies who provided the spread.
