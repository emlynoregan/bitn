Bob Heath’s insurance property management service has been bought by Neil Page & David Evans.  Sally Richardson, who has been clerk for R.A. Heath Agencies, will move across the road with the business.  The other half of the business – D & M Real Estate – has been bought by Knappsteins of Clare.

Bob will continue in real estate with D & M Real Estate’s Rural Property Division, based in Gawler.
