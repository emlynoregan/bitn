Burra Women’s LCL met for the first time this year on 6 October.   The death of former Vice-President and supporter Mrs A.L. Kellock was noted.  Mr Jim Riggs was elected Vice-President.  An open meeting at Burra Town Hall on 29 October will be addressed by Mrs Anne Neill who has operated as an undercover agent in the Australian Communist Party.
