Letter.  Ken Phillips writes re the Hanson-Burra Agricultural Bureau, which had been in recess for 12 months.  The Bureau had started at Hanson in 1928 and if ten or more interested farmers, employees, etc. would be willing to organise meetings, he would be prepared to restart the group.
