Weather.  Good opening rains have fallen in both the agricultural and pastoral areas, though totals vary considerably.  Burra recorded 136 points, Cappeedee 215, Old Koomooloo 101, Sturt Vale 130, Woolgangi 130, Glenora 100, Hallett 231 and Booborowie 160.
