Obituary.  Rev. Ross Lenthall, a former well-loved Rector of St Mary’s died in Adelaide and his funeral was held on 31 August.  [Lionel Rossiter Lenthall was born 12 June 1925 at North Adelaide.  He was the Rector of St Mary’s from November 1953 to June 1958.]
