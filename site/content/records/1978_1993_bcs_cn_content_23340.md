Burra & District Lions Club

The Lions Club Christmas Seals can be ordered from Les Gardner of Burra.  Club President Adrian Green said half the proceeds of sales by Burra & District Lions Club would be spent directly on local charities and the remainder to Lions Drug Awareness Program.
