Burra Community School students took the top four places in the Vocational Education and Training award presentations at Bentley’s Hotel in Clare on Monday night.  Annie Low was Student of the Year.  Annie has secured a Certificate I in Rural Operations and is currently undertaking a Certificate II with plans to do a Certificate III next year.  The other students with top placing in the list of twelve nominees were Jennifer Harbour, Melanie Watkins and Tayla Sims.

[Photo from left: VET Rural Operations instructor Dick Secomb, Melanie Watkins, Annie Low, Tayla Sims and school VET co-ordinator, Gus Wenzel.]
