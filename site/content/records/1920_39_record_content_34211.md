Hallett & District School Athletic & Exhibition Assoc.  The 3rd Annual Sports & Exhibition was held at Hallett 2 May.  It outstripped previous efforts (last year’s was held at Terowie).  School involved were: Yarcowie, Terowie, Booborowie, Hallett, Mt Bryan, North Booborowie, Ulooloo, Petherton, Willalo, Mt Bryan East & Sunny Brae.  [Details exceed 3½ columns.]
