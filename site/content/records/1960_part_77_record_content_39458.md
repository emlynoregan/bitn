Mrs M.A. Taylor celebrated her 90th birthday at Burra Hospital on 27 April.  She is a sister to the late Mrs A. Hirschausen and the late Mr W. Voumard and the only surviving member of a family of 13 children, being one of five of them who came to Australia from Switzerland, taking six weeks in a sailing ship.  Messrs Bert & Ivan Hirschausen and Mrs S. Scroop and Mrs J. Rowe Sen. are nephews and nieces resident in Burra.

[Marie Adelaide Taylor nee Voumard died 11 January 1971 Burra, residence Burra North.]
