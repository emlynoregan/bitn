Telephone Lines

[Inadequate capacity existed for the demand for services between Willalo, Spalding and Booborowie and between Booborowie and Mt Bryan.]  The Director of Posts and Telegraphs Mr J.R. O’Sullivan wrote in response to a query from Mr E.C. Allen MP explaining the situation and indicating that additional lines would be provided in the 1970-71 financial year.
