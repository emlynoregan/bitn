Earthquakes were recorded in the Burra region this year after a period of quiet.

On 23 January a 3.4 quake was centred near the Robertstown turn-off on the Morgan Road.

On 26 January a 3.2 quake had its centre 25 km northeast of Eudunda, just south of ‘The Gums’. 

In 1997 a 5.1 magnitude quake had its epicentre a few kilometres west of ‘The Gap’.
