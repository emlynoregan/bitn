Redruth Court.

The following were each fined for owning straying cattle:

Johanna Warner	Two offences	£1 + 10/- and £2 + 10/-

The following were each fined 5/- + 10/- costs:

Lord Bros & Humphris, W.L. James and Mrs N.H. Turner.
