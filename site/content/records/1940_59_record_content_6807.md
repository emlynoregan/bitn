Burra Primary School Arbor Day was held last 
Friday.  A dozen ornamental trees were planted.  Speeches were delivered by Head Teacher Mr Davis, Chairman of the School Council Mr H.J.B. Jennison and Rev. Gray.  Maxine Connors wrote the best essay on the day from Grade VII scholars.  [Essay printed.]
