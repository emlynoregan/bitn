Burra Small Bore Rifle Club held its AGM last Tuesday night and it was decided to open the season on 27 September.  Elected were: Captain-Treasurer, R. Angel; Vice-Captain, M. Thomas; Secretary, J. Bowden and Handicapper, R.J. Kellock.
