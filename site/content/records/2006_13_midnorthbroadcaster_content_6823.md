Paxton Square will be open to visitors and locals to inspect after the Australia Day breakfast on 26 January, from 9.30 a.m. to 11 a.m.  The Paxton Square Cottages Operations Committee will be on hand to answer questions and is seeking feedback from the public, especially ratepayers within the Goyder Council region.
