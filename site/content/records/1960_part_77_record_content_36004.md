Accidents

Two minor collisions occurred in the week.

On Friday about noon a utility driven by C.F. Pearce collided with one driven by Mr E. Heinrich on the main road near Richard’s Garage.

At 6.50 p.m. a sedan driven by Mr C.H. Nourse collided with another driven by Mr R. W. Coventry at the Junction of Market and Commercial Streets near the Post Office.
