Burra Rifle Club.  On Eight Hours Day Eudunda visited Burra.

Eudunda 727 defeated Burra 695.

There was also an Allcomers Match in which B. Lutz, A.C. Aughey, P. Oliver & F. Spencer all reached the limit of 68.  B. Lutz had the best score off the rifle with 68 and the other three then had a shoot-off for handicap honours which went to A.C. Aughey.  In the Ladies Match Mrs H.G. Oates and Mrs F.H.
