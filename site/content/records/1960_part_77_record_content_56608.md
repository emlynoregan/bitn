Inter-School Sports were held at Kapunda last Wednesday in near perfect weather conditions in contrast to the original day planned at the end of last term.  Burra was placed 4th in the Championship event (Ohlmeyer Shield) and 2nd in the Handicap Shield.  Burra has now been runner-up for this shield in three successive years.  Notable winners:

Michael Broderick	Open 100 m hurdles

Open High Jump (record)

Stephen Lane	U15 100 m hurdles (record)

U15 triple jump (record)

U15 discus

Tony Wall	U15 400 m (record)

Shane Wilkin	U13 long jump

Beverley Kleinig	Open 60 m hurdles

Open discus

Brenda Harris	U13 60 m hurdles
