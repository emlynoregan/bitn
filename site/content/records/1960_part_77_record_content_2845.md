Burra Small Bore Rifle Club held its AGM 18 June.

R.G. Bernhardt, W. Hempel and M. Mitchell were made life members in recognition of their role in setting up the club.  The club had a credit balance of Â£40.  The Captain M.E. Mitchell presented the 1st Annual Report.

Weekly shoots began in October 1959.

Thanks go to Mr W. Hempel for acting as guarantor for the fee of lighting installation and to Mr Eric Angel for the loan of finance to procure ammunition to commence the weekly shoots and to the Burra Rifle Club for their financial assistance.

Elected: Captain, R. Angel; Secretary, M. Thomas and Treasurer, K. Spackman.
