Other financial donations were made for a variety of purposes

The major fund raising events were Sports Day and Gala Night.

Sports Day was eventually washed out, but not before $178 had been raised.  (When completed some days later Aberdeen House won the shield.)

Gala Night raised over $300.

70 mothers attended the meeting.
