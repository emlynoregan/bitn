Trevor Forby of the Savings Bank staff at Burra was farewelled on Friday evening at a social and dance in the RSL Hall.  He is moving to Adelaide and his replacement is Mr A.R. Webber from Prospect.  He has been active in the Football and cricket Clubs.  A presentation of a wallet of notes was made.
