Denise Griffen, an outstanding Leaving student at BHS in 1965, has been awarded a Commonwealth Open Entrance Scholarship to study at the University.  This provides free tuition, a living allowance and other benefits at the University.  Denise is presently taking matriculation at Norwood High School.
