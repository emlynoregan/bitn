Jessica Humphrys has been nominated for the Haigh’s Chocolates Career Achievement Award.  These awards are part of the 2012 SA Community Achievement Awards.  Jessica completed a Bachelor of Education Junior/Primary at the University of SA and began her teaching career at St Joseph’s in Clare in 2003 and continued at other schools in the Mid North.  In 2009 she became a Tourist Officer with the Regional Council of Goyder.  She worked with the Goyder Tourism Working Party in helping to develop strategies from the Goyder Tourism Strategic Plan.  Recently she has worked on producing the Goyder Visitor Information Guide.
