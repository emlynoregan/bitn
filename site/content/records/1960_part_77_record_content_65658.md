Burra Apex Club will hold its Charter Dinner on Saturday 31 March.  About 250 people will attend of whom 197 will be visitors.  The dinner will be in the Town Hall and after the presentation of the charter a cabaret will be held.  A Sunday bus tour and barbecue has been arranged.
