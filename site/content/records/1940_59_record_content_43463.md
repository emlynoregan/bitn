Mr A.R. Downer, LCL Candidate for the District of Angas & Mr Hannaford, Senate Candidate, supported by G.S. Hawker MP, addressed a meeting in the Burra Town Hall on Thursday evening.  The Mayor F.T. Marston presided.

Mr Downer outlined his views and LCL policy on the big issues of the day: bank nationalisation,  the menace of Communism, the relationship with Great Britain and the outside world and possible abolition of appeals to the Privy Council â€“ all threatened by the Chifley Government.
