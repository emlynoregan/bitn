Geraldton Subterranean Clover has been a big success in many areas of Spalding, Jamestown and Burra Districts.  It has outclassed Dwalganup.  Its low growth is a drawback in weedy paddocks.  It thrives in areas with 16-19 inches of rain and is more lime tolerant than Dwalganup.
