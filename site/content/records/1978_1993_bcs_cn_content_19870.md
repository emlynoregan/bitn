Obituary.  Mr George Woodman, a long-time district resident, died on 24 January aged 86.

[Registered at birth as both George Albert and as George Alfred Woodman; born 4 March 1903, Kooringa.  He chose George Albert when he married Amy Amelia Barrett Hinks in 1930.]
