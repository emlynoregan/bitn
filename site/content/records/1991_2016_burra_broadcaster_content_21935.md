Burra & Districts Obedience Dog Club has reached its seventh year.  Membership has grown from an initial 10 to 35 this year.  Handlers need to be at least 12 years old to start training.  Classes range from learners to Class 3.  A Class 4 is possible for those considering Obedience Trialling and/or commencing Agility Training.  Training takes place on Sundays at the Burra Showgrounds.
