Advt.  Elders-GM under instructions from R.W. Humphrys, who is relinquishing his stud, will auction the property ‘Bonnie Brae’ at Burra RSL Hall on 23 September.

The property of 662½ acres freehold is situated 14 miles west of Burra and comprises sections 398, 400, 401 and 403 Hundred of Ayers and is subdivided into ten paddocks with a substantial stone house etc.
