Obituary.  Ellie Byrnes, wife of Percy Byrnes, died in Adelaide on 21 August.  She and her husband had conducted a shoe shop in Burra for many years before retiring to Adelaide.  She is survived by her husband and children, John, Des and Moira.  [Born Ellen Mabel Fitzgerald 17 May 1904 Solomontown.  The surname should be Byrne.]
