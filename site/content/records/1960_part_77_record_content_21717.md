Weather.  Further general soaking rains of between one and two inches have made this one of the best Septembers for years.  On the inside country crops have been progressing very favourably.  Over the weekend Burra recorded 81 points and rain continued on Monday.  Many areas close to the town received up to 150 points.  Mt Bryan East recorded 165 and Farrell Flat 132.  To the east Woolgangi received 131, Baldina 130, Pine Valley 116, Quondong 134, Old Koomooloo 150, Balah 94 and Mongolata 175 and still raining there heavily.
