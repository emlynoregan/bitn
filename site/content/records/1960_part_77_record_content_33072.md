Burra High School

The results of the Intermediate Examination for 1966 are printed.  Under the new system all candidates get certificates listing their results.  Subjects are graded with numbers from 1 to 6.  1 signifies the top 10%, 2 the next 25%, 3 the next 15%, 4 the next 25%, 5 the next 15 % and 6 the bottom 10%.
