Methodist Circuits

In the New Year the Hallett-Terowie Circuit will be dissolved.  Terowie will move to the Peterborough-Yongala Circuit.  Whyte Yarcowie & Hallett will go to the Jamestown United Parish and Willalo & Mt Bryan will join the Burra Circuit.

Rev. Kyle Waters President of the Methodist Conference will give the address at Burra High Schoolâ€™s Valedictory Service on 12 December at Burra Methodist Church. After the service a farewell will be given to the Headmaster Mr Lynden Beare who is going to Willunga.

The Burra Methodist Circuit Choir will sing the Christmas Cantata Night of Miracles in the Burra Methodist Church at 11 a.m. Sunday 19 December.  The Choir will be led by Mr Fred Atkin.

Rev. Edwards and family will be farewelled at a luncheon in the Church Hall after the service.  He will take up his new appointment at Elizabeth Downs on 23 January.  The incoming minister Rev. C.F. Hore will be inducted at a service at 7.30 p.m. on Friday 21 January.
