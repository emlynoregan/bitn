Burra Pre-School Kindergarten subscription list is at £11-15-0.  A list of requirements is in shop windows at Bence’s Ltd and Sara & Co.  Fees will be 5/- a week or 7/6 for two children in one family.  Ages 3½ to school age.  Secretary, Mrs G.L. Mellor.
