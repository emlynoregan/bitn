1936	Booborowie South, Booborowie North and Yakilo joined.

1938	Hilltown & Euromina joined.

1941	Spalding joined.

1943	Willalo joined.

1949	St Joseph’s Convent joined.

1950	Event cancelled due to polio epidemic.

1951	Gum Creek joined.

1953	Washpool & Hanson joined.

By 1962	Gum Creek & Washpool had left and Leighton had joined.

1965	North Booborowie School closed and merged with Willalo

[1972	Willalo left.]

By 1975	Only Leighton, Hilltown, Booborowie and Spalding remained.

1976	Mt Bryan & Hallett joined

By 1980	Leighton & Hilltown had left.

1988	Leighton re-joined.

Booborowie [South] was the only school to have continuous membership for the whole 65 years.

Booborowie and Mt Bryan will now join others in the Horrocks Hill hub of schools including Watervale, Auburn and Mintaro-Farrell Flat.

The final meet was non-competitive, but a traditional sports day will be held towards the end of term 3.
