Aileen Canny aged 13 has won the RSL Bursary with 643 marks, from 117 entrants from all over SA.  It provides three years free tuition at any approved school in SA.  She attended Gum Creek School till 1939 when she went to Leighton.  She has been dux of her class every year except for Grade II.
