Burra National Trust Branch held its AGM recently.

The Chair, Elizabeth Robertson said the year had been one of strategic planning and budgeting and redefining of the relationship with the Burra Burra DC.  The year’s projects had included:

Painting Redruth Gaol

Preservation of historic garments by Artlab

Preliminary conservation work on Kotz Cottage following a conservation study of the site.

Installation of motion detectors and alarms at its properties

Freda and Avian Pink were thanked for their many years of service to the Clare and Burra branches of the National Trust, after the announcement that they would not stand for positions this year.

This year saw the introduction of individual site budgets, a capital budget and forward planning.

The plans for 1995 include:

Interpretation for Redruth Gaol

A Victoriana Picnic

Selected displays of the branch’s extraordinary wealth of archival artefacts

Elected were: Chair, Elizabeth Robertson; Vice-Chair, Maureen Wright; Secretary, Tim Horsnell; Assistant Secretary, Jenny Field and Treasurer, Joan de Metris.
