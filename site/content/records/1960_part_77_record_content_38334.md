Burra High School.  Christopher freeman, son of Mr & Mrs A. Freeman of Booborowie has gained an Intermediate Bursary.

Malcolm White, son of Mr & Mrs K. White of Burra North and Andrew Ryan, son of Mr & Mrs J. Ryan of Burra won Continuation Exhibitions.  All of the scholarships are for two years and worth $50 for the first and $60 for the second year.

The Advertiser tennis coaching school visited on Tuesday last with Messrs Clem Teague and Alan Rose.
