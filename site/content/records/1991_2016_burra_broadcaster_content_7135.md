An Exhibition of ABC TV News Weather Shots is at the Burra Civic Centre 7-10 June.  Weather presenter and journalist Xavier Minniecon will officially open the exhibition at 11 a.m. on 8 June.  The exhibition consists of 38 extraordinary scenes from across SA.
