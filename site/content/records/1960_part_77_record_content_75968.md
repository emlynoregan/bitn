Burra Burra DC.

$500 was set as the Chairman’s allowance for 1975-76 and Mr Brown was re-elected as Chairman.

Cr R.B. Jennison was elected Deputy Chairman.

Committees and other positions were settled.

The old water fountain on the main road is to be removed, restored and relocated, but a new site has yet to be decided.

Hockey.  Burra 3 defeated Riverton 2 in the club’s first victory.

Burra Scouts held their dance on 19 July.  21 cubs attended their last meeting.
