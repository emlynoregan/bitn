Football.  North Eastern Association

A Grade	Spalding 11.11 defeated Hallett 11.10

Burra 15.11 defeated Leighton 1.8

Booborowie 18.31 defeated Redlegs 5.6

At the picnic match at Redbanks last Sunday Aberdeen, led by Kevin Lane defeated Kooringa, led by Ian Allen, by a margin of 12 points.  About 250 watched the game.
