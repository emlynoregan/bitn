M & BHA Burra Branch reports a successful year.  The September Baby Show raised $346.  A Christmas party was held at the home of Mr & Mrs Roy Jeffery.  Elected for 1974-75 were: Patron, Mrs E.T. Baulderstone; President, Mrs Peter Stockman; Vice-President, Mrs Allan Day; Secretary, Mrs Carey Noack; Assistant Secretary, Mrs Michael Miller and Treasurer, Mrs Ken Graetz.
