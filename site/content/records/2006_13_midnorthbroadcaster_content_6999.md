Peter Opperman from the Burra IGA store is one of 1,270 independent retailers who will help provide food vouchers to fire stricken Victoria via the IGA’s Community Chest program.  In SA the appeal has raised $262,000.  Supplier Metcash and IGA stores have in total contributed $1.5million for the cause.
