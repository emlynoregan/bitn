Accident/Obituary.  Wayne Raymond Jones of Hallett aged 16 was accidentally shot on Sunday when a .22 rifle was discharged on a shooting expedition on the Collinsville Road.  Four youths were homeward bound when two rifles resting against the front seat fell over when the car stopped.  One discharged as a passenger picked it up.  The deceased was the son of Mr & Mrs Ray Jones.
