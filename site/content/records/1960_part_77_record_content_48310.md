EFS School was held at Burra.  About 100 members of the SA Emergency Fire Services and District Fire Control Officers will attend a one-day school at the Burra Showgrounds on Wednesday 12 November.  Instructions will be given in First Aid, Electrical Fire Hazards, Powers and Duties of Fire Control Officers, Recommended EFS Radio Procedure and Chemical Fire Extinguishers.
