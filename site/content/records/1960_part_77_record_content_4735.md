Burra Hospital

The Combined Committee of Women’s Organisations held its half-yearly meeting on 28 November.  President Mrs A. Tennant presided.

Delegates discussed the long term program of hospital improvements proposed by the Hospital Board.  Among them were:

Painting the interior of the Mid wing

Innerspring mattresses throughout

Additional rainwater storage

Sealing areas in front and at the rear of the hospital

Certain repairs to the Hospital and to the Nurses’ Home

Donations raised in the past twelve months:

Burra CWA £75

Burra Red Cross £45

Burra North Red Cross £75

Mt Bryan CWA £59-5-0

Booborowie CWA £60

Hanson CWA £20

Farrell Flat CWA £10
