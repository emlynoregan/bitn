Coca Cola Games – State Schools Athletics Championships

Four BCS athletes took part.

Adam Day excelled and came 1st in 800 m, 400 m and 400 m hurdles, setting a record time of 56.1 secs in the hurdles.

Cheryl Day made the finals of the U17 100 m, 200 m, 400 m and long jump.

Natalie Taylor came 4th in the javelin.

Clay Wooton registered good performances in the javelin and shot-put for a 13-year-old competing in U15 group.
