St Mary’s.  The Lord Bishop of Willochra Rt Rev. Thomas Edward Jones was welcomed to St Mary’s with an impressive Liturgical Reception last Friday night.  After the service supper was served in the hall.

The Mayor & DC Chairman Mr G. Finch welcomed the Bishop on behalf of the civic authorities.  Father Hay of Farrell Flat Catholic Church, Rev. M. Lind from the Burra Methodist Circuit and Mr E. Pietsch of the Burra Lutheran Congregation welcomed the Bishop on behalf of their churches.  Bishop Jones recounted some of his life, his interests and his duties.  Robert Reed, a Trustee and Warden was a most capable compere.
