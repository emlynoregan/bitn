Heidi Thompson, who is in Year 8 at BCS was the state winner in the Feed the World 2020 Competition, organised by the SA Grain Industry Trust Fund.  Last week she received a presentation of $200 and $900 for the school, for her outstanding essay in the competition, from the Chairman of SAGITF, Mr Ken Schaefer.
