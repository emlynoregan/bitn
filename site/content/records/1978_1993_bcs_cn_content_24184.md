Burra Burra DC

Will meet with landowners adjacent to Redbanks and with police to formulate a submission to the Department of Lands re the future of the Redbanks Area.

Accepted a donation of a heritage seat with plaque in memory of past members of Burra North Red Cross – to be located alongside the bicycle track.

Will meet representatives of the Minister for Emergency Services about the rationalisation of the MFS & CFS.

Representatives attended the launch of the brochure ‘Unique Accommodation Experiences’ and the inspection of the ten establishments concerned on 17 June.
