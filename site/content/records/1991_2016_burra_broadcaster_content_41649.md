St Just Café opened on Commercial Street on 31 March.  The proprietors are Sandy & Ross Sims, who were previously in Burra as the proprietors of The Miner’s Couch a few years ago.  The cook is newcomer to the town, Michael Smitham.  His wife Fiona has begun a hairdressing business ‘The Burra Fringe’.  Fiona grew up in Jamestown, had a hairdressing salon in Clare six years ago and lately at Victor Harbor.  Michael grew up in Hart (near Blyth) and has managed the Bridgewater Inn restaurant and had his own café in Pt Elliot.  [2 photos.]
