Burra Rural Youth decided to hold a Debutante Ball on 29 July with the Dee Jay Juniors to provide the music.  Sir Thomas Playford will be asked to receive the debutantes.  On 25 February Shell Pictures were shown at Burra Town Hall.  It has been decided that the proceeds from the Easter Dance at the Burra RSL Hall will aid the Wool Pavilion.
