Burra Town Hall had about 100 people along on Saturday 24 November to celebrate its 150th birthday.  Having heard the voices of the Peterborough Sing Australia Choir, Burra has decided to start its own branch of Sing Australia.  John Fuss has been appointed conductor of this initiative.
