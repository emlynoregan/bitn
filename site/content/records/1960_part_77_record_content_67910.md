‘The Unknown City’, a carving of a city landscape some four feet square, of some 50 small buildings, will be on display for one day on 16 September at the Saddleworth Museum.  It was carved about 120 years ago by the late Leonard I’Anson (VII) and is kept at the family home at Steelton.
