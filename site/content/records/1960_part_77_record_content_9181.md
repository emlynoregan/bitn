Burra High School held its speech night on Thursday at the Burra Town Hall, which was packed with c. 500 people for the occasion.  Chairman of the High School Council H.J.B. Jennison presided.  Mr E.A. Haese was guest speaker.  Mr Haese was a former headmaster at Burra and later served at Peterborough, Nuriootpa, Mt Barker and Murray Bridge from which he retired. He has just returned from a six month tour overseas.  After his address prizes were distributed.  Various items by students were interspersed throughout the evening and a new summer uniform for the girls was displayed.
