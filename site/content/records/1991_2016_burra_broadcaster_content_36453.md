Three of the recipients started with the Mt Bryan Branch and transferred to Burra when that closed.  The conference was opened by Irene Hirschausen.  There was an address by SA Patron Mrs Val Broadbent and elections were held.  Jan Tiver is the new Northern Zone President.  After lunch Nathaniel, Danny and Karen O’Brien sang to delegates.  The 2006 conference on 3 April will be held at Jamestown.
