Accident/Obituary.  Just after 3 p.m. on Sunday 27 April a station wagon and a b-double collided on the Burra bypass road.  The driver of the station wagon was a 42-year-old woman from Pekina, who died at the scene.  The b-double driver received minor injuries, but the cab of the prime mover caught fire and was extinguished by CFS crews.
