The Therapeutic Goods Administration has another long list of goods manufactured by PAN Pharmaceuticals Ltd that were the subject of an Urgent Medical Recall, due to serious quality and safety breaches in the manufacturing process since 1 May 2002.  The quality, safety or effectiveness of these items could not be assured.
