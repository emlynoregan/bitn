Burra Rifle Club fired the 17th stage of the Pine Valley Trophy over 600 & 700 yards.  Best were W.K. Pexton, W. Woodman and T.R. Pexton.  Off the gun the best were W.G .Woodman, W.K. Pexton and K.S. Dawson.
