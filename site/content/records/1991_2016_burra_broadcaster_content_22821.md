Palliative Care.  Since October 1996 Burra Palliative Care Trained Support Workers, co-ordinated by Noreen Hallion (Clinical Manager) and Rosemary Seaford, (Community Health Nurse) have been functioning as a group of dedicated volunteers able to assist with the care of people diagnosed with a life-threatening illness.
