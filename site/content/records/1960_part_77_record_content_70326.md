Burra & District Ambulance Association held its AGM.  Mr R.L. Thamm was re-elected Chairman. In the year the ambulance travelled 5,300 miles attending 6 car accident cases and 17 sickness cases which had to be transferred to Adelaide hospitals and taking 11 accident and 19 sickness cases to the local hospital as well as attending 24 sporting fixtures.
