Paxton Square Cottages have won an SA Tourism Award in Category 22, Budget Accommodation, thanks to the efforts of manager Maureen Bevan and a carefully prepared submission by Secretary of the Lewis Board of Management, Helen Stockman.  The award was presented last week at the Adelaide Convention Centre.
