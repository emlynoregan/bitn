Football.  At Victoria Park on Saturday Burra 6.12 (48) defeated Petersburg 3.3 (21).

On Wednesday Kooringa 74 defeated Ramblers 34.

The Petersburg footballers were given a dinner at the Burra Hotel Friday evening, presided over by W.H. Hardy.  [The report runs to Â¾ column.]
