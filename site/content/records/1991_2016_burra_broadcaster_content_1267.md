David Robert Jennings of 8 Market Street.

With community help assisted in getting 13 local youth to gain Queen’s Scout Awards and nurtured 6 to become Young Australians of the Year.

On Burra School Council for 6 years.

With help organised an Army Cadet Unit and involved with Outdoor Education Program and with lifesaving and swimming instruction.

Lay reader and Councillor in the Anglican Church.

Interested in heritage and tourism.
