Weather.  Damaging winds swept across the state on Friday night causing widespread property damage and fallen trees.  Countless shed and fences were also damaged.  Sheds were damaged at Mt Bryan and fences at Booborowie.  A number of trees were toppled along Smelts Road and at the Burra Community School.  [Photo]
