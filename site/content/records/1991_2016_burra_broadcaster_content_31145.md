Burra Hospital Auxiliary.  At the AGM President Helen Dunow reported on an active year of fundraising:

Catering for the Antique Fair

Justices’ Dinner

Goyder Regional Council Dinner

Trading Table

Melbourne Cup Day Luncheon

Auction of old hospital furniture

Garden Party at Princess Royal

During the year more than $7,000 was raised, including $500 from Community Aid, $679 from the Red Cross and $614 from the Children’s Christmas Cake Auction.
