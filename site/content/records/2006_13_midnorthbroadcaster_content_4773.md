Burra National Trust recently received a cheque for $2,200 as an Arts SA grant to assist with the preservation and upgrading of the storage of its archives, presently housed in the old Police House at Burra North.  Local MP Mr Graham Gunn handed over the cheque last week.  [Photo.]
