Accident.  On Thursday evening 29 October a vehicle failed to take the corner at the intersection of Kooringa Road and the Goyder Highway.  The vehicle rolled some distance down a steep slope and ended in a small water-filled dam.  The driver was treated at Burra Hospital for mild concussion.  The vehicle could not be retrieved until Saturday.
