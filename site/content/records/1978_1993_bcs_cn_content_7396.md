Tragic Bush Fires broke out on Ash Wednesday 16 March with great loss of life and property.  CFS units and personnel from Hallett, Booborowie, Farrell Flat and Burra along with many private units went to the Clare fire.  Burra St John Ambulance was on standby and Burra SES attended.
