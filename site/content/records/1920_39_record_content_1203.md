Burra Show Soc. met last Friday.  Mr T. McWaters as Vice-President was in the chair and the Sec. Mr Foote read the balance sheet.  The year ended in credit Â£32.  The Hon. J. Lewis was re-elected Patron.  President I.J. Warnes was overseas on a holiday and was re-elected with M.A. Radford as a Vice-President as well as Mr McWaters.  Discussion on the show date was in favour of reverting to the end of September.  A general meeting was called for 30 April to resolve several outstanding issues.
