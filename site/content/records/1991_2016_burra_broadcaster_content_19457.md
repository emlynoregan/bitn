Friends of the Women’s & Children’s Hospital, Burra Auxiliary held its AGM recently.  For the year they had raised $2,334 from raffles and trading tables, $600 from the car lottery and $300 from the sale of hospital merchandise.  The auxiliary had been formed in 1956 to aid the then Adelaide Children’s Hospital.  Elected for 1999-2000: President, Leonie Ryan; Secretary, Colleen Spackman and Treasurer, Pat Taylor.
