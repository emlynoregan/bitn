Tuesday	2.40 p.m. Burra to Adelaide

Friday	10.30 a.m. Burra [or Adelaide?] to Peterborough

ANR train services depart Burra for Adelaide

Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday at 8.07 a.m. and Saturday 8.18 a.m.
