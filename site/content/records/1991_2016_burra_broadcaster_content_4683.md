The Veteran Car Club’s Diamond Jubilee Veteran Tour will meet in Burra on Sunday 18 September and will hold a series of runs based in Burra until 24 September, when many of the vehicles will then go on to take part in the Bay to Birdwood run on 25 September.

19 September	Visits to historic Burra sites

20 September	Saddleworth, Auburn, Martindale Hall and Sevenhill.

21 September	A free day with lunch at Princess Royal

22 September	Display of the vehicles on the Clare Oval and a run to Geralka Farm

23 September	Run to Hallett, Cappeedee & Ulooloo with dinner in the Burra Town Hall.

Three of the participating cars were once owned by the Warnes Family of Woolgangi, including the oldest; a 1904 Wolseley.

Bartholomaeus and Mr Pearce.]
