Kooringa Masonic Lodge.  Bro. Keith Robert Phillips was installed as Worshipful Master on Thursday night, after which a ball was held in the Town Hall and over 350 guests attended.  Angel’s Band from Balaklava provided the music.  Twenty-nine Worshipful Masters signed the visitors’ book.
