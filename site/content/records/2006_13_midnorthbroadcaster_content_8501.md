Ladies’ Golf.  The Champion of Champions trophy winners were decided at the Burra Golf Course on 4 September.  The winner was Cecelia Douglas of Penfield.  Winner of the silver salver in the handicap section was Robyn Sullivan of Gawler.  Andrea Rowe of Burra won the bronze salver.
