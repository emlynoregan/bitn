Great SA Bike Ride will bring 800 to 1,000 cyclists to Burra on Monday 28 September.  The 8 day ride is organised by Bicycle Victoria and leaves Sedan on 27 September, traveling through Eudunda, Burra, Clare Valley and Barossa to Adelaide, finishing on 4 October.
