Lighten Up SA.  Burra Hospital health resource nurse, Carrie Affolter, said the Burra Hospital teams managed to lose an impressive total of 72.3 kilograms in the challenge.  The aim of the program is to kick-start a healthier life style and so reduce the incidence of chronic disease.
