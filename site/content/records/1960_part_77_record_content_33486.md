Burra Football Club held its AGM last Wednesday with a good gathering of 37 players and supporters.  The general account shows a credit balance of $439.34 and the Benevolent Fund has a credit of $437.43.  Elected were: Patron, Reg Warnes; President, E. Pietsch; Vice-President, A. Baulderstone; Secretary, G. Baulderstone and Treasurer, B. Simpson.  The President reported a successful year in social and financial terms.  Barry Eberhard is to be complimented on his work as coach.  Tony Fillmore was responsible for the efficient management of the Colts and Barry Eberhard was also successful in winning the Mail Medal.  Improvements for the year were the erection of a tea room at a cost of $350, repairs to the showers and hot water system costing $70 and a new set of guernseys and tracksuits at $133.  In addition $150 was contributed towards oval improvements.
