The Adelaide Chemical Company announced on Monday that a 74% share in the production assets at Burra will be sold for $4 million.  The new controlling shareholder Ausminco will take charge from 1 July.  ACC’s subsidiary, Mt Gunson Mines makes up $500,000 of the deal.  Ausminco is a private company controlled by Adelaide Chemical Company’s Mr Mark Woodhead.  As of 1 July the company will adopt the name Adchem.
