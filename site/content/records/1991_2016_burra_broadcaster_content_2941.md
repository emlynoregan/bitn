Australia Day Citizen Awards

Approximately 200 people attended breakfast of Wednesday on the lawns in Market Square.

Harry Quinn received the award for Citizen of the Year.

The BCS Principal received the award on behalf of the Boarding House for the Community Event of the Year.

Brian Hastelow, Burra postmaster presented Mt Quinn with the Australia Post Award.
