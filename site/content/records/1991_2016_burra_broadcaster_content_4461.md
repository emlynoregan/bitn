Accident.  A collision between a utility and a car at Ford’s Corner caused $5,000 damage and resulted in the driver of the car being treated for minor injuries at the Burra Hospital.  The other driver was given an expiation notice for failing to give way and having a blood alcohol level of 0.07%.
