Letter.  Bob Disher of Booborowie writes re the Kokoda Trail, pointing out that Arthur Seekamp, former Booborowie Postmaster for many years and lawn bowler, served on the Trail.  He commanded a platoon in the 30th Battalion.  He had previously served with distinction in the 2/10th Battalion at Tobruk.
