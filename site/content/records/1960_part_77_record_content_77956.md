‘Glen View’ Mr Bryan was auctioned at the RSL Hall in Burra on 19 October.  The 2,733.8 ha property was offered in nine lots of which five were sold at auction:

‘Back Paddock’ of 97.2 ha for $15,000 to Dudley Cockington Nominees Pty Ltd of Burra.

‘Weber’s Front Paddock’ of 54.27 ha for $7,900 to H.D. & C.L. Jones of Hallett.

‘Weber’s Homestead’ of 167.67 ha for $11,000 to Alden Stud, Box 37, Hallett.

‘Scrub Paddock’ of 454.8 ha for $11,000 to A.W. & G.A. Thomas of Mt Bryan.

‘Ned Weber’s Paddock’ of 217.08 ha for $13,000 to D.B. Brooks.

Negotiations are proceeding for the other lots.
