Obituary.  Stella Alice Humphrys died suddenly 23 September at Medindie Gardens aged 84.  She was the wife of Roy W. Humphrys of East St Burra and mother of Gwen, Merv, Margaret, Colin, Ruth & Barbara.  There are 11 grandchildren and 2 great grandsons.  [Born Stella Alice Blunt 27 July 1888 Riverton.]
