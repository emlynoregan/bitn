East Bungaree On-Property Auction

92 rams sold for an average of $844 on 11 September.  The top price was $6,500 and the second was $4,000.  The proceeds of the sale of this second top priced ram were donated to Burra Community Development, free of commission and will be used for BCS’s 2003 ‘First Edge’ camp.
