The Peoplescape Figures from Burra will be on show 17 Sept to 15 October in the Burra Community Library, before they go to the lawns of Australia’s Federal Parliament.  Students from Sue Langford’s class are preparing a figure to represent Sir Hubert Wilkins.  Shirley Thamm is preparing an image of Ian McNamara and Brian Thamm will present a figure representing Goyder.
