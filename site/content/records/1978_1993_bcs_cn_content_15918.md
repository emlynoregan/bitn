Burra Burra DC.

Notice is given that Sean Bradshaw & Pamela Jane Brett of 3 Mt Pleasant Road, Burra, have applied to change the land use of Pt Lot 310 Mt Pleasant Road from Bed & Breakfast to Bed & Breakfast and BYO Licensed Restaurant.  Representations are to be lodged by Wednesday 24 February.
