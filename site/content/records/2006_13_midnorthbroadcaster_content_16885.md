The Mt Bryan-Hallett section of the Barrier Highway will be safer after the sealing of dangerous shoulders on the road later this month.  The aim is to provide traffic lanes of 3.3 m width with a one metre sealed shoulder.  Weather permitting it is hoped to have the work done by late July.
