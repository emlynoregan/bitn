Kate Bullen [Photo] studied art and worked in Adelaide as a radiographer.  When she later moved to the Mid North, she began painting landscapes and joined the Burra Art Gallery, where she has exhibited pastels and oils.  She exhibits with the Clare Valley Artists three times a year and in Burra’s ‘Art of the Flower’ and in ‘Artistic Licence’ in Adelaide.
