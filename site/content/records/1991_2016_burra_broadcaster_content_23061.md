‘Josie and the Pussycats’ (Josie Hill, Angie Webster, Julie Clarke and Pauline Thomas) will be hitting the road this Saturday, 19 August, at the start of the Konica 2K Sa Variety Club Bash from Hutt St in Adelaide.  They will cover some 3,000 km in eight days and end at Murray Bridge.  Some 320 people and 90 vehicles will participate.  So far ‘Effie’ and her crew have raised over $16,000 to aid the sick, disabled and disadvantaged children of SA.
