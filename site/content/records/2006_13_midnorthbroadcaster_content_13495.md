Patrick Le Vagueresse writes to review the Community School’s presentation ‘Burra’s Got Talent’, held on 26 October.  The items listed were:

The Real Crew dancers	[Friday to Sunday]

The All Girl Choir

Kaitlin Johnson	Guitar solo: [Boulevard of Broken Dreams]

Dakota Hammill, Shante Nugent & Emily Badman	Trio: Cooler Than Me

The Junior Band	Guitars, drums, pianist & two vocalists: Red Red Wine & Planets

Kieran Le Vagueresse	Piano: Harp in the Trees

Interval

Shona Mayne & Morgan Mudge	Duet: The Captain

Charlie McInnis & Rom Sumner	Drum & Guitar: 12 Bar Blues

Abbey Snook	Vocal: Worst Pies in London from ‘Sweeney Todd’.

The Real Crew Dancers	[We Run the Night]

Lillian Park & Harrison Quinn	Duet: [Price Tag]

The Senior Band	[Through Glass and Enter Sandman]

Thanks to Mrs Pudney, Miss O’Brien, the students behind the scenes and Kerry Davey, the music teacher.
