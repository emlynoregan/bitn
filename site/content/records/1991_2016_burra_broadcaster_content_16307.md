Dianne Day

Active member of Burra Tennis Club & a Life Member, holding many positions over the years

Chairperson of Women’s Golf in SA Mid North District

Last President of Burra Ladies’ Golf Club before it merged with the Men’s Club

Active member of the Burra Women’s Bowling Club

President of the North Eastern Women’s Bowling Association

Life Member of BBH Netball Club

Life Member of Burra Tennis Club

Foundation Member of Burra Community Sports Club and Treasurer for the last 18 year

Treasurer of the BBH Canteen
