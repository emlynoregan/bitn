Membership was 64 and average attendance was 18.  Valmai Williams had represented SA Rural Youth at the 1963 Perth Royal Show and David Kellock represented the club on the 1964 Merino Stud Tour.  Robert Neill has been elected to represent the club on the 1964 bus trip to Gippsland.  New office bearers: President, David Kellock; Vice-President, Keith Neill; Secretary, Lynette Nicholls; Assistant Secretary, Sandra Field and Treasurer, Robert Neill.
