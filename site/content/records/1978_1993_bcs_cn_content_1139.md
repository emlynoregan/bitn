‘The Burra Record’ began as the Burra News in July 1876 and became the ‘Record’ from July 1878.  It ceased operations in 1978.

[Actually the paper began as ‘The Northern Mail’ 30 June 1876 until 29 December 1876.

It was ‘The Burra News and Northern Mail from 5 January 1877 to 28 June 1878.

From 5 July 1878 its masthead was ‘The Record – A paper for Burra and the North East’ until the first issue as ‘The Burra Record’ on 4 October 1878.  It ceased publication with the issue of 8 March 1977.]
