Nathaniel O’Brien (14) of Burra continues his successes in Australian Country Music.  He has taken out the prestigious title of 2010-11 Junior SA Champion of Champions to add to his recent Junior Victorian Champion of Champions title.  He has secured a music scholarship at the Camerata Junior Music Academy in Tamworth in July 2011, which provides for a week of tutoring and mentoring from some of the industry’s most successful artists.
