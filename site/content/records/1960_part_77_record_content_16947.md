Mt Bryan Hogget Competition was won by Jack Connors.  Finals of the North Eastern Hogget Competition were held at Max Rayner’s ‘The Bluff’.  The winner was Mr S. Wood of Yarcowie.  In the single hogget section the Miller Bros. of Hallett came first.
