SA Co-operative Bulk Handling Ltd will hold a meeting of growers from Burra, Mt Bryan, Hallett and surrounding areas at the Mt Bryan Hall on 9 April at 7.30 p.m. to discuss bulk handling requirements.  An inspection of sites will take place at Hallett at 1.30 p.m., Mt Bryan at 4.30 p.m. and at Burra on Tuesday morning.  The first requirement is to convince directors that facilities are needed before settling down to argue where and how many.
