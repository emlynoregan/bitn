Basketball.  The State Under 16 Team visited Burra to play a combined Burra-Booborowie team.  The party with officials etc. numbered 34.  They visited the Mine area and had a barbecue lunch at the Small Bore Club.

In the game the State Team 39 defeated Burra-Booborowie 13.
