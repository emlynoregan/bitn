Burra Gold Associates had their closing day on Wednesday.  Trophies were presented by President Mrs D. Field:

‘A’ Grade Championship	Mrs J. Minchin

‘B’ Grade Championship	Mrs F. Reed

36 Hole Handicap	Mrs R. Mueller

24 Hole Handicap	Mrs D. Gebhardt

Grace Barker Salver	Miss E. Tiver

Seasonal Competition	Mrs R. Mueller

18 Hole Eclectic	Miss E. Tiver

9 Hole Eclectic	Mrs J. Minchin

Seasonal Bogey	Mrs J. Minchin
