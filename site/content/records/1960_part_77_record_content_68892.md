Advt.  Elders-GM Wether Lamb Sale 16 November: 10,000 sheep and lambs.

Advt.  Elders-GM will auction ‘Grassville’ 30 miles east of Burra comprising 33,790 acres of perpetual lease, at the Burra RSL Hall 14 December.  To be offered as a whole or if not sold in four lots.

Lot 1.  ‘Homestead’ 14,575 acres with six-roomed timber and iron homestead etc.

Lot 2.  ‘Sixty’ of 12,480 acres.

Lot 3.  ‘Tin Hut’ of 6.693 acres.

Lot 4.  ‘Red Hut’ of 43 acres freehold.

To be sold on instructions from Devere Pty Ltd.
