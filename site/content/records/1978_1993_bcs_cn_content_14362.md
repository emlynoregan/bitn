SA Little Athletics Individual Championships were held last weekend.

Petrea Piggott set a new Australian U15 record of 25.7 secs for the 200 m.

She also set two state records in:

100 m at 12.9 secs

400 m at 58.8 secs

She won silver in the triple jump and the 90 m hurdles.

Cheryl Day won bronze in the U13 200 m.

Andrew Kleinig won bronze in the U15 javelin.
