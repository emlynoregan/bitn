Junior Motor Cross

In the school holidays Daren Taylor, Michael McBride and Matthew McBride attended the Australian National Titles in Alice Springs.  Michael & Matthew rode in the under 8 Pee Wee Grade and came 12th and 13th respectively out of 26.  Daren rode in the 17 and over grade, since his 17th birthday was on the day the events started.  He came 4th on his 80cc and on his 125cc finished 17th out of 38.
