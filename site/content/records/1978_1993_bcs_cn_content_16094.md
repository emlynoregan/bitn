Shell Age Group Swimming Championship Area Finals were held at Riverton last Saturday.

Karen Jennings (12 year age group) won the breaststroke and came second in the freestyle, backstroke and butterfly.

Bronwyn Kurtzer (13 year age group) won her breaststroke.
