Brian Jefferies RDA, BAgSc, formerly of Mt Bryan, who also holds a wool classing diploma, has accepted a four year assignment with the Argentinean Government to set up a sheep breeding program based on Australian lines.  Brian was with the SA Agricultural Department for seven years and has recently resigned from his position as Chief Sheep & Wool Adviser for Tasmania, where he had served for seven years.  Brian demonstrated wool classing and Australian shearing methods on a trip to Uruguay in September 1956 for the UN Food & Agricultural Organisation.
