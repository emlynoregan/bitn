Burra National Trust will hold its AGM in the Burra Courthouse on Thursday 19 August.  Guest speakers will be Peter Hosking, President of the Theatre Organ Society of Australia (SA Division) and Ross Large.  They will speak on the restoration of the Wurlitzer Pipe Organ at the Capri Theatre in Goodwood.
