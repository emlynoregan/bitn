Burra Burra DC, 5 October

Mrs L.E. Fuller asked the Council to close and sell Elphick Street to her.  It was resolved to sell and the intention to sell is to be advertised.

The Burra Hotel was permitted to install temporary buildings as outlined for a maximum period of six months.
