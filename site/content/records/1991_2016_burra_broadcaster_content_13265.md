Goyder Council

With the council amalgamation there are new faces in the Council Office:

Carlene Heinrich is based at Robertstown, but is in Burra Monday and Friday.

Tammy Leibhardt is now based in Burra, but is in Hallett on Friday.

Stephanie Opie (previously Robertstown) is now in Burra as executive assistant.

Natasha Wilkins is in Eudunda Monday, Wednesday and Friday and in Burra on Tuesday & Thursday as Plant, Machinery and Stores Officer.
