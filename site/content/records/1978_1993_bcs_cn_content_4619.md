Bennetts Farmers.  Steve Watchman has been promoted and transferred to Mt Gambier as Merchandise Manager after four years in Burra.  The family will leave on 11 September.  Mr Kelly Evans will be the new stockman.  He comes from the Melrose branch with his wife Rosemary and twin sons.
