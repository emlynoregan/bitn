Regional Council of Goyder’s Goyder Heritage Fund has entered its 9th year of existence with $20,000 to expend on projects in the 2010-11 financial year.  Applications are invited for aid to essential conservation projects.  Stonework, roofing and timber conservation work are examples of eligible projects.  Funding needs to be applied for before the commencement of the work.
