National Trust Bus Tour will visit Clare, Blyth and Stringybark Reserve on Saturday 10 May.  In Clare the tour will visit the restored Wolta Wolta, burnt out in the Ash Wednesday fires and the National Trust Museum.  In Blyth they will visit Medika Gallery.
