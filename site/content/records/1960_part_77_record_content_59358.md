Burra Festival of Carols at the Swimming Pool on Sunday drew a smaller crowd this year, but was still a successful and pleasant event.  Rev. Milton Edwards was compere and Mrs Jack Oates played the organ.  A collection of $45 will aid the Save the Children Fund.
