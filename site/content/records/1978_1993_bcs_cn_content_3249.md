BCS at the Coca Cola Games in Adelaide

Outstanding was Danny Greet in the 1,500 metres, beating his best performance by over 15 seconds to win gold.  This has earned him a place in the SA team in Sydney in December.

Kathryn Blight gained the bronze medal in the U17 girls’ 800 m – breaking the school record by 6 seconds.

Libby Thamm gained bronze in the 400 m hurdles and also gained silver as a member of the U19 girls’ 4 x 100 m relay.  [Photo of the above.]

Miss Janet Durling, Burra’s entrant in the Miss Industry Quest was one of the six finalists.
