Burra Cubs & Scouts went to Renmark for a weekend camp on 14 November.  Despite 40°C temperatures, they enjoyed a visit to Bredl’s Reptile Farm and a two-hour cruise on a houseboat and later a barbecue with Renmark Cubs and Scouts.  On the way home they made a short stop at Monash Playground.
