BCS.  Mark & Helena Holman have given notice they will resign from teaching at the end of the term.  Mark joined the staff at BCS in 1997 and has taught mainly Years 4 & 5.  Helena came at the start of this year and took the Year 7 class when Deb. Marks was appointed principal at Spalding Primary School.
