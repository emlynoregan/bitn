Burra Community School

Each year the University of NSW runs national competitions in Science, Maths and English.  Certificated are awarded for those who perform particularly well against their peers.  Those who do particularly well are awarded Achievement Certificates and those in the top 10% of the State earn Distinctions.  Credits are awarded to other high achievers.

In Mathematics a Special Achievement Award has gone to Stewart Macdonald of Yr 8.

Joanna Guthrie and Stewart Macdonald, both of Yr 8, earned Distinctions.
