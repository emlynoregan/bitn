BCS.  Year 8 students are off to Barmera for their annual Aquatics Camp.

Year 12 Physical Education students had an Aquatics Camp at Murray Bridge last week.

Anne Breeding has been the temporary Boarding House supervisor and a selection committee to choose the permanent supervisor has been appointed.
