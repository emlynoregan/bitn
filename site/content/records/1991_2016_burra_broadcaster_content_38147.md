Tom Broad, Patron of the Burra Show Society, turned 80 on 2 December.  He has travelled all over Australia for the last 73 years showing poultry and has accumulated more than 10,000 ribbons.  He has been a show judge in Sydney, Adelaide, Melbourne, Darwin and Perth.  He was born in Burra and married Elva Todd.  He worked as a mechanic before joining the railways after the war.
