Burra Burra DC.  Elections.  Nominations are called by 23 March for the periodical election to be held on 2 May.  One Councillor is required for each of the wards: Booborowie, Eastern, Farrell Flat, Hanson, Kooringa, Leighton and Mt Bryan and four are required for the Burra Town Ward.
