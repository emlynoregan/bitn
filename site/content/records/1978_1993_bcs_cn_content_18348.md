The U16 and U18 National Athletics Titles were contested in Brisbane on 11 & 12 March.

Cheryl Day came 7th in the U16 400 m.

Petrea Piggott came 7th in the 400 m hurdles.

Adam Day was 6th in the U18 400 m hurdles and was in the 4 x 400 m relay team that came in 4th.

On 14 & 15 March Adam competed in the U18 decathlon where he finished 5th.
