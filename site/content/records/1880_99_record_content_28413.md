Kooringa Court, 17 April.

John Hynes fined 5/- or 3 days for drunkenness. (He went to jail.)

Hoare fined 5/- for a stray goat

W.A. Rabbich fined 2/6 for a stray goat.

J. Tobin fined 5/- for a stray cow.
