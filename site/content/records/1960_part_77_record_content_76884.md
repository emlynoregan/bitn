Burra Bowling Season was opened last Sunday by Arthur Kench, President of the Mid North Bowls Association.  He was introduced by Burra President Brian Riggs.  Mr Kench favours changing the arrangements so that there would be eight sides in Division 1; one from each club now playing and a second from each of the two larger clubs, Clare and Burra.  Then an 8 side 2nd Division and all others in 3rd Division, with relegation of the bottom sides in divisions and the promotion of top sides.
