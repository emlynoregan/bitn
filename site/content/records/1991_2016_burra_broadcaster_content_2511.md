Review of 1993

April

Film and TV star Ernie Dingo visits Burra.

Burra Art Group has a master class with noted artist Robert Hannaford.

Burra Picnic Races at Thistlebeds prove a big success.

Pattama Iomsuwan, exchange teacher at BCS.

Collinsville was put up for sale by tender.

Burra Scout Paul Jacka received the Queen’s Scout Award.

Large haystack fire at Hanson caused $115,000 damage.
