Aged Care – A Christian Perspective – was the title for a discussion held at St Mary’s Church Hall, Burra on 28 April, sponsored by the Burra Branch of the Mothers’ Union.  There were three speakers: Trevor Briggs, CEO of Willochra Home at Crystal Brook; Maureen Hobbs, supervisor of Care at Willochra and Noreen Hallion of the Burra Hospital.
