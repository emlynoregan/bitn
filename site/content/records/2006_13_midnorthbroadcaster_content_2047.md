Accident.  On Thursday 6 November the Burra MFS, Police, CFS, Ambulance and SES attended an accident on Copperhouse Street, when a truck collided with a power pole, leaving wires hanging across the road.  Only a few houses suffered loss of power until the pole was replaced.
