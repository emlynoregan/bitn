34169→Burra CWA.  The Annual International Day was held at the Town Hall.  The President Miss Rogers presided and Mrs I.J. Warnes, the first President, was welcomed.  This year’s country was South Africa and the speaker was Mr J. Rundle from Jamestown.  The meeting also celebrated the 21st birthday of the CWA in SA, with Burra being the first branch, followed by Spalding and then the Metropolitan Branch.
