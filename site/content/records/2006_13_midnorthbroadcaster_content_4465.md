Bohdan Wall, formerly of Burra, has been selected in the SA Outbacks squad for the 2008 Australian Country Cricket Championships at Harrup Park in Mackay, Queensland from 5-17 January 2008.  He is currently captain-coach of Tanunda Cricket Club.  He has been in three previous SA Outback teams competing in titles at Mildura, Mandurah and Lismore.  This time he will be Captain.
