Sporting Successes

BBH Colts, Bohdan Wall & Michael McBride took the two NE Football Association’s Colts’ medals: Boh for the Junior Best & Fairest and Michael for Senior Best & Fairest.

BBH B Grade was the club’s only finals representative and dropped out after a close match with Mintaro-Manoora in the preliminary final.
