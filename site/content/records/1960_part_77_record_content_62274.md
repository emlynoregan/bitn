Burra Burra DC, 3 July

Re-elected Councillors were J.W. Burge, B.A. Riggs, R.J. White & C.L. Phillips.

Results of the election in Kooringa Ward:

W. Brown	38

N.C. Finch	23

Cr R.J. Jeffery was elected Chairman 1872-73.

It was resolved to extend the classification of children at Burra Pictures to the end of secondary school.

Notices are to be erected forbidding driving on Aberdeen Oval.

Committee members and board representatives were elected.

Burra Motor Co. was granted permission to install a new petrol pump.
