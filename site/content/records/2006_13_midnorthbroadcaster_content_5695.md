Clare Valley Regional Tourism Awards were presented last Thursday night in the Burra Town Hall.

The Burra History Group President, Meredith Satchell received the Regional Council of Goyder’s Courtesy Award.  [Photo page 2.]

John Arthur of the Burra Visitor Information Centre received the Regional Council of Goyder’s People’s Choice Award.  [Photo.]
