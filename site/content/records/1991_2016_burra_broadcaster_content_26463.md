Merilyn Kuchel, SA Co-ordinator of the Open Gardens Scheme, writes thanking those who participated.  In the local area the open gardens were Ashrose at Hallett, Old North Bungaree at Booborowie and Princess Royal at Burra.  Visitors numbered 550, 580 and 800 respectively.  Those who catered for the visitors are also thanked.

Helen Szuty, has taken delivery of the first issue of The Visitors Monthly, a guide for the visitor to the Mid North, Barossa Valley and Yorke Peninsula.  [Photo.]
