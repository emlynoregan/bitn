Redruth Ladies Guild held its AGM 13 March.  Secretary, Mrs R.M. Jennison reported a membership of 30 with an average attendance of 21 at 11 meetings for the year.  The great success for the year was the fete on 30 November with takings a record $585.  $455 was donated to various causes throughout the year.
