Mr C.G. (Con.) Hartnett visited Burra last week.  He was born there 65 years ago to Jerry & Kate Hartnett.  The tailoring business of Mr Jeremiah Hartnett was conducted in the building now housing the museum.  Mr Stan Woollacott showed Mr Hartnett over the old family home.  Mr Hartnett recently retired as Deputy Director of the Department of Decentralisation and Development in NSW.
