Burra Mine

Heavy equipment has begun to arrive in the past week.  The secondary crusher of 13 tons arrived, with the primary crusher of 32 tons due on 17 December.  The crusher plant is beginning to take shape.  The Bon Accord 150,000 gallon tank has been tested and filled.  The administration buildings will be ready for occupation 14 December.  It is hoped to begin ore quarrying next week.
