Advt. Dalgety Bennetts Farmers Ltd is conducting an end of financial year Field & Information Day at Koomooloo Station 70 km east of Burra on 23 June.  There will be 20 various suppliers in attendance with displays and demonstrations of rural merchandise.  It will be opened at 11 a.m. by Michael McBride.
