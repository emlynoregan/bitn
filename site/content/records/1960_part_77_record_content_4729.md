Burra CWA held its 34th birthday celebrations and its International Day on 15 November in the Burra town Hall with President Mrs Jennison presiding.  It was resolved that the annual donation to the Burra Hospital would only be £75 in view of the branch’s building program.  Mrs L.H. Thomas and Mrs Satchell played and sang appropriate items before Mrs Thorpe of Farrell Flat gave a talk on Scotland.
