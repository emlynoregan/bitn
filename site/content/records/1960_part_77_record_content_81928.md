Burra Branch of the ALP was re-formed last night at a meeting at the Royal Exchange Hotel.  About 40 people attended and arrangements are made for the Premier to visit Burra on 15 February.  Elected: President, Ben Kotz; Secretary, Geoff Hirschausen and Treasurer, Dick Cuttle.
