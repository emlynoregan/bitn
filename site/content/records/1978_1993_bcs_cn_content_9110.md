Burra Branch of the Arts Council held its AGM at the Kooringa Hotel 28 February.  About 50 attended.  A surprise entertainer was Paul O’Bryan’s Country Rock Band.  Elected: President, Kath Millard; Vice-President, Ken Field; Secretary, Mary Morris and Treasurer, Robert Reed.
