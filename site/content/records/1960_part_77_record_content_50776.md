North-Eastern Combined School Sports took place at Spalding Oval Friday 10 April.  The event was opened by Lady Hawker.  School teams competing were: Spalding Hut, Spalding Hill, St Joseph’s, Booborowie Green, Booborowie Red, Leighton, Willalo & Hilltown.  [Results are printed.]
