The Regional Council of Goyder’s Bushfire Ready meetings are continuing to draw interest from community members.  About 25 attended the meeting at Mt Bryan and 35 the one at Booborowie.  About 35 also attended the Burra meeting on Monday night.  Tuesday saw a meeting at Farrell Flat, with further gatherings scheduled for Robertstown and Eudunda.
