13613→Several community members joined musical talents last Friday for a sing-along at the Burra weekender home of entertainer Billy Dee.  Billy Dee is a regular performer at the Brecknock Hotel in Adelaide and has performed in Burra for several years.  He also heads the country/folk band ‘Rare Old Times’.  At the gathering were local country music singers Danny & Karen O’Brien, members of the Burra Sing Australia Choir and Pat Kakoschke, Maureen Bevan and Gloria Woodley.
