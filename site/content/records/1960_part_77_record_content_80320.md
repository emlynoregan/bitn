Football.  North-Eastern League Grand Finals

‘A’ Grade	Burra 19.13 defeated North Clare 14.9

‘B’ Grade	Burra 11.10 defeated North Clare 7.13

Senior Colts	Blyth 7.4 defeated Min-Man 6.7

Junior Colts	Min-Man 15.14 defeated North Clare 7.7

Presentations:

Mr P. McInerney who won the Mail Medal in 1933 presented this year’s Mail Medal to Don Sommerville of North Clare.

Patrick Hill of Min-Man was presented with the Senior Colts’ ‘Bull Dog’ Medal.

‘B’ Grade leading goal kicker award went to Clive Clark of North Clare.

‘B’ Grade Best and Fairest, the Pink Medal, went to Colin Hall of Booborowie.

Barry Pontt of South Clare won the award for ‘A’ Grade leading goal kicker.

The Burra Record Trophy for the runner-up in the Mail Medal count went to Ian Roberts of Blyth.

Burra Pistol Club.  Graham Williams won the Karmia Trophy for marksman.  Norma Barker was most improved lady and Phillip Edwards was most improved gentleman.  Best scorers were A. Pearce, L. Whitrod, L. Broad and K. Burley.
