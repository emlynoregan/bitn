Obituary.  Mr John Robert Barker was born 6 November 1895 at Baldina Station nine miles east of Burra.  His grandfather Alfred Barker has taken up this property and it had been carried on by his father, William Pitt Barker.  When W.P. Barker died in 1914, Mr Barker assumed responsibility and ran Baldina and Grassville stations until his sudden death at Baldina on Friday 31 October.  He was associated with many town and district activities.  He served as a Councillor on the Mt Bryan DC prior to its amalgamation with the Burra DC, on which he then served for many years. As Councillor for Mongolata Ward.  He was for 52 year a trustee of St Mary’s Church, a position his father had held before him.  He was keenly interested in sporting bodies and played golf and tennis and was a member of the Burra Sports Association for many years.  He continued the family’s long standing active support of the Burra Hospital.
