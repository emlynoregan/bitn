The Woodville Choral Group, under the baton of Rosemary Nairn (nee Reed of ‘Kookaburra’) will perform at the Burra Uniting Church at 2 p.m. on 28 May, with a selection of sacred and popular items.  It was established in 1981 with 29 members and now numbers 70 from all over Adelaide.
