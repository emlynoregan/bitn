Burra Pony Club riders were successful at the Northern Zones Elimination at Pt Augusta last month and so qualify for the State Finals.  Samantha Angrave achieved first place in the Junior Rider of the Year, first in the A Grade Games and second in the D Grade Showjumping.  Carissa Svensdotter came first in the team on the flat and second in the D Grade Showjumping.  [Other results are printed.]
