Burra Primary School held its ‘Passing Out’ Parade.  The Mayor, Headmaster of Burra High School Mr Farrow, Primary School Committee Chairman Mr R. Moreland, President of the Welfare Club Mrs E. Carpenter, President of the High School Council Mr D. H. Field and Revs G.D. Sibly and M. Lind were guests along with a gathering of parents and friends.  Prizes were distributed and the Grade VII students were formally presented to the Headmaster of the High School.
