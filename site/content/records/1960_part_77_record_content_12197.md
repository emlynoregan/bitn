Obituary.  Albert George Dixon of the SAR (Two Wells) died on duty aged 62.  He was the third son of the late Mr & Mrs William Dixon of Hanson and brother to Gladys Mrs Fraser (Prospect), Jack (Hanson), Oliver (Melbourne) and Tom (deceased in WWI).  Mr Dixon joined the SAR at Burra in 1916.  He is survived by a wife, Lorna and children: Bob (Adelaide), Dorothy (Tasmania), Ruth (NSW) and Claire (Adelaide).

[Born 19 March 1900, Hanson: died 1 July 1962, Two Wells.]
