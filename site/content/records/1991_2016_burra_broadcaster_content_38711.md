Rev. Fr Laurie Quinn was guest of honour at the 50th anniversary of his ordination at St Joseph’s Catholic Church in Burra last month.  He was ordained there on 29 June 1963.  Bishop Greg O’Kelly led the service and Father Quinn received an Apostolic Blessing from Pope Francis.  [2 photos.]
