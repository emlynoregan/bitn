BCS.  New Staff:

Vince Fleming comes from Karcultaby Area School to teach Agriculture.

Mark Bullen comes from Ceduna Area School and Bill O’Malley from Clare High School.  Both will teach in secondary Maths/Science.

Miss Michelle Kimber will take over some of the work of Peter Ryan, who becomes Acting Deputy Principal.

R.J. Wenzel is Acting Principal and Mike Day is on long service leave.

Miss Jo Brooks returns as a Support Staff member.

Mrs Debbie Brown replaces Karen White who is on leave.
