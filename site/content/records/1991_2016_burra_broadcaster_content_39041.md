SA Merino Field Days.  35 Merino studs displayed at this year’s Elders SA Stud Merino Expo.  A good crowd attended.

Elders Ram of the Year was awarded to Nyowee, Balaklava.

Elders Champion Merino Pair was won by Mulloorie, Brinkworth.

Ramsey Bros All Purpose Pair of Ram Lambs award went to Roemahkita of Cummins.  [3 photos.]
