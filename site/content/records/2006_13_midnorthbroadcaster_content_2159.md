Fire.  Burra CFS attended a fire at Hallett on Wednesday 27 November, caused by lightning.  It burnt about 2 ha.  Lightning also started a fire at Booborowie turn-off on the same day, where less than 1 ha was consumed.

On 30 November broken power lines caused a grassfire on the Booborowie Road.
