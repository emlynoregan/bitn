Spalding-Burra Catholic Parish is having a Mission this week, conducted by Passionist Fathers Austin Kenny from Glen Osmond and Ambrose Glynn from Geelong, Victoria.  On Sunday there will be a Folk Mass at Booborowie and then a picnic and sports.
