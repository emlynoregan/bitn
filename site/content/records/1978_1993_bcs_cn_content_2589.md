19 June 1980

Res Warnes of ‘The Gap’ Burra has been awarded an OBE in the recently published Imperial Honours List for services to the Royal Agricultural and Horticultural Society.

Mr Ian Auhl has been awarded the Medal of the Order of Australia (OAM).  Mr Auhl, a former Burra Primary School Headmaster remains vitally interested in Burra’s Welfare and is still writing its history.

Burra Meals on Wheels AGM will be held in the Burra Hospital Board Room on 7 July at 8 p.m.

Burra Community Library has been granted $2,852 to complete the rebinding of its volumes of the SA Register.  The grant comes from the Local Government Assistance Fund.

Tenders are called for the removal of the prefabricated school building at Mt Bryan.

BCS Film Club, BCS 16 July and Booborowie Institute 17 July,

David Hartman & Donald Sinden in The Island at the Top of the World (1974)

Burra National Trust has received a bonus grant of $6,000 for the 1979-80 funding year and a guarantee of $7,000 for the 1980-81 year, which has enabled work on restoration of the Redruth Gaol to be continued.
