Burra Women’s Bowling Club hosted the Division 3 Bowls Championship last week.  [Photo.]

Pam Brooks (Hallett) won the singles.

A Crystal Brook team won the pairs.

A Port Pirie team won the threesome event.

The Booborowie team of J. McMahon, S. Willis, M. Baldock & M. Beinke won the fours.
