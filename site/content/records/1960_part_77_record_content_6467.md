Notice.  Burra Electric Supply Co. (In Voluntary Liquidation)

A meeting of shareholders will be held at the office of the Liquidator, Douglas J. Wilson 24-26 Eagle Chambers 7 Pirie St Adelaide 2 June 1961 when an account will be laid before them of how the winding up has been conducted and the property of the Company disposed of.
