Obituary.  Mr Rex Warnes, formerly of Koomooloo Station and ‘The Gap’, has died after a long illness.  He was President of the Burra Show Society 1954-56 and 1959-64 and was later its patron for some time.

[Rex Clement Warnes, born 31 March 1908 at Leighton.]
