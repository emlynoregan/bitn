Lost Walker.  Adelaide Woman Karen Emeny and her son Angus Whiley became lost in Tooralie Gorge after leaving their camp at c. 10.30 a.m. for a bushwalk.  A huge SES search was instigated when they failed to return.  The search started at 6.30 p.m. on Saturday and SES members were joined by Police, Hallett locals and two helicopters on Sunday and the pair were found late Sunday morning, having sheltered overnight in a toilet.
