Burra Burra DC

It is expected that changes to the rate payments will be in force from 1 July.  Payment of rates will become due sixty days after the serving of the notice.  Provisions allow for payment by quarterly instalments and application for this must be made within thirty days.

Council is investigating the cost of new railings at the Town Hall.

Election nominations received:

Mongolata Ward	J.P. Oates (replacing B.A. Riggs)

Leighton Ward	J.W. McMahon (replacing R.J. White)

Farrell Flat Ward	J.W. Burge (re-elected unopposed)

Kooringa Ward	W. Brown (re-elected unopposed)

Burra Town Ward	No nominations

A supplementary election is called for Burra Town Ward with nominations closing on 28 May.
