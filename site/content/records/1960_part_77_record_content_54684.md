The Director of Meals on Wheels Lt Colonel (Rtd) H.G. Frith MBE will visit Burra Methodist Women’s Fellowship on 17 February to explain the running of the organisation and outline the steps to be taken in the formation of a Meal on Wheels Service in towns of limited patient potential.
