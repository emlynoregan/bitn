Burra Community Swimming Pool is being prepared to open for business on 28 November, weather permitting.  Opening times will be as for 1997, except that the Ice breakers session will now be 7-7.30 a.m., Monday, Wednesday and Friday, for lap swimming.

When daily forecast temperature for Pt Pirie is 28ºC or more:

to 5.30 p.m. on school days and 2.00 to 5.30 p.m. weekends and holidays.

And if the forecast for Pt Pirie is 32ºC or more then also 6.30 to 9.00 p.m.
