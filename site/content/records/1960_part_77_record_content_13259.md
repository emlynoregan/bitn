Sagasco’s Cooking Demonstration at the Burra RSL Hall on Friday drew a large crowd.  There was also a fine display of gas stoves, coppers and fridges etc.

Burra Basketball & Football Clubs held a concert at the Burra Town Hall on 21September and drew a capacity crowd.  Door takings were a little over £75.

Adelaide Wool Sales.  Photo of Bill & Eric Ashby of Hallett with Goldsbrough’s Wool Supervisor, Mr Doug Richmond.
