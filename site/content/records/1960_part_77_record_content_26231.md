Aberdeen Football Club held a Presentation Night in the Oddfellows’ Hall with a sumptuous spread by perhaps the best Football Auxiliary in Australia.

Colts Trophies:

Fairest & Most Brilliant	G. Spackman

Best Team Man		T. Baker

Most Improved		T. Wall

Most Consistent		P. Byles

Most Effective		B. Kakoschke

Aberdeen Football Club Trophies 1965

‘A’ Grade	‘B’ Grade

Fairest & Most Brilliant	K. Lane	L. Robinson

Most Determined	M. Jones	L. Robinson

Most Consistent	B. Seaford	J. Taylor

Best Team Man	K. Lane	D. McGowan

Most Effective	G. Wooton	S. Beinke

Best First Year Player	J. Hirschausen

Most Improved Junior	P. Fiebig

Awards also to J. Robinson – boundary umpire – at all ‘A’ Grade games and to Mrs Scroop for laundering the uniforms each week.
