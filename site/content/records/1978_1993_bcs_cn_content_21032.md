Cheryl Day thanks all who supported her athletics trip to the USA & Canada.  In the Santa Monica meeting she won the 100 m and the 200 m and at Victoria on Vancouver Island she won the 400 m and came 3rd in the 100 m.

The main event was the British Columbis Track & Field Championships.  She took part in the 100 m, 200 m, 400 m and the 4 x 400 m relay.  In the 100 m and 200 m she finished 6th and in the 400 m was placed 3rd.  In the 4 x 400 m relay the team picked up the bronze medal.  [Photo.]
