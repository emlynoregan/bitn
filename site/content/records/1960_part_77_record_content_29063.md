North Eastern Table Tennis Association held its AGM in the RSL Room on 29 March.  Willalo had won the 1965 ‘A’ Grade shield with Hanson as runners-up.  In the ‘B’ Grade Spalding Blue won with Burra Blue as runners-up.

Elected: Patron, W. Warner; President, R. Shipard; Secretaries, A. Cameron & A. Webster.

This year there will be eight teams: Spalding White, Spalding Blue, Burra White, Burra Blue, Hanson, Booborowie, Mt Bryan and Willalo.  Matches begin 26 April with the Grand Final on August 30.
