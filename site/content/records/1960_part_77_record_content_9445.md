Burra Primary School presentation of prizes and certificates was done at the break-up before Christmas.  Headmaster Mr K. Hornell welcomed the Chairman and Secretary of the School Committee (Messrs M.G. Stockman & D.H. Field) and the President and Secretary of the Welfare Club (Mesdames D.H. Field & D. Bannister) and the High School Principal Mr R. Mueller.  The Dux of the School was Markku Sipari and the top girl was Marilyn Kotz.  Presentations were made to departing teachers:

Miss Coventry goes to Pt Elliott

Mrs Sladden goes to Adelaide

Mr Sullivan is going to Brisbane University to complete his degree.

Progress Certificates were awarded to:

Dawn Bogisch	Janice Pearce	Ann Brennon

Marilyn Kotz	Sandra Williams	Lorna White

Dianne Mitchell	Joan Kellock	Kathryn White

Richard Riggs	Bryan Napper	Russell Nicholls

Walter Fridd	Dennis Jenner	Kenneth Lloyd

Allan Garrett	Peter Thomas	Darrell Field

David Sladden	Mark Sipari	Malcolm Kakoschke

Trevor Williams
