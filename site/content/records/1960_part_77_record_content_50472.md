Burra Show Society AGM

Elected were: Patron, Mr Rex Warnes; President, Mr R. Mason; Vice-Presidents, Mr G. Kellock & Mr M.G. Wardle.  A secretary is required following the resignation of Miss Judy Beinke after two years.  Retiring President Mr R.H. Stockman reported on a successful 1969 show held on 11 October in perfect weather.  Gate takings were $681-16, which was up $200 on 1968.  The 2,986 entries were a record.  The Mid North Sheep Shearing Competition was a very successful innovation.  There were 432 Horses-in-Action entries.  Wool display was up to its usual high standard and though sheep numbers were down, the standards were well maintained.  Industrial displays were numerous.  The pavilion displays contained good quality in all sections and flowers and floral art entries had increased.  The school displays were pleasing.  Sideshows were down in number and less varies.  Financially the success of the show means the Society is without debt for the first time in years and there is a credit balance of $73-08.
