New Burra Town Clerk Appointed

Following the resignation of A.R. Graham as Town Clerk, the Town Council has appointed Mr R.K. Baker from a group of five applicants.  Mr Baker is 24 and married.  He is presently a clerk in the Rate Department of the Mitcham City Council.  He will take up his position on 1 July, but will come to Burra a week before to familiarise himself with the office routine.
