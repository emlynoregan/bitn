Anne & Wayne Thomsen have taken over management of the Burra Caravan Park from last Monday.  They have plans to improve facilities once the repainting and renovation of the office and residence is complete.  There will be an on site store and the provision of brochures for the area.  The couple have previous experience in city caravan parks.  [Photo of the proprietors.]
