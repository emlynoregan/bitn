Obituary.  Dorothy Monica Preiss died at Broken Hill Hospital on 21 December 1965.  She was the wife of John Matthew Preiss and the mother of Brian, Kevin, Clair, Shirley (Mrs Rose of New Zealand), and Trevor (deceased).  She was aged 57.  [Born Dorothy Monica Allen c. 1908, Broken Hill, NSW (family records): married John Preiss St Joseph’s, Burra 15 April 1929.]
