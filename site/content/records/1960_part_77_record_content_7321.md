Obituary.  ‘Lel’ Kellaway died suddenly on 5 July.  In the past year he has been the Burra Bowling Club greensman.  He was always very interested in sports and in his youth was a renowned cricketer, excelling in bowling.  He was one of the best ‘draw bowlers’ Burra Club has seen and was a top bowler and skipper for many years.  [Leslie Kellaway born 18 March 1893 Kooringa: died 5 July 1961 Burra.]
