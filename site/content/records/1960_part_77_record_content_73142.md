Obituary.  Charles Rodney Woollacott died last Thursday in Melbourne.  He was born in Burra and spent his youth here before taking an MA degree at University and having a respected and successful career as a teacher and principal.  He spent most of his life in Victoria.  His brother Stan still lives in Burra.  He is survived by a son and daughter in Melbourne.  [Born 19 September 1895 Redruth.]
