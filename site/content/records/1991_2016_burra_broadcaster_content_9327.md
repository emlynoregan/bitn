Burra Burra DC

Council has agreed to continue discussing the development conditions with Kooringa CoHousing Co-op.  They have been asked to reconsider the Co-op’s guidelines in the light of information from the SA Health Commission on the subject of composting toilets.

Council will be offering surplus assets for sale:

The Ballast Quarry Area

O’Connor’s Shed and Road Train Area

The Old Courthouse

Kotz Land Estate

Lot 169, Millerton

Bone & Tonkin, consultants, are being pressed to speed up their side of the changes needed to development of industrial sites.

$22,400 will be spent on disabled access to the Art Gallery.
