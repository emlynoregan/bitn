Guides & Leaders of the Midland Region will be touring the region on 18-20 April to showcase the movement and also to collect funds for Townsend House.  Their specific aim is to fund a FM Sound Amplification System to deliver the voices of teachers at a level that it can be heard by all the children in the class, wherever they may be sitting.
