Pip Edson, President of Burra Regional Tourism & Business Assoc. (BRTBA) thanks all those who contributed to the success of the Antique Fair.  The whole town benefits from the influx of tourists and the promotion of the town and region that flows from such events.  She urges organisations and individuals to support BRTBA by taking out memberships.
