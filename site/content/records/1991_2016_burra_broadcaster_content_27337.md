Goyder Council has sold O’Connor’s Shed [Drew & Crewe’s Bulk Store] at Burra North to A.P. & K.L. McBride, who in turn have transferred ownership of five vacant allotments on Taylor Street and one on Cooper Street to the Council.  McBrides have also transferred to Council four allotments adjacent to the cemetery.  These are earmarked for extensions to the cemetery.
