David the Shepherd Boy Cantata was performed at Redruth Memorial hall on 26 October.  A choir of about 40 voices was conducted by W.H. Gare.  It is reviewed in ½ column.  The event raised over £40 for Church Funds.  [Personal interest: Among the soloists the bass R. Fuss sang the role of Samuel.]
