The 7th Jailhouse Rock event will be held 25-27 February.  It begins with a Rock ’n’ Roll Dance in Burra Town Hall with Paul O’Bryan’s Band.  On Saturday morning Paul and his band will provide music in Market Square for a Rock ’n’ Roll dance demonstration.  The main event in the Gaol will have live music till after midnight with Paul O’Bryan’s Band and Jump ’n’ Jive – a top Adelaide Band.
