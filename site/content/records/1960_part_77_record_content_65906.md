Hallett & Districts Schools Athletics Competition was held on the Hallett Oval last Friday.  Six teams competed in 63 events.  Competing schools were: Hallett, Burra Green, Burra Purple, Burra Gold, Terowie and Mt Bryan.  Hallett won the shield for highest aggregate points, from Mt Bryan.  The shield for highest percentage of all events went to Terowie with Hallett second.  [Results next issue.]
