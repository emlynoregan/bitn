Accident/Obituary.  Retired grazier Mr Ross Rayner aged 90, of Whyte Yarcowie, was killed yesterday when the vehicle he was driving was struck by a train at a level crossing at Whyte Yarcowie at about 2 p.m.  The train was a special chartered by the Australian Railways Historical Society.  [Ross Rayner born 26 February 1878: died 10 June 1968 Whyte Yarcowie.]
