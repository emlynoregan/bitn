The Hallett Masonic Lodge closed on 8 November 1978 and its members offered the Lodge Hall to the Catholic Community as a free gift.  Hallett parishioners accepted the offer, which was approved by Bishop Gallagher of Pt Pirie.  Transfer arrangements are proceeding and once completed alterations to the building will commence.
