Burra’s Main Street Committee has been called on to arrange an information night to aid people seeking clarity on development requirements now that Burra is a proclaimed Heritage Area.  Such a night will be held on 31 August.  John Brak, Burra Council’s Planning Officer and Douglas Alexander, Heritage Advisor, will explain the situation and how the planning process will work.
