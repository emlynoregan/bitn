The Kooringa Masonic Lodge Installation Ball at Booborowie on 25 October was a great success, with a crowd of c. 170 people enjoying the music of En-novy.  A Life Education raffle run at the Ball was well patronised and raised $405.  The new Worshipful Master is Ian Tiver.
