Obituary.  Sister Mary F. (Molly) Stafford died at Torrens Park on 20 March.  She was well known in Burra, having lived with her aunt Miss MacGrath, who was on the staff of the Reformatory.  She attended Burra School, after which she took up nursing.  She was accorded full military honours â€“ the first time for a nurse in SA.
