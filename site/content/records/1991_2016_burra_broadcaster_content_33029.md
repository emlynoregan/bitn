Accident.  A B-double rolled over on the Burra By-pass on Saturday 31 January due to a mechanical fault.  It was loaded with pineapples and sweet potatoes and the total damage/loss is estimated at c. $90,000.

[There is no mention of injuries to the 34-year-old male driver.]
