Lower North Community Health Service is planning an open day to celebrate their first year of service.  There will be the opportunity to visit them at the rear of the Clare Hospital on Thursday 24 September from 3 p.m. to 7 p.m.  Visitors will be able to get their blood pressure checked and receive advice on lifestyle changes.
