Obituary.  Theodor Pryde died on 27 November at Strathalbyn Hospital aged 56.  He was formerly of Goolwa and Booborowie, the son of C. and the late G. Pryde of Booborowie and brother to Lila (Mrs E.J. Affolter) and Myra (Mrs A.J. Cousins).
