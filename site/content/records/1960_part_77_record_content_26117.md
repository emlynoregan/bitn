Basketball

Elaine Broderick, playing in Burra ‘C’ Grade, registered 583 goals for the season.

The ‘C’ Grade team won the Grand Final on Saturday, beating Spalding 42 to 20.

In ‘A’ Grade Burra Gold 41 defeated Burra Blue 37.

In ‘B’ Grade Leighton 31 defeated Booborowie 29.
