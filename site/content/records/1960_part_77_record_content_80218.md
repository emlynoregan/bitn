Burra Pistol Club.  A self-handicap shoot was held 1 September.  The winner was Andrew Pearce.

Winners of the Karmia Trophy contest were: A. Pearce, marksman; M. Hoskins, most improved gentleman and M. Rooke, most improved lady.
