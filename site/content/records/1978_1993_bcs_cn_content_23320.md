Burra Day Care is situated at the Burra Community Activities Centre, Burra North.

It is staffed by Marlene Wooton as co-ordinator and Margaret Neale as part-time assistant.  They are funded by the Burra Burra Hospital Inc.  Monthly attendance is 268.  The Day Care Service hopes to assist clients to:

Maintain general health

Encourage independence

Prevent mental deterioration

Develop and maintain social skills
