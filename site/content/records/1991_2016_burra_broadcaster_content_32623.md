Laura Hadley has been permanently appointed replacing Michelle Schaeffer.  She is a specialist in Middle School Music and Dramatic Arts and also in Primary School Physical Education.  She has been at Solomontown Primary for the last three years and previously at Hawker and Quorn Area Schools.
