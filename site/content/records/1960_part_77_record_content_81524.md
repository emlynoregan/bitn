BCS

Ian Allen reports as Chairman of the Management Committee.

The Committee had a role in:

Controlling community use of school facilities

Liaising with sporting and service clubs on the use of school and community facilities

Informing the public on the resources of the school

Assisting in organising Community Week

Promoting the Community Education concept

Implementing programs to meet identified needs

As a consequence the following had been done:

Community service groups and clubs had been identified

Community use of the School Library has been arranged and the move to a Community Library awaits further replies from affected parties.

A toy library will be ready for the start of 1977.
