16 September 1992

Adelaide Royal Show

BCS had two teams in the Engine Teardown Competition.  In the heats on Friday an all girls team from St Peter’s College were the fastest and they eventually won the title on Saturday in 9.35 minutes.  This was a minute faster than the record set by Jason White and Troy Lloyd two years ago.  Burra’s teams failed to reach the final with a best place of 5th.

BCS had three entries in the Led Steer Competition.

The Poll Shorthorn from Broughton Park, Spalding, was selected for the O’Connor Trophy Team and came 3rd in the John Gory Special Prize.

The Charolais from Kadlunga, Mintaro was also selected for the O’Connor Trophy Team.

The South Devon from Gum Hill, Mt Bryan was selected for the O’Connor Trophy Team in which it was judged Reserve Champion.

4th prize in the heavyweight Class

Highest scoring South Devon Steer

3rd prize overall Presentation on the hoof

2nd prize for the Handler’s Award – Alex Stockman
