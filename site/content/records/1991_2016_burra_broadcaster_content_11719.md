The Burra-Morgan Road will be closed between Burra and Robertstown turn-off for road construction and sealing.  The successful tender for the remaining 45 km is expected to be announced shortly.  The road is expected to be closed from 2 January 1997 from Burra to the Robertstown turn-off.  It is possible the remaining section to ‘The Gums’ will be completed by July 1998, ahead of schedule.
