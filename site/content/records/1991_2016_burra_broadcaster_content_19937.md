Burra Leisure Activity Group will celebrate the International Year of Older Persons by participating in the ‘Life is More than Just Living’ Global Walk on Friday 1 October.  Three stages of walks will be organised for people with different abilities and a barbecue lunch will follow at the Burra Creek.
