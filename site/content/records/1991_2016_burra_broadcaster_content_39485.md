Burra Red Cross.  Australian Red Cross celebrates its centenary this year and so does the Burra branch.  It will mark the occasion by planting red roses in the garden outside R. Murray’s Office in Commercial Street.  Local Member for Stuart, Dan van Holst Pellekaan will be invited to unveil the plaque.
