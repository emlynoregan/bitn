The Alf Romeo Owners’ Club of Australia will be in Burra next weekend for its National Rally.  It is celebrating its 21st anniversary.  The 120 or so attending will be accommodated at the Paxton Square Cottages, Burra Motor Inn and the Burra Hotel.  There will be a street parade on Saturday morning and a 160 km observation run, finishing at Leasingham Winery for lunch.  The Show ʼn’ Shine display will be on the BCS Oval on Sunday.
