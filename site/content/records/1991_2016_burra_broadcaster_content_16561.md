Burra Pony Club attended the Northern Zone Pony Club Eliminations at Marrabel 11-12 July.  The eliminations test dressage, show jumping and mounted games.  Burra was placed second in show jumping and won ten out of ten in the games and the club will now represent the Northern Zone at the State Championships at Glenelg in September.  Kimberley Field won Horse and Rider of the Year for the Northern Zone and will ride for the State title in September.  [Photo.]
