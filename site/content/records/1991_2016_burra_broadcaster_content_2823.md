NSW Bushfires.  Seven men from Burra and Mt Bryan CFS units left on Monday to go to Sydney to help fight the NSW bushfires: Arnie Lomman, Kenny Cox, David Thamm, Mark Bellman, Peter Mengersen, Norm Mason and Kim Haebich.  They will be on the job for five days.
