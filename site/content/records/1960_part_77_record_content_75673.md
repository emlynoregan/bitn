The Citizenship Ceremony at the Town Hall on Thursday drew about fifty people.  Mr & Mrs Derek Bos became Australian citizens.  The Chairman of the District Council Mr Bill Brown welcomed visitors and handed the new citizens their certificates after they had repeated the Affirmation of Allegiance.  The school Welfare Club provided supper.  Mr & Mrs Bos live in Lewis Street.
