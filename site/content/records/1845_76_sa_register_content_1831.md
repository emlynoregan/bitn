Advertisement

The ‘Reformer’ stage cart will leave for Burra Burra next Monday at 8 o’clock.  Fare £1 and baggage allowance 14 lb.  Apply to Nicholls & Ross, Temperance Hotel, Hindley Street, Adelaide.
