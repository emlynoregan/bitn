Richard J. Sinclair of the Railway Camp was sentenced to six months on Thursday for larceny from the Used Car Yard of Jennison’s Tyre Service on Wednesday night and three months concurrently for driving whilst under suspension.  Later on Thursday he escaped from the police cell at Burra North by tearing out the inner sheet of iron and bashing out the outer sheet.  He was found hiding in the ceiling space of a House in George St about 3.30 p.m.  He was sent to Pt Pirie lock-up and on Friday had another month added to his sentence.
