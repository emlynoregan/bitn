2011 Burra Cycling Classic

The interim police officer at Burra, Matt Daum, will ride in this event at the weekend.  The new permanent officer Jamie Lewcock will be present in an official capacity.  A second permanent officer will be posted to Burra in the near future.  Officers based at Burra serve a vast area from Robertstown to Spalding and further.
