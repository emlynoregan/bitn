Rev. Milton Edwards of Burra will go to Perth on the East-West Express on Saturday, leading a group of 18 young people giving voluntary service at the Mogumber Aboriginal Training Centre.  Work parties have been doing this for 15 years.  The Mission farm has 3,000 sheep.  The party returns to Adelaide on 15 January.
