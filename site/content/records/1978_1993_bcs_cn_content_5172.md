Jubilee 150.  A public meeting will be held at the Civic Centre at 2 p.m. Wednesday 25 November.  Mr Kym Bonython Chairman of the J150 Board will speak.  Topics under discussion will include:

Scale model of the Burra Mine in 1860

A facsimile newspaper

Merino sheep industry history

Preservation of the manual exchange.
