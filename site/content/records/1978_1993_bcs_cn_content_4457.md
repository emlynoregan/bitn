Burra & District Advisory Committee held its annual meeting last Thursday.  Brian Polkinghorne spoke of their achievements:

More than half the tree planting program had been done.

The townâ€™s signage had been upgraded.

Public seating had been placed in Market Square.

A public noticeboard had been erected in Market Square.

He hoped members would become more active in bringing forth ideas from the groups they represent.

Elected for the coming year: Chairman, B. Polkinghorne; Vice-Chairman, C. McCabe and Secretary, J. Brak.

Members suggested Council explore other means of control in place of burning off the creek area.

Des Woolley and John Brak will prepare two versions of the directional plate for the lookout for consideration at the next meeting.

The committee expressed its displeasure with the proposed subdivision at Burra North.
