Obituary.  Hedley Bradtke Stockman, husband of Xenia, died on 3 October aged 69.  He was the father of Avis (Mrs J.N .Clarke), John, Rex and Peter.  There are five grandchildren.

[Born 4 April 1898 Hundred of Mannanarie: died 3 October 1967 Broadview Estate, District of Burra.]
