The Guild had been founded in 1926 by the late Rev. Harry Alvey with the strong support of his wife.  The minutes of the first meeting were read again with the names of the ladies who had joined in the first year.  Other speakers were Rev. Don Haydon (President elect of the Methodist Conference), Rev. George O’Neill (Chairman of the Middle District), Rev. Pitman, Rev. Clem Christopher, Mrs M. Odgers, Mrs Boyce and Mr Colin Fuller.
