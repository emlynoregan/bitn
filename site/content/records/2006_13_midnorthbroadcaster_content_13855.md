Irene Hirschausen has been honoured by the Red Cross after exceptional contribution to the local organisation.  The current Burra Red Cross President Pat Kakoschke presented Mrs Hirschausen with the 70 Year Long Service Medal.  Mrs Hirschausen now lives at Carinya in Clare.
