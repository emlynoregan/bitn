Burra Burra DC has released a Draft Street Tree Policy.   Guidelines for the removal and modification of trees are to be developed.

Council should consider pruning trees in the 1st and 2nd years to minimise the impact of ETSA pruning in the 3rd year.

Tree planting in heritage area is to be examined and a working document to be developed.

Research history and significance of tree planting in Burra.

Research concerning the planting of preferred and non-preferred species.

Identify in-fill projects.

Reinforce the entrance to town plantings.

Screen existing service areas.

Public comment will be sought prior to adoption by the Council.
