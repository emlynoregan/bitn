Mick Brooks is the new Merchandise Manager for Elders in Burra.  Nick started work with Dalgety Bennetts Farmers in 1984 and moved to Wesfarmers.  In 2000 he resigned to work on the family farm at Hallett until 2007, when he applied for the position as Merchandise Manager with Elders.
