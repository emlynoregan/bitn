Burra Hospital AGM was held in the Burra Town Hall last Friday.  It was chaired by Mr R. K. Pearse, Chairman of the Board of Management.  A minute of appreciation was recorded for the services rendered by Mrs Andrew Tennant (perhaps 13 years, but not legible) and Mrs John Gebhardt (10 years), both of whom have retired.  Another minute of appreciation was recorded for Secretary-Manager Mr H.L. Foote, who has resigned.
