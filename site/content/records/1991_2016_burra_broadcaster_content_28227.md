Burra Golf Club hosted the Women’s Golf SA Bronze Plate golf meeting last week.  119 ladies played in the two day event last Tuesday and Wednesday and 57 pairs competed in the foursomes on Thursday when conditions were much colder.  The 36-hole Championship winner was Margaret Murray of Penola.
