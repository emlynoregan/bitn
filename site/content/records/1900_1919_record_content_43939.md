Redruth Methodist Sunday School anniversary was held last Sunday.  Rev. Thomas P. Willason of Saddleworth officiated.  The tea meeting on Monday was largely attended.  F. Sara (Sec.) said 84 girls and 64 boys were on the roll and average attendance was 114.  There were 25 teachers.  Two old scholars had so far been killed in the war: R.J. Woollacott and Pte Camp.  The Treasurer, Mr H. Fuss, reported receipts of £48-17-1½ and expenditure of £45-4-7½.  Anniversary takings were £12-10-1, so the new year starts with a credit of £16-2-6.  Primary and Kindergarten reports are printed.
