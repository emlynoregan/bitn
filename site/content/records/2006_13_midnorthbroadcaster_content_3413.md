Hoon Drivers are a nuisance in Burra and the surrounding districts.  Peter Staunton, owner of the Royal Exchange Hotel says young drivers are always rallying on the roads near the hotel.  He says that three out of four weeks they are kept awake by the noise from 1 a.m. to 3 a.m.  It is ridiculous.
