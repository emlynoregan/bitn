Redruth Methodist Women’s Fellowship held its AGM at Redruth Hall last Wednesday.  23 attended and elected: President, Jeannine Randall; Vice-President, Cynthia Walkley; Secretary, Mary Woollacott and Treasurer, Dianne Day.  A Senior Citizens Christmas Party is planned for 5 December.  Mary Woollacott’s Annual Report is printed.
