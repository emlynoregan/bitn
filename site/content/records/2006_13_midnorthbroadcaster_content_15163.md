The Burra Classic Cycling Race for 2012 was contested at the weekend. [Photo]

Results:

Winner of the 92nd Burra Handicap over 125 km was Thomas Kaesler (U19) who also recorded the fastest time.

Peter Reilly Handicap was won by Scott Nolan.

52 km U17 Peter Reilly A Grade Handicap was won by Matthew Holmes who also recorded the fastest time:

1 hour 20 minutes 50 seconds.  [Photo]

32 km U17 Peter Reilly B Grade Handicap was won by Cameron Waters who also had the fastest time:

58 minutes 2 seconds.
