The Mobile Breast X-Ray Unit will make its 4th visit to Clare to provide free screening mammograms to local women on 15 July and depending on demand will conclude in Clare in early September 1998.  Women aged 50 to 69 will receive an invitation, but all over 40 are eligible and can phone for an appointment.
