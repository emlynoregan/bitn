7 August 1978 [This irregular date must be an error for 10 August.]

The Covenant Players, a theatrical group that presents plays with a Christian theme will visit BCS on Thursday 14 September and will present short plays for students from Years 7 to 11. They will give a family concert at Redruth Camp Centre Hall at 7.30 p.m.

Burra Cricket

A meeting will be held at the Commercial Hotel at 8 p.m. on Wednesday 16 August to discuss the formation of a single cricket club in Burra.  The proposal is to merge the Koonaburra and Footballers Cricket Clubs to form two teams within the one club.
