Burra Town Council, 4 October

Among land transfers were Pt Block 13 from the Corporation of Burra to the SA Housing Trust and Pt Block 13 from the Corporation of Burra to ETSA.

Cr Cornelius resigned from the War Memorial Committee on account of poor health.

Council will write to Mr Quirke MP to find out what has developed re a Court House and Police Station at Burra.

Several private streets were declared public.
