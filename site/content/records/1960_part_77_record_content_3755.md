Football.  North Eastern Association Grand Final

Rain made the ground a little heavy and slippery in patches and a strong wind favoured the northeast pocket.  About 700 spectators saw the opening bounce at Spalding Oval.

Burra 7.4 defeated Booborowie 2.1
