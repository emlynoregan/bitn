Performance of Mendelssohn’s ‘Elijah’

On 18 September at 3 p.m. there will be an Endorsed Bicentennial Presentation of ‘Elijah’ at St Mary’s Anglican Church featuring the combined choirs of the SA Country Choral Association and the soloists: Jillian Chatterton (soprano), Guila Tiver (contralto), Malcolm Potter (tenor) and Ralph Hatcher (bass).  Professor David Galliver will be the organist and the conductor will be the President of the choirs Mr Boyd Dawkins.  There will be a choir of 120 voices.
