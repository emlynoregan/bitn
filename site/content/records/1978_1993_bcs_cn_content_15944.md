SA Decathlon-Heptathlon Championships were held in Adelaide last weekend.  Conditions on Friday night were atrocious.  Petrea Piggott, Cheryl Day, Adam Day & Tony Secomb competed creditably.  On Saturday:

Petrea won the U16 women’s heptathlon

Adam won the U18 men’s heptathlon

Tony won the U16 men’s heptathlon

Cheryl came 2nd in the U16 women’s heptathlon.
