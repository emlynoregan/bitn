The Burra & Communities Christmas Fair will take place on Friday 21 December.  There will be the usual pageant and Father Christmas will meet the children in the Rotunda.  Live entertainment will be provided by Kerry Davy & students.  The Youth Advisory Council will provide a bouncy castle and a rock climbing wall.  Burra Cricket Club have organised a barbecue.  Householders are invited to take part in the 2012 Christmas Lights Competition and there is a Christmas Fair Colouring Competition for the kids.
