The Bush to Burra Cattle Drive has received major sponsorship from AGL Energy Ltd and it will now officially be known as the AGL Bush to Burra Cattle Drive.  It will take place from 7 to 11 October 2008 and will raise money for much needed medical equipment for communities in the Mid North.
