Fire Ban.  Seasonal conditions have resulted in a complete fire ban over the Northeast and Northwest Fire Ban Districts and most of the Flinders Ranges until 31 March 1976.  Winter rain has produced a lot of growth this year.  Most fires so far have been started by lightning.
