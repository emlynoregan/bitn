Accident/Obituary.  A 43-year-old Mt Bryan man died when his silver Honda motorcycle collided with a blue Ford Falcon sedan on the Barrier Highway just north of Burra last Monday at c. 6.20 a.m.

[Page 13 supplies the details: Craig William Irlam born 12 September 1966: died 7 October 2009. Husband of Gayle, son of Kath & Ross Irlam (deceased), brother of Neil, Suzanne (deceased), Leeanne, Ray, Sharon, Sonya and Natalie.]
