This Special Christmas Edition of the Burra Broadcaster will be distributed to Broken Hill, Sevenhill, Kybunga, Yacka, Hart, Hoyleton, Hilltown, Blyth, Jamestown, Terowie, Whyte Yarcowie, Manoora, Mintaro and Spalding in addition to the usual districts of Burra, Mt Bryan, Farrell Flat, Hallett and Booborowie.  Over 5,000 copies will be mailed.
