Burra Burra DC

An offer of $40,000 for O’Connor’s Shed at Burra North has been received.  Council has decided to offer the shed for sale at $75,000 until the end of December, after which time it will also be offered for lease.

Council is to have the land owned by Stockman’s adjacent to the Burra Golf Club rezoned as industrial.
