Brian ‘Dusty’ Berryman and son Craig of Burra have begun a five-week dirt bike ride across the heart of Australia, beginning on Sunday morning at the Eastern Road turn-off.  The 12,000 km trip will encompass Marree, Birdsville, Gibb River, Halls Creek, Glen Helen and Broome.
