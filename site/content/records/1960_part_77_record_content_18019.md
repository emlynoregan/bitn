Burra Youth Club.  This recently formed club now numbers 137.  Friday’s meeting heard a First Aid talk by Mr R. Thamm, using an amplifier supplied by Gerald Baulderstone.  On Sunday there was a 25 mile cycling tour.  Arrangements are in hand for a week’s camp at the Gorge from 5 January.
