Rifle Club.  Match at Pt Adelaide 2 Feb. to decide the champion country club saw Burra come in first.  Burra 1,800, Wasleys 1,763, Murray Bridge 1,714, Gawler 1,708, Strathalbyn (playing with 1 man short) 1,664.
