Burra Swimming Pool Committee held its AGM 23 October.

Elected: Chairman, B. Polkinghorne; Secretary, J. Brak.  The committee donated $1,200 to the Burra Burra DC to cover the cost of repainting the amenity block at the pool and to do other necessary repairs to the building.
