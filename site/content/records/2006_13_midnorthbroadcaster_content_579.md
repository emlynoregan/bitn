Burra’s O’Brien family did very well at the Pt Augusta & Districts Country Music Festival in April, taking away 8 trophies:

Danny O’Brien gained 1st in golden oldies and 3rd in the open male sections.

Tracey Rains won 1st in open female and also in in duet, with her mother, Karen O’Brien.

Tracey Rains, Sam Rains, Karen O’Brien, Nathaniel O’Brien and fellow vocalist Krystal Collins came second in the open group section.

Nathaniel O’Brien came first in the junior vocal section and third in the 16 years and under gospel section.
