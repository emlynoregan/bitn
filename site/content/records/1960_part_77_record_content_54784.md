St Mary’s.  Two vacancies in the Trustees of the Church occurred with the death of J.R. Barker and the resignation of Mr Andrew Tennant.  Mr John Burge was appointed to replace Mr Barker and Mr Ronald Thamm to fill Mr Tennant’s vacancy.
