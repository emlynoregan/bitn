State Election

Mr E.C .Allen [Photo] has been re-elected for the seat of Frome.  Mr Allen was elected for Burra in 1968.  This electorate was incorporated in Rocky River and Frome in 1970.  He was returned at that election with a majority of 128 and this year had a majority of 1,645.

In the Northern District for the Legislative Council the LCL candidates Dick Geddes and Arthur Whyte [Photos] have defeated their ALP opponents.
