Ian or Ivan McGrath [depending on which line you are reading] of Burra North, recently won a trophy as the most proficient cadet of HMAS Leeuwin’s junior naval cadets.  Aged 16, he is the son of Mr & Mrs F.G. McGrath who made the trip to Perth for the passing out parade when 147 cadets graduated.
