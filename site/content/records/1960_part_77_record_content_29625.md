Obituary.  The late Maurice Ignatius Williams was the eldest son of Mrs and the late Mr T.M. Williams of ‘Willila’ Carrieton and was born at Quorn.  He was educated at Carrieton and Rostrevor College and spent his youth at Cultana via Whyalla and at Carrieton, Mt Bryan East and ‘Baldina Downs’.  In latter years he had been an ardent supporter of the Burra Football Club.  He was an enthusiastic worker for St Joseph’s Church and Convent.  He died aged 56 and is survived by a wife, daughter Pauline, his mother and five brothers and five sisters.  A Requiem Mass was held at St Joseph’s on 7 May.

[Born 26 May 1909 Quorn: died 5 May 1966, Burra.  Note: incorrectly entered on SA Deaths CD as Maurice Igantius Williams.]
