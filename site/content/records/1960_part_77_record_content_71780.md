Burra Bowling Club.  President Darrell H. Field reports on 1973.

The club had a successful year, though it did not win any pennants.  In the Mid North Championship Rinks the First Division team of Ian Allen, Wally Scott, Keith Phillips and Bill Corner, came up Champions.  The club tournaments were run efficiently.  Winners:

Championship Singles	Kies van der Does

Handicap singles	Edgar Pietsch

Night Singles	Bill Corner

Consistency 	Brice Stockman

Championship Pairs	Edgar Pietsch & Les Phillips

Night Pairs	Keith Phillips & Kies van der Does

The Open Tournament at the end of January had to be abandoned due to rain.
