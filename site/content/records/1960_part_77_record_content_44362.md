Obituary.  Rev. Geoff. Sibly conducted the funeral service of Mr Jack Clemson who had been a long term patient at Burra Hospital and who was well known for his hand loom weaving.

[Corrected 92, 9, 11 Mar. 1969, page 3 (misnumbered 2) to say that Rev. Sibly played the organ and Rev. J. Stephenson conducted the service.]

[John Kerwin Clemson died 1 March 1969 Burra aged 87.]
