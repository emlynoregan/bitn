Royal Adelaide Show.  Mid North Studs dominated the sheep judging.  Springvale North was named the most successful exhibitor in Merino and Poll Merino autumn shorn and open classes, winning 19 ribbons from 24 entries and also winning Grand Champion Autumn Shorn Ewe for the 3rd year in a row.

Greenfields of Hallett also received a string of victories and East Bungaree of Hallett also did well.
