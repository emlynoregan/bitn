Burra CFS has taken delivery of a new Australian Fire Co. Appliance, valued at $225,000.  It is an Isuzu FTS 750 4x4 Crew – Engine 150 Kw (200 Hp).  It has a 2000 L water tank.  Burra’s old Hino truck will be going out to Booborowie to replace their petrol driven International truck.
