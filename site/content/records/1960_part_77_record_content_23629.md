Swimming.  At the Inter-Club Carnival between Clare, Spalding and Burra on Friday night Burra regained the Swimming Shield.  Mr Eric Tilbrook, standing in for his father, presented the Tilbrook shield to the Captain of the Burra Club, Heather Angel.  [Results are printed.]

A leading swimming coach, Mr Tony Wall, who is sponsored by ‘Rothmans’, will visit Burra on Monday 8 March.  He will give instruction to swimming instructors on how to teach children.

Vandals struck the Burra Swimming Pool on Friday night and dumped many items into the pool, including tables, chairs, trestles, poles lifebuoys and a steel grating weighing half a hundredweight.
