Burra Art Gallery has yet to be officially opened, but has been very busy.

The current exhibition has artists rotating paintings and new items added on a weekly basis.  Jill Foster’s pottery is particularly interesting.  Future exhibitions will be Marie Johnson-Harrison 2-28 July and ‘Junk Bonds’, Art from junk 2031 August.
