Burra Town Hall.  After its extensive refurbishment, the hall will be officially re-opened on 15 October.  On Saturday 14 the SA Police Band will play for a dance and the hall will be open for viewing 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.

The opening on Sunday will commence at 2 p.m., with a concert celebrating the history of music in the hall.  Admission will be free.
