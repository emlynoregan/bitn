Car Thefts.  A utility owned by Malcolm Pettet was stolen from the depot of Burra Rural Supplies in Morehead Street, Burra North on Tuesday afternoon and was later recovered near Yunta undamaged.  At c. 9 p.m. Thursday Burra Police recovered a vehicle stolen in Adelaide and abandoned on the Morgan Road.  On Saturday morning Burra Police also recovered a vehicle stolen in the Northern Territory last May, following up on information supplied.
