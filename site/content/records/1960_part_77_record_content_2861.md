Weather.  There were good falls of rain over the weekend.  Burra registered 90 points and to the east some heavy falls were recorded.  Braemar had 150 points, Woolgangi 110, Oakbank 114, Balah 121, Redcliffe 92, Thistlebeds 105, Glenora 103, Parcoola 150, Gluepot 130 and Koomooloo 94.
