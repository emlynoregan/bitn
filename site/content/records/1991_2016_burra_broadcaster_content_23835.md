Vandalism

Six local businesses were targeted over the weekend when graffiti were scratched into the windows of Water’s Bakery, Oppy’s IGA, Australia Post, Joe’s Super Snacks, Burra Motor Co. and McPhee’s Butchers.  If the glass has to be replaced damages could reach $13,000.
