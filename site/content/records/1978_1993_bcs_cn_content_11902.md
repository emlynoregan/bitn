Pam Marlow’s Burra Mine Model

The display in Bence’s window shows Morphett’s Engine House and Boiler House, Morphett’s Shaft, the shears and capstan.  Pam Marlow spent 250 hours construction time on this section and when completed the entire model will have taken the builder about 4 years.  Sponsorship is sought for the following sections:

Miners and horses

Offices

Stables

Blacksmith’s shop

Ore floors and jigging sheds

Whims
