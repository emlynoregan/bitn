Outside School Hours Care Service will provide care for mainly primary school children after school, on pupil free days and during school holidays.  A recent BCS Governing Council meeting approved the development of OSHC and a committee has now been established.  OHSC at Burra is expected to be up and running at the commencement of term 1 2005.
