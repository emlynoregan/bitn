Indoor Hockey.  President Gary Williams reports on a successful year for the club’s first season.

There are now more than 80 players and interested supporters.  The club has successfully run four junior and four senior teams in the two school terms and placed a women’s, men’s and junior team in the outdoors competition.  Five members were selected to play in the Clare Hockey Association Team to participate in the Country Hockey Carnival in Adelaide.  In the Grand Finals:

Junior	Hockey Busters 5 defeated Terminators 3
