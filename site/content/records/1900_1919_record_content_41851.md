The Escott Murder

The case came to a conclusion on Monday.  His Honour, Mr Justice Murray, summed up and the jury retired at 5.43 p.m.  After 2½ hours they could not reach a verdict.  On being sent back they deliberated for a further 1½ hours before returning a verdict of not guilty and the accused, Harold Escott, was discharged.
