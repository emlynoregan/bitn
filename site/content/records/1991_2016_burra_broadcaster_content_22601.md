Burra Red Cross recently held its AGM.  Average attendance for the year had been 12.7.  Fundraising for the year exceeded $2,700, including $1,665 as part of Red Cross Calling Appeal.  The Annual Zone Conference was held in Burra this year with some 34 members coming from Adelaide, Broken Hill and nearby towns.  Tony Thorogood was the guest speaker.
