Burra CWA.  The annual report of activities by Secretary N. Nankivell runs for 1⅓ columns.  Membership was 55 and average attendance was 25.  Mrs Jennison was the President. Five weddings were catered for.  State and Group Officers and branch members visited the cemetery in July to pay homage to Mrs Warnes, the first President and to mark the 40th anniversary of the CWA in SA.  The exterior of the CWA rooms were painted and six roses and a shrub were planted to mark the 40th anniversary of the CWA.  Mrs Baker had to resign as Treasurer and Mrs Bill Salter took over.
