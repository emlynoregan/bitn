Mal & Laraine Leyland visited Burra for two days and visited the museums, gaol and other heritage sites.  They are returning after a visit seven years ago.  Many will remember Mal from the popular TV show with his brother Mike in Ask the Leylands, which ran from 1976 to 1984.
