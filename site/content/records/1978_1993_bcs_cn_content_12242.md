E.T. Baulderstone writes to congratulate the writers of Community News last week and urges the community to work together and forget terms like ‘fly-by-nighters’.

Mrs Ellen Kenny of Adelaide, mother of Fr Eugene Kenny of Burra, celebrates her 100th birthday today.

[Born Ellen Keeley 20 February 1886 at Kadina.]

John Barton, local chemist, was naturalised as an Australian Citizen on 10 February.  He was born in Huddersfield, Yorkshire.

Matthew Hall is completing his studies at RMC Duntroon after returning from West Point Military Academy in the USA.  He will be 21 on 23 February.

Burra Ambulance held its AGM on 10 February and elected: Divisional Officer, Rosemary Opperman; Transport Officer, Pat Van Gasteren; Training Officer, Peter Ryan; Store and Library Officer, Gilda Pearce and Secretary, Katrina Hanlin.  Personnel strength is 11 qualified officers.

Farrell Flat Tennis Club.  New courts will be officially opened on Sunday 23 February.
