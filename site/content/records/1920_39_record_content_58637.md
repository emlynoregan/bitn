Fairest Lady Player		Miss Joyce Halliday

Fairest & Most Gentlemanly Player	Norman Villis

Highest number of goals (Men)	Mr Ivon Wohling of White Team (184)

Highest number of goals (Ladies)	Miss Florence Lamey
