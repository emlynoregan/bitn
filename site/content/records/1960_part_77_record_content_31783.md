Burra Golf Associates elected for 1966:

Patron, Mrs A.L. Collins; President, Mrs T.A. Padbury; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames M. Nicholls & R. Wuttke; Captain, Mrs D.H. Field; Vice-Captain, Mrs N. Finch; Treasurer, Mrs H. Crump; Secretary, Mrs E. Jeffery and Assistant Secretary, Miss Judy Oates.

The President reported a successful year.

Club Champions:

A Grade	Mrs E. Jeffery

B Grade	Miss Judy Oates

C Grade	Mrs M. Kemp

Laurel Wreath	Miss J. Oates

Silver Spoon	Miss J. Oates


Grace Barker Prize	Mrs N. Finch

Monthly Pins	Mrs Braendler & Mrs Kirk
