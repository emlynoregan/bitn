Burra High School.  Three Leaving students sat for Supplementary Exams and this brings to 13 the number of certificates gained by 14 students and a further certificate was completed.  Pam Zanker, who had been awarded an English Q, passed English, Barry Carpenter passed English and Valerie Donhardt passed Modern History.  English Qs were awarded to Ray Pietsch & Arnold Lomman, who had both passed six subjects and who now get an Intermediate Certificate.
