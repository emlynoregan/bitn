Burra Golf Club opened its season on Saturday.  Club President Mr John Gordon declared the season open and asked Club Champion Captain Stan Longford to drive the first ball off for the season.  Prior to the competition getting under way Mrs E.T. Baulderstone, President of the Burra Branch of the Red Cross, presented the Club with the winning pennant for donating the most money to Red Cross in the 1968 season.
