Burra Services

Note that the Sunday service from Hallett is not a regular service; it will only operate on school exeat weekends; the next being 22 March.  If not well patronised, it will be discontinued.

The afternoon service leaving Burra for Adelaide at 2.50 p.m. will be discontinued on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
