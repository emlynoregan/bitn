Mr Will Adey was farewelled from Burra School on Friday.  After 3½ years he moves to Penong as head teacher.  He had been involved with the Boys’ Gymnasium and the Swimming Club.  Appreciative speeches were made by Mr Oborn, Mr J.E.H. Winnall, Mr Radford (for the Boys’ Club & Swimming Club) and Cr T.H. Woollacott.
