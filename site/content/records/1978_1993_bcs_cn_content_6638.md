The Clare Concert Band will perform at Burra Town Hall on 22 October in a show called Something Happened with the Clare Concert Band.  About a quarter of the 54 musicians hail from the Burra District.  The evening combines music from many styles by the band with musical and dramatic items staged by school groups.
