Aberdeen Tennis Club held its AGM 26 August and elected: Patron, F.H. Reed; President, Robert Reed; Senior Vice-President, Kevin Lane; Secretary, Sid. Scroop and Financial and Minute Secretary, F. Scroop.

It is intended to have two ‘A’ Grade and two ‘B’ Grade teams if the players are available.  It was resolved to hold an Easter Tournament.   Mr Ivan Hirschausen was made a Life Member.

The President reported on a successful year in which the ‘A’ Grade team won the Premiership.  Membership was c. 60.  Just over 90 competitors took part in the Easter Tournament.
