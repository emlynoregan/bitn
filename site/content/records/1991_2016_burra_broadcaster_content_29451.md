Public Notice

Doug Wiltshire give notice that he has made a preliminary submission to the Minister for Local Government concerning the operations of the Goyder Council.

He says there is sufficient evidence now to widen the scope of his enquiry into the Council’s operations.

He urges anyone with specific knowledge of questionable conduct to contact him – concerning for example:

Excessive wastage

Poor work practices or supervision

Failure to act

Tendering concerns
