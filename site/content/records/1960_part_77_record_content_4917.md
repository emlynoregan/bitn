Mr & Mrs Jim Battye & Family are leaving Burra.  Mr Battye has resigned from his position as branch manager of Goldsbrough Mort & Co. and intends accepting a position as Country Representative for Legal & General Assurance Pty Ltd.  No replacement as branch manager has yet been made.
