Port Wakefield is very busy at present.

On 20 November there were 19 vessels: 5 ships, 7 barques, 1 steamer, 3 schooners and 3 cutters.

Arrivals showed 1 from London, 1 from Liverpool, 2 from Cardiff, 6 from Hartlepool, 1 from Newcastle on the Hunter and 8 from Port Adelaide.

8 were discharging coal for the Copper Co.

5 were loading copper ore or wool for Melbourne.

1 was taking on copper and wool for England.

1 was taking on copper and wool for Port Adelaide.

2 were discharging general cargo.

The steamer was employed in towing.

1 had arrived with a supply of water.
