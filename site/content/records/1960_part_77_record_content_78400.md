Burra Burra DC, 8 March

Details are given of the transfer of some land at the corner of West Street from Ian Trevor Allen, carrier, and his wife Margaret Leslie Allen, to the Burra Burra DC from 23 August 1974 and now declared to be a public road.

Chairman and Overseer are to liaise with the Apex Club over the restoration work at the Burra North Playground.
