Bowls.  North Eastern Women’s Bowling Association Champion of Champions was contested at Burra, Wednesday 13 March.  Mrs E. Chapman of Spalding took the title with Mrs P. Hanlin of Hallett as runner-up.

Champion Rinks were decided at Burra 20 March:

In Section 1 Burra Green were winners.

In Section 2 Spalding Red were victorious.
