In the Grand Final on 18 September at Snowtown BBH 4.8 defeated Blyth/Snowtown 0.6.

Best players were T. Brak, A. Webster, S. Haverland, S. O’Bryan, S. Thompson, D. Schuppan, K. Thomas and S. Robinson.  Goal kickers were A. Webster (2), A. Spicer and S. O’Bryan.
