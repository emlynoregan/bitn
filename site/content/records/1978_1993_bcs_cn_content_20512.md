Sara’s Seconhand Goods & Chattels & Burra Wool.  Maureen & Barry Wright thank all customers for their support over the past seven years and advise:

As from 1 May the wool and stockfeed business will be owned by Lyndon & Michelle Plew, trading as Burra Wool & Rural Traders.

The Wrights will continue to operate Sara’s Secondhand Goods & Chattels.

Barry Wright will continue to buy wool at Broken Hill on a regular basis, trading as Barry & Maureen Wright.
