Maureen & Barry Wright are embarking on another voyage along the Murray River to update their book River Murray Charts.  This time they will also be part of the Centenary of Federation Sources to Sea event.  Close to 150 boats will take part in the ‘Great River Murray Paddlewheeler, Heritage and Leisure Boat Voyage 2001 – Echuca to Goolwa.  [Photo.]
