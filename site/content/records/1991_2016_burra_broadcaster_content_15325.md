Burra-Morgan Road

The celebration of the completion of the sealing of Highway 64 is being organised for Sunday 29 March, with a bike ride from Burra Creek to ‘The Gums’ for the opening and lunch and then on to Morgan.  Teams are being organised now and entry forms are at the Council Offices.  Prize money is being offered in several categories.
