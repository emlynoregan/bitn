Burra Burra DC

The burning off period has been extended to 29 November on account of the green grass around, but the potential fire hazard is quite alarming.

A management committee will be appointed for the Farrell Flat Hall.

Council has decided to exempt from the new building act (1974), new buildings outside the township for permanent occupation.

The delay in opening the swimming pool was due to difficulties with the filtration plant.
