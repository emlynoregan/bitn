On Friday night there was dancing to The Peter Gunn Band at a ball in Burra Town Hall, which resulted in proceeds of $1,137.  It was preceded by a pre-ball party at the home of Mr & Mrs L.A. Beinke at which the winner of the Miss Upper North Zone Competition was judged.  Winner was Miss Beverly Pink of Clare.

The competition on Saturday saw events for the boys in Merino judging, wool classing, AIS dairy cattle, welding and ewe culling.  And for girls in cooking, dressmaking, craft art and wheel changing.

There were also competitions in metal work, photography, sheaf tossing, shearing, poultry, pigs, cattle, live-weight judging and motor cycle skill trials.  One competition involved identification of weeds, seeds, machinery parts, liquids, cloths and glasses.

On Saturday evening there was a debate and public speaking, a mannequin parade and awards presentations.
