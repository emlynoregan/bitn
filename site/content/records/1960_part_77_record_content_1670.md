Mr Terry Welsh of the ANZ Bank in Burra has been transferred to the Grenfell Street Branch after almost two years here.  He was Captain of the Kooringa tennis team, a Burra footballer, a member of the Kooringa Methodist Choir, a Badminton Club member, leader of the Methodist Youth Fellowship and Treasurer of the Burra Rural Youth.  He leaves on 12 April to be replaced by Mr John Paynter.
