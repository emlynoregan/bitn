BCS has had success at the Royal Adelaide show in the Schools Led Steer Competition.

A Murray Grey steer Wyong Enzime gained 1st prize ‘On the Hook’ and 4th ‘On the Hoof’.

The Angus steer was 2nd in the heavy weight class and 2nd in the Cornell Suzuki ‘Hook and Hoof Trophy’.

The Poll Shorthorn steer gained 5th place ‘On the Hoof’ in the heavy weight class.
