Olive Groves.  The Regional Council of Goyder has adopted a policy concerning development approval for olive groves.  Olive groves constitute a change of land use and so become development under the Development Act 1993 and consent is required.  The Animal & Plant Control Commission of SA is concerned to minimise the spread of olives into native vegetation.
