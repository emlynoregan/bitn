SAPSASA Championships Country Day was held at Santos Stadium, Adelaide, 16 October.  52 athletes from 13 Mid North schools competed with students from other country areas of the state.  Burra Community School was represented by Jaide Bass, Anna Lehmann, Amy Berryman and Emma Stockman.  [Photo of the four girls.]
