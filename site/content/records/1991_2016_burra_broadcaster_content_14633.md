McPhee’s Butcher Shop

Gary & Joanne McPhee have been operating their shop in Burra for ten years and in that time have trained four apprentices and now have a staff of nine between their Burra and Clare meat stores.  Russell Skinner, Chairman of the Main Street Committee presented Gary with a Burra Achievers Award in recognition of their value to the community.
