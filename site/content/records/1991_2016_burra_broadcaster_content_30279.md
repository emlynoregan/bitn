BCS.

Rob Knight is the new Principal.  For the past three years he has been Principal of East Murray Area School.

Beth Wiley is the permanent school librarian.

Richard Everett is the permanent Technical Studies teacher.

Elaine Arthur has been appointed for 12 months for Reception/Year 1 teacher.

Judith Hawker has been appointed for 12 months for Special Education, Years 2/3 Class Teacher, Senior Secondary Work Education and SOSE

Grigor Fahlbusch has been contracted for Term 1 Art & English.

Robyn Nourse is the new permanent School Service Officer and Classroom Support.

Roger Boehm is Senior School Co-ordinator for Term 1 while David Hutchinson is on long service leave.

New students are listed.
