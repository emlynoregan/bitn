Burra Cards

The Burra Branch of the National Trust is sponsoring the book Burra Sketch Book with text by Ian Auhl and 30 drawings by the young Burra architect Maurice Perry.  From the 30 drawings five have been selected and reproduced on cards in time for Christmas.  The set of five with envelopes will cost 75c and individual cards will cost 20c.  The five selected are: 	Morphett’s Engine House  [Pictured in the paper.]

Redruth Gaol

Market Square

Bon Accord Mine Manager’s Residence & Engineer’s Shop

Burra Railway Station
