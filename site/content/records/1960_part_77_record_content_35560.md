Young People of Burra were addressed by Rev. David Merriman at a social evening on Friday night.  He talked on young people confronting the conformity of the day.  The evening included a number of musical items and concluded with dancing.  Rev. Merriman is a Baptist minister from Semaphore.  A collection of district clergy also attended.
