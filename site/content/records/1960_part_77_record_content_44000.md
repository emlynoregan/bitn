Accident.  A car driven by Mrs C. Skilton, resident at the Bon Accord Hotel, Burra North, skidded and overturned, ending back on its wheels at about 6 p.m. Friday.  The incident occurred on the Burra-Booborowie Road.  A seat belt helped Mrs Skilton to escape with only bruising and a shaking.
