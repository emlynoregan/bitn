Advt.  Elder’s will auction the pastoral holding of 318 square miles known as ‘Quondong’ on 3 September, for Gallagher Bros. who are relinquishing their pastoral interests.  The property has been held by the family for over 60 years.  There is a stone homestead of six rooms etc.
