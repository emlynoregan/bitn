Burra Homing Club.  The club’s champion race was flown from Roseworthy on 3 October.  First was Mr Josling’s b. c. h. Blair Athol & b. c. h. Atholine.  Flew 60 miles 360 yards in 95½ minutes at 1109 yards/min.
