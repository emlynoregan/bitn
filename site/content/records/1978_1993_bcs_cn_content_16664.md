Redruth Gaol Opening

A map showing the layout for the ceremony is printed.  The public is asked to be in place by 10.45 a.m. The Governor and Lady Dunstan will be escorted from the Tregony-Ludgvan Street intersection by the police greys and traffic will be restricted at that time.
