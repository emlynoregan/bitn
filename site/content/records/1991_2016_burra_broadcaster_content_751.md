The Last Passenger Train to Burra will arrive on 19 September.  It will be greeted on arrival by a light jazz combo and the National Trust fire engines and harness club will circulate in the area.  Clubs, groups or individuals desirous of having a promotional or sales table at the event are asked to phone 922438 to register their interest.
