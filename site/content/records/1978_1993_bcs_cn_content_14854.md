Obituary.  Mrs Marj. Jeffery died last week.  She was a teacher at Burra Primary School for many years and a long time Burra resident.

[Born Marjorie Olive Tiver, 27 May 1903 at Redruth: died 26 June 1987 at Burra.  Married Roy Joseph Jeffery in 1928.]
