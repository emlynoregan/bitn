The building was packed on Sunday for the Anniversary Service.  Mr Reg Harris on behalf of the Westbourne Park Church and other visitors gave a greeting.  Mr Harris spent his early days in Burra and is a great grandson of William Woollacott, who brought the large boiler to the Mine on the jinker.  Mr Lindsay Thomas, who spent all his life in Burra until recent years, returned and sang King All Glorious.
