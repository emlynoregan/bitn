Town Hall Concert.  A fine concert will be presented at the Burra Town Hall on 28 February featuring Thomas Edmonds (tenor), Genty Stevens (soprano) and accompanist Peter Tillett.  The evening is being sponsored by the Aberdeen Tennis Club.  Adults $1.
