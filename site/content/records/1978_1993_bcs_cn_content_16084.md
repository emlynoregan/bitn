Junior State Athletics Championships were held last weekend and Burra’s representatives performed brilliantly.

Cheryl Day won gold in the U14 200 m and U14 400 m.  She gained silver in the U14 100 m, discus and U16 300 m hurdles along with a bronze in the U14 800 m.

Petrea Piggott won the U16 800 m and gained four silver medals in U16 90 m hurdles, U16 200 m, U18 100 m hurdles and U18 400 m hurdles.

Tony Secomb won the U16 300 m hurdles and won bronze medals in the high jump and javelin.

Adam Day won gold in the U18 400 m hurdles and bronzes in U18 high jump and 800 m.
