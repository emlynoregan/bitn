Kevin Cailes of Hallett has recently been re-united with his son Greg, who he last saw 23 years ago when Greg was six months old.  His then partner took Greg to Perth and Greg has spent the last 10 years searching for his father.   Greg and his partner Jessie have a four-month-old son Kayden and have been staying with Kevin during the holidays.
