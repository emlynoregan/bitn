Obituary.  Olive May Hempel died at Burra Hospital on 25 April aged 54.  She was the wife of Arthur Hempel of Spalding and the mother of Avis, John, Lindsay, Jeffery, David and Brian.  There are nine grandchildren.

[Born Olive May Phillips 31 July 1912, Redruth.  She was first married to Kenneth Max Auhl.]

[See extended obituary 90, 19 (2), 16 May 1967, page 1.]
