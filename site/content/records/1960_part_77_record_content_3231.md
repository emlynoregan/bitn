Burra North Red Cross had only eleven members at its recent AGM.  Elected: President, Mrs Jennison; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames V. Cox & T. Wall; Secretary, Mrs E. Heinrich and Treasurer, Mrs S. Scroop.

Although the membership is 54 only about 25 are active and the average attendance at meetings is 15.  During the year three members died: Mrs Gare (Patroness), Mrs Sara and Mrs Clode.

[Clara Ethel Gare died 24 August 1959 aged 85, Emma Price Sara died 31 August 1959 aged 94 and Ada Jane Clode died 25 February 1960 aged 63.]
