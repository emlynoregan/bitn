J150 Plans

A John Williamson Concert on the Creek is planned for late January.

A special issue of the Burra Record is planned for December 1994.

A Kapunda to Burra Wheelbarrow Push is programmed for 20-22 January to end with the John Williamson concert.

Australia Day will see a big breakfast and Citizen of the Year Award, an Open Bowls event and the official launch of the 150 celebrations.

The Tennis Club will hold ‘In Cellar Cool’ again.

On 3 March the Law Society’s Country Legal Conference takes place.

Merino Field Days will also be held in March.
