The Cheer-O’s Concert.

The lady members of the Burra Cheer-up Society organised a concert for the purpose of raising funds for the supply of comforts to soldiers in the trenches.  The concert came off last Tuesday and featured: the Misses A. Harris, A. Bullen, L. Jesser, D. Harris, H. Hunt, B. Pearce, L. Scott, D. Herbert, L. Burns & M. Isaacs.  Nett proceeds were £17.
