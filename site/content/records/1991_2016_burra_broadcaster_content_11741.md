Mt Bryan School pupils, with the assistance of the Petherton Road Tree Planters Landcare Group and the Mt Bryan Community Group, planted five rows of trees and shrubs in the old railway yards opposite the school.  Last year 75 native shrubs were planted and this year 196.  [Further details and a photo are printed.]
