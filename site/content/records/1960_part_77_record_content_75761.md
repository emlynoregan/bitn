A letter to the editor expresses the writers’ extreme concerns about the disastrous course being pursued by the Federal Labor Government under Whitlam and the SA Labor Government under Dunstan and specifically against the escalation in pornography, easy divorce, increasing social service benefits and the general undermining of moral standards.  Signatories were:

Sylvia Pearce	M. Tiver	Maureen Furst

Glenda Bailey	Betty Cousins	Jo. Quinn

Olive Holmes	Melva McInnes	Eileen McMahon
