Mr & Mrs Pat Griffen celebrated their Golden Wedding at ‘Delora’ Mt Bryan on 20 November.  Some 30 friends and family attended.  Their daughter Kathleen (Mrs Max Mudge) came from Whyalla.  The couple were married in the Mt Bryan East Methodist Church 23 November 1915.
