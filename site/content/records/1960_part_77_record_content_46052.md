Mid North Basketball Ball was held at Burra Town Hall.  Door takings were $341.  Prior to the ball the presidents and secretaries of the clubs and judges of the Miss Mid North Basketball Competition were entertained by the committee of the Aberdeen Club, which hosted the event.  Mr & Mrs Scott of the Kooringa Hotel catered for the event.  Miss Carol Twigden of Riverton was chosen as Miss Mid North Basketball for 1969.
