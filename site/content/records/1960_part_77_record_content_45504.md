Burra Town Council, Final Meeting, 21 April

The meeting began at 7.30 p.m. in the [District] Council Chambers in Commercial Street.

An application will be made for a subsidy on Mine Area improvements to the value of $2,000.

It was resolved that Council call tenders for the supply and installation of heating at the Town Hall.

A penguin rubbish bin is to be bought and placed at Burra North at a site to be selected.

Crs Moreland and Kotz were thanked for their contribution during their terms as Councillors.

Burra Small Bore Club.  Best over double 50 yards were, J. Bowden, M. Thomas & R. Gilbert.
