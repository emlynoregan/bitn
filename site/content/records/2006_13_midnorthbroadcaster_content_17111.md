Theft.  In the early hours of 15 June the Burra Sports Complex was broken into.  Smallgoods and liquor were stolen and damage was sustained.  A 21-year-old Clare man has since been arrested for unlawful possession and theft.  Three other youths have also been charged in association with the break-in and a further two youths have been charged with receiving stolen property.  Investigations are ongoing.
