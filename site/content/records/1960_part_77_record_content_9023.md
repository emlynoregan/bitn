Notice.  Commonwealth of Australia Electoral Division of Wakefield

A poll will be contested on 9 December 1961.  The Candidates will be:

Charles Robert Kelly of Tarlee MHR

John William McMahon of Leighton, farmer

Collin James Wood of Elizabeth East, organizer

[Voting places in Burra were the RSL Hall and the Court House.  There were also polling places at Leighton Hall, Hanson Institute, Mt Bryan Hall and the Booborowie School.]
