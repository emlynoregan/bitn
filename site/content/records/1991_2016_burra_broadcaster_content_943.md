Burra Mine Sesquicentenary

In 1995 Burra will celebrate the 150th anniversary of the beginning of operations at the Burra Burra Mine and the consequent establishment of the town.  The Burra 150 in 1995 Committee invites participation in the planned celebrations and would welcome ideas for inclusion.  Their next meeting will be at the Civic Centre on 5 November at 8 p.m.  Come and contribute ideas or join the committee.
