Football. North Eastern Association

The ‘Mail’ Medal for the best and fairest player was decided at Booborowie on Friday night last.  The winner was Barry Eberhard of Burra.  The runner-up and winner of the ‘Burra Record’ Trophy was Don Villis of Booborowie.

In the B Grade the winner was R. Sanders of South Clare.

For the Colts the winner was G. Milgate of South Clare.

The Learn to Swim Campaign of the Education Department will take place at the Burra Swimming Pool 9-20 January.
