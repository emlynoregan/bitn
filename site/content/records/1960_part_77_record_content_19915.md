Burra Primary School

On 24 April Mr H. Cornelius addressed the students on Anzac Day and presented prizes in connection with an Anzac Project.

Primary winners were Kym Bannister and Melva Ellery.

Secondary winners were Helen Wurfel and Garry Costello.
