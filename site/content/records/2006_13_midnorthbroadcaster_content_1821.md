Peter Reilly was born in Burra and lived all his life in the area.  He was educated at Burra and Rostrevor College.  He is a farmer and has been heavily involved in sporting and community activities.  He is President of the Burra Show Society and a Patron of the Belalie Mokota Cricket Association.  His ambition is to maintain the local hospital and to raise the awareness of the need for youth employment to keep young people in the area.
