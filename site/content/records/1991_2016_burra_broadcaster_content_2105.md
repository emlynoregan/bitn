Paxton Square Cottages

The Lewis Board of Management announced a nett surplus of $38,944.81 for 1992-93.  In 1992 the Square won the State Tourism Award for budget accommodation.  Elected for the coming year: Chairman, Robyn Page (re-elected), Vice-Chairman, Helen Stockman (re-elected), Secretary, Jan Tiver and Assistant Secretary, Sue Warnes.
