Norman Hiles Pearse of ‘The Gums’ is growing ponderosa [sic] tomatoes of wonderful size.  One sent to us weighed 1lb 4½oz.  Other varieties were climbing potato leaf, white beauty and navel gem and each one weighed over 1lb.
