Wayne Burton, formerly a Mt Bryan student, recently earned a PhD from the University of Melbourne.  He attended Mt Bryan Primary School and then Burra High before completing his secondary education with two years at Norwood HS.  He now lives in Horsham, Victoria.  His thesis was on ‘Adaptation of canola quality Brassica juncea to low rainfall environments’.
