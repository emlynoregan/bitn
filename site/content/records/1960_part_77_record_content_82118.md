Booborowie Methodist Ladies’ Guild held its AGM and elected: President, Mrs A. McGuire; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames M. White and Sutton; Secretary, Mrs Janet Price & Treasurer, Mrs G. Dewhirst.  Mrs M. White retired as President after 13 years and Mrs M. Pearce retired as Treasurer after 16 years.
