Burra Golf Club Centenary Dinner was held at the Sports Complex on Saturday.  More than 90 members enjoyed the occasion.  On Sunday a day on the greens was enlivened by the local talent of Tracey Rains with the band Claypan and also Nathaniel O’Brien.  [9 photos.]
