Burra Traders’ Association will present another of their highly successful street fairs on Sunday 9 June – the Queen’s Birthday long weekend.  With the new motel now operating a good crowd is expected.  Colleen Spackman has organised a doll show with over 100 porcelain dolls on display.  Proceeds for this exhibition will go to the National Trust.
