BCS enrolments for the start of the school year:

Secondary	225

Primary	220

Total	445 (Up 6 from 1980.)

New Staff:

Mr Neil Brown from Pinnaroo Area School is teaching in the Year 5/6 unit.

Miss Bronwyn Howard a graduate of Adelaide CAE has taken over Commercial Studies.

Miss Joy Mason from Port Lincoln Junior Primary is teaching in the Junior Primary area.

Miss Cynthia Nenke from Crystal Brook Primary School is teaching in the Junior Primary area.

Mr Roman Piwowar from Alberta, Canada, is replacing Mr Geoff Bayly.
