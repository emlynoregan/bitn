Advt.  Goldsbrough, Mort will sell 8 March at Booborowie Hall 506½ acres 3 miles north of Booborowie for W.J. Kain who is relinquishing farming, comprising Section 837 Hundred of Ayers 150 acres freehold and Section 841 Hundred of Ayers, comprising 356½ acres perpetual lease.
