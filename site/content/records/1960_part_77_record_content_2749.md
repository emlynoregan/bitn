Obituary.  Mr Johannes Alwin Heinrich died at Tanunda on 31 May.  He was born at Baldina 17 April 1884, the eldest son of Gottlieb Wilhelm Heinrich and Anna Emilie nee Hetzel.  He was baptised at Baldina 18 May 1884 and confirmed at Immanuel College at Point Pass in 1897.  He had lived in the Apoinga District from about 1909, only moving to Tanunda three weeks before his death.  On 14 September 1911 he married Mathilde Maria Heinrich who died 18 April 1943 and on 30 September 1949 he married Mrs Hilda Maria Eckernann.  He took a keen interest in church and civic affairs and was church treasurer for 44 years and a frequent delegate to synods.  He is survived by a wife, son, and grandson, two stepsons, three brothers and four sisters.
