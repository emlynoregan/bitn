Adelaide Wool Sales.  In the final series of the season the top price for the district was 88d for Messrs S. Lord of Balah, E.L. McWaters & Co. of Samson’s Well and Radford Bros of Canegrass.  (H.J.C. Collins of Pulpara received 91¾d)
