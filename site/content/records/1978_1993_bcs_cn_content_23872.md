BCS.  Interschool Athletics

56 BCS students competed at the Olympic Sports Field in the Secondary Schools Sports Association Athletics Carnival.  The girls competed in Division B1 (the 3rd highest grade) and the boys in B2.  Both finished 6th in their divisions, which is a good result considering the size of the school compared with others.
