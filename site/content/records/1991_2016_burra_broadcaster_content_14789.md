Burra-Morgan Road

The section from Burra North to Ford’s Corner was sealed by April.

The next section to the Robertstown turn-off was sealed in June and the road re-opened to traffic.

The final section from Robertstown turn-off to ‘The Gums’ was then sealed by December.
