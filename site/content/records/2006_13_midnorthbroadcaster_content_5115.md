Burra Probus held its AGM and elected: President, Pauline Venning; Vice-President, John Fuss; Secretary, Darryl Venning and Treasurer, Murray Tiver.  Outgoing President John Fuss reported a busy year with highlights being a luncheon cruise on West Lakes and a visit to Haigh’s Chocolate Factory, visits to Sevenhill Wine cellars and a barbecue at Redbanks Conservation Park.
