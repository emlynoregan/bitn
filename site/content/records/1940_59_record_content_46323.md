Burra Town Council, 19 June

The last meeting of the old Council was chaired by the Mayor Mr F.T. Marston, who thanked Councillors for their support and wished the incoming Council well.  He was sorry to see the retirement of Councillors Lee and Field.  Cr Field was retiring on account of work and Cr Lee because of age and ill health.
