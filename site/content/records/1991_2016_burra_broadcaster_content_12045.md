Mr Steve Kerrigan, the CEO of Burra Burra DC has been appointed the interim CEO of the new Regional Council of Goyder.  The new council is expected to be proclaimed 3 February 1997.  Mr Kerrigan has been CEO at Burra for five years and prior service was with the District Council of Lower Eyre Peninsula and the City of Mitcham.
