The Barton Hill Ram Sale last Tuesday attracted buyers from all areas of the state as well as strong local support.  G.M. & E.P. Radford of Burra secured eight rams.  95 of the 100 on offer were sold with a top price of $4,900 and an average of $1,308.  David & Jo Gebhardt of Barton Hill said they were very pleased with the result.
