Burra Show drew a good crowd on a relatively fine warm day.  Gate takings were $3,200.  There were plenty of trade displays and the Flower Pavilion was filled.  Features this year included:

Obedience Dog Club demonstrations

Teddy bear display

Pet show

Adchem video display

Helicopter flights

Harley Davidson rides

Unveiling the new three-door hatch Toyota Echo

Performing cockatoos

Princess of Show was Kaye-Leigh Cunningham.

Prince of Show was Simon Helbig of Clare.

Junior Princess was Vivien Murphy.
