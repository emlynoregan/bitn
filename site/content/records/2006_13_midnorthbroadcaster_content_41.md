Commonwealth Games.  The Queen’s Baton Relay will pass through the Mid North next week.  It is expected in Farrell Flat at c. 9.04 a.m. and will then go on to Burra, where Tim Simpson will start the Burra leg at the Hospital at c. 9.41 a.m.  He will hand the baton to Sharyn Roberts and it should leave the town via Kingston Street by about 9.57 a.m. for Eudunda.
