Obituary.  Mr Ralph Thomas, well-known grazier of the Eastern Pastoral District, died on 22 May.  He was born at Tooralie north-east of Burra 15 April 1891 and died at the Royal Adelaide Hospital aged 73.  He was educated at Mt Bryan East and served in WWI.  He married Miss Tryphosa Thamm at Redruth Methodist Church 26 January 1921.  He lived at Mt Bryan East and served on the Hallett District Council for some years.  He then moved ‘out East’ where he remained until his death.  He is survived by his wife and children: Linda (Mrs Jones), Ida (Mrs Afford), Stuart and Bert.  There are 11 grandchildren.  He was buried at Burra Cemetery on 25 May.  Burra RSL Members conducted a ceremony of remembrance.

[George Ralph Thomas born 15 April 1891 Hundred of Wonna: died 22 May 1964, Adelaide, residence Hogback Station.]
