Burra Primary School

Mr Ian Auhl welcomed official guests at the traditional ‘passing out parade’.

The Mayor presented the prizes for the Local Government Essay Contest, with the 1st Prize going to Daphne Launer and a tie for second between Kym Bannister and Brenda Willmott.

Progress Certificates were presented to:

Kym Bannister	Peter Osterhage	Trevor Wall

Philip Edwards	Robert Pride	Daphne Launer

Roslyn Gill	Lynette Corner	Wendy Spackman

Christine O’Brien	Gay Williams	Melva Ellery

Lucy Field	Brenda Willmott	Malcolm White

Peter Opperman	David Mueller	Malcolm Napper

Gayle Brandt	Christine Gill	Jennifer Carpenter

Lorraine Jones	Pamela Rawnsley	Susan Richards

Christine Blieschke	Dianne Finch	Mandy Simmonds

Kym Bannister was Dux of the boys and Daphne Launer Dux of the girls.

Headmaster R.V. Mueller welcomed the students to the Secondary School.

The Education department will allow a Library Room to be established.
