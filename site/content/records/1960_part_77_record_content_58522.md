Burra EFS held its AGM on Wednesday.  23 people attended. Only two call outs were attended in the past year.  Elected: President, Bruce Stockman and Secretary, Ron Shipard.  It was proposed the EFS co-operate with the SAR in burning grass along the railway line which makes a great firebreak through the district.
