Burra Rural Youth held a very successful Debutante Ball in the Burra Town Hall on Friday night.  Attendance was c. 400.  Eight debutantes were presented to Mr Casey MP and Mrs Casey of Peterborough.  They were:

Susan Murphy with Michael Scholz

Julia Pens with Philip Lannon

Dianne Mitchell with Gordon Pearce

Jill Przibilla with Allan Drew

Raelene Atkins with John Kellock

Lynette Winders with Kevin Dixon

Janice Drew with Timothy Przibilla

Pam Mickel with Murray Mickel
