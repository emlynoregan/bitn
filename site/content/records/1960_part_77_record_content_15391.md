Football.  This year the Mid North Association will comprise:

A1: Clare South, Clare North, Aberdeen, Riverton, Auburn, Saddleworth, Farrell Flat and Robertstown.

A2: Marrabel, Manoora, Mintaro, Sevenhill, Watervale & Riverton.

Tarlee has gone into recess.
