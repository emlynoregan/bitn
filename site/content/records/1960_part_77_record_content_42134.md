Obituary.  Mr Bob Fuss died recently aged 82.  He will be buried at 11 a.m. tomorrow.  A life member of the Show Society, he will be missed for his years of voluntary work in the pavilions.

[Lawrence Robert Fuss born 25 October 1885 Aberdeen: died 7 October 1968 Prospect, residence Burra.]
