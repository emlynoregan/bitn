Birth.  To Patrick & Michele Mahoney of Sturt Vale Station, at Burra Hospital on 19 June, a daughter, Rosemary.  She is a sister for Rebecca, Craig and Joshua.

Rosemary is also the last baby booked in to be born at Burra Hospital and he birth marks the withdrawal of obstetrics services at Burra.
