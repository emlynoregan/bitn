Accident/Obituary.  Julie Kathleen Cullen (3), the daughter of Mr & Mrs Henry Cullen, died on Thursday after being run over by a truck being prepared for one of its regular trips to eastern stations.  At about 10.25 a.m. it ran forward and passed over her as Mr Cullen was about to enter the cab.  She was taken to Burra Hospital with internal injuries and died within half an hour.
