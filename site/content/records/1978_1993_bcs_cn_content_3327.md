The Melbourne Cup Luncheon on 4 November was an outstanding success.  One of the biggest crowds ever enjoyed magnificent wine and food and a mannequin parade of fashions from ‘Dress Sense’ of Clare.

The proceeds will be distributed:

$580 	Burra Hospital

$200	Burra Burra DC for the Town Hall

$100	Burra Red Cross

$50	M & BHA
