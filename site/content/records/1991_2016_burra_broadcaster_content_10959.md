Cancer Appeal

Mrs Elizabeth Hounsell of Burra & Mr Colin Springbett of Farrell Flat will ride horses 750 km from Broken Hill to Adelaide, starting on 20 September on a 30-day journey.  They are raising money to defeat cancer.
