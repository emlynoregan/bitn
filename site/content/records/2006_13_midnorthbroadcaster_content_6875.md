Regional Council of Goyder has announced dates for public meetings at various locations to discuss with electors the matters raised at the Ratepayers’ Action Group public meeting of 4 September last and any other matters of interest.  Key matters raised then were Paxton Square, Burra Caravan & Camping Park, Burra Swimming Pool, and Goyder Sports & Community Centre.  The Burra meeting is scheduled for Burra Town Hall Monday 23 February 7 p.m.
