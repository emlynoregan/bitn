Mrs R.A. Lynch and Mr Jack Lynch late of the Commercial Hotel Kooringa, left on Monday after 14 years in the town.  Mr J. Kemp has acted as manager since the death of Mr R.A. (Con.) Lynch in 1936.  Mrs Lynch has not been enjoying good health and so has decided to retire.  The Mayor S. Kellaway and a representative gathering of townspeople gave a farewell and presentation at the hotel on Saturday evening.  Mr S. Kellaway thanked the family for supporting town sport and other matters.  Mr A. Lomman referred to Mr Jack Lynchâ€™s sporting prowess as a member of the Cricket Club and at basketball.  Other speakers were Mr C.R Lott, Mr Horace Hill & Mr C.J. Butler.  Mr Kemp responded on behalf of Mrs Lynch and Mr Jack Lynch also responded appropriately.
