CWA Country Woman of the Year

Mrs Bruce Stockman, who was nominated in this state-wide contest by the Hanson Branch, was declared the winner of the Warnes’ Group section on Friday night.  The winner of the grand final on 29 April receives a trip to New Zealand for two.  Other Warnes’ Group candidates came from Black Springs, Burra, Clare, Farrell Flat, Riverton, Spalding and Yacka.
