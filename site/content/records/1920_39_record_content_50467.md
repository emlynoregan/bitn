Mongolata Goldfield

SA produced 3,014 fine oz in 1932 and 6,361 fine oz in 1933.

Over 70% of this passed through Government batteries and cyanide works.

Mongolata battery produced 39.8% of the total.

Tarcoola 15.9%

Mt Torrens 10.8%

Peterborough 4.5%
