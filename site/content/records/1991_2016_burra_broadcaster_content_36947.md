Burra Joey-Cub Scout Group.  The first investiture for this recently re-formed group was held on 29 June.  The new Joey-Cub leaders are Jack Webster and Anthony Jones.  There are about thirty members in the troop.  A leader for the 11-14 year age group is needed.  Meetings are held in the Guide Hall.  [2 photos of the group.]
