SA Tourism Commission CEO Mr Bill Spur launched the latest version of Clare Valley Secrets and the Heartland Heritage Trail booklet, based on the ideas of Andrew Glen, at Martindale Hall last week.  Clare Valley Secrets is a free 60-page booklet covering the area from the Clare Valley and Burra to the Eastern Region.
