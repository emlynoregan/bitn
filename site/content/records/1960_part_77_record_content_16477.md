Golf.  Burra’s annual inter-club match against Jamestown resulted in a win to Burra 12 matches to nil with one all square.  The Associates were also successful, beating Jamestown 4 to nil.

Burra Associates Open Day was held on Thursday last and was played in ideal conditions after the early morning frost.
