Australia Day was celebrated at Pickett Reserve.  The Sing Australia Choir provided the leading voice for the singing of the National Anthem.  Guest speaker was Mr Dan van Holst Pellekaan MP.

The Burra Citizen of the Year was Peter Cochrane.

Junior Citizen of the Year was Bethany Jones.

Event of the Year [by inference] was Burra Picnic Races.

Nathaniel O’Brien was given a posthumous award for Outstanding Contribution to Burra and Districts, which was accepted by his sister, Tracey Rains.  [5 photos.]
