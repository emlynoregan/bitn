St Mary’s will have a festival of Easter music this Sunday.  The service will be conducted by the Rector of St John’s Auburn, Rev. Fr L.E. Sutcliffe assisted by Rev. Milton Edwards of the Burra Methodist Church.  The music has been arranged by the Rector of St Mary’s Rev. John Stephenson who will play the organ.  The Quartet comprises Elizabeth Beare (soprano), Jan Cockington (alto), Allan Edwards (tenor) and Lynden Beare (bass).
