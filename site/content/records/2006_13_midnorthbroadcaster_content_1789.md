Burra Visitor Centre Manager Andrew Glen will hand over the reins to Anita Stokes after four years with the centre.  The centre is run by BRTBA.  Andrew will move on to a position as manager of the Gawler Health Foundation, which raises funds for the Gawler Health Service.  [Photo.]
