Paxton Square Chapel.  Paul Wilson has the job of re-roofing the Booking Office in Paxton Square, which was formerly the Bible Christian Chapel.  The work includes removing the hessian ceiling.  Removal of the iron revealed the original shingle roof c. 1850.  [Photo]
