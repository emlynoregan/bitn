Mr Killicoat was born in Parranwell [Perranwell] Cornwall on 18 August 1844 and came to Australia with his mother in the Malacca when aged 10.  His father had come earlier in the Abberton with a shipment of mules from Monte Video.  Mr Killicoat Senior purchased Abberton Park which, when he died in 1877 was one of the largest estates in the district.  The deceased took over the estate on his fatherâ€™s death and also held considerable land to the east.  He had been a mayor of Burra and was a member of the hospital Board for 16 years.  He was also President of the Show Society, a member of the Burra District Council and active in the Church of England.  He married in 1874 and had 5 sons and 7 daughters.  Mrs Killicoat was in Adelaide when the accident occurred.
