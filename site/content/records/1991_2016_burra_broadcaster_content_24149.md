Di Day has been presented with the Australian Sports Medal in recognition of her services to golf.  The medal has been awarded during the year 2000 to commemorate Australian sporting achievement and is a commemorative award within the Australian honours system.  She was presented with the medal at the Burra Golf Club on Friday night, receiving also a certificate signed by the Governor-General Sir William Deane and the Prime Minister, John Howard.
