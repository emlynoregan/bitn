Sportspersons of the Year

Estelle Nicholls is Senior Sportsperson for 1986.

She was the winner of the Singles Club Championship and a member of the Pairs and Triples teams and also Champion of Champions of the North Eastern Women’s Bowling Association.

Darren James is Junior sportsperson of 1986.

In cricket he plays for the Burra Senior Cricket side with a bating average of 94.8, including scores of 107 n.o. and 109 n.o.  He also took 25 wickets and 25 catches for the season.

In football he won the North Eastern Football association’s Junior Colts medal for Best & Fairest with a record 37 votes and won Burra’s Best & Fairest award.  He was also top goal-kicker with 68 goals.

In athletics he broke several school records in the under 14 discus, and shot put and was 2nd in the State Schoolboys’ Athletics Championships
