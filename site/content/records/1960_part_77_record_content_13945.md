Obituary.  Mr George Bass died at Dawes Road Repatriation Hospital last Saturday after a long illness.  He is survived by a widow and two daughters.  He will be remembered by old Burra residents as a good Burra footballer, tennis player and athletic runner.  After leaving Burra he played some matches for West Torrens in the SANFL.

[George Ernest Bass born 25 April 1911 Kooringa: died 17 November 1962 Springbank, residence Adelaide.]
