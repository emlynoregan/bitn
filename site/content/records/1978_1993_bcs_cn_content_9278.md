Burra Cubs and Scouts

A Venture Unit has been started under the leadership of David Jennings and is open to youths aged over 14 and young ladies are also welcome to attend meetings.  A self sufficiency exercise in the Wilpena Ranges is planned for the second week of the September holidays.
