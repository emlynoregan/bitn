Mongolata Goldfield

Baldina Mongolata GM Syndicate for fortnight to 30 October.

Farnden’s Hill.  Stoping continued 6ft to 40ft.  The leader is widening with values erratic.  Showing some micaceous iron at 18dwt per ton.  2½ tons or ore on hand.
