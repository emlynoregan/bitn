Burra’s Sesquicentenary

A public meeting to decide on how to celebrate this event was held last Wednesday and a steering committee was elected.  Mike Day chaired the meeting.  Suggestions included:

Peruse a list of suggestions from Ian Auhl

Look at booklets printed for the 1925 and 1945 celebrations for ideas

Art Exhibition of old Burra paintings

Displays through the year in the Burra Community Library

Research ethnic backgrounds

A multicultural event

Record oral histories

Archaeological studies

Mayoral Ball

Celebrate Thomas Pickett

Have school students write, produce and film a video of Burra’s History & enter it in Young Filmakers Awards

Re-walk the copper roads to Pt Adelaide

Celebrate the rural history of the district – particularly the Merino

A progressive dinner

The cycling classic

A Grand Concert

Another Burra to Broken Hill Barrow Push and/or a relay barrow push around Burra’s Heritage Trail

Conservation work at Hampton

Seal the Burra-Morgan Road

Complete photographic survey of Burra in 1995

Write the History of Burra from 1877

Pro-Am Golf Event

Run a marathon

Hold the Mid North Games here
