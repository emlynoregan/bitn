Peter Brodie writes to comment on the claim by The Minister of Health that services at the Burra Hospital were ‘as usual’.  This he disputes and cites his experience that requiring a very minor hospital procedure, which had previously been done there; he now has to go to Clare, get a referral and then make a second trip to have the procedure at Clare Hospital.
