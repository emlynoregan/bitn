SA Track & Field Championships were held last weekend.

Rosie Lloyd won the U18 400 m hurdles and U18 800 m as well as coming 2nd in U18 400 m.

Graham Kessell won the U18 javelin and was 2nd in the U20 javelin.

Justin Hall came 2nd in U16 300 m hurdles.

Petrea Piggott (13) came 3rd in the U16 300 m hurdles.

Craig McDowell came 2nd in the U20 high jump and 3rd in the U18 high jump.

Bill Stockman won the U16 pole vault.
