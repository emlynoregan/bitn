Accident.  Ronald W. Munn (13), a passenger in a vehicle driven by John Munn of Coonamia (Pt Pirie), suffered internal injuries and a fractured left leg when the vehicle overturned at the top of Flagstaff Hill on Saturday.  The driver received severe lacerations to his head, but did not seek treatment.  Another passenger Kenneth H. May (16) suffered severe bruising.  The vehicle was written off.
