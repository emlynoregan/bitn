The Schools Conference over two days recently was reported.  Dr John Mayfield reviewed recent policies and trends.  Miss Ailsa Farrow spoke on continuous fifth birthday admission.  Small group discussions on Thursday afternoon covered Maths, Science and Social Studies.  Mrs Margaret Haynes argued for the benefits of more traditional practices.  Fr Kevin Matthews outlined several educational models.  Don White spoke on community educational needs.  Tom Perry spoke on community, local government and school links.  Jim Giles addressed the varying educational methods in use today and a film on open space schooling followed with a talk on it by Harry Harris.  Finally David beck gave an account of how the Year 10 Religious Studies course was being taught at his school.

About 25 members of the local community attended along with teachers.
