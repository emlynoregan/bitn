Accident/Obituary.  Mr Max C. Kerslake aged 52, a contractor of Burra, was engaged in transferring drilling equipment from one site to another at the Burra Mine yesterday when the crane fell sideways and he was struck on the head by the falling jib.  He died instantly.

[Maxwell Carl Kerslake born 13 May 1914, New Glenelg: died 16 May 1966, Burra.]
