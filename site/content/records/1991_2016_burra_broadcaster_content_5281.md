Merino Field Days

54 studs took part in March.

Close to 700 people went to Burra Oval for the judging of the Merino Capital Champion Ram and Champion Pair awards.

Koomooloo Field Day attracted 35 suppliers and 200 spectators.

At Collinsville sales a Merino ram sold for $4,500 and a Poll Merino ram for $4,200.

96 rams brought an average of $865 and 24 Poll Merino rams averaged $1,117.

The East Bungaree sale averaged $641 for 88 rams with a top price of $2,000.
