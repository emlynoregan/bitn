Burra Races, 5 October.  The track was good and attendance fair.  It all went smoothly.  There were six races with the main race being the Burra Handicap over 1¼ miles for prize money of £35 for first, £7 for second and £3 for third.  It was won by Mrs L.M. Lewis’s ch. g. Centenary Star, 5 yr carrying 8 stone and ridden by M. Raven.
