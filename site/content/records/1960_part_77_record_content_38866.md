Geoff. Dare of Mt Bryan has been chosen to represent Australia as an International Farm Youth Exchangee.  He leaves Adelaide on Sunday for the USA.  He is a prominent member of Burra Rural Youth Club and of the Upper North Zone Committee.  He will take the P & O liner Canberra from Sydney to Vancouver and then travel to Seattle for orientation.  He will return to Australia in January.  He was farewelled at the Burra Rural Youth meeting last night.
