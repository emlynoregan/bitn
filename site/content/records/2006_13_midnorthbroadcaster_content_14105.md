Ken Grundy of Naracoorte writes expressing opposition to the inclusion of any mention of local government in the Constitution.  It would be dangerous for the state governments to be bypassed and for there to be direct links between the Federal Government and local bodies.  The issue goes far beyond funding, he asserts.
