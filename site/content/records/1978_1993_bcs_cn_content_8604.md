Burra Community Aid Abroad Group runs the Courthouse Craft Shop at Burra North.  The first target to raise $500 was reached in July and has helped to establish a palm leaf cottage industry at Madurai in South India.  The present target of $400 will aid displaced and orphaned children at the Karen Health Farm on Burma.
