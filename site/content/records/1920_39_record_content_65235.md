Glen Torr		Ronald Short

Kathleen Monaghan		Jean Angas

Harold Williams		Jean Keane

Aileen O’Connor		Joan Crossing

Nancy Short		Joyce Fiedler

Nellie Crocker

Addresses were given and Centenary Medals were distributed.
