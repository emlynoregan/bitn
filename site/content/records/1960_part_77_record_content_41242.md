Burra Town Council, 29 July

A plumber is to put the Town Hall toilets in order.

St Mary’s was given permission to have a winding pathway from the footpath erected.

Extra power outlets at the caravan park will be installed at a cost estimated at $324.  Application will be made to the Tourist Bureau for subsidy.

The Town Hall floor was discussed as noted above.
