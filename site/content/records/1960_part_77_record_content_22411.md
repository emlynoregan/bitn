Advt.  Kearns Brothers Pty Ltd will conduct an auction at the Burra RSL Rooms on 27 November under instructions from solicitors acting for the trustees in the Bessie Drew settlement.

Lot 1.	Land 85 ft 3 inches frontage to Commercial Street and a depth of 221 ft, running back to Mt Pleasant Street, on which is erected a stone and brick building of four shops with plate glass windows, storerooms and conveniences, subject to a lease to Bence’s Ltd, expiring in March 1965.  (One shop is subleased to Serv-Wel stores.

Lot 2.	Four lock-up shops built of bluestone and brick with plate glass windows.  Two let at £2 a week and two vacant.  Located in Market Street.
