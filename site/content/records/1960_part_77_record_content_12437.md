Burra High School.  The Jubilee History continued by R.V. Mueller.

1942

The staff comprised Leonard J. Easson acting headmaster, Marjorie Koehne, Joyce Brooks and Alan Hickinbotham.  The enrolment was 63.  Air raid trenches were dug early in the year and exercises held.
