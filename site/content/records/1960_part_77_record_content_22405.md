Burra Burra Hospital Inc.

The completion of the new private rooms and administrative block is now in sight.  Furnishings have been selected and an effective call system and telephone switchboard has been installed.  A car park has been provided and lawn areas are being prepared.  Vinyl tiles have been laid and painting is in progress.  J.W.M. Gillieson will take over from H.J .Foote as Secretary-Manager on 1 December 1964 and the new building will be opened by the Chief Secretary Hon. Sir Lyell McEwin on 11 December 1964.
