Burra Town Council, Special Meeting 22 January

Mr William Holness of Brown’s Well was appointed Town Clerk, to take up duties on 8 February.

Mr & Mrs Fullgrabe were given a surprise farewell party on Saturday night.  They leave next week for Robe.
