Kevin & Barbara Wallis have presented a proposal to Goyder council for the establishment and operation of a boutique beer brewery in the Unicorn Cellars in Burra.  Council decided that on receiving a business plan from Mr & Mrs Wallis, Burra Ward Councillors and Council staff would meet with them and the National Trust to investigate the proposal.
