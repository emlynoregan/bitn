The Great SA Bike Ride will be in Market Square on Monday 28 September, when some 450 riders and support staff will arrive from Eudunda on the second day of the event.  A gala day has been arranged with food stalls, and a pie and wine tasting by Glynne Ryan.  Riders will camp on the Town Oval and at night there will be a screening of the film ‘Breaker Morant’ at Redruth Gaol.
