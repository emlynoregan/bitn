‘Caroona’ Station was auctioned by Elders-GM last Friday in two lots.

‘Homestead’ and ‘Woolshed’ were purchased by Guardian Nominee Pty Ltd for $7.25 per acre for the 31,986½ acres.

‘Killicoat’s and Jones’s’ 13,988¼ acres went to H.J. Broad Pty Ltd of ‘Old Canowie’ for $8.40 per acre.

1,177 acres of ‘Homestead’ was excluded and will be kept by Flinders University for research purposes.
