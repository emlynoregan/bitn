Burra Town Council has accepted the resignation of the Town Clerk, Mr R.K. Baker, who will transfer to Henley and Grange on 14 November.  Mr F. Ascott, who has been Acting District Clerk during the absence of Mr T.G. Perry, has accepted the position of Acting Town Clerk till mid-January.  With amalgamation of the two Councils being discussed this will allow time for arrangements to be made.  The Town Councillors agreed unanimously in principle for amalgamation and will meet District Council members on early November to discuss acceptance or otherwise.
