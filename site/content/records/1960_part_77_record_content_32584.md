Teacher Transfers

Kev Reinke, head teacher at Farrell Flat we hear will move.

Ben Osman of Leighton School is transferred to Callington.

Jenny Overton of Hallett will go to Alice Springs.

Mr Gower of Booborowie will move to Robertstown.

Miss Lurelie Riggs of BHS will move.
