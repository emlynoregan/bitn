A large crowd of 250 old scholars and friends attended a function on Sunday 9 December to mark the closing of the convent.  Rev. Fr Kineary began with Mass at 10.30 a.m.  After the Mass as Angelus was sung by Mr Rod Gill of Victor Harbor, an original scholar in 1921.  Luncheon was then served and some sports run for the children.
