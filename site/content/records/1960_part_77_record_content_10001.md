Burra Open Swimming Carnival last Saturday was held in perfect weather.  Takings were £71 of which c. £40 should be profit.  [Results are printed.]

Burra Swimming Club.  Photo of the team which won the shield.

Burra Playground Committee has decided to improve the facility by installing a swinging boat.  Swings will be repaired and the seesaws, which are beyond repair, will be removed.  Council will be asked to co-operate in the erection of a new ladies and children’s toilet.
