Obituary.  Henry Hyde Winnall was born in Burra 8 May 1907 and died in Adelaide 21 February 1984.  He was educated at St Peter’s College and Roseworthy College, being the only person to be Dux in academics and Sporting Champion in the same year.  He bought a farm at Edillilie on the West Coast of SA, which he sold during WWII to become an Agricultural Economist with the Commonwealth Department of Primary Industry until he retired.  His father was Mayor of Burra 1902-3 and the family has a stained glass window in St Mary’s.  He asked that his ashes be scattered in the Mine Pool where he enjoyed swimming as a child.
