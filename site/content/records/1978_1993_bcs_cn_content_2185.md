BCS

Commercial Studies students visited the Burra Telephone Exchange on Monday and also had an introduction to the telex machine.

Instructors from Marleston College of Further Education have been conducting a wool classing course at BCS in the last three weeks.  [Photo.]

When it moved to its new offices the Burra Burra DC donated its ledger machine to the school’s commercial students.

Jan Davies and Ruth Bateman will soon open a specialty food store in Burra ‘Nuts ’n Stuff’.  They will stock a range of Continental gourmet supplies, Asian delicacies, health food and yeast cookery needs, herbs and spices.

Billets are sought for a possible visit for a weekend in Burra by Vietnamese refugee children.

Cricket.  Juniors Grand Final

Burra Juniors 170 defeated Booborowie Juniors 113.

20 March 1980
