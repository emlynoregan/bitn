North Eastern Hogget Competition.  The final was held at Burra Showgrounds on Wednesday. There were ten finalists.  The best were judged to be by S. & L.J. Wood from L.J. & H. Staker (Whyte Yarcowie).

In the single entry category the Champion Ewe Hogget was exhibited by L.J. & H. Staker.  The first two place getters were sheep of Bungaree blood.
