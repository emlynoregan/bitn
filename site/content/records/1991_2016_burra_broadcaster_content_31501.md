Peter Harris has been appointed Senior Authorised Officer of the Goyder Animal & Plant Control Board.  He has a family background in pastoral and farming areas and his recent experience in pastoral areas.  His role is to promote an advisory service, to identify, control and eradicate proclaimed pests and inform and advise the public and agricultural community of obligations and responsibilities under the Animal & Plant Control Act.
