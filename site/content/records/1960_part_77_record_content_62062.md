Burra Women’s Branch of the LCL held its AGM on Friday afternoon.  Hon. C.R. Kelly MHR attended as Member for Wakefield in the Federal Parliament and Mr Goldsworthy, the House of Assembly Member for Kavel attended as guest speaker.  Both paid tribute to Mr Claude Allen the local MP for his untiring effort.  Also attending were State politicians Messrs Gilfillan & Geddes, Mr & Mrs Allen, Mrs Goldsworthy & Mrs Daniel.  Mrs Daniel is the Chairman of the LCL Women’s Council.  Elected were: President, Mrs Ron Rollins; Vice-Presidents, Mesdames R. Pearse & W. Klem; Secretary, Mrs N. Finch & Treasurer, Mrs T. Padbury.  Mr Goldsworthy spoke on Education, focussing on the Karmel Report.
