Burra Sub-Branch of the ALP held its AGM on 26 April.  Elected were President, Jack Phelan; Vice-Presidents, W. Carpenter & V. Preiss and Secretary Mr Ben Kotz.  The guest speaker Senator Drury gave an informative address on the forthcoming Federal Referendums (27 May) urging a YES vote to both parts.  One part breaks the nexus between the House of Representatives and the Senate.  The other part relates to changing Section 127 of the Constitution to allow Aborigines to be counted as citizens.
