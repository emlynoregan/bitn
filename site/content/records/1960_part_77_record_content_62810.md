Burra High School held its Annual Ball in the Burra Town Hall on Friday with door takings of c. $400 and a profit of c. $300 is expected.    Kevin Kleinig was MC, the Talisman Band provided the music and Mr Claude Allen MP and Mrs Allen attended.
