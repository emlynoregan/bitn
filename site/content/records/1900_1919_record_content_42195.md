Redruth Court, 12 July

Carl Otto Ernst Kotz of Emu Downs, labourer, alleged that Benno Albert Frieddrick Kielow shot at him with intent to do grievous bodily harm.  Kotz had shot pigeons eating his fathersâ€™ wheat.  Kielow had then threatened to shoot him for shooting his pigeons.  Kielow had aimed at him and he then ran behind a tree.  The shot was fired from c. 120 yards.  Adjourned.
