Pacific School Games will be held in Sydney 2-9 December.  Forty SA athletes will take part and BCS will contribute three of them.  The games mark the official closing of the NSW Bicentennial Celebrations.

Petrea Piggott will contest the 400 m hurdles.

Cheryl Day will be in the 200 m and 400 m events.

Adam Day will participate in the decathlon.
