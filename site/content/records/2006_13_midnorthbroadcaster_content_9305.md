Dudley Afford (21), grandson of the late Ray Lloyd of Burra and son of Heather and Brett Afford, late of Saddleworth, received the prestigious Disability Rights Young Leader Award at the National Disability Awards in Canberra last month.  Mr Afford, who has cerebral palsy, is a spastic quadriplegic and legally blind, has not relented in his quest to contribute to the disability sector.
