Weather

It has been a week of fairly consistent rain, with strong gusty winds and hail and in some places snow.  Some shorn sheep and young lambs have succumbed to the conditions.  Mushrooms are plentiful.  So far in May Burra has received 539 points making 945 for the year to date.
