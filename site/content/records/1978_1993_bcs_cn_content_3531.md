Mrs Reta Jennison was last year given $5,000 for being the most active person over 60 in a national competition run by Legal and General Insurance.

The money was for community projects.  Mrs Jennison has handed a cheque to the District Clerk to be spent on Burra creek beautification, renovations to the Town Hall and seating in Market Square.
