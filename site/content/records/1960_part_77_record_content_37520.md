Accident.  Mr Leonard William McCann of Whyalla lost control of his car one mile east of Burra en route to a holiday in Sydney last week.  The car overturned, plunged through a fence and into the gully below.  The vehicle was a write-off, though neither the driver nor a passenger was injured.
