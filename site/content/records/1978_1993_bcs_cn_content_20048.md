State Athletics Championships at Kensington last weekend.

Cheryl Day competed in the U16 section and gained bronze medals in 400 m, 200 m and 100 m and was part of the Open Women’s 4 x 400 m relay and the U20 4 x 400 m relay, picking up a silver medal in each.

Adam Day won a gold medal as part of the Open Men’s 4 x 400 m relay.
