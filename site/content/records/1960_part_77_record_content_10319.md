State Elections: declaration of the poll at Burra Town Hall last Wednesday.

P.H. Quirke (Ind.)	3218

J.P. Bailey (Lib.)		2481

At the election Labor has won 19 seats and Liberals 18, with 2 Independents.  Mr Quirke has agreed to continue to support Sir Thomas Playford.  The final decision will rest with Mr T.C. Stott.  Sir Thomas Playford has resolved to test the matter on the floor of the House of Assembly.
