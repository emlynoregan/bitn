‘Skillshare’ has run a full-time six week Farm Operatives Course.  As well as practical skills, the course required research, job search skills and writing a résumé as well as work experience on farms.  Ben Browne, endorsed ALP candidate for Custance, presented participants with their certificates.

Local participants included Paul Hoffrichter of Leighton, John Whitford of Farrell Flat and Rino Kraus of Burra.
