Burra National Trust will conduct a bus tour to Waterloo and Apoinga 24 March, visiting Stilton Hotel Apoinga, Apoinga Smelting site, Apoinga Cemetery, Duke of Wellington Hotel at Waterloo for lunch and National Trust sites in Waterloo.  Coach rides will be taken at Waterloo.
