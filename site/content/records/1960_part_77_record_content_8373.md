Sir Walter Duncan will retire as Director on the Head Office Board of Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. Ltd on 30 September.  He will also retire as Chairman of the Adelaide Board, but continue as a director.  He has been Chairman of the Adelaide Board since its inception in 1924.  He was also Chairman of Bagot, Shakes & Lewis Ltd, which was absorbed by Goldsbrough, Mort & Co. in that year.  He served as a Director of Bagot, Shakes & Lewis for 11 years.  Sir Keith Angas will succeed Sir Walter as Chairman of the Adelaide Board.
