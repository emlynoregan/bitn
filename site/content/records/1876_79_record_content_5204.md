Cricket. Burra v. Seven Hills at Burra on 9 Nov. ended in a draw, though Burra was much 
in the lead with Seven Hills at 25 for 8 wickets & needing 48 to win. 
Burra 79 & 55 
Seven Hills 62 & 25 for 8 
On 11 Nov. Burra True Blues played Clare Young Australians at Farrell’s Flat. 
Burra 55 &3085 
Clare 45 &57= 102
