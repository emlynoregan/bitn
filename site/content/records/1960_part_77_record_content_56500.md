Reprint from SA Register 18 May 1871

‘With sincere regret we notify the sudden death of Mr John Winnall, draper of Rundle Street.  He was an old colonist and was formerly in business at the Burra where he will be interred in the family vault.’

[Obituary.  John Winnall died 16 May 1871 Glenelg, residence North Adelaide, aged 47.]
