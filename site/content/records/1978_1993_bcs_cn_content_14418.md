BCS.  Mid North Athletics Carnival

Burra won the trophy for the 7th year in succession last Thursday at Riverton Oval.

Petrea Piggott broke five records and Cheryl Day broke three.

Tasma Wooton, Nick Pearce, Justin Hall and David Madigan each broke one and the U15 Girls Relay Team and the Open Boys Relay Team also broke records.
