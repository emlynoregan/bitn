Peter Harvey & his wife Clare travelled to Sydney last week, where Peter was elected to the College of Fellows, Public Relations Institute of Australia.

He adds this honour to being a Life Fellow of the Australian Institute of Professional Communicators, a Fellow of the Society of Business Communicators and Fellow in Communication at the University of South Australia.
