Weather.  Last Friday and Saturday saw heavy showers bringing 157 points at Burra to bring the yearly total to 2,208 points.

Booborowie has recorded 2218 points, Hallett 2026, Mt Bryan 2012 and Farrell Flat 2344.

Falls to the east in the week ranged from 39 at Oakvale to 89 at Farella, with many falls in the 70s.
