Burra Tree Planting Committee has a grant of $600 from the ‘Greening Australia’ initiative.  Burra Burra DC will contribute $300.  The money will be spent on the Burra Creek, especially in areas adjacent to the bike track and some on the edges of the Burra-Mt Bryan Road.
