Mid Northern High Schools Sports were held at Riverton Oval on 7 May.

In the open section for the Ohlmeyer Shield	In the Handicap Section

Riverton	171	Riverton	215

Burra 	140.5	Burra	194.5

Clare	132	Clare	132

Balaklava	107	Balaklava	131

Kapunda	32.5	Kapunda	123.5

The Handicap Shield thus went to Burra.
