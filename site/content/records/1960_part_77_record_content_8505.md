The Prize List is continued.

Best exhibit in Floral Art was by R.V. Mueller.

M.R.E. Simon gained the highest aggregate points in Grain & Fodder.

In the Goat section the Champion Doe, Reserve Champion Doe and Junior Champion Doe all went to Mrs T.E. Gericke.

Aggregate points in Needlework: Mrs R.J. Jarmyn.

An extensive list of Horses in action is printed.

J.E. Zanker had the best collection of vegetables.

The school work display was again interesting.

S.H. & W.N. Wood of Kybunga exhibited the Grand Champion Ram, the Reserve Grand Champion and the Grand Champion Ewe.  The Grand Champion Ewe was entered by R.V. & N.S. Rzeszkowski of Arabar Mount Templeton.

Best Fleece was entered by K.R. Phillips and a list of awards in various classes is also printed.
