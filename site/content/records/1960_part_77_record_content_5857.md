Accident/Obituary.  Lloyd Cecil Pfitzner was found unconscious near a stationary engine attached to a corn crusher on his Robertstown property on Wednesday [8 March].  He had a fractured skull and died soon after admission to the Eudunda Hospital. He may have been caught or struck by a belt.  He was aged 30 and was single.
