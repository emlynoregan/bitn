Peter Thomas Fairchild, eldest son of Mr & Mrs Norman Fairchild of Mitchell Park and late of Booborowie, leaves Adelaide on 29 July to spend a year in the USA as an American Field Service Exchange student.  He is 18 and born at Booborowie and received most of his primary schooling there.  He has three brothers: Roland 14, Brenton 12 and Colin 7.
