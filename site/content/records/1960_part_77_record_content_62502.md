Metric Temperature measurement will be introduced on 1 September when measurement of temperature in degrees Fahrenheit will give way to degrees Celsius.

Apex.  A photo is printed of Apexians Grant Goode, Jeff Hirschausen, Gordon Wooton, Gino Santella & Phillip Ogden cutting wood for delivery to needy people in Burra. [Though six men are shown.]Next Wednesday at a dinner at the Burra Hotel President Terry Rule will hand over the presidency to Jeff Hirschausen.
