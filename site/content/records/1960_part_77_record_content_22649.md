Burra National Trust held its first AGM in the CWA Rooms 16 November.  Mr Andrew Tennant was re-elected Chairman.  Miss E. Tiver was re-elected Treasurer.  Mrs R. Pitcher resigned as Secretary and Misses P. & C. Brown were appointed Joint Secretaries.  During the year Aboriginal carvings at Baldina Creek were inspected and so were dugouts, Paxton Square and several shops and buildings.
