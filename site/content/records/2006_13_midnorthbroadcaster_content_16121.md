Murray Hall has opened a new hairdressing salon at Burra North – Hair By Murray.

He has more than 15 years’ experience in a variety of places, most recently at Jamestown.  He says he has been looking to find a house in Burra for some time and to set up a business here.  The salon is situated at No. 3 Young St. – formerly the Ice Creamery.
