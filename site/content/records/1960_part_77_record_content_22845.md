Burra Small Bore Club.  On 9 December a double 50 yards shoot saw P. Mengersen top both off the rifle and on handicap.  On Wednesday over double 75 yards Ross Gilbert was best off the rifle and Kevin Pope led on handicap.  On Monday night ten members took part in Morgan Club’s Christmas Shoot over 25 yards.  On Saturday night members visited Peterborough for that club’s Christmas Shoot.
