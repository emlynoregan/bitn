Work in Progress

This site is an early public preview. We are gradually importing Eric Fuss’s full archive into a searchable format. At present, the database includes four source documents:

  • SA Register (1845–1876) — early Adelaide coverage before a local paper.
  • The Record Series (1876–1879) — the first years of the local newspaper.
  • The Record Series (1880–1899) — late 19th-century local reporting.
  • Northern Argus (1985–1987) — regional coverage overlapping with local items.

More publications and years are being processed and will appear here soon.
